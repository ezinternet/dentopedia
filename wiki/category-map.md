## One-line Summary

Navigation layer grouping all ~95 method-based wiki categories under a 6-stage clinical workflow (diagnosis → hard-tissue surgery → perio/pharmacology → conservative/endodontics → prosthodontics/implants → orthodontics/specialty); folders are unchanged.
