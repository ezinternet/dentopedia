## 한줄요약
복합레진 및 세라믹 인레이·온레이·오버레이 장기 SR+MA (13편): 레진 5년 91%, 세라믹 10년 85% 생존 — 높은 장기 성적.

## Summary
A 2021 SR+MA in Operative Dentistry evaluating the long-term clinical performance of composite resin and ceramic inlays, onlays, and overlays. A systematic search to April 2019 identified 13 observational studies in meta-analysis. Composite resin inlay/onlay/overlay cumulative survival at 5 years: 91% (success rate 84%). Ceramic inlay/onlay survival: 90% at 5 years, 89% at 8 years, 85% at 10 years (success rate 88% at 5 yrs, 77% at 10 yrs). Secondary caries and endodontic complications were predominant failures for composite; fracture and endodontic complications for ceramic. No direct association between bruxism/parafunction and restoration fracture was found.

## Key Contributions
- **Composite resin inlay/onlay/overlay**: 91% survival, 84% success at 5 years
- **Ceramic inlay/onlay**: 85% survival at 10 years; 77% success at 10 years
- Secondary caries + endodontic complications: leading causes of composite failure
- Fracture + endodontic complications: leading causes of ceramic failure
- Nonvital teeth and multi-surface restorations → increased failure risk
- No direct link between parafunctional habits/bruxism and restoration fractures

## Methodology
- PubMed, Embase, Cochrane CENTRAL, Web of Science (≤April 2019)
- Inclusion: ≥5 years mean follow-up; RCT, prospective, and retrospective cohort studies
- Newcastle-Ottawa Scale for quality; random and fixed effects meta-analysis
- 2818 studies identified → 13 observational studies in meta-analysis
