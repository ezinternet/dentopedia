## 한줄요약
후향적연구(n=87,203 수복물, 5년): IPS e.max CAD CEREC 4종 — 전체 생존율 85.08%; 인레이/온레이 99.4%·베니어 98.6% 우수; FPD 52.9%로 유의하게 낮음(p<0.00001)

## Summary
The largest lithium disilicate survival dataset in this wiki: 87,203 restorations evaluated over 5 years at B&R Dental Center. Overall cumulative survival 85.08%. Inlay/onlay (99.4%) and veneers (98.6%) achieved excellent outcomes; FPDs performed significantly worse (52.9%, p<0.00001). Mean time to failure 52.4 months.

## Key Contributions
- Largest published lithium disilicate dataset (87,203 restorations)
- Inlay/onlay 99.4%, veneer 98.6%: clinically excellent at 5 years
- FPD 52.9% survival: significantly inferior to all other types (p<0.00001)
- Mean time to failure 52.4 months (CI 51.9–52.9)
- Failure causes: ceramic fracture, debonding, marginal adaptation loss, secondary caries

## Methodology
Retrospective analysis of lithium disilicate (IPS e.max CAD, CEREC) restorations placed March 2015–June 2020. Four types: inlay/onlay, veneers, single crowns, FPDs. Kaplan-Meier survival analysis. Log-rank test for group comparisons.

## Results
- Overall 5-year cumulative survival: 85.08%
- Inlay/onlay: 99.4%
- Veneer: 98.6%
- FPD: 52.9% (p<0.00001 vs others)
- Failure mode distribution: fracture > debonding > secondary caries
