## One-line Summary

SR+MA (33 studies, 10 in MA; GRADE low–very low): for storing an avulsed tooth before replantation, HBSS, propolis, oral rehydration salts, rice water, and cling film preserve periodontal-ligament-cell viability at least as well as (or better than) milk; saline and tap water are inferior to milk.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (33편, MA 10편; GRADE 낮음–매우 낮음): 탈구치(avulsed tooth) 재식 전 보관 매체로 HBSS·프로폴리스·경구수액염(ORS)·쌀뜨물·랩이 우유 이상으로 치주인대(PDL) 세포 생존율을 보존하고, 식염수·수돗물은 우유보다 나쁘다.

## Summary

When an avulsed permanent tooth cannot be replanted immediately, the storage medium determines periodontal-ligament (PDL) cell survival and therefore the prognosis (ankylosis / replacement resorption risk). This Cochrane First Aid / Belgian Red Cross SR+MA ranks layperson-accessible media against the field standards (milk, saliva) on a common surrogate — PDL-cell viability. It is the prevention-fails entry point of the wiki's avulsion management chain. Note: the outcome is an in-vitro/ex-vivo cell-viability surrogate, not clinical replantation healing, and GRADE certainty is low to very low.

## Key Contributions

- Confirms several accessible media — oral rehydration salts, rice water, cling film — perform at least as well as milk, broadening field options beyond HBSS/propolis.
- Delivers the clearest "do not use" message: saline and tap water are worse than milk.
- Saliva: insufficient evidence either way.

## Methodology

- Databases (Sept 2019): Cochrane Library, MEDLINE, Embase; 4118 references → 33 studies, 23 comparisons, 10 pooled.
- Dual independent screening/extraction/risk-of-bias; GRADE certainty.
- Effect measure: standardized mean difference (SMD) in PDL-cell viability vs milk.

## Results

Better than milk (higher PDL viability):
- HBSS: SMD 2.47 (95% CI 1.59–3.34), P<.00001
- Propolis: SMD 1.73 (95% CI 1.12–2.33), P<.00001
- Oral rehydration salts: SMD 4.16 (95% CI 2.10–6.23), P<.0001
- Rice water; cling film

Worse than milk (lower PDL viability):
- Saline: SMD −4.35 (95% CI −7.55 to −1.14), P=.008
- Tap water, buttermilk, castor oil, GC Tooth Mousse, turmeric extract

Certainty: low to very low (surrogate outcome, indirectness, imprecision).
