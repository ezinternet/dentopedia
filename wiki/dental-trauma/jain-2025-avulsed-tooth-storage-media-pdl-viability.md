## One-line Summary

In vitro study (n=62 premolars, 30-min dry + 45-min storage) finding that DNS and ORS maintain PDL cell viability equivalent to HBSS for avulsed tooth transport; fruit juice is the worst performer.

## 한줄요약

체외 실험 (n=62 소구치, 30분 건조 후 45분 보관) — 당포도당생리식염수 (Dextrose Normal Saline, DNS)와 경구수액 (Oral Rehydration Salt, ORS)가 한크평형염용액 (Hanks Balanced Salt Solution, HBSS)과 동등한 치주인대 (Periodontal Ligament, PDL) 세포 생존율 유지; 과일주스 최저, 에너지드링크 단기 대안 가능.

## Summary

This Indian in vitro study compared five storage media for maintaining PDL cell viability in avulsed teeth (simulated by freshly extracted premolars). After 30 minutes of dry time followed by 45 minutes of storage, PDL cells were enzymatically isolated and counted using trypan blue dye exclusion.

**Key finding**: DNS (5% dextrose + 0.9% NaCl) and ORS were statistically equivalent to HBSS in maintaining PDL viability. Energy drink (Monster) was comparable to DNS/ORS but significantly inferior to HBSS. Fruit juice (Frooti) was the worst performer due to low pH and high osmolality.

HBSS remains the gold standard, but DNS and ORS — both readily available in pharmacies, homes, and hospitals — provide clinically adequate alternatives when HBSS is not at hand.

## Key Contributions

1. **DNS and ORS = practical HBSS alternatives**: No statistically significant difference in mean viable PDL cells among HBSS, ORS, and DNS groups.
2. **Fruit juice not recommended**: Low pH + hypertonic osmolality significantly impairs PDL cell viability.
3. **Energy drink (Monster): short-term only** — acceptable if better media are unavailable, but inferior to HBSS.
4. Validates importance of storage medium over complete dehydration: negative control (8h dry) yielded mean viability of 1.0 vs. HBSS 341.8.

## Methodology

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Design | In vitro experimental |
| Sample | 62 freshly extracted premolars, patients <30y, closed apices |
| Prep | Coronal 3mm PDL scraped; 30 min dry time before storage |
| Storage duration | 45 min in each experimental medium |
| Groups | HBSS / DNS / ORS / Monster / Frooti / immediate (positive) / 8h dry (negative) |
| PDL isolation | Collagenase III + trypsin 10 min; FBS to neutralize |
| Viability assay | Trypan blue dye exclusion (0.4%), Neubauer hemocytometer, 10× |
| Statistics | One-way ANOVA; Tukey's HSD |

## Results

**Mean viable PDL cells by storage medium:**

| Group | Mean viable cells | SD | vs. HBSS |
|---|---|---|---|
| Positive control (0 min dry) | 521.8 | 22.6 | — |
| **HBSS** | **341.8** | **19.9** | reference |
| ORS | 320.0 | 23.4 | NS (p=0.187) |
| DNS | 316.2 | 22.7 | NS (p=0.065) |
| Energy drink | 298.1 | 15.0 | p<0.001 |
| Fruit juice | 287.5 | 19.1 | p<0.001 |
| Negative control (8h dry) | 1.0 | 1.7 | — |

**Pairwise conclusions:**
- ORS = DNS = HBSS (all NS)
- Fruit juice < HBSS, DNS, ORS (all significant)
- Energy drink = ORS = DNS (NS); energy drink < HBSS (p<0.001)
- Energy drink = Fruit juice (NS, borderline)
