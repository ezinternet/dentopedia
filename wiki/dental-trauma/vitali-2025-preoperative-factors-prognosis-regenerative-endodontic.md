## One-line Summary

SR+MA (20 studies, 13 observational + 7 RCT; GRADE very low): REP clinical/radiographic healing exceeds 81% regardless of pre-op factors, but trauma-related necrosis (RR 3.59), incisors (RR 1.90), and apical lesions (RR 2.55) carry a higher root-development-failure risk.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (20편, 관찰 13 + RCT 7; GRADE 매우 낮음): 재생근관치료(REP)의 임상·방사선학적 치유는 술전 인자와 무관하게 81%를 초과하나, 외상성 치수괴사(RR 3.59)·절치(RR 1.90)·치근단 병소(RR 2.55)는 치근발육 실패 위험이 더 높다.

## Summary

Regenerative endodontic procedures (REPs) revitalize necrotic immature permanent teeth, and trauma is a leading cause of such necrosis — making REP prognosis a core dental-trauma concern. This SR+MA separates two endpoints: the healing endpoint (clinical/radiographic resolution) is robust (>81%) and largely insensitive to pre-op factors, whereas the harder continued-root-development endpoint is sensitive. Critically, *trauma-related* pulp necrosis carries a 3.59× risk of root-development failure versus necrosis from an anatomic anomaly — a trauma-specific prognostic penalty. Incisors and pre-existing apical lesions add further risk. Certainty is very low.

## Key Contributions

- First synthesis to isolate aetiology (trauma vs anatomic anomaly) as a prognostic factor for REP root development.
- Distinguishes a robust healing endpoint from a factor-sensitive root-development endpoint.
- Quantifies tooth-type (incisor) and apical-lesion penalties.

## Methodology

- Six databases + grey literature.
- RoB: Newcastle-Ottawa Scale (observational), Cochrane RoB 2 (RCT).
- Meta-analyses of proportions (pooled rates) and effect sizes (RR); GRADE certainty.
- 20 studies (13 observational, 7 RCT); most moderate–high RoB.

## Results

- Clinical & radiographic healing: >81% across all pre-op factors.
- Root development achieved: 52–95%; complete apical closure: 32–91%.
- Trauma necrosis → root-development failure: RR 3.59 (95% CI 1.21–10.67, p=0.02) vs anatomic anomaly.
- Incisors → root-development failure RR 1.90 (1.37–2.63); incomplete apical closure RR 1.98 (1.34–3.13) vs premolars.
- Apical lesion → root-development failure RR 2.55 (1.63–4.86, p=0.01).
- Certainty: GRADE very low.
