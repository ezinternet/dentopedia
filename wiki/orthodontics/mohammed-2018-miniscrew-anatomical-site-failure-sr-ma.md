## 한줄요약
SR+MA (61편): 정중구개 삽입 실패율 1.3%로 최저, 광대뼈 지지부 16.4%로 최고; 치근 접촉 시 실패 위험비 8.7배 상승 (moderate quality evidence).

## Summary
A systematic review and meta-analysis of 61 studies examining failure rates of orthodontic miniscrew implants (OMIs) stratified by anatomical insertion site. Palatal sites — especially the midpalate — demonstrated the lowest failure rates, while the zygomatic buttress had the highest. Mandibular buccal interradicular sites showed intermediate failure rates. The most clinically important finding was that **root contact increased failure risk by a factor of 8.7** (RR 8.7, 95% CI 5.1–14.7), based on moderate quality evidence.

## Key Contributions
- First SR+MA to report failure rates separately for each anatomical insertion site (not just overall)
- Provides site-specific failure estimates with 95% CIs to guide clinical placement decisions
- Quantifies root contact as the dominant modifiable risk factor for interradicular miniscrews
- Evidence quality: Very low–low for palatal sites; moderate for root contact finding

## Methodology
- **Design**: SR + MA; PRISMA; pre-registered protocol (dissertation)
- **Search**: Cochrane CENTRAL, Web of Knowledge, Scopus, MEDLINE, PubMed up to October 2017; manual search
- **Inclusion**: RCTs + prospective non-randomised studies; ≥20 miniscrews per specific site; site-specific failure rates reported
- **Studies**: 61 studies in quantitative synthesis
- **Sub-grouping**: By site — midpalatal, paramedian, parapalatal, maxillary buccal (M1-P2, C-LI, zygomatic buttress), mandibular buccal (M1-P2, C-P1)
- **Statistics**: Random-effects model; 95% CI; sensitivity analyses; dual independent reviewers

## Results
| Insertion Site | Failure Rate (95% CI) |
|---|---|
| Midpalatal | 1.3% (0.3–6.0) |
| Paramedian palatal | 4.8% (1.6–13.4) |
| Parapalatal | 5.5% (2.8–10.7) |
| Maxillary buccal M1–P2 | 9.2% (7.4–11.4) |
| Maxillary buccal C–LI | 9.7% (5.1–17.6) |
| Zygomatic buttress | 16.4% (4.9–42.5) |
| Mandibular buccal M1–P2 | 13.5% (7.3–23.6) |
| Mandibular buccal C–P1 | 9.9% (4.9–19.1) |

- **Root contact**: RR 8.7 (95% CI 5.1–14.7) — moderate quality evidence; most powerful single predictor identified
- Evidence quality: very low to low for palatal sites; moderate for root contact
