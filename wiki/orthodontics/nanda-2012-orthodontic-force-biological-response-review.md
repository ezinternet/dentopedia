## 한줄요약
교정력에 대한 PDL·치조골 반응 요약 — 신경전달물질·사이토카인·cathepsin K·MMP 역할.

## Summary
Concise review of neurotransmitters, arachidonic acid metabolites, cytokines, and enzymes mediating PDL and alveolar bone remodeling under orthodontic force.

## Key Contributions
- Neurotransmitters: Substance P, CGRP, VIP released from sensory nerve endings
- Arachidonic acid metabolites: PGE₂, leukotrienes stimulate osteoclastogenesis
- Cathepsin K and MMPs essential for collagen matrix degradation

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
Optimal orthodontic force range concept: insufficient force → no movement; excessive → necrosis/hyalinization. Multiple mediators act in concert for PDL remodeling.
