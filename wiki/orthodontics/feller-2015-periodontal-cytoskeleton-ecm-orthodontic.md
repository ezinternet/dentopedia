## 한줄요약
교정적 치아이동 시 PDL 세포골격(actin)·ECM(fibronectin·collagen) 생체역학 — integrin 기계신호 전달.

## Summary
Review focusing on cytoskeletal and ECM biomechanics in PDL cell response to orthodontic force: integrin-mediated mechanotransduction.

## Key Contributions
- Actin cytoskeletal remodeling transmits mechanical force to nucleus
- ECM proteins (fibronectin, vitronectin, collagen I/III) expression changes under force
- Integrin-FAK outside-in signaling activates gene expression for bone remodeling

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
PDL cell mechanotransduction via integrin-FAK-MAPK pathway drives adaptive gene expression for osteoclast/osteoblast recruitment.
