## 한줄요약
교정력 반응 포괄 고찰 — 압박측 파골세포·장력측 조골세포 활성, 사이토카인(IL-1β·TNF-α·PGE₂) 신호.

## Summary
Comprehensive review integrating cellular, molecular, and tissue-level responses to orthodontic force: pressure-tension theory updated with cytokine and molecular signaling.

## Key Contributions
- Pressure side: osteoclast activation, resorption lacunae formation
- Tension side: osteoblast activation, new bone apposition
- Key signaling: IL-1β, TNF-α, PGE₂, substance P, CGRP, RANKL/OPG axis

## Methodology
Comprehensive narrative review of orthodontic biology literature.

## Results
Optimal orthodontic force induces bone remodeling without vascular occlusion. Hyalinization (cell-free necrotic PDL zone) occurs with heavy forces, delaying tooth movement.
