## One-line Summary

Retrospective single-arm case series (n=9 cephalometric, n=6 sEMG subgroup; mean age 9.8 yr) of AMCOP® "OPEN" elastodontic therapy for anterior open bite: overbite rose 0.1→2.2 mm, SN-GoGn/PP-MP fell 2.8°±1.9°, and Teethan® neuromuscular efficiency (IMP) increased ~40% — but no control group, so hypothesis-generating only.

## Summary

According to PubMed ([DOI 10.3389/fdmed.2025.1741153](https://doi.org/10.3389/fdmed.2025.1741153)), this retrospective single-arm pilot case series from the University of Bari treated 9 growing patients (5 F, 4 M; mean age 9.8 ± 1.4 yr) in deciduous/mixed dentition with anterior open bite using the AMCOP® "OPEN" elastodontic bioactivator (mean 14 ± 2 months, 1 h/day + passive overnight). It is the first to pair digital cephalometry (Deltadent®) with standardized surface electromyography (Teethan®, anterior temporalis + masseter, n=6 subgroup) to document vertical skeletal correction *and* neuromuscular rebalancing simultaneously. Cephalometrically, overbite increased from 0.1 to 2.2 mm (open-bite closure), SN-GoGn/PP-MP angles fell a mean 2.8° ± 1.9° (slight counterclockwise mandibular rotation), ANS-Me stayed stable, and ANB normalized toward Class I. Neuromuscularly, the barycenter (BAR) shifted toward midline, torsion (TORS) and asymmetry (ASIM) indices decreased, and the overall performance index (IMP) rose ~40%. The authors are emphatic that the absence of a control group in a growing sample prevents separating treatment effect from physiological growth — findings are preliminary and hypothesis-generating.

**Clinically:** In selected growing open-bite patients, AMCOP® "OPEN" elastodontic therapy was associated with anterior open-bite closure, reduced mandibular plane divergence, and improved masticatory-muscle symmetry/efficiency — supporting it as a minimally invasive, well-tolerated early interceptive option targeting neuromuscular imbalance, not just dentoalveolar camouflage. But the retrospective single-arm pilot design (no controls, n=9) means these results inform hypotheses, not treatment claims; controlled prospective trials are needed before relying on them.

## Key Contributions

- **First integrated cephalometry + sEMG** evaluation of elastodontic (AMCOP®) therapy in a pediatric/growing population — prior elastodontic evidence reported dental/skeletal outcomes only, and sEMG work was mostly adult.
- Correlates **vertical skeletal change** (Deltadent® digital cephalometry) with **functional neuromuscular rebalancing** (Teethan® sEMG of temporalis + masseter).
- Describes a **staged protocol**: AMCOP® "OPEN" (~9 mo to recover transverse/vertical corridors) → Class-specific device for sagittal refinement; total ~16 mo; no separate myofunctional exercises (swallowing supplies activation).
- Frames elastodontic therapy as **early interceptive** treatment addressing the underlying neuromuscular imbalance to reduce relapse risk.

## Methodology

- **Design**: retrospective single-arm case series (pilot); T0 (pre) vs T1 (post). Ethics: Policlinico di Bari (Prot. No. 971).
- **Sample**: n=9 cephalometric (mean age 9.8 ± 1.4 yr); sEMG valid in n=6 (66.7%; 3 failed reliable acquisition).
- **Inclusion**: deciduous/mixed dentition, anterior open bite (overbite ≤ 0 mm), AMCOP® "OPEN" as primary modality, no adjunctive fixed appliances. Exclusion: prior vertical-affecting treatment, syndromes/cleft, adeno-tonsillar surgery in interval, poor radiographs.
- **Intervention**: AMCOP® "OPEN" bioactivator (Micerium S.p.A.) — posteriorly raised occlusal plane + elastic flanges for mandibular counterclockwise rotation; mean 14 ± 2 mo.
- **Cephalometry**: Deltadent® 2.5.3. Primary outcomes overbite, SN-GoGn, PP-MP (ANS-PNS^Go-Gn), ANS-Me; secondary FMA, interincisal angle, U1-PP, L1-MP, overjet.
- **Reliability**: single calibrated examiner; 20% retraced ≥7 d later; ICC (≥0.80 accepted) + Dahlberg's error (≤0.5 mm/≤0.5°).
- **sEMG**: Teethan®, bilateral anterior temporalis + masseter per SENIAM; MVCs on cotton rolls (normalization) + intercuspal MVCs (5 s, 30 s rest); indices POC TA/MM, BAR, TORS, ASIM, IMP (+ CL).

## Results

- **Completion**: all 9 completed therapy (mean 14 ± 2 mo), excellent compliance, no adverse events.
- **Overbite**: 0.1 mm → **2.2 mm** (anterior open-bite closure).
- **SN-GoGn / PP-MP**: mean reduction **2.8° ± 1.9°** (improved vertical control, slight counterclockwise mandibular rotation).
- **ANS-Me** (lower anterior facial height): **stable**.
- **ANB**: normalization toward Class I (≈ −3.2° mean change); **U1-PP** decreased, **interincisal angle** increased; **overjet** reduced in most cases.
- **sEMG (n=6)**: **BAR** shifted toward midline/anterior; **TORS** and **ASIM** decreased; **IMP** rose ~**40%**.
- **Per-case illustrations**: Case 6 SN-GoGn 42.4°→36.4°, intermaxillary angle 30.5°→21.9° (hyperdivergent→mesofacial, full closure); Case 9 SN-GoGn 44.7°→38.0°. Individual tables are heterogeneous and the authors flag some values as possibly needing re-measurement.
