## One-line Summary

SR+MA (14 studies, n=1683 infrazygomatic crest miniscrews): pooled success 92% (95% CI 87–95%; I²=80%), 94% in studies with >100 screws; no significant age effect and only limited correlation for other stability factors.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (14편·관골하능 미니스크루 1683개): 통합 성공률 92% (95% CI 87–95%, I²=80%), 표본 >100 연구는 94%; 연령 영향 무의미, 기타 안정성 인자 상관성 제한적.

## Summary

This SR+MA isolates the extra-radicular infrazygomatic crest (IZC) miniscrew — a site placed parallel to the maxillary first-molar roots in dense, thick cortical bone — which earlier miniscrew meta-analyses (dominated by inter-radicular screws) under-covered. Across 14 studies and 1683 IZC-MSI, pooled success was 92%, rising to 94% in larger studies. Meta-regression found no significant age–success relationship, and most host/implant/loading factors showed only limited correlation amid high heterogeneity (I²=80%) and largely low-quality evidence. It reinforces the broader miniscrew-stability literature by adding a dedicated, favorable extra-alveolar success estimate while flagging that the factor-level conclusions remain low-certainty.

## Key Contributions

- First dedicated SR+MA of IZC miniscrew success (extra-radicular site).
- Pooled 92% success with size-stratified subgroup (94% at n>100).
- Shows age does not significantly affect IZC-MSI success; factor effects largely unconfirmed.

## Methodology

- PRISMA; PROSPERO CRD42024469048; PubMed, Scopus, EMBASE, Cochrane (7 Feb 2024).
- Single-arm proportion meta-analysis (no comparator); RCTs + non-randomized trials + cohorts.
- 196 records → 14 studies (n=1683); random-effects; RoB 2.0 + ROBINS-I (5 high / 5 moderate / 4 low).
- Subgroup + meta-regression on age, sex, position, loading.

## Results

- Pooled success: **92%** (95% CI 87–95%; prediction interval 66–99%; I²=80%).
- n>100 subgroup: **94%** (95% CI 91–96%; I²=63%).
- No significant age–success relation; limited correlation for other factors.
- Could not assess operator experience, insertion torque, sinus penetration (insufficient data).
