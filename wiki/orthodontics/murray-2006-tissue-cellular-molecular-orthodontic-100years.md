## 한줄요약
Sandstedt(1904) 100주년 고찰 — 고전 압박/장력 이론부터 현대 RANKL/OPG 기전까지.

## Summary
Historical and molecular review tracing orthodontic tooth movement biology from Sandstedt's 1904 pressure-tension theory to the RANKL-RANK-OPG paradigm.

## Key Contributions
- RANKL/OPG ratio determines bone resorption vs formation balance
- Osteocytes as primary mechanosensors in alveolar bone
- PDL mechanotransduction: integrin-FAK-MAPK-RANKL signaling cascade

## Methodology
Historical narrative review + current molecular biology synthesis.

## Results
Tooth movement governed by RANKL/OPG balance. Osteocyte mechanosensing orchestrates osteoclast/osteoblast recruitment. Classical tension-pressure duality confirmed at molecular level.
