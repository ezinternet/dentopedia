SR (11 studies, n=278, all Invisalign): clear aligners achieve maxillary molar distalization up to ~2 mm with good accuracy; achieved-vs-predicted was 36-75% (M6), 42-72% (M7), derotation 63-78%; moderate-quality, mostly retrospective evidence.

## Summary

According to PubMed ([DOI 10.4103/jispcd.jispcd_59_24](https://doi.org/10.4103/jispcd.jispcd_59_24)), this PROSPERO-registered SR puts numbers on the distalization predictability that Inchingolo treated qualitatively. Across 11 clinical studies (278 Class II patients, all treated with Invisalign), aligners achieved maxillary molar distalization ranging **0.67 +/- 0.5 mm to 2.54 +/- 0.12 mm** — a practical ceiling near **2 mm**.

The clinically important figures are the **achieved-vs-predicted ratios**: first molars (M6) expressed **36.5-75.5%** of planned distalization, second molars (M7) **41.9-72.2%**, and molar **derotation accuracy 62.7-77.5%**. In other words, roughly a quarter to two-thirds of planned molar movement is not expressed, which is why staging and overcorrection matter. Studies that added **attachments** or **microimplants with Class I elastics** mitigated anchorage loss. Strength of evidence was rated **moderate** (AHRQ), limited by predominantly retrospective designs, small samples, and heterogeneous measurement.

Clinically: plan aligner molar distalization with realistic expectations (~2 mm ceiling, sizeable under-expression), build in overcorrection, and reinforce anchorage with attachments/TADs — consistent with Inchingolo's anchorage thesis.

## Key Contributions

- Numeric achieved/predicted distalization efficiency by tooth and for derotation.
- ~2 mm practical distalization ceiling with aligners in Class II.
- Anchorage auxiliaries (attachments, microimplants) reduce under-expression.

## Methodology

SR (no MA; PROSPERO CRD42024531205). 5 databases to Dec 2023; Class II treated with CAT. 547 -> 11 studies (8 retrospective, 3 prospective), n=278. NOS + ROBINS-I; PRISMA. Confidence `sr`; PMC OA full text.

## Results

- Distalization 0.67-2.54 mm; ceiling ~2 mm.
- Achieved/predicted: M6 36.5-75.5%, M7 41.9-72.2%, derotation 62.7-77.5%.
- Attachments / microimplants + Class I elastics limit anchorage loss. Evidence moderate.
