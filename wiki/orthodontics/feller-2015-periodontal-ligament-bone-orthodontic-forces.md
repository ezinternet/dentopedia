## 한줄요약
교정력 적용 시 PDL·치조골 생물학적 사건 — 혈류 변화·세포 활성·사이토카인 신호.

## Summary
Review of biological events in PDL and alveolar bone in response to orthodontic force: blood flow changes, cellular activation, and cytokine signaling.

## Key Contributions
- Pressure side: ischemia, osteoclast recruitment, resorption
- Tension side: increased blood flow, osteoblast activity, bone deposition
- IL-1, TNF-α, PGE₂ mediate osteoclastogenesis; RANK/RANKL axis central

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
Optimal force: no complete PDL vascular occlusion. Heavy forces → hyalinization → delayed tooth movement. Cytokine cascade determines rate and quality of bone remodeling.
