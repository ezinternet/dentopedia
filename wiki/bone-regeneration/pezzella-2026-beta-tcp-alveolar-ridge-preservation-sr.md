## Summary
Systematic review (University of Naples Federico II, search to April 2026) evaluating β-tricalcium phosphate (β-TCP) for alveolar ridge preservation. Five RCTs met inclusion criteria. β-TCP alone shows variable results; combined formulations are more consistent and comparable to DBBM.

## Key Contributions
- **5 RCTs** met inclusion criteria (CBCT-based dimensional outcomes, ≥4 months follow-up)
- **β-TCP alone**: variable — ranging from greater resorption vs xenograft to comparable to FDBA
- **β-TCP combined with other biomaterials**: outcomes generally comparable to DBBM
- Considerable heterogeneity in formulations, socket management, outcome assessment
- **Evidence level: limited and heterogeneous** — provisional recommendation only

## Evidence Summary
| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| β-TCP alone vs xenograft | Variable; sometimes inferior |
| β-TCP alone vs FDBA | Comparable |
| β-TCP composite vs DBBM | Comparable |
