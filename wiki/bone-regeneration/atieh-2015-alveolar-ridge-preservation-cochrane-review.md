## Summary
Cochrane systematic review (2015, edited 2017) of 8 RCTs evaluating alveolar ridge preservation techniques for dental implant site development. Limited evidence supports ARP over extraction alone for reducing dimensional ridge changes, but evidence quality is low-to-moderate with high risk of bias. No convincing difference between graft materials.

## Key Contributions
- 8 RCTs; 233 extraction sites; 184 participants
- **Xenograft vs extraction** (2 RCTs, 70 participants, moderate quality):
  - Height loss ↓ by 2.60mm (95%CI 1.76–3.43)
  - Width loss ↓ by 1.97mm (95%CI 1.46–2.48)
- **Allograft vs extraction** (1 RCT, low quality): similar direction of benefit
- **Alloplast vs xenograft**: no significant difference in height or width
- No material proven superior for: augmentation need, complications, implant failure, peri-implant MBL
- 7/8 trials at high risk of bias; only 2/8 had no industry support
