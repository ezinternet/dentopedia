## One-line Summary
RCT (12 completers): 5 N vs. 30 N compressive force on particulate DBBM in molar open-healing ARP shows no significant difference in new bone formation, ISQ, or peri-implant bone level (P>0.05); only residual graft particle size differed — the "more force → more bone" finding from Cho 2017 did not replicate in molars with DBBM.

## 한줄요약
RCT(완료 12명): 대구치 open-healing ARP에서 입자형 DBBM에 5 N vs 30 N 압축력은 신생골·ISQ·임플란트 주위 골수준 모두 차이 없음 (P>0.05); 잔존 graft 입자 크기만 유의차. cho-2017의 "압축력↑→신생골↑"이 DBBM·대구치에서는 재현되지 않음.

## Summary
Quantified-force follow-up to cho-2017. In molar ARP with DBBM under a native bilayer collagen membrane, 5 N vs 30 N did not change new bone formation — narrowing where the earlier "more force → more bone" signal applies.

## Key Contributions
- Quantified force (5 N vs 30 N) rather than test/control
- Molar-specific, DBBM + NBCM
- Equivalence finding tempers cho-2017

## Methodology
- RCT; 12 completers; molar open-healing ARP; 5 N vs 30 N on particulate DBBM under NBCM; histomorphometry + ISQ + peri-implant bone level.

## Results
- New bone, residual graft, soft-tissue ratios and ISQ: NS (P>0.05).
- Residual graft particle **size**: significant difference (P<0.05).
- Conclusion: 5 N ≈ 30 N for new bone, implant feasibility, peri-implant bone level.

## Limitations
- Small (n=12); molar-only. Tension with cho-2017 likely due to material/site/force-operationalization differences.
