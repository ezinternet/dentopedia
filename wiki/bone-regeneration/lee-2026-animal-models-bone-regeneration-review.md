## Summary
Narrative review (Seoul National University, Jungwon Lee) summarizing preclinical animal model selection for bone regeneration research. Provides a practical hierarchy of species — rodent → rabbit → dog → minipig — with species-specific considerations for intraoral defect studies.

## Key Contributions
| Species | Use case | Key limitation |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Rodent | Early screening; mechanistic | No Haversian remodeling; strong healing |
| Rabbit | Medium defects; critical-size threshold | Faster healing than humans |
| Dog | Intraoral defects; human-like remodeling | Declining due to ethics |
| Minipig | Late-stage validation; human-like bone | Large body; strong healing |

- Acute vs chronic, infected vs non-infected, systemic conditions all influence outcomes
- Standardized defect dimensions + observation periods critical for reproducibility
