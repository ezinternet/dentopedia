## 한줄요약
sr+ma(J Dent Res 2014): RCT 메타분석; 발치와보존(ARP) vs 자연치유 — 협설폭 +1.89mm·협측높이 +2.07mm 유의하게 보존; 판막거상+막+이종/동종골이식 조합이 최우수.

## Summary
Systematic review and meta-analysis of RCTs comparing alveolar ridge preservation (socket grafting) to extraction alone in non-molar teeth (University of Iowa, J Dent Res 2014). ARP is effective across all dimensional outcomes except distal height. Optimal protocol: flap elevation + barrier membrane + xenograft or allograft.

## Key Contributions
- ARP significantly preserves ridge dimensions vs extraction alone
- Buccolingual width: +1.89mm (p<.001) — most clinically relevant gain
- Midbuccal height: +2.07mm (p<.001) — largest absolute benefit
- Midlingual height: +1.18mm (p=.022)
- Mesial height: +0.48mm (p=.002)
- Distal height: +0.24mm (NS, p=.102) — only non-significant dimension
- Subgroup superiority: flap elevation + membrane + xenograft/allograft for midbuccal/lingual height

## Results Table

| Mesial height | +0.48 | 0.18–0.79 | .002 |
| Distal height | +0.24 | −0.05–0.53 | .102 (NS) |

## Optimal Protocol (from subgroup analysis)
- **Flap elevation**: allows better access and graft adaptation
- **Barrier membrane**: superior height outcomes
- **Xenograft or allograft**: outperforms other materials for midbuccal/midlingual height
