## One-line Summary

Exploratory RCT (n=35, 3-year follow-up) comparing DBBM-C/CM, DBBM-C, and spontaneous healing (SH) before early implant placement (8 weeks): all groups showed statistically significant but clinically acceptable contour reduction at 3 years (−0.23, −0.21, −0.33 mm), with no significant between-group differences in marginal bone loss or clinical parameters.

## 한줄요약

조기 식립 (Early Implant Placement, 8주) 전 DBBM-C/CM, DBBM-C, 자발적 치유 (Spontaneous Healing, SH)를 비교한 탐색적 무작위 대조 시험 (RCT) (n=35, 3년 추적): 모든 군에서 외형 감소 통계적 유의 (−0.23, −0.21, −0.33 mm)이나 임상적으로 허용 가능한 수준이며, 군 간 변연골 소실 (Marginal Bone Loss, MBL) 및 임상 지표 차이 유의하지 않음.

## Summary

This exploratory RCT from the University of Zurich (Müller, Bienz, Thoma et al.) reports 3-year outcomes of early implant placement (8 weeks post-extraction, type 2 protocol) in 35 patients allocated to three socket management strategies: (1) 탈단백 소 골 미네랄 (Deproteinized Bovine Bone Mineral, DBBM)-C covered by a collagen matrix (CM) seal, (2) DBBM-C alone, or (3) 자발적 치유 (Spontaneous Healing, SH) as negative control.

Key finding: All three groups demonstrated statistically significant contour reduction from the time of 고정성 보철물 장착 (Fixed Dental Prosthesis insertion, FDPi) to 3-year follow-up (FU-3Y), with medians of −0.23 mm (DBBM-C/CM), −0.21 mm (DBBM-C), and −0.33 mm (SH). Despite SH showing the greatest reduction numerically, between-group differences were not statistically significant.

방사선학적 변연골 소실 (Radiographic Marginal Bone Loss, rMBL) was minimal and not statistically significant within or between groups (median: +0.02, −0.5, −0.18 mm respectively). Clinical parameters (치태 조절 지수 [Plaque Control Record, PCR], 탐침 시 출혈 [Bleeding on Probing, BOP], 탐침 깊이 [Probing Pocket Depth, PPD]) remained stable in all groups.

This is the first study to report 3-year outcomes for 치조제보존술 (ARP) combined with early implant placement, extending previously published 8-week and 1-year data from the same cohort.

## Key Contributions

- First 3-year RCT data combining ARP with early implant placement (type 2, 8 weeks)
- All ARP protocols and SH produce clinically acceptable outcomes at 3 years
- Contour losses are small (<0.4 mm) across all groups — ARP does not show statistically superior benefit over SH by 3 years in this underpowered exploratory trial
- Stable 변연골 소실 (Marginal Bone Level, MBL) and clinical parameters confirm peri-implant health maintenance in all three strategies

## Methodology

- **Design**: Exploratory parallel-group RCT; 3 arms; computer-generated randomization
- **n**: 35 patients (single-tooth sites; incisors, canines, premolars; no molars)
- **Inclusion**: ≤50% buccal plate loss at extraction; adequate oral hygiene (PCR ≤20%)
- **Groups**: DBBM-C/CM (Geistlich Bio-Oss Collagen + Mucograft Seal); DBBM-C alone; SH
- **Implants**: Astra OsseospeedEV, placed 8 weeks post-extraction (early/type 2)
- **GBR**: Performed simultaneously if buccal plate defect/thin bone (<1.5 mm) — adds confound
- **Prosthetics**: Zirconia crowns (screw-retained or cemented); FDPi = baseline for FU metrics
- **Profilometry**: Silicone impressions → digital scan superimposition at multiple timepoints
- **Radiography**: Periapical X-rays for MBL at FDPi, 1Y, 3Y
- **Follow-up duration**: 3 years post-FDPi

## Results

| Outcome | DBBM-C/CM | DBBM-C | SH |
|---|---|---|---|
| Contour change FDPi→FU-3Y (median) | −0.23 mm* | −0.21 mm* | −0.33 mm* |
| rMBL change FDPi→FU-3Y (median) | +0.02 mm | −0.5 mm | −0.18 mm |
| Between-group MBL difference | NS | NS | NS |
| Clinical parameters | Stable | Stable | Stable |

*P<0.05 within-group (significant reduction from FDPi to FU-3Y)

- No implant failures or peri-implantitis events reported
- GBR was performed in a subset of patients at implant placement — not stratified in outcome reporting
