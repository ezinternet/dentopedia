## One-line Summary

Beagle dog 2-wall defect RCT (3 groups, 8 weeks): sticky bone (PRF + DPBM) ± solid-PRF membrane equals GBR (DPBM + collagen membrane) in new bone formation; PRF modestly upregulates osteocalcin, VEGF, IL-6 at 1 week.

## Summary

Park et al. (2022, Yonsei) tested whether platelet-rich fibrin (PRF)-based sticky bone is a viable substitute for GBR in a beagle dog model of two-wall alveolar defects — simulating "damaged" extraction sockets. Three groups were compared: sticky bone alone (liquid PRF + DPBM), sticky bone covered with a solid compressed PRF membrane, and GBR with DPBM + collagen membrane. At 8 weeks, micro-CT and histology showed no significant differences in alveolar ridge area or graft consolidation between groups. PRF modestly elevated osteocalcin, calcitonin receptor, VEGF, and IL-6 at 1 week versus GBR, suggesting a mild pro-regenerative and pro-inflammatory early response that did not translate into superior bone volume outcomes.

## Key Contributions

- Provides direct preclinical evidence that sticky bone (i-PRF + DPBM) performs non-inferiorly to GBR with a collagen membrane in 2-wall defects.
- Molecular (qRT-PCR) profiling at 1 week shows PRF modulates bone turnover and angiogenesis markers, but effect size is modest.
- Solid-PRF compressed membrane as a cover did not significantly augment outcomes beyond sticky bone alone.

## Methodology

- **Model**: Beagle dogs, surgically created 2-wall alveolar defects
- **Groups**: (i) Sticky bone [liquid PRF + DPBM]; (ii) Sticky bone + solid PRF membrane; (iii) GBR [DPBM + collagen membrane]
- **1-week**: qRT-PCR — osteocalcin, calcitonin receptor (bone turnover), VEGF (angiogenesis), IL-6 (inflammation)
- **8-week**: Micro-CT (ridge area, bone volume) + histomorphometry (new bone, graft consolidation)

## Results

| Outcome | SB (PRF+DPBM) | SB + PRF membrane | GBR (collagen) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ridge area (8 wk) | Increased | Increased | Increased |
| Graft consolidation (8 wk) | No sig. diff. | No sig. diff. | Reference |
| Osteocalcin (1 wk) | Moderately ↑ | Moderately ↑ | Reference |
| VEGF / IL-6 (1 wk) | Modestly ↑ | Modestly ↑ | Reference |

**Bottom line**: PRF-based sticky bone is non-inferior to GBR for DPBM consolidation in 2-wall alveolar defects in this animal model.
