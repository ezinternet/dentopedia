## Summary
Systematic review with meta-analysis and trial sequential analysis (TSA) of 6 RCTs evaluating socket seal surgery (SSS) techniques in the esthetic zone. Techniques compared: FGG, collagen matrix (CM), collagen sponge (CS), acellular dermal matrix (ADM), and PTFE membrane. No single technique proven superior; FGG shows best evidence for height but data insufficient.

## Key Contributions
- 6 RCTs; 135 sockets; esthetic zone; minimum 3-month follow-up
- **FGG without bone fill**: best buccal (−1.42mm exp vs −0.01mm ctrl) and lingual bone height preservation
- **CM vs FGG with bone fill**: no clinical differences in dimensional changes
- **CM vs CS**: no differences in width or gingival thickness
- **Meta-analysis CM vs FGG**: trend toward less horizontal reduction with FGG at −1, −3, −5mm (NS)
- **TSA**: data insufficient — definitive superiority claims not yet supported
