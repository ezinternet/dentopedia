## One-line Summary

SR+MA (16 qualitative / 9 quantitative RCTs, CBCT-only) showing xenogenic graft materials produced significantly less vertical and horizontal ridge reduction than allogenic in alveolar ridge preservation, though no material fully prevented dimensional loss.

## 한줄요약

CBCT 측정만 모은 SR+MA(정성 16편·정량 9편)로, 치조제보존술에서 xenograft가 allograft보다 수직·수평 골 흡수를 유의하게 덜 일으켰으나(P<0.05) 어떤 재료도 치조제 치수 손실을 완전히 막지는 못했다.

## Summary

This systematic review and meta-analysis isolates CBCT-measured dimensional outcomes of alveolar ridge preservation (ARP), removing the measurement-method heterogeneity (periapical radiographs, casts, probing) that confounds earlier reviews. Across RCTs it compared graft origins and found xenogenic materials outperformed allogenic on both vertical and horizontal ridge maintenance, while confirming the well-established principle that ARP attenuates but never abolishes post-extraction atrophy.

## Key Contributions

- First ARP meta-analysis limited to CBCT-only measurements, reducing measurement heterogeneity.
- Direct xenogenic-vs-allogenic subgroup comparison at standardized vertical and horizontal landmarks.
- Quantifies that no graft material fully prevents ridge loss.

## Methodology

- PRISMA SR+MA; MEDLINE + Cochrane CENTRAL search (Nov 2019–Jan 2020); 1,582 records → 16 qualitative, 9 quantitative.
- RCTs in healthy adults, atraumatic extraction, ARP with autogenic/allogenic/xenogenic ± membrane; follow-up ≥3 months; CBCT-measured outcome.
- Outcomes: vertical midbuccal / midpalatal-midlingual; horizontal width at 1, 3, 5 mm from initial crest. Effect measure WMD (SE).

## Results

- Vertical midbuccal: xenogenic WMD −0.20 (SE 0.26) vs allogenic −0.90 (SE 0.22); xenogenic less reduction (P<0.05).
- Horizontal 1 mm: xenogenic −1.32 (SE 0.07) vs allogenic −2.99 (SE 0.96).
- Horizontal 3 mm: xenogenic −0.78 (SE 0.11) vs allogenic −1.63 (SE 0.40).
- Autogenic: insufficient data for pooling.
