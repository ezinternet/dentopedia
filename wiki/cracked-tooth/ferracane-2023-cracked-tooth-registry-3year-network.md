## One-line Summary
Multi-site observational cohort (National Dental PBRN, 8 articles 2017–2022, OHSU-led): 3-year follow-up of posterior cracked teeth answered 3 key clinical questions — which cracks worsen, when to intervene, and what is the best treatment.

## 한줄요약
Multi-site observational cohort (National Dental PBRN, 8 articles 2017-2022 통합 lessons learned, OHSU 주도): 후방 cracked teeth 3년 — **어떤 crack이 악화되는가, 언제 개입하나, best 치료는?** 3 핵심 임상 질문 답.

## Summary
CTS 분야 가장 큰 real-world cohort. SR/umbrella가 다루지 못한 actual practice 데이터로, 3가지 임상 질문에 PBRN 데이터로 답함. Kaur 2026 umbrella의 "endo + cuspal coverage" 결론과 일관된 신호.

## 3 Clinical Questions Answered

### Q1: Which cracked teeth will get worse?
- Deep crack into dentin/pulp
- Symptomatic (spontaneous, prolonged response)
- Pulp involvement
- Vital pulp with crack progression risk

### Q2: When should practitioners intervene?
- Spontaneous pain
- Periapical pathology
- Deep crack with imminent pulp risk
- Cuspal mobility

### Q3: What is the best treatment?
- **Cuspal coverage (full crown / onlay)** 강력 권장
- Endo + crown when pulp involvement
- Conservative restoration only for shallow craze line

## Methodology
- Multi-site observational study (National Dental PBRN)
- 8 published articles (2017-2022)
- 후방 cracked teeth 한정
- 3-year follow-up
