## One-line Summary
Umbrella review (Manipal CDS, JCDE 2026, PROSPERO registered): synthesizing 4 SRs (30 primary studies) — endodontic therapy + full cuspal coverage showed the best prognosis; AMSTAR-2 quality assessed; CCA 17%.

## 한줄요약
Umbrella review (Manipal CDS, JCDE 2026, PROSPERO registered): 4 SR (30 primary studies) 통합 — **endo therapy + full cuspal coverage가 최고 prognosis**. AMSTAR-2 평가, CCA 17%.

## Summary
CTS 분야 첫 umbrella review. 다수 SR이 분산된 evidence를 종합 — endo + crown coverage가 conservative restoration·crown only·endo only보다 우월. 단, 분야 자체의 primary study 부족 한계.

## Methodology
- Umbrella review (SR of SRs)
- PROSPERO CRD420250648720
- PRISMA 2020
- DB: PubMed, Cochrane, Scopus, EMBASE (2025-06-15까지)
- 2 reviewers, independent screening
- AMSTAR-2 quality + CCA overlap

## Results
- 10 SR identified → 4 included
- 30 primary studies (45 entries)
- CCA 17% (높은 overlap — primary study 중복 사용)
- **Endo + full cuspal coverage** 최고 prognosis 신호
- Other approaches (crown only, endo only) 열등

## Clinical Decision

| 임상 상황 | 권장 |
|---|---|
| Vital pulp, shallow crack | Conservative restoration + observation |
| Pulpitis 진행, deep crack | Endo + full cuspal coverage (crown/onlay) |
| Pulpal necrosis | Endo + crown |
| Vertical root fracture | Extraction |

## Strengths and Caveats
[근거강함] Umbrella review = 분야 최고 evidence level
[합의수준] "endo + crown" 권고는 [[endodontics/kwak-2024-endodontic-treatment-cracked-tooth-management]] (Kwak 한국 review) 보수적 입장과 부분 차이 — Kwak은 endo 회피 가능 case 강조, Kaur는 endo 필요 case에서 full coverage 강조
[claude해석] CTS 분야 primary RCT 부족 — umbrella review도 결국 동일 base에 의존
[미검증] 17% CCA는 결과 신뢰성에 약간의 caveat
