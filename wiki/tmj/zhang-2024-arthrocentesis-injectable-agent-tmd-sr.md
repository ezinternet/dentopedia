## 한줄요약
BMC Oral Health 2024 SR: 관절천자(Arthrocentesis) 시 히알루론산(HA) 병용이 가장 높은 근거; PRP는 유망한 신흥 옵션; 스테로이드는 단기 효과 있으나 반복 시 연골 손상 우려.

## Summary
This systematic review from Chengdu University (BMC Oral Health, 2024) evaluates 관절천자 (Arthrocentesis) augmented with various injectable agents for 악관절증 (TMD). Hyaluronic acid (HA) demonstrated the most evidence-supported benefit when added to arthrocentesis lavage, showing significant pain reduction and 하악 최대개구량 (Maximum Mouth Opening, MMO) improvement versus lavage alone. Corticosteroids provided rapid short-term pain relief but risk joint damage with repeated use. Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) is an emerging option with comparable or superior outcomes to HA in some studies, though protocol variability limits conclusions. Saline lavage alone showed significant benefit over no treatment. The review provides a clinical framework for injectable agent selection.

## Key Contributions
- Comprehensive synthesis of all injectable agent types used with arthrocentesis for TMD
- Establishes HA as the most evidence-supported adjunct to arthrocentesis lavage
- Positions PRP as promising emerging alternative with variable evidence
- Clarifies risks of repeated corticosteroid injections (cartilage concern)
- Informs clinical decision-making for agent selection in TMJ arthrocentesis

## Methodology
- Databases: PubMed, Embase, Cochrane, Scopus, Web of Science
- Inclusion: RCTs and controlled trials; arthrocentesis ± injectable agents for TMD
- Primary: pain (VAS/NRS), MMO (mm)
- Secondary: clicking/crepitus, jaw function, patient satisfaction
- Narrative synthesis ± meta-analysis where feasible
- Cochrane RoB 2.0

## Results
- **Hyaluronic acid (HA)**: Strongest evidence; significant pain and MMO improvement vs. lavage alone
- **Corticosteroids**: Short-term pain benefit; risk of cartilage damage with repeated injections — limit to 1–3 per year
- **PRP**: Comparable/superior to HA in some studies; high preparation protocol variability
- **BTX intra-articular**: Limited data; possible pain benefit in disc displacement with pain
- **Saline lavage only**: Significant benefit vs. no treatment; HA augmentation adds further benefit
- Optimal HA concentration, molecular weight, and injection frequency remain undefined
