## One-line Summary
SR (20 studies, Cureus 2024): combination manual therapy + counseling + splint is most effective for TMD; TENS/LLLT alone limited; >80% managed non-surgically; arthrocentesis reserved for conservative failures.

## 한줄요약
SR (20 연구, 2024): 수기치료+상담+스플린트 병용 최선; TENS·LLLT 단독 효과 제한; TMD 80%이상 비수술; 1차 실패 시 관절천자세정술.

## Summary
This systematic review (PRISMA, 20 studies from 1,437 screened) compared non-invasive and minimally invasive therapies for TMJ dysfunction. Key finding: combination therapy integrating manual therapy, counseling, and splint therapy demonstrated the most substantial improvements in pain reduction, depression, and anxiety. TENS and LLLT alone showed limited efficacy, while arthrocentesis is an effective minimally invasive option after exhausting conservative approaches. Over 80% of TMD patients can be managed non-surgically; surgery (arthrotomy) is ultimately needed in only ~5%.

## Key Contributions
- Combination (MT + counseling + splint): best outcomes for pain, psychological symptoms
- TENS alone: limited efficacy (not clearly superior to placebo in some studies)
- LLLT alone: inconsistent evidence
- Surface EMG recommended for precise diagnosis before treatment selection
- Establishes a stepped-care philosophy: non-invasive → minimally invasive → surgical

## Methodology
- PRISMA SR; PubMed / Google Scholar / ScienceDirect / Medline
- 20 RCTs and clinical trials included
- Outcomes: mandibular mobility, max pain-free opening, pain levels
- Statistical: p-values from paired t-test / ANOVA across studies

## Results

| Treatment category | Efficacy |
|---|---|
| Manual therapy + counseling + splint | Best (substantial ↓ pain + anxiety/depression) |
| Physical therapy alone | Effective |
| TENS alone | Limited / inconsistent |
| LLLT alone | Limited / inconsistent |
| Arthrocentesis (minimally invasive) | Effective after conservative failure |
| Injections (minimally invasive) | Effective; use after non-invasive exhausted |

- 80%+ of TMD patients managed with non-surgical approaches
- 5% ultimately require surgery

## Clinical Relevance
TMD 단계별 치료 알고리즘:
1. **진단**: 표면 EMG + DC/TMD 기준으로 정밀 분류
2. **1차 (비침습)**: 수기치료 + 상담 + 교합 안정장치 병용 → 최선 근거
3. **보조**: TENS·LLLT는 단독 사용 지양; 병용 보조로만
4. **2차 (최소침습)**: 관절천자세정술 또는 주사
5. **수술**: 극히 일부 (~5%)에서만
