SR+NMA (233 RCTs, n=8,713, 59 interventions, BMJ 2023): CBT+biofeedback (RD 36%), jaw mobilization (36%), manual trigger point therapy (32%) top 3 for chronic TMD pain with moderate-high certainty; pharmacological interventions low certainty.

## 한줄요약

## Summary
The largest and highest-quality network meta-analysis of TMD treatment to date. Screened MEDLINE/EMBASE/CINAHL/CENTRAL/SCOPUS through January 2023; 233 RCTs eligible, 153 included in NMA (n=8,713 participants, 59 interventions). Primary outcome: pain relief (RD for achieving ≥1 cm improvement on 10 cm VAS). High-to-moderate certainty evidence supported 8 interventions. Top performers: CBT+biofeedback/relaxation (RD 36%), therapist-assisted jaw mobilization (36%), manual trigger point therapy (32%). Five additional interventions showed 23–30% RD. Key conclusion: "interventions that promote coping and encourage movement and activity were most effective." Pharmacological agents had only low/very low certainty evidence.

## Key Contributions
- Definitive NMA for chronic TMD pain (supersedes older SR+MAs on this topic)
- Top 3 interventions all behavioral/physical, not pharmacological
- Pharmacological interventions uniformly low certainty → supports conservative-first approach
- Physical functioning outcomes: jaw exercise + manipulation + acupuncture equally effective
- Adverse events: insufficient evidence across all interventions
- Companion to BMJ 2023 clinical practice guideline (076227)

## NMA Results — Pain Relief

| Intervention | RD (95% CI) | Certainty |
|---|---|---|
| CBT + biofeedback/relaxation | 36% (33–39%) | High-moderate |
| Therapist-assisted jaw mobilization | 36% (31–40%) | High-moderate |
| Manual trigger point therapy | 32% (29–34%) | High-moderate |
| CBT (alone) | 23–30% range | Moderate |
| Supervised postural exercise | 23–30% range | Moderate |
| Supervised jaw exercise + stretching | 23–30% range | Moderate |
| Jaw exercise + stretching + MTrP | 23–30% range | Moderate |
| Usual care | 23–30% range | Moderate |

## NMA Results — Physical Functioning

| Intervention | RD for MID (SF-36 PCS) | Certainty |
|---|---|---|
| Supervised jaw exercise + stretching | 43% (33–51%) | Moderate |
| Manipulation | 43% (25–56%) | Moderate |
| Acupuncture | 42% (33–50%) | Moderate |
| Jaw exercise + mobilization | 36% (19–51%) | Moderate |

## Clinical Relevance

만성 TMD 통증 최적 치료 순위 (2023 NMA 최고 근거):

1. **CBT + 바이오피드백/이완** → 통증+심리 동반 만성 TMD의 최선
2. **치료사 보조 하악 가동화** → 관절 위주 TMD
3. **수기 트리거포인트 치료** → 근육 위주 TMD
4. **교합 안정장치 + 운동** → 단독 vs 복합 비교에서도 복합 우월
5. **약물**: 낮은 근거 수준 → 단기 보조 사용 원칙
