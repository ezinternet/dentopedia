## 한줄요약
대한치과의사협회지 2023 특집 종설: TMJ 골관절염 기전(과부하 → 저산소증/HIF-1 → VEGF·MMP → 연골·골 파괴), 역학(여성·고령 우세하나 비일관), 진단(CT가 gold standard: κ=0.71 vs MRI 0.46 vs 파노라마 0.16). DC/TMD 임상 기준 민감도 0.55·특이도 0.61로 영상 필수; 침식·연골하낭종(피질골 연속성 소실)이 단순 편평화보다 진단적.

## Summary
Companion mechanism/diagnosis review to the treatment paper in the same issue. Traces TMJ OA from mechanical overload to molecular bone destruction, summarizes epidemiology, and gives an imaging-based diagnostic framework where CT is the gold standard and cortical continuity (erosion/cyst) carries more diagnostic weight than condylar shape change.

## Key Contributions
- Mechanistic overload→hypoxia→VEGF/MMP→destruction chain
- Imaging reliability figures (κ, inter-examiner agreement) favoring CT
- Diagnostic emphasis on cortical integrity over shape

## Methodology
- Narrative literature review.

## Results
- **Anatomy**: condyle covered by dense fibrocartilage; proliferative / fibrocartilaginous (type II collagen + proteoglycan/aggrecan + HA) / calcified zones.
- **Pathophysiology**: overload > physiologic resistance → intra-articular pressure↑ → hypoxia → HIF-1 → VEGF → osteoclast migration + MMP (MMP-1/-3/-9 ↑ in OA synovial fluid) + aggrecanase → collagen/proteoglycan breakdown; reperfusion ROS degrades HA; IL-1β/IL-6/TNF elevated.
- **Epidemiology**: female-predominant, age-related, but inconsistent across studies.
- **Imaging reliability**: panorama κ=0.16 / MRI κ=0.46 / CT κ=0.71; positive agreement 19% / 59% / 84% → **CT = gold standard** for bony change.
- **5 bony change types**: flattening, sclerosis, erosion, subchondral cyst, osteophyte.
- **DC/TMD (2014)**: clinical sens 0.55 / spec 0.61 → imaging essential. Imaging dx needs ≥1 of subchondral cyst, erosion, generalized sclerosis, osteophyte. Flattening / cortical sclerosis = indeterminate (normal variant/aging/remodeling).
- **Reading principle**: intact-cortex flattening or cortical-confined sclerosis is not pathologic OA; attend to cortical continuity (erosion/cyst) over condylar shape on CT.

## Limitations
- Narrative review; no pooled estimates; epidemiology inconsistent.
