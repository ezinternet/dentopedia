## One-line Summary
SR + network meta-analysis (57 RCTs, 2,033 patients): NSAIDs/IA corticosteroid/hyaluronate for TMD-joint; cyclobenzaprine for TMD-muscle; clonazepam and capsaicin for BMS — all limited GRADE evidence.

## 한줄요약
SR+NMA (57 RCT, 2,033명): TMD-관절에 NSAIDs/관절강내 코르티코스테로이드·히알루론산; TMD-근육에 시클로벤자프린; BMS에 클로나제팜·캅사이신 — 전반적 GRADE 근거 제한.

## Summary
This health technology assessment by the Swedish SBU agency systematically reviewed pharmacological treatments for orofacial pain. After screening 1,552 articles, 41 RCTs (2,033 patients) met final criteria. Three subgroups were analyzed: TMD-joint (15 studies), TMD-muscle (9 studies), BMS (17 studies). Network meta-analysis was possible for TMD-muscle (8 studies) and BMS (5 studies). Key findings: NSAIDs and intra-articular injections (corticosteroid, hyaluronate) are effective for TMD-joint pain; cyclobenzaprine for TMD-muscle; clonazepam and capsaicin for BMS. Evidence quality is uniformly low–moderate per GRADE, limiting confident clinical recommendations.

## Key Contributions
- Only NMA-based SR for orofacial pain pharmacotherapy to date (2017)
- TMD-joint: NSAIDs effective systemically; IA corticosteroid and hyaluronate both effective
- TMD-muscle NMA: cyclobenzaprine top-ranked; no other agent significantly superior to placebo
- BMS NMA: clonazepam and capsaicin reduce pain intensity significantly
- GRADE evidence low across all categories — calls for more rigorous RCTs

## Methodology
- SBU health technology assessment framework + GRADE quality rating
- PubMed / Cochrane / EMBASE to 1 March 2017; hand search
- RCTs only; chronic orofacial pain ≥3 months; adults ≥18
- Risk-of-bias: Cochrane tool → only low/medium studies included in NMA
- Primary outcome: pain intensity reduction (VAS/NRS)

## Results

| Subgroup | Treatment | Effect | GRADE |
|---|---|---|---|
| TMD-joint | NSAIDs (systemic) | Effective | Moderate |
| TMD-joint | IA corticosteroid | Effective | Low |
| TMD-joint | IA hyaluronate | Effective | Low |
| TMD-muscle | Cyclobenzaprine | Effective (NMA) | Low |
| BMS | Clonazepam | Effective (NMA) | Low |
| BMS | Capsaicin | Effective (NMA) | Low |

- NMA (TMD-muscle): cyclobenzaprine SUCRA highest; diazepam, other relaxants not significantly superior to placebo
- NMA (BMS): clonazepam (topical > systemic) > capsaicin > placebo; alpha-lipoic acid inconsistent

## Clinical Relevance
| 상황 | 1차 선택 | 대안 |
|---|---|---|
| TMD-관절통 | NSAIDs (이부프로펜 400–600 mg) | IA 히알루론산 또는 코르티코스테로이드 |
| TMD-근육통 | 시클로벤자프린 (단기) | 스플린트 + 물리치료 병행 |
| BMS | 클로나제팜 국소 rinse | 캅사이신 topical / 전신 저용량 |

주의: 시클로벤자프린은 진정 부작용 있음; 클로나제팜 장기 사용 시 의존성 위험 — 단기 처방 원칙.
