## One-line Summary
BMJ Rapid Recommendations clinical practice guideline (2023, GRADE EtD) for chronic TMD pain: strong recommendation for conservative reversible care first, strong recommendation against irreversible occlusal modification, and conditional recommendation for botulinum toxin (BTX) in refractory myofascial pain.

## Summary
The BMJ Rapid Recommendations panel published this clinical practice guideline in December 2023 for managing chronic pain associated with 악관절증 (Temporomandibular Disorder, TMD). Using GRADE Evidence-to-Decision framework, the panel issued a strong recommendation for conservative, reversible treatments as first-line care. 교합안정장치 (Occlusal Splint) and physiotherapy/exercise received conditional recommendations as adjuncts. 보툴리눔독소 (Botulinum Toxin, BTX) was conditionally recommended for refractory myofascial pain unresponsive to conservative measures. A strong recommendation against all irreversible occlusal modifications (grinding, crowns for TMD indication) was issued. This guideline represents the current authoritative evidence-to-practice synthesis for chronic TMD pain.

## Key Contributions
- Authoritative evidence-based guideline for chronic TMD pain management (December 2023)
- Integrates evidence from Cochrane and SR+MA across all intervention categories
- Provides clear treatment hierarchy: conservative-first → reversible interventions → BTX if refractory
- Explicit strong recommendation against irreversible occlusal treatments
- Decision aids and patient-shared decision-making tools

## Methodology
- BMJ Rapid Recommendations framework
- Multidisciplinary panel: TMD specialists, pain medicine, physiotherapy, patient representatives
- GRADE Evidence-to-Decision (EtD) framework
- Linked systematic reviews commissioned for each intervention category
- Recommendation strength: strong vs. conditional; direction: for vs. against

## Results (Recommendations)
| Intervention | Recommendation | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| Education + self-care | For | Strong |
| 교합안정장치 (Occlusal Splint) | For (adjunct) | Conditional |
| Physiotherapy/exercise | For | Conditional |
| 보툴리눔독소 (BTX) for refractory myofascial pain | For | Conditional |
| NSAIDs / muscle relaxants | For (short-term) | Conditional |
| Amitriptyline for chronic pain | For | Conditional |
| 관절천자 (Arthrocentesis) as first-line | Against | Conditional |
| Irreversible occlusal modification | Against | Strong |
