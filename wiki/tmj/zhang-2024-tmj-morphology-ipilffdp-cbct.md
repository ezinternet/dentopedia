## One-line Summary
Retrospective CBCT study (n=12, 1 yr): IPILFFDP does not significantly alter TMJ morphology; posterior joint space increases; ~2 mm VD increase correlates with medial joint space enlargement.

## 한줄요약
후향적 CBCT 연구 (n=12, 1년): 즉시식립·즉시부하 전악 보철 후 TMJ 형태 유의 변화 없음; 후방 관절강 증가; VD 약 2 mm 상승은 내측 관절강 증가와 상관.

## Summary
This retrospective CBCT study is the first to quantitatively evaluate TMJ morphological changes before and after immediate implant placement with immediate loaded full-arch fixed dental prostheses (IPILFFDP) in 12 patients without pre-existing TMJ symptoms. Pre- and 1-year post-treatment CBCT scans were analyzed using Mimics software across 18 TMJ and VD measurement items. Overall, no significant bilateral TMJ morphology differences were found (null hypothesis not rejected). However, posterior joint space significantly increased post-treatment. A mean VD increase of approximately 2 mm was recorded and correlated with increased medial joint space. The authors attribute TMJ stability to preservation of the patient's neuromuscular memory and adapted centric posture (ACP), which served as a reliable guide for prosthetic jaw position reconstruction.

## Key Contributions
- First quantitative CBCT assessment of TMJ morphology pre/post IPILFFDP
- No significant change in condyle volume, surface area, or overall joint space at 1 year
- Posterior joint space significantly increased — likely reflecting condylar seating change
- VD increase of ~2 mm: safe (within Abduo ≤5 mm threshold); correlates with medial joint space
- ACP-guided IPILFFDP may preserve jaw relationship and minimize TMD risk

## Methodology
- n=12 (no TMJ symptoms; DC/TMD criteria); retrospective; single center (Zhejiang, China)
- CBCT at baseline and 1-year post-loading; 18 measurement items in Mimics 21.0
- VD increase controlled ≤5 mm
- Statistics: t-test / Wilcoxon; Pearson / Spearman correlation; SPSS 24.0

## Results
| Parameter | Finding |
|---|---|
| Overall TMJ morphology | No significant pre/post difference |
| Posterior joint space | Significantly increased post-IPILFFDP |
| VD change | ~+2 mm (within safe range) |
| VD increase vs medial joint space | Significant positive correlation |
| TMD adverse events | None at 1 year |
