## One-line Summary
SR (15 RCTs, J Clin Med 2023): physiotherapy — especially manual therapy combined with therapeutic exercise — effectively reduces TMD pain, improves function and QoL; combination therapy yields best results.

## 한줄요약
SR (15 RCT): 수기치료+운동치료 병용이 TMD 통증·기능·삶의 질에 가장 효과적인 물리치료 접근.

## Summary
This systematic review (PRISMA, PROSPERO-registered) evaluated physiotherapy interventions for temporomandibular disorders. From 656 articles screened across four databases, 15 RCTs were included. All major physiotherapy modalities — manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, photobiomodulation, cervico-mandibular mobilization, and electrotherapy — were effective for reducing TMD pain. The critical finding: combination of manual therapy and therapeutic exercise consistently achieved the best treatment outcomes across multiple studies. This SR supports physiotherapy as evidence-based first-line conservative treatment for TMD.

## Key Contributions
- All physiotherapy modalities effective; combination therapy (MT + exercise) optimal
- Photobiomodulation alone or combined with MT effective
- Cervical mobilization + exercise effective for TMD with cervical involvement
- Evidence supports conservative physiotherapy before invasive intervention
- Multidisciplinary approach (physio + dentistry + psychology) recommended

## Methodology
- SR; PubMed / ScienceDirect / Dialnet / PEDro; 2010–June 2022
- Inclusion: RCTs in adults with diagnosed TMD; Spanish or English
- Quality: PEDro scale (scores ranged 5–8 in most included studies)
- Outcomes: pain (VAS), mandibular ROM, QoL, psychosocial measures

## Results

| Intervention | Outcome | Key study |
|---|---|---|
| Manual therapy alone | ↓ pain, ↑ ROM | Multiple |
| Therapeutic exercise alone | ↓ pain, ↑ strength | Abboud, Calixtre |
| MT + exercise (combined) | Best outcomes overall | Multiple |
| Photobiomodulation | ↓ pain (combined > alone) | Brochado 2018 |
| Cervico-mandibular mobilization + exercise | ↓ pain, ↑ ROM, ↑ QoL | Calixtre, Delgado |

- Disc displacement with click: conservative physiotherapy effective (Craane)
- Painful TMJ clicks: physiotherapy comparable to occlusal approaches (Madani)

## Clinical Relevance
TMD 1차 치료 프로토콜 (물리치료 기반):
1. **근육 위주 TMD** → 수기치료 + 치료적 운동 병용
2. **관절 위주 TMD** → 경부-하악 가동화 + 운동 + 필요 시 교합 안정장치 병행
3. **디스크 변위** → 보존적 물리치료 먼저 (수술·교합치료 전)
4. 약물치료([[tmj/haggman-henrikson-2017-pharmacological-orofacial-pain-sr-ma]])와 병행 시 시너지 가능
