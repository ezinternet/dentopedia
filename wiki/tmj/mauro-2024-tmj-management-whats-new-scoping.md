## One-line Summary
Scoping review of 109 SRs (Dent J 2024): tiered TMD management — non-invasive first (CBT/PT), then minimally invasive (splints/BTX/PRP/injections), surgery last; emerging: PRP, O2O3, stem cells show promise.

## 한줄요약
109편 SR 대상 scoping review (2024): TMD 단계적 치료 — 비침습 우선(CBT·PT), 최소침습(스플린트·BTX·PRP·관절주사), 수술 최후; 신흥 치료(PRP·오존·줄기세포) 주목.

## Summary
Comprehensive scoping review (PRISMA-ScR) of 109 SRs and MAs published 2019–2024 across Scopus/PubMed/Embase/Web of Science. Categories: 39 non-invasive articles, 120 minimally invasive references (7 sub-categories), 15 surgical articles. Key finding: PT+manual therapy has the strongest non-invasive evidence; multimodal approaches (CBT + PT + splint) are optimal; emerging modalities (PRP, O2O3, stem cells) show promise but need more RCTs. Surgery (arthroscopy > open) remains last resort after conservative failure.

## Key Contributions
- Most comprehensive 2019–2024 evidence map for TMD treatment
- Physical therapy + MT: consistently strongest non-invasive evidence
- CBT and counseling: effective for chronic/biopsychosocial TMD
- PRP/PRF: emerging regenerative option with growing evidence
- O2O3 (ozone) therapy: anti-inflammatory/analgesic adjunct
- BTX: well-supported for myofascial/bruxism components
- Arthroscopy preferred over open joint surgery

## Treatment Evidence Summary

| Category | Key treatments | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Non-invasive | CBT, PT+MT+exercise, O2O3 | Strong (PT/CBT); Moderate (ozone) |
| Minimally invasive | Splints, IA injections, BTX, PRP, acupuncture | Variable; splints/injections strongest |
| Surgical | Arthroscopy, open surgery | Reserved for severe/refractory cases |

## Emerging Treatments
- **PRP/PRF/stem cells**: intra-articular injection; regenerative potential; 23 SRs identified — growing body of evidence
- **O2O3**: anti-inflammatory + antimicrobial injection; 5 SRs; promising analgesic adjunct
- **Acupuncture**: 8 SRs; moderate evidence for pain reduction; compatible with PT protocols
