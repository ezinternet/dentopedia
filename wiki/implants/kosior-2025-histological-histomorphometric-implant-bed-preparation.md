## 한줄요약
SR(J Clin Med 2025, 29편): 5개 implant bed prep 방법(drilling, laser, piezo, osteotome, osseodensification) 조직학·조직형태계측 비교 — 전통 drilling이 microcracks·부정 골연·osteocyte 손상·발열 최다, %BIC 최저; 대체법이 우수하나 — 적절 parameter 사용 시 drilling도 동등 가능.

## Summary
A PRISMA SR (29 articles) compared five implant bed preparation methods on histological and histomorphometric outcomes. Classical rotary drilling produced more microcracks, uneven bone margins, osteocyte damage, and thermal injury, with lower %BIC than alternatives. Laser, piezoelectric surgery, osteotomes (Summer's technique), and osseodensification each yielded fewer microcracks, less inflammation, lower thermal risk, denser/more regular bone, and higher %BIC. The authors qualify the contrast: drilling can match alternatives if rotation speed, cooling, and load are within optimal ranges — implying that the drilling penalty observed is largely protocol-mediated, not inherent.

## Key Contributions
First SR to compare all five preparation families on histological/histomorphometric endpoints simultaneously. Sets %BIC and microcrack reduction as the comparator metrics rather than peak temperature alone.

## Methodology
- PRISMA SR; PubMed, Scopus, WoS (March 2025).
- 338 → 29 included.
- 5 method families compared.
- Heterogeneity precluded meta-analysis.

## Results
| Method | Microcracks | Osteocyte damage | Thermal injury | %BIC | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Classical drilling | Most | Highest | Highest | Lowest | Protocol-dependent |
| Laser | Few | Low | Low | Higher | Selective ablation |
| Piezoelectric | Few | Low | Variable | Higher | aquilanti caveat |
| Osteotome | Few | Low | Low | Higher | Lateral condensation |
| Osseodensification | Few | Low | Low | Highest reported | D3/D4 큰 이득 |

## Clinical Implications
- D3/D4 등 저밀도 골에선 osseodensification 우선 고려 — BIC·primary stability·thermal 모두 유리.
- 전통 drilling 유지 시: drill 교체 주기 + 차가운 saline + 적정 load·RPM + intermittent drilling이 alternatives와 비슷한 결과 만들 수 있음.
- piezo의 thermal 이슈는 aquilanti·jain SR과 일치 — 적절 protocol 준수가 전제.
