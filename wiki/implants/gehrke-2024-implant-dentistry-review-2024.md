## 한줄요약
현대 임플란트 치의학 서술적 고찰 — 표면 처리(SLA·SLActive·UV), 지르코니아 임플란트, 디지털 가이드 수술.

## Summary
Narrative review of contemporary implant dentistry covering surface modifications, zirconia implants vs titanium, and digital guided surgery accuracy.

## Key Contributions
- Surface modifications: SLA, SLActive, UV photofunctionalization — clinical outcomes compared
- Zirconia implants: growing evidence; 3-year survival 95–97%; esthetic advantage in thin biotype
- Digital guided surgery: 3D-printed guides achieve <1mm accuracy in positioning

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
Titanium remains gold standard. Zirconia implants gaining evidence, especially for esthetics. Digital guided surgery improves predictability for complex implant positioning.
