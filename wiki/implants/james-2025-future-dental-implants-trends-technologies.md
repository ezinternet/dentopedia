## Summary
Narrative review (Cureus 2025) covering advancements in implant systems, digital technologies, and patient-centered considerations over 2015–2025. Covers material evolution, AI/robotic integration, bioactive surfaces, and smart implant sensor technologies.

## Key Contributions
- **Material advances**: Ti → zirconia, Ti-Zr alloys (Roxolid type), scaffold-based designs
- **Digital technologies**: AI, robotics, 3D printing → enhanced surgical precision
- **Bioactive surfaces** + regenerative biomolecules → improved osseointegration
- **Smart implants**: embedded sensors for real-time monitoring of implant health/loading
- **Limitations**: high cost, steep learning curves, inconsistent long-term data, regulatory gaps
- Minimally invasive techniques + patient-centered design = future direction
