## 한줄요약
전향적 연구(n=14, D3/D4 골, Maestro™ 임플란트, 12개월): 생존율 92.3%; 골소실 통계적 유의차 없음 — 저밀도 골에서 특수 설계 임플란트의 임상 가능성 입증.

## Summary
Prospective clinico-radiographical study evaluating Maestro™ implants (square-thread design, D3/D4 optimized) in 14 patients with low-density (D3/D4) jaw bone. At 12 months: 92.3% survival rate, statistically non-significant bone loss, and acceptable peri-implant soft tissue health. Demonstrates that poor bone quality is not an absolute contraindication when appropriate implant design is used.

## Key Contributions
- Supports clinical viability of implants in D3/D4 bone with appropriate design selection
- Square-thread design: 10× less shear force than V-thread — biomechanical rationale for D3/D4
- 92.3% survival rate meets acceptable clinical threshold for challenging bone
