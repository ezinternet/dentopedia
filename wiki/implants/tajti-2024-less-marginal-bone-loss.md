## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 8 studies (bone-level platform-switched implants): long abutments (≥2 mm) preserve more marginal bone than short (<2 mm) — at 1 year (6 studies/384 implants) MD 0.26 mm (95% CI −0.02 to 0.53, I²=73%), moderate GRADE; probing depth and bleeding unaffected.

## 한줄요약

8편 SR+MA(골수준 플랫폼스위칭 임플란트): 긴 어버트먼트(≥2 mm)가 짧은 것보다 변연골소실 적음 — 1년 6편/384 임플란트 MD 0.26 mm(95% CI −0.02~0.53), GRADE 중등도; 탐침깊이·BOP는 차이 없음.

## Summary

A PRISMA 2020 systematic review and meta-analysis (PROSPERO CRD42022331923) testing one prosthetic variable — abutment height — under tightly controlled conditions: bone-level, platform-switching titanium implants restored with a one-abutment-one-time protocol, deliberately excluding the guided bone regeneration, tissue-level, zirconia, and retrospective/animal studies that biased earlier abutment-height reviews. Across 8 studies (7 meta-analyzed), long abutments (≥2 mm) consistently preserved more marginal bone than short (<2 mm), with the effect strengthening from 6 months to 1 year. This extends the implant-type MBL literature by isolating the abutment-height lever within a methodologically clean dataset.

## Key Contributions

- A clean, current abutment-height meta-analysis free of the retrospective/animal contamination of prior reviews.
- Long abutments (≥2 mm) reduce marginal bone loss, directionally consistent across timepoints, moderate GRADE certainty.
- The benefit is bone-specific — no difference in probing pocket depth or bleeding on probing.

## Methodology

PICO comparison of long vs short abutments; random-effects (Hartung-Knapp) pooled mean difference for MBL/PPD and risk ratio for BOP, Mantel–Haenszel pooling in R. Split-mouth correlated data handled with ICC=0 and ICC=0.5 sensitivity. Heterogeneity by Cochrane Q and I²; RoB 2 + ROBINS-I; GRADE certainty.

## Results

| Outcome | Studies / implants | Effect (long vs short) | Heterogeneity / certainty |
|---|---|---|---|
| MBL at 6 mo | 3 / 174 | long favored, MD ≈0.63 mm (95% CI −0.16 to 1.42) | I²=93% |
| MBL at 1 yr | 6 / 384 | long ~0.26 mm less loss, MD 0.26 (95% CI −0.02 to 0.53) | I²=73%, moderate GRADE |
| Probing pocket depth (1 yr) | 2 / 154 | no difference, MD −0.05 (95% CI −1.11 to 1.01), p=0.650 | I²=0% |
| Bleeding on probing (1 yr) | 4 / 256 | no difference, RR 0.97 (95% CI 0.76–1.23), p=0.927 | I²=0%, moderate |

Several pooled CIs cross zero — the bone-protective direction is consistent but not always statistically conclusive.
