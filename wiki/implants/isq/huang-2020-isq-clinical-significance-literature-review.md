## One-line Summary
Narrative literature review of factors influencing ISQ: identified 17 factors total, of which only 6 are clinically predictive and only 2 (local bone quality and implant macro-design) have a well-documented scientific basis — ISQ should be interpreted together with bone type, not in isolation.

## Summary
Narrative review with systematic database search identifying all factors reported to influence ISQ. Found 17 factors total (13 basic + 4 bone quality subfactors). Critical filtering: only **6 clinically predictive**, only **2 with well-documented scientific basis** — local bone quality and implant macro-design.

## 17 ISQ-Influencing Factors

| Tier | Factors |
|---|---|
| **Well-documented (2)** | Local bone quality, implant macro-design |
| **Clinically predictive (6 total)** | + Insertion torque, site preparation, healing time, measurement direction |
| **Other identified (11)** | SmartPeg type, measurement direction variation, device type, resonance frequency, implant length, diameter, surface treatment, patient factors, etc. |

## Clinical Takeaway
- ISQ is a valid stability indicator but **must be contextualized** with bone type and site
- A single ISQ number without bone quality context is insufficient for clinical decisions
- Local bone quality (cortical thickness, density) is the dominant driver — consistent with CBCT-ISQ correlation studies
- Macro-design (tapered vs parallel) is the most modifiable controllable factor
