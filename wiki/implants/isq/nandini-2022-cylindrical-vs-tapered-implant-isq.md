## 한줄요약
분구강 in vivo 연구(30쌍): 테이퍼 임플란트가 모든 시점에서 원통형보다 유의하게 높은 ISQ; 통증 VAS 및 치주 지표도 테이퍼 우세; 두 군 생존율 100%

## Summary
Split-mouth in vivo study comparing tapered vs cylindrical implants in 30 patients (60 implants total). At all timepoints (T0, 3mo, 6mo), tapered implants showed significantly higher ISQ, lower pain VAS, and better peri-implant tissue health. 100% survival in both groups.

## Results

| Parameter | Tapered | Cylindrical | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| ISQ (all timepoints) | Higher | Lower | p<0.05 |
| Pain VAS | Lower | Higher | p<0.05 |
| BOP | Less | More | p<0.05 |
| Probing depth | Shallower | Deeper | p<0.05 |
| Survival | 100% | 100% | NS |

## Clinical Takeaway
- Tapered implant design provides superior initial and secondary stability (ISQ) compared to cylindrical
- Additional benefits: less postoperative pain and better peri-implant tissue response
- Both designs are survivable, but tapered is preferred for ISQ-based immediate/early loading protocols
