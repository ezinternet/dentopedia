## 한줄요약
후향적 단변량 분석(745 Neoss, 334명; 5년 CSR 97.3% 임플란트수준 / 95.8% 환자수준): 식립·로딩 시 ISQ<70/<75가 실패 위험↑(비보정 OR). ⚠ 다변량 회귀 아님 — 카이제곱·Fisher + 비보정 OR이며 환자내 군집 미보정.

## Summary
Large retrospective study of 745 Neoss implants (334 patients), ≥5-year follow-up. 5-year CSR 97.3% (implant-level) / 95.8% (patient-level). Low ISQ (<70, <75) at placement or loading is associated with higher failure on UNADJUSTED odds-ratio analysis.

> ⚠ **Methods correction (2026-06-14):** despite the article subtitle, there is **no multivariate model**. Analysis is univariate — Pearson chi-square / Fisher exact + Kruskal–Wallis / Mann–Whitney + **unadjusted** odds ratios at ISQ thresholds — and **implant-within-patient clustering is not accounted for** (745 implants nested in 334 patients). For survival, bone quality/quantity, jaw, position, diameter and length were NOT significant; only full-arch (vs partial) rehabilitation and low ISQ were.

## Key Results

| ISQ threshold | Timing | Unadjusted OR (95% CI) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| < 70 | placement | 3.9 (1.6–9.7) | 0.004 |
| < 70 | loading | 6.3 (1.9–20.2) | 0.003 |
| < 75 | placement | 6.9 (2.0–23.8) | 0.001 |
| < 75 | loading | 17.9 (2.3–142.9) | <0.001 |
| < 60, < 65 | either | n.s. (sparse data) | >0.05 |

- 5-year CSR 97.3% (implant) / 95.8% (patient); actuarial life-table, **no CI reported**
- Survival predictors (univariate): full-arch rehab (p=0.003) + low ISQ only
- Bone quality affected ISQ (stability) but NOT survival (p=0.740); OR CIs very wide (20 failures total)

## Clinical Takeaway
- **ISQ <70 at placement = red flag** — consider delayed loading, augmentation, or re-evaluation
- ISQ <75 also warrants caution; bone quality did not independently affect survival here
- ISQ at placement is *associated* with 5-year survival, but this study cannot establish *independent* prediction (univariate, unadjusted, clustering unmodelled, manufacturer-employed statistician)
