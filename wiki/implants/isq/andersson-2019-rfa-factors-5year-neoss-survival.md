## 한줄요약
후향적 다변량 분석(745 Neoss 임플란트, 5년 CSR 97.3%): 식립 시 ISQ<70이 통계적으로 유의한 실패 예측인자; ISQ<75도 유의한 위험 임계치

## Summary
Large retrospective multivariate study of 745 Neoss implants with 5-year follow-up. 5-year cumulative survival rate: 97.3%. **ISQ <70 at placement significantly predicts failure** — the strongest individual predictor in multivariate analysis. Bone type D3/D4, posterior maxilla, and shorter implant length are secondary risk factors.

## Key Results

| ISQ at Placement | Failure Risk | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| ISQ < 70 | Significantly elevated | p<0.05 |
| ISQ < 75 | Significant (broader threshold) | p<0.05 |
| ISQ ≥ 75 | Low baseline risk | — |

- 5-year CSR: 97.3%
- Multivariate factors: ISQ, bone type, location, implant length, loading protocol
- Bone type D3/D4 associated with lower ISQ and higher failure

## Clinical Takeaway
- **ISQ <70 at placement = red flag** — consider delayed loading, augmentation, or implant removal/re-placement
- ISQ <75 also warrants caution, especially in D3/D4 bone
- ISQ is a valid long-term survival predictor when measured at placement
