## 한줄요약
RCT (n=100, 5개 골형성 프로토콜, 3개월 추적): 중속 드릴링(300rpm)이 T4 최고 ISQ; 고속(800rpm)이 고밀도골에서 최고 삽입토크; 골밀도화(OD)는 중간 치유기 일시적 ISQ 상승(유지 안됨); 골농축(bone condensation)은 ISQ 점진적 하락 (CIDRE 2026).

## Summary
Five-group parallel RCT (n=20/group, 100 total) comparing high-speed (800 rpm), moderate-speed (300 rpm), ultra-low-speed (50 rpm), osseodensification, and bone condensation osteotomy protocols on ISQ trajectory over 3 months. All patients received identical macrodesign implants. IT recorded at placement; ISQ measured at T0–T4 (placement through 3 months). Moderate-speed 300rpm demonstrated the highest ISQ at T4 (Bonferroni p<0.05). High-speed 800rpm yielded highest initial IT especially in dense bone, but not best long-term ISQ. Osseodensification showed transient mid-healing ISQ gains without sustained advantage. Bone condensation showed progressive ISQ decline. All groups: mean IT>30 Ncm, baseline ISQ>80. Postoperative anxiety (VAS): osseodensification = highest residual anxiety; bone condensation = greatest anxiety reduction.

## Key Contributions
- First RCT comparing all 5 major osteotomy protocols in a single trial with longitudinal ISQ trajectory
- 300 rpm moderate-speed: optimal protocol for long-term stability
- Osseodensification: initial/mid-healing benefit is transient — sustained advantage not confirmed at T4
- Bone condensation: progressive ISQ decline is concerning — least favorable long-term profile
- High-speed 800 rpm: good initial IT in dense bone but not superior at 3 months
- Postoperative anxiety differs by protocol — novel secondary outcome

## Methodology
5-group parallel RCT. n=100, single implant per patient, identical macrodesign. Groups (n=20 each): 800rpm, 300rpm, 50rpm, osseodensification, bone condensation. IT at placement. ISQ at T0/T1(1wk)/T2(1mo)/T3(2mo)/T4(3mo). Exclusion: IT<25 Ncm at placement. Primary: ISQ change T0→T4. Bonferroni correction for pairwise comparisons.

## Results
| Protocol | Initial IT | ISQ at T4 | Trend | VAS Anxiety |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 800 rpm | Highest (dense bone) | Mid-range | Stable | Moderate |
| 300 rpm | High | **Best** (p<0.05) | ↑ Progressive | Moderate |
| 50 rpm | Moderate | Mid-range | Stable | Low |
| Osseodensification | Moderate | Mid-range | Transient ↑ then plateau | Highest |
| Bone condensation | Moderate | Lowest | Progressive ↓ | Lowest |
