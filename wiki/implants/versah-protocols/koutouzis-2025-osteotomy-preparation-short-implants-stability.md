## One-line Summary

Animal study (90 short 6mm implants, porcine tibia): OD significantly increased insertion torque only for wide (5.4mm) implants (50.0 vs 28.0 Ncm, p=0.005); narrow (4.2mm) showed no IT advantage; histomorphometry unchanged.

## 한줄요약

동물 연구(돼지 경골, 6mm 임플란트 90개): OD는 광폭(5.4mm) 임플란트에서만 유의하게 높은 삽입 토크(50.0 vs 28.0 Ncm, p=0.005)를 보였고, 세폭(4.2mm)에서는 이점 없었으며 조직계측적 차이도 없었다.

## Summary

Ninety Astra Tech EV short (6mm) implants were placed in cancellous-dominant porcine tibia across 9 groups (3 diameters × 3 preparation techniques). The critical finding: OD's IT advantage over standard drilling is **only significant for wide (5.4mm) implants** — not for narrow (4.2mm) or regular (4.8mm).

W-OD achieved 50.0 Ncm vs W-ST 28.0 Ncm (p=0.005), while N-OD at 31.5 Ncm did not significantly differ from N-ST. The osteotome technique similarly improved W group IT (46.87 Ncm, p=0.026), suggesting the mechanical advantage is not exclusive to OD. No histomorphometric differences in bone marrow space or connective tissue contact were observed — the IT difference is a mechanical compaction effect without early biological tissue differentiation.

**Clinical implication:** When OD is chosen for short implants in low-density sites, selecting wider diameter (≥5.4mm) may be needed to realize the IT benefit. Simply applying OD to narrow short implants does not guarantee improved stability.

## Key Contributions

- Diameter × technique interaction: OD IT benefit requires wide (5.4mm) diameter in cancellous bone
- Diameter effect within OD: W-OD (50.0 Ncm) > N-OD (31.5 Ncm, p=0.04) — widening is additive
- Osteotome IT improvement comparable to OD for wide implants — OD is not uniquely superior
- Histomorphometry NS: immediate bone condensation effect is mechanical, not histologically distinguishable at early stage

## Methodology

- 90 Astra Tech EV implants (6mm length; 4.2, 4.8, 5.4mm diameter) in porcine tibia plateau
- 9 groups (n=10 each): N-ST, N-OT, N-OD, R-ST, R-OT, R-OD, W-ST, W-OT, W-OD
- Histomorphometry: 4 implants per N group only (N-ST, N-OT, N-OD)
- Measurements: insertion torque (Ncm), ISQ, bone marrow space %, connective tissue contact %
