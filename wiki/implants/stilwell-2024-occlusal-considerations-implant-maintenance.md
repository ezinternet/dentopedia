## Summary
British Dental Journal overview article on occlusal considerations for annual implant maintenance. Covers hardware risks (implant fracture, screw loosening, restoration fracture) and biological risks (MBL, peri-implantitis) arising from occlusal overload, and proposes a 4-step assessment protocol.

## Key Contributions
- Implant fracture rate: ~0.5% — rare but serious
- Osseointegration = rigid connection (no PDL): reduced shock absorption and proprioception
- Parafunction (bruxism): major risk → splint/protection mandatory
- 4-step annual occlusal check:
  1. Patient-reported changes
  2. Restoration integrity (screw loosening, material fracture)
  3. Occlusal scheme (contact pattern, excursive guidance)
  4. Spatial changes (supra-eruption, drift)
