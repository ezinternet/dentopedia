## One-line Summary

RCT 1-year peri-implant outcomes (n=30/40 completed) comparing Ti-reinforced d-PTFE membranes vs Ti meshes + collagen membranes after VRA in posterior mandible: equivalent PBL change (0.67 vs 0.61 mm) and all soft-tissue parameters.

## Summary

This RCT (registration NCT04332679, Bologna/Rome) is the 1-year peri-implant follow-up paper in the Cucchi series on VRA comparing **Ti-reinforced d-PTFE membranes (Group A)** vs **Ti meshes + resorbable collagen membranes (Group B)** with simultaneous implant placement in the posterior mandible.

At 1 year post-prosthetic loading, both groups showed comparable outcomes:
- **Peri-implant bone loss**: 0.67 mm (d-PTFE) vs 0.61 mm (Ti mesh), difference -0.05 mm (95% CI: -0.27 to 0.16, p>0.9337)
- All soft-tissue parameters (PPD, BoP, mPI, mGI, tKT, wKT, fornix depth) showed no significant between-group differences
- Strong IBP–PBL correlation (p<0.0001): interproximal bone peaks are predictors of peri-implant bone levels
- Both groups: MBL <1.0 mm in first year — within clinical acceptance thresholds

**Clinical implication**: Choice between d-PTFE and Ti mesh + collagen for VRA does not affect 1-year peri-implant bone or soft-tissue maintenance.

## Key Contributions

1. **Biomaterial equivalence for peri-implant maintenance**: No advantage of either technique at 1 year
2. **IBP–PBL correlation** identified as a strong predictor (p<0.0001) — relevant for treatment planning
3. Confirms <1.0 mm bone loss threshold achievable with both VRA methods
4. Part of the Cucchi RCT series: pilot (bone gain) → this paper (1-year peri-implant) → extended (CAD/CAM, 2024)

## Methodology

- **Design**: RCT, n=40 enrolled, n=30 completed; posterior mandible
- **Group A**: Ti-reinforced d-PTFE membranes + simultaneous implants
- **Group B**: Ti mesh + collagen membranes + simultaneous implants
- **Evaluation timepoints**: Baseline (prosthetic restoration) and 1 year
- **Outcomes**: PBL, IBP, PPD, BoP, mPI, mGI, tKT, wKT, fornix depth
- **Statistics**: p=0.05 significance threshold
- **Note**: Abstract-only; detailed per-parameter statistics not fully available
