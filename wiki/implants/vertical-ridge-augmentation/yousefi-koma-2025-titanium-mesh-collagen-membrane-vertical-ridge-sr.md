## One-line Summary

Systematic review (3 RCTs + 1 case series, 119 patients across 9 articles): adding a collagen membrane on top of titanium meshes or Ti-reinforced PTFE membranes made no significant difference in vertical bone gain or surgical/healing complication rates, suggesting the collagen membrane may be unnecessary in GBR-based VRA.

## Summary

This PRISMA-registered systematic review (no meta-analysis) asks whether the collagen membrane commonly placed over titanium meshes or perforated Ti-reinforced dPTFE membranes is actually necessary in GBR-based vertical ridge augmentation. The rationale for adding a collagen membrane is to restore the cell-occlusion property lost when the mesh/membrane is perforated to improve graft revascularization. Pooling the limited human evidence, the review found no significant outcome benefit from the collagen membrane, but the small evidence base (3 RCTs + 1 case series) precludes a firm recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- First SR focused specifically on the with-vs-without collagen membrane question for Ti meshes / Ti-reinforced dPTFE membranes in VRA.
- Synthesizes both randomized and non-randomized human studies (case reports excluded).
- Frames the trade-off: perforations aid revascularization but lose cell occlusion, which a collagen membrane is meant to compensate.

## Methodology

- PRISMA 2020; PROSPERO CRD420251002615. MEDLINE/PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Embase, Cochrane up to 5 June 2025.
- PICO: GBR-based VRA with Ti meshes/membranes ± collagen membrane in adults with vertical atrophy needing implants; primary outcome vertical bone gain.
- Eligible: RCTs, controlled trials, case-controls, case series; case reports and buccal dehiscence defects excluded.
- Two reviewers + third for disputes; qualitative synthesis (no meta-analysis).

## Results

- Included 119 patients from 3 RCTs and 1 case series across 9 articles.
- Adding a collagen membrane caused no significant difference in vertical bone gain or surgical/healing complication rates.
- Conclusion: a collagen membrane over titanium meshes/Ti-reinforced membranes might not be necessary in GBR-based VRA; further human RCTs are required for a reliable conclusion.
