## 한줄요약
등가성 RCT 5년 추적(n=60명, 128 임플란트): PS 생존율 96.6%; PS MBL 0.23 mm 낮음(p=0.025); 두 군 비동등 선언 — PS가 골보존 면에서 우수

## Summary
5-year follow-up of an equivalence RCT comparing platform switching (PS) vs platform matching (PM). 60 patients, 128 implants at final appointment. Overall survival 96.6%. PS implants showed 0.23 mm lower MBL (95% CI 0.03–0.43, p=0.025). The two groups were declared non-equivalent, confirming PS advantage for bone preservation at 5 years.

## Key Contributions
- Longest follow-up in this RCT series: 5 years post-loading
- 60 patients, 128 implants (65 PS, 63 PM) at final visit
- Global survival: 96.6%, no significant difference between groups
- PS: 0.23 mm lower MBL (p=0.025) — clinically meaningful
- Groups declared non-equivalent: PS superior for bone preservation
- Equivalence trial design strengthens conclusion

## Methodology
Extension of multicentre equivalence RCT (Rocha 2016). 33 patients/72 PM + 35 patients/74 PS implants enrolled. Final appointment at 5 years: 31 PS patients (65 implants), 29 PM patients (63 implants). Radiographic MBL measured on standardized periapical x-rays. Non-equivalence margin pre-specified. Kaplan-Meier survival analysis.

## Results
- Global survival: 96.6% (no group difference, p=0.647)
- PS MBL: 0.23 mm lower than PM (95% CI 0.03–0.43, p=0.025)
- Non-equivalence confirmed: PS significantly better for bone preservation
- No adverse events specifically related to connection type
- Benefit maintained and significant through 5 years
