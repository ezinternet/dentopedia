## One-line Summary

SR+MA (6 human RCT/NRCT studies) of OD vs conventional drilling in low bone density: ISQ non-significant at both time points (SMD 2.13, p=0.06; SMD 1.81, p=0.11); bone density significantly higher immediately post-placement (SMD 2.14, p=0.004) but not at 3–7 months.

## 한줄요약

저밀도 골 SR+MA(인간 RCT/NRCT 6편): OD는 ISQ에서 비유의(즉시 p=0.06, 추적 p=0.11)였으나 식립 직후 골밀도는 유의하게 높았고(SMD 2.14, p=0.004), 3–7개월 후 비유의였다.

## Summary

This SR+MA restricted inclusion to human-based RCTs and NRCTs, capturing 6 studies out of 1,454 screened. The conclusions mirror Mohammadi 2025 (7 studies, broader inclusion): OD does not significantly outperform conventional drilling in ISQ-measured stability at either immediate or follow-up time points. However, peri-implant bone density is significantly higher immediately after OD placement (SMD 2.14, p=0.004), confirming the mechanical compaction effect — though this advantage disappears by 3–7 months.

The ISQ immediate p-value of 0.06 (just missing significance) and the confidence intervals crossing zero (−0.08 to 4.35) suggest a consistent positive trend that current study sample sizes lack power to confirm. Alongside Mohammadi 2025, this second parallel SR+MA from the same year strengthens the "ISQ trend, not significance" interpretation.

## Key Contributions

- Independent corroboration of Mohammadi 2025's ISQ NS finding (different team, same year, human-only RCT/NRCT restriction)
- Bone density immediate advantage (SMD 2.14, p=0.004): confirms OD's compaction creates denser peri-implant bone at placement — even without ISQ difference
- Bone density advantage disappears at 3–7 months: biological remodeling normalizes initial density difference
- ISQ CI just barely crosses zero (-0.08 to 4.35): trend toward benefit not ruled out, but not established

## Methodology

- Electronic + manual literature search; 1,454 publications screened
- 6 studies included (human RCTs and NRCTs; low bone density sites)
- RoB 2.0 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (NRCTs); RevMan 5.4; random-fixed effects; Q test + I²
- Outcomes: ISQ (immediate, follow-up); bone density (immediate, 3–7 months)

## Results

| Outcome | SMD | 95% CI | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| ISQ (immediate) | 2.13 | −0.08, 4.35 | 0.06 (NS) |
| ISQ (follow-up) | 1.81 | −0.41, 4.03 | 0.11 (NS) |
| Bone density (immediate) | 2.14 | 0.68, 3.59 | **0.004** |
| Bone density (3–7 months) | 1.54 | −0.34, 3.43 | 0.11 (NS) |
