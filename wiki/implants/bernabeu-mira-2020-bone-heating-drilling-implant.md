## 한줄요약
인비트로(Materials 2020, 800 rpm, irrigation 없음, 자동press): drill **design**(twisted < straight)이 **material**(SS ≈ DLC)보다 발열에 큰 영향; initial·pilot drill > progressive·final drill 발열; 50회 drilling 동안 점진적 온도 상승.

## Summary
Three drill groups — stainless-steel (SS) straight, DLC-coated straight, DLC-coated twisted — were tested in a fully automated press across 50 drillings per drill, in standardized synthetic bone, without irrigation. The overall mean thermal change did not differ significantly between SS and DLC, isolating drill **design** (twisted blade) as the dominant thermal driver: twisted geometry produced significantly less heat than straight blades for progressive and final drills (p<0.01). Initial and pilot drills generated more heat than progressive and final drills. All drills, irrespective of group, showed gradual temperature elevation across 50 uses, confirming wear as an independent factor.

## Key Contributions
First study to decompose material, design, and wear contributions in a single controlled design — concluding that geometry (twisted vs straight) outranks material (SS vs DLC) for thermal management.

## Methodology
- 3 groups × 4 sequential drills (initial, pilot, progressive, final).
- 800 rpm, intermittent gradual drilling, 2 kg axial load via automatic press.
- Synthetic bone blocks.
- **No irrigation** (deliberate variable isolation).
- Thermographic camera for mean thermal change.
- 50 drillings/drill, sterilized every 5 uses.
- Stats: t-test, ANOVA, multiple linear regression (α = 0.05).

## Results
| Effect | Direction | p |
|---|---|---|
| Material (SS vs DLC) overall mean | NS | >0.05 |
| Material on pilot drill specifically | Different | <0.05 |
| Design: twisted < straight (progressive) | Lower heat | <0.01 |
| Design: twisted < straight (final) | Lower heat | <0.01 |
| Initial/pilot vs progressive/final | Higher heat | Significant |
| 50-use trend | Progressive ↑ | — |

## Clinical Implications
- Drill 선택 시 재질 변경보다 **twisted flute 디자인 채택**이 발열 감소에 더 효과적 — 특히 progressive·final drill 단계에서.
- Initial·pilot drill 단계 발열이 큼 — 가장 작은 직경이지만 cortical bone과 접촉 면적/시간이 길어 마찰열 누적. 이 단계 irrigation에 주의.
- 50회 사용 동안 누적 마모로 점진적 발열 — 교체 주기 준수 근거.
- **단, 본 실험은 irrigation 미사용** — 임상 절대온도는 직접 적용 불가. 상대적 비교는 유효.
