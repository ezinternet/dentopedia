Systematic review (38 clinical trials) of marginal bone loss (MBL) around bone-level (BL) vs tissue-level (TL) implants: high heterogeneity prevents a definitive BL-vs-TL verdict, but platform-switching, Morse taper connection, and SLActive surface each significantly reduce MBL, and most total MBL occurs within the first year.

## Summary
Systematic review of 38 clinical trials examining marginal bone loss (MBL) around bone-level (BL) and tissue-level (TL) implants. High heterogeneity prevents definitive BL vs TL comparison, but key modifiers are clear: **platform-switching**, **Morse taper connection**, and **SLActive surface** all significantly reduce MBL.

## BL vs TL: Core Concepts

| | BL (Bone-Level) | TL (Tissue-Level) |
|---|---|---|
| Platform position | At crestal bone | At soft tissue |
| Microgap location | Crestal (bone level) | Supracrestal (tissue level) |
| Esthetic zone | Preferred | Gray metallic shadow risk |
| MBL mechanism | Bacterial contamination at crestal microgap | Microgap above bone → less crestal inflammation |

## Key MBL Data Points

| Study | Design | MBL | Follow-up |
|---|---|---|---|
| Filippi 2013 | BL, SLActive, 908 implants | >1mm in <5% | 1–3 yr |
| Wu 2018 | BL, 114 implants | 1.2mm (yr 1) + 0.78mm (yr 1–5) | 5 yr |
| Lago 2017 | BL, platform-switched | 0.28mm total | 5 yr |
| Vanlıoğlu 2014 | BL, platform-switched | 0.12mm final | 4 yr |
| Buser 2009 | BL, GBR+SLActive, maxilla ant | 0.18mm | 12 mo |

## Platform-Switching: Key Modifier

- Conventional connection: **0.7–2.5 mm** bone loss at 6 months
- Platform-switched: **0.12–0.29 mm** at 1 year
- Mechanism: shifts stress and bacterial interface inward → away from crestal bone

## Abutment-Fixture Connection and Bacterial Leakage

```
External hex > Internal hex > Conical > Morse taper
(most leakage)                          (least leakage)
```
→ Morse taper = best seal, but dynamic loading can increase bacterial penetration

## Clinical Takeaways
- **BL preferred in esthetic zones**: placed more apically, better emergence profile
- **TL advantage**: microgap above bone → less crestal inflammation risk
- **Platform-switching**: use whenever possible in BL implants to protect crestal bone
- **Connection choice**: Morse taper or internal conical for lowest MBL risk
- **SLActive**: reduces osseointegration time, associated with minimal early bone loss
- **First-year MBL is critical**: majority of total MBL occurs in year 1 (Wu 2018: ~1.2mm yr1 vs 0.78mm over next 4 years)
