## 한줄요약
인비트로(IJN 2011): TiO2 나노결절(300 nm) + 마이크로피트 하이브리드 표면 → UV 광기능화 (Photofunctionalization) 후 7일까지 생체활성 유지 (마이크로 단독 대비 30–50% 감소 방지); 기전은 친수성이 아닌 양전하 (Electropositivity) 지속.

## Summary
In vitro study showing that TiO2 nanonodules (300 nm) deposited on micropit titanium create a micro-nano-hybrid surface that sustains bioactivity for ≥7 days after UV photofunctionalization — in contrast to micropit-only surfaces which show 30-50% bioactivity decay. Key finding: the anti-aging mechanism is sustained electropositivity from TiO2 nanonodules, independent of hydrophilicity (hybrid surface became hydrorepellent at day 7 but retained bioactivity).

## Key Contributions
- Nano-topography as "anti-biological aging" surface design strategy
- Electropositivity (not hydrophilicity) = primary anti-aging mechanism
- TiO2 nanonodule electropositivity nullified by Cl⁻ → bioactivity lost — confirms mechanism

## Results
| Surface | Bioactivity at day 7 post-UV | Hydrophilicity at day 7 |
|---------|------------------------------|------------------------|
| Micropit only | 30-50% decay | Hydrophilic |
| Micro-nano-hybrid | Maintained (≈fresh) | Hydrorepellent |
