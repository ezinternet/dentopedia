## 한줄요약
전향적 임상연구(JCM 2020, n=70 임플란트, 7년): UV 광기능화 (Photofunctionalization) 15분 전처리 — 일반·복잡증례 100% 성공; 암절제 증례 22.2%; 복잡증례 임플란트 안정성 지수 (ISQ) +21.9 상승; 골질·부위·암절제가 실패 독립 예측인자.

## Summary
Prospective single-center 7-year study of UV-photofunctionalized implants (15-min pre-placement UV irradiation) in 16 patients (70 implants). Regular and anatomically complex (severely atrophied, requiring GBR/sinus augmentation) cases both achieved 100% success at 7 years. Cancer-resection cases showed 22.2% success. ISQ increased markedly in complex cases (+21.9 at second-stage surgery), confirming accelerated secondary osseointegration from UV-PF.

## Key Contributions
- Longest (7-year) prospective follow-up of UV-PF implants
- UV-PF overcomes anatomical compromise (severe atrophy) but NOT pathophysiological compromise (cancer resection/radiation)
- ISQ +21.9 in complex cases = quantified evidence of UV-PF facilitating secondary osseointegration

## Results
| Group | n | 7-yr success | ISQ change (placement → 2nd stage) |
|-------|---|-------------|-----------------------------------|
| Regular | 30 | **100%** | +3.2 |
| Complex (atrophied) | 21 | **100%** | **+21.9** |
| Cancer resection | 15 | **22.2%** | −3.5 |

Multivariate predictors of failure: bone quality (p<0.05), location (p<0.05), cancer resection (p<0.01).
