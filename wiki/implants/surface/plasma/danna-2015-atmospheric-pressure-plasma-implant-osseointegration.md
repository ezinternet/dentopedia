## One-line Summary

In vivo beagle study (7 dogs, paired radius, 3 & 6 wk): 80-s air-based APP significantly increased BIC for textured titanium at 6 weeks (P<0.001) but not at 3 weeks and not for CaP surfaces; BAFO showed no difference for either material at any timepoint (P=0.57).

## 한줄요약

비글견 in vivo(7마리, 요골 paired, 3·6주): 80초 공기 APP는 texture 티타늄 6주 BIC만 유의 증가(P<0.001), 3주·CaP는 무효; BAFO는 두 재료 전 시점 차이 없음(P=0.57).

## Summary

An early, instructive conditional result: plasma's osseointegration benefit appeared only for one surface (textured Ti) at one timepoint (6 wk), with the bone-area metric (BAFO) flatly negative. It establishes that "plasma helps" is contingent on surface chemistry and timing — not universal.

## Key Contributions

- Surface- and time-dependent APP benefit (textured Ti @ 6 wk only); CaP unaffected.
- XPS carbon reduction documented.

## Methodology

- 7 beagles; untreated vs 80-s air APP, textured Ti and CaP. 3 wk one radius, 6 wk contralateral. BIC, BAFO.

## Results

- XPS: ↓ carbon, ↑ Ti/O (Ti), ↑ Ca/O (CaP).
- BIC: significant for APP textured Ti at 6 wk (P<0.001); not 3 wk, not CaP.
- BAFO: NS for both materials, both timepoints (P=0.57).

**Boundary (living-document):** benefit limited to one surface/timepoint; BAFO null; animal model.
