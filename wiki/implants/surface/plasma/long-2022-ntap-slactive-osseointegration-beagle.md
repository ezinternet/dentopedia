## One-line Summary

In vitro + in vivo beagle study: non-thermal atmospheric plasma (NTAP)-functionalized titanium achieved a hydrophilic surface and early osseointegration comparable to commercial Straumann SLActive — positioning chairside NTAP as a cost-effective alternative free of SLActive's time-dependent biological aging.

## 한줄요약

비글견 in vitro+in vivo: NTAP 처리 티타늄이 친수성·초기 골유착에서 상용 Straumann SLActive에 필적 — 생물학적 노화가 없고 비용효율적인 chairside 대안 가능성.

## Summary

Frames plasma against the commercial gold-standard hydrophilic surface. NTAP matches SLActive's early osseointegration in dogs while avoiding SLActive's two practical drawbacks (cost, shelf-life biological aging). It's a non-inferiority argument, not superiority.

## Key Contributions

- Direct comparison NTAP vs SLActive (the commercial benchmark).
- Chairside, aging-free, cost-effective positioning.

## Methodology

- NTAP assessed in vitro (cells); in vivo early osseointegration in beagles vs SLActive.

## Results

- NTAP: hydrophilic surface, early osseointegration comparable to SLActive.

**Boundary (living-document):** animal/in vitro; comparable (not superior) to SLActive; no long-term/loaded human data.
