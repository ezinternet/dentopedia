## One-line Summary

Retrospective single-arm cohort (73 plasma-treated implants in 47 patients): chairside NTAP before placement was associated with progressive ISQ rise (78.97±5.52 → 83.74±4.36 at week 8, p<0.001) and no stability dip; gains greatest in low-initial-stability, mandibular, shorter, and higher-torque implants.

## 한줄요약

후향 단일군 코호트(47명, 73 임플란트): 식립 직전 chairside 비열 플라즈마 후 ISQ 78.97±5.52→83.74±4.36(8주, p<0.001)·stability dip 없음; 저ISQ·하악·짧은 fixture·높은 토크에서 이득 최대.

## Summary

Human, real-world, but uncontrolled. It shows a clean ISQ climb with no dip and a clinically intuitive subgroup gradient (the weakest sites gain most). The crucial caveat is the single-arm design: without a control, "no dip" cannot be causally attributed to plasma. Complements the controlled RCTs.

## Key Contributions

- Real-world human ISQ trajectory + subgroup gradient.

## Methodology

- 73 plasma-treated implants/47 patients (Jun 2023–Oct 2024), ACTILINK Reborn NTAP. ISQ baseline, weekly ×4, week 8. Subgroups by initial ISQ, jaw, size, torque.

## Results

- No stability dip; ISQ 78.97±5.52 → 83.74±4.36 (week 8, p<0.001).
- Greatest gains: low initial stability, mandible, shorter fixtures, higher insertion torque.

**Boundary (living-document):** no control group; retrospective; short-term ISQ only — needs RCT confirmation.
