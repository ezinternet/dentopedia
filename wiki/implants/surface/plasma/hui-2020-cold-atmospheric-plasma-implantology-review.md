## One-line Summary

Narrative review (Nanomaterials 2020) of CAP in implantology: improves surface wettability, removes hydrocarbon contamination, enhances osteoblast adhesion/osseointegration, and has antimicrobial effects against peri-implantitis pathogens — proposed as a chairside implant-surface activation tool.

## 한줄요약

서술적 리뷰(Nanomaterials 2020): CAP의 임플란트학 응용 — 젖음성 개선, 탄화수소 오염 제거, 골모세포 부착·골유착 향상, 임플란트주위염 병원균 항균. 의자옆(chairside) 표면 활성화 도구로 제안.

## Summary

Lays out CAP's dual clinical rationale that organizes this whole cluster: surface activation (→ osseointegration) and decontamination (→ peri-implantitis). Its key practical claim is chairside use — activating an implant surface in the operatory immediately before placement to reverse hydrocarbon "biological aging."

## Key Contributions

- Consolidates the surface-activation vs decontamination dual rationale.
- Emphasizes chairside, low-temperature applicability.

## Methodology

- Narrative literature review.

## Results

- Improves wettability; removes hydrocarbon contamination.
- Enhances osteoblast adhesion and osseointegration (preclinical).
- Antimicrobial against peri-implantitis-associated pathogens.
- Narrative — no pooled estimates.

**Boundary (living-document):** narrative; clinical data limited.
