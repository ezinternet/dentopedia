## Summary
Concise narrative review covering bone density classification systems (Misch D1–D4, Lekholm-Zarb), measurement by CBCT Hounsfield units, and the clinical implications for implant dentistry. Provides practical guidance for adapting surgical technique and loading protocols based on bone density: undersized drilling in D3/D4 bone, modified healing times, and appropriate implant design selection.

## Key Concepts
| Misch type | Description | HU range | Clinical approach |
|------------|-------------|----------|------------------|
| D1 | Dense cortical | >1250 | Standard; careful heat management |
| D2 | Thick cortical + coarse trabecular | 850–1250 | Standard |
| D3 | Thin cortical + fine trabecular | 350–850 | Undersized osteotomy; extended healing |
| D4 | Fine trabecular only | 150–350 | Significant modification required; OD beneficial |
