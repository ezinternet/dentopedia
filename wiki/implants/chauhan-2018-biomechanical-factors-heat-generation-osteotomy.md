## 한줄요약
SR(Indian J Dent Res 2018, 34편, 1992-2015): osteotomy 발열은 multifactorial — drill 디자인·재질·마모, drilling method, irrigation, 골질이 모두 독립적·상호작용적으로 작용. 단일 요인 통제만으로는 부족.

## Summary
A narrative systematic review of 34 articles (1992–2015) cataloguing the biomechanical determinants of osteotomy heat generation. The principal conclusion is that heat is governed by multiple interacting factors — drill geometry, material, wear, drilling method (intermittent/continuous/sequential), irrigation type and temperature, bone quality, speed, and load. No single variable dominates; clinical management must address several simultaneously.

## Key Contributions
Provides the canonical factor-list framework that subsequent SRs (chakraborty, saxena, jain, kosior, bento) refine. Pre-osseodensification era — captures the pre-2015 evidence base.

## Methodology
- PubMed + manual search; 1992–December 2015.
- 123 titles → 34 included after multistage screening.
- Narrative synthesis, no meta-analysis or quality scoring beyond inclusion.

## Results — Factor Categories
| Factor | Direction of effect | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Drill design (twisted flute, sharp cutting edges) | Better design ↓ heat | Strong qualitative |
| Drill material (SS vs ceramic vs coated) | Inconsistent | Mixed |
| Drill wear | Worn ↑ heat | Strong |
| Drilling method (intermittent > continuous) | Intermittent ↓ heat | Moderate |
| Irrigation (external/internal/temperature) | Cooled ↓ heat | Strong |
| Bone density (D1 > D4) | Dense ↑ heat | Moderate |
| Speed / load | Both modulate | Mixed |

## Clinical Implications
- 발열 관리는 multi-factor 접근 — irrigation 하나만으로 부족, drill 교체 + 디자인 + 간헐 drilling + 골밀도 고려 병행.
- D1 dense cortical bone에서 모든 절제 요인 강화 필요.
- 2015년 이전 문헌 기반 — osseodensification, 최신 piezo, guided surgery 연구는 미반영.
