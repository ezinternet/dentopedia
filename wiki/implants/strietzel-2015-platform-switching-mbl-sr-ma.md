## 한줄요약
SR+MA(22편: 15 RCT + 7 PCCS): PS MBL 0.49 mm vs PM 1.01 mm(p<0.0001); 플랫폼 스위칭이 변연골 소실 유의하게 감소; 이질성으로 신중한 해석 필요

## Summary
SR+MA (22 publications: 15 RCTs + 7 prospective controlled cohort studies) examining impact of platform switching on marginal bone levels. Meta-analysis of 13 RCTs: PS MBL 0.49 mm (CI 0.38–0.60) vs PM MBL 1.01 mm (CI 0.62–1.40), p<0.0001. Clear tendency favouring PS technique despite heterogeneity and unclear/high risk of bias in most included studies.

## Key Contributions
- 22 publications included (15 RCTs + 7 PCCS)
- Meta-analysis of 13 RCTs: PS MBL 0.49 mm vs PM 1.01 mm (p<0.0001)
- Qualitative: 13/15 RCTs and 3/7 PCCS show significantly less MBL for PS
- Clear tendency favouring PS confirmed statistically
- Limitation: unclear/high ROB in most studies; short follow-up periods
- Search covers 2005 to June 2013

## Methodology
SR+MA. Databases: PubMed, Web of Science, Journals@Ovid, Embase. Inclusion: RCTs and prospective controlled cohort studies comparing PS vs PM implant-abutment connections; published 2005–June 2013. Primary outcome: MBL changes. Meta-analysis: weighted mean difference; random-effects model. Quality assessment: ROB evaluation.

## Results
- 22 publications eligible; 13 RCTs in meta-analysis
- PS: weighted mean MBL 0.49 mm (95% CI 0.38–0.60)
- PM: weighted mean MBL 1.01 mm (95% CI 0.62–1.40)
- Difference: p<0.0001 favouring PS
- Qualitative: 13/15 RCTs favoured PS significantly
- Risk of bias: mostly unclear or high in included studies
