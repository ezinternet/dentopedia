## 한줄요약
체계적 고찰(Cureus 2024, 11편): 가이드 드릴링이 비가이드 대비 골내 온도 유의하게 더 상승 — 슬리브가 cooling saline 도달을 차단; 대부분 47°C 미만이지만 1500–2000 RPM 가이드 setting에선 괴사역치 초과 사례 존재. 차가운 saline(10°C)이 가장 일관된 완화책.

## Summary
Eleven studies (2010–2024) on guided vs. non-guided implant osteotomy temperature were synthesized. The recurring mechanism: metal/plastic guide sleeves obstruct external irrigation, raising peak bone temperatures. In most included studies, peak temperatures remained below the 47°C/1 min necrosis threshold, but at least one study (Barrak 2018) reported necrotic-threshold breaches at 1500 and 2000 RPM in guided settings. Modifiable mitigators identified: chilled irrigation (10°C consistently lowered peaks), drill diameter, drill length, drilling speed.

## Key Contributions
First focused systematic review on the guided-drilling heat penalty, separating mechanism (sleeve-irrigation interference) from mitigation (chilled saline, speed, geometry).

## Methodology
- PubMed, EBSCOhost, Google Scholar (2010–2024).
- PRISMA flow: 548 → 11 after duplicate/title/abstract/full-text/design exclusions.
- Data extracted: bone model, drill/RPM, diameter/depth, irrigation type/temperature, study conclusion.

## Results
| Variable | Guided vs. non-guided signal | Practical impact |
|---|---|---|
| Peak temperature | Guided ≥ non-guided across all studies | Routinely +0.5–5°C |
| 47°C threshold breach | Rare but documented (Barrak 2018) | High-speed guided drilling = risk |
| Chilled saline (10°C) | Consistently lowers peak | Strongest mitigation |
| Irrigation volume↑ | No consistent benefit | Volume alone insufficient |
| Drill diameter/length | Modulate heat | Bigger/longer = more friction |

## Clinical Implications
- 풀가이드(full-guided) surgery 시 냉장 saline(약 10°C) irrigation 우선 고려.
- 고속 회전(>1500 RPM)은 가이드 setting에서 위험성↑ — 800–1200 RPM 범위 + 충분한 cooling 권장.
- Sleeve가 막힐 수 있는 후방 구치부·깊은 osteotomy에서 간헐 drilling + 외부 추가 cooling 의식적으로 적용.
- D1 dense bone에서 효과 특히 중요 — 마찰열 절대량이 큼.
