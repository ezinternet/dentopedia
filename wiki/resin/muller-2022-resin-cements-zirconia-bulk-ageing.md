## Summary
This study evaluated adhesion of different resin cements to zirconia, testing the effects of incremental versus bulk build-up, use of a mould, and ageing (thermocycling). It informs both technique (build-up/mould) and material selection for cementing zirconia restorations; TheraCem showed high shear bond strength in dry and some aged conditions among the cements compared.

## Key Contributions
- Isolates application-technique variables (incremental vs bulk, mould) alongside material and ageing.
- Provides comparative zirconia-bonding ranking including TheraCem.

## Methodology
In-vitro adhesion of multiple resin cements to zirconia; factors: incremental vs bulk build-up, mould use, ageing (thermocycling); shear bond strength.

## Results
Build-up technique, mould use, and ageing all influence zirconia bond strength; TheraCem among the highest performers in dry/some aged conditions.
