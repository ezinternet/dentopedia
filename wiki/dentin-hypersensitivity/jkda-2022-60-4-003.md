## Mechanism
- **Hydrodynamic theory (Brännström)**: 노출된 상아질 세관 내 유체 이동 → odontoblast layer 자극 → 통증

## Treatment Options
1. **At-home**: desensitizing toothpaste (KNO3, NovaMin, arginine)
2. **In-office (occlusion)**: oxalate, glutaraldehyde, varnish
3. **In-office (sealing)**: laser (Nd:YAG, diode), resin sealant
4. **Restorative**: deep lesion → composite restoration
