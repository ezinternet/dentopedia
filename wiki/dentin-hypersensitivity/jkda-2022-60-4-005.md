## One-line Summary
Clinical strategy (Yonsei Univ. Dept. of Conservative Dentistry, Kim, JKDA 2022): prevention of hypersensitivity after Class II direct composite restoration — microleakage control, incremental filling, slow curing, and sectional matrix.

## 한줄요약
Clinical strategy (연세대 보존과 김도현, JKDA 2022): Class II direct composite 수복 후 hypersensitivity 예방 — microleakage 차단·incremental filling·slow curing·sectional matrix.

## Summary
DH 특집 3번. 수복 직후 hypersensitivity의 원인을 polymerization shrinkage에 의한 microleakage로 규정하고, 술식 단계별 예방 protocol 제시.

## Prevention Protocol

| 단계 | 권장 |
|---|---|
| Adhesive technique | Selective etching, adequate primer 적용 |
| Filling | Incremental (얇은 layer로 분할) |
| Curing | Slow-start (낮은 출력 시작 → 증가) |
| Proximal box | Pre-wedging, sectional matrix system, proper contact restoration |
