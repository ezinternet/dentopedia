## One-line Summary
SR+MA (28 studies, 27,927 older adults) - pooled oral frailty prevalence 32%; oral frailty associated with physical frailty (OR 1.67), malnutrition (OR 2.27), low dietary variety (OR 1.98), social withdrawal (OR 1.42).

## 한줄요약
SR+MA(28편, 27,927명) - oral frailty 통합 유병률 32%; 신체쇠약(OR 1.67)·영양실조(OR 2.27)·식이다양성 저하(OR 1.98)·사회적 위축(OR 1.42)과 연관.

## Summary
oral frailty 유병률(32%)과 불량결과 위험(OR)을 정량화한 SR+MA. [[geriatric-dentistry/dibello-2022-oral-frailty-indicators-adverse-health-outcomes]]의 개념을 정량 근거로 보강.
