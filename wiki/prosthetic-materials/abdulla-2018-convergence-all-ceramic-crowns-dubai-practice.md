## 한줄요약
cross-sectional (J Clin Exp Dent 2018;10(12):e1192-7, MBRU Dubai, 154 dentist × 206 prep): 두바이 개원의 all-ceramic bonded crown prep 평균 수렴각 MD 24.6° (SD 11.8) / BL 32.6° — Shillingburg 이상치 6°와 Tiu 2015 SR의 현실치 10-22° 둘 다 초과; 실제 임상은 교과서·SR 권장보다 훨씬 더 over-tapered.

## Summary
Cross-sectional clinical practice survey (Journal of Clinical and Experimental Dentistry December 2018) of all-ceramic bonded crown preparations from Dubai private practitioners. 206 preparations from 154 dentists scanned via Carestream CS 3500 and measured digitally.

## Key Contributions
- **Real-world TOC documentation** from a private practice cohort — rare empirical data
- BL > MD pattern confirmed in clinical setting (matches preclinical literature)
- Highlights persistent **textbook-to-practice gap**: 6° ideal → ~25-33° reality

## Methodology
- Convenience sample, 154 dentists
- 206 all-ceramic crown preparations (72 anterior, 134 posterior)
- Cast scanning via Carestream CS 3500 intraoral scanner
- Digital image assessment, axial wall taper measurement
- MD and BL convergence calculated separately

## Results

| Direction | Mean | SD |
|---|---|---|
| Mesio-distal (MD) | 24.6° | 11.8° |
| Bucco-lingual (BL) | 32.6° | ~11.8° |

- Both substantially exceed:
  - Shillingburg 6° ideal (4-5× over)
  - Tiu 2015 SR realistic 10-22° range
- BL > MD direction pattern consistent with literature

## Clinical Implications [근거강함 for the numbers; claude해석 for application]
- The 6° taper is essentially a fiction in clinical practice. Updating patient/staff communication and grading rubrics to reflect 20-30° empirical reality
- All-ceramic bonded crowns can tolerate higher TOC than cement-retained (adhesive retention compensates) — possibly why these clinicians don't crown-failure-feedback themselves into tighter taper
- Korean private practice TOC values not directly measured here — extrapolation suggests similar gap (data needed for Korean validation)
- BL > MD: anatomic + access constraint, especially for posterior teeth — accept and design accordingly

## Limitations
- Convenience sample (not random)
- Single geographic context (Dubai)
- No outcome correlation — high TOC may or may not predict crown failure given adhesive cementation
