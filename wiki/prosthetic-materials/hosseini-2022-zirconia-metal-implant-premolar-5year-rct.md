## One-line Summary
5-year RCT (n=50) comparing zirconia-based vs metal-based abutment+crown for premolar implant-supported restorations: survival (96% vs 96%) and marginal bone loss (~-1.1mm) comparable, with zero chipping and zero abutment fractures in the zirconia group.

## Summary
5-year RCT comparing zirconia-based abutment+crown vs metal-based abutment+ceramic crown for premolar implant-supported restorations.

## Key Contributions
- 5-year survival: zirconia 96% vs metal-based 96% — no significant difference
- MBL comparable between groups (-1.1mm at 5 years)
- Zirconia group: zero chipping events vs one in metal-ceramic group

## Methodology
RCT; n=50; 5-year follow-up; University of Copenhagen.

## Results
Zirconia-based restorations non-inferior to metal-based for premolar implant crowns at 5 years. Abutment fractures: zero in zirconia group.
