## Summary
A comprehensive systematic review and meta-analysis of 64 studies covering 11,560 tooth-supported single crowns (SCs), comparing survival and complication rates across all major crown materials and designs. **Monolithic lithium-disilicate** achieved the highest 5-year survival rate at 98.5%, statistically comparable to veneered densely-sintered zirconia (97.3%) and metal-ceramic (97.1%). Monolithic zirconia (96.8%) also performed well. Older all-ceramic materials (feldspathic, glass-infiltrated and densely-sintered alumina) showed significantly lower survival. Monolithic designs — both lithium-disilicate and zirconia — showed significantly fewer ceramic fractures and chipping than veneered alternatives.

## Key Contributions
- Most comprehensive up-to-date SR+MA comparing all crown material/design types in a single analysis
- Establishes monolithic lithium-disilicate as the top-performing material for 5-year tooth-supported crown survival
- Statistically ranks materials, providing p-values vs the best-performing reference (monolithic lithium-disilicate)
- Demonstrates that monolithic design is the key driver of reduced chipping regardless of material
- Shows anterior/posterior performance is equivalent for all material types except feldspathic and metal-ceramic

## Methodology
- **Design**: SR + MA
- **Search**: MEDLINE (PubMed), Embase, CENTRAL; 2014–2024; plus 30 studies from prior reviews
- **Inclusion**: Clinical studies on tooth-supported FDPs; ≥3 years mean follow-up
- **Studies**: 64 (12 metal-ceramic, 49 all-ceramic, 3 both)
- **Sample**: 3,509 metal-ceramic + 8,051 all-ceramic = ~11,560 SCs
- **Statistics**: Robust Poisson regression for pooled 5-year survival estimates; p-value comparisons vs monolithic lithium-disilicate reference

## Results
| Material/Design | 5-Year Survival |
|---|---|
| Monolithic lithium-disilicate | **98.5%** |
| Veneered densely-sintered zirconia | 97.3% |
| Metal-ceramic | 97.1% |
| Monolithic densely-sintered zirconia | 96.8% |
| Veneered leucite/lithium-disilicate | 95.7%* |
| Densely-sintered alumina | 94.5%* |
| Glass-infiltrated alumina | 94.3%* |
| Feldspathic/silica-based | 90.4%*** |

*Significantly lower than monolithic lithium-disilicate (p<0.05)
***p<0.0001

**Chipping/fracture:** Monolithic lithium-disilicate and monolithic zirconia → significantly fewer ceramic fractures vs veneered alternatives

**Regional performance:** Similar in anterior and posterior for all material types except feldspathic/silica and metal-ceramic
