## One-line Summary
SR+MA of RCTs comparing monolithic zirconia vs metal-ceramic posterior implant-supported single crowns: equivalent survival (RR 1.0) and marginal bone loss (MD -0.05mm), with significantly fewer technical complications for monolithic zirconia (OR 0.31, mainly chipping).

## Summary
SR+MA of RCTs comparing monolithic zirconia vs metal-ceramic for posterior implant-supported single crowns.

## Key Contributions
- Technical complication OR: 0.31 (MZ significantly fewer complications, mainly chipping)
- Survival rate RR: 1.0 (no difference)
- MBL MD: -0.05mm (clinically equivalent)

## Methodology
PubMed/Embase/Cochrane; RCT only; meta-analysis.

## Results
Monolithic zirconia posterior implant crowns: equivalent survival and MBL, with significantly fewer technical complications (chipping) vs metal-ceramic. Valid clinical alternative confirmed.
