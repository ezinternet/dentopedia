## One-line Summary
In vitro study (Ivorine short molar, 22° TOC × 3.0 mm height × chamfer; 3 groups, n=10 each) of two rescue strategies for overtapered short molars: both proximal grooves (156.75 N mean dislodgement force) and cervical-only TOC reduction (22°→4° in cervical 1.5 mm) significantly restored resistance form versus control.

## 한줄요약

## Summary
In vitro study (Journal of Prosthetic Dentistry November 2011) testing two practical auxiliary features for compromised short molar preparations. Ivorine tooth substrate, milled to 22° TOC + 3.0 mm height + chamfer finish line. Three configurations tested against ANOVA.

## Key Contributions
- Quantifies two distinct rescue strategies for overtapered short molars
- **Proximal grooves** (Group Grv) — 156.75 N mean dislodgement force
- **Cervical-only TOC reduction** (Group Rdc, 22°→4° in cervical 1.5 mm) — also significantly higher than Ctrl
- Both options recover resistance form despite poor base prep

## Methodology
- Single Ivorine tooth milled with controlled geometry: 22° TOC, 3.0 mm occlusocervical height, chamfer finish line
- 3 groups (n=10 each):
  - Ctrl — 22° TOC baseline
  - Grv — Ctrl + proximal grooves
  - Rdc — TOC reduced 22°→4° in cervical 1.5 mm
- Co-Cr copings cast, zinc phosphate cement
- 45° buccal-to-lingual load via universal testing machine
- One-way ANOVA + Bonferroni post-hoc, α = .05

## Results
- Proximal grooves (Grv): 156.75 N mean dislodgement force, significantly > Ctrl
- Cervical TOC reduction (Rdc): also significantly > Ctrl
- Both auxiliary strategies restore resistance form

## Clinical Implications [claude해석]
- For short molar preps where TOC inevitably exceeds 22°, **proximal grooves** are the simpler clinical add-on — drill MD or DM proximal groove with cylindrical bur
- **Cervical-only TOC reduction** requires more precise instrumentation but preserves occlusal aspect for retention while improving cervical fit
- Both options should be in routine clinical toolkit for short molar / overprepared cases
- 22° TOC + 3.0 mm height is the explicit failure-prone configuration documented — clinical recognition is the first defense
- For Korean clinics: 짧은 대구치 prep 시 인접면 groove 추가가 가장 실용적 retention/resistance recovery
