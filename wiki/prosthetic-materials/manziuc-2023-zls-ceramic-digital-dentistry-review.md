## Summary
Comprehensive literature review of Zirconia-Reinforced Lithium Silicate (ZLS) ceramic — a hybrid material combining lithium metasilicate (Li₂SiO₃) glass-ceramic matrix with 8–12% ZrO₂. Commercial products: Vita Suprinity PC and Celtra Duo (CAD/CAM milling); Vita Ambria and Celtra Press (heat-press). Mechanical hierarchy: ZLS fracture toughness, flexural strength, elastic modulus, and hardness are higher than feldspathic, lithium disilicate, hybrid, and resin nanoceramics — but lower than translucent/high-translucency zirconia. Optical translucency reduced vs lithium disilicate due to tetragonal zirconia content. Standard bonding: HF etching, air abrasion, or laser etching followed by silane and resin cement.

## Key Contributions
- Consolidated reference for ZLS chemistry, microstructure, optics, mechanics, and cementation
- Maps commercial products (Vita Suprinity, Celtra) to fabrication mode
- Mechanical positioning: between lithium disilicate and zirconia
- Identifies aesthetic limitation: translucency reduced vs pure lithium disilicate

## Methodology
- **Design**: Comprehensive narrative literature review
- **Databases**: PubMed, Web of Science, Cochrane Library
- **Inclusion**: English clinical + in vitro on ZLS color, mechanical behavior, cementation
- **Exclusion**: abstracts, questionnaires, case reports, narrative reviews, non-English

## Results
**Composition**: Lithium metasilicate (Li₂SiO₃) glass-ceramic + 8–12% ZrO₂ → fine-grained Li₂O-ZrO₂-SiO₂ after crystallization.

**Mechanical hierarchy (relative)**:
- ZLS > feldspathic, lithium disilicate, hybrid ceramic, resin nanoceramic
- ZLS < translucent zirconia, high-translucency zirconia

**Optical**:
- ↓ translucency vs lithium disilicate (zirconia content)
- Final shade depends on: material thickness, underlying tooth color, cement shade

**Cementation**:
- Surface: HF etching, air abrasion, or laser etching (Er:YAG / Er,Cr:YSGG)
- Silane application before resin cement
- Adhesive resin cementation preferred

**Commercial product map**:
| Product | Manufacturer | Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Vita Suprinity PC | Vita Zahnfabrik | CAD/CAM milling (pre-crystallized) |
| Celtra Duo | Dentsply Sirona | CAD/CAM milling |
| Vita Ambria | Vita Zahnfabrik | Heat-press |
| Celtra Press | Dentsply Sirona | Heat-press |

## Clinical Implications
- ZLS occupies the mechanical "middle ground": stronger than lithium disilicate, less than zirconia
- For thin/translucent monolithic anterior restorations where lithium disilicate strength may be insufficient
- Avoid for high-load posterior single crowns where translucent zirconia is preferred
- Standard bonding protocol (HF etch + silane + resin cement) applies
