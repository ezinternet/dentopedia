## 한줄요약
치아 지지 단관 SR+MA — 5년 생존율 금속도재 96.2%, veneer형 94.7%, 단일층 지르코니아 97.1%; 지르코니아 칩핑 최소.

## Summary
SR+MA comparing survival, failure, and complication rates of metal-ceramic, veneered all-ceramic, and monolithic all-ceramic tooth-supported single crowns.

## Key Contributions
- 5-year survival: MC 96.2%, veneered all-ceramic 94.7%, monolithic zirconia 97.1%
- Chipping complications: veneered all-ceramic highest; monolithic zirconia lowest
- Monolithic zirconia recommended where esthetic requirements permit

## Methodology
PRISMA SR+MA; clinical studies with minimum 3-year follow-up.

## Results
Monolithic zirconia: highest survival + lowest technical complications. Veneered ceramics acceptable but chipping remains concern. Metal-ceramic remains reliable standard.
