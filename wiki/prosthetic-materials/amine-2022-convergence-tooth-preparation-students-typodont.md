## 한줄요약
in-vitro/preclinical (Int J Dent 2022:7615892, Casablanca, 35명×4 prep=140편): 3학년 학생 단일 크라운 prep TOC — 타이포돈트 11.99° ± 4.48, 시뮬레이터 11.31° ± 4.16 — 모두 권장 6-16° 범위 내; BL 약간 > MD; 6° 이상적 taper는 학생 환경에서도 거의 달성 불가.

## Summary
Preclinical evaluation (International Journal of Dentistry 2022) of third-year dental student preparation skill. 35 students × 4 preparations each = 140 preps on typodont and simulator scanned with IDENTICA HYBRID optical scanner.

## Key Contributions
- Direct student-skill TOC measurement on both typodont and simulator platforms
- Both platforms within the 6-16° clinically achievable band
- Confirms BL > MD convergence pattern even in preclinical setting

## Methodology
- 35 third-year students, Faculty of Dental Medicine Casablanca (FDMC)
- 4 prepared crown forms × 35 students = 140 preparations
- Cast crowns and metal-ceramic prep types
- Scanner: IDENTICA HYBRID (optical)
- STL processing: 3D-TOOL-FREE software
- MD and BL convergence measured via MB-RULER
- Statistics: Student's t-test, SPSS

## Results

| Platform | Overall (Mean ± SD) | MD direction | BL direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| Typodont | 11.99° ± 4.48 | 11.40° ± 5.09 | 12.58° ± 4.74 |
| Simulator | 11.31° ± 4.16 | 10.81° ± 4.29 | 11.80° ± 5.44 |

- Simulator marginally lower than typodont (NS likely, not reported as significant)
- BL > MD consistent with anatomy + access constraints
- Majority of preps fall within 6-16° recommended range

## Clinical/Educational Implications [claude해석]
- Preclinical TOC values match Marghalani (King Abdulaziz) and Tiu et al. (Otago) findings — generalizable across institutions
- Typodont vs simulator results are essentially equivalent — no compelling reason to prefer one over the other for TOC training
- The textbook 6° ideal is unattainable even in controlled preclinical setting — curricular grading should reflect this
- For Korean dental schools: scanning + STL measurement is an objective grading tool worth adopting; eliminates inter-instructor visual estimation bias
