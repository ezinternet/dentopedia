## One-line Summary

Prospective multicenter RCT (64 implants, 12 months, Pusan National University + Yonsei, Korea) comparing BSRP vs SCRP: 100% survival both groups, no significant MBL or pocket depth difference; BSRP had more abutment loosening while SCRP had more screw fractures.

## 한줄요약

전향적 다기관 RCT(64개 임플란트, 12개월, 부산대+연세대)에서 볼-스프링 유지 보철 (Ball-and-Spring-Retained Prosthesis, BSRP)과 나사-시멘트 혼합유지 보철 (Screw-and-Cement-Retained Prosthesis, SCRP) 모두 100% 생존율이었으며, 변연골흡수 (Marginal Bone Loss, MBL)·탐침 깊이 차이 없음; BSRP 어버트먼트 풀림↑, SCRP 나사 파절↑.

## Summary

A two-center (Pusan National University Dental Hospital + Yonsei University Dental Hospital) randomized controlled trial prospectively compared the detachable BSRP system (zirconia balls + nitinol spring) to the established SCRP system over 12 months. Among 64 implants in 64 patients, both groups achieved 100% cumulative survival rate with no functional mobility observed. MBL was significantly higher in SCRP at baseline delivery (p=0.043) but no significant differences were found at any follow-up time point. Probing depth remained ≤3.0mm in all cases (classified as mild). Oral hygiene was similarly maintained. The main complication in BSRP was abutment screw loosening (11 events); SCRP showed screw fractures (2 events) and fewer food debris impactions. The authors concluded that BSRP is a viable clinical alternative to SCRP, pending long-term evidence.

## Key Contributions

- 한국 최초 BSRP vs SCRP 무작위 전향적 다기관 RCT
- BSRP의 핵심 장점: 어버트먼트 분리 없이 보철 탈착 → 점막 장벽 보호, 나사 변형 방지
- 1년 관찰 동등 생존율 확인
- BSRP 어버트먼트 호환성 문제(다제조사 Morse taper)가 나사 풀림 원인 지목

## Methodology

- **Design**: 무작위 배정, 단일 맹검, 전향적 다기관 RCT
- **n**: 64명 (남31, 여33), 64개 임플란트
- **기간**: 2018–2020 보철 장착
- **BSRP**: EZ crown (Samwon DMP, 양산); 지르코니아 볼 3개 + 니티놀 스프링 캡
- **SCRP**: Transfer Abutment (Osstem Implant, 서울)
- **Randomization**: computer-generated blocked randomization
- **Follow-up**: baseline (보철 장착일), 3·6·12개월
- **주요 평가**: 생존율, MBL (portable X-ray, 평행촬영), PD (6점법), mPI, mBI, 합병증
- **통계**: independent t-test (MBL·PD), chi-square (mPI·mBI); IBM SPSS v25

|------|-------------|-------------|---|

**합병증 요약**

| 합병증 | BSRP | SCRP |
|--------|------|------|
| 나사 풀림 | 11건 | 0 |
| 나사 파절 | 1건 | 2건 |
| 음식 잔류 | 4건 | 2건 |
