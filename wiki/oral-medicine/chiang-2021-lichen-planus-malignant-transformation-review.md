## 한줄요약
OLP 악성전환 위험인자·추적 프로토콜 고찰 — 6개월 주기 임상+조직학적 평가 권고.

## Summary
Review of OLP malignant transformation focusing on risk predictors and clinical surveillance protocols.

## Key Contributions
- Erosive/atrophic OLP subtype highest transformation risk
- 6-month surveillance with clinical and histological evaluation recommended
- Tobacco/alcohol co-exposure amplifies risk

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
MTR approximately 1% with strict criteria. Regular surveillance critical for early detection.
