## 한줄요약
최근 5년 OLK 악성전환 SR+MA — pooled rate 3.5%, 비균질형 OR 4.1.

## Summary
SR+MA of 2015–2020 OLK studies quantifying malignant transformation rate and risk predictors.

## Key Contributions
- Pooled MTR 3.5% (95%CI 2.2–5.1%) from 32 studies
- Non-homogeneous OLK: OR 4.1 vs homogeneous
- Excision does not eliminate risk; long-term surveillance mandatory

## Methodology
PubMed/Embase/Cochrane 2015–2020; GRADE evidence appraisal.

## Results
32 studies included. Pooled MTR 3.5%. Non-homogeneous OLK OR 4.1. Median follow-up 6 years.
