## 한줄요약
RAS 임상양상·병인론(면역·유전·영양·스트레스)·치료 단계적 접근 고찰.

## Summary
Review covering RAS epidemiology (~20% adults), pathogenesis, trigger factors, and stepwise treatment algorithm.

## Key Contributions
- ~20% adult prevalence; family history common; onset in childhood/adolescence
- Triggers: trauma, stress, iron/B12/folate deficiency, gluten sensitivity
- Step-up treatment: topical → systemic for frequent/major ulcers

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
RAS is self-limiting but recurrent; quality of life impact significant. Nutritional correction (iron, B12, folate) resolves subset.
