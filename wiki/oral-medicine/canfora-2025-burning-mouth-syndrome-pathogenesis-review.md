## 한줄요약
BMS 병인론 최신 업데이트 + ICD-11 기반 진단 알고리즘 — 신경병증·심리·호르몬 통합 모델.

## Summary
Updated review of BMS pathogenesis integrating peripheral/central neuropathy, hormonal (estrogen decline), and psychological factors; ICD-11 diagnostic algorithm proposed.

## Key Contributions
- Small fiber neuropathy as peripheral mechanism of BMS
- Central sensitization: altered pain processing in trigeminal pathways
- Diagnostic algorithm: primary (no cause) vs secondary (treatable cause) BMS

## Methodology
PubMed narrative review with diagnostic algorithm proposal.

## Results
BMS etiology multifactorial: neuropathic + hormonal + psychological. Primary BMS: diagnosis of exclusion. Secondary: treat underlying cause first.
