## 한줄요약
BMS 포괄적 관리 전략 — clonazepam 국소 1차 치료, CBT, 저용량 TCA 다학제 접근.

## Summary
Comprehensive management review for BMS covering pharmacological (topical clonazepam, TCAs, gabapentinoids), psychological (CBT), and multimodal approaches.

## Key Contributions
- Topical clonazepam rinse-and-spit: strongest RCT evidence for pain reduction
- CBT reduces psychological distress and catastrophizing in BMS
- Low-dose TCA (amitriptyline) and gabapentinoids for neuropathic component

## Methodology
PubMed systematic review base with narrative synthesis.

## Results
No single cure for BMS. Combination approach most effective. Topical clonazepam highest evidence (multiple RCTs).
