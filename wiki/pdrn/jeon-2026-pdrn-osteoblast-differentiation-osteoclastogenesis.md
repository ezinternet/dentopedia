## One-line Summary

In vitro study (MC3T3-E1 + primary BMM co-parallel model) demonstrating PDRN selectively promotes osteoblast differentiation (Runx2↑, ALP↑, mineralization↑) at 10–20 mcg/mL without any direct effect on osteoclast precursor activity or resorption, establishing its profile as a selective anabolic bone agent.

## 한줄요약

MC3T3-E1 오스테오블라스트와 골수유래 대식세포(BMM) 오스테오클라스트를 이용한 시험관 연구에서 PDRN(10–20 mcg/mL)이 오스테오블라스트 분화(Runx2↑, ALP↑, 광화↑)를 선택적으로 촉진하고 오스테오클라스트 생성 및 기능에는 직접적 영향을 주지 않음을 증명.

## Summary

PDRN (폴리뉴클레오티드, Polydeoxyribonucleotide) is a salmon DNA-derived nucleotide polymer with established tissue-regenerative properties, but its direct cellular mechanisms in bone remodeling were unclear prior to this study.

Jeon et al. (2026) tested PDRN simultaneously on two parallel cell models — MC3T3-E1 pre-osteoblasts and primary bone marrow-derived macrophages (BMM, osteoclast precursors) — using matched concentrations (10 and 20 mcg/mL). The study found a clear selective effect: PDRN significantly promoted osteoblast differentiation while showing zero effect on osteoclast formation or function.

The critical finding is the **selectivity**: PDRN does not act by suppressing osteoclasts (as bisphosphonates or denosumab do), but by directly upregulating osteoblastogenesis. This distinction matters clinically because an agent that only builds bone without impairing bone resorption may carry a different safety profile and different coupling effects on long-term bone turnover.

## Key Contributions

- Establishes PDRN as a **selective anabolic bone agent**: osteoblast-promoting without osteoclast-suppressing
- First dual-model study testing PDRN on matched osteoblast + osteoclast populations
- Proposes A2A purinergic receptor → Wnt/β-catenin → Runx2 signaling axis as the mechanistic pathway
- Runx2 protein and mRNA significantly upregulated at 20 mcg/mL
- ALP activity (Day 7) and mineralized matrix (Alizarin Red S, Day 28) significantly enhanced
- Osteoclast metrics: TRAP staining, resorption pit formation, BMM metabolic activity — ALL non-significant vs control
- Transient NFATc1 upregulation in osteoclasts at Day 1 did not translate to sustained osteoclastogenesis

## Methodology

- **Cell lines**: MC3T3-E1 (mouse pre-osteoblasts); primary BMM from mice (osteoclast precursors)
- **PDRN concentrations**: 10, 20 mcg/mL
- **Assays**: MTS (viability), qRT-PCR (Runx2, osteocalcin mRNA), Western blot (Runx2 protein), ALP, Alizarin Red S, TRAP, pit formation
- **Differentiation timeline**: 7-day ALP, 28-day mineralization; TRAP evaluated at standard osteoclast differentiation timepoints

## Results
