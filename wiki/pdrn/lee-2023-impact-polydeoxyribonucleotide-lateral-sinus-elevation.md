## 한줄요약

Beagle 4마리 lateral sinus floor elevation 모델에서 PDRN-soaked synthetic graft가 Schneiderian membrane 근접 apical zone에서 new bone area·BIC apical을 유의하게 향상. Coronal/middle zone은 효과 없음.

## Summary

This split-sinus study in four beagles evaluated whether adding polydeoxyribonucleotide (PDRN) to a collagenated synthetic bone graft improves histologic outcomes after lateral sinus floor elevation with simultaneous implant placement. After 8 weeks, the PDRN group showed significantly greater bone-to-implant contact in the apical zone (BIC_a apical) and significantly different histomorphometric area proportions (new bone, residual particle, fibrovascular tissue) in the apical area of interest. Coronal and middle zones did not differ between groups. The osteoinductive effect of PDRN was therefore localized to the **zone farthest from pristine bone** — the region where new bone formation is hardest.

## Key Contributions

- Localizes PDRN's preclinical effect to the **apical, membrane-adjacent zone** — historically the lowest-yield region for new bone formation.
- Reports a meaningful BIC_a apical difference (PDRN 76.7 ± 9.3% vs. control 55.6 ± 22.1%, P = 0.038).
- Negative finding in coronal/middle zones suggests PDRN does not uniformly amplify all augmentation outcomes — it acts where the baseline is poorest.

## Methodology

- 4 mongrel-style beagle dogs.
- Bimaxillary premolar (P2/P3/P4) extraction 2 months pre-surgery.
- Lateral sinus floor elevation, split-sinus randomization to PDRN vs. control.
- Both arms received collagenated synthetic bone graft; PDRN arm soaked the graft in PDRN.
- 8-week histomorphometry — linear measures (RBH, AH, PH, BIC_p, BIC_a total/coronal/middle/apical) and area proportions (pNB, pRBP, pFVT) across three AOIs.

## Results

- BIC_a apical: PDRN 76.7 ± 9.3% vs. control 55.6 ± 22.1%, P = 0.038.
- AOI_A pNB / pRBP / pFVT: P = 0.038 / 0.028 / 0.007.
- AOI_C and AOI_M: no significant differences.
- Linear measures (AH, PH, BIC_p, BIC_a total, coronal, middle): no significant differences.
