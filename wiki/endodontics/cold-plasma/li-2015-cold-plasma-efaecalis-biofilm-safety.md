## One-line Summary

In vitro study showing AC argon/oxygen cold plasma completely eradicates 3-week mature E. faecalis root canal biofilm in 12 minutes — outperforming 1-week Ca(OH)₂ and chlorhexidine — with no significant dentin microhardness or roughness change.

## 한줄요약

## Summary

This in vitro study from Peking University (Li et al., 2015) evaluated atmospheric pressure AC argon/oxygen cold plasma as an endodontic disinfectant against fully matured 3-week E. faecalis biofilms in extracted human root canals. Cold plasma achieved complete sterilization (0 CFU) at 12 minutes, confirmed by both SEM and CLSM. This was superior in both efficacy and speed to conventional intracanal medicaments (Ca(OH)₂, 2% CHX gel, and their combination) tested over 1 week. Crucially, Vickers microhardness and 3D laser profilometry showed no significant dentin structural change after plasma treatment, establishing mechanical safety. The bactericidal mechanism is attributed to plasma-generated reactive oxygen species (ROS).

## Key Contributions

1. **Complete 3-week biofilm eradication in 12 min** — first AC Ar/O₂ plasma demonstration against fully matured clinical-analog E. faecalis root canal biofilm
2. **Superior to conventional medicaments** — Ca(OH)₂, CHX gel, and combination each failed to achieve complete eradication even after 1 week
3. **Mechanical safety confirmed** — no significant dentin microhardness (Vickers) or surface roughness (3D profilometry) change
4. **ROS mechanism** — plasma-generated reactive oxygen species identified as primary bactericidal agent
5. **Dual morphologic validation** — SEM + CLSM confirming complete biofilm structural disruption at 12 min

## Methodology

- **Biofilm model**: 3-week E. faecalis biofilm in extracted human single-root canal teeth
- **Plasma device**: AC argon/oxygen (Ar/O₂) atmospheric cold plasma jet (low temperature)
- **Treatment groups**: Plasma (3, 6, 9, 12 min) vs Ca(OH)₂ (1 wk) vs 2% CHX gel (1 wk) vs Ca(OH)₂+CHX (1 wk) vs saline control
- **Efficacy endpoints**: CFU quantification, SEM morphology, CLSM live/dead staining
- **Safety endpoints**: Vickers hardness, 3D laser surface roughness (Ra)

## Results

| Treatment | Duration | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Cold plasma | 12 min | 0 CFU — complete sterilization |
| Cold plasma | 3–9 min | Progressive log reduction, incomplete |
| Ca(OH)₂ | 1 week | Incomplete; residual viable bacteria |
| 2% CHX gel | 1 week | Incomplete; better than Ca(OH)₂ alone |
| Ca(OH)₂ + CHX | 1 week | Incomplete; inferior to 12-min plasma |
| Dentin microhardness | — | No significant change (p > 0.05) |
| Dentin roughness (Ra) | — | No significant change (p > 0.05) |

SEM and CLSM at 12 min confirmed total biofilm disruption and 100% cell death by fluorescent live/dead assay.
