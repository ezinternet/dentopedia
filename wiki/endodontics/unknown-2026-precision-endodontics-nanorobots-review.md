## 한줄요약
나노로봇 근관치료 미래 고찰 — 자기유도 나노입자 바이오필름 파괴, 광열 항균, 조직재생 전략.

## Summary
Preclinical and conceptual review of nanorobot applications in endodontics: targeted biofilm disruption, photothermal antimicrobial action, and tissue regeneration.

## Key Contributions
- Magnetically guided nanoparticles: targeted disruption of root canal biofilm
- Photothermal nanorobots: localized antimicrobial heating without systemic effects
- Nanoparticle-based scaffold delivery for pulp-dentinal regeneration

## Methodology
Narrative review; preclinical and conceptual literature.

## Results
Nanorobot endodontics: preclinical stage only. Theoretical advantages: access to anatomically complex areas, localized high-concentration drug delivery. Clinical translation requires safety/regulatory pathway.
