## 한줄요약
SR+MA (29개 이란인 연구): MB2 유병률 상악 제1대구치 60%, 제2대구치 33% — 현미경으로 최고 (78%/61%), 치근단 방사선으로 최저 (15%/4%); I²=97% 매우 높음.

## Summary
Systematic review and meta-analysis of 29 studies on 제2근심협측 근관 (MB2) prevalence in 이란인 (Iranian population). PROSPERO-registered (CRD42024542862). MB2 prevalence: 60% (95% CI 53–67%) in 상악 제1대구치 and 33% (95% CI 25–42%) in 상악 제2대구치. Detection rates vary dramatically by method: 현미경 (microscope) highest (78%/61%), 치근단 방사선 (periapical radiograph) lowest (15%/4%). Heterogeneity extremely high (I²=97%) in all subgroups, driven primarily by detection method differences.

## Key Contributions
- Population-specific reference: Iranian MB2 prevalence benchmarks
- Quantifies detection method gap: microscope vs X-ray for MB2 (5× difference)
- First SR+MA including Persian-language literature (Magiran, SID, IranMedex)

## Methodology
- PRISMA SR+MA; 6 databases; through May 2024; JBI quality appraisal; STATA18; inverse variance method
