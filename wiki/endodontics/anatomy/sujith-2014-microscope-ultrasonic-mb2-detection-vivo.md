## 한줄요약
In vivo 60명(상악 제1대구치): MB2 발견율 — 육안 20% → DOM 55% → DOM+초음파 70%; 단계적 프로토콜 통계적 유의(p<0.001)

## Summary
In vivo study (60 patients, maxillary first molar RCT) evaluating sequential escalation of MB2 detection tools. Each step — naked eye explorer → dental operating microscope (DOM) → DOM + ultrasonic trough — significantly increases detection. Overall detection rate 70% with full protocol.

## Detection Results (Cumulative)

| Method | MB2 Detected | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Naked eye (explorer) | 12 / 60 | **20%** |
| + DOM | 33 / 60 | **55%** |
| + DOM + Ultrasonic trough | 42 / 60 | **70%** |

- p<0.001 (ROC curve analysis across methods)

## Technique: Ultrasonic Trough
- 3-mm groove cut ultrasonically from MB1 orifice toward palatal canal
- Removes dentin bridges hiding MB2 orifice
- Examined under DOM magnification throughout
