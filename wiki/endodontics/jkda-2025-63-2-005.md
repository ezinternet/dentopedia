## One-line Summary
Review (Song MJ, NHIS Ilsan Hospital, JKDA 2025): VPT for immature permanent teeth — framework for overcoming Ca(OH)₂ limitations using MTA, Biodentine, and BioRoot (HCSC) + Korean application of AAPD guidelines.

## 한줄요약
Review (NHIS 일산병원 보존과 송민주, JKDA 2025): 미성숙 영구치 VPT — Ca(OH)2 한계를 MTA·Biodentine·BioRoot (HCSC)로 극복하는 framework + AAPD 가이드라인 한국 적용.

## Summary
미성숙 영구치에서 vital pulp 보존이 치근 발육·apex closure에 핵심. 전통적 Ca(OH)2의 prognosis 한계를 hydraulic calcium silicate cement (HCSC) 신재료로 극복하는 contemporary approach.

## Treatment Decision

| 임상 상황 | 시술 | 재료 |
|---|---|---|
| Pulpal exposure, no symptoms | Direct pulp capping | MTA, Biodentine |
| Pulpitis (reversible), partial | Partial pulpotomy (Cvek) | MTA, Biodentine |
| Pulpitis 진행, immature | Full pulpotomy | MTA, Biodentine |
| Pulp necrosis, immature | Apexification (단계적) or REP | MTA apical plug |
| Open apex with pulp 생활 | Apexogenesis (VPT) | MTA, Biodentine |

## Materials Comparison
- **Ca(OH)2**: 전통, 저렴, hard tissue formation 약함
- **MTA**: gold standard, dentinal bridge, discoloration risk
- **Biodentine**: 신속 설정, less discoloration
- **BioRoot RCS**: bioactive, root canal sealer 변형
