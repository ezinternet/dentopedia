## Summary
In vitro study (n=48 extracted premolars) using FTIR spectroscopy, SEM smear layer scoring, and Vickers microhardness to compare the effects of eight irrigating solutions on root dentin structure. Silver citrate and ozonated olive oil caused the least FTIR-measured chemical alteration to dentin compared to NaOCl 5.25%. Silver citrate demonstrated smear layer removal on SEM comparable to NaOCl. All irrigants reduced microhardness compared to the distilled water control. The NaOCl 5.25% + EDTA combination produced the best smear layer removal but also caused the greatest microhardness reduction.

## Key Contributions
- **NaOCl 5.25%**: greatest collagen denaturation by FTIR (mineral/organic ratio shift); effective smear removal
- **Silver citrate 0.12%**: effective smear removal (SEM); minimal FTIR dentin alteration → favorable safety-efficacy balance
- **Ozonated olive oil**: minimal dentin alteration (FTIR near-baseline); minimal smear removal — not suitable as sole irrigant
- **NaOCl + EDTA combination**: best smear removal but most microhardness reduction
- **CHX 2%**: poor smear removal; moderate dentin alteration
- All tested irrigants significantly reduced microhardness vs. distilled water

## Methodology
- **n**: 48 human extracted single-rooted premolars; standardized preparation to ISO #40
- **Groups** (n=8 each): distilled water (control), NaOCl 5.25%, NaOCl 2.5%, EDTA 17%, NaOCl 5.25% + EDTA 17%, silver citrate 0.12%, ozonated olive oil, CHX 2%
- **FTIR**: mineral/organic ratio (phosphate peak ~1035 cm⁻¹; amide I/II peaks 1640/1540 cm⁻¹)
- **SEM**: smear layer score 1–5 by canal third
- **Microhardness**: Vickers indentation

## Results

### Smear layer removal (SEM — less smear = better)
| Irrigant | Coronal | Middle | Apical | Overall |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| NaOCl 5.25% + EDTA | Best | Best | Best | ★★★★★ |
| NaOCl 5.25% | Good | Good | Moderate | ★★★★ |
| Silver citrate | Good | Moderate | Moderate | ★★★ |
| NaOCl 2.5% | Moderate | Moderate | Poor | ★★★ |
| EDTA | Moderate | Moderate | Poor | ★★ |
| CHX 2% | Poor | Poor | Poor | ★ |
| Ozonated olive oil | Minimal | Minimal | Minimal | ★ |

### FTIR dentin alteration (collagen integrity, low = better)
NaOCl 5.25% > NaOCl 2.5% > EDTA > CHX > Silver citrate > Ozonated olive oil ≈ Control

### Microhardness
All irrigants significantly reduced vs. control (p < .05); NaOCl 5.25% + EDTA showed greatest reduction.
