## 한줄요약
우산 리뷰(SR 4편+MA 9편): PUI가 미생물 감소(RD −0.15, p=0.03) 및 근첨 치유(RD −0.09, p=0.01) 모두에서 가장 강한 근거; 관개 프로토콜 간 높은 이질성

## Summary
Umbrella review (UR) synthesizing 4 descriptive SRs and 9 meta-analyses on irrigation protocol effectiveness in endodontic therapy. PUI (passive ultrasonic irrigation) emerges as the most evidence-supported technique for both microbial reduction and apical healing. High heterogeneity across irrigant protocols observed throughout the evidence base.

## Key Quantitative Findings (PUI vs Conventional Irrigation)

| Outcome | Risk Difference | 95% CI | p-value | I² |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Microbial reduction | **RD −0.15** | −0.28, −0.01 | **0.03** | 60% |
| Apical healing | **RD −0.09** | −0.16, −0.02 | **0.01** | 0% |

- 8 of 13 included articles rated high methodological quality
- Low overlap of primary studies across MAs (confirms independence of evidence)

## Evidence Hierarchy Context
- This is the highest-level synthesis available: umbrella review of SRs/MAs
- Confirms findings of Kumar 2023 (SR+MA) and Tonini 2022 (SR) at a higher synthesis level

## Limitations
- Dependent on quality of included SRs/MAs
- High heterogeneity in primary studies (irrigant type, concentration, volume, activation parameters)
- Long-term outcome data sparse
