## 한줄요약
서술적 리뷰(홍콩대): 소닉·초음파(PUI)·ANP(EndoVac)·레이저 등 관개액 활성화 기법 비교; 모든 활성화 기법이 기존 주사기 관개보다 세정력 우수, 레이저는 임상 근거 부족

## Summary
Narrative review from University of Hong Kong synthesizing clinical evidence for all major irrigant activation methods. Framework: PUI (strong RCT evidence), ANP/EndoVac (superior apical delivery, minimal extrusion), sonic methods (effective, simpler), laser (emerging, inconsistent clinical evidence). Activation consistently superior to conventional needle irrigation (CNI) for debridement.

## Method Comparison

| Method | Mechanism | Clinical Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| **PUI** (Passive Ultrasonic) | Acoustic microstreaming + cavitation | Strong; consistently superior to CNI in RCTs |
| **ANP** (EndoVac) | Suction draws irrigant apically | Best for WL delivery; minimal extrusion risk |
| **Sonic** (EDDY, VibraShaker) | High-frequency oscillation | Effective; simpler setup, lower cost |
| **Laser** (Er:YAG, diode) | Photoacoustic streaming | Promising but limited RCT data |

## Key Principles
- **NaOCl + EDTA combination** essential; activation enhances both tissue dissolution and smear layer removal
- NaOCl activation → enhanced tissue dissolution + antimicrobial effect
- EDTA activation → improved smear layer removal
- Sonic: lower cost, no special generator needed vs PUI

## Limitations
- Narrative review — subject to selection bias
- Laser evidence quality notably lower than PUI/ANP
- Heterogeneous clinical protocols across cited studies
