## 한줄요약
우산 리뷰(SR 7편): EAL과 방사선 근관장 측정은 전반적으로 동등; EAL 3–6세대 간 유의차 없음; 유치에서도 허용 가능(낮은 근거); NaOCl 존재 시 EAL 수치 영향받음

## Summary
Umbrella review (7 SRs) from University of Salerno on EAL accuracy in modern endodontics. Covers EAL vs radiography, EAL generations (3–6), EAL in primary teeth, and influencing factors. Overall conclusion: EAL and radiography "equally valid" for WL determination, with low evidence levels across all domains.

## Included SRs Summary

| SR | Comparison | Key Finding |
|---|---|---|
| Kaur 2024 | EAL vs radiography (818 pts) | WL adequacy: EAL better (RR 1.10, p=0.02); WL accuracy: radiography better |
| Martins 2014 | EAL vs radiography (2498 pts) | EAL better for AC localization (43.9–89.1% vs 14.6–32.7%) |
| Amin 2019 | CBCT vs EAL | Comparable; discrepancy 0.51mm |
| Nasiri 2022 | Generations 3–6 | **No significant differences** between any generation |
| Paradiso 2022 | Primary teeth | EAL vs CR: OR 0.73 (NS) |
| Vitali 2022 | Primary teeth | 69.3% of EAL readings within ±0.5mm |
| Tsesis 2015 | Influencing factors | NaOCl: smaller file–AC distance with RootZX and Justy II (p<0.05) |

## Key Practical Takeaways
1. **EAL generations 3–6 are equivalent** — upgrade for ergonomics, not accuracy
2. **NaOCl in canal** can shorten EAL readings (Tsesis 2015) — measure before flooding with NaOCl or verify afterward
3. **Primary teeth**: EAL usable but evidence quality is low
4. **EAL vs radiography**: both valid; EAL better for AC localization specifically (Martins)

## Evidence Caveat
- Low evidence levels overall — heterogeneous primary studies in all included SRs
- Conflicting direction between Kaur 2024 (radiography better for WL accuracy) and Martins 2014 (EAL better for AC localization)
