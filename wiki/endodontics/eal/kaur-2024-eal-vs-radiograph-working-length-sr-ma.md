## 한줄요약
SR+MA (11개 RCT): EAL은 방사선보다 근관치료 적합성 10% 향상, 그러나 정확도는 방사선이 우세하며 통증 차이는 없음 — 전반적 근거 수준 매우 낮음.

## Summary
Systematic review and meta-analysis of 11 RCTs comparing 전자근단측정기 (Electronic Apex Locator, EAL) vs 구내방사선사진 (intraoral radiograph) for 근관장측정 (working length determination, WLD). Pain during WLD was identical (SMD 0.00, P=0.98). Radiograph produced more accurate WLD (SMD 0.55, P=0.02), but EAL showed 10% better treatment adequacy (RR 1.10, P=0.006). Overall evidence certainty was very low due to heterogeneity and risk of bias.

## Key Contributions
- First SR+MA directly comparing EAL vs radiograph for WLD on pain and accuracy outcomes
- Confirms EAL superiority in treatment adequacy despite inferior single-measurement accuracy
- Highlights need for higher-quality RCTs; current evidence remains very low certainty

## Methodology
- PRISMA-compliant SR+MA; databases: PubMed, Cochrane, Web of Science, Scopus, Google Scholar
- 11 eligible RCTs included
- Outcomes: pain (SMD), WLD accuracy (SMD), treatment adequacy (RR)
- Random-effects model; heterogeneity assessed with I²

## Results
| Outcome | Result | P-value |
|---|---|---|
| Pain during WLD | SMD 0.00 (no difference) | 0.98 |
| WLD accuracy | SMD 0.55 (radiograph better) | 0.02 |
| Treatment adequacy | RR 1.10 (EAL 10% better) | 0.006 |
| Evidence certainty | Very low | — |
