## 한줄요약
In vitro 30개 소구치: 4종 EFL 모두 정상 근첨에서 정확; 발산형(divergent) 근첨공에서 Raypex 6·CanalPro 정확도 저하(p<0.05); RootZX II·EPex Pro는 영향 없음

## Summary
In vitro study (30 mandibular premolars, alginate model) comparing RootZX II, Raypex 6, EPex Pro, and CanalPro across 3 foramen morphologies: fully formed, immature parallel walls, immature divergent walls. All devices accurate in normal anatomy; divergent foramen negatively affects Raypex 6 and CanalPro at 0.0mm penetration.

## Device Comparison by Foramen Morphology

| Device | Fully Formed | Divergent AF (0.0mm) |
|---|---|---|
| **RootZX II** | Accurate | **Not affected** (p>0.05) |
| **EPex Pro** | Accurate | **Not affected** (p>0.05) |
| Raypex 6 | Accurate | **Accuracy ↓** (p<0.05) |
| CanalPro | Accurate | **Accuracy ↓** (p<0.05) |

- Apical penetration depth (0.0 vs −1.0mm): no significant influence on any device
- EPex Pro ≈ RootZX II in operating mechanism; CanalPro ≈ Raypex 6

## Clinical Relevance
- In cases of **open/divergent apices** (young patients, resorption, overinstrumentation): RootZX II or EPex Pro preferred over Raypex 6 or CanalPro
- Measuring at −1.0mm (1mm short of foramen) is equally accurate and clinically safer
- Normal, fully formed foramen: all 4 devices interchangeable
