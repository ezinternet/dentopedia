## One-line Summary
Chinese expert Delphi consensus on regenerative endodontic procedures (REP) for immature necrotic teeth: protocol of 1.5% NaOCl irrigation, TAP/DAP antibiotic paste, blood-clot induction, and MTA barrier; reported success rates of 85–90% periapical healing, 60–80% root maturation, and 30–60% vitality response.

## 한줄요약

## Summary
Chinese expert panel Delphi consensus on regenerative endodontic procedure (REP) clinical protocols for immature necrotic teeth.

## Key Contributions
- Protocol: NaOCl 1.5% irrigation → TAP/DAP antibiotic paste → blood clot induction → MTA barrier
- Success criteria: apical closure, root development, clinical healing — all ≥80%
- Vital pulp cell scaffold approach as alternative to blood clot

## Methodology
Delphi consensus + literature review by Chinese endodontic experts.

## Results
REP success rates: periapical healing 85–90%, vitality response 30–60%, root maturation 60–80%. TAP concentration: max 1mg/mL to avoid EDTA-induced cell toxicity.
