## One-line Summary
SR+MA of RET vs MTA apical plug for necrotic immature teeth: survival (RET 97.8% / MAP 97.1%) and success (RET 91.3% / MAP 94.6%) — no significant difference; low evidence level.

## 한줄요약
미성숙 치수괴사치 RET vs MTA apical plug SR+MA: 생존율(RET 97.8%/MAP 97.1%), 성공율(RET 91.3%/MAP 94.6%) — 유의차 없음, 근거 수준 낮음.

## Summary
J Endod 2017 SR+MA. 미성숙 영구치, 치수괴사, 개방 치근단 대상 RET vs MAP 비교. Pooled survival/success rates 분석.

## Key Contributions
- 생존율: MAP 97.1% ≈ RET 97.8% (유의차 없음)
- 성공율: MAP 94.6% ≈ RET 91.3% (유의차 없음)
- 근거 수준: LOW (대부분 case series/retrospective)
- 임상적 시사: 두 방법 모두 수용 가능한 outcome

## Methodology
Systematic search; inclusion: immature permanent, necrotic pulp, open apex; MAP and RET outcomes; pooled survival + success rates

## Results
| Outcome | MAP | RET | Sig. |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pooled survival | 97.1% | 97.8% | NS |
| Pooled success | 94.6% | 91.3% | NS |

**근거 수준: Low** (RCT 없음)
