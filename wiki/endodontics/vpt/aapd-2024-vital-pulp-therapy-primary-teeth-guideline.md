## One-line Summary
AAPD 2024 consensus guideline on vital pulp therapy (VPT) for primary teeth (IPC, DPC, pulpotomy): MTA is the first-choice pulpotomy material (~93% 24-month success) with formocresol still acceptable (85–92%) when MTA is unavailable, and MTA/Biodentine preferred over Ca(OH)₂ for direct pulp capping.

## Summary
AAPD 2024 guideline on vital pulp therapy for primary teeth: IPC, DPC, and pulpotomy indications, materials, and protocols.

## Key Contributions
- IPC: remove near-pulp caries to firm dentin; MTA/Ca(OH)₂/Biodentine acceptable
- DPC: small exposure (<1mm²); MTA or Biodentine preferred over Ca(OH)₂
- Pulpotomy: MTA ≥ formocresol; FC still acceptable but biocompatible alternatives preferred

## Methodology
Evidence-based guideline; systematic review base; 2024 edition (supersedes 2017).

## Results
MTA pulpotomy: 24-month success ~93%. FC pulpotomy still acceptable (85–92%) when MTA unavailable. DPC not recommended for primary teeth with reversible pulpitis only.
