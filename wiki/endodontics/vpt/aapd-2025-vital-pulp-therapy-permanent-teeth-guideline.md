## One-line Summary
First standalone AAPD 2025 consensus guideline on vital pulp therapy (VPT) for permanent teeth: VPT may be considered even for symptomatic irreversible pulpitis in select teeth, with MTA/Biodentine as first-choice materials for direct pulp capping (≥90% 5-year success in mature teeth) and full pulpotomy (80–90% success), avoiding root canal treatment in select cases.

## Summary
First standalone AAPD guideline on vital pulp therapy for permanent teeth: indications, protocols, and outcomes.

## Key Contributions
- First AAPD guideline dedicated to VPT in permanent teeth
- Expanded indication: VPT may be considered for symptomatic irreversible pulpitis in select permanent teeth
- MTA/Biodentine: first-choice materials for DPC and pulpotomy in permanent teeth

## Methodology
Evidence-based guideline; systematic literature review.

## Results
Mature permanent tooth DPC with MTA: 5-year success ≥90%. Full pulpotomy for irreversible pulpitis: 80–90% success. Avoids root canal treatment in select cases.
