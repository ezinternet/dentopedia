## One-line Summary

In-vitro MC3T3 pre-osteoblast study (ISO 10993-5): CeraSeal, BioRoot RCS and AH Plus were all non-cytotoxic at 48 h (BioRoot transiently cytotoxic at 24 h, 1:10), and differentially induced inflammation/mineralization genes (AH Plus → Tnf; CeraSeal/BioRoot → Ptgs2 and Dmp1) without changing 28-day mineralization-nodule formation.

## 한줄요약

In-vitro MC3T3 전조골세포 연구 (ISO 10993-5): CeraSeal·BioRoot RCS·AH Plus 모두 48시간 시점 비독성 (BioRoot만 24시간·1:10에서 일시적 독성), 염증·광화 유전자를 차등 유도 (AH Plus는 Tnf↑, CeraSeal/BioRoot는 Ptgs2·Dmp1↑) 했으나 28일째 광화 결절 형성에는 차이가 없었다.

## Summary

This in-vitro study compared the premixed calcium-silicate bioceramic sealer **CeraSeal** (MetaBiomed, Korea), the tricalcium-silicate sealer **BioRoot RCS** (Septodont), and the epoxy-resin gold-standard **AH Plus** (Dentsply) in **MC3T3 mouse pre-osteoblast cells**, following ISO 10993-5. It separates two questions: are the sealers cytotoxic to bone-lineage cells, and do they drive osteo/odontogenic bioactivity?

The answer is that none of the three sealers — including CeraSeal — was cytotoxic at 48 h, with only BioRoot showing a transient viability drop at 24 h (1:10). However, the sealers produced **distinct gene-expression signatures**: AH Plus alone raised the inflammatory cytokine **Tnf (TNF-α)**, whereas the two calcium-silicate sealers raised **Ptgs2 (COX-2)** and the dentin-matrix marker **Dmp1**. Despite these transcriptional differences, none of the sealers altered functional **mineralization-nodule formation** at 28 days. This seeds the wiki's first cell-level CeraSeal biocompatibility evidence to complement its clinical cohort pages.

## Key Contributions

- First demonstration that **AH Plus is not cytotoxic** to MC3T3 osteoblast-lineage cells.
- Head-to-head osteoblastic-model profile of **CeraSeal** vs BioRoot RCS vs AH Plus.
- Shows that **non-cytotoxic ≠ osteoinductive**: divergent inflammation/mineralization transcription did not translate to different mineralized-nodule output.

## Methodology

- **Standard / cells**: ISO 10993-5:2009 extraction method; MC3T3 (ATCC CRL-2594) mouse pre-osteoblast, passages 5–10, α-MEM + 5% FBS, triplicate.
- **Extracts**: 24 h sealer extracts, 0.22 µm filtered, dilutions 1:10 and 1:100.
- **Viability**: MTT at 24 h and 48 h (570 nm); DMSO positive control, FBS-free negative control.
- **Gene expression**: qRT-PCR (TaqMan, 2^-ΔΔCt, Gapdh/β-actin reference) at 1:100, 24 h — inflammation **Tnf, Ptgs2**; mineralization/osteo-odontogenic **Runx2, Msx1, Ssp1, Dmp1**.
- **Mineralization**: Alizarin Red S at 28 days, cetylpyridinium-chloride elution at 570 nm, protein-normalized.
- **Stats**: one-way ANOVA + Tukey, α = 0.05.

## Results

| Endpoint | CeraSeal | BioRoot RCS | AH Plus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Viability 24 h (1:10) | ≈ control | reduced (p<0.05) | higher than control |
| Viability 48 h | difference vs control | not cytotoxic | not cytotoxic |
| Tnf (TNF-α) | ≈ control | ≈ control | **up (p<0.05)** |
| Ptgs2 (COX-2) | **up (p<0.05)** | **up (p<0.05)** | ≈ control |
| Runx2 | ≈ control | up (p<0.05) | up (p<0.05) |
| Dmp1 | **up (p<0.0001)** | **up (p<0.0001)** | ≈ control |
| Msx1 / Ssp1 | no change (p=0.1769) | no change | no change |
| Mineralization nodules (28 d) | ≈ control | ≈ control | ≈ control |

- DMSO (positive control) reduced both viability and mineralization-nodule formation (p<0.0001).
