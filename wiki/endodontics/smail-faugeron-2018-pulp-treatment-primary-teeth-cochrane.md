## Summary
Cochrane review comparing pulp treatment modalities (IPC, DPC, pulpotomy, pulpectomy) for extensive caries in primary teeth.

## Key Contributions
- MTA pulpotomy: 24-month success ~93% across studies
- FC pulpotomy: 85–92% success — still acceptable but biocompatible alternatives preferred
- IPC without complete caries removal: ~90% success in selected cases

## Methodology
Cochrane SR+MA; RCT only; 2018 update.

## Results
MTA pulpotomy superior to FC in biocompatibility and comparable in success rates. IPC valid option in reversible pulpitis cases. FC acceptable when alternatives unavailable.
