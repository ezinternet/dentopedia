## 한줄요약
QMix 근관 세정액 개요(IEJ 판) — EDTA+CHX+surfactant 복합 작용, 도말층 제거+항균 이중 효과.

## Summary
Overview of QMix irrigation solution (EDTA + chlorhexidine + surfactant), comparing its smear layer removal and antimicrobial properties to single-component irrigants.

## Key Contributions
- QMix dual action: EDTA-mediated smear layer removal + CHX antimicrobial effect in single solution
- Effective against E. faecalis; superior to EDTA alone in antimicrobial activity
- NaOCl tissue dissolution cannot be replaced; QMix used as final rinse

## Methodology
Narrative review.

## Results
QMix effectively removes smear layer and kills residual bacteria when used as final irrigant after NaOCl. Cannot substitute NaOCl for tissue dissolution. Convenient for combined chelation + disinfection step.
