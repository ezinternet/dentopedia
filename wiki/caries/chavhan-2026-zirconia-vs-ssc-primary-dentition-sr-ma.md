## One-line Summary

Abstract-only SR+MA (7 RCTs, 700 crowns; 3 pooled): prefabricated zirconia crowns and stainless steel crowns had comparable retention (OR 0.96, 95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93) in primary molars, with PZCs showing better gingival health and less plaque but possibly more antagonist wear and strongly higher parental esthetic satisfaction.

## Summary

> [!note] Abstract-only page
> No PMC open-access full text was available at ingest; under wiki Rule #1 only the PubMed abstract was used ([DOI 10.4103/jisppd.jisppd_462_25](https://doi.org/10.4103/jisppd.jisppd_462_25)).

According to PubMed, this PROSPERO-registered SR+MA compares prefabricated zirconia crowns (PZCs) — the tooth-colored esthetic option — against the workhorse stainless steel crown (SSC) for primary molars. Pooling 3 of 7 included RCTs (700 crowns total), **retention did not differ significantly (OR 0.96, 95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93)**. PZCs showed **better gingival health and less plaque accumulation**, but the review flags **possible increased antagonist (opposing-tooth) wear** from zirconia's hardness, plus higher cost and technique sensitivity. **Parental satisfaction strongly favored PZCs** for esthetics.

Clinically: PZCs are a reliable esthetic alternative to SSCs with comparable retention/durability and gingival advantages — best reserved for cases where esthetics and caregiver preference are priorities, weighing cost and antagonist wear. This extends the wiki's primary-molar crown cluster (Hall/SSC) toward material choice; the split-mouth RCT [[caries/muller-bolla-2025-zirconia-vs-ssc-primary-molars-split-mouth-rct]] provides concordant primary data (similar success, slightly lower ZC retention).

## Key Contributions

- PZC ≈ SSC retention (OR 0.96, NS) in primary molars.
- PZC: better gingival health/less plaque; caveats = antagonist wear, cost.
- Parents strongly prefer PZC esthetics.

## Methodology

SR+MA (PROSPERO CRD420251016795); PRISMA 2020; RCTs 2018–2024; Cochrane RoB2; 7 RCTs (700 crowns), 3 pooled. Confidence `sr+ma`; abstract-only ingest (no PMC OA).

## Results

- Retention: OR 0.96 (95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93), NS.
- PZC better gingival health, less plaque; possible ↑ antagonist wear.
- Parental satisfaction favors PZC (esthetics).
