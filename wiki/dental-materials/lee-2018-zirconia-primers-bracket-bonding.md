In vitro (Restor Dent Endod 2018): ceramic bracket bonding to zirconia — Scotchbond Universal (no separate primer required) achieved highest SBS and ARI score 3 (cohesive failure); universal adhesive outperformed dedicated zirconia primers.

## Summary
In vitro study comparing SBS of ceramic brackets bonded to zirconia using four different primers/adhesives plus control. Scotchbond Universal (MDP-containing universal adhesive, no separate primer) achieved highest SBS and cohesive-type failure, outperforming dedicated zirconia primers. Practical finding for orthodontic treatment of patients with zirconia restorations.

## Key Contributions
- Scotchbond Universal self-primes zirconia — embedded MDP sufficient without separate zirconia primer
- ARI 3 (cohesive failure in adhesive): clinically ideal — bracket debonds through adhesive, not ceramic
- Dedicated zirconia primers (Z-Prime, Zirconia Liner): lower SBS than universal adhesive with integrated MDP
- Clinical implication: for orthodontic bracket bonding to zirconia, use MDP-containing universal adhesive

## Results
| Group | SBS | ARI score |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| Scotchbond Universal | Highest | 3 |
| Z-Prime Plus | High | 0 |
| Zirconia Liner | Intermediate | 0 |
| Metal/Zirconia Primer | Low | 0 |
| Control | Lowest | 0 |
