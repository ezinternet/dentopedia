## Summary
In vitro study evaluating the effect of saliva contamination and various cleaning methods on the bondability of resin cement to zirconia ceramic. Contact angle measurement used as a surrogate for surface free energy and bondability. Ivoclean (NaOH/ZrO2-based) achieved the lowest contact angle and highest shear bond strength after contamination.

## Key Contributions
- Contact angle as quantitative predictor of zirconia bondability post-contamination
- Ivoclean superior to phosphoric acid and acetone for surface energy restoration
- 10,000 thermocycles: bond strength reduction greater in inadequately cleaned groups

## Results
| Cleaning method | Contact angle | Relative bond strength |
|----------------|--------------|----------------------|
| Ivoclean | ~10° (lowest) | Highest |
| Phosphoric acid | Intermediate | Intermediate |
| Acetone | Higher | Lower |
| Water only | Highest (worst) | Lowest |
