## One-line Summary
In vitro (Materials 2022): SBS of 5 cleaning methods on contaminated zirconia — all cleaned groups significantly higher than contaminated untreated control; confirms cleaning is mandatory before cementation.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro(Materials 2022): 오염 지르코니아 5가지 세정법 SBS — 세정 전군이 오염 대조군보다 유의하게 높음; 접착 전 세정 필수 확인.

## Summary
In vitro investigation of five different cleaning methods on contaminated zirconia shear bond strength to resin cement. Confirms the universal principle that any cleaning method improves bond strength compared to contaminated-uncleaned control; contributes to growing evidence base for zirconia cleaning protocols.
