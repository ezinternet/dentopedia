## Summary
In vitro comparison of five surface treatments on two polymer-containing CAD/CAM restorative materials (Lava Ultimate: resin-ceramic; Vita Enamic: polymer-infiltrated ceramic) bonded with two dual-cure resin cements (TheraCem, RelyX U200). Material-specific surface treatment protocols required — HF etching only appropriate for materials with ceramic content.

## Key Contributions
- CoJet tribochemical silica coating + Z-Prime: universally highest SBS regardless of material
- HF acid: effective only for Vita Enamic (ceramic-rich); ineffective/damaging for Lava Ultimate
- Z-Prime (MDP primer): essential chemical bonding component for resin-based CAD/CAM materials
- Laser: no consistent advantage
