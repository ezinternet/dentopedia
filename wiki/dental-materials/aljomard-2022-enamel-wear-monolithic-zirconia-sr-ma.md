## Summary
Systematic review and meta-analysis of in vitro studies on enamel wear against monolithic zirconia. 25 articles met SR criteria; 3 included in meta-analysis (standardized at 50N, 240–250k cycles). Concludes enamel wear is within accepted levels, and polished surface causes less wear than glazed.

## Key Contributions
- Meta-analysis criteria: 50N vertical load; 240,000–250,000 cycles (≈1 year in vivo)
- Result: enamel wear vs monolithic Zr = within statistically accepted level
- Polished monolithic Zr: less enamel wear than glazed
- Surface texture is essential determinant of wear outcome
- Evidence base: predominantly in vitro — clinical RCTs needed
