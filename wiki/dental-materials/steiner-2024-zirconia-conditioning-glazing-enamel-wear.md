## 한줄요약
in-vitro(Clin Oral Investig 2024): 3Y-TZP 6조건(조면/소결/연마 위 유약 + 무유약 3종), 10,000사이클 — 연마 단독: 최소 대합치 마모(0.4mm³); 조면 위 유약: 연마만큼 마모 감소 안 됨; 유약 아래 표면조도가 대합치 마모 결정.

## Summary
2024 study demonstrating that the surface condition BENEATH the glaze determines antagonist enamel wear. Glazing over a rough surface does not achieve the same protection as polishing alone — only polishing as a solo treatment minimizes wear.

## Key Contributions
- 6 groups; 10,000 cycles; 3D volumetric scan
- Volume loss order (most→least): rough Zr > glazed-rough > glazed-sintered > 46μm-finish ≈ control > glazed-polished > **polished alone (0.4mm³)**
- Key message: **glazing does not cancel out underlying roughness**
- Polish → glaze: better than rough → glaze, but still worse than polish alone
- Sub-surface condition is the key determinant of antagonist wear
