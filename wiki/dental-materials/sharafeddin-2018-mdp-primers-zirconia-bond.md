## 한줄요약
in-vitro(J Dent Shiraz 2018): MDP 프라이머 비교 — Z-Prime Plus(22.45 MPa) > All-Bond Universal(17.51) > 무처리(7.58); 전용 MDP 프라이머가 범용 접착제보다 우수.

## Summary
In vitro comparison of zirconia-specific conventional MDP primer (Z-Prime Plus) vs universal MDP-containing adhesive (All-Bond Universal) on shear bond strength of zirconia to resin cement. Zirconia-specific primer significantly outperforms universal adhesive; both greatly surpass unprimed control.

## Key Contributions
- Zirconia-specific Z-Prime Plus (dedicated MDP primer): 22.45 MPa — highest
- Universal adhesive (All-Bond Universal): 17.51 MPa — significant improvement over control, but less than Z-Prime
- Clinical implication: dedicated zirconia primers contain optimized MDP concentrations vs diluted MDP in universal adhesives

## Results
| Group | SBS (MPa) |
|-------|-----------|
| Z-Prime Plus | 22.45 ± 3.60 |
| All-Bond Universal | 17.51 ± 1.34 |
| Control (no primer) | 7.58 ± 1.62 |
