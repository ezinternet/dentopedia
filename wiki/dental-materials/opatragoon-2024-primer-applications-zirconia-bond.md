## Summary
In vitro study comparing single vs multiple applications (1, 2, 3 coats) of MDP-containing primer on dental zirconia shear bond strength. Key finding: additional primer coats do not significantly increase bond strength — single application is sufficient if protocol is followed correctly.

## Key Contributions
- No dose-response relationship for primer application number on zirconia
- 1 coat = 2 coats = 3 coats for SBS (NS differences)
- Clinical protocol simplification: single primer application sufficient; no advantage to multiple layers
- Avoids over-priming which may interfere with cement polymerization
