## 한줄요약
CAD/CAM 수복물 임상 실패 원인 SR+MA — 파절 55%, 탈락 20%; 두께 <1mm 파절 위험 증가.

## Summary
SR+MA identifying and quantifying failure modes of CAD/CAM restorations in clinical studies.

## Key Contributions
- Fracture: 55% of failures; most common in posterior zirconia and nanoceramic
- Debonding/detachment: 20% of failures; adhesive protocol deviations major cause
- Restoration thickness <1mm significantly increases fracture risk

## Methodology
PubMed/Embase systematic review + meta-analysis.

## Results
Fracture dominant failure mode. Adequate thickness (≥1.5mm occlusal) and strict adhesive protocol reduce failure risk significantly.
