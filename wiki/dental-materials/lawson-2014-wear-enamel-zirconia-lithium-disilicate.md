## Summary
Head-to-head comparison of zirconia vs lithium disilicate across three finishing states (adjusted, polished, glazed) for enamel wear and material wear. Key clinical guidance: always polish after occlusal adjustment; polished zirconia is most enamel-compatible material tested.

## Key Contributions
- AP Zr: least enamel wear (0.33±0.11mm³) — comparable to enamel-enamel
- AP LD: 0.36±0.09mm³ — similar to AP Zr
- Veneering porcelain: most enamel wear (2.15±0.58mm³)
- Adjusted (A) and glazed (AG) Zr: more enamel wear than AP Zr
- Polishing after adjustment: significantly reduces enamel wear for BOTH materials
- Zr: no detectable material wear (A and AP); LD shows material wear
