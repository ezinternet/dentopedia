## Summary
Umbrella review (4 systematic reviews, 2013–2023) providing the highest level of synthesis on antagonist enamel wear opposing zirconia crowns. Concludes polished monolithic zirconia is the most enamel-friendly of tested ceramic materials.

## Key Contributions
- Polished monolithic Zr ≤ natural enamel wear over time
- Polished < glazed for antagonist enamel wear
- Polished Zr < metal ceramics, feldspathic porcelains, lithium disilicate
- Meta-analysis not possible due to heterogeneous study designs and outcome variables
- Clinical guideline: polish monolithic zirconia; do not routinely glaze if enamel preservation is priority
