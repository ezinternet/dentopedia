## One-line Summary
In vitro shear bond strength study (Indian J Dent Res 2017; saliva-contaminated zirconia, 4 cleaning protocols × 2 resin cements): air abrasion gave the highest SBS, Ivoclean outperformed water rinsing, and self-adhesive MDP-containing cement exceeded conventional resin cement across all cleaning groups.

## Summary
In vitro comparative evaluation of four cleaning protocols on saliva-contaminated zirconia bonded with two cement types. Confirms that mechanical decontamination (air abrasion) outperforms chemical methods alone, and that self-adhesive MDP-containing cement provides higher bond strength regardless of cleaning protocol.

## Key Contributions
- Air abrasion: most effective mechanical decontamination
- Ivoclean: chemical superiority over water cleaning
- MDP-containing self-adhesive cement: higher SBS across all cleaning groups vs conventional cement
- Clinical protocol: air abrasion + self-adhesive MDP cement = optimal combination
