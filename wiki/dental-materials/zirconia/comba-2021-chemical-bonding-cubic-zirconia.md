## One-line Summary
In vitro (Materials 2021): chemical bonding to cubic (5Y) zirconia — CoJet + silane + MDP primer showed best 6-month bond stability; MDP alone also significantly improved bond; cubic zirconia responds similarly to 4Y/3Y for adhesion.

## Summary
In vitro investigation of chemical-based bonding techniques on cubic (5Y-PSZ) high-translucency zirconia bond strength stability. Confirms that MDP chemical bonding principles applicable to standard Y-TZP extend to cubic zirconia; tribochemical silica coating (CoJet) provides additional micromechanical interlocking for optimal long-term stability.

## Key Contributions
- First confirmation that cubic 5Y zirconia responds to MDP bonding similarly to 3Y-TZP
- CoJet + silane + MDP: optimal protocol for long-term bond stability
- Bond degradation after aging: greater in single-mechanism groups; combined treatments more durable
- Clinical implication: same MDP-based protocol applicable regardless of zirconia translucency grade
