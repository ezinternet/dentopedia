In vitro retention strength study (BMC Oral Health 2022; monolithic zirconia crowns × 3 primer-cement systems after 10,000 thermocycles): Panavia V5 + Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus gave the highest retention, showing that matched same-manufacturer primer-cement systems outperform cross-brand combinations.

## Summary
In vitro retention strength study comparing three complete primer-cement systems for monolithic zirconia crowns on standardized molar preparations after 10,000 thermocycles. Confirms that matched primer-cement systems (from same manufacturer) optimized for chemical compatibility outperform cross-brand combinations.

## Key Contributions
- Panavia V5 + Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus: highest retention (MDP-MDP chemical synergy)
- System matching within manufacturer: improved cement-primer chemical compatibility
- 10,000 thermocycles: all groups tested for aging resistance
- Clinical protocol: use primer and cement from same matched system

## Results
| System | Retention (relative) |
|--------|---------------------|
| Panavia V5 + Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus | Highest |
| Multilink Speed + Monobond N | Intermediate |
| Duo-Link + Z-Prime Plus | Lower |
