## Summary
In vitro study on two Y-TZP brands (Katana, ZirCAD) evaluating Ivoclean cleaning agent, Monobond Plus coupling agent, and their combination on shear bond strength at 24h and after 1 year water storage. Key finding: 50% bond strength loss after 1 year is universal — no protocol prevented aging degradation.

## Key Contributions
- Combination Ivoclean + Monobond Plus: optimal protocol for Katana at 24h
- Long-term (1 year water aging): ~50% bond loss regardless of protocol — major clinical implication
- Brand-specific response: Katana vs ZirCAD differ in response to combined treatment
- Primer alone more important than cleaning agent alone for bond strength

## Results
| Protocol | Katana 24h | ZirCAD 24h | 1-year (all) |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Ivoclean + Monobond Plus | Highest | High | −50% |
| Monobond Plus alone | High | High | −50% |
| Ivoclean alone | Intermediate | Intermediate | −50% |
| Control | Lowest | Lowest | −50% |
