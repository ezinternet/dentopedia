## One-line Summary
In vitro comparison (JKDA 2023): Cention N (alkasite) demonstrated sustained 3-month fluoride release and antibacterial activity with the highest pH among tested materials; however, S. mutans antibacterial activity was lower than Fuji IX GP and Fuji II LC, and fluoride release was significantly highest for Fuji IX GP; clinical validation is incomplete.

## 한줄요약
In-vitro 비교(대한치과의사협회지 2023): Cention N(alkasite)은 3개월간 지속적 불소 유리 + 항균성 보유, pH는 시험 재료 중 최고. 단 S. mutans 항균력은 Fuji IX GP·Fuji II LC보다 낮고, 불소 유리량은 Fuji IX GP가 유의하게 최다. 임상 검증은 미완.

## Summary
Bench comparison of Cention N (alkasite) against Fuji IX GP (conventional GIC) and Fuji II LC (RMGIC) on antibacterial activity, pH, and fluoride release. Cention N behaves GIC-like (continuous fluoride, measurable antibacterial effect) and has the highest pH, but is not the strongest on either antibacterial inhibition or fluoride output.

## Key Contributions
- Alkasite vs conventional/resin-modified GIC on one material set
- Three properties measured (antibacterial / pH / fluoride to 84 d) with CHX control
- Bench positioning of Cention N relative to established fluoride-releasing materials

## Methodology
- Cention N, Fuji IX GP, Fuji II LC; CHX positive control.
- Agar diffusion (inhibition zone, 24 h) vs *S. mutans* and *L. casei*.
- Fluoride release days 1–84; pH at 1–24 h.

## Results
- All materials antibacterial.
- *S. mutans*: CHX > Fuji IX GP > Fuji II LC > Cention N (p<0.05).
- *L. casei*: CHX > Fuji II LC > Cention N > Fuji IX GP (p<0.05).
- Fluoride: continuous in all; **Fuji IX GP highest** (vs Fuji II LC and Cention N).
- pH: **Cention N highest** > Fuji IX GP > Fuji II LC.
- Conclusion: Cention N gives 3-month fluoride release + antibacterial activity like GICs, with highest pH; needs clinical validation.

## Limitations
- In vitro only; no clinical caries/restoration endpoints.
- Agar diffusion = diffusible effect, not contact killing.
