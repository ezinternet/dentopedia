## One-line Summary
In vitro (Materials 2022): MDP-containing cleaner (Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus) achieved significantly higher SBS on contaminated monolithic zirconia than water or alcohol cleaning; MDP phosphate groups provide dual-function contamination removal and chemical bonding.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro(Materials 2022): MDP 함유 세정제(Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus) — 오염 단일체 지르코니아에 수세·알코올보다 유의하게 높은 SBS; MDP 인산기가 오염물 제거+화학결합 이중 기능.

## Summary
In vitro study demonstrating that MDP-containing cleaner (acting simultaneously as cleaning and priming agent) significantly improves shear bond strength to contaminated monolithic zirconia compared to water or alcohol cleaning alone. MDP's phosphate functional group displaces organic contaminants while forming chemical bond with zirconia.

## Key Contributions
- Dual-function MDP cleaner: removes contaminants + establishes chemical bond in one step
- Superior to water and alcohol cleaning in all contamination scenarios
- Clinical convenience: single agent replaces separate cleaning + priming steps
- Mechanism: phosphate-zirconia chemical bonding (P–O–Zr) remains even after cleaning
