## 한줄요약
구치부 직접 레진 vs 아말감 코크란 리뷰 — 5년 생존율 유사, 이차우식 복합레진군에서 비열등 불확실.

## Summary
Cochrane review comparing clinical performance of direct composite resin vs amalgam fillings in permanent posterior teeth.

## Key Contributions
- 5-year survival rates: composite and amalgam broadly equivalent (high heterogeneity)
- Secondary caries risk: composite may be slightly higher; evidence uncertain
- Environmental concerns driving amalgam phase-out despite comparable clinical performance

## Methodology
Cochrane SR; RCT only; 2021 update.

## Results
Amalgam and composite statistically similar in survival. Secondary caries signal in composite warrants strict adhesive technique. Amalgam declining due to Minamata Convention.
