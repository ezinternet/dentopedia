in-vitro(Braz Oral Res 2016): Y-TZP 연삭(200·160·25μm) ± 열처리(1200°C 2h) — 연삭: 단사정 상↑·특성강도↑; 열처리: 단사정 역변환→제어 수준; 조립→세립 순서 마감; 과도 연삭 후 열처리 권장.

## Summary
UNESP/UFSM study demonstrating that grinding Y-TZP increases monoclinic phase and characteristic strength, but heat treatment at 1200°C reverses the monoclinic transformation. Provides guidance for laboratory handling of ground zirconia.

## Key Contributions
- Grinding increases monoclinic phase → compressive stress → characteristic strength ↑
- No correlation between surface roughness (Ra, Rz) and flexural strength
- Heat treatment (1200°C, 2h): reverses monoclinic transformation to tetragonal phase
- G80A (coarsest + heat treated): similar strength to control
- Other heat-treated groups: higher strength than control
- Finer grit (G600): smoother surface for final adjustment

## Clinical Implication
- Laboratory heat treatment after heavy grinding recommended when monoclinic content is concern
- Finer grit burs for final occlusal adjustment = better surface quality
