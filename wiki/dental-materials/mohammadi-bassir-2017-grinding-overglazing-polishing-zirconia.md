## Summary
Iranian study evaluating whether overglazing or polishing can compensate for the surface damage from coarse grinding of Y-TZP. Significant differences found among groups in roughness and flexural strength; polishing partially restores surface while overglazing alone is insufficient.

## Key Contributions
- 5 groups: standard polish (SP), grind (Gr), grind+overglaze (Gl), grind+2-step polish (BP), grind+polishing kit (MP)
- Significant differences: Ra, Rz (p<0.001) and flexural strength (p<0.001)
- Polishing after grinding: partially restores surface and mechanical properties
- Overglazing alone: insufficient to compensate for grinding damage
- XRD and SEM used for phase and topography analysis
