## 한줄요약
4쪽짜리 익명 SR 비판적 평가 체크리스트 (CASP/PHRU 양식 추정): PICO·검색·포함기준·질평가·heterogeneity·forest plot 해석을 Yes/No/Unclear로 점검; 출처 미상이므로 인용 불가, 학습용으로만 사용.

## Summary
This is a "fill-in" worksheet, not a publication. It is useful as a chairside or journal-club checklist when reading an SR. It is **not** citable — no author, no date, no journal.

## Key Contributions
- Provides a concrete decision tree for reading a systematic review: PICO → search comprehensiveness → inclusion criteria → study quality → heterogeneity → pooled estimate.
- Numerical heterogeneity cutoffs (Q/df > 1 = possible heterogeneity; p < 0.10 conventional significance threshold for Cochran Q).
- Canonical forest plot anatomy walkthrough using a 5-trial worked example.

## Methodology
- Educational worksheet format. Not a research paper.

## Results
- Worked example: 5-trial mortality MA, pooled OR 0.66 (95% CI 0.56–0.78), p<0.0001, homogeneity confirmed (Q/df 0.23). Illustrative only.

## Clinical Applicability
- Useful as a printable in-clinic checklist for journal club. Photocopy the 6-box checklist and run it against any SR before letting its conclusions drive a protocol change.
- **Caution — citation rule for this wiki:** because the worksheet has no named author or date, do NOT cite it as a methodology source in any clinical write-up. Use Kiriakou 2014 or Shin WJ 2015 (both properly cited) for that purpose.

## Caveats
- No author attribution. Possibly CASP, PHRU, or derivative. Unable to verify version or whether it has been superseded.
- Pre-PRISMA 2020 / pre-GRADE — does not include risk-of-bias 2.0 (RoB 2) or AMSTAR 2 checks that modern SR appraisal demands.
- Treated as a teaching artifact, not as primary literature.
