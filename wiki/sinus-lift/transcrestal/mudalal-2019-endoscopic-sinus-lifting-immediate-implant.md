## 한줄요약
잔존골 높이(Residual Bone Height, RBH) 3.5 mm 케이스 리포트: 혈소판 풍부 피브린(Platelet-Rich Fibrin, PRF) + 내시경 + 즉시식립 + 상악동거상술(PESS) 조합으로 12 mm 거상, 임플란트 안정성 지수(Implant Stability Quotient, ISQ)가 3개월 67.4 → 6개월 71.0로 상승, 치료 기간을 12개월에서 3개월로 단축.

## Summary
This case report describes the first use of a combined PESS (PRF + Endoscope + Simultaneous implant placement + Sinus floor elevation) protocol in a patient with severe atrophy of the posterior maxilla (RBH 3.53 mm). Endoscope-guided transcrestal sinus elevation using PRF as the sole graft allowed simultaneous placement of a 4.8 × 12 mm Straumann implant at 30 N·cm torque. ISQ rose to 67.4 at 3 months and 71.0 at 6 months, and a zirconia-ceramic crown was delivered at 3 months — compressing a conventional 12-month workflow to 3.

## Key Contributions
- Demonstrates feasibility of immediate implant placement with transcrestal sinus elevation in cases with RBH < 4 mm, traditionally considered indications for staged lateral window.
- Introduces endoscopic visualization to directly verify Schneiderian membrane integrity during osteotome elevation.
- Uses PRF alone (no xeno-/allograft) as a delivery vehicle and growth-factor source for sinus floor regeneration.

## Methodology
- Female, 40 y, non-smoker, missing #16 with RBH 3.53 mm on CBCT.
- Flapless punch access via 4.8-mm tissue bur; pilot drill stopped 1 mm short of sinus floor.
- 4.3-mm rounded osteotome (Kohler) up-fractured the sinus floor; endoscope (POLYDIAGNOST) verified membrane integrity during two-stage elevation.
- Three PRF clots prepared (10 mL × 3, 3000 rpm × 10 min, glass-coated tubes, no anticoagulant) and pressed into the sinus; elevation completed to 12 mm.
- Straumann 4.8 × 12 mm implant at 30 N·cm; healing cap placed.
- Antibiotics (metronidazole + amoxicillin × 3 d) and CHX 0.12% × 7 d.
- ISQ measured at 2, 3, and 6 mo; CBCT at 2 mo.

## Results
- Uneventful healing; CBCT at 2 mo showed good osseointegration.
- ISQ 67.4 at 3 mo; 71.0 at 6 mo.
- Zirconia-ceramic crown delivered at 3 mo.
- 6-mo follow-up: stable bone height, no peri-implant pathology.
