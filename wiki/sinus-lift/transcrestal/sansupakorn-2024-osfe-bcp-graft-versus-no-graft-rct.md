## One-line Summary
RCT (n=30, 1 yr, TCTR20210517008): BCP (HA30:TCP70) graft in OSFE shows no benefit over graftless OSFE in ISQ (p=0.56), survival, or bone gain; graftless group had significantly less marginal bone change.

## 한줄요약
RCT(30임플란트, 1년): OSFE+BCP(HA30:TCP70)는 무이식 OSFE 대비 ISQ·생존율·골이득 모두 차이 없으며, 오히려 변연골 변화는 무이식 군이 유의미하게 적음.

## Summary

Sansupakorn & Khongkhunthian (Clin Oral Investig 2024) randomized 30 patients (30 implants, single upper premolar/molar) to OSFE+BCP vs OSFE-alone. At 1-year functional loading, mean ISQ was 79.18±3.43 with no significant between-group difference (p=0.56). Both groups showed steadily increasing ISQ without dip differences. Failure rate: 2/15 in BCP group, 1/15 in graftless group — not statistically different. Critically, mean marginal bone change (MMBC) was significantly lower in the graftless group at 6, 9, and 12 months (p<0.05), suggesting that BCP not only fails to improve outcomes but may introduce some marginal bone disadvantage. The authors conclude BCP provides "no extraordinary benefit" and recommend OSFE without grafting.

This result stands in tension with Shahood 2024, where Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF showed superior outcomes over no-graft — the divergence likely reflects differences in graft type (alloplastic BCP vs xenograft+autologous growth factors) and possibly RBH eligibility thresholds.

## Key Contributions

One of the few RCTs directly comparing graft vs no-graft in OSFE. The finding that BCP increases marginal bone change is clinically significant. The CBCT-based ESBG-CT measurement adds 3D volumetric assessment beyond conventional 2D radiographs.

## Methodology

- OSFE+BCP (n=15) vs OSFE-alone (n=15); single edentulous site, upper PM/molar
- Assessments at 3, 6, 9, 12 months post-loading: ISQ, ESBG-2D, ESBG-CT, MMBC
- CBCT at baseline and 12 months

## Results

| Outcome | OSFE+BCP | OSFE alone | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| ISQ at 1 yr | 78.72±3.46 | 79.65±3.52 | 0.56 NS |
| Failures | 2/15 | 1/15 | NS |
| MMBC (6–12 mo) | Higher | **Lower** | <0.05 |
| ESBG-CT/2D | No difference | No difference | NS |
