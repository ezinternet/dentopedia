## 한줄요약
증례보고 (RBH 3.5mm): 내시경 무피판 TSFE + PRF + 동시 12mm 임플란트 식립; 식립 ISQ=72.6; 12주에 최종 보철 완료

## Summary
Case report (26F, #16, RBH **3.5 mm**): endoscopic flapless TSFE + PRF (no xenograft) + simultaneous 12 mm implant. **Placement ISQ = 72.6** (mean of 5 directions). Crown delivered at 12 weeks. Demonstrates feasibility of TSFE below traditional 4–5 mm RBH threshold.

## Procedure Summary

| Step | Detail |
|---|---|
| Approach | Endoscopically controlled, flapless transcrestal |
| Membrane elevation | Two-step under direct vision |
| Graft | PRF clots only (no xenograft / autograft) |
| Implant | 12 mm length, placed simultaneously |
| Loading | Crown at 12 weeks |

## ISQ at Placement
- **Mean 72.6** across 5 directions
- Above Oh-2008's immediate/early-loading threshold (>70)
- Achieved despite RBH 3.5 mm because:
  1. Apical engagement in compacted PRF + condensed bone
  2. Endoscope-guided controlled lift
  3. No membrane perforation (verified visually)

## Clinical Relevance
- Endoscope **extends TSFE indication below 4 mm RBH**
- PRF alone may substitute xenograft when primary stability is achievable
- 12-week loading possible when initial ISQ >70 (but n=1 evidence — not generalizable)
- Equipment cost + learning curve are practical barriers

## Caveats — Evidence Strength
- **n=1**, no control, no long-term follow-up
- One favorable patient (young, healthy, no contraindications)
- Cannot conclude "RBH 3.5 mm safe for TSFE" in general
