## 한줄요약
TSFE 25년 scoping review (37논문): 4가지 transcrestal 술식 분류; 5년 이상 방사선적 골소실·실패율·ISQ·합병증 종합

## Summary
PRISMA-ScR scoping review of 25-year TSFE literature. 633 records → 37 included. Identifies **4 treatment modalities** (osteotome + 3 alternatives). Primary endpoints: radiographic bone levels + implant failure >5 yr; secondary: ISQ at placement + complications.

## TSFE Modality Landscape
1. Traditional osteotome (control reference)
2. + 3 alternative modalities (osseodensification, balloon, hydraulic, etc. — see paper for full taxonomy)

## Why This Paper Matters
- **Synthesizes 25 years of TSFE evolution** — useful map of what's been studied
- Primary outcome focus on **5-year failure** is unusually long for sinus lift reviews
- Includes ISQ as secondary outcome — bridges to RFA literature
- Same Michigan group as Stuhr 2025 (long-term review) → consistent framework

## Clinical Relevance
- For evidence-based TSFE decisions, this is the **landscape map**
- For long-term stability framework, pair with [[sinus-lift/transcrestal/stuhr-2025-long-term-stability-transcrestal-sinus-augmentation]]

## Caveats
- Scoping review — no statistical pooling
- Heterogeneous study designs included
- Outcome reporting variability across studies
