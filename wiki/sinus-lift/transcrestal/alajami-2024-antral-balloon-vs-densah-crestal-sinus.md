## One-line Summary
RCT (12-month, posterior maxilla, crestal sinus lift + simultaneous implant): Densah osseodensification (OD) bur gave significantly higher primary stability (p=0.004) and bone density, while the antral membrane balloon achieved greater immediate vertical bone gain that significantly resorbed by 6 months (p<0.0001) — both techniques reached 100% implant success at 12 months.

## 한줄요약
경치조골 상악동 거상 RCT(12개월): Densah(OD)가 1차 안정성(p=0.004)·골밀도 우수, antral balloon은 즉시 수직 골높이 이득이 크나 6개월에 유의하게 흡수 — 둘 다 12개월 성공.

## Summary
Randomized clinical trial comparing antral membrane balloon vs Densah (OD) bur for crestal sinus lift with simultaneous implant placement, 12-month clinical and radiographic outcomes.

## Key Contributions
Adds the balloon-vs-OD axis to transcrestal technique selection. Key clinical nuance: the balloon's superior immediate vertical bone gain partially resorbs by 6 months, whereas OD delivers higher primary stability and bone density.

## Methodology
RCT, posterior maxilla, crestal sinus lift + simultaneous implant. Groups: antral membrane balloon vs Densah bur. Outcomes over 12 months: implant success, primary/secondary stability, vertical bone height, bone density.

## Results
- All implants successful at 12 months.
- Primary stability: significantly favored Densah (p = 0.004); no difference at 6 months (p = 0.07).
- Vertical bone height: balloon higher immediately (p < 0.0001), significant reduction by 6 months (p < 0.0001).
- Bone density: Densah significantly higher (p ≤ 0.05).
