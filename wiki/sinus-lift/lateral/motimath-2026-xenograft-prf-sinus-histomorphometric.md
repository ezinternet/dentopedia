## One-line Summary

Split-mouth prospective RCT (n=30, 60 sinuses, 9 months): A-PRF+DBBM significantly increased new bone formation (42.8% vs 31.2%, p=0.001) and reduced residual graft (28.4% vs 41.6%, p=0.001) vs DBBM alone; 100% implant survival both groups.

## Summary

This prospective, randomized, split-mouth RCT (March 2021–December 2023; n=30 patients, 60 sinuses) compared DBBM+A-PRF (test) versus DBBM alone (control) in lateral window sinus augmentation for patients with residual bone height ≤4 mm. A-PRF was prepared at 2700 rpm/12 min (Choukroun low-speed protocol) with liquid i-PRF (700 rpm/3 min). Sinus height, bone density (CBCT, HU), postoperative morbidity (VAS), and histomorphometry (H&E + trichrome; 9-month biopsy) were assessed. Implants (Straumann Bone Level Tapered SLActive) were placed simultaneously where residual bone height >3 mm and primary stability >25 Ncm, loaded 4–6 months later.

At 9 months, A-PRF+DBBM showed significantly greater new bone formation (42.8 ± 6.4% vs 31.2 ± 5.9%; p=0.001), significantly less residual graft material (28.4 ± 4.7% vs 41.6 ± 6.1%; p=0.001), improved bone density, and better graft height stability. Postoperative morbidity was also reduced in the A-PRF group. Both groups achieved 100% implant survival.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrates A-PRF benefit in sinus augmentation histomorphometry: ~37% relative increase in new bone, ~32% relative reduction in residual graft
- Uses A-PRF specifically (2700 rpm, 12 min) — distinct from classical L-PRF; lower centrifugation is hypothesized to preserve more viable platelets and growth factors
- Split-mouth design controls for inter-patient variability
- First study to specifically address A-PRF (not generic PRF/L-PRF) in sinus augmentation with full histomorphometric analysis

## Methodology

- Design: Prospective, randomized, controlled, split-mouth RCT
- N: 30 patients; 60 sinuses (30 test, 30 control)
- Test: 1.5 g DBBM + 4 A-PRF membranes + 2 tubes i-PRF
- Control: 2.0 g DBBM (Bio-Oss, 0.25–1 mm)
- PRF protocol: A-PRF (2700 rpm, 12 min, compressed 8 min); i-PRF (700 rpm, 3 min)
- Biopsy: 3.0 mm trephine at 9 months; H&E + Masson trichrome; Image-Pro Plus 7.0
- CBCT: pre-op, immediate post-op, 9 months
- Blinded histopathologist
