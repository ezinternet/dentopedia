## One-line Summary

Retrospective CBCT study (n=29, mean follow-up 3.3y) demonstrated 100% implant and grafting survival despite 93.1% prevalence of mucosal thickening (65.5% severe >5mm); periodontal disease history was the sole independent predictor.

## 한줄요약

CBCT 후향적 연구(n=29, 평균 추적 3.3년): 93.1%에서 점막비후(65.5%가 중증 >5mm)에도 임플란트·골이식 생존율 100%, 치주질환 과거력만이 유의한 예측인자.

## Summary

This University of Michigan retrospective study addressed a key clinical question: does preexisting maxillary sinus mucosal thickening compromise implant and grafting success after sinus floor elevation (SFE)? With stringent inclusion criteria (n=29 from 4,000 screened), all cases showed 100% implant survival regardless of mucosal thickening severity.

**Mucosal Thickening Index** (proposed by this study):

| Category | Threshold | Prevalence in cohort |
|---|---|---|
| No thickening | ≤1mm | 6.9% |
| Minimal | >1-2mm | 6.9% |
| Moderate | >2-5mm | 20.7% |
| Severe | >5mm | 65.5% |

Mean maximum thickening: 8.34±5.70mm (range 1.55-22.81mm). Distribution: middle section highest (4.87mm), posterior section lowest (3.46mm).

**Critical clinical conclusion**: Physiologic mucosal thickening — even severe — is NOT a contraindication for SFE and implant placement, provided no active sinusitis or periapical pathosis is present.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed 4-tier "Mucosal Thickening Index" (widely referenced)
- 100% implant survival with physiologic mucosal thickening up to 1/3 of sinus volume
- Periodontal disease identified as the single significant predictor of mucosal thickening (p=0.004)
- Clinical recommendation: consult ENT if sinusitis suspected; otherwise proceed with SFE

## Methodology

- Retrospective CBCT (i-CAT), University of Michigan 2004-2014
- n=29 (11F, 18M), mean follow-up 3.3±2.2y (range 1-7y)
- Measurements at ¼, ½, ¾ of AP sinus width + maximum point (sagittal views)
- Linear mixed models; intra-examiner agreement 99%
- Variables tested: age, gender, smoking, respiratory/cardiovascular/DM, periodontal disease, endodontic treatment, ridge height

## Results

- **Implant survival**: 100% (all cases, including severe thickening)
- **Significant predictor**: periodontal disease history (p=0.004)
- **Not significant**: gender (p=0.054), age, smoking (p=0.541), endodontic treatment (p=0.379), ridge height (p=0.889)
- No sinusitis/periapical pathosis visible in any included case — cases with pathologic thickening excluded
