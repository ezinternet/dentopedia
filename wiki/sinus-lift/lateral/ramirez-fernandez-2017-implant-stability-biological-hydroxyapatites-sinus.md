## One-line Summary

Split-mouth prospective study (10 patients, 60 implants) quantifying ISQ trajectory after sinus lift with bovine HA (BBM) vs porcine HA (PBM): BBM 63.8 (placement) → 73.5 (3 mo) → 74.65 (6 mo); higher graft crystallinity (BBM) drives faster early stability gain, with PBM catching up by 6 months.

## 한줄요약
분할구강 전향 (10명, 60임플란트): ISQ 실측 — T0(식립)=63.8 → T2(3mo)=73.5 → T3(6mo)=74.65(BBM); 결정화도↑→ 초기 안정성 빠른 증가

## Summary
Split-mouth prospective (10 patients, 60 implants, two-stage). **Reports quantified ISQ trajectory T1→T2→T3 (0/3/6 mo)** after sinus lift with bovine HA (BBM) vs porcine HA (PBM). BBM with high crystallinity/low porosity reaches ISQ>70 by 3 mo; PBM catches up by 6 mo.

## ISQ Trajectory — Quantified

| Time | BBM (bovine HA) | PBM (porcine HA) |
|---|---|---|
| **T1 (placement)** | **63.8 ± 2.97** | **62.6 ± 2.11** |
| **T2 (3 mo)** | **73.5 ± 4.21** | **67.0 ± 4.99** |
| **T3 (6 mo)** | **74.65 ± 2.93** | **72.9 ± 2.63** |

ΔISQ (T1→T3): BBM **+10.85**, PBM **+10.3**

## Key Insight
- **Two-stage** sinus lift (6-mo healing → implant) gives baseline ISQ in the **60s** (acceptable range)
- 3 months further healing → typically crosses **70 threshold** (Oh-2008 loading-safe)
- **Graft chemistry matters at 3 months**: BBM (sintered, high crystallinity) ahead of PBM, but the difference narrows by 6 months
- Stable peri-apical crystallinity → faster BIC formation → faster ISQ climb

## Clinical Relevance
- For **two-stage protocol**: expect baseline ISQ ~63, mid-healing (3 mo) ~67–74, loading-ready (6 mo) ~73–75
- **HA with higher crystallinity** preferred when faster early loading is goal
- Combine with [[sinus-lift/lateral/allurkar-2025-implant-stability-residual-bone-macrodesign]]: macro-design (TE) raises baseline ISQ; graft chemistry shapes mid-healing slope
- For one-stage with immediate placement, baseline ISQ likely lower; cf. [[sinus-lift/transcrestal/liu-2018-endoscopically-controlled-flapless-transcrestal-sinus]] (ISQ 72.6 at placement with PRF + immediate)

## Caveats
- n=10 (small)
- HA-only comparison — no β-TCP or autograft arm here
- No stability dip observation (only 3 timepoints)
