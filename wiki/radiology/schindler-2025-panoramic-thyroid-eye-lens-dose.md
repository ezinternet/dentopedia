## One-line Summary

Phantom dosimetry across 4 panoramic units: thyroid equivalent dose 0.30–1.46 µSv, eye lens 0.88–4.24 µSv, with significant device differences (p<0.001).

## 한줄요약

파노라마 4기종 팬텀 선량: 갑상선 등가선량 0.30~1.46 µSv, 수정체 0.88~4.24 µSv. 기기간 유의차(p<0.001).

## Summary

Absolute organ doses are very low and device-dependent, reinforcing that thyroid/eye doses from panoramic are small — context for shielding-discontinuation guidance.

## Key Contributions

- Thyroid equivalent dose 0.30–1.46 µSv; eye lens 0.88–4.24 µSv across 4 modern panoramic devices.
- Significant inter-device differences (Friedman p<0.001); anterior dose follows the fan-beam rotational isocenter trajectory.
- Radiochromic-film mapping from orbital floor to thyroid characterizes the dose distribution.

## Methodology

Female Alderson-Rando phantom with 3D-printed slabs; ionization chamber in orbit and thyroid + 5 radiochromic films; repeated exposures on Orthophos XG Plus, ProMax 2D Classic, Viso G7, Veraview X800.

## Results

Absolute organ doses are very low and device-dependent, reinforcing that thyroid/eye doses from panoramic are small — context for shielding-discontinuation guidance.
