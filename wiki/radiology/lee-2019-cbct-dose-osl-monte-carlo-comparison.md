## One-line Summary

OSLD vs Monte Carlo CBCT dosimetry across 2 units/4 modes; effective-dose discrepancy 4.0–14.3%, narrowing as FOV shrinks.

## 한줄요약

CBCT 선량 OSLD vs 몬테카를로(2기종·4모드). 유효선량 차이 4.0~14.3%, FOV 작을수록 감소.

## Summary

Both methods are usable; choice is a practicality/efficiency trade-off, with closer agreement at smaller FOV — informs how clinics interpret published CBCT dose figures.

## Key Contributions

- Head-to-head OSLD measurement vs PCXMC Monte Carlo across CS9300 and RAYSCAN α+, 2 modes each.
- OSLD effective dose generally higher than MC (except CS9300 dual-jaw); percent difference 4.0–14.3%.
- Method discrepancy shrinks as FOV decreases; organ-dose patterns broadly consistent between methods.

## Methodology

22 calibrated OSLDs in head-and-neck anthropomorphic phantom; organ + effective dose computed and compared with PCXMC MC for each unit/mode.

## Results

Both methods are usable; choice is a practicality/efficiency trade-off, with closer agreement at smaller FOV — informs how clinics interpret published CBCT dose figures.
