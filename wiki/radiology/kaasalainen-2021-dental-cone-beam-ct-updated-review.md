## One-line Summary

Medical-physics review of dental CBCT: scanner technical features, dose optimization, dosimetry methods, and diagnostic reference levels (DRLs).

## 한줄요약

치과 CBCT 의학물리 리뷰: 스캐너 기술특성, 선량 최적화, 선량측정법, 진단참고수준(DRL) 종합.

## Summary

Establishes CBCT as a heterogeneous 3D modality whose dose/quality must be managed through optimization and DRLs; identifies unresolved issues for clinically more effective, patient-optimized exams.

## Key Contributions

- Summarizes technical features of current dental CBCT scanner models and recent detector/scan-mode developments.
- Reviews dose optimization, dosimetry approaches, and the role of diagnostic reference levels (DRLs).
- Frames regulatory/justification perspective for optimized, patient-benefit CBCT.

## Methodology

Narrative medical-physics review spanning acquisition technology, image quality, dosimetry, and regulatory context.

## Results

Establishes CBCT as a heterogeneous 3D modality whose dose/quality must be managed through optimization and DRLs; identifies unresolved issues for clinically more effective, patient-optimized exams.
