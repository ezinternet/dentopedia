## One-line Summary

Narrative review giving justification/optimization recommendations for CBCT in oral implantology; effective dose spans 2–200 panoramic equivalents across machines.

## 한줄요약

임플란트 CBCT의 정당화·최적화 권고 내러티브 리뷰. 기기별 유효선량이 파노라마 2~200장 범위로 편차.

## Summary

Wide machine-dependent variability in both dose and diagnostic image quality precludes transferring evidence from one CBCT system to another; optimization must be machine-dependent, patient-specific, and indication-oriented.

## Key Contributions

- CBCT justified for presurgical diagnosis, preoperative planning, and peroperative transfer (guides/navigation/3D printing).
- Quantifies dose spread: effective dose ≈ 2–200 panoramic radiographs even for similar indications.
- Reports accuracy limits: segmentation/linear accuracy ~200 µm achievable but up to ~5× worse depending on machine; degraded by motion/metal artefacts.

## Methodology

Narrative review of evidence on dose, image quality, segmentation/linear accuracy, and therapeutic (surgical-guide/prosthetic) applications of dental CBCT in implant dentistry.

## Results

Wide machine-dependent variability in both dose and diagnostic image quality precludes transferring evidence from one CBCT system to another; optimization must be machine-dependent, patient-specific, and indication-oriented.
