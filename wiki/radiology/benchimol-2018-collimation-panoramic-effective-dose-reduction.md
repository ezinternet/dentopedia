## One-line Summary

Phantom MOSFET dosimetry (full panoramic 17.6 µSv) + 252-referral audit: collimated panoramic protocols cut dose without losing diagnostic info.

## 한줄요약

팬텀 MOSFET 선량측정(풀 파노라마 17.6 µSv)+의뢰 252건 분석: collimation 파노라마가 진단정보 손실 없이 선량저감.

## Summary

Because most diagnostic tasks need only part of the panoramic field, collimation should be applied routinely whenever the task allows.

## Key Contributions

- Full-size panoramic effective dose 17.6 µSv (ICRP 2007) measured with MOSFET on Rando phantom.
- 9 collimation protocols mapped to common diagnostic questions.
- Retrospective 252-referral audit shows many exams allow collimation → routine dose savings without diagnostic loss.

## Methodology

MOSFET dosimetry of full + 9 collimated panoramic protocols on Planmeca ProMax 2D (10 exposures each); retrospective audit of 252 specialist-clinic referrals categorized by indication and achievable collimation.

## Results

Because most diagnostic tasks need only part of the panoramic field, collimation should be applied routinely whenever the task allows.
