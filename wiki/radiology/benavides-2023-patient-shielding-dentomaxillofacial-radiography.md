## One-line Summary

AAOMR position statement: discontinue gonadal/pelvic/fetal AND thyroid shielding for intraoral, panoramic, cephalometric, and CBCT imaging.

## 한줄요약

AAOMR 합의 권고: 구내·파노라마·측모두부·CBCT에서 생식선/골반/태아 차폐 및 갑상선 차폐 모두 중단.

## Summary

On contemporaneous-dose evidence, contact shielding provides negligible benefit and can interfere with diagnosis; unequivocal guidance to stop routine patient shielding.

## Key Contributions

- Recommends discontinuing gonadal, pelvic, and fetal shielding for all dentomaxillofacial radiography (no radiation-induced heritable effects in humans; negligible gonad/fetus dose).
- Recommends NOT using thyroid shielding for intraoral, panoramic, cephalometric, and CBCT imaging (negligible thyroid cancer risk; collars can degrade images / require retakes).
- Calls for state/local regulators to update rules accordingly.

## Methodology

Ad hoc committee review of radiation-protection monographs and studies reporting gonad, breast, and thyroid doses from dentomaxillofacial imaging.

## Results

On contemporaneous-dose evidence, contact shielding provides negligible benefit and can interfere with diagnosis; unequivocal guidance to stop routine patient shielding.
