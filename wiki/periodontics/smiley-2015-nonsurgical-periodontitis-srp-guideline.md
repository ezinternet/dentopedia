## One-line Summary
ADA evidence-based guideline (SR+MA) on nonsurgical treatment of chronic periodontitis: SRP strongly recommended as first-line; systemic sub-/doxycycline and local antimicrobials give modest adjunctive benefit (PPD 0.2–0.4mm).

## 한줄요약
만성 치주염 비수술 치료(SRP ± 보조) 근거기반 가이드라인 — SRP 강력 권고, 전신 독시사이클린 추가 PPD 0.2–0.4mm 감소.

## Summary
Evidence-based clinical practice guideline on SRP with or without adjuncts for chronic periodontitis.

## Key Contributions
- SRP alone reduces PPD 1–2mm, gains 0.5–1mm CAL
- Systemic doxycycline adjunct: additional PPD reduction 0.2–0.4mm, CAL gain 0.3mm
- Local delivery antimicrobials (minocycline, chlorhexidine chip): effective at PPD ≥5mm sites

## Methodology
Systematic review + meta-analysis + expert consensus panel (ADA).

## Results
SRP is strongly recommended as first-line for chronic periodontitis. Doxycycline adjunct beneficial especially for PPD ≥5mm. SDD (subantimicrobial doxycycline) also supported.
