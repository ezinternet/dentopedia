## 한줄요약
EFP S3 수준 임상 지침(15개 SR 기반): 1기–3기 치주염의 단계적 치료(행동 변화 → SRP ± 보조요법 → 수술 → SPT) 표준 프로토콜.

## Summary
The European Federation of Periodontology (EFP) S3 level clinical practice guideline for the treatment of stage I–III periodontitis, based on 15 commissioned systematic reviews and GRADE methodology. The guideline introduces a stepwise approach to therapy aligned with the 2018 periodontitis classification. A companion guideline for stage IV periodontitis was published separately (Herrera 2022).

## Key Contributions
- First comprehensive EFP S3 CPG for periodontitis treatment following 2018 stage/grade classification
- Systematic, stepwise therapy framework with level-of-evidence grading
- Distinguishes between steps, not just procedures, creating a structured pathway
- Highlights SPT as indispensable fourth step

## Stepwise Treatment Framework
| Step | Interventions |
|------|--------------|
| 1 | Behavioural change; supragingival biofilm control; risk factor management (smoking, diabetes) |
| 2 | Subgingival instrumentation (SRP); adjunctive systemic/local antibiotics in selected high-risk cases |
| 3 | Periodontal surgery (resective/regenerative) if step 2 goals unmet at re-evaluation |
| 4 | Supportive periodontal therapy (SPT) — recall 3–12 months, individually tailored |

## Key Recommendations
- Systemic antibiotics: adjunct to SRP for stage III grade C/D only; not stages I/II
- Local antibiotics and antiseptics: limited use in specific sites
- Photodynamic therapy/lasers: insufficient evidence for routine recommendation
- Full-mouth disinfection protocols: comparable efficacy to quadrant-by-quadrant SRP
