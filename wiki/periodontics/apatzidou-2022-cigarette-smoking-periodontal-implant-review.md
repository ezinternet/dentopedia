## 한줄요약
Periodontology 2000 review (Apatzidou 2022): 흡연이 치주질환과 임플란트 결과에 미치는 영향의 기전 종합 — neutrophil 기능 손상, MMPs 상승, 치주미생물군 변화, 조직 항상성 손상. Periodontitis 2-6배, peri-implantitis 위험 유의 증가의 mechanistic 근거.

## Summary
Periodontology 2000 narrative review providing the mechanism-focused complement to the clinical SR cluster (Naseri/Mustapha/Fan/Caggiano). Integrates inflammatory + host-response perturbation, subgingival microbial community shifts (using deep sequencing and bioinformatics), and compromised tissue healing into a coherent picture of how cigarette smoking damages the periodontal and peri-implant tissue homeostasis.

## Key Contributions
- Mechanism-focused (one of the most-cited reviews on smoking-periodontal mechanism).
- Modern microbiome data integration.
- Local + systemic pathway synthesis.

## Methodology
Narrative review structured around mechanisms; supplementary epidemiologic context.

## Results
[합의수준] Mechanism summary:
- Neutrophil function impaired
- MMPs (especially MMP-8) elevated
- Subgingival microbial community shifts
- Wound healing compromised → impaired tissue regeneration
Epidemiologic: periodontitis risk ~2-6x; peri-implantitis risk significantly elevated.

## Limitations
- Narrative (not systematic)
- Source data heterogeneity inherent to reviews
