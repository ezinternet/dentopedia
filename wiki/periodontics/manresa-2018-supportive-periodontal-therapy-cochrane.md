## 한줄요약
SPT 효과 코크란 리뷰 — 순응군 치아 상실 RR 0.47, CAL 안정, 3–6개월 간격 권고.

## Summary
Cochrane review evaluating SPT for dentition maintenance after periodontitis treatment: tooth loss, CAL, and recurrence.

## Key Contributions
- SPT compliant patients: tooth loss RR 0.47 over 10 years
- Clinical attachment level stability maintained with regular SPT
- 3–6 month recall interval supported by available evidence

## Methodology
Cochrane SR; RCT and quasi-experimental studies.

## Results
SPT compliance reduces tooth loss by 53% over 10 years. CAL maintained with regular recall. Non-compliance leads to disease recurrence.
