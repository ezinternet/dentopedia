## One-line Summary
In-vitro fatigue-bond-strength study (J Funct Biomater 2022) finding the two-step HEMA-free universal adhesive (G2-Bond Universal) achieves the highest enamel fatigue bond strength in etch-and-rinse mode.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro 피로결합강도 연구 — 2-step HEMA-free universal(G2-Bond)이 E&R 모드 법랑질 피로결합강도 최고.

## Summary
This study compared bond durability via fatigue bond strength (plus SEM of enamel/dentin interfaces) for OptiBond FL, Prime&Bond NT, Clearfil SE Bond 2, G2-Bond Universal (two-step HEMA-free), and Scotchbond Universal Plus. In etch-and-rinse mode, G2-Bond Universal showed significantly higher enamel fatigue bond strength than the others, while Prime&Bond NT showed the lowest dentin fatigue bond strength. The work supports the durability advantage of a HEMA-free two-step universal formulation.

## Key Contributions
- Uses fatigue bond strength (cyclic loading) rather than static bond strength, a more clinically representative durability metric.
- Demonstrates HEMA-free two-step universal advantage in enamel.

## Methodology
In-vitro fatigue bond strength testing + SEM of bonded enamel and dentin interfaces; multiple adhesive categories incl. gold-standard OptiBond FL (E&R) and Clearfil SE Bond 2 (SE).

## Results
E&R mode: G2-Bond Universal enamel fatigue bond strength significantly highest; Prime&Bond NT dentin fatigue bond strength lowest.
