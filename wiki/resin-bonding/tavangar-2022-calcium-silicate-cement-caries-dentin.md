## 한줄요약
in-vitro(Clin Exp Dent Res 2022): 칼슘실리케이트 자가접착형 시멘트 vs 전통 자가접착형 — CaSi 즉시 SBS 낮지만 6개월 우식 상아질 재광화 유도; 전통 시멘트: 즉시 SBS 높지만 재광화 없음.

## Summary
In vitro study comparing calcium silicate-based self-adhesive resin cement vs conventional self-adhesive resin cement for SBS to caries-affected dentin (CAD), with 6-month follow-up for remineralization assessment. Trade-off identified: calcium silicate offers biological benefit (remineralization) at the cost of lower immediate bond strength.

## Key Contributions
- Caries-affected dentin: lower SBS than healthy dentin for both cements initially
- Calcium silicate cement: induces CAD remineralization at 6 months — bonds improve over time
- Conventional self-adhesive cement: higher immediate SBS but no remineralization benefit
- Clinical scenario: conservative preparations preserving CAD may benefit from calcium silicate cement
- TheraCem as a calcium silicate cement: gaining interest as caries-protective luting agent
