## One-line Summary
In-vitro study (Sci Rep 2024) examining how dentin surface treatments and bonding type affect dentin elemental composition and bond strength.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — 상아질 표면처리와 본딩 방식이 상아질 원소 조성·결합강도에 미치는 영향 평가.

## Summary
This laboratory study evaluated the effect of different dentin surface treatments and bonding type (etch-and-rinse vs self-etch approaches) on both the elemental composition of the dentin surface and the resulting bond strength. By pairing compositional analysis with bond-strength testing, it links surface chemistry changes induced by pretreatment to adhesive performance on dentin.

## Key Contributions
- Couples elemental/compositional analysis of treated dentin with bond-strength outcomes.
- Clarifies how surface treatment + bonding mode interact to determine dentin adhesion.

## Methodology
In-vitro: dentin specimens receive different surface treatments and bonding types; elemental composition (surface analysis) and bond strength measured.

## Results
Surface treatment and bonding type both modulate dentin elemental composition and bond strength (treatment-dependent effects).
