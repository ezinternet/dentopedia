## 한줄요약
KU Leuven Van Meerbeek 그룹의 µTBS 연구: 2-step SE Clearfil SE Bond 2(CSE2)는 타액 오염 후 추가 처치 없이도 결합강도 유지 가능, 1-step SE Clearfil Universal Bond Quick(CUBQ)는 Katana Cleaner 또는 CSE2 primer로 decontamination 필요.

## Summary
In vitro µTBS study from KU Leuven BIOMAT (Van Meerbeek group). Two Kuraray adhesives — Clearfil SE Bond 2 (2-step SE, CSE2) and Clearfil Universal Bond Quick (1-step SE, CUBQ) — were tested for bond strength to saliva-contaminated bur-cut dentin under various decontamination protocols: Katana Cleaner (KC, 10-MDP-containing), CSE2 primer (CSE2p), no decontamination (negative control), and no saliva (positive control). Specimens tested immediately (1-week water storage) and aged (50,000 thermocycles). Linear mixed modeling (REML, α=0.05).

**Key finding**: CSE2 (2-step SE) outperformed CUBQ (1-step SE) overall and maintained satisfactory bond strength without separate decontamination. CUBQ required either CSE2p or KC decontamination for full bond recovery on saliva-contaminated dentin.

## Key Contributions
- First head-to-head test of Kuraray's gold-standard 2-step SE vs simplified 1-step SE under clinically realistic saliva contamination
- Validates Katana Cleaner as effective intraoral decontaminant (rivals CSE2p)
- Provides adhesive-specific clinical decision rule
- Reinforces classical Van Meerbeek finding that 2-step SE remains more robust than 1-step SE

## Methodology
- **Design**: In vitro µTBS comparative
- **Adhesives**: Clearfil SE Bond 2 (CSE2, 2-step SE), Clearfil Universal Bond Quick (CUBQ, 1-step SE) — both Kuraray Noritake
- **Decontamination**:
  - Katana Cleaner (KC) — 10-MDP-containing
  - CSE2 primer (CSE2p)
  - Negative control: saliva(–) without decontamination
  - Positive control: clean(+) no saliva
- **Substrate**: Bur-cut human dentin
- **Aging**: Immediate (1-week water) vs aged (50,000 thermocycles)
- **Statistics**: LMM with REML estimation, α=0.05

## Results
- **Overall**: CSE2 > CUBQ
- **Best decontamination**: CSE2p, closely followed by KC
- **CSE2 without decontamination**: satisfactory performance — saliva contamination NOT a significant disruptor
- **CUBQ without decontamination**: bond strength reduced; recoverable with KC or CSE2p

## Clinical Implications
- For 2-step SE Clearfil SE Bond 2: no mandatory separate decontamination after saliva contamination
- For 1-step SE Clearfil Universal Bond Quick: ALWAYS perform decontamination (Katana Cleaner or CSE2 primer) after suspected saliva contamination
- Generalization to other adhesives requires caution — only Kuraray products tested
- Rubber dam isolation remains primary defense; decontamination is a backup
