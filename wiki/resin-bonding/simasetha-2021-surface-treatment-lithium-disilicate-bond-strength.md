## One-line Summary

In vitro (n=70, 7 groups): adding a resin adhesive after silane on HF-etched lithium disilicate was the key variable — experimental silane + Adper Scotchbond adhesive achieved the highest SBS (45.49 MPa); silane alone (KS) was no better than the untreated control.

## 한줄요약

인 비트로 (n=70, 7그룹): HF 산처리된 리튬 디실리케이트에 실란 후 접착제 추가가 핵심 — 실험적 실란+Adper Scotchbond 조합에서 최고 전단결합강도(45.49 MPa) 달성; 상용 실란 단독(KS)은 무처리 대조군과 유의미한 차이 없음.

## Summary

This in vitro study from Chulalongkorn University evaluated seven surface treatment protocols on Vintage LD Press lithium disilicate glass-ceramic (LDGC) prior to cementation with RelyX U200 dual-cure resin cement. All specimens received 4.5% HF etching for 20 seconds followed by ultrasonic cleaning as a baseline. The study compared three silane agents and two adhesive co-applications. Results demonstrated that the combination of experimental silane (pure γ-MPS formulation) with either Signum Ceramic Bond II or Adper Scotchbond Multi-purpose adhesive produced significantly higher shear bond strength than silane alone, while Kerr Silane Primer alone provided no statistically meaningful benefit over the no-treatment control.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrated adhesive layer application after silane as the critical step in optimizing LDGC-to-resin-cement bond
- Showed pure experimental γ-MPS silane outperforms commercial silane primers containing additional co-monomers (BisEMA, TEGDMA)
- Provided SBS benchmarks after thermocycling (5,000 cycles) under ISO 29022:2013 geometry
- Failure mode analysis confirmed higher-SBS groups showed more mixed/cohesive fractures vs predominantly adhesive failures in lower-SBS groups

## Methodology

- Material: Vintage LD Press (Shofu), pressed, 5 mm Ø × 3 mm discs
- All specimens: polished (320–800 grit) → 4.5% HF 20 s → ultrasonic 10 min
- 7 groups (n=10): Control (no silane), KS (Kerr Silane Primer), SGI, SGI/SGII, EXP (experimental γ-MPS), EXP/SGII, EXP/ADP
- Cemented with RelyX U200; stored 37°C/24h; 5,000 thermal cycles (5–55°C)
- Shear bond test; stereo microscope failure analysis
- Statistics: Welch ANOVA + Games-Howell post hoc (α=0.05)

## Results

| Group | Mean SBS (MPa) |
|-------|---------------|
| Control | 18.36 |
| KS | 20.17 (≈ Control, ns) |
| SGI | ~25.6 |
| SGI/SGII | ~30.1 |
| EXP | ~33.5 |
| EXP/SGII | 41.38 |
| EXP/ADP | 45.49 (highest) |

EXP/ADP ≈ EXP/SGII (ns). KS ≈ Control (ns). Adding adhesive after silane is the critical step.
