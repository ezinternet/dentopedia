## One-line Summary
In-vitro study (Materials 2023) finding Clearfil SE Bond and Scotchbond Universal maintain higher µTBS and lower nanoleakage to dentin than Xeno Select after 6-month water aging.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — 6개월 수중 노화 후 CSE·Scotchbond Universal이 Xeno Select보다 높은 µTBS·낮은 nanoleakage.

## Summary
This study evaluated the long-term (6-month water storage) microtensile bond strength (µTBS) and nanoleakage of universal adhesives to dentin. Clearfil SE Bond (with Scotchbond Universal) outperformed Xeno Select, showing higher µTBS and lower nanoleakage after aging, reinforcing the durability advantage of 10-MDP-containing systems.

## Key Contributions
- Long-term (6-month) aging data with both µTBS and nanoleakage endpoints.
- Reinforces 10-MDP system durability versus a weaker comparator (Xeno Select).

## Methodology
In-vitro µTBS + nanoleakage of universal adhesives to dentin after 6-month water storage.

## Results
CSE and Scotchbond Universal > Xeno Select: higher µTBS, lower nanoleakage after 6-month aging.
