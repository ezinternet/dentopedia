## One-line Summary

JPD 2007 in-vitro µTBS study (50 human molars, OptiBond FL + SE Bond, 10 groups): all immediate dentin sealing (IDS) conditions (control, IDS-2W/7W/12W) retained µTBS >45 MPa with no significant difference up to a 12-week provisional period, whereas delayed dentin sealing (DDS) was very low (SE Bond 1.81 MPa) — confirming IDS bond stability provides a safety margin for lab delays.

## Summary

This Magne lab follow-up answers the practical question: if a patient cancels or the lab delays, does the IDS-sealed dentin survive a long provisional period? Using 50 molars across 10 groups (OptiBond FL × SE Bond × C/DDS/IDS-2W/7W/12W), all IDS conditions retained µTBS comparable to fresh direct bonding (>45 MPa) up to 12 weeks. DDS remained catastrophically low, especially with SE Bond (1.81 MPa).

Clinically: a sealed prep can sit with provisional for 1–3 months without requiring re-treatment of the bonding interface — important for indirect workflows with lab turnaround variability or patient scheduling delays.

## Key Contributions

1. Established IDS robustness across a clinically realistic provisional window (12 weeks).
2. Showed SE Bond is unsuitable for DDS (1.81 MPa vs OptiBond FL 11.58 MPa, P=.026).
3. Identified IDS-7W as peak µTBS (66.59 MPa with OptiBond FL) — suggesting 1.5–2 months may be optimal.

## Methodology

50 freshly extracted human molars, 10 groups (2 DBAs × 5 conditions). DBAs: OptiBond FL (3-step E&R) + SE Bond (2-step SE, Clearfil). Provisional: Temp fill inlay. Definitive: composite overlay (Z100). µTBS: 10–11 beams per tooth, 24h after definitive. Stats: 2-way ANOVA + Tukey HSD (α=.05). SEM analysis.

## Results

| Condition | OptiBond FL (MPa) | SE Bond (MPa) |
|---|---|---|
| C (direct) | >45 | >45 |
| DDS | 11.58 | 1.81 |
| IDS-2W | >45 | >45 |
| IDS-7W | 66.59 (peak) | 51.96 |
| IDS-12W | 59.11 | 45.76 |

- All IDS vs C: not different.
- DDS < all other sequences (P<.001).
- OptiBond FL vs SE Bond at IDS-7W (P=.001) and IDS-12W (P=.003).
- Failures: DDS all interfacial adhesive; IDS-7W·12W consistently between resin coating and overlay composite.
