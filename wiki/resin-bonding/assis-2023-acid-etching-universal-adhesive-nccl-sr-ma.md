## 한줄요약
비우식성 치경부 병소(NCCL) 20개 RCT 메타분석 결과, 유니버설 접착제 산부식-세척법(E&R)이 자가산부식법(SE) 대비 중기(12–36개월) 유지율·변연적합도·변연착색에서 우수하나, SE는 술후 과민증이 더 낮음.

## Summary
This PROSPERO-registered SR+MA included 20 RCTs comparing etch-and-rinse (E&R) and self-etch (SE) strategies for universal adhesives in NCCLs. E&R demonstrated better medium-term (>12–36 months) retention, lower marginal discoloration, better marginal adaptation, and lower secondary caries rates. However, SE was associated with lower postoperative sensitivity, representing a clinically meaningful trade-off. Databases searched through December 2021 (PubMed/MEDLINE, Scopus, Web of Science, Cochrane Library).

## Key Contributions
- E&R strategy favored for medium-term retention, marginal adaptation, marginal discoloration, and secondary caries in NCCLs
- SE strategy favored for reduced postoperative sensitivity
- Largest NCCL-specific universal adhesive SR+MA to date (20 RCTs)
- Trade-off between durability (E&R) and sensitivity (SE) informs clinical decision-making

## Methodology
- **Design**: SR + meta-analysis (PRISMA, PROSPERO-registered)
- **Search**: PubMed/MEDLINE, Scopus, Web of Science, Cochrane Library; up to December 2021
- **Records**: 170 after duplicate removal → 20 RCTs included
- **Population**: NCCLs restored with universal adhesive systems
- **Comparison**: E&R vs SE strategy
- **Outcomes**: Retention, marginal discoloration, marginal adaptation, secondary caries, postoperative sensitivity

## Results
| Outcome | Favored strategy | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|
| Retention | Etch-and-rinse | Medium-term (>12–36 months) |
| Marginal discoloration | Etch-and-rinse | Medium-term |
| Marginal adaptation | Etch-and-rinse | Medium-term |
| Secondary caries | Etch-and-rinse | Medium-term |
| Postoperative sensitivity | Self-etch | — |
