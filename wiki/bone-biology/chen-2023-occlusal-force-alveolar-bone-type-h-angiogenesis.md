## One-line Summary
Mouse + in-vitro mechanistic study (J Dent Res 2023) showing that occlusal unloading causes alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis (ABDO) by reducing periodontal-ligament type H vessels (CD31^high/EMCN^high) and coupled OSX^+ osteoprogenitors via a PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 mechanosensory circuit; periodontal recombinant SLIT3 injection partially rescued the vasculature and bone loss.

## 한줄요약
마우스·in-vitro 기전 연구(J Dent Res 2023) — 교합력 소실은 치주인대(PDL)의 type H 혈관과 OSX⁺ 골전구세포를 줄여 치조골 폐용성 골다공증(ABDO)을 유발하며, 기계감지 회로는 PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3; 재조합 SLIT3 국소 주사가 type H 혈관과 골손실을 부분적으로 회복시킴.

## Summary
This study explains *why teeth lose alveolar bone (AB) when they stop chewing* — the converse of occlusal overload. Following occlusal unloading, remodeling becomes net-resorptive, a state termed **alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis (ABDO)**. The authors localize **type H endothelium** (CD31^high / endomucin^high capillaries, the subtype known to couple angiogenesis with osteogenesis) chiefly to the **periodontal ligament (PDL)**, rarely to alveolar bone itself. In hypofunctional PDL, type H vessel density and the **osterix (OSX)^+ osteoprogenitors** that travel with them both drop sharply. Mechanistically the PDL acts as the mechanotransducer: occlusal force is sensed by **PIEZO1**, raising intracellular **Ca²⁺**, stabilizing **HIF-1α**, and inducing the angiogenic ligand **SLIT3** — a circuit validated by applying cyclic compression to 3D-cultured PDL cells (Flexcell FX-5000). SLIT3 is downregulated in disused PDL, and **periodontal injection of recombinant SLIT3 partially rescued type H vessels and AB loss in ABDO mice**, nominating SLIT3 as a therapeutic target.

Note: the retrieved PDF is the publisher abstract/reference landing page (paywalled full text); claims here are grounded in the abstract, and figure-level quantitative magnitudes were not recoverable.

## Key Contributions
- First mechanistic account of ABDO (disuse alveolar bone loss after occlusal unloading).
- Localizes type H vasculature to PDL (not the alveolar bone proper) and shows it is occlusal-force dependent.
- Links type H vessel loss to coupled decline of OSX^+ osteoprogenitors — angiogenesis–osteogenesis coupling in the periodontium.
- Defines the PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 mechanosensory signaling circuit in PDL cells.
- Therapeutic proof of concept: local recombinant SLIT3 partially reverses ABDO.

## Methodology
- In vivo: mouse occlusal-hypofunction model producing ABDO; CD31/endomucin immunostaining for type H vessels; OSX^+ osteoprogenitor quantification in functional vs hypofunctional PDL.
- Molecular: SLIT3 expression in disused PDL; rescue via periodontal recombinant SLIT3 protein injection.
- In vitro: 3D-cultured PDL cells under cyclic compression (Flexcell FX-5000) to validate the PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 axis.

## Results
- Type H endothelium (CD31^high/EMCN^high) concentrated in PDL, rare in AB.
- Hypofunctional PDL → significant decline in type H vessel density and coupled OSX^+ osteoprogenitors.
- SLIT3 downregulated in disused PDL.
- Periodontal recombinant SLIT3 injection partially ameliorated type H vessel dysfunction and AB loss in ABDO mice.
- Cyclic compression of 3D PDL cells confirmed the PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 circuit.
- (Numeric BV/TV and vessel-density values were in the paywalled full text, unavailable here.)

## Clinical Implications (preclinical)
- Mechanistic basis for the long-observed clinical pattern that **non-functional / unopposed teeth and hypofunctional segments lose supporting bone** — and a counterpart to occlusal-overload bone loss (see fan-2018, di-fiore-2022).
- Frames occlusal force as a *positive* maintainer of alveolar bone density through PDL vascular biology, not merely a destructive load when excessive.
- SLIT3 (or upstream PIEZO1/HIF-1α modulation) is a future candidate to protect alveolar bone after tooth loss, in occlusal hypofunction, or around non-loaded implant components — but unvalidated in humans.

## Limitations (Living-document note)
- Animal (mouse) + in-vitro PDL cells; no human translation.
- SLIT3 rescue was partial, not full restoration.
- Quantitative outcomes not available in the retrieved abstract-only PDF — re-extract from full text if numeric values are later needed.
- Implant peri-implant tissue lacks a PDL, so direct extrapolation of this PDL-centric mechanism to implants is uncertain (claude해석).
