## 한줄요약
SR + Bayesian NMA (21 RCT, PROSPERO CRD42024568586): 즉시식립 시 연조직 이식재 (CTG·L-PRF·동종·이종) 비교 — CTG만 STT 유의 증가; SUCRA 순위에서 CTG가 MIBL/KTW/STT/PH/MGML 우위, 단층 이종 콜라겐 매트릭스가 PES 우위; 통계적 유의차 대부분 없음.

## Summary
PICO: in patients with non-restorable teeth receiving immediate dental implants, which adjunctive soft-tissue graft (autogenous/allogenous/xenogenous) best achieves desired hard/soft tissue outcomes vs no graft. 21 RCTs analyzed in Bayesian random-effect network meta-analysis. Outcomes: PES, MIBL, BBT, KTW, STT, PH, MGML (12-24 mo for MIBL, 6-12 mo for others).

## Key Contributions
- 핵심 데이터는 Results 섹션 참조.

## Methodology
PubMed/Scopus/WoS search; only RCTs; Bayesian random-effect NMA; SUCRA ranking. Risk of bias assessed. Search to identify all immediate implant placement studies comparing different soft tissue grafting materials.

## Results
- 21 RCTs included from 903 screened.
- Only CTG showed significantly higher STT gain vs no graft.
- SUCRA: CTG ranked best for MIBL, KTW, STT, PH, MGML.
- Uni-layer xenogenic collagen matrix ranked best for PES.
- No significant difference among grafting materials for most outcomes.

## Clinical Relevance
즉시식립 시 연조직 두께가 얇은 케이스만 CTG 우선 고려; 그 외에는 이종 콜라겐 매트릭스 등 대체재로도 PES 등 심미 결과 충분히 확보 가능. 환자 부담(2차 술부)을 줄이는 의사결정 근거.
