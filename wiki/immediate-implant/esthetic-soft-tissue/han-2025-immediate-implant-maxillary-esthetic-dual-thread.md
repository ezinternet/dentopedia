## One-line Summary

Single-arm prospective study (n=20, maxillary esthetic zone IIP with a dual-thread [upper U-shaped + lower V-shaped] 11° internal-hex IU implant): 85% success / 100% survival after loading, ISQ >70 by 70 days, minimal 1-year buccal-bone loss (−0.34 to −0.48 mm), midfacial recession improved (1.86→1.21 mm), and MBL retained above the implant shoulder.

## Summary

This Seoul National University prospective single-arm study (n=20, 17 analyzed) tested whether a specific implant macrodesign — the **dual-thread IU implant** (Warantec): an **upper U-shaped thread** for easy fixation plus a **lower narrow V-shaped thread** for fixation force, an **11° internal hexagonal connection**, platform switching, and a combined straight/tapered geometry placing the stress point 3.0 mm below bone level — could deliver favorable soft and hard tissue outcomes in **maxillary esthetic-zone immediate implant placement (IIP)** to 1 year (V8). The lower V-shaped thread drove a fast stability climb (ISQ ~60 at 10 days → >70 at 70 days), and the platform-switched 11° connection was associated with minimal marginal bone resorption and, notably, midfacial recession that *improved* rather than worsened. The honest 85% success / 100% post-loading survival split (3 early osseointegration failures, all before prosthesis) and candid multifactorial failure analysis (periodontitis, periapical lesions, narrow ridge near incisive canal) make this a useful macrodesign-specific data point, tempered by the single-arm, short-follow-up, single-manufacturer design.

## Key Contributions

- **Macrodesign-specific clinical evidence** for IIP esthetic outcomes: links a named thread/connection design to measured ISQ, CBCT ridge, MBL, and recession — not generic protocol data.
- **Stability trajectory**: ISQ buccal 60.0→65.0→74.0 and palatal 59.4→64.9→76.4 across V2/V3/V4, average >70 by 70 days, supporting the lower V-shaped thread for early fixation.
- **Tissue stability**: 1-year buccal bone thickness −0.34 to −0.48 mm; buccolingual width −0.63 to −0.78 mm; MBL retained above the implant shoulder; midfacial recession decreasing from 1.86 to 1.21 mm.
- **Transparent failure accounting**: 3/20 early failures with case-level causes, reinforcing the role of preoperative risk assessment over device alone.

## Methodology

- **Design**: Prospective, single experimental group ("extraction + IIP"), no randomization/blinding, no control. Powered on alveolar bone volume change (95% 1-sided CI, α=5%, power 70%; n=20). KCT0008231.
- **Patients**: Single anterior maxillary tooth for extraction + implant; socket ≥2 intact walls; ≥20 teeth; plaque/bleeding index <20%; non-smokers; controlled systemic status.
- **Implant/protocol**: IU implant (SLA, dual thread, 11° internal hex, platform switching). IIP after atraumatic extraction + debridement; ISQ >50 → 1-stage, ≤50 or GBR → 2-stage. Gap/buccal defects grafted with bovine bone mineral (Cerabone) + collagen membrane (Jason). Prosthesis at 90 days.
- **Outcomes**: 3D intraoral scan (Medit; HW-1/3/5, midfacial recession), CBCT horizontal ridge (buccal bone thickness + buccolingual width at 1/3/5 mm; V1→V8), 2D MBL (mesial/distal; V6→V8), ISQ (Osstell RFA, buccal + palatal), ICOI Pisa success/survival, AE monitoring. Paired t-tests, SPSS 21.
- **Visits**: V0 pre-extraction, V1 IIP, V2 10 d, V3 40 d, V4 70 d, V5 90 d prosthesis, V6 180 d, V7 270 d, V8 450 d (1 year post-prosthesis).

## Results

- **Success/survival**: 85% success (3 early osseointegration failures pre-prosthesis); 100% survival after functional loading.
- **ISQ**: buccal 60.0→65.0→74.0; palatal 59.4→64.9→76.4 (V2→V3→V4); average >70 by V4.
- **Soft tissue (HW)**: HW-1 8.68→7.43 (V5, sig)→7.76 (V8, sig); HW-3 10.62→9.74 (V8, sig). Change smaller than the 1.3 mm reported elsewhere.
- **Midfacial recession**: 1.86 (V0)→1.82 (V1)→1.25 (V6)→1.21 (V8); recession decreased (improved) at V6/V8 vs V0.
- **CBCT V1→V8 (all sig)**: buccal bone thickness −0.43 (1 mm) / −0.34 (3 mm) / −0.48 (5 mm); buccolingual width −0.76 (1 mm) / −0.63 (3 mm) / −0.78 (5 mm). Largest change at 5 mm.
- **MBL V6→V8**: mesial −2.13→−1.56 (p=0.053, NS); distal −1.37→−1.78 (p=0.58, NS); bone retained above implant shoulder.
- **Safety**: no AEs beyond 3 failed-implant removals; 0 SAE.
- **Limits**: 1-year follow-up, n=20→17, no control, no bone-defect-wall stratification, single-manufacturer device.
