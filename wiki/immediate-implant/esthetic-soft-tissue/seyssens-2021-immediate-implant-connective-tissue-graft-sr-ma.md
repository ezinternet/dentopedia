## 한줄요약
SR+MA (Cosyn 그룹, 5 RCT + 3 NRS, n=409 임플란트 IIP+CTG 246 vs IIP 163, follow-up 12-?개월): 협측 vertical midfacial 연조직 변화 — IIP+CTG 군이 recession 유의하게 적음.

## Summary
To assess effect of CTG (connective tissue graft) on vertical midfacial soft tissue change at buccal aspect following single IIP. PubMed/WoS/EMBASE/Cochrane to Jan 2020. RCTs and NRSs comparing IIP+CTG vs IIP without CTG, mean follow-up ≥12 mo. MAs on RCT data.

## Key Contributions
- 핵심 데이터는 Results 섹션 참조.
- 자세한 contribution은 원문 Introduction/Discussion 참고.

## Methodology
PRISMA. Two reviewers. RoB Cochrane (RCT) and ROBINS-I (NRS). Random-effects MA.

## Results
- 5 RCTs + 3 NRSs included; 409 implants (IIP+CTG 246, IIP 163).
- IIP+CTG: vertical midfacial soft tissue change significantly smaller (less recession).
- Pooled effect favors CTG by ~0.4–0.7 mm in midfacial level preservation.

## Clinical Relevance
Thin biotype 또는 협측 골 결손 risk가 있는 IIP에서 CTG 동반이 midfacial recession 예방에 유효. Type 1A esthetic zone에서 standard add-on으로 권장하는 group도 있음. 본 SR+MA가 그 근거 제공.
