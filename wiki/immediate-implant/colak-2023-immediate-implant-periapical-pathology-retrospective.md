## 한줄요약
Turkey 후향 연구 (BMC Oral Health 2023, Çolak n=69 환자/124 implants): 만성 근첨병변 (chronic periapical pathology) 발치 소켓에 즉시 식립 — 3 그룹 (G1 즉시 식립, G2 즉시+GBR, G3 sinus lift+즉시) 생존율 비교. 만성 근첨병변 즉시 식립의 contributing 임상 근거.

## Summary
Retrospective Turkish study (Tokat Gaziosmanpasa / Sakarya OMFS, BMC Oral Health 2023) examining the survival rate of immediate implants placed in extraction sockets that had chronic periapical pathology — a long-debated indication where conventional wisdom favored delayed placement after socket healing. The study compares three augmentation/placement strategies:
- **G1**: extraction + immediate implant placement (alone)
- **G2**: extraction + immediate implant + GBR
- **G3**: extraction + sinus lift + immediate implant

Provides outcome data informing whether chronic periapical pathology must always be a contraindication to immediate implant placement.

## Key Contributions
- Outcome data for chronic periapical pathology + immediate placement (a contested indication).
- Three-arm comparison covering common augmentation contexts.

## Methodology
Retrospective; n=69 patients / 124 implants; 3-arm; t-test/ANOVA/chi-square as appropriate.

## Results
[합의수준] Survival rates per group reported in source. Immediate placement in chronic periapical lesion sockets feasible with appropriate debridement + augmentation strategy (specifics in paper).

## Limitations
- Single-center retrospective
- Limited follow-up
- Selection bias (cases chosen by operator)
- Acute infection vs chronic lesion distinction critical — paper addresses chronic
