AI-assisted bibliometric analysis of 157 socket-shield-technique articles (Scopus, 2010–2024) mapping a steadily rising output (especially post-2015), with the USA leading total and international collaborative publications, China most single-country publications, and Germany the highest total and average citations.

## Summary

This bibliometric study mapped the socket shield technique (SST) literature using an AI-assisted Scopus workflow (2010–2024, 157 articles). Publication output has risen steadily since the technique's 2010 introduction, accelerating after 2015. The USA led total and collaborative international output, China produced the most single-country publications, and Germany accrued the highest total and average citations, with early conceptual work retaining lasting influence. The paper provides a research-landscape layer above the clinical-outcome literature rather than new clinical evidence.

## Key Contributions

- First dedicated bibliometric map of the SST field.
- Demonstrates an AI-assisted screening + expert-verification evidence-mapping method.

## Methodology

- Scopus metadata query (2010–2024); AI-assisted screening via Scopus API + manual expert verification; R bibliometrix, VOSviewer, Python.

## Results

- 157 articles; rising output, especially post-2015.
- USA highest overall/collaborative output; China most single-country; Germany highest citations.
