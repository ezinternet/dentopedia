## Summary
1-year prospective study (n=35) of immediately placed and provisionalized HA-coated implants in maxillary anterior single-tooth sites. Demonstrates feasibility of immediate placement + immediate provisionalization with favorable hard and soft tissue outcomes within clinical expectations.

## Key Contributions
- 100% survival at 12 months — no implant failures
- MBL change: -0.26±0.40mm mesial, -0.22±0.28mm distal (significant but clinically acceptable)
- Midfacial gingival recession: -0.55±0.53mm
- Papilla levels: mesial -0.53mm, distal -0.39mm
- Patient satisfaction: 100% (none perceived tissue level changes)
- Immediate provisionals allow contour shaping of emerging profile

## Methodology
- 35 patients; maxillary anterior single-tooth replacement
- HA-coated threaded implants; immediate placement + same-day provisional
- Definitive restoration at 6 months
- Assessments at 0, 3, 6, 12 months (clinical + radiographic)
