## 한줄요약
RCT: 구치부 즉시식립 시 CAD-CAM socket sealing abutment (SSA) 사용으로 점막 emergence profile 형성 — 무피판 sealing 어려운 큰 발치와에 해부학적 healing abutment 적용; 연조직 conditioning 우위 평가.

## Summary
Anatomically formed healing abutments were proposed to address large posterior extraction sockets that are hard to seal without flap reflection, irregular socket anatomy unsuitable for stock healing abutments, and high occlusal stresses precluding temporary implant-supported provisional. RCT compared CAD-CAM socket sealing abutments to standard care during immediate posterior implant placement.

## Methodology
Cairo Univ. RCT, CAD-CAM SSA designed pre-surgery from CBCT + scan and milled chairside; placed at immediate implant placement of posterior sites. Soft-tissue architecture and emergence profile evaluated.

## Clinical Relevance
구치부 immediate implant에서 큰 socket·해부학적 부적합 케이스에 CAD-CAM SSA가 mucoperiosteal flap 없이 sealing과 emergence profile 형성 동시 달성 가능. 디지털 워크플로우 연계.
