## 한줄요약
in-vivo(J Clin Periodontol 2004): 18명 21개 즉시식립, 막/골이식 없이 4개월 — 협측골 56% 흡수, 구개/설측 30%; 즉시식립은 치조제 보존 불가.

## Summary
Prospective human in-vivo study (18 subjects, 21 immediate implants) with 4-month surgical re-entry. No membrane or grafting material was used. Demonstrates that significant buccal bone resorption occurs despite immediate implant placement. Foundational reference establishing buccal wall resorption after immediate implant.

## Key Contributions
- Buccal bone height reduced by ~56% over 4 months
- Lingual/palatal bone height reduced by ~30%
- Marginal gap fills with woven bone — osseointegration confirmed
- Immediate implant does NOT substitute for ridge preservation
- No membrane / no graft: natural healing course documented

## Methodology
- 18 subjects, premolar/molar extraction sites
- No augmentation procedures
- Measurements at implant placement and surgical re-entry (4 months)
- Buccal/lingual/mesial/distal bone height assessed clinically

## Results
| Parameter | Baseline | 4 months |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Buccal bone height loss | — | ~56% |
| Lingual/palatal bone height loss | — | ~30% |
| Gap fill | — | Complete woven bone |
