## 한줄요약
SR+MA (Cosyn, 15 RCT, n=577명, 604 임플란트 IIP+SG 298 vs IIP 306, follow-up 4-36개월): IIP 시 implant-socket gap에 골이식 (SG) 동반 vs 비동반 — hard tissue·일부 soft tissue 보존 유의.

## Summary
To assess effect of grafting gap between implant surface and alveolar socket on hard/soft tissue changes following single IIP. PubMed/WoS/Embase/Cochrane to Aug 2021. RCTs comparing IIP+SG vs IIP only. 15 RCTs included.

## Key Contributions
- 핵심 데이터는 Results 섹션 참조.
- 자세한 contribution은 원문 Introduction/Discussion 참고.

## Methodology
PRISMA. Two reviewers. Cochrane RoB. Random-effects MA.

## Results
- 15 RCTs; 577 patients, 604 implants (IIP+SG 298 in 292 pt, IIP 306 in 285 pt).
- Follow-up 4-36 mo.
- Socket grafting: horizontal hard tissue loss 유의하게 감소.
- Buccal bone thickness 보존, vertical soft tissue change 일부 우위.
- Implant survival 동등.

## Clinical Relevance
단일 즉시식립에서 jump space ≥2 mm 시 골이식 (xenograft 등) 동반 standard. Cosyn 시리즈 4편 중 핵심. Pitman 2023/2022 + Seyssens 2021과 함께 'immediate placement bundle' 의사결정 근거.
