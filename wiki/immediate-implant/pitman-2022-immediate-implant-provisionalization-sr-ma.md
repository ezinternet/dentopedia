## 한줄요약
즉시식립 임시치관 유무 비교 SR+MA — 생존율 동등, 임시치관군에서 연조직 안정성 유리.

## Summary
SR+MA comparing immediate implant placement with vs without immediate provisionalization on survival, MBL, and soft tissue outcomes.

## Key Contributions
- Survival rates equivalent regardless of immediate provisionalization
- Immediate provisional group: better papilla preservation and soft tissue contour
- Buccal gap management (graft material) more predictive than provisionalization alone

## Methodology
PubMed/Medline/Cochrane systematic search; RCT and prospective studies.

## Results
5-year survival ~97% in both groups. Immediate provisional benefits soft tissue esthetics. Jumping gap management remains critical for hard tissue outcomes.
