## 한줄요약
SR+MA: 즉시식립에서 즉시임시치관 (IP) 유무에 따른 PES (pink esthetic score) 중심 연조직 심미 결과 — BoP, PD, plaque index, papilla recession, midfacial mucosal recession 보조 평가; GRADE 적용.

## Summary
PRISMA-compliant SR+MA on PES and secondary soft tissue outcomes (BoP, PD, plaque index, mesial/distal papillary recession, midfacial mucosal recession) of immediately placed implants in anterior maxilla with vs without IP. MEDLINE/Cochrane/Science Direct/GS, English-only, no time limit. Evidence Project RoB tool. GRADE for evidence quality.

## Methodology
PRISMA. Two reviewers. Evidence Project RoB tool. GRADE. Random-effects MA.

(본문 기반: IP 군이 PES 약간 우위; midfacial mucosal recession 감소 경향; 생존율 동등.)
