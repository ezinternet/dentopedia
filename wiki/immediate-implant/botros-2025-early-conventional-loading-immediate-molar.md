## 한줄요약
RCT (n=27 환자, 30 임플란트): 구치부 즉시식립 + 무피판 가이드 수술 후 6주(G1) vs 3개월(G2) 부하 — 생존율 양군 100% (p=1); ISQ/RFA·DCA 차이 없음; 6주 조기부하 가능.

## Summary
PICO: in patients >18 yr, does early loading of immediately placed molar implants result in similar implant survival and MBL as conventional loading? 27 patients, 30 implants placed immediately after molar extraction with flapless atraumatic protocol; restored with screw-retained monolithic zirconia at 6 wk (G1) or 3 mo (G2).

## Key Contributions
- 핵심 데이터는 Results 섹션 참조.
- 자세한 contribution은 원문 Introduction/Discussion 참고.

## Methodology
Non-invasive quantitative ISQ (RFA, DCA) at multiple time points. 6-week (G1, n=15) vs 3-month (G2, n=15) loading. Survival assessed via Kaplan-Meier and log-rank.

## Results
- Survival: 100% both groups (p=1).
- ISQ at prosthetic phase: G1 RFA 74.4 (5.54), DCA 79.07 (5.75); G2 RFA 73.67 (5.7), DCA 78.93 (4.48) — no significant difference.
- No significant ISQ difference between loading time points.

## Clinical Relevance
구치부 단일치 즉시식립에서 atraumatic flapless + 가이드 수술 + 적절한 1차 안정성 확보 시 6주 조기 부하가 임상적으로 안전. 환자 치료기간 단축. 단, 표본 작고 추적 짧음.
