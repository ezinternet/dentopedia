## 한줄요약

## Summary
CBCT-based proposal of classification criteria for inter-radicular septum shape in maxillary molars, with explicit linkage to prosthetic-driven IIP planning.

## Key Contributions
Translates anatomical IRS variations into a clinical taxonomy that supports prosthetic-driven placement decisions — bridge between Smith-Tarnow and modern digital workflow.

## Methodology
Retrospective CBCT review. IRS shape categorization and association with optimal implant trajectory derived from prosthetic plan.

## Results
Defines shape categories and quantifies their prevalence; identifies categories most amenable to predictable IIP.

## Limitations
Anatomy-only; classification not validated against outcome data within this paper.
