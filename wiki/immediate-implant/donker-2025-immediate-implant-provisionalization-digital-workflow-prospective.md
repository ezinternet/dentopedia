## One-line Summary

1-year prospective case series (30 maxillary esthetic-zone patients) of fully digital immediate implant placement and provisionalization (IOS+CBCT planning, static CAIS, prefabricated temporary); 90% implant survival (3 early failures), PES+WES 15.4/20, MBL −0.18 mm (mesial)/−0.44 mm (distal), patient satisfaction 9.2/10.

## 한줄요약

1년 전향 케이스시리즈(심미부 30명) — 완전 디지털 워크플로우(IOS+CBCT 계획·정적 가이드수술·사전제작 임시보철) 즉시식립·즉시 임시수복에서 임플란트 생존율 90%(조기실패 3례), PES+WES 15.4/20, 변연골소실 −0.18/−0.44 mm, 환자 만족도 9.2/10.

## Summary

This prospective case series applied a fully digital workflow — integrated intraoral scanning and CBCT for prosthetically driven planning, static guided surgery, and a prefabricated chairside temporary — to immediate implant placement and provisionalization in 30 maxillary esthetic-zone patients. Restorative and esthetic outcomes were favorable at 1 year (PES+WES 15.4/20, minimal marginal bone loss, stable buccal bone, high satisfaction), but three early implant failures yielded a 90% implant survival rate. The workflow is feasible and esthetically reliable, though immediate-placement biological risk persists.

## Key Contributions

- Feasibility evidence for a prefabricated-temporary, fully digital immediate-implant workflow.
- Integrated clinical, esthetic, radiographic, and patient-reported 1-year outcomes.

## Methodology

- 30 failing maxillary esthetic teeth; immediate placement + bone graft + prefabricated temporary; definitive at 3 months.
- Static CAIS from IOS+CBCT planning; outcomes to 1 year post-definitive.

## Results

- Implant survival 90% (3 early failures); 27 evaluated at 1 year.
- Definitive restoration success 96%; PES+WES 15.4 (2.5).
- MBL −0.18 mm mesial / −0.44 mm distal; buccal bone stable; satisfaction 9.2/10.
