## 한줄요약
무피판 즉시식립 협측 틈새 이종골 충전 RCT — 충전군에서 6·12개월 협측 골 흡수 유의 감소.

## Summary
RCT assessing flapless immediate implant placement with vs without xenograft filling on buccal bone level at 6 and 12 months.

## Key Contributions
- Flapless approach preserves blood supply and soft tissue contour
- Xenograft filling group: significantly less buccal bone resorption at 6 and 12 months
- Both groups: no implant failures reported

## Methodology
RCT; anterior and premolar sites; flapless immediate implant.

## Results
Xenograft filling beneficial even with flapless approach. Flapless technique reduces tissue trauma. Combined approach (flapless + graft) optimal.
