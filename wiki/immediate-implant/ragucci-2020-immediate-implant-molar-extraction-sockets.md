## 한줄요약
SR+MA: 구치부 즉시 식립의 생존율은 통상 ≥97%로 지연 식립과 동등 수준이며, 격중격(inter-radicular septum) 이용·primary stability 확보가 핵심 변수.

## Summary
Systematic review + meta-analysis of immediate implant placement in molar extraction sockets. Pooled survival rates are comparable to delayed placement when primary stability and septum-driven positioning are achieved.

## Key Contributions
First major SR+MA quantifying molar-IIP outcomes; establishes survival benchmarks (~97%) and identifies inter-radicular septum anchorage as a key technique variable.

## Methodology
Systematic literature search; pooled survival and complication rates; subgroup analyses by site (maxillary vs mandibular) and protocol.

## Results
Pooled implant survival ~97–98%, comparable to delayed protocols. Heterogeneity moderate; complication rates dominated by primary stability failures rather than late biology.

## Limitations
Heterogeneity in implant systems and surgical protocols. Limited long-term (>5 yr) data.
