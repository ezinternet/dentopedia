## 한줄요약
소켓실드 vs 이종골 이식 심미부 즉시임플란트 RCT — 협측 골 흡수 소켓실드 -0.28 vs 이종골 -0.94mm.

## Summary
RCT comparing socket shield technique vs xenograft for buccal bone and soft tissue preservation around single immediate implants in the esthetic zone.

## Key Contributions
- Buccal bone change: socket shield -0.28mm vs xenograft -0.94mm (p<0.05)
- Buccal mucosal recession: socket shield 0.18mm vs xenograft 0.52mm
- Both groups: 100% survival at 12 months

## Methodology
Prospective RCT; n=40 (20 per group); 12-month follow-up.

## Results
Socket shield significantly better buccal bone preservation. Soft tissue recession also reduced. Technique-sensitive: requires experienced operator.
