## One-line Summary
Direct head-to-head in vitro comparison of current IOS full-arch accuracy — trueness 50–200 µm, significant inter-device differences, error increases with posterior extension.

## 한줄요약
최신 IOS 전악 정확도 in vitro 직접 비교 — trueness 50–200μm, 기기 간 유의 차이, 구치부 연장 시 오차 증가.

## Summary
Head-to-head in vitro comparison of current IOS accuracy for full-arch impressions using industrial CT as reference standard.

## Key Contributions
- Direct comparison of 4–6 current IOS devices under identical conditions
- Trueness range: 50–200μm; significant inter-device differences
- Error propagation increases in posterior extensions; scan strategy critical

## Methodology
In vitro; standardized arch models; industrial CT reference standard.

## Results
Current IOS trueness 50–200μm range. All devices adequate for single crowns/short-span FDPs. For complex full-arch prosthetics, error must be considered in clinical decision-making.
