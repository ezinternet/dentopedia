## Summary
Narrative review (Ministry of Health, Saudi Arabia) on integration of robotic technology and AI in implant dentistry. Covers robotic precision in implant placement, AI-driven diagnostics and planning, successful case outcomes, ethical considerations, and future directions.

## Key Contributions
- **Robotic systems**: unparalleled placement accuracy; reduces human error; improves treatment outcomes
- **AI algorithms**: analyze patient data for diagnosis, treatment planning, implant design optimization
- Case studies: improved implant survival rates and patient satisfaction demonstrated
- **Challenges**: ethical considerations, balance between human expertise and technology reliance
- **Future**: digital workflow integration; interdisciplinary collaboration essential
