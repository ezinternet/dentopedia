## 한줄요약
IOS 전악 스캔 정확도 SR + 네트워크 MA — 기기별 trueness·precision 순위 제시, 스캔 전략·악궁 연장 시 오차 증가.

## Summary
SR and network meta-analysis evaluating and ranking complete-arch scanning accuracy (trueness, precision) across multiple IOS devices.

## Key Contributions
- Network MA ranks IOS devices by trueness and precision for complete-arch scans
- Error propagation increases with arch length and extended edentulous spans
- Scan strategy (scan path) significantly affects accuracy

## Methodology
PubMed/Embase systematic search; in vitro studies; network meta-analysis.

## Results
Significant inter-device accuracy differences for complete-arch scans. All devices show error accumulation with arch extension. No single device universally superior.
