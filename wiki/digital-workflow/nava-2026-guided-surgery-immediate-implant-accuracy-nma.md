## 한줄요약
Univ Michigan SR + network MA (Clin Oral Implants Res 2026, 18연구·780 즉시 임플란트): 즉시 식립(IIP)에서 모든 guided(static/dynamic/robotic)가 freehand보다 angular·platform·apex 정확도 유의 우수, rCAIS·dCAIS 최상위. 단 비-RCT 다수로 전반 근거 certainty는 LOW.

## Summary
Systematic review and frequentist network meta-analysis of guided implant surgery accuracy restricted to immediate implant placement (IIP) — a context underrepresented in prior NMAs that focused on healed ridges. Eighteen studies (780 immediately placed implants) compared freehand (FH) against half- and fully-guided static (HG/FG-sCAIS), dynamic (dCAIS), and robotic (rCAIS) protocols across angular, platform, and apex deviation.

Every guided protocol significantly outperformed FH on all three deviation metrics, with rCAIS and dCAIS ranking highest. However, RCT-only sensitivity analysis blurred distinctions among guided protocols (and excluded rCAIS entirely), and CINeMA rated overall certainty as low owing to the predominance of non-randomized designs.

## Key Contributions
- IIP-specific guided-surgery accuracy NMA (prior NMAs centered on healed ridges).
- 5-arm comparison (FH/HG-sCAIS/FG-sCAIS/dCAIS/rCAIS).
- By admitting non-RCT designs it could evaluate rCAIS — unlike Schiavon 2025 (RCT-only) — at the cost of lower certainty.

## Methodology
PRISMA-NMA, PROSPERO-registered; 4 databases + manual search; 18 studies (6 RCT, 2 PCS, 7 RCS, 3 Retro), 541 patients / 780 immediate implants; outcomes angular (°), platform & apex deviation (mm); frequentist weighted mixed-effects NMA (REML, random intercept per study; covariates jaw, anterior%, design, RoB); RoB2/JBI/NOS; CINeMA certainty.

## Limitations
- 후향 cohort·case series·single-arm 다수 → CINeMA 전반 certainty LOW (within-study/reporting bias 주도)
- RCT-only 민감도분석선 guided 간 차이 불명확 + rCAIS arm 부재 → robotic 우위는 hypothesis-generating
- software/implant system 이질성, cost-effectiveness 미평가

## Clinical Implication
즉시 식립에서 "guided > freehand"는 근거가 견고하다 [근거강함]. 발치와의 빈 공간이 드릴을 least-resistance 경로로 밀어 FH 편차가 커지기 때문 — IIP일수록 guided 이득이 크다. 반면 robotic이 dynamic보다 낫다는 결론은 잠정적이다 [claude해석]: 숙련자 데이터·후향연구 비중·RCT-only에서 rCAIS 부재 탓. 임상 함의는 "초보 술자나 anterior/open-ended socket처럼 편차 위험이 큰 IIP일수록 guided(특히 dynamic 이상)를 우선" 정도로, 시스템 간 우열은 단정하지 않는다.
