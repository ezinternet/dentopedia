## Summary
Systematic review (Harvard/Washington/Tufts/Texas A&M, J Prosthet Dent 2021) of AI applications in implant dentistry across three domains: implant type recognition, success prediction, and design optimization. 17 studies included; all AI models in development phase requiring further clinical validation.

## Key Contributions
- **17 studies** included; 5 databases searched; search to February 2021
- **Three AI domains**:
  1. **Implant type recognition** from periapical/panoramic: accuracy **93.8–98%**
  2. **Osseointegration/success prediction**: accuracy **62.4–80.5%**
  3. **Implant design optimization**: stress reduction 36.6% vs FEA baseline
- JBI Critical Appraisal Checklist applied; quasi-experimental quality
- **Conclusion**: great potential but additional multicenter prospective studies indispensable
