## Summary
Narrative review (Saudi Arabia) on AI transformation in implant dentistry. Covers ML, DL, and ANN applications for diagnostics, treatment planning, and patient-specific strategies. AI promises personalized, efficient oral healthcare but lacks standardized clinical validation.

## Key Contributions
- AI applications: diagnosis, treatment planning, image analysis, implant placement guidance
- **DL image analysis**: enhances precision of implant placement; reduces risks; optimizes esthetics
- **AI-driven data analytics**: patient-specific treatment strategies; improved success rates
- Promise: personalized, efficient oral healthcare transformation
- Limitation: limited standardization across AI models; no single clinical guideline
