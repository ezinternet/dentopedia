## 한줄요약
Univ Zagreb 다전공 narrative review (Dent J 2024): 치과 시술 전 항생제 예방 — IE 고위험군, 인공관절, 임플란트, 면역억제 환자 시나리오별 indication·regimen·AMR 고려사항. 2007/2017 AHA + 2014 ADA 입장 정리.

## Summary
Narrative review across the main dental antibiotic prophylaxis scenarios — infective endocarditis (IE), prosthetic joint, dental implant placement, and immunocompromised patients. Integrates AMR-aware stewardship reasoning with the indication-specific evidence base. Aligns with the 2007/2017 AHA IE prophylaxis recommendations and the 2014 ADA position retracting routine prosthetic-joint prophylaxis.

## Key Contributions
- Indication-by-indication breakdown of when prophylaxis is supported vs not.
- Explicit AMR framing — defaults to "no prophylaxis unless indicated."
- Practical regimen guidance (amoxicillin doses, alternatives for penicillin allergy).

## Methodology
Narrative review, multi-author multidisciplinary (Zagreb).

## Results
[합의수준] Indication summary:
| Indication | Current position |
|---|---|
| IE high-risk patient (prosthetic valve, prior IE, complex CHD, post-transplant valvulopathy) | Prophylaxis indicated |
| IE moderate/low risk | Not indicated (per AHA 2007/2017) |
| Prosthetic joint | Not routinely indicated (ADA 2014) |
| Dental implant placement | Single preop amoxicillin dose may reduce early failure (low-moderate evidence) |
| Immunocompromised | Case-by-case with consultant |

## Limitations
- Narrative not systematic
- EU regulatory context
- Implant prophylaxis evidence still maturing
