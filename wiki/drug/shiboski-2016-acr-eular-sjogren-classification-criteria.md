## 한줄요약
원발성 쇼그렌증후군(pSS) 2016 ACR-EULAR 분류기준 — 항-SSA/Ro 항체 가중치 최고, 민감도 96%.

## Summary
Data-driven consensus establishing the 2016 ACR-EULAR classification criteria for primary Sjögren syndrome, validated in three international cohorts.

## Key Contributions
- 5-item weighted score: anti-SSA/Ro Ab (3), minor salivary gland biopsy (3), ocular staining (1), Schirmer (1), UWSF (1)
- Threshold ≥4: sensitivity 96.1%, specificity 95.2%
- Supersedes 2002 AECG criteria; improves consistency for clinical trials

## Methodology
Multinational cohort data-driven analysis (n=6,608) + expert consensus.

## Results
New criteria superior to AECG in sensitivity/specificity. Anti-SSA/Ro highest weighted item. Validated across three independent cohorts.
