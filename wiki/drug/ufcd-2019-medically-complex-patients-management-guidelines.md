## Summary
148-page institutional clinical practice guideline covering dental management of all major systemic conditions. The most comprehensive single-source reference in this wiki for medically compromised patient protocols. Covers 23 disease categories + ASA classification + vasoconstrictor use.

## 의과 협진 필수 적응증

다음 중 하나라도 해당 시 반드시 의사 협진 후 치료:
- 협심증, 심근경색 병력, 뇌졸중/TIA 병력
- CHF, 고혈압(BP >140/90), 부정맥
- 당뇨, COPD, 중증 천식, 간질(월 1회 이상)
- 간염/간부전, CKD/투석, 부신부전
- 면역억제 상태(HIV, 백혈병, 림프종, 스테로이드 장기 복용)
- 치료 중 사용 약물(항생제, LA, 혈관수축제, N2O, NSAIDs)에 대한 이상반응 위험

## ASA Classification (Dental Modified)

| Class | Description | Dental Approach |
|---|---|---|
| I | Healthy | Normal care |
| II | Mild systemic disease, well-controlled | Normal care + monitoring |
| III | Significant systemic disease, functional limitation | Precaution; consult if needed |
| IV | Incapacitating disease, constant threat to life | Emergency only; hospital setting |
| V | Moribund (not expected to survive >24h) | Palliative only |

## Vasoconstrictor Protocol (Section 23) — 임상에서 가장 자주 참조

| Condition | Epinephrine Use | Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Controlled HTN, IHD | ✅ Safe | 2 cartridges (0.036 mg epi 1:100,000) |
| Uncontrolled HTN | ⚠️ Caution | Minimize or plain LA |
| Unstable angina / Recent MI <6mo | ❌ Avoid | Plain LA or felypressin |
| Uncontrolled arrhythmia | ❌ Avoid | Consult cardiologist |
| Pheochromocytoma | ❌ Contraindicated | — |
| Uncontrolled hyperthyroidism | ❌ Contraindicated | — |
| Patients on non-selective β-blockers | ⚠️ Hypertensive rebound risk | Plain LA preferred |

## Key Protocols by Disease

### Anticoagulants (Section 3)
- **Continue warfarin** for routine extractions if INR ≤3.5
- DOACs: continue; for major surgery — skip morning dose, consult prescriber
- Local hemostasis: tranexamic acid 4.8% mouthwash, oxidized cellulose, pressure

### Bisphosphonates / MRONJ (Section 6)
- Oral BP <3 years, no risks: proceed normally
- Oral BP ≥3 years or any IV BP: drug holiday (2 months before + after), specialist consult
- Staging: Stage 0–1 = conservative; Stage 2–3 = no invasive procedures without specialist

### Diabetes (Section 12)
- HbA1c ≥9% = poorly controlled → delay elective surgery
- Morning appointments; patient must eat; glucose check ≥70 mg/dL before procedure
- Hypoglycemia emergency kit: oral glucose + glucagon injection

### Hypertension (Section 15)
- BP ≥180/110: **defer all elective treatment**
- BP 160–179/100–109: defer elective; emergency with precautions
- BP <160/100: proceed with monitoring

### Infective Endocarditis (Section 16)
- AHA 2007: prophylaxis only for HIGH-RISK cardiac conditions
- Amoxicillin 2g PO (or clindamycin 600mg if penicillin allergic) 30–60 min pre-procedure

### Prosthetic Joints (Section 18)
- AAOS/ADA 2012 joint statement: **antibiotic prophylaxis NOT routinely recommended** for prosthetic joint patients
- Exception: immunocompromised + invasive dental procedure → individual risk/benefit assessment
