## 한줄요약
SR(9편, 2000–2025): 임신 중 치과마취 혈관수축제 비교 — 에피네프린 1:100,000–1:200,000만 허용; 레보노르데프린 근거 불충분; 펠리프레신 옥시토신 유사 작용으로 절대 금기

## Summary
Systematic review (9 studies/guidelines, 2000–2025) comparing vasoconstrictor safety in dental anesthesia during pregnancy. **Epinephrine is the only acceptable agent.** Felypressin is contraindicated (oxytocic effect → uterine contractions). Levonordefrin lacks safety data.

## Vasoconstrictor Safety in Pregnancy

| Agent | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Epinephrine 1:100,000–1:200,000** | ✅ Acceptable | Low dose; minimal systemic absorption; safer than maternal pain/anxiety |
| **Epinephrine 1:50,000** | ⚠️ Avoid | Higher concentration; ↑ systemic absorption risk |
| **Levonordefrin** | ❌ Insufficient data | Potential uterine vasoconstriction; avoid |
| **Felypressin** | ❌ **Contraindicated** | Oxytocic properties → uterine contractions → fetal distress |

## Epinephrine Rationale
- Endogenous epinephrine release from untreated pain/anxiety >> exogenous dental injection dose
- At 1:100,000–1:200,000: systemic absorption minimal with correct technique
- Key: **atraumatic injection + aspiration before injection** → avoids intravascular delivery
- 2 cartridges maximum (0.036–0.072 mg epi) — well within safe range

## Timing of Dental Treatment in Pregnancy

| Trimester | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| 1st | Emergency only; avoid elective (organogenesis) |
| **2nd** | **Preferred for elective procedures** |
| 3rd | Possible with precautions; supine hypotension risk → semi-supine position |

## Clinical Protocol
1. Use **1:200,000 epinephrine** (lower concentration preferred)
2. Maximum **2 cartridges** per appointment
3. Aspirate before every injection
4. Semi-supine position in 3rd trimester (avoid aortocaval compression)
5. Consult obstetrician for complex procedures
6. **Avoid felypressin** (felypressin = Octapressin = common in Europe) — Korean formulations check required
