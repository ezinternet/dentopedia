## One-line Summary
JKDA special article introducing the 2022 Korean sedation guideline to clinical dental audience: focuses on moderate sedation, 15 PICO recommendations, applicable to non-anesthesiologist providers.

## 한줄요약

2022년 발표된 한국 진정·수면 가이드라인을 임상가 관점에서 해설한 JKDA 특집. Moderate sedation 중심, 15개 PICO 권고, 비마취과 전문의 포함 대상.

## Summary

This special article introduces the new Korean sedation guideline (published 2022) to a general dental audience. The guideline targets moderate sedation across the medical system — including non-anesthesiologist providers — and addresses the realistic risk of unintentional progression to deep sedation. It is structured around 15 PICO questions and covers provider training, drug/equipment requirements, patient selection, referral thresholds, fasting, adult/pediatric usage, drug comparison, intraoperative monitoring (respiratory, cardiovascular, sedation depth), pediatric respiratory complication management, and discharge criteria.

## Key Contributions

- Surfaces the 15 PICO question structure of the 2022 Korean sedation guideline.
- Frames sedation as a continuum (moderate → deep), with explicit monitoring and rescue requirements for the transition.
- Aimed at non-anesthesiologist dental practitioners — clarifies institutional and referral expectations.

## Methodology

## Results
