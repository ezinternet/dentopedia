## Summary
Questionnaire survey of Italian dentists examining antibiotic prescribing habits. Documents systematic overprescription, deviation from guideline indications, and a persistent gap between AMR awareness and actual prescribing behavior — the well-known knowledge-behavior gap in antibiotic stewardship.

## Key Contributions
- Snapshot of European (Italian) dental antibiotic-prescribing reality.
- Highlights specific overprescription patterns (prophylaxis without indication, broad-spectrum default, extended duration).
- Provides targets for stewardship education and electronic prescribing guardrails.

## Methodology
Questionnaire to Italian dentists; categorical analysis of indications, drug choice, duration, route.

## Results
[합의수준] Overprescription common; indications frequently not guideline-supported; AMR awareness high but not translated to restraint.

## Limitations
- Self-report bias
- Italian context (regional/cultural prescribing norms)
- No outcome data
