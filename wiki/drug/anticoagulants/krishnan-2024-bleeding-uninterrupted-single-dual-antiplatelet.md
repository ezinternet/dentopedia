## 한줄요약
인도 Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham 비교연구 (Ann Maxillofac Surg 2024): 단일/이중 항혈소판제 지속하 단순 발치 — DAPT가 출혈 시간 다소 길지만 모두 국소 지혈로 통제; DAPT 발치 전 중단 불필요라는 umbrella review 권고와 일치.

## Summary
Comparative single-center study at Amrita School of Dentistry (Kerala, India) evaluating bleeding time after dental extractions in patients on uninterrupted single (SAPT) or dual antiplatelet therapy (DAPT). Confirms — for Indian population — that DAPT need not be discontinued for routine extractions; local hemostatic measures suffice.

## Key Contributions
- South Asian real-world data on uninterrupted DAPT extractions.
- Quantifies the bleeding-time differential between SAPT and DAPT without clinical bleeding sequelae.

## Methodology
Comparative observational study, dental extraction in patients on continuing SAPT vs DAPT; primary outcome bleeding time, secondary outcome clinically significant bleeding.

## Results
Both groups: extractions completed without clinically significant bleeding; local hemostasis (oxidized cellulose / Surgicel + sutures) sufficient. DAPT bleeding time longer than SAPT but within manageable range.

## Limitations
- Single center, small sample, observational (no randomization)
- No DOAC or triple-therapy comparator
- No long-term follow-up
