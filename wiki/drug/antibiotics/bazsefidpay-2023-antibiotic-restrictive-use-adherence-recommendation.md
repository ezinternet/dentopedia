## One-line Summary
Swedish Karolinska cross-sectional study (BMC Oral Health 2023): adherence to restrictive dental antibiotic guidelines and safety evaluation — restrictive prescribing confirmed safe, adherence variability present, targeted intervention needed.

## 한줄요약
스웨덴 Karolinska (BMC Oral Health 2023): 제한적 (restrictive) 치과 항생제 가이드라인 adherence + safety 평가 — 제한적 처방 안전성 확인, adherence 변동성 존재, 타겟 intervention 필요성.

## Summary
Swedish Karolinska Institutet observational study assessing adherence to restrictive antibiotic prescribing guidelines in dentistry and the safety profile of the restrictive approach. Confirms that restrictive prescribing — long the Swedish norm — is clinically safe and identifies adherence gaps as the next intervention target.

## Key Contributions
- Swedish national-level data supporting the restrictive-prescribing approach.
- Demonstrates safety of restrictive use (no excess complications).
- Identifies adherence variability as the implementation challenge.

## Methodology
Adherence audit + complication monitoring within Swedish restrictive prescribing context.

## Results
[합의수준] Restrictive prescribing safe; adherence variable across dentists/practices.

## Limitations
- Single country; Sweden has long-established restrictive prescribing culture
- Observational
