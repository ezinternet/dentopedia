## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - 핵심: 임플란트 정착(Osseointegration)의 **기초 생물학·역사(Axis 1)**와 그 수치에 걸어야 할 **근거품질 할인계수(Axis 2)**를 5편으로 묶은 anchor overview — 기초 3편(buser·bosshardt·gaviria) + 방법론 2편(musskopf·popelut). lee(짧은임플란트)·mohammed(교합력-ISQ)는 각자 제자리에 둠.
> - 기원사는 정착: **1957 Brånemark**(Gothenburg, 기계가공 티타늄, "osseointegration") + **Schroeder**(Bern, ITI 티타늄 플라즈마분사 TPS, "functional ankylosis") 이원 창시 학파에 동등 credit(buser); Mayan 600 AD → 1965 첫 사람 fixture(>40년 생존) → 1982 FDA·Toronto Conference(gaviria).
> - 표면 거칠기가 합의된 master-variable: **기계가공 Sa~0.5µm → 중등도 거칠기 Sa 1~2µm**(SLA·TiUnite)로 전환, **SLA가 기준(gold standard)**, 양호골에서 **3~6주 조기부하** 가능(buser·gaviria).
> - 표면 생물학 사다리 (bosshardt, %골접촉 BIC): machined < TPS < SLA < **SLActive**(친수성 화학활성) — SLActive가 **2·4주 조기 %BIC 유의 우월**(미니피그+사람).
> - 재료 생물학은 티타늄에서 대체로 해결: **TiZr(Roxolid) ≈ cpTi grade4**(%BIC 무차이), **Ti6Al4V는 열등**(peak %BIC↓ + 다핵거대세포 MNGC↑); 지르코니아는 정착하나 MNGC 더 많고 alumina-toughened ZrO₂는 %BIC↓, **산부식 good·알칼리부식 detrimental**.
> - 미해결 논쟁 2개(buser 명시): **① 임플란트주위염**(환자수준 ~10~20%@≥5년, case-definition 이질성으로 CI 넓음; biofilm vs Albrektsson 이물반응 모델), **② 지르코니아 임플란트**(Y-TZP 전임상 유망하나 2017 시점 장기 RCT 부재).
> - Axis 2 상류 경고 (musskopf SR+MA, 55편): 미니피그 구강내 모델 **pooled BIC 59.88%(95% CI 57.43~62.33)**, 2주 40.93→4주 58.37→>4주 66.33%(p<0.001) — 번역타당성은 있으나 **I²>90% 초고이질성**(주 조정인자=표면·관찰기간) + **보고품질 전반 저조**(masking·표본수 계산 누락).
> - Axis 2 하류 경고 (popelut SR, 41편): **산업연계 실패율 OR 0.21(95% CI 0.12~0.38, 약 5× 낮게 보고)**, unknown-funding OR 0.33, 평균연간실패율 전체 1.09% vs 비산업 2.73%(~3×), **63%(26/41)가 funding 미보고·66% high risk of bias·1개 브랜드가 59% 차지**.
> - 종합 결론: **Axis 1(기초 생물학)은 견고·내적일관**하지만, 거기 붙은 **정량 신뢰도는 보이는 것보다 물렁**하다 — 전임상 %BIC 근거는 이질적·저보고(musskopf), 임상 성공/실패 수치는 문서화된 ~5× 스폰서십 왜곡(popelut).
> - 사용법: 이 페이지는 임플란트 카테고리의 **landing anchor** — 표면·골질·안정성·주위염 세부 overview로 가기 전 "정착 생물학은 무엇이고 그 숫자를 얼마나 믿을 것인가"의 backbone.
> - 시간축 caveat: buser·bosshardt·gaviria는 2014~2017 narrative review라 SR+MA가 아님 — 최신 표면·지르코니아·주위염 정량치는 각 세부 overview(surface·peri-implantitis)가 갱신.

## One-line Summary

Anchor synthesis of 5 papers (3 narrative reviews, 1 SR+MA, 1 SR) organizing implant knowledge into two axes: Axis 1 — the settled osseointegration foundations (dual founding schools, moderately rough Sa 1–2 µm surfaces as master-variable, SLA→SLActive %BIC ladder, TiZr≈cpTi material biology, with peri-implantitis and zirconia as open controversies); and Axis 2 — the evidence-quality discount factor (minipig-model heterogeneity I²>90% and low reporting quality upstream; industry-sponsorship inflation OR 0.21 downstream) that softens the quantitative confidence attached to Axis 1.

## Summary

The implants category holds nearly 200 papers on surfaces, bone quality, stability, and peri-implant disease — but a newcomer needs two things before diving into any of them: **what the settled biology of osseointegration actually is**, and **how much to trust the numbers attached to it**. This anchor overview supplies both from five papers, split into two axes. Axis 1 (Buser 2017, Bosshardt 2017, Gaviria 2014) is the settled foundational narrative; Axis 2 (Musskopf 2022, Popelut 2010) is the meta-level caution — the discount factor to apply when quoting Axis 1's figures.

The intentionally excluded papers matter too: `lee-2009` (short-implant marginal bone) and `mohammed-2026` (bite force × ISQ) are clinical studies that belong to the short-implant and ISQ-stability overviews respectively, not to a foundations page. Forcing them here would dilute the anchor's coherence.

**핵심 명제 (Evidence Propositions)**

### Axis 1 — Osseointegration foundations (settled biology)

1. **The origin story is convergent and settled: two co-equal founding schools.** Buser et al. (2017) credits both Brånemark (Gothenburg, machined titanium screw, "osseointegration") and Schroeder (Bern, ITI implant with titanium-plasma-sprayed [TPS] surface, "functional ankylosis"); Gaviria et al. (2014) supplies the deeper timeline — Mayan mandibular shell implants ~600 AD, Brånemark's 1957 discovery, the first human fixtures in 1965 (serving >40 years), and 1982 FDA approval with the Toronto Conference. The two histories are complementary (Gaviria explicitly `extends` Buser). [근거강함 — 역사 합의]

2. **Surface roughness is the consensus master-variable, with SLA as the reference and Sa 1–2 µm the target.** All three foundational papers converge: the field shifted from machined/turned surfaces (Sa ≈ 0.5 µm) to moderately rough Sa 1–2 µm (SLA, TiUnite). Buser et al. (2017) notes >10 years of high-quality SLA evidence enabling early loading at 3–6 weeks in good bone; Gaviria et al. (2014) calls SLA the "current gold standard." [근거강함]

3. **The mechanistic %BIC ladder is machined < TPS < SLA < SLActive, driven by micro-topography plus surface chemistry.** Bosshardt et al. (2017) shows the chemically activated, hydrophilic SLActive surface yields significantly higher bone-to-implant contact (%BIC) at 2 and 4 weeks (miniature pig + human) than SLA — the surface-biology layer beneath the clinical loading protocols. [근거강함 — 전임상]

4. **Titanium material biology is largely resolved: TiZr equals cpTi; Ti6Al4V underperforms.** Bosshardt et al. (2017) found TiZr (Roxolid) showed no significant %BIC difference from cpTi grade 4, while Ti6Al4V had significantly lower peak %BIC and more multinucleated giant cells (MNGCs) — though MNGCs are present on all biomaterials and cannot predict implant loss. Zirconia osseointegrates but shows more MNGCs than cpTi, with alumina-toughened ZrO₂ giving lower %BIC and etch chemistry mattering (acid beneficial, alkaline detrimental). [근거강함 — 전임상]

5. **Two controversies remain explicitly open: peri-implantitis and zirconia implants.** Buser et al. (2017) frames peri-implantitis prevalence as unresolved (~10–20% patient-level at ≥5 years, wide CI from non-standard case definitions) with competing biofilm vs foreign-body-reaction (Albrektsson) etiology models; Bosshardt reframes part of it as *unexplained marginal bone loss* (titanium particle/ion release). Both agree zirconia (Y-TZP) is preclinically promising but lacked long-term RCT data as of 2017. [합의수준 — 미해결로 명시]

### Axis 2 — Evidence-quality (the discount factor)

6. **Upstream caution — the preclinical %BIC evidence is heterogeneous and under-reported.** Musskopf et al. (2022), an SR+MA of 55 minipig intraoral-implant studies, established a pooled mean BIC of 59.88% (95% CI 57.43–62.33) rising with time (40.93% @2 wk → 58.37% @4 wk → 66.33% @>4 wk, p<0.001) — validating the model's translational relevance — but with I²>90% heterogeneity (driven by implant surface and observation interval) and generally low reporting quality (missing masking, sample-size calculations). The very surface rankings Axis 1 cites rest on a noisy, under-reported base. [근거강함 — SR+MA, 고이질성]

7. **Downstream caution — clinical success/failure figures carry a documented ~5× sponsorship inflation.** Popelut et al. (2010), an SR of 41 trials, found industry-associated studies reported failure at OR 0.21 (95% CI 0.12–0.38) — ~5× less likely — with unknown-funding studies at OR 0.33; mean annual failure was 1.09% overall vs 2.73% in non-industry trials. Critically, 63% (26/41) never reported funding, 66% were high risk of bias, and a single brand appeared in 59% of trials. [근거강함 — SR, 편향 정량화]

8. **Combined: settled biology, softer numbers.** Axis 1's osseointegration narrative is biologically robust and internally consistent, but the quantitative confidence attached to it is softer than it looks — apply the musskopf (heterogeneity/reporting) and popelut (sponsorship) discounts when quoting %BIC or survival figures from the primary literature. [합의수준 — 방법론적 종합]

### 공백·미해결 (Open Questions)

- **시간축**: Axis 1 3편은 2014~2017 narrative review — 최신 표면·지르코니아·주위염 정량치는 각 세부 overview가 갱신(이 페이지는 backbone 역할).
- **임플란트주위염 병인**: biofilm vs 이물반응(Albrektsson) 미해결; unexplained marginal bone loss와의 경계.
- **지르코니아 임플란트**: 장기 RCT 근거 여전히 부족(2017 시점 기준).
- **근거 정화**: popelut 이후 funding 공개·bias 관리가 개선됐는지 후속 검증 필요(이 5편 안에서는 미검증).
