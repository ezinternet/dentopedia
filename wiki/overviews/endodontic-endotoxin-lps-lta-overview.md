> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - **핵심 명제**: 근관 감염의 병태생리는 살아있는 세균뿐 아니라 세포벽 독성인자 — 그람음성 내독소 LPS(Lipopolysaccharide)와 그람양성 LTA(Lipoteichoic Acid, 지질테이코산) — 가 함께 구동한다. 현 치료는 이들을 **유의하게 감소시키나 완전히 제거하지는 못한다(persistent-endotoxin problem).**
> - **임상 상관(Godoi 2025)**: 재근관치료 근단치주염 40치 전부(100%)에서 LPS(평균 5.1 EU/mL)·LTA(평균 537.5 pg/mL) 검출. LPS가 높을수록 근단 골흡수 병소(Periapical Radiolucency)가 크고 증상(Signs/Symptoms)이 동반.
> - **수복 봉쇄 연결(Godoi 2025)**: 결손 치관수복물 → 근관 내 독성인자 부하 증가 → 치관 봉쇄(Coronal Seal) 원칙의 분자 수준 근거.
> - **증상 상관 재확인(Ahmad 2022 overview)**: 내독소 수치는 삼출(exudation)·기왕 통증·타진통(percussion pain)이 있는 증상치에서 유의하게 높음.
> - **활성화 세정 비교(Alquria 2023)**: 70개 단근치 dual-species 바이오필름에서 PUI·XP-endo Finisher·GentleWave 모두 세균·LTA·LPS를 유의 감소시켰고 GentleWave+최소침습기법(MIT)이 최강, XP-EF ≈ PUI. 단 어느 것도 LTA/LPS를 100% 표본에서 제거 못 함.
> - **Ca(OH)₂ 첩약(Bedran 2020 SR+MA)**: 수산화칼슘이 LPS를 화학기계적 형성 전후 유의 감소(SMD 약 −0.9~−1.1, p<0.001)시키나 완전 제거는 불가. 항균제 병용 시 감소 발생률 98.9% vs 단독 61.7%. 근거 확실성(GRADE)은 매우 낮음.
> - **최상위 근거(Ahmad 2022 umbrella)**: 5개 SR 종합 — 화학기계적 형성·Ca(OH)₂가 내독소를 감소시키나 제거 못 하며, 증상성 근단치주염에서 **다회 내원(multi-session) > 단회 내원(single-session)**.
> - **신규 생물학적 첩약(Yoon 2024)**: 역설적으로 LTA를 치료제로 전환 — 프로바이오틱 *L. rhamnosus* GG 유래 LTA(Lgg.LTA, 50 μg/ml)가 사람 상아질 실린더 2주 *E. faecalis* 바이오필름을 Ca(OH)₂와 동등하게 억제(in-vitro, 초기 단계).
> - **개념 프레임(Neelakantan 2018)**: 치수·치근단 감염은 바이오필름 매개이며, 바이오필름 구조 + 다균종 상호작용이 항균저항성과 잔존 독성인자 지속의 근본 원인 — 표적 소독이 치료 목표.
> - **임상 결론**: 내독소·LTA는 측정 가능한 증상·병소 상관 마커이자 치료 표적. 현 표준(화학기계적 청소 + 활성화 + Ca(OH)₂)은 부하를 낮추는 것이지 멸절시키는 것이 아니므로, 증상치에서는 다회 내원·치관 봉쇄 강화가 합리적이며, 완전 제거를 향한 차세대(활성화 시스템·생물학적 첩약) 전략이 필요하다.

## One-line Summary

Six papers converge on one thesis: Gram-negative endotoxin (LPS) and Gram-positive lipoteichoic acid (LTA) are biologically active bacterial cell-wall components that correlate with endodontic symptoms and lesion size (Godoi 2025; Ahmad 2022), and current chemomechanical preparation with irrigant activation (PUI/XP-endo/GentleWave; Alquria 2023) plus calcium hydroxide medication (Bedran 2020 SR+MA; Ahmad 2022 overview) significantly REDUCE but never fully ELIMINATE them — the persistent-endotoxin problem — while an emerging biologic medicament repurposes probiotic-derived LTA as therapy (Yoon 2024), all framed within biofilm-mediated infection (Neelakantan 2018).

## 한줄요약

6편 종합: 근관 내독소(LPS)와 LTA는 증상·병소크기와 상관하는 생물학적 활성 세포벽 인자이며(Godoi 2025·Ahmad 2022), 화학기계적 청소+활성화 세정(Alquria 2023)과 수산화칼슘 첩약(Bedran 2020 SR+MA·Ahmad 2022)이 이를 유의하게 감소시키나 완전 제거는 못 하고(잔존 내독소 문제), 신규로 프로바이오틱 유래 LTA를 치료제로 전환하는 시도(Yoon 2024)가 있으며, 전체가 바이오필름 매개 감염(Neelakantan 2018) 틀 안에 놓인다.

## Summary

### The problem: endotoxins as virulence drivers, not just markers

Endodontic infection is biofilm-mediated (Neelakantan 2018): pulpal and periradicular disease arises from a polymicrobial biofilm whose antimicrobial resistance comes from both biofilm *structure* and the *dynamics of inter-microbial interaction*. Within that biofilm, two cell-wall molecules are the principal pro-inflammatory virulence factors driving periapical bone destruction — **LPS (lipopolysaccharide)** from Gram-negative bacteria and **LTA (lipoteichoic acid)** from Gram-positive bacteria. Disinfection must therefore clear not only viable bacteria but these residual endotoxins; killed bacteria still leave biologically active wall fragments behind.

### Clinical correlation: load tracks symptoms and lesion size

Godoi (2025) quantified both molecules in 40 retreatment teeth with apical periodontitis under rigorous endotoxin-free sampling. **Both LPS and LTA were present in 100% of canals** (mean LPS 5.1 ± 2.83 EU/mL; mean LTA 537.5 ± 134.1 pg/mL). Higher LPS load tracked with the clinical phenotype: larger periapical radiolucency and the presence of pain/signs/symptoms. Defective coronal restorations carried higher virulence-factor load — a molecular-level argument for the coronal-seal principle. Ahmad (2022) independently confirms the symptom link at the top of the evidence pyramid: endotoxin is significantly higher in teeth with exudation, prior pain episode, and pain on percussion.

### Removal by treatment: significant reduction, never elimination

This is the unifying clinical finding across the activation and medication literature:

- **Irrigant activation (Alquria 2023)**: In a 70-premolar dual-species (*E. faecalis* + GFP *E. coli*) biofilm model, PUI, XP-endo Finisher, and GentleWave all significantly reduced bacteria, LTA, and LPS (p<0.05). GentleWave + minimally invasive technique was most effective overall and for intraradicular (cryogenically ground) residual infection; XP-EF ≈ PUI. **But LTA and LPS remained detectable in 100% of samples after disinfection and after grinding** — no protocol eliminated endotoxin.
- **Calcium hydroxide medication (Bedran 2020 SR+MA)**: Ca(OH)₂ significantly reduced LPS both before (SMD −1.087) and after (SMD −0.919) chemomechanical preparation, p<0.001, holding across irrigant subgroups. Adding an antimicrobial substance raised the LPS-reduction *incidence* to 98.9% vs 61.7% for Ca(OH)₂ alone. **Endotoxin was not completely eliminated**; GRADE certainty very low (pre-post designs, heterogeneity).
- **Umbrella synthesis (Ahmad 2022)**: Overview of 5 SRs (2 high-quality by AMSTAR2). Chemomechanical preparation significantly reduces endotoxin; Ca(OH)₂ in symptomatic teeth gives SMD −1.051 (very low certainty, I²=83.3%); and **multi-session treatment outperforms single-session for endotoxin removal in symptomatic apical periodontitis**. The ceiling: no conventional procedure eliminates endotoxin.

### Emerging biologic strategy: LTA repurposed as therapy

Yoon (2024) inverts the frame. LTA is normally a Gram-positive virulence factor and a disinfection *target* — yet probiotic-derived LTA from *Lacticaseibacillus rhamnosus* GG (Lgg.LTA, 50 μg/ml) inhibited 2-week *E. faecalis* intracanal biofilm in a human dentinal cylinder model **as effectively as calcium hydroxide**, reducing live and dead intratubular bacteria, while water and vehicle did not. This is a postbiotic candidate that could sidestep CH's *E. faecalis* limitation and extrusion toxicity. It is early-stage in-vitro evidence — a direction, not yet a protocol.

### The persistent-endotoxin problem

The throughline: every conventional and contemporary modality reduces but cannot abolish residual LPS/LTA, and residual load correlates with worse clinical signs and larger lesions. The clinically actionable implications are (1) reinforce the coronal seal (defective restoration → higher load), (2) favour multi-session disinfection with Ca(OH)₂ in symptomatic apical periodontitis, (3) use activation systems (GentleWave/PUI/XP-EF) as the best available reducers while recognising their ceiling, and (4) watch biologic medicaments as the candidate path toward elimination.
