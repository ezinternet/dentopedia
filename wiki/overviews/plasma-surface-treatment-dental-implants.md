> - 핵심 명제: 저온/비열 플라즈마(Cold Atmospheric Plasma, CAP)는 새 임플란트 종류가 아니라 체어사이드 표면 재활성화 도구다.
> - 작동 기전: 티타늄 표면은 생물학적으로 노화(탄화수소 축적·습윤성 저하)하는데, 플라즈마가 이를 역전 — 탄소↓·친수성/표면에너지↑(형태·조성은 불변); 동일한 반응성 산소/질소종이 세균도 죽여 제염이라는 2차 근거 제공.
> - 두 역할: ① 골유착 촉진 ② 표면 제염(치주염 peri-implantitis).
> - 사람 근거(신규): 작지만 부분적으로 유의한 초기 안정성 이득 — Stacchi RCT는 60/90일에만 임플란트 안정성 지수(ISQ) 유의(절대차 작음), Kwon RCT는 안정성 음성(변연골 변화만 양성), Kim 코호트는 대조군 없음.
> - 전 근거단계 공통 패턴: 최종치를 올리기보다 초기 치유를 앞당김 — 무처리 대조군이 8주경 따라잡는다(Jang·Danna). 더 높은 최종 plateau는 미입증.
> - 용량 비단조성: 60초는 좋으나 300–600초는 오히려 세균 부착 증가(Lee); 항균효과는 ~24h 지속·72h엔 소실(Yang) → 짧은 프로토콜 + 처리 직후 즉시 식립.
> - 정량 MA는 플라즈마가 아니라 UV에 기반(Pesce, I²>75%) — 플라즈마 특이 통합 효과크기는 아직 없음.
> - 한계: 보철 적용은 노화(thermocycling) 후 이득 소실(Kim-2021); 표면 의존적(Danna: textured Ti엔 도움, CaP엔 무효); 장기/하중/생존 데이터 전무(최장 추적 90일).
> - 임상 frame: 노화 fixture의 식립 직전 재활성화·연조직(D3/D4)·하악·짧은 fixture에서 가장 타당; SLActive 대비 비열등(Long, 동물)이나 우월성은 아님; "더 오래 처리 = 더 좋음"은 틀림(과처리 역효과).

## One-line Summary

Across 25 wiki papers (4 reviews/SR/MA, 6 animal in vivo, 3 human clinical, 11 in vitro, 1 mouse safety), cold/non-thermal plasma re-activates implant surfaces with two coherent jobs — boosting osseointegration and decontaminating surfaces — and the now-available human evidence (2 RCTs + 1 cohort) shows a small but partly significant *early* stability benefit; the recurring pattern across every tier is that plasma accelerates early healing rather than raising the final endpoint, with effects that are modest, dose-narrow (over-treatment backfires), time-decaying for antimicrobial action, and still without long-term/survival data.

## Thesis

Plasma is a chairside surface re-activation tool, not a new implant class. Titanium surfaces age biologically (hydrocarbon accumulation, falling wettability); plasma reverses that — carbon down, hydrophilicity/surface energy up, morphology and elemental composition unchanged — which improves early cell response and, in animals, early bone-to-implant contact. The same reactive oxygen/nitrogen species kill bacteria, giving a second rationale (decontamination / peri-implantitis). The 2nd-batch human studies upgrade the evidence from "pre-clinical only" to "early human signal present, but small": the controlled RCT (Stacchi) reached significance only at 60/90 days with a limited absolute ISQ gap, the other RCT (Kwon) was negative on stability, and the cohort (Kim) lacks a control. The honest through-line — visible in rat, dog, and human alike — is that plasma buys *speed* in the early healing window; the untreated control tends to catch up, and a higher final plateau is not demonstrated.

## Where the evidence converges (direction)

- **Surface physics.** Carbon ↓ and wettability/surface energy ↑ without topography/composition change — Zheng (contact angle ↓~100%, carbon ↓~80%), Lee-H (DBD, carbon ↓~60%), Ho (carbon p<0.001, hydrophilicity p<0.0001), Wagner (composition unchanged), Zeng (nanostructure preserved), Jeon, Yang (O/C↑).
- **Cells.** ↑ early osteoblast and fibroblast adhesion/proliferation/differentiation in vitro — Wagner, Zheng, Zeng, Hadzik, Ho (with Apln/Crabp2 upregulation), Lee-H, Jeon.
- **Bone (animal).** ↑ early BIC/bone volume — rat (Zheng, BIC ↑25–40%, osteoclasts ↓~50%), pig (Naujokat 90.4 vs 86.5%), foxhound (Nevins, BIC sig at 4 wk), dog plasma-jet (Jang, BV/BIC sig at 4 wk), beagle (Danna, textured-Ti BIC sig at 6 wk), beagle vs SLActive (Long, comparable to commercial hydrophilic).
- **Human (new).** Early stability signal — Stacchi RCT (ISQ sig higher at 60/90 d), Kim cohort (ISQ 78.97→83.74 at 8 wk, no dip); Kwon RCT (greater marginal bone change, stability NS).
- **Antimicrobial.** Broad in-vitro pathogen/biofilm reduction — Jungbauer SR (55 studies, time-dependent), Alqutaibi SR (15 studies, implant surfaces), Lee (zirconia), Zeng (S. aureus), Yang (S. mutans).

## Where the evidence draws boundaries (magnitude / conditions)

- **Benefit is early and transient, not a higher endpoint.** Jang and Danna: control catches up by 8 wk / advantage only at one timepoint. Nevins: significance timepoint-specific. Ho: cell-attachment difference gone by 12 h.
- **Human effect is small and mixed.** Stacchi: significant only at 60/90 d, absolute ISQ gap limited. Kwon: stability/defect NS (only marginal-bone-change positive). Kim: uncontrolled single-arm.
- **Effect can be non-significant.** Naujokat ("slight"); Kahm (rabbit vacuum plasma — BA/bone volume/ISQ higher but NS).
- **Quantitative MA rests on UV, not plasma.** Pesce pooled 4 UV-rabbit studies (plasma stayed qualitative), I²>75%.
- **Dose is non-monotonic / time-decaying.** Lee: 60 s good, 300–600 s *increases* bacterial adhesion. Yang: antibacterial effect lasts ~24 h, gone by 72 h. → short protocol, placement promptly after treatment.
- **Prosthetic benefit does not survive aging.** Kim-2021: NTP zirconia retention gain lost after thermocycling; NTP+silane/primer worse after aging.
- **Surface-dependent.** Danna: APP helped textured Ti but not calcium-phosphate surfaces.
- **No long-term/survival data.** Longest human follow-up is 90 days (Stacchi); no loaded/MBL/survival endpoints.

## Clinical decision frame

| Scenario | Plasma rationale | Evidence basis | Caveat |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chairside re-activation immediately before placement (aged fixture) | Reasonable | Stacchi RCT, Kim cohort; animal (Nevins/Jang/Naujokat/Zheng) | small human effect; place promptly after treatment |
| Soft (D3/D4) / low-initial-stability sites where early stability matters | Most plausible | Kim subgroup (low-ISQ gains most); animal BIC↑ | short-term ISQ only |
| Mandible, shorter fixtures, higher insertion torque | Greater relative gain | Kim subgroup | uncontrolled cohort |
| Faster osseointegration / earlier loading ambition | Speed only, not higher final | Jang/Danna convergence; Stacchi late-only | no loading/survival data |
| Decontaminating contaminated/peri-implantitis surface | Plausible adjunct | Alqutaibi, Jungbauer, Lee, Zeng, Yang | in-vitro; short protocol; effect time-decays |
| Plasma as cheaper alternative to SLActive | Defensible (non-inferiority) | Long (beagle, comparable) | animal; not superiority |
| Transgingival/soft-tissue collar | Emerging | Hadzik (fibroblast↑) | preliminary in vitro |
| Zirconia crown cement retention | Not supported after aging | Kim-2021 | benefit lost on thermocycling; +primer worse |
| Longer treatment = better | No | Lee (≥300 s↑ adhesion), Yang (24 h decay) | over-treatment backfires |
| Mucosal safety of chairside CAP | Screened pre-clinically | Jablonowski (mouse) | device/dose-specific; short-term |

## Open questions / verification needed

- Larger, longer human RCTs with loading, MBL, and survival endpoints (current max follow-up 90 d).
- Reconcile the mixed human stability result (Stacchi positive-late vs Kwon negative).
- Plasma-specific (not UV) pooled effect size for osseointegration.
- Standardized device/gas/time protocol and a defined treatment→placement window (given 24-h antimicrobial decay).
- Clinical peri-implantitis decontamination outcomes (vs laser / air-abrasion / CHX).

## Sibling overview — UV photofunctionalization

UV photofunctionalization is the **sibling re-activation technology** to cold plasma: same biological-aging premise (hydrocarbon removal → superhydrophilic, high-energy surface; topography unchanged), but a separate and now larger clinical base. The quantitative MA referenced above (Pesce 2020) is in fact UV-driven; UV-specific clinical evidence has since strengthened — a photofunctionalization-specific SR+MA (Lang 2022: OSI MD=2.13, p=0.007; 2-month ISQ MD=3.48, p=0.04), a split-mouth RCT in controlled diabetics (Krishna 2025: higher 3-month ISQ/OSI, less 9-month crestal bone loss), a fractal-analysis split-mouth RCT (Farsiani 2025: peri-implant trabecular FD ↑), and a 2026 systematic review reframing UV as a *surface-agnostic, positive-sum* strategy (Komatsu 2026). For the consolidated UV evidence see **[[overviews/uv-photofunctionalization-dental-implants]]** (13 papers). The plasma vs UV through-line is identical: both buy early-healing *speed*, neither has demonstrated a higher final endpoint or long-term loaded-survival advantage.
