## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - 핵심: 상아질 과민증(Dentin Hypersensitivity, DH)의 **원인(기전)→진단→치료 의사결정**을 위키 19편으로 종합. 노출된 상아세관(dentinal tubule) 안 액체 이동이 치수 신경을 자극한다는 **유체역학설(Hydrodynamic Theory)**이 지배적 기전(Kaur 2026·Li 2026·Ramli 2022).
> - 치료 표적은 단 둘 — ① **세관 폐쇄(tubule occlusion)** ② **신경 차단(neural blocking)**. 모든 제제·술식은 이 둘 중 하나(또는 둘 다)로 작동.
> - 진단은 **배제 진단**: 우식·균열치·치수염·수복물 결함·치경부 병소를 먼저 배제하고, 증발(air)/접촉(tactile) 자극으로 유발(Kaur 2026·JKDA 2022-003). 통증척도는 Schiff(SCASS)·VAS.
> - 장기(≥6개월) 효능 순위(Corrêa 2025 SR+MA): **글루타르알데히드(Gluma)·저출력레이저(LLLT)가 최대 효과**, **접착시스템·칼슘인산염(CPP-ACP)계는 장기 유의효과 없음** — 단기 효과가 지속성을 보장하지 않음.
> - 1차(자가) 치료: 탈감작 치약 — 주석불소(SnF₂, Qiu 2026), CSPS/NovaMin(Creeth 2026, 3일째부터 효과), 질산칼륨, CPP-ACP(Ayan 2025 두head-to-head 최대), 저농도 생활성(deFreitas 2021 SR). 제제 지도: 칼륨염·CSP가 최다(Martins 2022 scoping, 138 RCT).
> - 2차(in-office, 난치성): 글루타르알데히드(Gluma)·레이저. 레이저 SR-MA — 고출력=저출력 동등·3개월 유효(Faraoni 2023), LLLT는 위약보다 우수하나 타 탈감작제 대비 우위는 불소에만(Shan 2021), 단기만 우세(Naghsh 2024).
> - **안전성 상충(중요)**: 글루타르알데히드는 효능 최상위지만 **농도의존적 치수세포 독성**(Scheffel 2015 경상아질 모델·Jiang 2022) — Gluma는 상아질액 존재 시에만 세관 폐쇄·세포독성 공존. 효능 vs 안전 균형 필요.
> - 세관 폐쇄 근거: NovaMin·Gluma 단회 도포 SEM 폐쇄(Joshi 2013); NaF/실리카겔은 생체적합하나 타액 침적 후 투과도 반등(Jiang 2022) → 지속성 한계.
> - 구조적 결함(비우식성 치경부 병소 등 상아질 노출)·Class II 수복 후 과민은 **수복·예방 술식**으로 해결(JKDA 2022-005: 미세누출 차단·적층충전·서행중합).
> - 결정 트리: 배제진단 → 유발인자 제거+교육(항상) → 경증·전반: 자가 탈감작 치약 2–8주 → 지속·국소·중증: in-office Gluma/LLLT(장기근거 최상, 단 Gluma 독성 주의) → 구조결함: 수복. **지속 성공은 환자 순응·유발인자 관리에 좌우**(Ramli 2022·JKDA 2022-004).

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 19 wiki papers on dentin hypersensitivity (DH): the hydrodynamic theory (fluid flow in exposed dentinal tubules stimulating pulpal nerves) is the dominant mechanism, so every treatment works by either occluding tubules or blocking nerve transmission; diagnosis is by exclusion (rule out caries/cracked tooth/pulpitis/defective restorations) using evaporative and tactile stimuli with Schiff/VAS scales; first-line care is at-home desensitizing toothpaste (SnF₂, CSPS/NovaMin, potassium nitrate, CPP-ACP) and refractory cases escalate to in-office glutaraldehyde or laser — with glutaraldehyde and low-level laser holding the strongest long-term meta-analytic evidence (Corrêa 2025) but glutaraldehyde carrying concentration-dependent pulp cytotoxicity (Scheffel 2015), while adhesives and calcium-phosphate agents lack durable benefit.

상아질 과민증(DH) 위키 19편 종합 — 유체역학설(노출 상아세관 내 액체 이동→치수신경 자극)이 지배 기전이라 모든 치료는 세관 폐쇄 또는 신경 차단으로 작동. 진단은 배제진단(우식·균열·치수염·수복결함 제외)+증발/접촉 자극·Schiff/VAS. 1차는 자가 탈감작 치약(SnF₂·CSPS/NovaMin·질산칼륨·CPP-ACP), 난치성은 in-office 글루타르알데히드·레이저 — 장기 근거는 Gluma·저출력레이저가 최상(Corrêa 2025)이나 Gluma는 농도의존 치수독성(Scheffel 2015), 접착·칼슘인산염계는 장기 효과 부족.

## Summary

**Dentin hypersensitivity (DH, 상아질 과민증)** is short, sharp pain from *exposed* dentin in response to thermal, evaporative, tactile, osmotic, or chemical stimuli — in the absence of any other pathology. This page synthesizes the 19 DH papers in the wiki into a single **mechanism → diagnosis → treatment** workflow.

### 1. Mechanism / Etiology — two targets only

The accepted mechanism is the **hydrodynamic theory**: stimuli drive fluid movement within open **dentinal tubules**, which mechanically excites pulpal A-δ nerves (Kaur 2026, Ramli 2022). Li 2026 frames three classical theories — Direct Innervation, Odontoblast Receptor, and Hydrodynamic — with the hydrodynamic one dominant; Kaur 2026 adds modern layers (TRP channels, neuroplasticity/central sensitization). The clinical consequence is decisive: **every therapy works by one of just two routes — occluding the tubules (stop the fluid) or blunting nerve transmission.** Tubule patency is the prerequisite; SEM work shows agents like NovaMin and Gluma physically occlude tubules after a single application (Joshi 2013), and Gluma occludes (and is cytotoxic) only when dentinal fluid/protein is present (Jiang 2022).

### 2. Diagnosis — by exclusion

DH is a **diagnosis of exclusion** (Kaur 2026, JKDA 2022-003): take a history, reproduce pain with **controlled evaporative (air) and tactile** stimuli, grade it (Schiff/SCASS, VAS), and **rule out** caries, cracked-tooth, reversible/irreversible pulpitis, defective or leaking restorations, and post-operative sensitivity. JKDA 2022-004 lays out an outpatient pain-assessment-to-treatment decision framework.

### 3. Treatment — staged decision tree

| Tier | Options (wiki evidence) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **0. Always** | Remove triggers (acid diet, abrasive brushing), patient education | Durable success hinges on adherence (Ramli 2022, JKDA 2022-004) |
| **1. At-home (first-line)** | Desensitizing toothpaste: SnF₂ (Qiu 2026, > standard fluoride), CSPS/NovaMin (Creeth 2026, relief by Day 3), potassium nitrate, CPP-ACP (Ayan 2025, greatest in 5-agent head-to-head), low-conc bioactives (deFreitas 2021 SR) | 138-RCT formulation map: K-salts + CSP most common actives (Martins 2022) |
| **2. In-office (refractory/localized)** | Glutaraldehyde (Gluma); laser (LLLT / high-power) | **Long-term winners = glutaraldehyde + LLLT** (Corrêa 2025 SR+MA); adhesives & calcium-phosphate NOT durable |
| **3. Restorative** | Restore exposed cervical lesion / structural defect; prevent post-restoration DH | NCCL exposure; Class II composite sensitivity prevention (JKDA 2022-005) |

**Laser evidence** is favorable but bounded: laser (high- or low-power) reduces DH at 3 months with **no high- vs low-power superiority and no standard protocol** (Faraoni 2023); LLLT beats placebo across timepoints but is **not superior to other desensitizers except fluoride**, with high heterogeneity (Shan 2021); a diode laser led at 1 month but differences vanished by 3 months (Naghsh 2024); Er,Cr:YSGG ± Gluma outperformed NaF varnish over 6 months (Forouzande 2022).

### 4. The central tension — efficacy vs safety, short- vs long-term

- **Glutaraldehyde** is a long-term efficacy leader (Corrêa 2025) but is **concentration-dependently cytotoxic to pulp cells** through dentin (Scheffel 2015) and acts only with dentinal fluid present (Jiang 2022) — use judiciously.
- **Calcium-phosphate (CPP-ACP) and adhesive systems** perform well short-term/in head-to-heads (Ayan 2025) but **lack significant long-term benefit** (Corrêa 2025) — set expectations and re-treat.
- **At-home toothpastes** (SnF₂, CSPS, K-nitrate) are the right first move for mild/generalized DH; **in-office Gluma/laser** is for persistent or localized cases.

## Decision Tree (condensed)

1. **Confirm DH by exclusion** — rule out caries, cracked tooth, pulpitis, restorative/marginal defects.
2. **Remove triggers + educate** (diet, atraumatic brushing) — always, regardless of tier.
3. **Mild / generalized →** at-home desensitizing toothpaste (SnF₂ / CSPS-NovaMin / K-nitrate / CPP-ACP); trial 2–8 weeks.
4. **Persistent / localized / severe →** in-office **glutaraldehyde (Gluma)** or **LLLT** (strongest long-term evidence); use high-power laser as an option; mind glutaraldehyde pulp toxicity.
5. **Structural defect (exposed cervical lesion / NCCL) →** restore; prevent post-restoration sensitivity.
6. **Reassess** — durability depends on adherence and trigger control more than on the specific agent.

## Limitations of the Evidence Base

- Many trials are **short-term**; the only long-term (≥6 mo) MA (Corrêa 2025) is **abstract-only** here and could not pool per-agent CIs.
- **High heterogeneity** in laser MAs (Shan 2021 I² 64–95%) and **no standardized protocols** (Faraoni 2023).
- Several mechanism/strategy sources are **narrative reviews** (Kaur 2026, Li 2026, Ramli 2022) — strong for framing, weak as efficacy proof.
- Glutaraldehyde safety data are **in-vitro/transdentinal** (Scheffel 2015, Jiang 2022), not long-term clinical.
- Industry sponsorship is common in toothpaste RCTs (e.g., Creeth 2026 / Haleon).
