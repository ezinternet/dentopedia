## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - **논지**: 치과 감염관리는 서로 다른 물리·생물학을 가진 **세 전선(front)** 으로 나뉜다 — ①기기·표면 멸균/소독 (Spaulding 분류 기반), ②치과 유니트 수관 (Dental Unit Waterline, DUWL) 바이오필름 오염, ③에어로졸 발생 술식 (Aerosol-Generating Procedure, AGP) 의 생물학적 에어로졸(bioaerosol) 확산. 위키 보유 5편(멸균 리뷰 1, 전해수 SR 1, DUWL 리뷰 1, bioaerosol 리뷰 1, AGP 합의문 1)으로 이 세 축을 종합한다.
> - **전선 1 — 멸균/소독**: 세척이 항상 멸균·소독에 선행해야 하며(유기물이 미생물을 차폐), 자동 세척소독기는 >7-log₁₀ 감소로 수기보다 재현성 우월. Spaulding: 비critical=저수준, semicritical=고수준(글루타르알데히드 2%·OPA·과산화수소·과초산), critical=멸균 (Patiño-Marín 2025, narrative-review).
> - **멸균 검증**: 생물학적 지시계 (Biological Indicator, BI) 가 WHO·ADA·CDC가 인정하는 유일한 방법 — 오토클레이브는 *Geobacillus stearothermophilus*, 건열은 *Bacillus atrophaeus*, UVC/감마는 *B. pumilus*. 검사 빈도 ADA/CDC 주간. **멸균 실패의 1차 원인은 인적 오류**(교육·유지보수 부족) → 직원 교육이 최고 수율 개입 (Patiño-Marín 2025).
> - **전선 2 — DUWL 바이오필름**: 급수 <100 CFU/mL여도 핸드피스 출수는 바이오필름 탈락으로 **100,000–1,000,000 CFU/mL** 까지 상승. 좁은 관경(높은 표면적/부피)·정체(야간·주말)·PVC/폴리우레탄 재질이 증폭 (Samaranayake 2024, narrative-review).
> - **DUWL 규제 임계값**: CDC ≤500 CFU/mL, ADA/호주 ≤200, UK ≤100, EU는 음용수 기준 대용. 주요 병원체 *Legionella* 12%·*Pseudomonas aeruginosa* 8%(메타분석), DUWL 유래 레지오넬라 폐렴 사망 사례(82세, 분자유형 확인) 보고. 미국 치과직원 23%가 레지오넬라 IgG 양성(지역사회 8% 대비) — 만성 직업적 흡입 노출 근거 (Samaranayake 2024).
> - **DUWL 소독 계층**: 공학적(독립 저수조·≤0.2 µm 필터·역류방지밸브) → 절차적(하루 시작 ≥2분·환자간 20–30초 flushing, 단 바이오필름 제거 못 함) → 화학적(과산화물·염소계 + EDTA/구연산 킬레이트, 지속 저농도+주기적 shock) → 자동화(Poseidon-S·Planmeca). **단일 방식으로 불충분, 다중 접근 필수** (Samaranayake 2024).
> - **전해수 (Electrolyzed Water, EW) 근거**: 63편 SR — 표면·인상재·보철물·DUWL에 광범위 항균·항진균·항바이러스, DUWL에서 최대 98.1% 살균(글루타르알데히드 동등, ≤6주 유지). 다만 비뚤림 위험 높음(NPQIP Yes율 35.18%) → 방향성은 일관되나 근거 질 낮음 (Cárdenas 2022, SR).
> - **전선 3 — AGP/bioaerosol**: 에어터빈 (air turbine) 핸드피스는 조용한 호흡의 **~1000배** 세균 CFU 분산(재채기의 2배). 고전적 "<5 µm=airborne" 이분법은 오류 — **최대 100 µm 입자도 부유·>2 m 이동**. 치과팀 혈청양성 ~16% vs 일반인구 ~6% (Allison 2024, narrative-review).
> - **AGP 완화 계층(효과추정치, Allison 2024)**: ①생성감소(전동 마이크로모터가 에어터빈보다 에어로졸·미생물 적음) → ②미생물부하감소(러버댐 1 m에서 최대 99%·2 m 70%↓, 술전 가글 33–94%↓) → ③포집/제거(고용량 흡인 (High-Volume Evacuation, HVE) 80–90%↓, 국소배기 89–93%↓) → ④환기(~10 ACH, 소형 에어로졸 청소 10 ACH서 ~30분) + PPE(airborne엔 FFP3/N95).
> - **AGP 합의(Ghoneim 2024, consensus, 초록만)**: 보안경+마스크+안면보호대 병용 PPE, HVE±구내흡인·타액흡인기·러버댐, 적절한 환기·여과 권고. 구강세척제는 **세균** 오염은 줄이나 **SARS-CoV-2엔 효과 제한적**. 정량 효과크기는 초록에 미보고.
> - **임상 결론**: 세 전선은 각각 다른 프로토콜이 필요하며 어느 하나도 대체 불가 — BI로 검증된 멸균, 다중 DUWL 소독으로 ≤500 CFU/mL(가능하면 더 낮게) 유지, 러버댐+HVE+술전가글+환기+PPE로 에어로졸 노출 저감이 통합 체크리스트다. 근거 강도: 멸균/DUWL/bioaerosol은 narrative-review, 전해수는 SR(고 RoB), AGP 완화는 consensus(초록만).

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 5 held papers framing dental infection control as three biologically distinct fronts — instrument/surface sterilization & disinfection (Spaulding classification, biological-indicator verification, human error as the dominant failure mode), dental-unit-waterline (DUWL) biofilm contamination (feed <100 CFU/mL → output up to 10⁶ CFU/mL; CDC ≤500 CFU/mL target requiring multi-modal engineering + procedural + chemical + automated control, with electrolyzed water as an emerging biocide), and aerosol-generating-procedure (AGP) bioaerosol dispersion (air turbine ~1000× the CFU of quiet breathing; the <5 µm airborne cut-off is invalid; a reduce-production → reduce-load → capture/remove → ventilation + PPE hierarchy lowers exposure) — no single measure suffices on any front.

## 한줄요약

치과 감염관리를 기기·표면 멸균/소독, DUWL 바이오필름 오염, AGP 에어로졸 확산의 세 전선으로 종합한 5편 기반 오버뷰 — 멸균은 Spaulding 분류·BI 검증·인적오류가 핵심이고, DUWL은 급수 <100→출수 최대 10⁶ CFU/mL로 다중 소독이 필요하며(전해수 신흥 대안), 에어로졸은 에어터빈이 호흡의 ~1000배 세균을 내고 <5 µm 통념이 틀렸으며 생성감소→부하감소→포집→환기+PPE 계층으로 통제한다. 어느 전선도 단일 방법으로 불충분.

## Summary

1. **Cleaning always precedes disinfection and sterilization.** Organic/inorganic burden shields microorganisms; automated washer-disinfectors achieve >7-log₁₀ reduction and beat manual cleaning on reproducibility (Patiño-Marín 2025, narrative-review).

2. **The Spaulding classification sets the required processing level.** Noncritical items (intact-skin contact) → low-level disinfection (QACs, alcohols); semicritical (mucosa) → high-level (glutaraldehyde 2%, OPA, H₂O₂, peracetic acid, chlorine); critical (sterile tissue) → sterilization (Patiño-Marín 2025).

3. **Biological indicators are the only globally accepted sterilization-verification method** (WHO/ADA/CDC). Organism is method-specific: autoclave → *Geobacillus stearothermophilus*; dry heat → *Bacillus atrophaeus*; UVC/gamma → *B. pumilus*; EtO → *B. atrophaeus*. ADA/CDC frequency is weekly (Patiño-Marín 2025).

4. **Human error — not equipment — is the dominant cause of sterilization failure** (inadequate training, poor maintenance, wrong time/temperature/preheating, misread BI). Staff training is framed as the highest-yield intervention (Patiño-Marín 2025).

5. **DUWL biofilm amplifies low-count feed water into heavily colonized output.** Feed water <100 CFU/mL can exit the handpiece at 100,000–1,000,000 CFU/mL because biofilm sheds from the narrow-bore tubing's interior; stagnation (nights/weekends) and PVC/polyurethane materials worsen it (Samaranayake 2024, narrative-review).

6. **DUWL regulatory thresholds vary by jurisdiction** — CDC ≤500 CFU/mL, ADA/Australia ≤200, UK ≤100, EU applies drinking-water proxies (<100 CFU/mL at 22°C/72h). *Legionella* (12%) and *Pseudomonas aeruginosa* (8%) predominate; a molecularly confirmed DUWL-sourced *Legionella* pneumonia death and 23% staff *Legionella* IgG seropositivity (vs 8% community) evidence real occupational/patient risk (Samaranayake 2024).

7. **No single DUWL modality suffices — control is tiered.** Engineering (independent reservoir, ≤0.2 µm point-of-use filters, antiretraction/antistagnation valves, RO/deionizer pre-treatment) + procedural flushing (≥2 min at day start, 20–30 s between patients — reduces density but does NOT remove biofilm) + chemical biocides (peroxide/chlorine + chelators; continuous low-dose plus periodic shock; single-biocide use risks habituation) + automated systems (Poseidon-S, Planmeca; consistent dosing but still need manual oversight) (Samaranayake 2024).

8. **Electrolyzed water is a broad-spectrum emerging biocide but on low-quality evidence.** A 63-study SR (58 in vitro + 5 RCTs) found EW effective against caries/periodontal/endodontic pathogens, *Candida*, and enveloped/non-enveloped viruses, with DUWL kill up to 98.1% (≈ glutaraldehyde, sustained ≤6 weeks) and no material damage to impressions/prostheses/titanium — but overall risk of bias is high (NPQIP "Yes" 35.18%), so directional consistency, not RCT-grade certainty, underpins the claim (Cárdenas 2022, SR).

9. **Dental instruments turn the oral cavity into a bioaerosol source.** Air-turbine handpieces disperse ~1000× the bacterial CFU of quiet breathing (≈2× a sneeze); bioaerosol bacteria arise from *both* the oral cavity and DUWLs (DNA sequencing). This placed high-speed procedures among WHO high-risk AGPs during COVID-19 (Allison 2024, narrative-review).

10. **The classical <5 µm droplet/airborne dichotomy is invalid.** Particles up to 100 µm can stay suspended and travel >2 m; a 5 µm particle takes ~33 min to settle from 1.5 m vs ~5 s for 100 µm. Dental-team seroprevalence (~16%) far exceeds the general population (~6%), marking dentistry among the highest bioaerosol-exposure professions (Allison 2024).

11. **Aerosol mitigation is a hierarchy with quantified effect (Allison 2024).** *Reduce production*: electric micromotor << air turbine. *Reduce microbial load*: rubber dam ↓ up to 99% at 1 m / 70% at 2 m; pre-procedural mouthrinse ↓ 33–94%; waterline disinfectant ↓ 99% infectious phage at 0.5 m. *Capture/remove*: high-volume evacuation ↓ 80–90%; local exhaust/extra-oral suction ↓ 89–93% at 0.1 m. *Ventilation*: ~10 ACH proposed, small-aerosol clearance ~30 min at 10 ACH; HEPA where ventilation can't be upgraded. *PPE*: standard precautions baseline, FFP3/N95 for airborne route.

12. **The CDHA/ADHA consensus endorses combined controls but flags evidence gaps** (Ghoneim 2024, consensus — abstract-only). Eyewear + mask + face shield prevents facial/nasal contamination; HVE ± intraoral suction, saliva ejector, and rubber dam reduce spread; appropriate ventilation + filtration limits it. Mouthrinses cut *bacterial* contamination but show *limited* effect against SARS-CoV-2; hygienist↔patient transmission evidence remains limited (no pooled effect sizes in the abstract).

## Clinical Decision Points

A practical three-front infection-control checklist grounded in the held papers:

**Front 1 — Instrument & surface reprocessing**
- [ ] Clean (ideally automated washer-disinfector, >7-log₁₀) *before* any disinfection/sterilization — never sterilize soiled instruments.
- [ ] Match processing level to Spaulding class: critical → sterilize; semicritical → high-level disinfection; noncritical → low-level.
- [ ] Run a **biological indicator** on the correct spore for the method (autoclave = *G. stearothermophilus*), at least weekly (ADA/CDC).
- [ ] Treat a failed BI as a human-factor signal first (training, maintenance, cycle parameters, BI interpretation).
- [ ] Store sterilized packs using event-related sterility; keep storage 20–25°C, 30–60% RH, separate from treatment areas.

**Front 2 — Dental unit waterlines**
- [ ] Know your jurisdiction's threshold (CDC ≤500 / ADA ≤200 / UK ≤100 CFU/mL) and monitor output water via a certified lab; act on exceedances.
- [ ] Do not rely on flushing alone — it lowers density but leaves biofilm intact; flush ≥2 min at day start and 20–30 s between patients as a *baseline*.
- [ ] Layer engineering (independent reservoir, ≤0.2 µm point-of-use filter, functioning antiretraction valves) + a chemical biocide program (continuous low-dose + periodic shock; rotate/monitor to avoid habituation).
- [ ] If using electrolyzed water or another emerging biocide, weigh its low-RoB evidence and confirm material compatibility; keep verifying output counts.
- [ ] Log DCU service/maintenance vs manufacturer schedules; automated systems still need manual oversight.

**Front 3 — Aerosol-generating procedures**
- [ ] Reduce production: prefer an electric micromotor over the air turbine where clinically acceptable, especially if rubber dam is impossible.
- [ ] Reduce load at source: apply rubber dam (↓ up to 99% at 1 m) whenever appropriate + a pre-procedural mouthrinse (↓ 33–94% bacterial; limited vs SARS-CoV-2).
- [ ] Capture/remove: use **high-volume evacuation** (↓ 80–90%) ± intraoral suction/saliva ejector; add local/extra-oral exhaust for close-range capture.
- [ ] Manage operatory air: target adequate ventilation (~10 ACH; ~30 min small-aerosol clearance) or add HEPA filtration; apply fallow time by clearance reasoning.
- [ ] PPE: eyewear + mask + face shield as baseline for AGPs; escalate to FFP3/N95 for airborne-route infections, per per-patient risk assessment.

## Bottom Line (한 줄 결론)

치과 감염관리는 멸균(BI 검증·인적오류 관리)·DUWL(다중 소독으로 ≤500 CFU/mL 유지)·에어로졸(러버댐+HVE+술전가글+환기+PPE)의 세 전선을 각각 통제해야 하며, 어느 하나도 단일 방법으로는 충분하지 않다.
