## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - 핵심: 임플란트 출현윤곽(Emergence Profile) 설계와 맞춤 연조직 컨디셔닝 6편 종합 — 개념틀(gomez-meda) → 실행 장치(ruales-carrera·azpiazu-flores) → 캡처 근거(li) → 주변 임상(han·lim).
> - 개념틀 (gomez-meda 2021, narrative): 심미생물학적 윤곽(Esthetic Biological Contour, EBC) **4-zone** — Zone1 임플란트-지대주 계면, Zone2 준임계윤곽(Sub-critical), Zone3 임계윤곽(Critical), Zone4 치은연상.
> - 결정적 원칙: **임계윤곽(Zone3) 볼록도↑ → 치은연 근단측 이동(퇴축 위험), 오목도↑ → 치은연 치관측 상승**; **준임계윤곽(Zone2)은 오목~중립이 치조정골 안정**, 볼록하면 골소실·염증↑.
> - 인용 벤치마크: 골수준(Bone-level) 임플란트 **1~3mm 치조정하(sub-crestal)** 식립, 생물학적 폭(Biologic Width) **~3~4mm**, 협측 연조직 **≥2mm** → 치조정골 안정(Linkevicius), 지대주 높이 **≥3mm** → 골소실 방지(Galindo-Moreno).
> - 캡처의 물리학 (li 2019, prospective, n=12): 임시보철(Interim Restoration) 제거 **0초 즉시** 출현윤곽 붕괴 시작, 시간의 **로그함수(ln)**로 회복 — 반면 유두·치은연은 20분간 **≤0.27mm** 변화(임상적 무의미) → 인상은 임시보철 유지 상태 또는 윤곽 복제 scan body로 떠야 함.
> - 실행 장치 1 (ruales-carrera 2019, case series): 즉시식립 당일 chairside **맞춤 치유지대주(Customized Healing Abutment, CHA)** — PEEK/PMMA+티타늄 인서트로 발치치아 형태 복제, 임계+준임계윤곽 동시 재현 → 임시보철·재개방수술 불필요.
> - 실행 장치 2 (azpiazu-flores 2025, case report): **3D 프린팅 출현윤곽 성형체(EPF)**를 규격 티타늄 치유지대주에 장착 — 연조직 성형 + 구강스캐너(IOS) 등록을 동시 수행(scan body 대체), 임시지대주 나사 불필요, 수평골결손 대구치를 협측 이식 없이 심미 수복.
> - 공유 어휘: 임계/준임계윤곽(Su 2010), dual-zone(Chu 2012)이 core 3편의 공통 언어 — **최종 크라운이 아니라 임시보철·치유지대주가 1차 연조직 성형 도구**라는 합의.
> - 주변 근거 (han 2025, prospective n=20): dual-thread 상악 심미대 즉시식립 — 성공 85%/생존 100%, ISQ 70일에 >70, **중안면 퇴축 1.86→1.21 개선**, 1년 협측골 두께 −0.34~−0.48mm; 출현윤곽 설계가 아니라 macrodesign·1차안정성 축.
> - 주변 근거 (lim 2022, animal, 개 5마리): 즉시식립 협측 gap에서 **결합조직이식(SCTG) 단독이 치관측 점막 최우수**(MM-IS 1.99mm), GBR은 견부 하방에만 작용, **GBR+SCTG 병용은 오히려 최저(1.24mm, 비가산적)** — 단 모두 NS(underpowered).
> - 임상 bottom line: 견부 상방 연조직 두께·출현윤곽은 능동적으로 지지·복제해야 하는 **labile 차원**이고 유두·치은연은 상대적으로 안정 → **osseous가 아니라 connective/맞춤윤곽이 심미대 value-add**.
> - 공백: core 3편에 정량 심미결과(PES/WES) 부재(전부 case/qualitative), 준임계 오목 vs 중립 head-to-head 미검증, 디지털 캡처 3방식(임시보철 유지 vs 맞춤 scan body vs 3D EPF) 정확도 비교 부재.

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 6 papers (1 narrative-review, 2 prospective, 2 case reports/series, 1 animal) establishing how peri-implant emergence profile is designed (the 4-zone critical/subcritical framework), executed (customized healing abutments and 3D-printed emergence-profile formers), and captured (interim-restoration-in-place scanning), with adjacent evidence on macrodesign-driven stability and connective-tissue vs bone augmentation.

## 한줄요약

임플란트 출현윤곽 설계·맞춤 연조직 컨디셔닝 6편 종합: 임계/준임계 4-zone 개념틀(gomez-meda)을 맞춤 치유지대주·3D 프린팅 성형체(ruales-carrera·azpiazu-flores)로 실행하고, 임시보철 제거 0초에 윤곽이 붕괴하므로(li) 임시보철 유지 상태로 캡처해야 하며, 최종 크라운이 아니라 임시보철·치유지대주가 1차 성형 도구임.

## Summary

Peri-implant esthetics in the anterior (and increasingly posterior) zone is decided less by the definitive crown than by how the transmucosal **emergence profile** is shaped, held, and recorded during healing. This synthesis draws on six papers to trace a single clinical arc — from the **conceptual framework** that classifies the transmucosal contour into zones, through the **physical devices** that execute that framework chairside or digitally, to the **capture problem** created by the emergence profile's own instability — plus two adjacent papers that supply the stability and augmentation context in which conditioning happens.

The tight core is three papers. Gomez-Meda et al. (2021) provides the vocabulary: the **Esthetic Biological Contour (EBC)** organizes the emergence profile into four zones, of which the **critical contour** (Zone 3, at the junctional epithelium) governs the gingival margin position and the **subcritical contour** (Zone 2) governs crestal bone stability. Ruales-Carrera et al. (2019) and Azpiazu-Flores et al. (2025) then execute that framework — the former with a chairside **customized healing abutment (CHA)** placed at immediate surgery, the latter with a **3D-printed emergence-profile former (EPF)** that doubles as a scan-body surrogate. Li et al. (2019) supplies the reason the whole workflow must be disciplined: the emergence profile collapses the instant the interim restoration is removed, so the contour must be captured *before* it distorts.

The two looser-fitting papers frame the surgical context. Han et al. (2025) tracks a dual-thread implant macrodesign for esthetic-zone immediate placement — its value here is quantitative recession and buccal-bone data, not contour design. Lim et al. (2022) is a canine histologic comparison of GBR vs connective tissue graft vs their combination, informing which augmentation actually adds tissue *coronal to the implant shoulder* — the exact dimension the emergence profile then sculpts.

**핵심 명제 (Evidence Propositions)**

1. **The transmucosal emergence profile divides into a critical and a subcritical contour with opposite biological jobs.** Gomez-Meda et al. (2021) formalize this as the EBC 4-zone model: the critical contour (Zone 3, immediately supra-junctional) controls the free gingival margin — added convexity displaces the margin apically (recession risk), while concavity elevates it coronally — whereas the subcritical contour (Zone 2) governs crestal bone, where a concave-to-neutral shape maintains bone and an over-convex shape raises bone-loss and inflammation risk. This is the design grammar every subsequent device obeys. [근거강함 — 개념 합의]

2. **The provisional/healing abutment — not the definitive crown — is the primary tissue-shaping instrument.** All three core papers (Gomez-Meda 2021, Ruales-Carrera 2019, Azpiazu-Flores 2025) treat contour development as a healing-phase task executed by an interim device, with the final restoration merely copying the matured profile. [합의수준]

3. **The emergence profile is a labile dimension that collapses within seconds of interim-restoration removal, while papilla and gingival margin are stable.** Li et al. (2019), a prospective study of 12 esthetic-zone sites in 10 patients with serial intraoral scans, found the emergence profile discrepancy was already significant at 0 seconds and recovered as a logarithmic function of time (∝ ln[time]), whereas mesial papilla, distal papilla, and gingival margin all changed ≤0.27 mm over 20 minutes. Practical consequence: **impress/scan with the interim restoration in place or with a customized scan body replicating the profile** — a bare stock scan body records a collapsed contour. [근거강함 — prospective, 소표본]

4. **A chairside customized healing abutment reproduces both critical and subcritical contours at immediate placement, eliminating provisionals and reopening surgery.** Ruales-Carrera et al. (2019) fabricated CHAs from a PEEK/PMMA block plus a titanium insert, matched to the extracted tooth's emergence geometry, and used the dual-zone (Chu 2012) and critical/subcritical (Su 2010) frameworks to shape papilla-supporting subcritical and margin-defining critical zones. Indicated for Type 1 (immediate) placement when primary stability is insufficient for immediate loading. Evidence is case-series/qualitative — no PES/WES or recession numbers. [사례수준]

5. **A 3D-printed emergence-profile former can unify soft-tissue conditioning and digital registration on a stock titanium healing abutment.** Azpiazu-Flores et al. (2025) designed an EPF in free software (Meshmixer, 0.02 mm Boolean offset) that seats on a stock healing abutment collar, requires no temporary abutment screw, and serves simultaneously as tissue conditioner and scan-body surrogate — delivering an esthetic mandibular-molar crown despite horizontal buccal deficiency and only 1 mm attached gingiva, without buccal grafting. Single case. [사례수준]

6. **Connective tissue graft, not bone augmentation, builds mucosa coronal to the implant shoulder — and combining them is not additive.** Lim et al. (2022), a randomized canine histomorphometric study (5 dogs, 4 sites each, immediate narrow implants, 4-month non-submerged healing), ranked mucosal height as SCTG 1.99 > GBR 1.45 > control 1.40 > GBR+SCTG 1.24 mm, with GBR producing mineralized tissue only apical to the shoulder and the combination performing worst. All differences were non-significant (underpowered, 2 implant losses) — a mechanistic signal, not proof, that soft-tissue grafting is the coronal value-add. [동물·NS — 신호 수준]

7. **Macrodesign-driven primary stability can improve midfacial recession in esthetic-zone immediate placement, but this is design-specific evidence.** Han et al. (2025), a single-arm prospective study of a dual-thread 11° internal-hex implant (n=20, 17 analyzed), reported 85% success / 100% survival, ISQ exceeding 70 by ~70 days, midfacial recession improving 1.86 → 1.21 mm, and 1-year buccal-bone thickness loss of −0.34 to −0.48 mm (gap grafted with bovine bone mineral + collagen membrane). Single-manufacturer, single-arm, 1-year — generalizability open. [prospective 단일군]

### 공백·미해결 (Open Questions)

- **정량 심미결과 부재**: 맞춤 지대주 core(Ruales-Carrera, Azpiazu-Flores)는 전부 case/qualitative — PES/WES·퇴축 정량치 없음.
- **준임계 오목 vs 중립** head-to-head 미검증 — 인용된 연구에서 주장될 뿐 이 페이지들 안에서 직접 비교 없음.
- **디지털 캡처 3방식**(임시보철 유지 / 맞춤 scan body / 3D EPF) 등록 정확도 비교 부재 — Li는 "왜", Azpiazu-Flores는 하나의 "어떻게"를 제시할 뿐.
- **GBR+SCTG 비가산성**(Lim)은 underpowered·NS — 기전 신호이지 증명 아님; 출현윤곽 컨디셔닝과의 상호작용 미검증.
