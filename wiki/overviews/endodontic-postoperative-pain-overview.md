## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - 핵심 명제: 근관치료 후 통증(post-endodontic pain)은 다인자성이며, 술후 통증을 좌우하는 최강 변수는 시술 술식(기구·kinematics·근관장측정법)이 아니라 **환자의 술전 증상 상태(preoperative symptomatic status)**다. wiki 5편(RCT 3·SR 2)을 시술 변수 축으로 통합. [근거강함]
> - 공통 시간 경과: 통증은 모든 술식에서 첫 6~24시간에 최고, 이후 감소해 7~14일이면 최소 — 이 곡선 자체는 변수와 무관하게 재현됨. [근거강함]
> - 근관장(Working Length, WL) 축: standalone 전자근단측정기(Electronic Apex Locator, EAL)와 방사선 근관장측정 간 술후 통증·진통제·소실시간 차이 없음(4h–7d, RCT 4편 n=414, 매우 낮은 근거). 단 EAL+방사선 hybrid(SLC)가 증상성 치아에서 초기 통증을 낮춘 소규모 신호 1편. [근거약함/가설생성]
> - Kinematics 축: 재근관치료에서 회전(rotary) vs 왕복(reciprocating) NiTi 간 술후 통증 차이 없음(SR, RCT 5편 n=554, 낮음~매우낮음). 단 손파일(hand file)은 초기 48h 유의하게 더 아픔 — 근단 밖 압출(apical extrusion)이 통증의 실질 매개. [합의수준]
> - 세정 활성화(irrigation activation) 축: Day-1 통증에 대해 7기법 네트워크 메타회귀(31편 정량 n=2595) — 레이저활성화세정(Laser-Activated Irrigation, LAI)이 1위(SUCRA 85%), 수동역동세정(Manual Dynamic Irrigation, MDI)이 최하위(SUCRA 1.5%). LAI vs MDI(MD −2.46 VAS)만 임상적 최소중요차(MCID 1.0)를 넘음. [근거강함(순위)/근거약함(신뢰도)]
> - 세정 전달 RCT: 괴사 하악 대구치 단일내원에서 수동초음파세정(PUI)·측방배출침(SNI) 모두 일반침(CNI)보다 24–72h 통증·부종·진통제 감소, PUI=SNI. 근단 밖 압출 최소화(측방류)가 음향 활성화만큼 중요할 수 있음. [근거강함, RCT]
> - 보조수단 축: 증상성 치근단치주염에서 저출력레이저치료(Low-Level Laser Therapy, LLLT, 30초)와 수산화칼슘(Calcium Hydroxide, CH) 첩약이 술후 통증·염증매개체 감소에서 동등 — LLLT가 단일내원 대안이 될 수 있음. 단 CH의 항균 역할은 미평가. [근거약함, 단일 RCT]
> - 관통 원리: 여러 축을 관통하는 실제 매개변수는 **근단 밖 압출(NaOCl·debris·세균)**이다. 압출을 줄이는 어떤 선택(측방배출침, 손파일 회피, MDI 회피)도 통증을 줄이는 방향으로 정렬됨. [claude해석]
> - 임상 권고: ① 증상성 치아를 술후 통증 고위험군으로 분류해 예방적/구조적 진통 계획 우선(술식 선택보다 영향 큼), ② 근단 밖 압출 최소화(측방배출침·MDI 회피·근단 patency 절제·정확한 WL), ③ 세정 활성화는 통증 목적이면 LAI 선호·MDI 회피(그러나 대부분 근거 low confidence), ④ EAL vs 방사선·rotary vs reciprocating는 통증만으로 결정하지 말고 정확도·해부·비용·피폭으로 결정.
> - 근거 한계: SR 2편은 이질성으로 메타분석 불가(정성종합, GRADE 낮음~매우낮음), 네트워크 메타회귀는 CINeMA 신뢰도 대부분 낮음, RCT는 소표본·단일센터·비맹검 다수. 임상 외삽은 상당 부분 [claude해석].

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 5 post-endodontic-pain papers (3 RCTs + 2 systematic reviews, incl. one Bayesian network meta-regression): post-operative endodontic pain is multifactorial and dominated by preoperative symptomatic status rather than by the choice of instrument kinematics or working-length method — pain peaks at 6–24 h and resolves by 7–14 days across all techniques; EAL vs radiographic working length and rotary vs reciprocating kinematics show no pain difference, whereas irrigation choices that reduce apical extrusion (laser-activated or ultrasonic activation, side-vented needles; avoiding manual-dynamic irrigation and hand files) consistently lower early pain, and a single 30-s LLLT application matches a 1-week calcium-hydroxide dressing for acute symptom control.

## 한줄요약

근관치료 후 통증 RCT 3편·SR 2편 통합. ①시간 경과 — 모든 술식에서 6~24h 최고 후 7~14일 소실. ②근관장측정(EAL vs 방사선)·kinematics(rotary vs reciprocating)는 통증 차이 없음. ③세정은 근단 밖 압출을 줄이는 선택(LAI·초음파·측방배출침, MDI·손파일 회피)이 초기 통증을 낮춤 — LAI 1위, MDI 최하위. ④LLLT 30초 = 수산화칼슘 1주 첩약(급성 증상). ⑤최강 변수는 술식이 아니라 술전 증상 상태.

## Summary

This page integrates five wiki pages that each isolate one procedural variable's effect on post-endodontic pain (working length, instrument kinematics, irrigation activation, irrigation delivery, and a photobiomodulation/medicament adjunct). The unifying finding is that **post-operative pain is multifactorial**, and that the single largest driver is not the procedural choice under study but the **patient's preoperative symptomatic status** — a variable that Hatipoğlu 2025 modeled formally and found to dominate the Day-1 pain signal regardless of technique.

The common time course is reproducible across every axis: pain peaks in the first 6–24 h and declines to minimal by 7–14 days, independent of technique (Singh 2026, Usman Sultan 2026, Zarei 2026). What separates techniques is the magnitude of the early peak, and the mechanistic lever connecting the axes is **apical extrusion** of NaOCl, debris, and bacteria beyond the foramen.

Key propositions:

1. **Preoperative symptomatic status dominates post-endodontic pain more than any procedural choice.** [근거강함] In the 7-technique network meta-regression, all treatment-specific slopes for symptomatic status were positive with credible intervals excluding zero — symptomatic teeth had substantially higher Day-1 pain regardless of activation method (Hatipoğlu 2025). Symptom-based risk stratification plausibly matters more than picking an "optimal" technique.
2. **Pain follows a technique-independent 6–24 h peak → 7–14 day resolution curve.** [근거강함] Rotary and reciprocating retreatment (Singh 2026), EAL vs radiographic single-visit (Usman Sultan 2026), and all three irrigation-delivery arms in necrotic molars (Zarei 2026) share this decline; techniques modulate peak height, not the curve shape.
3. **Working-length method (standalone EAL vs radiographic) does not affect post-op pain.** [근거약함, 매우 낮은 근거] 4 RCTs (n=414) showed no difference in pain incidence, intensity, analgesic use, or resolution time (4 h–7 d); one small three-arm trial suggested an EAL+radiograph hybrid (SLC) lowered early pain in symptomatic teeth (Usman Sultan 2026).
4. **Instrument kinematics (rotary vs reciprocating) does not affect retreatment pain, but hand files do worsen it.** [합의수준] 5 RCTs (n=554) found rotary ≈ reciprocating; where compared, hand files were significantly more painful in the first 48 h — implicating apical debris extrusion, not kinematics per se, as the operative mechanism (Singh 2026).
5. **Irrigation activation and delivery that minimize apical extrusion lower early pain.** [근거강함(방향)] LAI ranked best (SUCRA 85%) and MDI worst (SUCRA 1.5%) among 7 techniques; only LAI vs MDI (MD −2.46 VAS) exceeded the 1.0 MCID (Hatipoğlu 2025). In a necrotic-molar RCT, both PUI and a side-vented needle beat conventional open-ended needle irrigation at 24–72 h, with no PUI advantage over the side-vented needle — suggesting lateral-flow extrusion control rivals acoustic activation (Zarei 2026).
6. **A single LLLT application equals a 1-week calcium-hydroxide dressing for acute symptom control.** [근거약함, 단일 RCT] In symptomatic apical periodontitis (n=46), LLLT (976 nm, 30 s) and CH were statistically indistinguishable on pain (6/12/24/48 h), 1-week percussion pain, and periapical IL-8/Substance P reduction — positioning LLLT as a single-visit option, though CH's antimicrobial role was not tested (Otaify 2026).

The cross-cutting mechanistic thread — **apical extrusion** — explains why interventions across different axes align: any choice that reduces what is pushed past the foramen (side-vented needle, avoiding MDI, avoiding hand files, accurate WL keeping the tip within canal) points in the pain-reducing direction. ## Clinical Decision Points

1. **Stratify by preoperative symptoms first, not by technique.** Symptomatic teeth carry substantially higher post-op pain regardless of the instrument/irrigation choice (Hatipoğlu 2025). Prioritize symptom-based analgesic planning (e.g., pre-emptive/structured NSAID regimen) over chasing a marginally "better" technique — the symptom effect dwarfs most technique contrasts.
2. **Minimize apical extrusion as the common lever.** Prefer a side-vented needle to open-ended needle irrigation (Zarei 2026), avoid purely mechanical manual-dynamic irrigation (worst-ranked; plausibly raises apical pressure, Hatipoğlu 2025), avoid hand files where rotary/reciprocating is feasible (hand files more painful, Singh 2026), and keep the instrument/needle tip within the canal via accurate working length.
3. **If the goal is minimizing early pain from irrigation, prefer laser-activated irrigation and avoid MDI** — but temper expectations: only LAI vs MDI cleared the clinical threshold and most contrasts are low-confidence (Hatipoğlu 2025). Ultrasonic activation and side-vented needle delivery are reasonable, comparably effective options in practice (Zarei 2026).
4. **Do not choose EAL vs radiographic WL, or rotary vs reciprocating, on the basis of pain.** They are pain-equivalent (Usman Sultan 2026; Singh 2026). Drive those choices by accuracy, canal anatomy, filling-removal efficiency, cost, radiation avoidance (ALARA — pregnancy/pediatric), and operator familiarity instead.
5. **LLLT is a legitimate single-visit symptom-control adjunct, not a disinfection substitute.** A 30-s LLLT application matches a 1-week CH dressing for acute pain/inflammation and enables single-visit treatment while avoiding inter-appointment flare-up risk — but CH's antimicrobial disinfection role was not tested, so LLLT does not replace CH where long-term disinfection is the objective (Otaify 2026).

## Bottom Line (한 줄 결론)

근관치료 후 통증을 좌우하는 최강 변수는 술식이 아니라 술전 증상 상태이며, 술식 선택은 "근단 밖 압출을 줄이는 방향"으로 정렬하되(측방배출침·LAI/초음파, MDI·손파일 회피) EAL vs 방사선·rotary vs reciprocating은 통증이 아닌 정확도·해부·피폭 기준으로 결정한다.
