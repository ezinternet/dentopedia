## 한국어 핵심요약

> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
> - **결론(bottom line)**: 인사돌(Insadol)·이가탄(Igatan)으로 대표되는 OTC 잇몸약은 **기전상 항염 신호 억제는 일관되게 보이나, 사람에서 hard endpoint(치주낭깊이 Probing Depth, PD / 임상부착수준 Clinical Attachment Level, CAL)를 스케일링·치근활택술(Scaling and Root Planing, SRP) 이상으로 개선한다는 근거는 약하다.** 효과는 잇몸 염증 지표(치은지수 Gingival Index, GI / 탐침시출혈 Bleeding on Probing, BoP) 수준에 국한된다.
> - **두 계열로 나뉜다**: ① 인사돌 계열 = **옥수수 불검화물 (Zea mays Unsaponifiable)** ± **후박 (Magnoliae Cortex)** — 항염 host-modulation. ② 이가탄 계열 = **CELC** = 비타민C 150mg + 비타민E 10mg + **리소짐 (Lysozyme) 30mg** + **카르바조크롬 (Carbazochrome) 2mg** — 항산화·지혈·효소.
> - **인사돌 기전 근거는 탄탄**: in vitro에서 옥수수 불검화물+후박이 NF-κB·ERK1/2 MAPK·iNOS를 억제하고 NO·PGE₂·IL-1β·IL-6를 낮춤, 병용 시 시너지 (Kim 2018, 제조사 동국제약 funding).
> - **인사돌 동물 근거**: 비글견 결찰 치주염 RCT에서 옥수수+후박 경구 8주가 GI·PPD·CAL·BoP를 위약 대비 유의 개선, tissue TNF-α 감소 (Kim 2024) — 단 n=5/군, 인공 결찰 모델.
> - **인사돌 사람 근거는 빈약**: 제조사가 인용한 4개 임상시험(1982·1988·1991·1994)은 무작위배정 실패·표본수 근거 부재·부적절 통계검정으로 **설득력 있는 효능 근거 없음**, 1994 이후 신규 시험 0건 — 그런데 2011년 한국 치주약 시장 **66.8% 점유** (Choi 2015, 고려대·Mayo Clinic).
> - **국제판 자매물 ASU**: 아보카도/대두 불검화물 (Avocado/Soybean Unsaponifiables, ASU; 브랜드 Piascledine)이 쥐 치주염+관절염 모델에서 골소실 감소·치유 개선 (de Oliveira 2024) — 불검화물 host-modulation 계열을 보강하나 역시 동물 근거.
> - **인사돌을 기준약으로 쓴 연구**: 참나물(Pimpinella) 추출물 쥐 연구가 **ISD(=인사돌플러스 처방, 옥수수+후박 250mg/kg)를 양성대조약**으로 사용 — 새 herbal이 인사돌과 효과 동등 (Park 2026). 인사돌이 "활성 기준약"으로 통용됨을 보여주나 사람 효능을 증명하진 않음.
> - **이가탄 사람 근거**: 다기관 이중맹검 RCT(n=100, 8주)에서 CELC가 SRP 보조로 **GI만 위약 대비 소폭 개선**(Δ−0.18 vs +0.01, p=0.015; GEE OR=2.46) — **PD·CAL·치태지수는 유의차 없음** (Hong 2019, 명인제약 funding). 즉 잇몸 염증 증상만, 질환 자체는 못 바꿈.
> - **이가탄 성분별 근거**: (vit C) 국소 비타민C±주사형 혈소판농축섬유소(I-PRF)가 2기 치주염 RCT에서 SRP 단독 대비 **추가 임상이득 없음**(통증만 감소, Sherif 2025); (lysozyme) 리소짐·락토페린 함유 치약은 치은염 RCT에서 GI −0.53·치태 −0.96으로 **명확한 효과**(Hu 2025) — 단 **국소(치약) 전달**이지 정제 복용이 아님 → 전달 경로가 효과를 가른다.
> - **임상 메시지**: 환자에게는 "저위험·저비용의 가벼운 보조제로, 잇몸 붓기·출혈 증상은 다소 줄여줄 수 있으나 SRP를 대체하지 못하고 뼈·부착 회복은 기대 근거가 없다"로 설명. 가격 대비 효용은 논쟁적. 같은 패턴(국소 효과 O / 전신·gel 효과 미미, 이질성·비뚤림 부풀림)은 코엔자임Q10 (Coenzyme Q10, CoQ10) 보조제에서도 반복됨 (Rasoolzadeh 2022).

Synthesis of 9 wiki pages on Korea's two dominant OTC "gum drugs" — Insadol (Zea mays unsaponifiable ± Magnoliae cortex) and Igatan/CELC (vitamin C/E + lysozyme + carbazochrome): mechanistic and animal anti-inflammatory signals are consistent, but human evidence shows benefit confined to gingival-inflammation surrogates (GI/BoP) with no demonstrated effect on PD/CAL beyond SRP, and the registered-drug evidence base is methodologically weak.

## Summary

Korean patients routinely ask about over-the-counter "잇몸약" — chiefly **Insadol** (인사돌, DongKook Pharm) and **Igatan F** (이가탄에프, Myung-In Pharm). This page synthesizes what the wiki holds on their active ingredients, mechanism, clinical efficacy, and prognosis, and places them against a comparator nutraceutical adjunct (CoQ10).

The two products belong to different pharmacologic families but share the same evidentiary shape: **strong mechanistic/animal anti-inflammatory data, weak or surrogate-limited human efficacy.** Neither is a disease-modifying therapy; both are at best mild adjuncts to scaling and root planing (SRP) that nudge gingival-inflammation indices.

A recurring theme — visible across Igatan's lysozyme, vitamin C, and the CoQ10 literature — is that **delivery route, not just the molecule, decides the effect**: locally delivered/topical formulations (e.g., lysozyme toothpaste) outperform systemic tablets or gels pooled across routes.

## The Two Drug Families

### Family A — Insadol (host-modulation / unsaponifiables)

| Ingredient | Korean | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Titrated unsaponifiable *Zea mays* L. fraction | 옥수수 불검화정량추출물 | The single registered active of classic Insadol; anti-inflammatory host modulation |
| *Magnoliae* cortex extract | 후박 추출물 | Added in **Insadol Plus (인사돌플러스)**; magnolol/honokiol anti-inflammatory |

**International sister compound:** Avocado/Soybean Unsaponifiables (ASU, brand Piascledine) — same "unsaponifiable host-modulation" concept, also used in osteoarthritis.

### Family B — Igatan F (CELC: antioxidant / enzyme / hemostatic)

| Ingredient | Dose | Korean | Role |
|---|---|---|---|

| Vitamin E (tocopherol) | 10 mg | 비타민E | Antioxidant |
| Lysozyme | 30 mg | 리소짐 | Antibacterial enzyme, anti-inflammatory |

## Key Cross-Paper Findings

1. **Mechanism ≠ clinical proof.** The Insadol active-ingredient story is mechanistically credible (NF-κB/MAPK suppression, well-precedented for magnolol/honokiol), and confirmed at tissue level in dogs — but the registered human evidence remains weak (Choi 2015), and the newer combination animal work tests *Zea mays + Magnoliae*, which is **not** the same as the classic single-ingredient registered product.

2. **Igatan's effect is real but small and soft.** CELC produces a statistically significant ~0.2-point GI reduction beyond SRP at 4 weeks, but **no change in PD or CAL** — i.e., it touches gingival redness/bleeding, not attachment or bone. The carbazochrome (hemostatic) and vitamin-C (antioxidant) components are consistent with a "less bleeding, mildly less inflammation" symptom effect rather than disease modification.

3. **Route trumps molecule.** Lysozyme delivered *topically* in toothpaste gives a clear anti-gingivitis effect (Hu 2025), whereas vitamin C delivered into the pocket adds nothing over mechanical therapy (Sherif 2025). The same lesson recurs for CoQ10: pooled-route SR favors gel (Rasoolzadeh 2022) but is undermined by I²=72–89% heterogeneity and an effect-inflation gradient in high-risk-of-bias trials; the route-stratified evidence finds the gel route null.

4. **Market dominance ≠ evidence.** Insadol held 66.8% of the Korean periodontal-drug market (2011) on an evidence base whose newest trial is from 1994 — a cautionary EBM example.

## Clinical Bottom Line / Patient Counseling

- **Frame honestly**: "저위험·저비용 보조제. 잇몸 붓기·출혈 같은 증상은 다소 줄여줄 수 있으나, 잇몸병(뼈·부착 소실) 자체를 치료하거나 스케일링을 대체하지는 못합니다."
- **Not a substitute for SRP/OHI.** Every positive trial here tested the drug *as an add-on after* mechanical therapy.
- **No basis for bone/pocket regeneration claims** from any of these OTC products.
- **Safety is favorable** short-term (Igatan 8-wk no specific TEAEs; Insadol/dog labs normal), so it is a low-harm choice — the open question is cost-effectiveness for a surrogate-only benefit.
- **If recommending a topical**, ingredient-bearing dentifrices (lysozyme/lactoferrin) have better-controlled efficacy data for gingivitis than systemic tablets.
