> [!summary] 한국어 핵심요약
>
> - **핵심 메시지**: 기계적 치주치료(비수술 SRP·PMPR 또는 수술)에 더하는 숙주조절·영양보조제(오메가-3, 코엔자임Q10(CoQ10), 항산화 비타민, 아보카도-대두 불검화물(ASU), 매트릭스 분해효소 억제제(MMP-inhibitor))는 통계적으로 검출되긴 하나 임상부착수준(Clinical Attachment Level, CAL)·치주낭깊이(Probing Pocket Depth, PPD) 이득이 대부분 **1 mm 미만**이고 근거 확실성이 **low~very low**다. 마케팅이 근거를 앞서간 영역.
> - **오메가-3 (ω-3 다불포화지방산, PUFA)**: 13편 무작위대조시험(RCT) 메타분석에서 PPD −0.44 mm, CAL −0.51 mm, 탐침시출혈(Bleeding on Probing, BOP) −9.45%로 유의 — 비뚤림·출판비뚤림 낮음 (Heo 2022). 그러나 효과 크기가 작고 **유럽치주연맹(European Federation of Periodontology, EFP) 가이드라인은 보조 사용을 반대 권고** → 통계적 유의 ≠ 임상 권고.
> - **코엔자임Q10 (CoQ10) — 경로(route)가 결과를 가른다**: 신형 route-stratified SR(10편 RCT)은 **경구 120 mg/일만** 12주에 작은 유의 개선(PD −0.41 mm, CAL −0.52 mm), **국소 겔은 효과 없음**, 확실성 very low로 권고 불가라 결론 (Fernandez 2025).
> - **CoQ10 — 구형 SR과의 충돌**: 기존 11편 SR+MA는 CoQ10가 5개 지표를 유의 개선(PD SMD −0.96)하며 **겔 사용을 권장**한다고 결론했으나, 이질성 매우 높고(I² 72–89%) 비뚤림 위험 높은 연구에서 효과가 과대평가됨 (Rasoolzadeh 2022). → 두 SR의 상반된 결론은 RoB·route 층화로 설명됨; 신형 SR이 우선.
> - **항산화 비타민 (Vitamin C/E)**: 비타민C/E + 라이소자임 + 카르바조크롬(CELC, 국내 잇몸약 IGATAN F) 다기관 RCT(n=100)에서 치은지수(Gingival Index, GI)만 4주에 유의 개선(GEE OR=2.46, p=.022), **PD·CAL·PI는 유의차 없음** (Hong 2019) → 염증 지표 일부만 건드림.
> - **비타민C + 생체재료 병용**: 비타민C 함유 주사형 혈소판농축피브린(injectable PRF, I-PRF) 3군 RCT(n=45)에서 기계적 치석제거(PMPR) 단독 대비 6개월 BOP·PD·CAL·골 변화 **추가 이득 없음**, 유일한 차이는 술후 통증 감소뿐 (Sherif 2025).
> - **ASU (아보카도-대두 불검화물)**: 쥐 결찰 치주염 + 실험적 관절염 모델(n=60)에서 SRP 후 보조 ASU가 치주 골소실을 줄이고 관절염 동물의 악화 결과를 완화 — **동물·전임상 수준 근거** (Oliveira 2024). 류마티스-치주염 양방향 연결의 숙주조절 가설.
> - **MMP-억제 / 국소 독시사이클린**: 고전 숙주조절요법(Host Modulation Therapy, HMT)의 대표는 저용량 독시사이클린(sub-antimicrobial dose doxycycline, SDD)의 MMP 억제 작용. Aimetti 2025 3중맹검 RCT(n=60, 3·4기)는 수술 2주 전 **국소** 독시사이클린(BOOST) → 최소침습수술(MIST)+법랑기질단백(EMD)+이종골에서 CAL 4.1 vs 3.2 mm, 복합성공률 83.3 vs 46.7%(NNT=2.73)로 유의 우월. → 영양보조제군 중 **유일하게 임상적으로 의미있는 효과 크기**이나, 이는 **수술-재생 맥락의 국소 약물**이지 단독 보충제가 아님.
> - **공통 결론(임상 위계)**: ① 기계적 치면세균막 제거(SRP/PMPR)가 비타협 기반, ② 영양·항산화 보조제는 잘해야 sub-mm 부가효과(EFP 반대 또는 very-low 확실성), ③ 임상적 의미있는 부가효과는 약물-수술 병용(국소 DOX+재생술)에서만 관찰됨.
> - **구분되는 인접 주제**: 본 종합은 *전신·국소 숙주조절/영양* 보조제에 한정. 생균제(probiotics)·항균광역동치료(aPDT)는 [[periodontal-adjunctive-therapy-probiotics-pdt-overview]], 국내 OTC 잇몸약(인사돌·이가탄 성분)은 [[otc-periodontal-adjunct-drugs-insadol-igatan]]로 분리되어 있다.

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 7 papers on host-modulation and nutraceutical adjuncts to mechanical periodontal therapy (omega-3, CoQ10, antioxidant vitamins, ASU, MMP-inhibition): these agents consistently produce statistically detectable but sub-millimeter, low-to-very-low-certainty PD/CAL gains; positive meta-analyses conflict with guideline recommendations (EFP against omega-3) and with each other (CoQ10 by route and risk-of-bias), and the only clinically meaningful effect comes from local doxycycline within a regenerative-surgical protocol (NNT=2.73) — not from standalone supplements. Mechanical debridement remains the non-negotiable foundation.

## 한줄요약

기계적 치주치료에 더하는 숙주조절·영양보조제(오메가-3·CoQ10·항산화 비타민·ASU·MMP 억제) 7편 종합: 모두 통계적으로는 검출되나 1 mm 미만·low~very-low 확실성의 PD/CAL 이득에 그치고, 양성 메타분석이 가이드라인(EFP 오메가-3 반대) 및 서로(CoQ10 경로·비뚤림)와 충돌하며, 임상적으로 의미있는 효과는 단독 보충제가 아니라 재생수술에 병용한 국소 독시사이클린(NNT=2.73)에서만 나온다 — 기계적 치석제거가 비타협 기반.

## Thesis

"Host modulation" in periodontics covers any adjunct that targets the host inflammatory–destructive response rather than the biofilm directly: enzyme inhibitors (sub-antimicrobial doxycycline → MMP inhibition), anti-inflammatory/pro-resolving lipids (omega-3 PUFA → resolvins/protectins), antioxidants (CoQ10, vitamins C/E), and plant unsaponifiables (ASU). The marketing premise is that these "treat the cause" of tissue breakdown. The evidence premise, across this cluster, is far weaker and remarkably consistent:

1. **Effect sizes are sub-millimeter.** Omega-3 (PPD −0.44 mm, CAL −0.51 mm; Heo 2022) and oral CoQ10 (PD −0.41 mm, CAL −0.52 mm; Fernandez 2025) cluster around half a millimeter — the same magnitude as the ~0.3 mm benchmark for *all* NSPT adjuncts (John 2017 NMA, see [[periodontal-adjunctive-therapy-probiotics-pdt-overview]]). This is at or below the threshold of clinical detectability.
2. **Certainty is low to very low, and meta-analyses disagree.** The CoQ10 literature is the cautionary case: an older SR+MA recommended CoQ10 gel (PD SMD −0.96; Rasoolzadeh 2022) on the strength of high-heterogeneity, high-risk-of-bias trials, whereas a route-stratified SR found the gel inert and only oral 120 mg/day weakly effective at very low certainty (Fernandez 2025). The conflict is resolved by risk-of-bias and delivery-route stratification, not by recency alone.
3. **Statistical significance ≠ guideline endorsement.** Heo 2022 is a low-RoB positive meta-analysis, yet the EFP S3 guideline recommends *against* routine omega-3 supplementation — an explicit reminder that a significant pooled estimate does not clear the bar for a clinical recommendation.
4. **The only clinically meaningful effect is drug-plus-surgery, not a supplement.** Local doxycycline applied 2 weeks before MIST + EMD + xenograft produced a genuine effect size (CAL 4.1 vs 3.2 mm; composite success 83.3% vs 46.7%, NNT=2.73; Aimetti 2025) — but this is an MMP-modulating *antibiotic in a regenerative-surgical workflow*, not an over-the-counter nutraceutical taken in isolation.

**Bottom line:** mechanical biofilm removal (SRP/PMPR, ± regenerative surgery) is the non-negotiable foundation. Host-modulation/nutraceutical adjuncts are, at best, marginal add-ons; recommend them (if at all) with explicit framing of small effect size and weak certainty, and never as substitutes for debridement.

## Agents & Evidence

### 1. Omega-3 polyunsaturated fatty acids (anti-inflammatory / pro-resolving)
- **Heo 2022** (MA, 13 RCTs): supplement or dietary omega-3 → PPD −0.44 mm, CAL gain −0.51 mm, BOP −9.45%; low risk of bias, no publication bias.
- **Tension with guidelines:** despite the clean meta-analysis, EFP guidance does not endorse routine adjunctive omega-3 — small effect, heterogeneous dosing, uncertain durability. Mechanism (resolvin/protectin-mediated resolution of inflammation) is biologically plausible but clinically marginal at current doses.

### 2. Coenzyme Q10 (antioxidant / mitochondrial) — the route × risk-of-bias case study
- **Fernandez 2025** (route-stratified SR, 10 RCTs): **oral 120 mg/day** → PD −0.41 mm, CAL −0.52 mm at 12 weeks; **local gel → no significant effect**; overall very low certainty → no evidence-based recommendation.
- **Rasoolzadeh 2022** (SR+MA, 11 trials): CoQ10 improved PI, BI, PD (SMD −0.96), CAL (SMD −0.73), GI, favoring intra-pocket delivery — but I² 72–89% and larger effects in high-RoB studies.
- **Reconciliation:** the contradiction (gel recommended vs gel inert) is an artifact of pooling high-RoB trials without route stratification. Prefer the newer route-stratified appraisal; treat any CoQ10 claim as very-low certainty.

### 3. Antioxidant vitamins (C / E)
- **Hong 2019** (multicenter RCT, n=100, 8 wk): adjunctive vitamin C/E + lysozyme + carbazochrome (CELC, IGATAN F) → GI significantly lower at 4 wk (Δ −0.18 vs +0.01, p=0.015; GEE OR=2.46, p=0.022); **no significant difference in PD, CAL, or PI** — touches a gingival-inflammation index only. (CELC/IGATAN is also covered as a Korean OTC product in [[otc-periodontal-adjunct-drugs-insadol-igatan]].)
- **Sherif 2025** (3-arm double-blind RCT, n=45, stage II grade A): vitamin-C-loaded I-PRF (and plain I-PRF) added **no** clinical benefit over PMPR alone at 6 months on BOP/PD/CAL/PI/bone — only lower post-operative pain. Vitamin C as a biomaterial additive did not change periodontal endpoints.

### 4. Plant unsaponifiables — ASU (avocado/soybean)
- **Oliveira 2024** (animal: rat ligature periodontitis + experimental arthritis, n=60): adjunctive ASU 0.6 mg/kg after scaling reduced periodontal bone loss and blunted the worse healing seen in arthritic animals. **Preclinical only** — a host-modulation hypothesis tied to the rheumatoid-arthritis ↔ periodontitis axis, not yet a clinical recommendation.

### 5. MMP-inhibition / locally delivered doxycycline (classic host-modulation drug)
- **Aimetti 2025** (triple-blind RCT, n=60, stage III/IV): local doxycycline 2 weeks before MIST + EMD + xenograft (BOOST) → CAL gain 4.1 vs 3.2 mm (p=0.019), composite success 83.3% vs 46.7% (NNT=2.73), radiographic bone fill 3.5 vs 2.7 mm at 1 year.
- **Interpretation:** the one cluster member with a clinically meaningful effect — but it is an MMP-modulating antibiotic deployed inside a regenerative-surgical protocol, the opposite of a standalone supplement. It anchors the "host modulation works *when integrated with definitive therapy*" end of the spectrum. (Systemic sub-antimicrobial-dose doxycycline / Periostat is the textbook HMT agent and shares this MMP-inhibition mechanism.)

## Decision Framework

| Adjunct | Best evidence | Effect size | Certainty | Practical stance |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Omega-3 PUFA | MA, 13 RCT (Heo 2022) | PPD −0.44, CAL −0.51 mm | low; EFP **against** | Not routine; mention small effect if patient asks |
| CoQ10 (oral 120 mg/d) | route-strat SR (Fernandez 2025) | PD −0.41, CAL −0.52 mm | **very low** | No EBM recommendation; gel inert |
| CoQ10 (gel) | SR conflict (Rasoolzadeh vs Fernandez) | SMD −0.96 (high RoB) vs ~0 | very low | Do not rely on older positive MA |
| Vitamin C/E (CELC) | RCT (Hong 2019) | GI only; PD/CAL/PI ns | low | Gingival index effect only |
| Vitamin C + I-PRF | RCT (Sherif 2025) | no benefit over PMPR | moderate (clear null) | No added clinical value |
| ASU | animal (Oliveira 2024) | bone-loss reduction | preclinical | Hypothesis-generating only |
| Local doxycycline + regen surgery | RCT (Aimetti 2025) | CAL +0.9 mm, NNT 2.73 | moderate | Meaningful — but surgical/regenerative context |

**Clinical rule of thumb:** Foundation = SRP/PMPR (± regenerative surgery for deep intrabony defects). Supplements (omega-3, CoQ10, antioxidant vitamins, ASU) are optional, low-yield add-ons — counsel honestly that benefit is sub-millimeter and certainty weak. If host modulation is genuinely pursued for advanced disease, the evidence points to MMP-targeting drugs integrated with definitive (often surgical) therapy, not to consumer nutraceuticals.
