>
> - **핵심 메시지**: 외상치 관리는 단계마다 지배적 예후인자가 바뀌는 사슬(chain)이며, 전 과정을 관통하는 두 변수는 **근첨 성숙도(apex maturity)**와 **치주인대(PDL) 세포 생존**이다.
> - **예방 (prevention)**: 맞춤형(custom-made) 마우스가드가 탈구(avulsion)·치아파절 등 치아안면 손상을 유의하게 감소시키며 기성품보다 우월 — 단 기저 SR 질은 낮음 (Agarwal 2025, 우산고찰 9 SR, AMSTAR-2).
> - **응급 — 보관매체(storage medium)가 PDL 생존을 좌우**: 탈구치 재식 전 보관에서 **HBSS(Hank's Balanced Salt Solution)·프로폴리스·ORS(경구수분보충액)**가 PDL 세포 생존을 우유 이상으로 보존; **식염수·수돗물은 우유보다 열등** (De Brier 2020, 33편 SR+MA; 대리지표·GRADE low~very low).
> - **고정 (splinting)**: 재식한 탈구 영구치(소아)에서 **유연(flexible)·단기(short-term) 고정**이 더 나은 경향 — IADT 권고를 잠정 지지하나 대부분 비유의, GRADE very low (Kumari 2025, 7편/치아 975개).
> - **치수 예후 — 근첨 성숙도가 결정**: 측방탈구(lateral luxation) 후 치수괴사(pulp necrosis) 전체 57%, **성숙치 58% vs 미성숙치 12%**; 염증성 치근흡수(EIRR) ~11% — 성숙치라도 예방적·절대적 즉시 근관치료는 신중히 (Tewari 2024, 13편 SR+MA).
> - **재생근관치료(Regenerative Endodontic Procedure, REP) 예후**: 전반적 치유 >81%로 양호하나, **외상성 괴사는 치근발육(root development) 실패 위험 RR 3.59**, 전치 RR 1.90, 치근단병소 RR 2.55 — 외상 기원이 REP 결과를 악화 (Vitali 2025, 20편 SR+MA).
> - **근거 수준**: 5편 모두 GRADE very low~low — IADT 가이드라인이 임상시험을 앞서가는(guidelines outrun trials) 영역.
> - **임상 사슬 요약**: 맞춤 마우스가드(예방) → 우유/HBSS/프로폴리스/ORS 보관·식염수·수돗물 금지(응급) → 유연·단기 고정(안정화) → 근첨 성숙도로 치수 모니터링·미성숙치는 REP(치수 예후).
> - **인접 주제 연결**: 미성숙치 REP 술식 세부는 [[regenerative-endodontics-rep-overview]], 외상 후 활력치수 보존 결정은 [[vital-pulp-therapy-decision-ladder]], 고정의 교합·치주 측면은 [[occlusal-trauma-periodontitis-overview]].

## One-line Summary

Synthesis of 5 SR/MA/umbrella reviews across the dental-trauma timeline: custom mouthguards reduce avulsions/fractures (prevention); avulsed-tooth PDL-cell viability is best preserved by HBSS/propolis/ORS and milk, never saline or tap water (emergency); flexible short-term splinting is tentatively favored (stabilization); and pulpal prognosis is governed by apex maturity (lateral-luxation necrosis 58% mature vs 12% immature) with trauma-origin necrosis tripling REP root-development failure (RR 3.59) — apex maturity and PDL-cell survival recur as the decision drivers across the whole chain, all at GRADE very-low certainty.

## 한줄요약

외상치 전 과정 SR/MA 5편 종합: 맞춤 마우스가드가 탈구·파절을 줄이고(예방), 탈구치 PDL 세포 생존은 HBSS·프로폴리스·ORS·우유가 보존하며 식염수·수돗물은 금물(응급), 유연·단기 고정이 잠정 우세(안정화)하고, 치수 예후는 근첨 성숙도가 좌우(측방탈구 괴사 성숙치 58% vs 미성숙 12%)하며 외상성 괴사는 REP 치근발육 실패를 3.6배 높인다 — 근첨 성숙도와 PDL 생존이 전 사슬의 결정인자이고 근거는 모두 GRADE very-low다.

## Thesis

Traumatic dental injury (TDI) is not one decision but a sequence, and at each link a different variable dominates the prognosis. Reading the five reviews as one timeline yields a coherent clinical chain:

1. **Prevention.** Custom-made mouthguards significantly reduce dentofacial injuries (avulsions, fractures) and outperform stock/boil-and-bite types (Agarwal 2025). The strongest, cheapest intervention happens before the injury.
2. **Emergency / first-aid — the PDL is the asset.** Once a tooth is avulsed, prognosis is set by periodontal-ligament cell survival, which is a function of extra-oral time and storage medium. HBSS, propolis, and ORS preserve viability at least as well as milk; saline and tap water are actively worse than milk (De Brier 2020). The practical message for patients and first responders: milk if nothing better, never tap water.
3. **Stabilization — splint, don't rigidly fixate.** For replanted avulsed permanent teeth, flexible and short-term splinting trends toward better outcomes (Kumari 2025), supporting IADT guidance even though most comparisons are non-significant. Over-rigid or prolonged splinting is the avoidable error.
4. **Pulpal prognosis — apex maturity decides.** After lateral luxation, pulp necrosis is common in mature teeth (58%) but uncommon in immature/open-apex teeth (12%) (Tewari 2024). This argues against reflexive prophylactic endodontics in every luxated tooth and for maturity-stratified monitoring.
5. **When the pulp dies in an immature tooth — REP, but trauma worsens it.** Regenerative endodontic procedures heal >81% overall, yet trauma-origin necrosis triples the risk of root-development failure (RR 3.59), with incisor location and apical lesions adding risk (Vitali 2025). Trauma cases need REP with tempered expectations and close follow-up.

**Two variables recur across the entire chain — apex maturity (necrosis risk, REP candidacy and outcome) and PDL-cell survival (replantation prognosis).** Anchor decisions on those. And note the whole base is GRADE very-low: IADT guidelines are the operating standard precisely because the trials are thin.

## Decision Framework

| Stage | Dominant lever | Best action | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prevention | Mouthguard type | Custom-made > stock | Agarwal 2025 |
| Avulsion first-aid | Storage medium + EO time | Milk / HBSS / propolis / ORS; **never** saline/tap | De Brier 2020 |
| Replantation stabilization | Splint rigidity/duration | Flexible, short-term (IADT) | Kumari 2025 |
| Luxation pulp decision | Apex maturity | Monitor (esp. immature); avoid reflexive endo | Tewari 2024 |
| Immature necrotic tooth | Trauma etiology | REP, expect lower root-development success | Vitali 2025 |
