## One-line Summary

SR (26 studies, ≥24 mo): fiber post + all-ceramic SC achieves best nonvital survival (95.0% at 5 yr); no-post nonvital SCs drop to 85.7%; tooth vitality significantly predicts FDP survival (84.9% vs 81.3%, P=.049).

## Summary

This systematic review synthesized 26 studies (minimum 24-month follow-up) to evaluate survival of full-coverage tooth-supported fixed restorations — single crowns (SCs) and fixed dental prostheses (FDPs) — disaggregated by tooth vitality, post type, and prosthetic material. Key finding: tooth vitality contributes positively to restoration survival across all material and post-type combinations. Among nonvital teeth, fiber post + all-ceramic SC is the highest-performing combination; absence of post carries the steepest survival penalty.

## Key Contributions

- Quantifies the three-way interaction of vitality × post type × crown material on 5-year SC survival
- Demonstrates all-ceramic SCs with fiber post (95.0%) statistically outperform metal-ceramic with fiber post (91.3%) on nonvital teeth
- No-post nonvital SCs (85.7%) represent the lowest-survival identifiable subgroup — a clinically actionable threshold
- FDP survival penalty for nonvital abutments is modest but statistically significant (−3.6%, P=.049)

## Methodology

- Study design: Systematic review
- Included studies: 26 (all ≥24 months clinical observation)
- Primary outcome: 5-year survival rates, stratified by vitality, post type, and crown material
- Note: full text not retrieved; data from abstract

## Results

**Single Crown 5-Year Survival:**

| Crown type | Post/tooth status | 5-yr survival |
|---|---|---|
| All-ceramic | Vital (no post) | 98.3% |
| All-ceramic | Nonvital + fiber post | **95.0%** |
| Metal-ceramic | Vital (no post) | 97.1% |
| Metal-ceramic | Nonvital + cast metal post | 90.7% |
| Metal-ceramic | Nonvital + fiber post | 91.3% |
| Metal-ceramic | Nonvital, no post | **85.7%** |

Key contrast: all-ceramic + fiber post (95.0%) > metal-ceramic + fiber post (91.3%) — statistically significant.

**FDP 5-Year Survival:**
- Vital abutments: 84.9%
- Nonvital abutments: 81.3% (P=.049)
