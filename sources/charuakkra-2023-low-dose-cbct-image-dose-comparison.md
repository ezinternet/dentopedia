## One-line Summary

Phantom study: ULD/LD CBCT (ProMax 3D Mid, Orthophos SL) cut dose ~6× with 'acceptable-to-good' quality; high tube voltage reduces dose without quality loss.

## 한줄요약

팬텀 연구: ULD/LD CBCT(ProMax 3D Mid·Orthophos SL)가 선량 ~6배 절감하며 '수용~양호' 화질. 고관전압이 화질저하 없이 선량저감.

## 1. Document Information

- Title: Comparison of subjective image analysis and effective dose between low-dose cone-beam computed tomography machines
- Authors: Charuakkra A, Mahasantipiya P, Lehtinen A, Koivisto J, Järnstedt J
- Year: 2023 | Journal: Dentomaxillofacial Radiology | DOI: 10.1259/dmfr.20220176
- Study type: in-vitro

## 2. Key Contributions

- ULD and LD protocols achieved 'acceptable-to-good' subjective quality on 5-point Likert across 4 radiologists.
- ~6-fold dose reduction vs standard protocols.
- High-tube-voltage protocols reduce dose without degrading image quality → ULD/LD adoptable as routine diagnosis/planning.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

3 human-equivalent phantoms scanned in ULD/LD/STD modes on ProMax 3D Mid and Orthophos SL; quality scored by 4 OMF radiologists; intra/inter-rater agreement; effective doses computed.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Standard mode gives highest quality but ULD/LD remain diagnostically acceptable at a fraction of the dose; supports defaulting to lower-dose modes for routine tasks.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Subjective (not task-diagnostic) quality; phantom; 2 units; inter-rater agreement moderate (0.44–0.76).
