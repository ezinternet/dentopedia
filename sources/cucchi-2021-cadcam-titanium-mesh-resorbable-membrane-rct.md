## Why Ingested

GBR 6패널 분류 인터랙티브 도구(Panel IV)의 핵심 근거 논문. CAD/CAM 맞춤형 Ti mesh에 흡수성 막을 추가하는 것(Mesh+)이 막 없이 사용하는 것(Mesh-)보다 치유 합병증·골재생률을 유의하게 개선하는지를 최초로 RCT로 검증. "막 추가군 수치상 우수(합병증 13% vs 33%, RR 82% vs 74%)이나 비열등성 검정 inconclusive" — Panel IV 사용 결정에 직접 영향. [[wiki/implants/vertical-ridge-augmentation/cucchi-2017-dptfe-membrane-vs-titanium-mesh-collagen-rct]] 계열에 추가되는 막 유무 비교 근거.

## One-line Summary

RCT (n=30) comparing CAD/CAM customized Ti mesh alone (Mesh−, 33% complications) vs with cross-linked collagen membrane (Mesh+, 13% complications) for vertical/horizontal ridge augmentation: non-inferiority inconclusive; all outcomes numerically favor Mesh+ but NS.

## 한줄요약

RCT(n=30): CAD/CAM 맞춤형 Ti 메시 단독(합병증 33%) vs +가교화 콜라겐막(13%)으로 수직·수평 골증대 — 비열등성 검정 inconclusive; 막 추가군 수치상 우수하나 모두 유의차 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: RCT (noninferiority design, double-blind)
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Implants Research 2021;32(12):1411-1424
- **Period**: Patients treated 2017–2019; 6-month re-entry

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **First RCT** comparing Ti mesh ± resorbable membrane for GBR — no prior comparative study existed
2. **Healing complications**: Mesh− 33.3% vs Mesh+ 13.3% (NS, p=0.39); exposure: 26.7% vs 6.7% — numerically halved by membrane
3. **Regeneration rate**: Mesh− 74.3% vs Mesh+ 82.3% (NS, p=0.44); VBG 4.7 mm vs 6.4 mm (NS, p=0.08)
4. **Noninferiority inconclusive** for healing complications AND regeneration rate — neither group proven definitively equivalent or superior
5. All 30 patients received 71 implants; survival rate 94% (Mesh−) vs 97% (Mesh+); 3 total failures

## 3. Methodology

- **Design**: Parallel-group, randomized, controlled, noninferiority RCT; noninferiority margin Δ=10%
- **Group A (Mesh−)**: CAD/CAM Ti mesh alone (BTK 3D-Mesh); fixed with osteosynthesis screws
- **Group B (Mesh+)**: CAD/CAM Ti mesh + cross-linked collagen membrane (Cytoplast RTM) over mesh
- **Graft**: Autologous bone (ramus scraper) + xenograft (Zcore, Osteogenics) 50:50 mix
- **Outcomes**: Healing/surgical complications, pseudo-periosteum type (1–3), bone density, PBV/RBV/RR, VBG, implant stability/osteointegration
- **Volumetric analysis**: CBCT pre/post; PBV = planned volume, RBV = regenerated, RR = RBV/PBV × 100

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Mesh− (n=15) | Mesh+ (n=15) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Healing complications | 33.3% | 13.3% | 0.39 |
| Exposure rate | 26.7% | 6.7% | 0.33 |
| Pseudo-periosteum Type 3 | 4/15 (26.7%) | 1/15 (6.7%) | NS |
| RBV (mm³) | 803 | 843 | NS |
| RR (%) | 74.3 | 82.3 | 0.44 |
| VBG (mm) | 4.7 | 6.4 | 0.08 |
| Implant survival | 94.0% | 97.0% | NS |

- Noninferiority CI for healing complications: −0.44 to 0.17 (includes Δ and 0) → **inconclusive**
- Noninferiority CI for RR: includes Δ and 0 → **inconclusive**

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (n=15/group); underpowered for superiority analysis
- No post-loading follow-up; bone stability after prosthetic loading unknown
- Both anterior and posterior sites pooled; site-specific analysis missing
- Single surgeon; technique-dependent results

## 7. Glossary

- **CAD/CAM Ti mesh**: Patient-specific sintered laser melting (SLM) titanium mesh; passive fit, no intraoperative bending
- **Mesh−**: Ti mesh alone, no additional membrane
- **Mesh+**: Ti mesh + cross-linked collagen membrane (Cytoplast RTM)
- **PBV**: Planned Bone Volume — from digital planning
- **RBV**: Regenerated Bone Volume — from CBCT at re-entry
- **RR**: Regeneration Rate — RBV/PBV × 100%
- **VBG**: Vertical Bone Gain — linear measurement
- **Pseudo-periosteum**: Fibrous connective tissue under mesh; Type 1 (<1mm) / Type 2 (1–2mm) / Type 3 (>2mm)
