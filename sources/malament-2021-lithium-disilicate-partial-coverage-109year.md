## 2. Key Contributions
- 10-year cumulative survival 95.6%
- Annual failure rate 0.3%/year (6 failures total)
- Inlays (n=246): 93.9% survival at 9.9 years
- Onlays (n=305): 98.3% survival at 9.8 years
- Molar teeth: higher failure risk
- Failure mode: bulk fracture (no debonding reported)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Long-term retrospective study of pressed acid-etched lithium disilicate (IPS e.max Press) partial coverage restorations in private practice. 551 restorations (inlays n=246, onlays n=305), mean patient age 62 years, follow-up to 10.9 years. Overall failure rate 0.3%/year; 10-year estimated cumulative survival 95.6%. Inlay survival 93.9% (9.9yr), onlay survival 98.3% (9.8yr). 6 total failures (bulk fracture). Molar teeth: higher failure risk. Age, sex: no significant effect.
