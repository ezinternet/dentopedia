## Why Ingested

OMS 클러스터([[wiki/oral-medicine/psillas-2020-odontogenic-maxillary-sinusitis-comprehensive-review]])의 핵심 임상 질문 — "근단병소 크기/유형이 상악동 점막비후에 어느 정도 기여하는가?" — 에 직접 답하는 정량적 CBCT + 조직면역병리 데이터 제공. 기존 OMS 페이지들이 병인 서술에만 머문 부분을 MSMT 수치 및 면역세포 프로필로 보강.

## One-line Summary
Prospective observational study (n=50 patients, 328 teeth) using CBCT + histology/IHC showing periapical lesions correlate with significantly greater maxillary sinus mucosal thickening (3.95 mm vs 1.23 mm, p<0.01), with 50% granuloma and 40% cyst distribution and characteristic CD20+/CD79α+-dominant immune infiltrate.

## 한줄요약
CBCT + 조직/면역병리 관찰연구(n=50, 치아 328개) — 근단병소 있는 치아 인접 상악동 점막두께 3.95 mm vs 병소 없는 치아 1.23 mm (p<0.01); 병소 분포는 육아종 50% + 낭종 40%; 면역세포는 CD20+/CD79α+ B림프구 우세.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Medicina (MDPI), 2021, 57(8), 840
- **DOI**: 10.3390/medicina57080840
- **Type**: Observational (prospective cohort/case series)
- **Setting**: University of Medicine and Pharmacy "Grigore T. Popa," Iași, Romania; 2017–2020
- **Ethics**: Helsinki Declaration compliant; Ethics Committee approval nr. 14.05.2015

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Quantified CBCT-measured 맥실러리 사이너스 점막두께 (Maxillary Sinus Mucosa Thickness, MSMT) adjacent to periapical lesions vs. healthy teeth: 3.95 ± 0.8 mm vs 1.23 ± 0.6 mm (p < 0.01).
2. Provided CBCT Periapical Status Index (PSI) distribution across 328 maxillary premolars and molars in 50 patients.
3. Demonstrated that 92/100 sinuses showed mucosal thickening, with 33% having >10 mm (MSMT Class 5).
4. Histopathological breakdown: 50% 치근단 육아종 (periapical granuloma), 10% granuloma with cystic potential, 40% 치근단 낭종 (periapical cyst).
5. IHC profiling: CD4+, CD8+ T-lymphocytes and CD20+ B-lymphocytes diffuse in all cysts and some granulomas; CD79α+ plasma cells predominantly in granulomas.
6. Discussed clinical implications for sinus-lifting and implant planning post-periapical surgery.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Sample**: 1450 initial sinusitis patients screened; 628 had maxillary periapical lesions; 50 enrolled (strict inclusion/exclusion).
- **Inclusion**: Systemically healthy adults 18–70, chronic periapical lesions in maxillary premolar/molar zone, no treatment in preceding 6 months.
- **CBCT protocol**: Planmeca 3D Mid scanner, FOV 10×200 mm, voxel 200–400 µm, 80–85 kV.
- **PSI classification**: Periapical Status Index 1–5 (Estrela 2008 CBCT-based index).
- **MSMT classification**: Class 1 (none) to Class 5 (>10 mm), threshold >1 mm considered abnormal.
- **Histology**: Hematoxylin-eosin after curettage/apicectomy.
- **IHC panel**: CD4, CD8, CD20, CD68, CD79α antibodies; BenchMark XT auto-staining.
- **Statistics**: Mann-Whitney U test, Pearson chi-square, Pearson correlation; SPSS 20.0.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Mean MSMT (teeth with periapical lesions) | 3.95 ± 0.8 mm |
| Mean MSMT (teeth without periapical lesions) | 1.23 ± 0.6 mm |
| Mean MSMT overall | 4.30 ± 2.23 mm |
| MSMT Class 5 (>10 mm) frequency | 33% of sinuses |
| Periapical granuloma | 50% of cases |
| Periapical cyst | 40% of cases |
| Granuloma with cystic potential | 10% of cases |
| Male predominance (periapical lesions) | 74%, χ²=6.21, p=0.002 |
| MSMT prevalence peak age | 41–50 years (33.2%) |

- PSI 4–5 most frequent in right M2 (52.8%) and right PM2 (27.0%).
- CD79α+ plasma cells predominantly in periapical granulomas.
- CD8+ cytotoxic T cells more numerous in outer wall of periapical cysts.
- CD68+ macrophages in all cysts, primarily subepithelial zone.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small final sample (n=50) due to strict inclusion/exclusion criteria; no multivariate logistic regression.
- No control group without sinusitis symptoms.
- Authors recommend larger prospective studies.
- MSMT threshold for clinical significance not definitively established.

## 7. Glossary
- **CBCT (Cone-Beam Computed Tomography, 콘빔 컴퓨터 단층촬영)**: 3D dental/maxillofacial imaging modality.
- **PSI (Periapical Status Index, 치근단 상태 지수)**: CBCT-based 5-class periapical lesion severity index.
- **MSMT (Maxillary Sinus Mucosa Thickness, 상악동 점막두께)**: CBCT-measured mucosal thickness on coronal/sagittal reconstructions.
- **IHC (Immunohistochemistry, 면역조직화학염색)**: Antibody-based tissue staining to identify cell types.
- **CD20+**: B lymphocyte marker (humoral immunity).
- **CD79α+**: Plasma cell / mature B cell marker.
- **CD4+**: Helper T lymphocyte marker (cellular immunity).
- **CD8+**: Cytotoxic T lymphocyte marker.
- **CD68+**: Macrophage marker.
- **OMS (Odontogenic Maxillary Sinusitis, 치성 상악동염)**: Sinusitis of dental origin.
