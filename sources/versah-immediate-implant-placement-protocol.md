## One-line Summary
Versah manufacturer protocol (2-page) for immediate implant placement into extraction sockets using osseodensification, emphasizing apical anchorage and compaction allografting.

## 1. Document Information
- **Title:** Osseodensification Immediate Implant Placement Protocol
- **Authors:** Versah LLC
- **Year:** 2024
- **DOI:** (manufacturer document)
- **Study type:** narrative-review

## 2. Key Contributions
Vendor reference codifying the apical-engagement principle: implant diameter > tooth apex + final Densah bur ≥ socket apex. Pairs with composite 70/30 cancellous/cortical allograft compacted by the second-to-last bur.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Three-step protocol with explicit selection rules for implant diameter and final bur diameter based on socket apex dimensions.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Protocol outcome: primary stability provided by apical socket (not coronal walls); standardized allograft selection (70/30 mix) and compaction technique.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Manufacturer marketing document — not peer-reviewed. Apical-anchorage principle requires sufficient apical bone (not all socket morphologies). Allograft selection ratio (70/30) not validated against alternatives in this document.
