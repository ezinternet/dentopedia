## Why Ingested

임플란트–어버트먼트 연결부의 안착 효과(settling effect)가 보철물 오적합(misfit)에 미치는 영향을 in vitro로 정량화한 연구. [[prosthetic-materials/couso-queiruga-2025-misfit-implant-prosthesis-bone-loss]] 의 임플란트–보철물 미스핏 임상 결과를 연결부 유형별 기계적 기초 근거로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (n=32) comparing settling effect (axial displacement) across four internal implant–abutment connection types (internal hex, internal octagon, Morse hex 6°, Morse hex 11°) at three torque levels (20, 25, 30 Ncm); octagon showed consistently the least settling and internal hexagon the most.

## 한줄요약

내부 연결형 임플란트 4종(내부 육각, 내부 팔각, 모스테이퍼 6°·11°)에서 반복 토크(20·25·30 Ncm) 적용 시 안착 효과(axial displacement)를 비교한 in vitro 연구(n=32); 팔각형이 가장 적은 안착량, 내부 육각형이 가장 큰 안착량을 보였다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Quantified axial displacement (settling effect) across four internal implant–abutment connection geometries under clinically relevant torque levels.
- Demonstrated that connection geometry significantly affects the magnitude of settling at every stage of prosthetic fabrication (impression coping, resin pattern, final metal frame, abutment connection).
- Established that repeated application of 30 Ncm torque reaches a plateau after the second application (no further significant settling on 3rd–5th repetitions).
- Identified octagon connection as the most dimensionally stable; internal hexagon as the most prone to settling-induced misfit.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Sample**: 32 implant analog–abutment assemblies; 8 per group (internal hexagon, internal octagon, Morse hex 6°, Morse hex 11°). Periosave system (Mana Idea Bartar). Normal diameter 4 mm; narrow abutment 4.5 mm.
- **Mounting**: Analogs embedded in type IV dental stone (GC Fujirock EP) using a surveyor for vertical uniformity.
- **Stages measured**:
  1. Impression coping connection (hand-wrench then torque)
  2. Abutment connection on master cast
  3. Resin abutment (autopolymerized resin, ~2 mm layer)
  4. Final metal framework (Ni-Cr alloy, phosphate-bonded investment)
- **Torques applied**: 20, 25, 30 Ncm via digital torque controller (Lutron TQ-8800)
- **Displacement measurement**: Digital indicator (Mitutoyo, 0.001 mm resolution); measurements repeated 3×, mean reported
- **Statistics**: Repeated-measures ANOVA (MANOVA) and one-way ANOVA with Tukey post hoc; SPSS 17; significance p<0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Stage | Highest settling | Lowest settling |
|---|---|---|
| Impression coping (20 Ncm) | Hexagon (12.5 µm) | Octagon (4.1 µm) |
| Impression coping (30 Ncm) | Hex = MT6° = MT11° (~24 µm) | Octagon (12.3 µm) |
| Resin abutment (30 Ncm) | Hexagon (29.5 µm) | Octagon (15.6 µm) |
| Final frame (30 Ncm) | Hexagon (30.3 µm) | Octagon (14.5 µm) |
| Abutment connection (30 Ncm, 5th rep) | Hexagon (34.7 µm) | Octagon (18.1 µm) |

- All group differences statistically significant (p<0.001) at all torque levels and stages.
- Repeated 30 Ncm torque: significant difference between 1st and 2nd application; no significant additional settling from 2nd through 5th repetition.
- Settling increases with torque magnitude across all connection types.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Pure in vitro design; clinical conditions (mucosal compliance, saliva, occlusal load) not replicated.
- Single implant system (Periosave); generalizability to other brands uncertain.
- Only narrow (4.5 mm) abutment heights (~5 mm) tested; wider or taller abutments may behave differently.
- No fatigue (cyclic loading) component — settling under functional cycling not assessed.
- Clinical misfit tolerance thresholds for internal connections remain undefined in the literature.

## 6. Related Work

- Branemark: acceptable misfit ≤10 µm; Jemt et al. suggest ≤150 µm clinically acceptable for external connections.
- Literature gap noted: no established clinical misfit tolerance for internal connections.
- Morse taper implants theoretically provide cold-welding/friction-lock seating — yet this study shows intermediate settling compared to hexagon designs.

## 7. Glossary

- **Settling effect (안착 효과)**: Axial displacement occurring when micro-rough metal contact surfaces are compressed under torque, reducing total length of the abutment–implant assembly.
- **Passive fit (수동적 적합)**: Ideal seating of prosthesis on implant with minimal induced stress.
- **Internal hexagon (내부 육각)**: Internal connection with hexagonal anti-rotation feature.
- **Internal octagon (내부 팔각)**: Internal connection with octagonal anti-rotation feature.
- **Morse hex taper (모스 헥스 테이퍼)**: Conical internal connection with hexagonal indexing; angle variants 6° and 11°.
- **Axial displacement (축 방향 변위)**: Vertical shortening of abutment–implant stack measured along the long axis.
