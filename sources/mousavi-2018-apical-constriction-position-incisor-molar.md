## One-line Summary
In vitro (90 teeth, Iranian population): AC–AF distance 0.847mm (incisor) vs 0.709mm (molar), p=0.035; AC–AA 1.23mm vs 1.01mm (p=NS); recommends WL endpoint 0.85mm short of foramen for incisors, 0.70mm for molars.

## 1. Document Information
Isfahan University of Medical Sciences, Iran. European Journal of Dentistry 2018. Cross-sectional in vitro study measuring AC and AF positions under 25× magnification with Motic camera.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Population-specific AC position data (Iranian population)
- Incisors have significantly wider AC–AF distance than molars (p=0.035)
- AC–AA distances not significantly different between tooth types (p=0.172)
- Clinical implication: WL should be set tooth-type-specifically, not uniformly 0.5mm
- Proposed endpoints: **0.85mm for incisors, 0.70mm for molars** (short of AF)

## 3. Methodology
- 90 extracted teeth: 45 incisors (single-rooted), 45 molars (multi-rooted)
- Isfahan Province; reasons: periodontal disease or orthodontic extraction
- Sectioning oriented to expose foramen exit direction
- 25× stereomicroscope + Motic camera for distance measurement
- Statistics: descriptive statistics + independent t-test; p=0.05

## 4. Key Results

| Measurement | Incisors | Molars | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| AC to AF (mean ± SD) | **0.847 ± 0.33 mm** | **0.709 ± 0.27 mm** | **0.035** (significant) |
| AC to AA (mean ± SD) | 1.23 ± 0.39 mm | 1.01 ± 0.38 mm | 0.172 (NS) |

- Recommended WL terminus: 0.85mm from AF (incisors), 0.70mm from AF (molars)
- Conventional "0.5mm from AF" rule: slightly short for incisors, appropriate for molars

## 5. Limitations
- In vitro; no age/sex data on donors
- Single-race population (Iranian) — generalizability uncertain
- AC defined anatomically (narrowest constriction) — clinical EAL correlation not tested
- No micro-CT; relies on cross-sectional microscopy

## 6. Related Work
- Pisano 2024 — umbrella review on EAL accuracy; AC as reference point
- Versiani 2022 — in vivo micro-CT EAL accuracy at major foramen
- Ahn 2016 — clinical survey: WL set 0.5–1.0mm short of radiographic apex (78%)

## 7. Glossary
- **AC**: Apical Constriction (minor foramen; narrowest point)
- **AF**: Apical Foramen (major foramen)
- **AA**: Anatomical Apex (tip of root)
- **CDJ**: Cementodentinal Junction (AC approximates this)
