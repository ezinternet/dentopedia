## One-line Summary

Abstract-only multicentre split-mouth RCT (105 children, 210 teeth): zirconia crowns and stainless steel crowns had comparable 2-year success (RR 0.99, 95% CI 0.96–1.01; p=0.350) in primary molars, but zirconia retention was lower (RR 0.95, 95% CI 0.92–0.98; p=0.001); ~2/3 of parents preferred zirconia.

## 한줄요약

abstract-only 다기관 split-mouth RCT(105명, 210치): 유구치에서 지르코니아 크라운과 SSC의 2년 성공률 동등(RR 0.99, 95% CI 0.96–1.01; p=0.350)이나 지르코니아 유지력은 낮음(RR 0.95, 0.92–0.98; p=0.001); 부모 약 2/3가 지르코니아 선호.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Multicentre split-mouth head-to-head of zirconia vs SSC in primary molars over 2 years (success + retention + preference).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Split-mouth multicentre RCT; 105 children, 210 teeth randomized to ZC or SSC; primary outcome 2-yr success (absence of major failure); Poisson regression, paired t-tests, ANCOVA. (Abstract-level only.)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Success comparable: RR 0.99 (95% CI 0.96–1.01; p=0.350).
- Retention lower for ZC: RR 0.95 (95% CI 0.92–0.98; p=0.001).
- ~2/3 of parents preferred ZC; children no clear preference.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- 2-year follow-up; retention disadvantage for ZC; abstract-only (no per-failure detail).

## 7. Glossary

- **Split-mouth**: each child receives both treatments (one per side), controlling patient factors.
- **Zirconia crown (ZC)**: esthetic preformed crown; here lower retention than SSC.
