## One-line Summary
Sci Rep 2026;16:11237: 3D-printed hybrid ceramic (Saremc Print Crowntec) + NTAP plasma × sandblasting µSBS — SP50 (50µm SB+NTAP) 2.70 MPa highest; NTAP alone competitive; all values low (2.12–2.70 MPa) suggesting challenging material to bond.

## 1. Key Results
- 5 groups: NTAP alone, 50µm SB, 110µm SB, SB50+NTAP, SB110+NTAP
- SP50 (50µm SB + NTAP): 2.70 ± 0.49 MPa (highest, p<0.05 vs S110 and S50)
- NTAP alone: 2.35 MPa — comparable to sandblasting
- All values relatively low (2.12–2.70 MPa) vs conventional zirconia/ceramic bonding
- 3D-printed hybrid ceramics: challenging substrate for adhesive bonding
- Thermocycling: performed before testing
