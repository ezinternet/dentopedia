## Why Ingested

GentleWave® multisonic (negative-apical-pressure) activation is marketed as the most powerful irrigant delivery system, but [[wiki/endodontics/irrigation/boutsioukis-2022-irrigants-irrigation-methods-status-directions]] holds that no activation method has been proven to improve clinical RCT outcomes over the NaOCl+EDTA standard. This SR directly tests multisonic vs ultrasonic activation on the most clinically relevant target (multispecies biofilm) and finds them broadly equivalent — reinforcing the "no method proven superior" thesis even at the bench level.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (3 in-vitro studies, multispecies biofilm in extracted teeth) finding GentleWave multisonic activation achieved bacterial reduction similar to passive ultrasonic irrigation — multisonic did not consistently outperform ultrasonic.

## 한줄요약

다균종 biofilm을 심은 발치치아 모델 3편(in vitro)을 모은 체계적 문헌고찰: GentleWave 멀티소닉 활성화는 초음파 활성화(PUI)와 세균 감소량이 비슷했고, 멀티소닉이 일관되게 더 우수하지는 않았다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Systematic review (PRISMA; PROSPERO CRD42024583277), no meta-analysis
- **Journal**: Cureus 17(3): e80938 (2025)
- **Authors**: Preethi Varadan, Sangavi Ra, Mathan R. Rajendran — Sri Ramachandra Institute, Chennai, India
- **Databases**: SCOPUS, PubMed, Cochrane, EBSCO host, LILACS + Google Scholar manual search (inception–Dec 2024)
- **Included studies**: 3 in-vitro studies (Coaguila-Llerena 2022, Zhang 2018, Alquria 2023)
- **Risk of bias**: modified CRIS + JBI criteria for in-vitro studies

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR specifically comparing **multisonic (GentleWave®) vs ultrasonic** irrigant activation restricted to **polymicrobial/multispecies biofilm** models (excludes monospecies E. faecalis models as non-representative of in-vivo flora).
- Bottom line: multisonic demonstrated **similar efficacy** to ultrasonic in reducing intraradicular biofilm; superiority of GWS was study-dependent, not consistent.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **PICOS**: P = polymicrobial biofilm models; I = multisonic irrigant activation; C = ultrasonic irrigant activation; O = biofilm removal; S = in-vitro studies.
- Eligibility limited to extracted-tooth models incubated with multispecies/dual-species biofilm; monospecies models, reviews, abstracts, case reports excluded.
- Outcome assays: qPCR (16S rRNA, NGS) in 2 studies; CFU in 1 study.
- 10 records identified → 5 screened → 3 included.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Study | Biofilm | Comparison | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Coaguila-Llerena 2022 | 10-taxon polymicrobial (molars, sealed apex) | GWS vs PUI (EndoUltra) | **Similar** bacterial reduction; no significant difference |
| Zhang 2018 | E. faecalis + S. mutans (molars) | GWS vs ProUltra PiezoFlow | GWS 99.91% vs PiezoFlow 98.01% DNA reduction (GWS higher) |
| Alquria 2023 | E. faecalis + E. coli (premolars) | GWS+MIT vs PUI vs XP-Endo Finisher | GWS + minimally invasive technique **most effective**; PUI and XPEF also highly effective |

- Apical prep: ultrasonic groups prepared to 35/.04; GWS used smaller minimal preps (20/.06, 15/.04, 20/.04) — confounds direct comparison.
- Except Coaguila-Llerena, GWS edged out ultrasonic, but the overall qualitative synthesis concluded **comparable/similar** efficacy.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 3 in-vitro studies; high heterogeneity in biofilm species, incubation, apical prep size, irrigation protocol, and assay (qPCR vs CFU) — no meta-analysis possible.
- Apical preparation size differed systematically between GWS (minimal) and ultrasonic (35/.04) arms, confounding the activation comparison.
- 2 of 3 studies lacked a control group and randomization.
- No clinical (in-vivo healing) outcomes — bench-only.
- In-vitro only; cannot inform whether multisonic improves real RCT outcomes.

## 6. Related Work

- Boutsioukis 2022 — status/directions on irrigants and activation methods.
- Paixão 2022, Cheung 2021 — sonic/ultrasonic activation reviews.

## 7. Glossary

- **Multisonic activation (GentleWave®/GWS)**: System combining broad-spectrum acoustic frequencies + negative apical pressure + continuous fluid delivery; generates hydrodynamic cavitation circulating irrigant through all canals simultaneously at minimal apical prep sizes.
- **PUI (passive ultrasonic irrigation)**: Acoustic streaming from an oscillating ~30 kHz file agitating irrigant in each canal individually.
- **CUI**: Continuous ultrasonic irrigation (irrigant delivered through the ultrasonic handpiece).
- **qPCR / CFU**: Quantitative PCR (bacterial DNA) and colony-forming-unit culture — the two biofilm-quantification assays used.
- **Multispecies/polymicrobial biofilm**: Mixed-flora biofilm model more representative of endodontic infection than monospecies E. faecalis.
