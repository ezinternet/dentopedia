## Why Ingested

즉시식립 임플란트에서 하중(loading) 및 골이식(grafting)이 골유착과 연조직 치유에 미치는 영향은 임상적으로 중요하나 근거가 불명확하다. 본 연구는 4-group 분할구강 동물실험으로 이 두 변수를 동시에 평가한 드문 조직계측학적 데이터를 제공한다. [[wiki/overviews/type-1a-immediate-placement-immediate-loading-decision]]의 즉시 하중 의사결정 근거를 조직학적으로 보강하며, [[wiki/immediate-implant/seyssens-2022-immediate-implant-socket-grafting-sr-ma]]의 이식재 효과 논의를 동물실험 수준에서 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

Animal study (minipig, n=56 TLX implants, 4 groups, 4 and 12 weeks) showing that neither immediate loading nor gap grafting with bovine bone mineral significantly affected BIC or peri-implant soft-tissue dimensions at immediately placed progressive-thread tissue-level implants.

## 한줄요약

미니피그 동물실험(n=56 TLX 임플란트, 4그룹, 4·12주)에서 즉시 하중 여부 및 소뼈유래 골이식재 사용 여부는 즉시식립 임플란트의 골-임플란트 접촉률(BIC) 및 연조직 치유에 유의한 영향을 미치지 않았다.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First controlled 2×2 factorial histomorphometric study evaluating both loading AND grafting simultaneously at immediately placed progressive-thread tissue-level implants (TLX, Straumann) in a minipig model.
2. BIC values were high (74.5–83.8% at 4 weeks; 75.5–79.9% at 12 weeks) and statistically indistinguishable across all four groups, supporting safety of both immediate loading and non-grafting protocols for TLX implants.
3. Grafting under unloaded conditions produced significantly lower first BIC (fBIC) values at 12 weeks vs. non-grafted unloaded sites (p=.01), interpreted as grafting shifting BIC initiation more apically — suggesting grafting may reduce risk of supracrestal rough-surface exposure rather than improving osseointegration per se.
4. Junctional epithelium (JE), connective tissue contact (CTC), and biological width (BW) did not differ significantly between any groups at either time point.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Animal model**: 14 female Ellegaard Goettingen minipigs (22–29 months, ~40 kg). Surgeries at Biomedical Department, Lund University.

**Design**: Split-mouth, 2×2 factorial:
- Group 1: Unloaded + Grafted (bovine bone mineral, Cerabone® 0.5–1.0 mm, Straumann)
- Group 2: Unloaded + Non-grafted
- Group 3: Loaded + Grafted
- Group 4: Loaded + Non-grafted

**Implants**: Straumann® TLX RT Roxolid® SLActive® (Ø 3.75 mm × 6 mm), fully tapered, progressive thread, 2.8 mm machined neck, hydrophilic SLActive® endosseous surface. Placed in mandibular P3 and P4 after atraumatic extraction of P2–P4 and M1.

**Loading definition**: Loaded = healing abutment (height 4.5 mm, RT TLX); Unloaded = closure cap (height 1.5 mm, RT TLX). Healing abutments were verified not to be in occlusal contact with opposing dentition.

**Grafting**: Cerabone® bovine bone mineral placed into circumferential peri-implant gap and adjacent extraction sockets in grafted hemimandible.

**Healing periods**: 4 weeks (n=7 animals) and 12 weeks (n=7 animals). Sacrifice by intra-cardiac pentobarbital. Non-decalcified histological sections (200 µm, bucco-lingual), paragon stained (toluidine blue + basic fuchsin).

**Outcome measures**:
- Primary: BIC (%) from smooth-rough border (RL) to onset of apical curvature (OAC)
- Secondary: fBIC (vertical distance from most coronal BIC to RL), SE, JE, CTC, BW (JE+CTC) — measured buccal and lingual separately

**Statistics**: Multivariate mixed linear regression; random effect = individual animal; fixed effects = loading, grafting, side, position; Dunnett-Hsu multiple comparison correction; Wilcoxon signed-rank test for paired comparisons.

**Power**: 0.893 (α=0.05, d=1.25 based on El Chaar et al. 2021 data).

**Exclusions**: 7 implants excluded due to loss of healing abutment during healing; 1 implant (Group 3, 4 weeks) failed histologically (fibrous encapsulation). Final analysis: n=48 implants.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Group | n (4 wk) | BIC 4 wk | BIC 12 wk |
|---|---|---|---|
| G1: Unloaded + Grafted | 6 | 83.8 ± 13.3% | 79.9 ± 8.6% |
| G2: Unloaded + Non-grafted | 7 | 74.5 ± 11.6% | 75.5 ± 7.9% |
| G3: Loaded + Grafted | 5 | 83.8 ± 13.3% | ~78% |
| G4: Loaded + Non-grafted | 6 | ~77% | ~77% |

- No statistically significant BIC differences between any groups at 4 or 12 weeks (multivariate mixed regression).
- fBIC (12 wk): Group 1 (grafted+unloaded) showed significantly lower (more apical) fBIC than Group 2 (non-grafted+unloaded), p=.01 — at buccal and lingual. Grafted+loaded (G3) also trended toward lower fBIC vs. G4 (non-grafted+loaded), not significant.
- Soft tissues: No significant differences in JE, CTC, or BW (total, buccal, or lingual) between any groups at 4 or 12 weeks.
- JE higher buccal vs. lingual; CTC higher lingual vs. buccal — consistent with anatomy.
- BW ranged 2481–4091 µm (4 wk) and 2501–3802 µm (12 wk) — within the range reported for TL implants in prior canine/minipig studies.
- BIC values comparable to or higher than previously reported delayed TLX (El Chaar 2021: 61.3%) and delayed/immediate TL implants in literature, attributed to bone-condensing progressive thread and hydrophilic SLActive® surface.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

1. **No measurement of initial circumferential defect dimensions** (size and buccal/lingual plate thickness): confounding variable not controlled, may have influenced group outcomes.
2. **Healing abutment loading** ≠ functional loading with prosthetic crown; occlusal contacts verified absent — may underestimate loading effects.
3. **Examiner masking not feasible**: macroscopic (healing cap vs. closure) and microscopic (graft particles) group identifiers were visible; potential observer bias.
4. **Focus on buccal-lingual sections only**: mesial/distal aspects (where grafting is predominant at molar sites) not assessed.
5. **Animal model**: Minipig mandibular molar region approximates clinical immediate placement at molar sites, but systemic, salivary, and oral hygiene factors differ from humans.
6. **Short-term outcomes**: 12-week histology captures early osseointegration; long-term crestal bone maintenance and soft-tissue stability unknown.
7. **Industry funding** (Straumann): TLX implant is a Straumann product; independent replication warranted.

## 6. Related Work

- El Chaar et al. 2021 (*Clin Oral Investig*): TLX vs. TL minipig comparison — provided BIC data used for power calculation.
- Blanco et al. 2010 (*J Clin Periodontol*): Comparable BIC at loaded vs. unloaded TL implants (flapless, beagle dog).
- Liñares et al. 2011: Immediate vs. delayed loading of TL implants in minipigs (lower BIC values due to different ROI definition).
- Francisco et al. 2021 (*Clin Oral Investig*): Submerged vs. transgingival healing of BLX (same progressive thread, bone-level) in minipigs — comparable BIC.
- Schwarz et al. 2007, 2014, 2015: TL delayed placement data for BIC, JE, CTC benchmarking.
- Seyssens et al. 2022 (*J Clin Periodontol*): SR+MA on socket grafting at immediate implants — provides clinical-level evidence context.

## 7. Glossary

- **TLX**: Tissue-Level Xperience — Straumann's fully tapered, progressive-thread, one-piece tissue-level implant with SLActive® hydrophilic surface
- **BIC (골-임플란트 접촉률)**: Bone-to-Implant Contact — percentage of implant surface in direct contact with bone in the region of interest
- **fBIC (최초 골-임플란트 접촉 위치)**: First BIC — vertical distance from most coronal bone-to-implant contact point to the smooth-rough border (reference line); a negative value = bone above RL; positive = bone apical to RL
- **JE (접합상피)**: Junctional Epithelium — epithelial attachment zone; measured vertically
- **CTC (결합조직 접촉)**: Connective Tissue Contact — zone of supracrestal connective tissue attachment
- **BW (생물학적 폭경)**: Biological Width = JE + CTC
- **SE (열구상피)**: Sulcular Epithelium — distance from mucosal margin to most coronal extent of JE
- **RL (기준선)**: Reference Line — smooth-to-rough transition at TLX implant neck (= clinical landmark for crestal bone position target)
- **OAC**: Onset of Apical Curvature — apical boundary of the endosseous BIC measurement region
- **Cerabone®**: Bovine bone mineral xenograft (0.5–1.0 mm; Straumann) used for gap grafting
- **SLActive®**: Hydrophilic sand-blasted, large-grit, acid-etched surface (Straumann); chemically modified to accelerate osseointegration
- **Split-mouth design (분할구강 설계)**: Each animal receives multiple treatments (one per quadrant/hemimandible), controlling for inter-individual variability
