## 2. Key Contributions
To evaluate prevalence and incidence of soft tissue dehiscences at implant sites in absence of disease, with risk/protective factors. Cross-sectional and prospective studies. Mixed-effects uni-/multi-level regression to identify predictive factors.

## 3. Methodology
Systematic search per AO/AAP standards. 221 eligible studies included. Mixed-effects meta-regression. Subtypes defined: PSTD (peri-implant soft tissue dehiscence), ML apical shift, MREC.

## 4. Key Results
- 221 studies included.
- Mean PSTD prevalence 46.2%; MREC 23.1%.
- Incidence of PSTD, MREC, ML shift quantified.
- Risk factors: thin biotype, lack of keratinized mucosa, buccal implant position, immediate placement, smoking, lack of CTG.
- Protective factors: ≥2 mm KT, CTG, adequate emergence profile, soft tissue volume augmentation.

## 5. Clinical Relevance
임플란트 주위 연조직 dehiscence는 'disease 없음'에도 매우 흔함 (PSTD ~46%). 식립 전 risk assessment + 필요 시 CTG·SCTG·KT 증대 술식 적용으로 예방. AO/AAP consensus급 reference.
