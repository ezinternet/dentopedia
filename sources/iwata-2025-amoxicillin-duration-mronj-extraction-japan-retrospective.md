## One-line Summary
일본 4 기관 후향 연구 (J Bone Miner Metab 2025, n=160 저용량 BP 환자): 발치 시 MRONJ 예방을 위한 amoxicillin (AMPC) 투여 — 단일 술전 500mg vs 3일 코스 비교; MRONJ 발생률 동등 → 단일 술전 dose로 충분, AMR 회피 가능.

## 1. Document Information
- Authors: Iwata E et al., Kobe + multi-center Japan
- Type: Retrospective multi-center comparative study
- Journal: J Bone Miner Metab 2025;43:525-534
- n=160 low-dose bisphosphonate patients

## 2. Key Contributions
1. 첫 multi-center Japanese 비교 study on AMPC duration for MRONJ prevention.
2. Single preoperative 500mg = 3-day course in efficacy.
3. Supports shorter prophylaxis duration in BP patients.

## 3. Methodology
- Retrospective 4-center Japanese study, 2019-2024
- n=160 patients on low-dose oral bisphosphonates undergoing extraction
- 2019-2021 cohort: AMPC 500mg pre-op + 750mg/day for 2 days post-op
- 2022-2024 cohort: AMPC 500mg single preop dose only
- All patients: tension-free wound closure + regular follow-up
- Outcome: MRONJ development rate

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] MRONJ rate equivalent between groups → single preop 500mg AMPC sufficient. 추가 detail in paper.

## 5. Limitations
- Retrospective; not randomized
- Low-dose BP only (no high-dose IV antiresorptives, no denosumab)
- 4 Japanese centers; generalization to other ethnicities/protocols cautious
