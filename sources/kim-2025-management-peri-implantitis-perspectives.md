## One-line Summary
Narrative review by Korean periodontist summarizing peri-implantitis definition (2017 World Workshop), 2024 Korean Academy of Periodontology consensus classification, and management options in the Korean clinical landscape.

## 2. Key Contributions
Bridges the international consensus (2017 World Workshop, recent reviews) with Korean Academy of Periodontology 2024 consensus classification — useful as the local-language summary for Korean clinicians.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Narrative review. International + Korean guideline synthesis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Reviews classification criteria, non-surgical and surgical options, decontamination strategies, and reinforces SPT importance. Concludes with practical perspectives.
