## One-line Summary
Yonsei University pilot RCT (n=21, BoNT/A vs. saline, 12 weeks) — significant within-group decreases in orofacial VAS, tender points, headache VAS and frequency in BoNT/A arm but not control; tender-point count differed between groups.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Toxins 2023;15(10):597
- **DOI**: 10.3390/toxins15100597
- **Published**: 4 October 2023
- **Lead institution**: Yonsei University College of Dentistry — Dept of Orofacial Pain and Oral Medicine (Seoul)
- **Design**: Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled pilot study
- **Population**: Adults with myogenous TMD and masticatory muscle pain + concurrent headache

## 2. Key Contributions
- First RCT to simultaneously evaluate both masticatory muscle pain (MMP) and headache outcomes under BoNT/A treatment.
- Injects up to 16 tender muscle areas including masseter, temporalis, splenius capitis, sternocleidomastoid, and trapezius — broader than masseter-only studies.
- Quantifies tender point (TP) count change over 12 weeks — useful objective surrogate.
- Establishes feasibility for a larger RCT in MMP + headache populations.
- Reports no AEs over 12 weeks.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 21 patients (19F/2M, age 21-53 y) with clinically diagnosed MMP; randomized to BoNT/A (n=14) or saline (n=7).
- Injections at tender sites after palpation across 16 muscle areas (each masseter, each temporalis, splenius capitis, SCM, trapezius bilaterally).
- Outcomes: OVAS (orofacial VAS), HVAS (headache VAS), TPs (number of tender points), MMO, HF (headache frequency).
- Timepoints: pre-injection, 4 wk, 8 wk, 12 wk.
- Analysis: Friedman (within-group) and Mann-Whitney (between-group, including baseline homogeneity); Wilcoxon signed-rank post-hoc.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
**Experimental group (BoNT/A, n=14) — Friedman across time:**
- OVAS: pre 5.00 → 4 wk 3.00 → 8 wk 2.50 → 12 wk 2.50 (medians); p = 0.003 *
- TPs: pre 9.50 → 4 wk 7.00 → 8 wk 6.00 → 12 wk 4.50; p < 0.001 *
- HVAS: pre 4.50 → 4 wk 1.50 → 8 wk 0.00 → 12 wk 0.00; p = 0.005 *
- HF: pre 2.00 → 8 wk 0.00 → 12 wk 0.00; p = 0.002 *
- MMO: no significant change.

**Control group (saline, n=7) — Friedman across time:**
- No significant change in OVAS, TPs, HVAS, HF, or MMO.

**Between-group:**
- Decline in TPs significantly differed between groups at all timepoints, especially after 4 and 12 weeks vs. pre-injection.
- Authors conclude BoNT/A is "relatively effective" for MMP and headache in TMD patients vs. saline.

**Safety:**
- No reports of side effects during 12-week study period.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small sample (n=21), unequal allocation (2:1).
- Pilot design; not powered for primary efficacy claim.
- Short follow-up (12 weeks).
- Mostly female cohort (19/21).
- Authors call for larger RCTs evaluating MMP and headache together.

## 6. Related Work
- Minston 2025 — independent pilot RCT in DC/TMD myalgia with null primary outcome.
- Sahin 2024 — head-to-head 4-arm RCT showing BoNT-A non-superior to DN/PT/MT.
- AAN Level B evidence statement for BoNT/A in TMD myogenous pain.
- AAN Level A evidence for chronic migraine.

## 7. Glossary
- **BoNT/A** (보툴리눔독소 A형, Botulinum Toxin Type A).
- **MMP** (저작근 통증, Masticatory Muscle Pain): Persistent, dull pain or fatigue localized to jaw and TMJ-area muscles.
- **OVAS** (구강안면 VAS, Orofacial Visual Analog Scale): 0-10 pain intensity rating for orofacial region.
- **HVAS** (두통 VAS, Headache VAS): 0-10 pain intensity for headache.
- **TPs** (압통점 개수, Tender Points): Count of muscle palpation tender points.
- **HF** (두통 빈도, Headache Frequency): Number of headache episodes per unit time.
- **MMO** (최대개구량, Maximum Mouth Opening).
- **AAN** (미국신경학회, American Academy of Neurology): Provides evidence levels (A/B/U) for clinical interventions.
