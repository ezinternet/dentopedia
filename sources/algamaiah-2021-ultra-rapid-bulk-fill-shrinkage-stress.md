## Why Ingested

[[resin/parra-gatica-2023-flowable-bulk-fill-composites-sr]]와 [[resin/boaro-2019-bulk-fill-composites-clinical-properties-sr-ma]]는 표준 광중합 벌크필을 다루지만, 임상에서 확산 중인 3초 초고조사(>3000 mW/cm²) URPBF 소재의 수축 strain/stress 거동, 그리고 AFCT(addition-fragmentation chain transfer) 첨가제가 stress를 낮추는 기전은 위키에 없었다. Watts 그룹의 본 연구는 "초고조사 = strain 약간↓이지만 stress 약간↑(network mobility 감소)" 트레이드오프와 AFCT의 stress-relaxation 효과를 보강한다. abstract-only.

## One-line Summary
In-vitro bench study of ultra-rapid (3 s, ~3000-3200 mW/cm²) bulk-fill composites (PFill with an AFCT agent, PFlow) vs comparators, finding ultra-high irradiance over ultra-short periods slightly lowers shrinkage strain but slightly raises stress (reduced network mobility), while the AFCT agent in PFill measurably lowers shrinkage stress even under high-irradiance curing.

## 한줄요약
In-vitro (초고속 3초·~3000 mW/cm² 벌크필 PFill/PFlow vs 비교군): 초단시간 고조사는 수축 strain은 약간 낮추나 stress는 약간 높임(network mobility 감소); PFill의 AFCT 첨가제는 고조사에서도 수축응력을 유의하게 낮춤.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Dental Materials, 2021;37(4):559-567
- e-pub 2021-03-06
- Materials: PFill (AFCT-containing), PFlow, comparators ECeram, EFlow
- LCUs: Bluephase PowerCure, VALO

## 2. Key Contributions
- Characterizes shrinkage strain (%) and stress (MPa) of ultra-rapid photo-polymerized bulk-fill (URPBF) under 3 s high-irradiance protocols (3000/3200 mW/cm²) vs 10 s standard (1200 mW/cm²)
- Shows the strain↓/stress↑ trade-off of ultra-short ultra-high irradiance — attributed to reduced network mobility limiting flow-based stress relaxation
- Documents AFCT chemistry as a stress-reducing strategy effective even with high-irradiance curing (PFill consistently lower stress than ECeram)
- Flowable PFlow only showed significantly higher stress under the 3 s VALO protocol

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Polymerization shrinkage % by bonded disk; shrinkage stress MPa by Bioman II; 60 min at 23±1 °C (n=5)
- Maximum shrinkage-rate and maximum shrinkage stress-rate over 15 s by numerical differentiation
- Three protocols: 3 s @3000 (PowerCure), 3 s @3200 (VALO), 10 s @1200 (PowerCure)
- Multiple one-way ANOVA + Tukey (α=0.05)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- PFill: significantly lower PS than ECeram regardless of protocol (p<0.05); lower stress than ECeram (p<0.05), comparable across both high-irradiance protocols
- PFlow: PS comparable to EFlow/ECeram; significantly higher stress only with 3 s VALO (p<0.05)
- Max shrinkage strain-rate lower for PFill-10s and PFill-3s (PowerCure) vs ECeram
- Max shrinkage stress-rate comparable across protocols and vs ECeram

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Bench-only (bonded-disk/Bioman), n=5, no cavity/C-factor or tooth model
- Abstract-only ingest; full per-group numbers not extracted
- Long-term conversion/mechanical consequences of 3 s curing not assessed here

## 6. Related Work
- Mechanistically complements Meereis 2018 (matrix modification reduces stress) — AFCT is a matrix/network strategy
- Curing-protocol angle relevant to Yang 2022 (kinetics) and Magne 2023 (dark-cure stress development)

## 7. Glossary
- **URPBF**: ultra-rapid photo-polymerized bulk fill. **AFCT**: addition-fragmentation chain transfer agent — enables network rearrangement to relax stress. **Bioman II**: contraction-force shrinkage-stress instrument. **Bonded disk**: shrinkage-strain measurement technique.
