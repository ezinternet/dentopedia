## Why Ingested

Gluma(글루타르알데히드)계 탈감작제의 치수 안전성 우려를 기초세포 수준에서 정량. [[dentin-hypersensitivity/jiang-2022-desensitizing-agents-permeability-cytotoxicity]]의 GLUMA 세포독성 소견을 기전적으로 뒷받침하고, [[overviews/dentin-hypersensitivity-overview]]의 안전성 단락 근거.

## One-line Summary
In vitro transdentinal model (MDPC-23 odontoblast-like cells under dentin discs) quantifying dose-dependent cytotoxicity of glutaraldehyde-containing desensitizing solutions/materials.

## 한줄요약
In vitro 경상아질 모델(상아질 디스크 하 MDPC-23 오도노블라스트 유사세포): 글루타르알데히드 함유 탈감작제의 농도 의존적 세포독성을 정량.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dentistry, 2015. Authors incl. Pashley & Hebling (Araraquara/UNESP, Georgia Regents).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Establishes that glutaraldehyde diffuses transdentinally and harms pulp-side odontoblast-like cells.
- Dose/concentration-dependent viability reduction relevant to Gluma-type agents.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Artificial pulp chambers with dentin discs; MDPC-23 cells seeded pulpal side; occlusal surface treated with glutaraldehyde-containing solutions/materials; viability/metabolism assays.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Glutaraldehyde-containing treatments reduced odontoblast-like cell viability in a concentration-dependent manner across the dentin barrier.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; dentin-disc thickness governs diffusion; clinical pulpal response may differ.

## 6. Related Work
- Mechanistic safety basis for Gluma clinical use and the permeability/cytotoxicity comparison work.

## 7. Glossary
- Transdentinal cytotoxicity: cell damage from agents diffusing across a dentin barrier to the pulp side.
- MDPC-23: immortalized odontoblast-like cell line.
