## Why Ingested

ENT-dental 협진 프로토콜에서 술전 상악동점막두께 (MSMT)가 임플란트 시술 후 악화되는지를 종단적으로 확인하기 위해 인제스트. 기존 [[sinus-lift/lateral/lee-2020-msmt-mod-ratio-sinus-augmentation-risk]]의 MSMT/MOD 비율 위험지표 제안을 보강하며, 술후 MSMT가 유의하게 변하지 않음을 실증한 후속 데이터를 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective study (n=9) of ENT-screened patients with preoperative maxillary sinus mucosal thickening found no significant MSMT change post-implantation across sinus augmentation, bone grafting, and no-grafting groups (p > 0.05).

## 한줄요약

이비인후과 의뢰 후 술전 상악동점막비후 환자 9명을 후향 분석한 결과, 상악동 거상·골이식·무이식 세 군 모두에서 술후 MSMT가 유의하게 변하지 않았다 (p > 0.05).

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Medicina (Kaunas)
- **Institution**: The Catholic University of Korea, Seoul St. Mary's Hospital
- **Study design**: Retrospective, single-center
- **n**: 9 patients (December 2019 – August 2022)
- **Ethics**: IRB KC23RISI0252, approved April 2023

## 2. Key Contributions

- Provides longitudinal MSMT data (pre- and post-treatment CBCT at mean 7.0 ± 1.8 months) for ENT-assessed patients with preoperative mucosal thickening
- Confirms MSMT/MOD ratio as a useful monitoring metric without demonstrating significant group differences
- Demonstrates safety of proceeding with implant-related surgery (including lateral window sinus augmentation) in ENT-cleared patients with anatomical variations (Haller cells, concha bullosa, paradoxical middle turbinate)
- Zero incidence of postoperative sinusitis despite anatomical variations

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Patients referred from Periodontology to Otolaryngology preoperatively, underwent nasal endoscopy and CBCT evaluation
- CBCT analysis using InVivoDental v6.0.5 (Anatomage): measured MSMT, MOD (ostium-to-floor distance), MSMT/MOD ratio
- Three groups: sinus augmentation (lateral window, n=3), bone grafting (GBR, n=3), no grafting (implant only, n=3)
- Statistics: Kruskal–Wallis test (between groups), Wilcoxon signed-rank test (pre vs. post); significance α = 0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Parameter | Preoperative | Postoperative | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| MSMT (overall) | 4.3 ± 2.0 mm | 4.5 ± 2.3 mm | > 0.05 (NS) |
| MSMT/MOD ratio | 0.13 ± 0.05 | 0.15 ± 0.06 | > 0.05 (NS) |
| MOD | 31.4 ± 4.6 mm | — | — |

- Sinus augmentation group preop MSMT: 5.1 ± 0.6 mm; bone grafting: 4.9 ± 3.3 mm; no grafting: 3.0 ± 0.8 mm (NS between groups)
- Mean alveolar bone height gain in sinus augmentation group: 6.0 ± 6.0 mm
- No postoperative sinusitis in any patient
- Anatomical variations present: concha bullosa (2), Haller cells (4), paradoxical curvature of middle concha (4)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small sample (n=9); limits generalizability
- Short-term follow-up only (~7 months postoperative)
- Retrospective design; no control group without preoperative ENT evaluation
- Cannot distinguish between approach (crestal vs. lateral) effects on MSMT change
- Prospective studies with larger samples and longer follow-up recommended

## 6. Related Work

- Lee et al. proposed the MSMT/MOD ratio as a risk indicator for sinusitis post-sinus augmentation
- Prior studies: chronic sinusitis with mucosal thickening does not significantly affect postoperative bone height or healing
- Odontogenic causes of MSMT: periodontitis (47.1%), periapical pathology (23.5%), root canal treatment (23.1%)
- Previous literature: asymptomatic sinus findings (mucosal edema, cysts, polyps) may not need resolution before sinus augmentation in asymptomatic patients; complete opacification warrants ENT referral

## 7. Glossary

- **MSMT**: Maxillary sinus mucosal thickness (상악동점막두께)
- **MOD**: Distance between maxillary sinus ostium and sinus floor (골하구-동저 거리)
- **MSMT/MOD ratio**: Ratio used as a preoperative risk indicator for sinusitis
- **ENT-dental collaboration**: 이비인후과-치과 협진
- **Haller cells**: Air cells within the floor of the orbit near the ethmoid sinus; anatomical variant
- **Concha bullosa**: Pneumatization of the middle turbinate; anatomical variant
