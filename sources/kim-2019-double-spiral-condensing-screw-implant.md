대화에서 다룬 해냄버(HaeNaem bur)의 회전방향-압축 메커니즘의 **공학적 1차 근거 문서**다. 임상 데이터 [[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/changrani-2024-haenaem-zero-bone-loss-indirect-sinus-lift]] 는 "HaeNaem이 시계방향(CW)으로 골치밀화한다"는 결과만 보여줄 뿐 *왜·어떻게*는 설명하지 못한다. 이 (주)해냄 특허는 그 스크류 기하(이중 스파이럴: 압착나사산부 + 본파우더안내홈 + 하부압착돔)와 "회전방향과 반대방향 나사산" 원리를 직접 기술해, 골밀도화(Osseodensification, OD) 발명 원리를 정리한 [[wiki/implants/huwais-2017-novel-osseous-densification-osteotomy-primary-stability]] 와 대비되는 대안 엔지니어링을 문서화한다. 사용자 요청으로 인제스트.

## One-line Summary

Korean patent application (KR 10-2021-0014513 A; applicant HaeNaem Co.) for a dental "condensing screw" with a double-spiral geometry that simultaneously expands the osteotomy and densifies bone both laterally AND apically (via a lower compression dome + bone-powder guide grooves), enabling transcrestal sinus elevation in a single instrument pass.

## 한줄요약

(주)해냄 특허출원(공개 10-2021-0014513): 이중 스파이럴 구조 콘덴싱 스크류로, 압착나사산부가 골삭제·측방 골치밀화를 동시에 수행하고 본파우더안내홈이 분쇄골을 하부압착돔으로 보내 내측(근단)까지 골치밀화 — 측방만 압축하던 종래 버의 한계를 보완해 단일 기구로 상악동 거상까지 수행.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Double-spiral geometry**: a compression thread (압착나사산부, 111) interleaved with bone-powder guide grooves (본파우더안내홈, 115), both formed in the direction *opposite* to the screw's rotation direction.
2. **Apical (inner) densification, not just lateral**: a convex lower compression dome (하부압착돔, 117) at the screw tip, fed by the guide grooves, forms a densified zone toward the osteotomy floor — solving the prior-art weakness where conventional OD burs densify the side wall well but the apical region poorly.
3. **Single-pass workflow**: combines osteotomy expansion + densification (+ sinus-floor elevation) in one instrument, shortening the conventional "pilot → sequential drills → separate densification → final drill → implant" sequence.
4. **Membrane-sparing sinus lift**: by densifying bone under the Schneiderian membrane and elevating it hydraulically with the bone column, it aims to lift the membrane without tearing it.
5. **Patient-comfort claim**: the powder-channeling design disperses continuous lateral pressure, reducing the pain caused by purely lateral compaction in conventional burs.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Device structure:
- **Screw body (스크류본체, 110)**: inserted into the pilot-drilled osteotomy; expands it to fixture diameter while forming a densification zone on the side wall and inner (apical) region.
- **Shank (샹크, 120)**: couples to the handpiece and transmits rotation.
- **Compression thread (압착나사산부, 111)**: helical, opposite to rotation direction; on rotation provides cutting force toward the insertion (apical) direction AND presses the released bone powder laterally against the osteotomy wall (densification). Peaks (산, 112) carry a densification face (치밀화면, 112b, surface contact) and a cutting edge (절삭날, 112a, line contact).
- **Bone-powder guide grooves (본파우더안내홈, 115)**: helical recesses, opposite to rotation, channel detached bone powder + irrigation water down to the screw's lower region.
- **Lower compression dome (하부압착돔, 117)**: convex tip with radial connecting grooves (안내홈연결홈, 117a); forward-rotation side has an inclined guide surface (안내곡면, 117b), reverse-rotation side a vertical surface (수직면, 117c).

Mechanism principle (claim language): because the thread is formed opposite to the rotation direction, the bone powder cannot escape outward and is instead compacted against the wall — i.e. the osteodensification (no-bone-removal compaction) principle. The novelty over prior art is routing that powder apically to the dome so the floor/inner region is also densified.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

No clinical or bench measurements (patent document). Asserted technical effects only:
- Lateral densification zone thickness (d1) vs inner densification thickness (d2): prior-art burs give d2 ≪ d1 (insufficient for sinus lift); the invention raises apical/inner densification.
- Workflow shortened to: initial drill → condensing screw (expansion + densification simultaneously) → implant placement.
- Claimed reduction of patient pain via pressure dispersion. None of these are quantified or independently validated.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **No evidence weight**: a patent demonstrates a design claim, not clinical efficacy or safety. All "effects" are inventor assertions.
- Closest clinical data point is the single-arm n=12 study [[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/changrani-2024-haenaem-zero-bone-loss-indirect-sinus-lift]] (HaeNaem Zero Bone Loss kit), which itself lacks a control group and ISQ values.
- Direction convention: the patent describes thread geometry tuned to a single working rotation direction; it does NOT describe a reversible cut/densify dual-direction tool the way Densah (Huwais) does (CW=extraction / CCW=densify). This is the key contrast with the Versah/Densah system.

## 7. Glossary

- **콘덴싱 스크류 (condensing screw)**: a bur-like screw that compacts (condenses) rather than cuts away bone.
- **골치밀화 (Osseodensification, OD)**: compaction of bone against osteotomy walls to raise density and primary stability without removing bone.
- **본파우더 (bone powder)**: bone particles released at the wall, re-pressed/channeled rather than evacuated.
- **하부압착돔 (lower compression dome)**: convex tip feature that densifies the osteotomy floor/apical region.
- **슈나이더막 (Schneiderian membrane)**: the sinus lining elevated during transcrestal sinus lift.
