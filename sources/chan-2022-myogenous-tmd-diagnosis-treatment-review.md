## One-line Summary
Clinical update review (HK Univ, Diagnostics 2022): myogenous TMD — DC/TMD classification (myalgia/tendonitis/myositis/spasm), trigger-point diagnosis, multidisciplinary treatment from conservative to minimally invasive.

## 한줄요약
임상 업데이트 (2022): 근육형 TMD — DC/TMD 분류(근통·건염·근염·경련), 트리거포인트 감별, 보존적~최소침습 다학제 치료.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Diagnostics 12(12):2914 (2022)
- **Study type**: Narrative review (clinical update)
- **Focus**: Myogenous TMD (M-TMD) — most common TMD subtype (45.3% of all TMDs)

## 2. DC/TMD Classification of M-TMD
### 4 major muscle pain classes:
1. **Myalgia** (근통)
   - Local myalgia: pain localized to palpation site
   - Myofascial pain (근막통): spreads within muscle boundary
   - Myofascial pain with referral: spreads beyond muscle boundary
2. **Tendonitis** (건염)
3. **Myositis** (근염)
4. **Spasm** (경련)

Additional differentials: contracture, hypertrophy, neoplasm, movement disorders (dystonia), fibromyalgia overlap.

## 3. Key Risk Factors
- Psychosocial: stress, anxiety, depression, sleep disturbances
- Bruxism: positive correlation with myofascial pain and masseter/temporalis EMG
- COVID-19 lockdown: increased TMD symptoms via stress/clenching
- Central sensitization: possible mechanism for chronic M-TMD

## 4. Diagnosis
- History: dull aching pain, localized muscle stiffness/tenderness, pain on jaw opening/chewing
- Clinical exam: palpation (masseter, temporalis; 1 kg × 5 sec) + jaw movement provocation
- Imaging: CBCT, MRI (mainly to exclude articular causes)
- Adjunct: surface EMG, algometry (pressure pain threshold)
- Key: pain replication on palpation = diagnostic criterion for myalgia

## 5. Treatment
### Conservative:
- Patient education, jaw rest, soft diet
- Heat/cold therapy, oral exercises
- Physical therapy, manual therapy (as per González-Sánchez 2023)
- CBT/psychological support
- Occlusal splint (stabilization appliance)
- Pharmacotherapy: NSAIDs, muscle relaxants, TCA, benzodiazepine (short-term)

### Minimally invasive:
- Trigger point injection (local anesthetic ± corticosteroid)
- Dry needling
- BTX injection (masseter, temporalis)
- Low-level laser, ultrasound therapy

## 6. Glossary
- **M-TMD**: Myogenous TMD — muscle-origin TMD
- **MTrP**: Myofascial trigger point (근막 트리거포인트)
- **Myofascial pain with referral**: 근막통 — 근경계 넘어 방사통
- **TCA**: Tricyclic antidepressants (삼환계 항우울제) — for chronic pain
