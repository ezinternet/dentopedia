## Why Ingested

PSQI(피츠버그 수면 질 지수)를 1차 결과 지표로 사용한 드문 브럭시즘 BoNT-A 연구. 기존 보유 논문들이 주로 통증(VAS)·기능을 평가하는 반면, 본 연구는 수면 질 개선이 통증 개선보다 더 지속적임을 6개월 종단 데이터로 보임. [[botulinum-toxin/ergezen-2025-botulinum-toxin-sleep-quality-bruxism]]과 함께 "BoNT-A → 수면 질 개선" 축 구성.

## One-line Summary

Prospective study (n=80, 6-month follow-up) of masseter BoNT-A injection for bruxism showed rapid BDI reduction (20.8→4.3 at 1mo), sustained PSQI improvement (10.0→2.8 at 6mo), and significant VAS pain reduction; sleep quality improvement was the most durable outcome. Abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

교근 BoNT-A 주사 브럭시즘 전향 연구(n=80, 6개월)에서 BDI가 조기에 크게 감소(20.8→4.3, 1개월)하고 PSQI는 6개월까지 지속 개선(10.0→2.8)되어, 수면 질 개선이 가장 오래 지속되는 치료 효과임을 확인. 초록만 수집.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Uses PSQI as a systematic sleep quality outcome measure alongside BDI and VAS — rare combination in bruxism BoNT-A literature
- Demonstrates **differential durability**: sleep quality improvement (PSQI) more sustained at 6 months than bruxism severity (BDI, partial rebound)
- Provides temporal profile: BDI peaks at 1 month → partial rebound by 6 months; PSQI improvement sustained throughout
- Direct clinical implication: BoNT-A may be particularly valuable for sleep-bruxism patients with sleep quality complaints

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Prospective clinical study
- **Sample**: n=80 patients diagnosed with bruxism
- **Intervention**: Botulinum toxin injection into masseter muscle
- **Follow-up**: Baseline, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months
- **Outcome measures**:
  - **BDI** (Beck Depression Inventory): bruxism severity / depressive symptom proxy
  - **PSQI** (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index): global sleep quality (≤5 = good sleep)
  - **VAS**: pain intensity
- **Statistics**: Repeated measures ANOVA

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Baseline | 1 month | 6 months | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| BDI | 20.8 ± 4.3 | 4.3 ± 1.5 | (partial rebound) | <0.001 |
| PSQI | 10.0 ± 2.3 | — | 2.8 ± 1.0 | <0.001 |
| VAS pain | (baseline) | (significant reduction) | — | 0.004 |

- BDI: rapid large reduction at 1 month → **partial rebound by 6 months**
- PSQI: **sustained improvement to 6-month endpoint** (most durable outcome)
- VAS: significant reduction (p=0.004); temporal pattern not specified in abstract

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract only — injection dose, injection sites not specified
- No control group; no sham injection arm
- BDI is a depression screening tool; using it as a bruxism severity proxy requires validation
- Small sample (n=80); single center
- Future: PSQI as primary outcome in RCT; standardized polysomnography confirmation of bruxism diagnosis

## 7. Glossary

- **BDI** (Beck Depression Inventory, 벡우울척도): depression severity questionnaire; used here as proxy for bruxism-related psychological burden
- **PSQI** (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index, 피츠버그수면질지수): validated 19-item questionnaire; global score ≤5 = good sleep quality
- **VAS** (Visual Analogue Scale, 시각아날로그척도): 0–10 pain intensity scale
- **Bruxism severity** (브럭시즘 중증도): clinical composite including muscle pain, jaw mobility, and tooth wear
