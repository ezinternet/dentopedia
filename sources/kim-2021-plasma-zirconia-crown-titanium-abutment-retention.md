## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치(2026-05-31)에서 골유착·항균이 아닌 **보철 시멘트 유지력** 축. 비열 플라즈마가 지르코니아 크라운–티타늄 지대주 유지력을 높이는지, 그리고 그 효과가 열순환(aging)·프라이머 병용에서 어떻게 달라지는지 검증. [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/wagner-2022-cold-atmospheric-plasma-implant-materials]]의 지르코니아 젖음성 결과와 연결.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (24 zirconia blocks for surface free energy; 120 zirconia-crown/Ti-abutment assemblies for retentive strength, self-adhesive resin cement): non-thermal atmospheric plasma (NTP) raised zirconia surface free energy and, without thermocycling, increased retentive strength vs control — but after 5000-cycle thermocycling the NTP-vs-control difference disappeared, and combining NTP with silane/primer gave significantly lower retention than silane/primer alone.

## 한줄요약

in vitro(표면자유에너지용 지르코니아 24블록; 유지력용 지르코니아크라운-Ti지대주 120쌍, 자가접착 레진시멘트): 비열 대기압 플라즈마(NTP)는 지르코니아 표면자유에너지를 높이고 비열순환 조건에서 유지력을 대조군보다 증가 — 그러나 5000회 열순환 후에는 NTP-대조 차이가 사라졌고, NTP+실란/프라이머 병용은 실란/프라이머 단독보다 유지력이 유의하게 낮았다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Materials 2021, 14(9):2352
- DOI: 10.3390/ma14092352
- Study type: in vitro (prosthetic bonding/retention), with thermocycling aging

## 2. Key Contributions

- Extends plasma application to prosthetic cement retention on zirconia.
- Critically tests aging (thermocycling) and primer/silane interaction — revealing conditional/negative effects.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Surface free energy (SFE) by Owens–Wendt on 24 zirconia blocks.
- Retentive strength (RS) on 120 zirconia-crown/Ti-abutment assemblies (G-CEM LinkAce self-adhesive cement).
- Groups: Control, NTP, Si (silane), NTP+Si, Pr (Z-Prime Plus), NTP+Pr. Half thermocycled 5000× at 5–55 °C.
- ANOVA + Tukey HSD; independent t-test for thermocycling (p<0.05).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- SFE: NTP group significantly higher than Control (p<0.05).
- RS, non-thermocycling: NTP significantly higher than Control (p<0.05).
- RS, thermocycling: no significant difference between Control and NTP (p>0.05) — benefit lost after aging.
- Non-thermocycling: NTP+Si and NTP+Pr not different from Si or Pr alone.
- Thermocycling: NTP+Si and NTP+Pr significantly LOWER than Si and Pr alone (p<0.05).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro; benefit of NTP alone does not survive thermocycling.
- NTP combined with primer/silane appears detrimental after aging — clinical caution.
