## Why Ingested

사용자 요청 — 임플란트 유지관리(supportive peri-implant therapy, SPiT) 프로토콜 논문 감시·인제스트. 기존 위키는 주위염 *치료*(decontamination, GBR, PDT, minocycline) 페이지는 풍부하나 유지관리 *프로토콜*(recall 주기·자가관리) 자체를 다룬 1차 근거가 부족했음. 본 SR(25편, RCT 9편)은 SPiT 효과의 최신 종합으로, [[implants/peri-implantitis/jepsen-2015-primary-prevention-periimplantitis-managing-mucositis]]의 1차 예방 원칙을 정량적 근거로 보강하고 [[implants/peri-implantitis/mauriello-2026-peri-implant-mucositis-adjunctive-narrative-review]]의 mucositis 보조요법 논의를 SPiT recall 맥락으로 확장.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (25 studies: 9 RCTs, 13 cohorts, 3 case-control; narrative synthesis) finding that individualized, risk-based supportive peri-implant therapy (SPiT) recall intervals consistently improve probing depth and bleeding on probing versus fixed-interval or no maintenance — though high heterogeneity precluded meta-analysis.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰(25편: RCT 9·코호트 13·환자대조 3, 정성 종합) — 환자별 위험도 기반(risk-based) 개별화 SPiT recall 주기가 고정 주기나 무관리 대비 탐침깊이·탐침시출혈을 일관되게 개선했으나, 높은 이질성으로 메타분석은 불가.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Synthesizes the effectiveness of *professionally delivered* SPiT across the full disease spectrum (peri-implant mucositis + peri-implantitis), focused on radiographic bone loss, clinical parameters (PD, BoP), and patient-reported outcomes.
- Core finding: **individualized risk-based recall** > standard fixed-interval maintenance > no maintenance, for clinical outcomes and implant longevity.
- Identifies the field's main weakness: high clinical/methodological heterogeneity, inconsistent documentation of systemic comorbidities, variable adjunctive therapies — limiting definitive conclusions.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Search**: MEDLINE, Embase, Cochrane Library, Web of Science through 2025-04-30.
- **Eligibility**: RCTs, cohort, and case-control studies of professionally delivered SPiT.
- **Risk of bias**: Cochrane ROB 2.0 (RCTs) + Newcastle-Ottawa Scale (observational).
- **Synthesis**: narrative (no meta-analysis) due to considerable heterogeneity.
- **Included**: 25 studies — 9 RCTs, 13 cohort, 3 case-control.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- SPiT with individualized, risk-based recall intervals **consistently improved PD and BoP** vs standard or no maintenance.
- Substantial variability in adjunctive therapies, patient compliance, and reporting of systemic comorbidities.
- Authors' bottom line: risk-based SPiT should be integrated into routine implant maintenance; tailor recall + adjuncts to patient-specific risk (history of periodontitis, smoking, systemic health).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract reports no pooled effect estimates (narrative synthesis only).
- High heterogeneity, inconsistent systemic-condition reporting, variable adjuncts limit generalizability.
- Calls for standardized reporting, better patient-risk accounting, and clarification of long-term adjunctive-therapy effectiveness.

## 7. Glossary

- **SPiT** — Supportive Peri-implant Therapy: structured professional maintenance after implant placement/active therapy.
- **Risk-based recall** — recall interval set by patient risk profile rather than a fixed calendar.
- **BoP / PD** — Bleeding on Probing / Probing Depth.
