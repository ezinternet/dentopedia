## Why Ingested

즉시부하(Immediate Loading) 프로토콜 하에서의 ISQ 변화 궤적을 초기 데이터셋으로 확립하기 위해 인제스트. 기존 [[implants/isq/andersson-2019-rfa-factors-5year-neoss-survival]] 페이지는 5년 생존·ISQ 임계값에 집중하나, 부하 후 0→30→60→90일간의 ISQ 안정성 dip 패턴을 시계열로 기록한 초기 임상 근거가 없었음. Balshi 2005는 Brånemark 시스템에서 즉시부하 후 1개월 ISQ 저하→2·3개월 회복의 "안정성 골재형성 궤적"을 처음 기술한 기초 참고문헌.

## One-line Summary

Prospective study (n=51 patients, 344 implants, Brånemark/Teeth-in-a-Day protocol) showing ISQ dips from 70.35 at placement to 66.38 at 30 days then recovers to 68.82 by 90 days, with lower stabilities in softer bone, posterior regions, and females; 98.5% survival.

## 한줄요약

전향적 연구(51명, 344개 임플란트, Brånemark Teeth-in-a-Day 즉시부하): 식립 시 ISQ 70.35 → 30일 66.38(최저)→ 90일 68.82 회복; 연조직·구치부·여성에서 낮은 안정성; 임플란트 생존율 98.5%.

- **Follow-up**: 90 days post-surgery (4 timepoints: placement, 30d, 60d, 90d)

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **ISQ stability dip trajectory**: Established the now-classic pattern — immediate loading causes an ISQ decrease in the first month (placement 70.35 → 30d 66.38), followed by recovery (60d 68.01 → 90d 68.82). This "osseointegration dip" was attributed to adaptive bone remodeling.
2. **Predictor stability levels**: Defined clinically relevant "predictor" ISQ values for different bone conditions in immediately loaded splinted implants — soft bone, reduced bone, and long-span lever arms identified as highest risk.
3. **Bone type / jaw position / sex effects**: Lower ISQ in softer bone types (Lekholm-Zarb classification), posterior vs anterior jaw, and female vs male patients — all consistent with reduced bone density.
4. **Clinical protocol implication**: Recommended a minimum 2-month undisturbed healing period to allow the ISQ dip to pass before considering modification of immediate loading protocols.
5. **High survival in immediate loading**: 98.5% implant survival in 51 patients under Teeth-in-a-Day protocol demonstrated the clinical viability of immediate loading with proper case selection.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Protocol**: Teeth-in-a-Day (immediate functional loading) using Brånemark System implants placed in edentulous bone or extraction sites
- **RFA measurement**: Osstell device (SmartPeg); measured at placement (T0), 30 days (T1), 60 days (T2), 90 days (T3)
- **ISQ metric**: 임플란트 안정성 지수 (Implant Stability Quotient, ISQ) scale 1–100
- **Analysis**: Means ± SE reported; comparisons across bone type (I–IV), jaw position (anterior/posterior), and sex
- **Exclusion from analysis**: 68 implants missing one or more RFA timepoints → 276 analyzed (276/344 = 80.2%)
- **Bone classification**: Lekholm-Zarb bone density types I–IV used for stratification

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Timepoint | Mean ISQ ± SE |
|---|---|
| Placement (T0) | 70.35 ± 0.50 |
| 30 days (T1) | 66.38 ± 0.50 |
| 60 days (T2) | 68.01 ± 0.50 |
| 90 days (T3) | 68.82 ± 0.49 |

- **Implant survival**: 98.5% at 90 days (5 failures out of 344)
- **Bone type**: Type IV (soft) lowest ISQ at placement; ISQ increases from type IV→I (denser)
- **Jaw position**: Posterior ISQ < Anterior ISQ at all timepoints
- **Sex**: Female ISQ < Male ISQ; difference most pronounced in soft bone types
- **Most critical "predictor" ISQ concern**: Soft bone + posterior + female combination → lowest stability profile; longest span between implants creates lever-arm risk

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Short follow-up (90 days)**: No long-term survival or marginal bone level (MBL) data beyond 3 months
- **No control group**: All patients received immediate loading; no delayed loading comparison
- **Missing RFA data (19.8% excluded)**: 68/344 implants lacked complete timepoint data, potentially biasing results
- **No multivariate analysis**: Bone type, position, and sex effects reported descriptively without adjusted modeling; interaction effects not tested
- **Single implant system**: Brånemark System only; generalizability to other implant surfaces/geometries limited
- **No DOI**: PDF is a ResearchGate image scan; full text not machine-readable

## 6. Related Work

- Osstell RFA device first described by Meredith et al. (1996, 1997) — same measurement principle used here
- Teeth-in-a-Day protocol developed by Balshi TJ and Wolfinger GJ — this study is a stability documentation of their own clinical protocol
- ISQ dip → recovery pattern later confirmed in multiple prospective studies and incorporated into loading decision algorithms

## 7. Glossary

- **RFA (공명주파수분석, Resonance Frequency Analysis)**: 임플란트의 공명주파수를 측정해 골-임플란트 접촉 강성을 정량화하는 비침습적 방법
- **ISQ (임플란트 안정성 지수, Implant Stability Quotient)**: RFA 측정값을 1–100 스케일로 변환한 무차원 안정성 지수 (≥65 통상 안정 범위)
- **Teeth-in-a-Day**: 발치/식립 당일 고정성 임시보철을 장착하는 즉시부하 프로토콜
- **즉시부하 (Immediate Functional Loading)**: 임플란트 식립 후 48시간 이내 교합력이 가해지는 보철물 장착
- **Lekholm-Zarb 골질 분류 (Bone Quality I–IV)**: 피질골 두께·해면골 밀도 기반 4단계 분류; Type I(가장 치밀)→Type IV(가장 연함)
