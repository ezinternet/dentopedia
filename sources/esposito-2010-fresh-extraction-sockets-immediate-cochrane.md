## Why Ingested

즉시식립(즉시·즉시지연·지연) 시기 비교에 대한 Cochrane 체계적 문헌고찰로, 현재 wiki에서 가장 상위 수준의 근거가 필요한 식립 시기 결정 문제를 다룬다. 기존 [[immediate-implant/araujo-2019-socket-healing-immediate-implant-review]]가 생물학적 기전을 설명하는 반면, 이 Cochrane SR+MA는 RCT 합성을 통해 임상 결과(생존율·합병증·심미) 비교 근거를 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

Cochrane SR+MA of 7 RCTs (2010) found insufficient evidence to favor any implant placement timing (immediate, immediate-delayed, or delayed), with early placement potentially offering aesthetic benefits but higher complication risks.

## 한줄요약

7개 RCT를 합성한 2010년 Cochrane 체계적 문헌고찰로, 즉시·즉시지연·지연 식립 중 어느 시기도 명확히 우월하지 않으며 조기 식립은 심미적 이점 가능성과 함께 합병증 위험이 높을 수 있음을 보고했다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews
- **Publication Date**: 08 September 2010
- **Study Type**: Cochrane Systematic Review + Meta-analysis (pub3 version)
- **DOI**: 10.1002/14651858.CD005968.pub3
- **Search Date**: 2 June 2010

## 2. Key Contributions

- Defined and compared three implant placement timing categories: immediate (Type 1), immediate-delayed (Type 2, weeks to ~2 months post-extraction), and delayed (Type 3, after bone healing)
- Identified only 7 eligible RCTs from 14 initially identified, highlighting the paucity of high-quality evidence
- Reported that immediate-delayed implants showed better patient satisfaction and mucosal aesthetics at 2 years vs. delayed, but significantly more complications at 5 years (RR = 4.20; 95% CI 1.01–17.43)
- Found no significant differences between immediate and delayed implants in 2 RCTs (n = 126)
- Evaluated bone augmentation techniques at immediate implant placement, finding no superiority of autogenous bone over substitutes (anorganic bovine bone acceptable)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Search databases**: Cochrane Oral Health Group's Trials Register, CENTRAL, MEDLINE (1950–2010), EMBASE (1980–2010); supplemented by handsearching dental journals
- **Inclusion criteria**: RCTs comparing immediate/immediate-delayed/delayed implants or bone augmentation procedures; ≥1 year follow-up after functional loading
- **Data extraction**: Independent duplicate screening, methodological quality assessment, and data extraction; trial authors contacted for missing data
- **Statistical model**: Random-effects models; mean differences (continuous), risk ratios (RR) with 95% CI (dichotomous); patient as statistical unit
- **Eligible RCTs**: 14 identified; 7 included (4 timing RCTs + 3 augmentation RCTs)
- **Risk of bias**: Many trials judged at high risk of bias; trials frequently underpowered

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Comparison | N patients | Key finding |
|---|---|---|
| Immediate vs. delayed | 126 (2 RCTs) | No statistically significant differences |
| Immediate-delayed vs. delayed | 46 (1 RCT) | Better aesthetics and satisfaction at 2y; more complications at 5y (RR 4.20; 95% CI 1.01–17.43) |
| Immediate vs. immediate-delayed | 16 (1 RCT) | No differences at 2 years |
| Autogenous bone vs. no augmentation | 26 (1 RCT) | No significant difference |
| Augmentation technique comparisons | 56 (2 RCTs) | No technique superiority demonstrated |

- Immediate-delayed group reported shorter perceived treatment time and higher satisfaction at 2 years (RR = 1.68; 95% CI 1.04–2.72 for appropriate mucosal level)
- Differences between immediate-delayed and delayed groups disappeared at 5 years, but complication burden persisted
- Anorganic bovine bone can substitute for autogenous graft at immediate placement sites

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- All included trials underpowered; insufficient numbers to detect clinically meaningful differences
- High risk of bias in most included trials
- Only 7 of 14 identified trials were eligible; evidence base very limited for 2010
- Authors conclude "preliminary conclusions based on few underpowered trials often judged to be at high risk of bias"
- Future well-powered RCTs needed to clarify timing advantages and augmentation indications

## 7. Glossary

- **즉시식립 (Immediate implant, Type 1)**: Implant placed into fresh extraction socket on same day as tooth removal
- **즉시지연식립 (Immediate-delayed implant, Type 2)**: Implant placed weeks to ~2 months after extraction, following soft tissue healing
- **지연식립 (Delayed implant, Type 3)**: Implant placed after substantial bone healing (>2 months post-extraction)
- **위험비 (Risk Ratio, RR)**: Ratio of event probability in intervention vs. control group
- **골이식 (Bone augmentation)**: Procedure to fill gap between implant and bony socket wall, with autogenous or substitute materials
- **무기 소뼈 (Anorganic bovine bone, ABB)**: Deproteinized bovine bone mineral; xenograft substitute for autogenous bone
