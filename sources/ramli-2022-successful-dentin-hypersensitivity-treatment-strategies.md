## Why Ingested

[[overviews/dentin-hypersensitivity-overview]]의 진단·기전·치료 단계 흐름에 임상 의사결정 narrative를 보강. 환자 동기·술자 지식이 장기 결과를 좌우한다는 점을 강조해 in-office/at-home 옵션 선택의 맥락 제공.

## One-line Summary
Narrative review summarizing DH mechanisms (hydrodynamic theory), diagnosis, and at-home/in-office treatment options, emphasizing that successful outcomes depend on patient motivation and clinician knowledge.

## 한줄요약
Narrative review: DH 기전(유체역학설)·진단·가정용/진료실용 치료를 정리하고, 성공적 결과는 환자 동기와 술자 지식에 달렸음을 강조.

## 1. Document Information
- Dental and Medical Problems 2022;59(3):451–460. Universiti Sains Malaysia.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Consolidates current DH mechanism, diagnosis, and management updates.
- Frames treatment success as a shared patient–clinician effort.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative review (non-systematic); thematic synthesis of mechanisms, treatment options, management approaches.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Prevalence ≥1 in 10 adults; significant OHRQoL impact.
- Stepwise management from at-home desensitizers to in-office occlusion/nerve-modulation agents.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Narrative (low evidence weight); no pooled estimates.

## 6. Related Work
- Clinical-decision framing complements the laser/toothpaste meta-analyses.

## 7. Glossary
- Hydrodynamic theory: fluid movement in tubules stimulating pulpal nerves (Brännström).
- OHRQoL: oral health-related quality of life.
