## One-line Summary
J Esthet Restor Dent 2022;34(3):473-489: SR+MA (25 studies SR; 3 in meta-analysis); enamel wear vs monolithic Zr within accepted levels; polished < glazed enamel wear; surface texture is essential factor.

## 1. Key Results
- 3968 articles screened; 25 met SR criteria; 3 included in meta-analysis (50N, 240-250k cycles)
- Meta-analysis: enamel wear against monolithic Zr = within statistically accepted levels
- Polished monolithic Zr: less enamel wear than glazed
- Conclusion: surface texture (polish vs glaze) is essential determinant of enamel wear
- Published online 2021; volume 2022
