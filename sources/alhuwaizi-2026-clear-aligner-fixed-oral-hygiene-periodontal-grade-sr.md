## Why Ingested

`clear-aligner` sweep의 **치주·구강위생 축에 GRADE 등급을 입힌** RCT-only SR. periodontal SR+MA [[wiki/orthodontics/crego-ruiz-2023-periodontal-gingival-recession-aligner-vs-fixed-sr-ma]] 의 신중한 결론("CA가 치주에 다소 유리하나 근거 약함")을 GRADE 인증서로 보강 — 본 리뷰는 oral hygiene level은 **very low**, periodontal health는 **low** 등급으로 못 박고, CA 우위가 **순응도 의존(compliance-dependent)**임을 명시. paywall이라 abstract-only.

## One-line Summary

Qualitative SR with GRADE (6 RCTs, 2015–April 2025): periodontal/hygiene parameters significantly better with clear aligners than fixed appliances, but 5/6 RCTs at high RoB and certainty only very-low (oral hygiene) to low (periodontal) — the CA advantage is highly compliance-dependent and no firm conclusion is possible.

## 한줄요약

GRADE 기반 정성 SR(RCT 6편, 2015–2025.4): 치주·구강위생 지표는 투명교정장치(CA)가 고정장치(FA)보다 유의하게 양호하나, 6편 중 5편이 high RoB이고 근거확실성은 구강위생 very-low·치주 low. CA의 우위는 순응도(compliance)에 크게 좌우되어 확정 결론 불가.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR to apply a **dual RoB 2 + GRADE** appraisal specifically to the CA-vs-FA oral-hygiene / periodontal-health question.
- Anchors the periodontal CA advantage to formal certainty grades (very-low / low) rather than narrative claims.
- Reframes the CA periodontal "edge" as **compliance-dependent**, not an intrinsic device superiority.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Search: Scopus, PubMed, Web of Science, Google Scholar; RCTs published in the last 10 years (2015 to April 2025).
- Independent eligibility assessment, data extraction, filtering.
- Quality: RoB 2 for included RCTs; certainty of evidence by GRADE.
- Primary outcome: oral hygiene / periodontal health by clinical periodontal indices; secondary outcomes also extracted.
- 1098 records screened → **6 RCTs** included for qualitative synthesis (no meta-analysis performed).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Qualitative synthesis: periodontal parameters **significantly better with CA than FA**.
- **5 of 6 RCTs at high RoB**, with inconsistencies across oral hygiene instructions (OHI), patient age, follow-up, diagnosis, and measurement methods.
- GRADE certainty: **very low** for oral hygiene level, **low** for periodontal health.
- Bottom line: CA advantage over FA is **highly compliance-dependent**; heterogeneity + high RoB + low/very-low certainty prevented a firm conclusion.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- No meta-analysis (qualitative only) due to heterogeneity.
- 5/6 included RCTs at high risk of bias; inconsistent OHI, age, follow-up, diagnosis, and measurement protocols.
- Certainty very-low to low — conclusions cannot be considered firm.
- Need for standardized, lower-RoB RCTs controlling for OHI and compliance. Abstract-only ingest.

## 7. Glossary

- **CA / FA**: clear aligner / conventional fixed appliance.
- **GRADE**: Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation — rates certainty of evidence (high/moderate/low/very-low).
- **RoB 2**: Cochrane risk-of-bias tool v2 for RCTs.
- **OHI**: oral hygiene instruction.
- **Compliance-dependent**: the observed benefit hinges on patient adherence (wear time, hygiene), not solely on the device.
