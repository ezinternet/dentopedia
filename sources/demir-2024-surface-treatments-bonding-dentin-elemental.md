## One-line Summary
In-vitro study of how dentin surface treatments and bonding type affect dentin elemental composition and bond strength.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — 상아질 표면처리·본딩 방식이 원소 조성·결합강도에 미치는 영향.

## 1. Document Information
Scientific Reports 2024; 14: article s41598-024-75709-2. DOI 10.1038/s41598-024-75709-2. Open access. Turkish dental faculties.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Couples elemental/compositional dentin analysis with bond-strength outcomes.
- Clarifies surface-treatment × bonding-mode interaction.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro: dentin specimens, multiple surface treatments and bonding types; surface elemental composition + bond strength measured.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Surface treatment and bonding type both modulate dentin elemental composition and bond strength (treatment-dependent).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; specific materials/treatments; clinical generalization limited.

## 6. Related Work
Sirirangsee 2025 (papain pretreatment of eroded dentin).

## 7. Glossary
- Elemental composition: surface element profile (e.g., Ca, P) via EDX/spectroscopy.
- Bonding type: E&R vs self-etch approach.
