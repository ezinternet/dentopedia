## Why Ingested

NaOCl(차아염소산나트륨) 노출 후 저하된 dentin bond strength를 collagen crosslinker로 회복하는 전략을 정량화. 근관세정([[endodontics/irrigation/fortea-2024-chelating-agents-endodontic-treatment-sr]])과 접착([[overviews/adhesive-bonding-strategies-overview]])의 교차점. endo-restorative interface 실무에 직접 연결.

## One-line Summary
SR+MA (14 in vitro studies, 11/14 low RoB) showing collagen crosslinkers (EDTA, IP6, PA, RA, sodium ascorbate) overall improve bond strength of NaOCl-treated dentin, modulated by NaOCl concentration and exposure duration.

## 한줄요약
SR+MA(in vitro 14편·11편 low RoB): 콜라겐 가교제(EDTA·IP6·PA·RA·소듐 아스코르베이트)가 NaOCl 처리 상아질의 결합강도를 전반적으로 개선하며, 효과는 NaOCl 농도·노출시간에 의존.

## 1. Document Information
- Frontiers in Bioengineering and Biotechnology 2025;13:1547158. Southern Medical University, Guangzhou.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pools crosslinker effect on NaOCl-compromised resin-dentin bond.
- Subgroups by crosslinker type and by NaOCl concentration/duration.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PubMed, EMBASE, Cochrane, Scopus, Web of Science, SinoMed; in vitro crosslinker-on-NaOCl-dentin BS studies.
- 1,538 → 14 included; 11/14 low RoB; meta-analysis of bond-strength SMD/MD.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Overall advantage of crosslinkers for restoring BS on NaOCl-treated dentin.
- Magnitude influenced by NaOCl concentration and exposure time.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; heterogeneous crosslinkers/protocols; clinical durability untested.

## 6. Related Work
- Bridges endodontic irrigation chemistry and adhesive recovery strategies.

## 7. Glossary
- Collagen crosslinker: agent restoring collagen-matrix integrity (e.g., proanthocyanidin, sodium ascorbate) to recover bonding after oxidation.
- NaOCl: sodium hypochlorite.
