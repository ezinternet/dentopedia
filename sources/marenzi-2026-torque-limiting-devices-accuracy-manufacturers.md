## Why Ingested

어버트먼트 스크류 조임 토크(Abutment Screw Tightening Torque)의 정밀도는 임플란트-어버트먼트 계면(Implant-Abutment Interface) 미세간극(Microgap) 방지에 직결됨. 기존 [[prosthetic-materials/kim-2020-abutment-screw-retorquing-preload-loss]] 등과 연계하여, 제조사 제공 기계식 토크 제한 장치(Mechanical Torque-Limiting Device)가 실제로 설정값을 정확히 전달하는지에 대한 in vitro 근거를 추가함.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (3 manufacturers, 5 wrenches each, 20 measurements × 3 torque levels) found all mechanical torque-limiting devices delivered less torque than set (error 11–29.3%); all wrenches at 25 Ncm eliminated microgap regardless of manufacturer.

## 한줄요약

기계식 토크 제한 렌치(Mechanical Torque-Limiting Wrench) 3종 제조사 비교 in vitro 연구: 모든 장치가 설정값보다 낮은 토크(오차 11–29.3%)를 전달하나, 25 Ncm 적용 시 전 제조사에서 미세간극(Microgap) 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health 2026;26(1)
- **Study type**: In vitro experimental study
- **Wrenches tested**: Biotech Dental (A), Biosafin (B), Global D (C) — all friction/toggle-type, 10–35 Ncm or 70 Ncm range
- **Torque levels tested**: 15, 20, 25 Ncm (20 measurements per wrench per level, n=5 wrenches per manufacturer)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First direct head-to-head comparison of three commercially available mechanical torque-limiting wrenches under standardized universal testing machine conditions.
- Quantified systematic under-delivery of torque across all manufacturers and settings (error range 11–29.3%).
- Demonstrated that at 25 Ncm all wrenches, despite under-delivery, produced zero microgap at the implant-abutment interface (SEM confirmation).
- At 20 Ncm, significant inter-manufacturer differences in microgap distribution were found (A < B and C).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Wrench accuracy**: 5 unused wrenches per manufacturer set at 15, 20, 25 Ncm; 20 measurements each on Universal Testing Machine (Sun 500, Galdabini); 3D-printed PLA support to standardize wrench orientation; torque = lever arm × max force at click.
- **Microgap assessment**: 3 groups of 20 implants (Kontact/Biotech, TTc/Biosafin, Inkone/Global D); conical Morse taper connections (5°, 3°, 8° taper angles); tightened at 20 or 25 Ncm (n=10 each); embedded in epoxy resin, longitudinally sectioned, SEM analysis (600× magnification) at implant shoulder–abutment interface; ImageJ measurement (3–5 measurements/image).
- **Statistics**: Kolmogorov-Smirnov normality; paired t-test (actual vs. nominal); one-way ANOVA + Bonferroni post-hoc for inter-wrench and inter-implant group comparisons.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Setting | Wrench A (mean ± SD) | Wrench B (mean ± SD) | Wrench C (mean ± SD) | Error range |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------|----------------------|-------------|
| 15 Ncm | 14.6 ± 0.56 | 14.1 ± 0.74 | 14.1 ± 0.72 | −3–−6% (A); ~−6% (B,C); max 29.3% seen at B/15 Ncm |
| 20 Ncm | 19.6 ± 0.48 | 18.9 ± 0.64 | 19.0 ± 0.71 | 11% min error (A/20 Ncm) |
| 25 Ncm | 24.0 ± 0.77 | 23.7 ± 0.83 | 23.7 ± 0.94 | ~4–5% error |

- Wrench A significantly closer to set values at all levels vs B and C (p<0.001–0.047); B vs C not significant.
- At 25 Ncm: zero microgap in all groups (all manufacturers).
- At 20 Ncm: Implant group A showed smaller gaps than B and C (p<0.05).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro only — no simulation of clinical factors (saliva, repeated use, sterilization cycles).
- Only friction/toggle-type wrenches tested; spring-style and electronic devices not included.
- Limited manufacturer set (3); results may not generalize to all market products.
- No assessment of wrench accuracy degradation with repeated use.

## 6. Related Work

- Mechanical and biological screw loosening studies; preload loss and micro-gap literature.
- SEM gap measurement methodology builds on prior implant-abutment interface characterization studies.

## 7. Glossary

- **토크 제한 장치 (Torque-Limiting Device, TLD)**: 설정 토크에서 자동으로 멈추는 렌치
- **미세간극 (Microgap)**: 임플란트-어버트먼트 계면의 미세한 틈새; 세균 침투·스크류 풀림 원인
- **사전하중 (Preload)**: 스크류 조임 시 발생하는 압축력; 토크-조임 관계의 핵심 변수
- **마찰계수 (Coefficient of Friction)**: 표면조도·경도와 연관; 마찰↑ → 동일 토크 대비 사전하중↓
- **Morse taper connection**: 원추형 내부 연결 방식; 마찰 잠금으로 미세동요 억제
