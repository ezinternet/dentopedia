## Why Ingested
Durability benchmark for HEMA/BPA-free universal adhesives; supports [[resin-bonding/tang-2024-hema-bpa-free-universal-adhesives-dentin]] and the [[resin-bonding/clearfil-se-bond-product-reference]] page.

## One-line Summary
In-vitro multi-parameter study using CSE Bond 2 as gold standard; HEMA/BPA-free universals match CSE2 and beat Scotchbond Universal after 50k thermocycles.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — CSE2 기준, HEMA/BPA-free universal이 CSE2 동등·Scotchbond Universal 우위(50k thermocycle).

## 1. Document Information
J Adhes Dent 2024. DOI 10.3290/j.jad.b4949669. KU Leuven (BIOMAT, Van Meerbeek group) and collaborators.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Establishes CSE2 as comparative gold standard.
- Multi-parameter (beyond bond strength) characterization of HEMA/BPA-free universals.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro dentin bonding; 50,000-thermocycle aging; comparison vs CSE2 and Scotchbond Universal.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
HEMA/BPA-free universals ≈ CSE2 and > Scotchbond Universal after 50k thermocycles.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; lab aging proxy; clinical correlation needed.

## 6. Related Work
Etiennot 2025 (saliva-contaminated dentin); HEMA-free formulation literature.

## 7. Glossary
- HEMA: 2-hydroxyethyl methacrylate (hydrophilic monomer).
- BPA: bisphenol-A.
- Thermocycling: thermal aging (e.g., 5–55 °C cycles).
