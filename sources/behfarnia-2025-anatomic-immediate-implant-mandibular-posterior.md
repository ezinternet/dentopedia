## Why Ingested

기존 [[immediate-implant/aldahlawi-2023-cbct-alveolar-bone-maxillary-mandibular-molars]]가 단일 CBCT 연구로 하악 구치부 골 해부를 다뤘다면, 본 논문은 12편·1,834명을 pooled한 SR+MA로 하악 구치부 즉시식립(Immediate Implant Placement, IIP)의 해부학적 안전 한계를 정량화한다. 근 (Inferior Alveolar Canal, IAC) 이격거리와 설측판 천공 (Lingual Plate Perforation, LPP) 위험을 부위별(2PM/1M/2M)로 수치화해 [[immediate-implant/pavlovic-2022-interradicular-septum-morphology-molar-iip]]의 치근간중격 1차 안정성 논의를 해부학적 위험 측면에서 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (12 CBCT studies, 1,834 patients): in mandibular posterior IIP the pooled root-apex-to-IAC distance is 5.14 mm (2nd premolar), 6.32 mm (1st molar) and 4.65 mm (2nd molar), and undercut (U-type) ridge morphology dominates the 2nd molar site (73%), driving lingual plate perforation risk.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰+메타분석 (12편 CBCT 연구, 1,834명): 하악 구치부 즉시식립에서 치근첨–하치조관 (Inferior Alveolar Canal, IAC) 평균 거리는 제2소구치 5.14 mm, 제1대구치 6.32 mm, 제2대구치 4.65 mm이며, 제2대구치 부위는 73%가 언더컷(U형) 치조제라 설측판 천공 (Lingual Plate Perforation, LPP) 위험이 가장 높다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR+MA to pool CBCT-based root-apex-to-IAC distances across the entire mandibular posterior sextant (2nd premolar through 2nd molar) — gives site-specific safety numbers rather than single-study estimates.
- Quantifies mandibular ridge morphology prevalence (Chen C/P/U classification) by site, identifying the undercut (U-type) ridge as dominant at the 2nd molar (73%).
- Links ridge morphology to lingual plate perforation (LPP) risk, consolidating reported LPP rates (e.g., Yahya 70% 1M / 76% 2M; Sun U-type 2M 22.2% with 4.8 mm implants).
- Synthesizes sex/age effects: females and patients < 40 tend to have shorter root-to-IAC distances (higher IAN injury risk).
- Reinforces the ≥ 2 mm IAC safety margin and ≥ 4 mm native apical bone requirement for primary stability in the posterior mandible.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Databases: PubMed, Cochrane, Web of Science, ProQuest, Scopus + manual cross-referencing; English only; 2006–October 2024.
- Inclusion: clinical trial, cohort, cross-sectional CBCT studies reporting IAN–root apices relationship, mandibular cross-sectional morphology, lingual undercut, or LPP risk for posterior mandibular IIP. Exclusion: case reports/series, non-CBCT, reviews.
- Screening: 1,151 records → 933 unique (+1 manual) → 21 full-text → 12 included (9 cross-sectional, 3 retrospective); inter-reviewer kappa 0.91.
- Quality: Modified STROBE checklist (12 items, score 0–12); cutoff < 8 excluded. All 12 studies scored 11–12 (mean 11.416), low risk of bias.
- Synthesis: Comprehensive Meta-Analysis V3 (Biostat), random-effects model; pooled ridge-morphology prevalence and mean IAC–apex distance with 95% CIs; heterogeneity by I²; publication bias by funnel plot, Egger's regression, Begg's rank correlation.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Pooled mean root-apex-to-IAC distance (random-effects):
- **2nd premolar: 5.146 mm** (95% CI 3.832–6.460; 4 studies; I² = 97.1%)
- **1st molar: 6.329 mm** (95% CI 4.477–8.182; 3 studies; I² = 98.0%)
- **2nd molar: 4.653 mm** (95% CI 4.057–5.249; 3 studies; I² = 81.1%) — shortest, IAC closest here

Ridge morphology (Chen C/P/U), pooled event rates:
- 1st molar: Convergent 29.5%, Parallel 35%, **Undercut 41.1%**
- 2nd molar: Convergent 9.0%, Parallel 15.4%, **Undercut 73%** (95% CI 62.5–81.4%)
- Combined: Convergent 25%, Parallel 25.2%, Undercut 49.2%

Lingual plate perforation (LPP):
- Highest in U-type cross-sections and more posterior sites.
- Yahya: LPP risk 70% (1M), 76% (2M); high-risk 18% (1M), 44% (2M).
- Sun: U-type 2M with 4.8 mm implant → 22.2% LPP incidence.
- Huang: LPP probability falls 34% per 1 mm increase in root-apex-to-canal (RAC) distance.
- Rameswaran: low overall incidence (1.2%) but rises with implant diameter.

Sex/age: females and patients < 40 years show shorter RAC/DLC and higher IAN injury risk; canal–apex distance generally increases with age.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Extreme between-study heterogeneity (I² up to 98%) in pooled distance estimates; few studies per tooth (3–4) limits reliability.
- Funnel-plot asymmetry suggests publication bias (smaller distances possibly underreported).
- CBCT measurements sensitive to voxel size, beam hardening, landmark identification error.
- Descriptive anatomical review — no clinical outcome (survival/failure) endpoint; PICOS 'Comparison' element not applicable.
- Authors call for standardized protocols, larger diverse populations, and study of interradicular septa for primary stability.

## 6. Related Work

## 7. Glossary

- **IIP** — Immediate Implant Placement (식립을 발치와 동시 시행).
- **IAC / IAN** — Inferior Alveolar Canal / Nerve (하치조관/하치조신경).
- **LPP** — Lingual Plate Perforation (설측 피질골판 천공).
- **RAC / DTC** — Root-apex-to-(alveolar)Canal distance / Distance to Canal.
- **DLC** — Distance to outer Lingual Cortex.
- **C/P/U ridge (Chen classification)** — Convergent / Parallel / Undercut mandibular cross-sectional morphology.
- **CBCT** — Cone Beam Computed Tomography (콘빔 전산화단층촬영).
