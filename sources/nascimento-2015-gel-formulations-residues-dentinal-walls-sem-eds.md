## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/irrigation/cruz-2014-debris-apical-third-naocl-glyde-in-vivo]]의 paste 잔류 우려를 gel 제형(NaOCl gel·CHX gel·EDTA gel)으로 확장. 최종 세척·초음파 활성화 후에도 dentinal wall에 잔류물이 남는지 SEM/EDS로 정량.

## One-line Summary
In vitro SEM/EDS study (48 single-rooted teeth) evaluating residues of 5.5% NaOCl gel, 2% CHX gel, and 24% EDTA gel on dentinal walls after canal preparation and final saline + ultrasonic irrigation.

## 한줄요약
In vitro SEM/EDS(48 단근치): 근관성형 + 식염수·초음파 최종 세척 후 5.5% NaOCl gel·2% CHX gel·24% EDTA gel의 상아질벽 잔류물을 분석.

## 1. Document Information
- Microscopy Research and Technique 2015;78(6):495–499.

## 2. Key Contributions
- SEM + elemental (EDS) detection of gel-formulation residues on canal walls.
- Assesses whether final irrigation/ultrasonic activation removes gel residues.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 48 single-rooted teeth; GI 5.5% NaOCl gel, GII 2% CHX gel, GIII 24% EDTA gel, negative control; final 6 mL saline + 1 min passive ultrasonic; SEM/EDS analysis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Distinct residue/elemental signatures per gel persisted on dentinal walls despite final irrigation/ultrasonic activation.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; single-rooted teeth; qualitative-semiquantitative residue scoring.

## 6. Related Work
- Gel-residue complement to paste-debris (Glyde) in vivo study.

## 7. Glossary
- EDS: energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (elemental analysis).
- Passive ultrasonic irrigation (PUI): ultrasonic activation of irrigant.
