## Why Ingested

구강상악동루 (Oroantral Fistula, OAF) 의 외과적 폐쇄 술식 전반을 체계적으로 정리한 고전 기술 리뷰로, 기존 OMS 클러스터 [[wiki/oral-medicine/psillas-2020-odontogenic-maxillary-sinusitis-comprehensive-review]] 및 [[wiki/oral-medicine/lin-2024-odontogenic-maxillary-sinusitis-expert-consensus]]가 다루는 치성 상악동염의 원인·관리에서 결손부 폐쇄 술기 선택의 실제(buccal flap 크기 기준, palatal island flap 적응증, tongue/temporalis 원거리 피판 기준)가 빠져 있는 공백을 보완.

## One-line Summary

Narrative review (1999) of oroantral fistula (OAF) closure techniques covering buccal sliding flap, buccal fat pad, palatal rotation/island flap, tongue flap, temporalis flap, and alloplastic grafts, with a 4-step size-based protocol: acute primary repair ~95% success vs chronic secondary repair as low as 67%.

서사 리뷰(1999) — 구강상악동루 (Oroantral Fistula, OAF) 폐쇄 술식(협측 활주 피판, 협지방체 이식, 구개 회전/도서 피판, 설 피판, 측두근 피판, 동종이식재) 전반 정리; 급성 1차 수복 성공률 ~95% 대 만성 2차 수복 최저 67%, 결손 크기 기반 4단계 프로토콜 제시.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Provides a historical overview of all major soft tissue flap techniques for OAF closure spanning ~70 years of literature (1920–1993).
2. Articulates two universal principles for OAF closure: (a) infection-free sinus with adequate drainage (Caldwell-Luc or FESS); (b) tension-free, broad-based, well-vascularized flap over intact bone.
3. Offers a practical 4-step size-based protocol matching flap type to defect dimension:
   - 5–10 mm lateral/midalveolar → buccal sliding flap (Berger technique)
   - 5–10 mm palatal/midalveolar → palatal rotation flap
   - 10–20 mm anterior → anteriorly-based dorsal tongue flap
   - >2 cm posterior → temporalis myofascial flap
4. Highlights buccal fat pad graft (Egyedi 1977) for defects >2 cm as avoiding vestibular depth loss.
5. Documents success rate differential: acute primary repair ~95% vs chronic secondary repair as low as 67%.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Literature synthesis of published case reports, case series, and technique descriptions from 1920–1993.
- No prospective or randomized data; technique descriptions with illustrated figures (7 diagrams).
- 30 references cited.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Technique | Defect size / Location | Key advantage | Key disadvantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Buccal sliding flap (Berger 1939) | 5–10 mm, lateral/midalveolar | Simple, single-stage | Loss of vestibular depth |
| Buccal fat pad graft (Egyedi 1977) | >2 cm | No vestibular depth loss, readily reepithelializes | Requires periosteal incision under zygomatic buttress |
| Palatal rotation flap (Ashley 1939) | 5–10 mm, palatal/midalveolar | Axial blood supply (greater palatine artery) | Exposed raw bone at donor site, kinking risk |
| Palatal island flap (James 1980) | Small–moderate | Flexible rotation, less raw bone | Neurovascular pedicle vulnerable to trauma |
| Anteriorly-based tongue flap (Sachs 1979) | 10–20 mm, anterior | Hearty tissue bulk | 2-stage (pedicle division at 3 weeks), general anesthesia |
| Temporalis myofascial flap | >2 cm, posterior | Large reliable axial tissue bulk | Hemicoronal approach, significant morbidity |
| Gold plate alloplast | Variable (spanner) | Provides scaffold for mucosal regrowth | Expected extrusion by 6 weeks |

**Success rates**:
- Acute primary repair: ~95%
- Chronic secondary repair (OAF established >3 weeks): as low as 67%

**Spontaneous healing**: small acute communications ≤2 mm often heal without intervention; defects >5 mm present >3 weeks almost always epithelialize into chronic OAF.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Pure narrative review — no systematic search, no statistical data pooling.
- Pre-FESS era context: FESS mentioned as alternative to Caldwell-Luc, but evidence base not discussed.
- No patient-level outcomes data.
- Does not address guided tissue regeneration (GTR) membrane approaches (only briefly cited in Reference 3) or resorbable membrane-based closure now more common.
- Does not address the role of sinus lift sequencing relative to OAF closure.

## 7. Glossary

- **구강상악동루 (Oroantral Fistula, OAF)**: Epithelialized tract connecting oral cavity to maxillary sinus; develops from oroantral communication >5 mm lasting >3 weeks
- **구강상악동 교통 (Oroantral Communication, OAC)**: Acute opening between oral cavity and maxillary sinus (pre-epithelialization)
- **협지방체 (Buccal Fat Pad, BFP)**: Pedicled fat graft from buccal space; axial supply from maxillary and transverse facial arteries
- **구개 도서 피판 (Palatal Island Flap)**: Full-thickness palatal mucoperiosteal flap on neurovascular pedicle (greater palatine artery/nerve), tunneled under intact palatal bridge
- **Caldwell-Luc 술식**: Antrostomy via canine fossa to drain and debride maxillary sinus — historical gold standard; now largely replaced by FESS
- **기능적 내시경 부비동 수술 (Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery, FESS)**: Endoscopic sinus surgery for sinus drainage restoration — preferred over Caldwell-Luc in modern OAF management
