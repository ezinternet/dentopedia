## Why Ingested

Adds the large-language-model (LLM) arm to the `artificial-intelligence` category — a dentistry-specific (OMFS) ChatGPT SR, contrasting with the image-diagnosis SR+MAs: GPT-4 scores well on consent documents and patient communication (76.8% MCQ accuracy) but fails on complex clinical decisions. Reinforces the "LLM as clinician-reviewed draft tool, not autonomous decision-maker" staging in [[overviews/ai-dentistry-reviews-2024-2025-synthesis]].

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (10 studies; PRISMA, PROSPERO) of ChatGPT in oral and maxillofacial surgery: GPT-4 reached 76.8% accuracy on MCQs and excelled at informed-consent generation/patient communication (outperforming residents on accuracy/completeness/readability), but underperformed in pharmacology and complex clinical decisions — complement to, not replacement for, human judgment.

## 한줄요약

구강악안면외과에서 ChatGPT의 영향을 다룬 체계적 문헌고찰(10편): GPT-4는 객관식 76.8% 정확도·동의서 작성·환자 소통에서 우수(전공의 능가)하나 약리·복잡 임상결정에서는 미흡 — 인간 판단의 보조이지 대체 불가.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Dentistry-specific (OMFS) LLM evidence, vs the general-healthcare ChatGPT umbrella reviews.
- Maps where ChatGPT helps (consent docs, patient education, postoperative follow-up) vs fails (pharmacology, oral cancer/orthognathic decisions).
- Reinforces the human-supervision-required framing.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PRISMA-guided; 10 studies on clinical decision-making, surgical planning, patient education, research.
- ChatGPT (GPT-3.5/GPT-4) performance on MCQs, document generation, follow-up responses.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Task | Finding |
|---|---|
| MCQ accuracy (GPT-4) | 76.8% (highest among models) |
| Informed-consent docs | Outperformed other AI models and human residents (accuracy, completeness, readability) |
| Postoperative follow-up | Accurate, supportive responses |
| Pharmacology / complex decisions | Lower, variable performance |
| Personalised advice (oral cancer, orthognathic) | Limited |

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 10 studies; no quantitative pooling.
- Accuracy and personalisation limits require human supervision and clinical-database integration.
