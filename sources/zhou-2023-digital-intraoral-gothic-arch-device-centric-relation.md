## Why Ingested

This paper extends the Gothic-arch / jaw-relation cluster of the wiki into the **complete-denture centric-relation (CR)** workflow with a CAD/CAM-fabricated intraoral tracer. It complements [[wiki/occlusion/kwak-2024-masticatory-positioning-occlusal-stability-full-mouth]] (mandibular positioning and occlusal stability) and sits alongside the vertical-dimension determination evidence in [[wiki/complete-denture/fayad-2025-vertical-dimension-determination-prosthodontics-review]] — jaw-relation registration in edentulous prosthodontics requires both a vertical (VDO) and a horizontal (CR via Gothic-arch tracing) record, and this study addresses the horizontal axis with a digital device. Source is PubMed/PMC; Chinese-language journal (Journal of Peking University, Health Sciences). Retrieved as **abstract-only** — full text not available from PMC.

## One-line Summary

Prospective in-mouth paired comparison (n=10 edentulous patients) showing a digitally manufactured simple intraoral Gothic arch tracer (T1) matches the conventional intraoral tracer (T2) in centric-relation registration — all mandibular positional deviations <0.5 mm — while cutting clinical chair time from ~57.5 to ~41.9 min (P<0.01).

## 한줄요약

무치악 환자 10명을 대상으로 한 구내 짝지움 전향적 비교 연구로, 디지털 제작 간이 구내 고딕아치 장치(T1)가 전통적 구내 장치(T2)와 정중관계(centric relation) 등록에서 일치(하악 위치 편차 모두 0.5 mm 이내)하면서도 임상 의자 시간을 약 57.5분에서 41.9분으로 단축(P<0.01)함을 보였다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Validates a **digitally manufactured (CAD/CAM) simplified intraoral Gothic arch tracer** as a substitute for the conventional intraoral Gothic arch device for determining **centric relation** in complete-denture fabrication.
- Quantifies agreement between the two methods by 3D best-fit superimposition of scanned CR casts (Geomagic Studio), reporting sub-0.5 mm mandibular positional deviation in both midline and molar regions.
- Demonstrates a clinically meaningful **chair-time saving (~15.6 min, P<0.01)** without loss of registration accuracy.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Population:** 10 edentulous outpatients; each patient received **both** methods (within-patient paired design).
- **T1:** digitally manufactured simple intraoral Gothic arch device. **T2:** conventional intraoral Gothic arch device.
- **Outcomes:**
  - Clinical operating time for CR determination (minutes), compared by paired test.
  - Positional accuracy: maxillary/mandibular CR casts scanned → STL → imported into **Geomagic Studio**; maxillary cast multi-point complete best-fit alignment, then **maximum positional deviation of the mandible (T1 vs T2)** measured in the **midline region** and **molar region**, in vertical and labial(buccal)-lingual directions.
  - Clinical try-in: presence of denture midline deviation and posterior occlusal stability (tipping/rocking).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Chair time:** T1 = (41.90 ± 2.64) min vs T2 = (57.50 ± 2.37) min; paired test **P < 0.01** (significant).
- **Mandibular positional deviation (T1 vs T2):**
  - Midline region — labial-lingual (0.32 ± 0.14) mm; vertical (0.40 ± 0.23) mm.
  - Molar region — buccal-lingual (0.35 ± 0.23) mm; vertical (0.33 ± 0.20) mm.
  - All deviations, vertical and horizontal, **controlled within 0.5 mm**.
- **Clinical try-in:** neither group showed denture midline deviation or instability (no tipping/rocking).
- **Conclusion:** the digital simple intraoral Gothic arch device is clinically usable for CR determination — saves time while maintaining accuracy.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (n=10) and single-center; no long-term denture-outcome follow-up.
- Reference standard is the conventional intraoral tracer (concordance study), not a gold-standard CR; agreement ≠ truth.
- **Abstract-only ingest:** device-fabrication detail, scanner specifics, statistical detail (CIs), and operator-blinding are not captured here — full text not retrieved.
- Chinese-language journal; details limited to translated abstract.

## 7. Glossary

- **Centric relation (CR / 正中关系):** maxillomandibular relationship independent of tooth contact; the horizontal jaw-relation record needed for complete dentures.
- **Gothic arch tracing (哥特式弓描记):** arrow-point / needle-point tracing used to locate the retruded mandibular position; intraoral (in-mouth tracer) vs extraoral variants.
- **STL / best-fit superimposition:** scanned casts aligned by multi-point complete fit in Geomagic Studio to measure positional deviation between methods.
