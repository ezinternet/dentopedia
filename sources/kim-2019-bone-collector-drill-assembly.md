## One-line Summary

Granted Korean patent (KR 10-2327565 B1; patentee HaeNaem Co.) for an autogenous bone-collector drill whose elastic stopper sleeve has a thin folding band that visibly indicates insertion depth and softens contact pressure, reducing patient pain versus rigid-cup collectors.

## 한줄요약

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Depth-indicating elastic stopper (탄성스토퍼, 120)**: an opaque elastic sleeve over the drill body with a thin, larger-diameter folding band (접힘밴드, 125) that folds and expands radially in proportion to insertion depth, externally signaling how deep the drill has entered the alveolar bone.
2. **Pain reduction**: replaces the conventional rigid (hard plastic) collection cup pressed by an elastic member — which exerted large pressure P on the wound — with a soft elastic stopper, lowering patient pain.
3. **Bone + blood collection**: a collection hole (수집공, 113) through the drill body gathers harvested bone and blood for autogenous grafting.
4. **Depth limit feedback**: at maximum set depth the upper/lower folding grooves (상부접힘홈 127 / 하부접힘홈 126) meet, giving a tactile/visual stop.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Device structure:
- **Drill (110)**: drill body (드릴본체, 111) with a cutting edge (절삭날, 111a) at the tip; shank (샹크, 118) on top; shank-coupling step (샹크결합턱, 117); stopper-coupling tube (스토퍼결합관, 115); a collection hole (수집공, 113) through the body.
- **Elastic stopper (탄성스토퍼, 120)**: an open-top/bottom cylindrical, opaque, elastically deformable sleeve. Comprises a drill-coupling tube (드릴결합관, 121) above and a drill-protection tube (드릴보호관, 123) below, with a central folding band (접힘밴드, 125, arc cross-section) between them. Upper/lower folding grooves (127/126) are recessed facing each other to control the fold.
- **Operation**: as the drill cuts into alveolar bone (치조골, B), the band folds and expands radially by the insertion depth, displaying depth; at the preset max depth the two folding grooves' inner walls meet.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

No clinical/bench measurements (patent). Asserted effects: surgeon can easily read collected bone/blood volume and insertion depth; reduced patient pain versus rigid-cup collectors. Not quantified or independently validated.

## 7. Glossary

- **본콜렉터 (bone collector)**: instrument that harvests autogenous bone (and blood) during drilling for grafting.
- **자가골 (autogenous bone)**: the patient's own bone, harvested to fill defects/grafts.
- **접힘밴드 (folding band)**: thin elastic ring that folds radially to display drill insertion depth.
- **수집공 (collection hole)**: opening in the drill body through which bone/blood is gathered.
