## Why Ingested

IOS + CBCT 기반 3차원 보철주도 계획 + static CAIS + 사전제작 임시보철을 통합한 **완전 디지털 워크플로우**로 심미부 즉시식립·즉시 임시수복을 수행한 1년 전향 결과. 동일 Groningen 그룹의 [[immediate-implant/staas-2026-fiipp-palatal-position-cbct-3year]]·[[immediate-implant/meijer-2024-immediate-implant-bony-defect-10year-rct]]와 디지털 적용으로 연결된다.

## One-line Summary

1-year prospective case series (30 maxillary esthetic-zone patients) of fully digital immediate implant placement and provisionalization (IOS+CBCT planning, static CAIS, prefabricated temporary); 90% implant survival (3 early failures), PES+WES 15.4/20, MBL −0.18 mm (mesial)/−0.44 mm (distal), patient satisfaction 9.2/10.

## 한줄요약

1년 전향 케이스시리즈(심미부 30명) — 완전 디지털 워크플로우(IOS+CBCT 계획·정적 가이드수술·사전제작 임시보철) 즉시식립·즉시 임시수복에서 임플란트 생존율 90%(조기실패 3례), PES+WES 15.4/20, 변연골소실 −0.18/−0.44 mm, 환자 만족도 9.2/10.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Clinical Implant Dentistry and Related Research 2025;27:e70079. Open access.
- UMC Groningen, Netherlands. Funding: Nobel Biocare. Trial: NL8264.
- Design: prospective single-arm case series, 1-year follow-up.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrates feasibility of a fully digital, chairside-restoration workflow (prefabricated temporary) for immediate esthetic-zone implants.
- Reports combined clinical, esthetic, radiographic, and patient-reported outcomes.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 30 patients with a failing maxillary esthetic tooth; immediate placement + bone graft + prefabricated temporary restoration.
- Definitive restoration at 3 months.
- Outcomes pre-treatment, 6 weeks post-temp, 1 month and 1 year post-definitive.
- Static computer-assisted implant surgery from integrated IOS + CBCT planning.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Prefabricated temporary placed in all patients.
- 3 early implant failures → implant survival 90%; 27 patients evaluated at 1 year.
- Temporary restoration survival/success 100%; definitive survival 100%, success 96%.
- PES + WES (0–20): 15.4 (SD 2.5).
- MBL (placement → 1 yr): −0.18 mm (SD 0.57) mesial, −0.44 mm (SD 1.23) distal.
- Median buccal bone thickness stable; patient satisfaction 9.2/10 (SD 0.8).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Single-arm case series (no control), small n, 1-year follow-up.
- Early-failure rate (10%) warrants attention despite favorable restorative metrics.

## 6. Related Work

- Digital/guided immediate implant protocols; prefabricated provisional restorations.

## 7. Glossary

- **CAIS**: computer-assisted implant surgery (static guide).
- **PES/WES**: pink/white esthetic score.
- **Prefabricated temporary**: chairside provisional made before surgery from the digital plan.
