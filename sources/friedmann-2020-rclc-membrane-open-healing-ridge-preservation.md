## Why Ingested

가교화 콜라겐(RCLC)의 독특한 open-healing 특성을 임상적으로 검증한 파일럿 연구. 리보오스(ribose) 가교화가 막을 콜라게나아제로부터 보호해 노출 상태에서도 이차 상피화·골재생이 이루어짐을 조직학적으로 확인. [[wiki/bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation]] 카테고리의 일반 콜라겐막 open-healing 대안으로 RCLC의 차별점을 문헌화.

## One-line Summary

Pilot study (n=18 pts, 20 sockets): RCLC membrane open healing (no primary closure) → 100% implant placement without additional augmentation; vertical change 0.45 mm (NS); secondary epithelization in 100% by 4 weeks.

## 한줄요약

파일럿 연구 (n=18환자, 20발치와): RCLC막 open healing(1차 폐쇄 없음) → 100% 추가 증대 없이 임플란트 식립; 수직 변화 0.45 mm(NS); 4주 이내 100% 이차 상피화 완료.

## 1. Document Information

- **Study type**: Prospective pilot series (no control group)
- **Journal**: Journal of Periodontal & Implant Science
- **Year**: 2020
- **DOI**: 10.5051/jpis.2000400020
- **PMCID**: PMC7758302
- **Material**: OSSIXPlus (porcine RCLC, Datum Dental, Lod, Israel)
- **N**: 18 patients, 20 sockets (10 contained, 10 non-contained)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First to validate RCLC open healing for BOTH contained and non-contained sockets
- Demonstrates RCLC can perform ridge preservation without primary closure (simplifies surgery, preserves KM)
- Histological proof: new bone formation with RCLC residues showing NO inflammatory infiltrate
- Keratinized mucosa preserved at original position (vs coronal advancement which displaces MG junction ~4mm)
- No 2nd surgery needed (RCLC is resorbable, unlike d-PTFE)
- Clinically: all 20 implants placed without augmentation

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Open healing: no primary flap advancement, flap margins fixed in original position with double-interrupted sutures
- RCLC membrane interposed between flap and alveolar crest on buccal and lingual sides
- Non-contained sockets: bone substitute first (calcium sulfate or bovine xenograft) to support membrane
- Monitoring: weekly × 4 weeks (CHX rinse + H₂O₂ disinfection)
- Implants at 4 months
- Outcomes: clinical (occlusal photos + ImageJ 2D measurement), radiographic (periapical X-ray), histological (core + crestal biopsies)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Value |
|---|---|
| Implant placed without augmentation | 20/20 (100%) |
| Soft tissue closure by 3 weeks | 15/20 (75%) |
| Soft tissue closure by 4 weeks | 20/20 (100%) |
| Vertical height change | 0.45 mm (NS, p=0.450) |
| Cross-sectional area change | −9.60 mm² (~9.75%) (NS overall) |
| Muco-gingival junction maintained | 20/20 (100%) |

Histology: new woven bone + vital osteoblasts; RCLC residues non-inflammatory; perimembranous osteogenesis confirmed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Pilot, no control group
- No CBCT (2D measurements only)
- X-rays not standardized
- Small n; single center
- Only 4-month follow-up before implant placement

## 7. Glossary

- **RCLC**: ribose cross-linked collagen — collagen membrane treated with ribose sugar to resist collagenase
- **OSSIXPlus**: commercial RCLC product (Datum Dental, Israel)
- **Open healing**: intentional exposure of membrane; secondary epithelization expected
- **Contained socket**: extraction socket with intact buccal and lingual walls
- **Non-contained**: socket with >30% loss of buccal/lingual wall
- **Perimembranous osteogenesis**: bone formation at the membrane surface (unique to RCLC)
