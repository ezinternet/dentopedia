## Why Ingested

임플란트 치유 기간 중 연조직 형태 형성(emergence profile) 관리는 심미 임플란트 보철의 핵심 단계이며, [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/pirc-2026-emergence-profile-angle-peri-implant-health]]의 출현각(emergence profile angle) 논의를 보완하는 디지털 기법 적용 증례 보고. 3D 프린팅 치유 지대체 대용물을 통한 chairside 시간 단축 및 IOS 등록 정밀도 향상 전략을 제시.

## One-line Summary

Clinical report describing a 3D-printed emergence profile former retained by a titanium stock healing abutment that shapes peri-implant soft tissue and simplifies intraoral scan registration, demonstrated in one mandibular molar case with horizontal tissue deficiency.

## 한줄요약

티타늄 기성 치유 지대체에 유지되는 3D 프린팅 출현 형태 형성체를 이용해 임플란트 주위 연조직을 형성하고 구강 내 스캔 등록을 단순화한 증례 보고.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Introduced a **3D-printed emergence profile former** that mounts on a standard titanium stock healing abutment (no custom temporary abutment or abutment screw needed)
2. Demonstrated design workflow using free software (Autodesk Meshmixer) merging a digital scan body and diagnostic wax-up to define subgingival contours
3. Showed simultaneous function as a **soft tissue conditioner** and **IOS registration aid** — the former replaced the scan body during intraoral scanning, reducing steps
4. Described fabrication using autoclavable DLP resin for clinical safety

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Case**: 67-year-old male, missing mandibular #30 (2 years post-extraction), horizontal buccal bone deficiency, ~1 mm attached gingiva buccally.

**Digital planning workflow**:
- CoDiagnostiX 10.8.1 for implant position planning (CBCT + IOS + diagnostic wax-up merge)
- Implant: Straumann BLX Ø5.0×10 mm, slight lingual inclination to avoid buccal augmentation
- s-CAIS (static computer-assisted implant surgery) surgical template: DLP-printed with Straumann P Pro resin

**Emergence profile former design** (Autodesk Meshmixer 3.5):
- Measured titanium healing abutment (Straumann WB Ti, Ø6mm, GH 1.5mm, AH 4mm) with Boley gauge
- Scan body geometry modified to match healing abutment dimensions; screw access closed
- Diagnostic wax-up extended apically to encircle subgingival portions; Boolean subtraction of offset scan body (0.02 mm) created the internal sleeve
- Horizontal cut at greatest buccal height of contour
- Final STL exported and printed in DLP autoclavable photopolymer

**Clinical sequence**:
1. s-CAIS guided placement → BLX implant placed
2. 3D-printed former placed over titanium healing abutment intraoperatively
3. Former retained during healing phase (soft tissue conditioning + volume preservation)
4. At restorative appointment: intraoral scan with former in place → removed for conventional healing abutment scan → final prosthetics

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Buccal soft tissue **volume enhancement** observed post-healing despite initial horizontal tissue deficiency
- Aesthetically pleasing emergence profile achieved without buccal contour bone augmentation
- Reduced chairside time vs. chairside customization with flowable composite
- IOS registration simplified: peri-implant tissue contours and implant position captured simultaneously with the former as reference geometry

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Single case report** — no controlled comparison, no quantitative outcome measures (no PES scores, no volumetric measurements)
- Technique requires basic 3D modeling proficiency with Autodesk Meshmixer
- DLP resin biocompatibility for subgingival placement warrants further study; autoclavability noted but long-term subgingival tissue response not reported
- Authors acknowledge need for randomized or prospective studies to validate efficacy

## 7. Glossary

- **EPF (Emergence Profile Former)**: 출현 형태 형성체 — 임플란트 플랫폼에서 치은 변연까지 연조직 형태를 결정하는 임시 구조물
- **s-CAIS**: static computer-assisted implant surgery — 정적 컴퓨터 보조 임플란트 수술, 수술 가이드 사용
- **DLP (Digital Light Processing)**: 디지털 광조형 — 3D 프린팅 방식의 하나
- **BLX**: Bone-Level Xtend — Straumann 임플란트 시스템으로 자가-절삭형 디자인
- **IOS**: Intraoral Scanner — 구강 내 스캐너
- **Boolean difference**: 불리언 차감 — 3D 모델링에서 두 개체의 교집합 부분을 빼내는 연산
