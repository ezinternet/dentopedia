## Why Ingested

BoNT-A 근막통증 치료의 최장 추적 관찰 데이터(2년, n=200 완료) 확보. 기존 보유 논문들이 단기(3–6개월) 관찰에 집중하는 반면, 본 연구는 2년 시점에서도 통증 감소 효과가 유지됨을 보임. 또한 인구통계·사회경제적 요인이 초기 결과에 영향을 미치지만 장기 결과는 동질화된다는 clinical moderator insight 제공. [[botulinum-toxin/angelo-2026-incobotulinumtoxina-standardized-protocol-myogenous-tmd]]의 12개월 결과를 2년 시점으로 연장하는 보완 근거.

## One-line Summary

Prospective study (n=200 completed, 2-year follow-up) of BTX injection into masseter and temporalis for myofascial pain showed sustained pain reduction (VAS 7.99→3.87 at 2yr); early outcomes influenced by age/socioeconomic factors, long-term results converged. Abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

저작근(교근·측두근) BTX 주사로 근막통증을 치료한 전향 연구(n=200, 2년 추적)에서 통증이 지속적으로 감소(VAS 7.99→3.87)하였으며, 초기 효과는 연령·사회경제적 인자의 영향을 받으나 장기 결과는 동질화됨을 확인. 초록만 수집.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Longest published prospective follow-up for BTX injection targeting masseter + temporalis tender points for myofascial pain (2 years)
- Demonstrates **sustained long-term effect**: pain reduction initiated early and maintained to 2-year endpoint
- Identifies demographic moderators of early (not late) treatment response: age (26–35 yr optimal), income, education, marital status
- Large real-world sample (320 enrolled, 200 completed); multi-year recruitment (Jan 2019 – Nov 2024)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Prospective clinical study; Basrah, Iraq
- **Enrollment period**: January 2019 – November 2024
- **Sample**: 320 enrolled; 200 completed 2-year follow-up
- **Intervention**: BTX injections at tender points of masseter and temporalis muscles
- **Primary outcome**: Pain intensity on a pain scale (baseline and serial follow-ups through 2 years)
- **Subgroup analyses**: Age groups, gender, marital status, income, education level

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Mean pain score: **7.99 (SD 0.79) at baseline → 3.87 (SD 1.53) at 2 years** (sustained reduction)
- Highest pain reduction: early follow-up phase
- Age 26–35 yr: lower pain scores at 8-week follow-up (vs other age groups)
- Income and education: positively correlated with early pain reduction; convergence by 2-year assessment
- Gender difference: small, not dominant
- **Conclusion**: Large and long-lasting pain reduction; demographic/socioeconomic factors influence early but not long-term outcomes

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract only — injection dose, volume, preparation, exact injection sites not detailed
- No control group; no sham injection
- High dropout rate (120/320 = 37.5% did not complete)
- Iraq single-center cohort; generalizability unclear
- Demographic subgroup analyses likely underpowered

## 7. Glossary

- **Myofascial pain** (근막통증): pain originating from trigger points within masticatory muscles
- **VAS** (Visual Analogue Scale, 시각아날로그척도): 0–10 pain intensity scale
- **Tender points** (압통점): specific muscle sites targeted for BTX injection in myofascial pain protocols
