## One-line Summary
Prospective cohort (US PBRN PREDICT) showing endodontists were significantly more likely than general dentists to use >5× magnification, rubber dam isolation, NaOCl, and EDTA/H2O2/chlorhexidine irrigation during non-surgical root canal treatment.

## 한줄요약
전향 코호트(미국 PBRN PREDICT): 비외과 근관치료(NSRCT)에서 치과의사(GD) 대비 근관전문의가 >5× 확대경·러버댐·NaOCl·EDTA/H2O2/CHX 세정 사용률이 유의하게 높음.

## 1. Document Information
- Int Endod J 2025;58:1408–1419. National Dental PBRN PREDICT Project. Prospective cohort.
- Consecutive enrolment by GDs and endodontists.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Quantifies real-world practice-pattern gaps between endodontists and GDs in NSRCT.
- Benchmarks adoption of standard-of-care steps (isolation, magnification, irrigation).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Prospective cohort within the US National Dental PBRN; GDs and 49 endodontists; consecutive case enrolment; comparison of technique choices.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Endodontists significantly more likely to use magnification >5×, rubber dam isolation, NaOCl irrigation, and EDTA/H2O2/chlorhexidine.
- Differences framed around quality-relevant procedural steps and outcomes (pain resolution).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Observational; self-reported practice; network-enrolled clinicians may not represent all practices.

## 6. Related Work
- Practice-pattern benchmark for endodontic SOP and clinic policy.

## 7. Glossary
- NSRCT: non-surgical root canal treatment.
- PBRN: practice-based research network.
