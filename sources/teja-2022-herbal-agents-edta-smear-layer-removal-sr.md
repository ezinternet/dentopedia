## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/irrigation/fortea-2024-chelating-agents-endodontic-treatment-sr]]의 EDTA 기준에 herbal 대체제(천연 킬레이터) 비교 축 추가. quixabeira·noni·oregano·neem이 smear layer 제거에서 EDTA에 비빌 수 있는지 정리.

## One-line Summary
SR of in vitro studies comparing herbal agents with EDTA for smear-layer removal, finding quixabeira, Morinda citrifolia, oregano extract, and neem better than other herbals but EDTA generally remaining the reference.

## 한줄요약
In vitro SR: smear layer 제거에서 herbal 대체제와 EDTA 비교 — quixabeira·noni·오레가노·님이 타 herbal보다 우수하나 EDTA가 대체로 기준.

## 1. Document Information
- Int J Environ Res Public Health 2022;19:6870. MDPI. Open Access.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Systematic comparison of natural/herbal chelating alternatives vs EDTA.
- Ranks herbal agents by smear-layer-removal performance.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PRISMA SR of in vitro smear-layer-removal studies; herbal agents vs EDTA.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Quixabeira, Morinda citrifolia, oregano extract, neem showed better smear-layer removal than other herbals; EDTA remained a strong comparator.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; heterogeneous herbal concentrations/protocols; limited standardization.

## 6. Related Work
- Herbal-alternative angle to the chelating-agents SR.

## 7. Glossary
- Smear layer: debris layer on instrumented canal walls.
- Herbal chelators: plant-derived agents proposed as biocompatible EDTA alternatives.
