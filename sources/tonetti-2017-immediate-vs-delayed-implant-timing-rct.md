## Why Ingested

전치/소구치 단일치 발치 후 즉시식립(IMI) vs 12주 지연식립을 직접 비교한 대표적 다기관 RCT. 즉시식립의 심미·합병증 위험을 정량화해 [[immediate-implant/buser-2017-implant-placement-timing-post-extraction-esthetic]] 의사결정 narrative와 [[immediate-implant/chen-2014-esthetic-outcomes-immediate-early-implant]] 심미 결과를 1차 RCT 근거로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

Multicenter RCT (124 subjects, anterior/premolar single teeth) comparing immediate vs 12-week delayed implant placement; immediate placement required more bone augmentation (72% vs 43.9%), had more wound failures (26.1% vs 5.3%), deeper 1-year probing depths, and more inadequate pink-esthetic scores (42% vs 19%), leading to a recommendation against immediate placement where esthetics matter.

## 한줄요약

다기관 RCT(124명, 전치·소구치 단일치) — 즉시식립이 지연식립보다 골증대 필요(72% vs 43.9%)·창상실패(26.1% vs 5.3%)·1년 탐침깊이·부적절 심미점수(42% vs 19%)가 모두 많아, 심미가 중요한 경우 즉시식립을 권장하지 않는다는 결론.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Clinical Periodontology 2017;44:215–224. Open access.
- Sponsor: European Research Group on Periodontology (ERGOPerio); unrestricted grant from Thommen Medical; materials from Geistlich.
- Seven private practices; calibrated, masked examiners; restoratively driven implant position.

## 2. Key Contributions

- One of the few well-controlled multicenter RCTs directly comparing immediate vs delayed placement timing.
- Quantifies the augmentation burden, wound-healing risk, and esthetic penalty of immediate placement.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 124 subjects randomized to immediate implant (IMI, at extraction) or delayed (12 weeks).
- Single tooth in anterior/premolar regions; restoratively driven placement.
- Outcomes: need for bone augmentation, surgical complications, periodontal/radiographic/aesthetic and patient-reported outcomes; up to 3-year radiographic follow-up.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- IMI unfeasible in 7.5% of intended cases; one implant lost in IMI group.
- Bone augmentation required: IMI 72% vs delayed 43.9% (p = 0.01).
- Wound failure: 26.1% (IMI) vs 5.3% (delayed) (p = 0.02).
- 1-year probing depth: 4.1 ± 1.2 mm (IMI) vs 3.3 ± 1.1 mm (delayed) (p < 0.01).
- Radiographic bone loss: trend toward greater loss with IMI over 3 years (p-trend < 0.01).
- Inadequate pink esthetic score: 42% (IMI) vs 19% (delayed) (p = 0.03).
- Patient-reported outcomes: no differences.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- 1-year primary endpoint; longer follow-up needed for complication/survival differences.
- Outcomes operator-dependent; restoratively driven protocol may not generalize.

## 6. Related Work

- Buser et al. (placement timing decision framework); Chen & Buser (esthetic outcomes by timing).

## 7. Glossary

- **IMI**: immediate implant (placed at extraction).
- **PES**: pink esthetic score.
- **Wound failure**: incomplete soft-tissue closure / dehiscence after surgery.
