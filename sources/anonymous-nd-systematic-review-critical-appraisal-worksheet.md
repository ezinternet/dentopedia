## One-line Summary
A 4-page anonymous educational worksheet that guides the reader through a structured Yes/No/Unclear appraisal of a systematic review, organized around PICO definition, search comprehensiveness, inclusion criteria, study quality, and meta-analytic interpretation (forest plot + Cochran Q heterogeneity).

## 1. Document Information
- No named authors, no publication date, no journal, no DOI.
- 4 pages. Two-column "What is best? / Where do I find the information?" worksheet format consistent with CASP (Critical Appraisal Skills Programme) and Public Health Resource Unit (PHRU) templates.
- Includes a fully worked forest plot example (5 trials, pooled OR 0.66, 95% CI 0.56–0.78, Q/df < 1, p=0.92).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Operationalizes SR appraisal as a checklist: PICO, search strategy, inclusion criteria, study validity, heterogeneity assessment, forest plot reading.
- Provides numerical cutoffs for Cochran Q heterogeneity interpretation (Q/df > 1 = possible heterogeneity; significance threshold often set at p=0.10 due to low power).
- Reproduces the canonical forest plot anatomy (point estimate as black square, horizontal line as 95% CI, diamond as pooled estimate, vertical line at OR=1 as "no effect").

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Question-and-answer worksheet. Not a research paper.
- 6 appraisal domains, each with one yes/no/unclear box plus a comment field.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- The worked example (5-trial mortality MA, OR 0.66, 95% CI 0.56–0.78, p<0.0001 for overall effect, homogeneity confirmed) is illustrative — not a real dataset.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- No author attribution, no version control. Cannot be cited as a primary methodology source.
- Likely pre-PRISMA-2020; does not cover GRADE, risk-of-bias 2.0 (RoB 2), or AMSTAR 2.

## 6. Related Work
- Standalone analog to the SR appraisal section of Kiriakou 2014 (which is dental-specific and named-author).

## 7. Glossary
- PICO: Patient/Population, Intervention, Comparator, Outcome.
- Cochran Q: chi-square test for heterogeneity in MA.
- Forest plot: visual display of individual and pooled effect estimates with CIs.
