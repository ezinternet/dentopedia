## Why Ingested

식립 시점 프로토콜 비교에서 **조기식립(early placement)**이 즉시·지연 식립 대비 실패율은 동등하면서 주위 경조직 안정성에서 즉시식립보다 우수한지에 대한 정량 근거. 즉시 vs 지연을 다룬 [[immediate-implant/tonetti-2017-immediate-vs-delayed-implant-timing-rct]]과 [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/buser-2017-implant-placement-timing-post-extraction-esthetic]]의 의사결정 틀에 early arm을 정량적으로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 12 studies finding early implant placement yields implant failure rates equivalent to immediate and delayed protocols, but with significantly less marginal bone loss than immediate placement into fresh sockets (WMD −0.14 mm, 95% CI −0.22 to −0.05).

## 한줄요약

12편 SR+MA — 조기식립은 즉시·지연 식립과 임플란트 실패율이 동등하나, 신선 발치와 즉시식립보다 변연골소실이 유의하게 적었다(WMD −0.14 mm).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Periodontology 2019;90(5):493–506. (HHS Public Access manuscript.)
- Harvard School of Dental Medicine / Stony Brook / Univ. Bern / Univ. Pittsburgh.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Positions early placement quantitatively against both immediate and delayed protocols.
- Identifies a peri-implant hard-tissue stability advantage of early over immediate placement.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- SR+MA; electronic + manual search of clinical studies comparing early vs immediate or delayed placement.
- Primary outcome: number of failed implants; secondary: peri-implant marginal bone level, probing depth, soft-tissue level.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 12 studies included.
- Implant failure: early vs immediate RD −0.018 (95% CI −0.06 to 0.025, p = 0.416) — NS; early vs delayed RD −0.008 (95% CI −0.044 to 0.028, p = 0.670) — NS.
- Marginal bone level: significantly less MBL for early vs immediate (fresh-socket) placement — WMD −0.14 mm (95% CI −0.22 to −0.05, p = 0.001).
- Other secondary outcomes: no significant protocol differences.
- Key finding: early ≈ immediate/delayed for failure, but superior to immediate for peri-implant hard-tissue stability.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Heterogeneous study designs; few head-to-head RCTs.
- "Early" definitions (4–8 weeks soft-tissue vs partial bone healing) vary across studies.

## 6. Related Work

- Tonetti et al. (immediate vs delayed RCT); Buser et al. (timing framework); Chen & Buser (esthetic outcomes).

## 7. Glossary

- **Early placement**: implant placed after soft-tissue (Type 2, 4–8 wk) or partial bone (Type 3, 12–16 wk) healing.
- **RD / WMD**: risk difference / weighted mean difference.
