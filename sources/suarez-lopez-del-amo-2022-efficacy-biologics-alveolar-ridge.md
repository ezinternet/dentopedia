## Why Ingested

[[wiki/bone-regeneration/avila-ortiz-2019-alveolar-ridge-preservation-interventions]]가 ARP 중재 전반의 효과를 평가했다면, 본 AAP Best Evidence Consensus (Suárez-López del Amo & Monje 2022)는 그 범위를 biologics(ABP·EMD·rhPDGF-BB·rhBMP-2)와 ARR·ARA·MSFA까지 확장해 "생물학적 제제가 표준 프로토콜을 능가하는가"라는 별도 질문에 답한다 — anchor의 ARP 효과 평가를 biologics 차원으로 확장.

## One-line Summary

AAP Best Evidence Consensus systematic review of 39 RCTs (qualitative only, heterogeneity precluded meta-analysis) concluding biologics (ABP, EMD, rhPDGF-BB, rhBMP-2) generally do not yield superior clinical/radiographic ARP/ARR/ARA/MSFA outcomes versus controls, but improve histomorphometric bone formation and wound healing.

## 한줄요약

39편 RCT의 AAP Best Evidence Consensus SR(이질성으로 정량분석 불가)로, biologics(ABP·EMD·rhPDGF-BB·rhBMP-2)는 ARP/ARR/ARA/상악동거상 임상·방사선 결과에서 대조군 대비 우월하지 않았으나, 골조직형태계측상 신생골 형성과 창상치유는 개선했다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Periodontology, 2022;93:1827-1847
- DOI: 10.1002/JPER.22-0069
- Design: AAP Best Evidence Consensus systematic review (RCTs; qualitative synthesis only)
- Search: MEDLINE/EMBASE/CENTRAL, Jan 2000–Nov 2021
- Referee for disagreements: Gustavo Avila-Ortiz

## 2. Key Contributions

- Defines the AAP consensus position on four biologic classes across ARP/ARR and implant site development (ARA, MSFA).
- Separates indications: ARP (attenuating post-extraction change), ARR (extensive socket damage), ISD (healed ridge correction via ARA & MSFA).
- Distinguishes clinical/radiographic outcomes (no superiority) from histomorphometric/wound-healing outcomes (improved).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PICO: adults; intervention = ABPs/EMD/rhBMP-2/rhPDGF-BB in ARP/ARR/ARA/MSFA, monotherapy or with scaffold/graft; comparison = conventional protocols without biologics; primary outcome = linear/volumetric dimensional change + histomorphometry.
- Eligibility: RCTs (parallel/split-mouth), English, ≥2000, ≥10 sites/arm, follow-up ≥2 months (ARP/ARR) or ≥4 months (MSFA/ARA).
- Risk of bias: Cochrane RoB1.
- 39 articles included, qualitative synthesis only (high heterogeneity precluded quantitative pooling).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **ARP/ARR**: biologics generally rendered results similar to conventional protocols; but vs unassisted healing or collagen-sponge fill, biologics attenuated post-extraction atrophy in most studies; histomorphometry positively influenced.
- **ARA**: biologics did not yield superior clinical/radiographic outcomes; ABPs enhanced new bone formation and reduced early wound dehiscence.
- **MSFA**: no superior clinical/radiographic outcomes; some biologics promoted earlier-stage bone formation.
- **PROMs**: only 4 trials evaluated; modest beneficial impact on pain/swelling.
- Safety: no severe adverse events reported.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- High heterogeneity prevented meta-analysis (qualitative only).
- Few PROM-evaluating trials.
- Outcome definitions and follow-up windows vary widely.

## 6. Related Work

- Extends ARP-intervention efficacy syntheses (Avila-Ortiz 2019) into the biologics domain.
- Complements APC-focused ARP SR (Siawasch 2025).
