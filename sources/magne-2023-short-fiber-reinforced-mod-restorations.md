## Why Ingested

위키의 중합수축 페이지들([[resin/meereis-2018-polymerization-shrinkage-stress-resin-sr-ma]], [[resin/parra-gatica-2023-flowable-bulk-fill-composites-sr]])은 수축·수축응력의 in-vitro 수치를 다루지만, 그 수축응력이 실제 치아에서 어떤 임상 손상(법랑질 균열·교두 변형·파국적 실패)으로 이어지는지를 보여주는 fatigue/crack 연구는 없었다. Magne 2023은 대형 MOD에서 "수축응력 → 법랑질 crack" 인과를 직접 시각화하고, V-factor를 낮추는 inlay 기법과 SFRC base가 이를 완화함을 보강한다. 또한 동일 SFRC라도 everX Posterior(-1.52%) vs everX Flow(-2.58%)의 수축 차이가 crack 발생을 가르는 결정적 변수임을 보여준다.

## One-line Summary
In-vitro fatigue + enamel-crack study (36 maxillary molars, n=12/group) showing that polymerization-shrinkage stress causes large (>3 mm) enamel cracks in 67-83% of direct MOD restorations of severely undermined cusps, whereas CAD/CAM inlays with a short-fiber-reinforced composite (SFRC) base (lowest V-factor) produce essentially no shrinkage cracks and the best survival and most reparable failure modes.

## 한줄요약
In-vitro 피로·균열 연구(상악 대구치 36개, 군당 12): 심하게 undermine된 교두의 대형 MOD에서 중합수축응력이 직접수복의 67-83%에 >3 mm 법랑질 균열을 유발; SFRC base + CAD/CAM 인레이(최저 V-factor)는 수축균열 거의 0·최고 생존율·가장 reparable한 파절양상.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: The Journal of Adhesive Dentistry, 2023;25:99-106
- e-pub 2023-04-25; PMCID PMC11734284 (full text)
- IRB: University of Southern California (#HS-21-00568)
- Materials: Gradia Direct, everX Flow (SFRC), Cerasmart 270 (CAD/CAM), Optibond FL adhesive — all GC/Kerr

## 2. Key Contributions
- Directly visualizes shrinkage-stress-induced enamel cracking (transillumination tracking before/24h/1week) — links bench shrinkage values to a clinical failure mode
- Quantifies V-factor effect: CAD/CAM inlay (thin luting layer) → 0% cracks >3 mm vs 67-83% for direct
- Shows the flowable everX Flow SFRC base improves fatigue resistance and failure mode but only modestly reduces crack propensity — because its shrinkage (-2.58%) is higher than original everX Posterior (-1.52%)
- Direct control (no SFRC) had 100% catastrophic (irreparable) failures
- Reframes "soft-start/slow" curing: most shrinkage stress develops during/after vitrification ("dark-cure"), so slow protocols cannot relax it at clinically relevant conversion

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 36 caries-free maxillary 3rd molars; standardized slot MOD 5 mm wide × 5 mm deep, severe undercuts (1 mm residual enamel)
- Triplet-matched then randomized, n=12/group: (1) direct Gradia Direct only; (2) everX Flow base + Gradia Direct; (3) everX Flow base + Cerasmart 270 inlay (IDS)
- Accelerated fatigue (MTS Acumen 3): 30° load, antagonist composite cusp on palatal slope, submerged, 5 Hz, start 200 N +100 N/2000 cycles to fracture
- Enamel-crack tracking by transillumination/photography (none / <3 mm / >3 mm)
- Kaplan-Meier + log-rank post-hoc (α=0.05)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Survival: Inlay SFRC base > Direct SFRC > Direct control (log-rank p<0.000); all survived to 1000 N
- Cracks >3 mm at 1 week: Direct control 83% (10/12), Direct SFRC 67% (8/12), Inlay SFRC base 0%; inlay group 92% crack-free
- Failure mode: Direct control 100% irreparable; Direct SFRC 33% reparable/25% possibly/42% irreparable; Inlay 75% reparable/8% possibly/17% irreparable
- Shrinkage difference: everX Posterior -1.52% vs everX Flow -2.58% (explains residual cracking with flowable SFRC)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In-vitro, extreme/accelerated loading; no inlay-without-SFRC control group
- Thin SFRC layer (1-2.5 mm) used — thicker/full-SFRC restorations not tested
- Third molars; single adhesive/protocol

## 6. Related Work
- Provides the clinical-consequence endpoint for the shrinkage-stress mechanisms in Meereis 2018, Algamaiah 2021, Yang 2022
- everX/bulk-fill material context overlaps with Boaro 2019 and Parra-Gatica 2023

## 7. Glossary
- **MOD**: mesio-occluso-distal cavity. **SFRC**: short-fiber reinforced composite (everX). **V-factor**: volume of shrinking material (lower with inlays → less stress). **IDS**: immediate dentin sealing. **Dark-cure**: post-irradiation stress development during/after vitrification. **CEJ/ARB**: cementoenamel junction / acrylic-resin base (failure-mode reference planes).
