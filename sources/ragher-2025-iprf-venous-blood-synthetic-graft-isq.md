## Why Ingested

간접 상악동거상술(indirect sinus augmentation, ISA) 후 이식재 종류에 따른 임플란트 안정성(ISQ) 차이를 비교한 RCT가 없었음. 본 논문은 4개 군(무이식, 정맥혈, i-PRF, 합성골)을 동일 프로토콜 하에 직접 비교하여 [[wiki/implants/isq/lai-2008-rfa-iti-osteotome-sinus-elevation-without-graft]]의 무이식 경치조골 데이터를 생물학적 강화재(i-PRF, 합성골) 비교 맥락으로 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

RCT (n=64, 4 groups) comparing no graft, venous blood, i-PRF, and synthetic graft (NovaBone) for indirect sinus augmentation found that i-PRF and NovaBone achieved significantly higher ISQ values at 6 months (ISQ ~70–76) vs. no graft or venous blood (ISQ ~55–62), with no significant difference between i-PRF and synthetic graft.

## 한줄요약

간접 상악동거상술 후 i-PRF군과 합성골(NovaBone)군이 무이식군·정맥혈군 대비 6개월 ISQ를 유의하게 높였으며(~70–76 vs ~55–62), 두 군 간 차이는 없었다 (RCT, n=64).

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Journal of Indian Prosthodontic Society (JIPS)
- **Year**: 2025
- **DOI**: [10.4103/jips.jips_529_25](https://doi.org/10.4103/jips.jips_529_25)
- **Study type**: Prospective, parallel-group, randomized controlled trial, double-blinded
- **Registration**: CTRI/2025/03/081640
- **n**: 64 participants (16 per group), 64 implants
- **Follow-up**: 6 months

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First RCT to directly compare venous blood, i-PRF, and synthetic graft (NovaBone) vs. no graft in indirect crestal sinus augmentation under a standardized protocol.
2. Demonstrates that i-PRF achieves ISQ values comparable to synthetic graft (both ≥70), surpassing the clinically meaningful ISQ 70 threshold.
3. Clarifies that venous blood alone provides minimal additional benefit over no graft — no structured fibrin matrix or concentrated growth factors.
4. Demonstrates i-PRF's practical advantage: injectable, flowable, autologous, avoids foreign biomaterial, low centrifugation (60 g / 700 rpm / 3 min).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Population**: Partially edentulous, missing posterior maxillary teeth, residual bone height 4–6 mm, aged 18–60.
- **Exclusions**: Uncontrolled systemic disease, sinus pathology, periodontitis, smoking, bisphosphonate therapy, pregnancy/lactation.
- **Surgical protocol**: CAS Kit (Osstem); sequential CAS drills 2.8→3.1→3.3 mm; sinus floor breached with 4 mm stopper-controlled drill; membrane elevated ~5 mm with 0.5 mL saline via hydraulic lifter.
- **Implant**: Noris Tuffimplant 4.2 × 8 mm, 30 Ncm insertion torque.
- **i-PRF preparation**: 10 mL venous blood, 60 g (700 rpm), 3 min centrifugation; yellow i-PRF aspirated from upper layer.
- **Groups**:
  - Group 1: No graft
  - Group 2: 0.5 mL autologous venous blood
  - Group 3: 0.5 mL i-PRF
  - Group 4: 0.5 cc NovaBone alloplastic putty
- **Outcome**: ISQ via Osstell (SmartPeg), buccal + mesial directions at baseline and 6 months.
- **Statistics**: Shapiro-Wilk normality, one-way ANOVA, Tukey's post hoc, p < 0.05 threshold.
- **Blinding**: Outcome assessor and statistician blinded; allocation by sequentially numbered opaque sealed envelopes.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Baseline ISQ** (all groups comparable, p > 0.05):
- Buccal: ~50.1–50.4 across groups
- Mesial: ~53.1 across groups

**6-month ISQ**:

| Group | Buccal (Mean ± SD) | Mesial (Mean ± SD) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 (No graft) | 54.81 ± 6.18 | 55.75 ± 8.10 |
| 2 (Venous blood) | 56.75 ± 5.63 | 61.50 ± 9.30 |
| 3 (i-PRF) | 70.06 ± 6.32 | 74.75 ± 2.52 |
| 4 (NovaBone) | 75.88 ± 2.87 | 76.38 ± 2.94 |

- ANOVA: p < 0.001 for both directions at 6 months
- Groups 3 and 4: significantly superior to Groups 1 and 2 (p = 0.001 to p < 0.001)
- Group 3 vs Group 4: no significant difference (buccal p = 0.546; mesial p = 0.749)
- Group 1 vs Group 2: no significant difference (buccal p = 0.732; mesial p = 0.535)
- Groups 3 and 4 surpassed ISQ 70 threshold (Glauser 2004 criterion for favorable stability)
- Groups 3 and 4 showed lower coefficient of variation (more consistent outcomes)
- No implant failures, membrane perforations, or postoperative infections.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short follow-up (6 months only)
- No CBCT or histological analysis — ISQ reflects mechanical stability, not actual bone volume
- Single center, single surgeon
- Relatively small sample per group (n=16)
- Future studies: larger cohorts, extended follow-up, radiographic and histological assessment

## 7. Glossary

- **i-PRF** (injectable Platelet-Rich Fibrin): Low-speed centrifugation product (60 g / 700 rpm / 3 min); yellow liquid layer above buffy coat; contains PDGF, TGF-β1, VEGF in flowable fibrin matrix
- **ISQ** (Implant Stability Quotient): RFA-derived value 0–100; ≥70 associated with favorable stability and early loading suitability
- **RFA** (Resonance Frequency Analysis): Non-invasive technique measuring implant-bone interface stiffness via SmartPeg vibration frequency
- **CAS Kit** (Crestal Approach Sinus Kit): Osstem system for indirect crestal sinus augmentation with stopper-controlled drills
- **NovaBone**: Bioactive calcium-phosphosilicate synthetic bone graft (alloplastic putty)
- **Indirect sinus augmentation**: Crestal (transalveolar) approach to sinus floor elevation, lower morbidity than lateral window
- **Residual bone height (RBH)**: Bone available between alveolar crest and sinus floor; 4–6 mm is borderline/challenging range
