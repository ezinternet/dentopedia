## One-line Summary
교정적 치아이동(OTM) 기전 동물 연구 critical review (JCM 2021): 압박측 파골세포생성·인장측 골형성; RANKL·TNF-α·DKK1·sclerostin·TGF-β·BMP 신호; 형질전환 동물 모델로 인과관계 규명.

- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Medicine 2021, 10, 1733

- **Institution**: University of Pennsylvania, School of Dental Medicine

## 2. Key Contributions
- Summarizes transgenic animal model studies to establish cause-and-effect in OTM cellular/molecular mechanisms
- OTM phases on compression side: (1) PDL compression 4–7 days → (2) hyalinization/cell death 7–14 days → (3) secondary direct bone resorption
- Tension side: PDL stretching → osteoblastic proliferation → osteoid deposition and mineralization
- Key cell types: PDL fibroblasts, mesenchymal stem cells, osteoblasts, osteocytes, osteoclasts
- Key intercellular signals: RANKL, TNF-α, DKK1, sclerostin, TGF-β, BMPs
- Mechanosensing: cilia and ion channels transduce mechanical force → biochemical signals → osteoclast or osteoblast differentiation
- Sterile inflammation (pathogen-free): TNF, IL-1, IL-6, prostaglandins, MMPs released within hours of force application

## 3. Methodology
- Critical review of transgenic animal model studies
- Focus on cause-and-effect relationships not achievable in human GCF or in vitro studies
- Key transgenic models: conditional knockouts of RANKL, OPG, DKK1, sclerostin, TGF-β, BMPs in PDL or osteocyte-specific contexts

## 4. Key Results
| Signal/Factor | Role in OTM |
|---|---|
| RANKL (up-regulated by compression) | Drives osteoclastogenesis → bone resorption |
| TNF-α | Induces RANKL expression; pro-inflammatory; synergistic osteoclastogenesis |
| DKK1 (Wnt inhibitor) | Compression-side: inhibits Wnt → reduces bone formation |
| Sclerostin (osteocyte-derived) | Inhibits Wnt signaling → regulates bone formation |
| TGF-β | Couples resorption to formation; PDL homeostasis |
| BMPs | Tension-side osteogenesis; Runx2 upregulation |
| Focal adhesion kinase (FAK) | Mechanosensor in PDL cells → prostaglandin secretion |

- Runx2, osteocalcin, osteopontin significantly up-regulated by tension forces
- Transgenic animals (knockouts) confirm specific molecular roles not possible in correlational studies

## 5. Limitations
- Primarily animal-based; human translation not directly established
- Rapid technology evolution in transgenic models
- PDL cell responses may vary by animal species
