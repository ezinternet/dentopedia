## Why Ingested
TMD 및 BMS 약물치료 근거를 망라한 최초의 network meta-analysis 기반 health technology assessment. [[tmj/tmj-management-overview]] 에서 참조할 약물치료 evidence base 제공; BMS 약물치료([[oral-medicine/]]) 및 TMD-근육·관절 통증 관리 지침에 직접 연결.

## One-line Summary
SR+NMA (n=57 RCTs, 2,033 patients): NSAIDs/corticosteroid/hyaluronate for TMD-joint; cyclobenzaprine for TMD-muscle; clonazepam and capsaicin for BMS — all with limited GRADE evidence.

## 한줄요약
SR+NMA (57 RCT, 2,033명): TMD-관절에 NSAIDs/코르티코스테로이드/히알루론산; TMD-근육에 시클로벤자프린; BMS에 클로나제팜·캅사이신 — 전반적으로 GRADE 근거 제한적.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Oral Rehabilitation 44(10):800–826 (2017)
- **Study type**: SR + network meta-analysis (health technology assessment)
- **Search**: PubMed, Cochrane Library, EMBASE to 1 March 2017; 1552 articles screened
- **Included**: 57 studies → 41 after risk-of-bias assessment
- **Subgroups**: TMD-joint (15 studies, n=790), TMD-muscle (9 studies, n=375), BMS (17 studies, n=868)
- **NMA subsets**: TMD-muscle (n=8 studies), BMS (n=5 studies)

## 2. Key Contributions
- **TMD-joint**: NSAIDs and intra-articular corticosteroid/hyaluronate injections shown effective (narrative synthesis)
- **TMD-muscle NMA**: cyclobenzaprine (muscle relaxant) has positive treatment effect
- **BMS NMA**: clonazepam and capsaicin reduce pain intensity
- GRADE evidence limited overall — few studies per drug class, heterogeneous populations
- First network meta-analysis comparing multiple pharmacological agents for orofacial pain

## 3. Methodology
- Swedish Agency for Health Technology Assessment (SBU) framework + GRADE
- Network meta-analysis (random effects) for TMD-muscle and BMS subgroups (≥3 RCTs per condition)
- Primary outcome: pain intensity reduction post-treatment
- Risk of bias: Cochrane tool; only low/medium risk studies retained for NMA

## 4. Key Results

| Subgroup | Effective treatment | Evidence quality |
|---|---|---|
| TMD-joint | NSAIDs (systemic) | Moderate (GRADE) |
| TMD-joint | Corticosteroid injection (IA) | Low |
| TMD-joint | Hyaluronate injection (IA) | Low |
| TMD-muscle | Cyclobenzaprine (muscle relaxant) | Low |
| BMS | Clonazepam (topical/systemic) | Low |
| BMS | Capsaicin (topical) | Low |

- Network meta-analysis (TMD-muscle): cyclobenzaprine ranked highest; other agents (diazepam, placebo) not significantly superior
- Network meta-analysis (BMS): clonazepam > capsaicin > placebo; alpha-lipoic acid, clonazepam systemic also shown effective in individual studies

## 5. Limitations
- Small number of studies per drug category
- Heterogeneous diagnostic criteria across studies (pre-DC/TMD era for many)
- Publication bias possible (positive results over-represented)
- Short follow-up (most studies ≤3 months)
- Limited generalizability: most studies on chronic pain ≥3 months

## 6. Clinical Implication
TMD-관절통: 1차 NSAIDs (이부프로펜 600mg tid), 반응 없으면 히알루론산 또는 코르티코스테로이드 관절강 내 주사 고려. TMD-근육통: 시클로벤자프린 단기 사용 (주의: 진정 부작용). BMS: 클로나제팜 국소 rinse 또는 전신 저용량 효과적; 캅사이신 topical 병행 가능.

## 7. Glossary
- **NMA**: Network meta-analysis — 직접·간접 비교를 통합해 여러 치료 간 상대적 효과 추정
- **BMS**: Burning mouth syndrome (구강작열감증후군)
- **TMD-joint**: 관절 위주 악관절장애 (골관절염, 관절원판변위 등)
- **TMD-muscle**: 근육 위주 악관절장애 (근육통, 근막통)
- **Cyclobenzaprine**: 근이완제 (근육 TMD용)
- **Clonazepam**: 벤조디아제핀 계열; BMS에 국소/전신 투여
- **GRADE**: 근거 질 평가 체계 (high/moderate/low/very low)
