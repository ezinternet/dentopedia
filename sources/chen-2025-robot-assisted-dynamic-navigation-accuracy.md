## Why Ingested

[[digital-workflow/yu-2025-autonomous-robotic-versus-dynamic-navigation]], [[digital-workflow/wei-2025-autonomous-robotic-surgery-dynamic-navigation]]와 함께 robotic vs dynamic navigation 정확도 근거군을 형성. 본 연구는 semi-autonomous(sa-RASS) 로봇이라는 변형을 비교해 robotic 카테고리 내부 이질성을 보강.

## One-line Summary
Retrospective clinical study (57 implants) — semi-autonomous robot (sa-RASS) gave lower platform and apex deviation than dynamic navigation, with comparable angular deviation.

## 한줄요약
후향 임상연구(임플란트 57개): 반자율로봇(sa-RASS)이 동적내비게이션(DNS)보다 플랫폼·첨부 편차는 낮고 각도 편차는 동등.

## 1. Document Information
- Clinical Oral Implants Research 2025;36:725–735. Accepted 2 Feb 2025.
- Funding: Zhejiang Provincial Key R&D Program; Major Sci-Tech Project of Zhejiang Province.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Adds a semi-autonomous robot variant (haptic-constrained master-slave) to the robotic-vs-navigation evidence.
- Platform and apex deviation favored sa-RASS; angular deviation not significantly different.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Retrospective chart review, Jan–Dec 2023, partially edentulous patients.
- 57 patients/57 implants: sa-RASS 29, DNS 28. CBCT-based platform, apex, angular deviation. KS test, t/Mann-Whitney.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Platform: 0.91 ± 0.46 (sa-RASS) vs 1.26 ± 0.51 mm (DNS), p < 0.001.
- Apex: 1.06 ± 0.60 vs 1.51 ± 0.56 mm, p < 0.001.
- Angular: 3.07° ± 1.66° vs 3.71° ± 1.64°, p > 0.05 (NS).
- Region and implant length did not affect accuracy.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Retrospective, single center, small n, single-tooth focus. Semi-autonomous robot still operator-dependent; angular benefit not demonstrated.

## 6. Related Work
- CAIS accuracy benchmarks (Tahmaseb 2018); autonomous robot clinical series.

## 7. Glossary
- sa-RASS: semi-autonomous robotic-assisted surgery system. DNS: dynamic navigation system.
