## One-line Summary

Review (J Dent 2025; 4-database search, 154 publications) of cold atmospheric pressure plasma (CAP) in prosthodontics across three applications: dentin bonding (enhanced resin monomer penetration/polymerization), antibacterial action (ROS/RNS inactivating bacteria and biofilm), and implant surface modification (improved surface properties, osseointegration, lower infection, soft-tissue healing).

## 한줄요약

리뷰(J Dent 2025; 4개 DB, 154편): 저온대기압플라즈마(CAP)의 보철치과 응용을 ① 상아질 접착(레진 단량체 침투·중합↑) ② 항균(ROS/RNS로 세균·바이오필름 불활성화) ③ 임플란트 표면개질(표면물성·골유착↑·감염↓·연조직치유↑) 세 축으로 정리.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Four-database search; two-reviewer screening; 154 publications included (original research; reviews/case reports/editorials excluded).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Dentin bonding: CAP enhances resin monomer penetration and polymerization.
- Antibacterial: ROS/RNS effectively inactivate oral bacteria and biofilm, lowering drug-resistance likelihood.
- Implant surface: CAP may improve surface properties, promote osseointegration, lower infection risk, promote soft-tissue healing.
- (Review — no pooled quantitative estimates.)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Narrative/scoping synthesis (no meta-analysis); clinical protocols unsettled.
