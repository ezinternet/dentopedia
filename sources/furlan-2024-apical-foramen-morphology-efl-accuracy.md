## One-line Summary
In vitro (30 premolars): RootZX II, Raypex 6, EPex Pro, CanalPro all accurate across foramen morphologies; divergent apical foramen reduces accuracy of Raypex 6 and CanalPro at 0.0mm but not at −1.0mm.

## 1. Document Information
Bauru Dental School, USP Brazil + Federal University of Ceará. Published in European Journal of Dentistry 2024. In vitro study using alginate model with 3 foramen morphology groups: fully formed, immature parallel walls, immature divergent walls.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First study to compare 4 EFLs across different foramen morphology types
- Divergent apical foramen: negative influence on RAY and CNP at 0.0mm penetration
- RootZX II and EPex Pro: no interference from foramen morphology (p>0.05)
- Apical penetration depth (0.0 vs −1.0mm): no significant influence on accuracy
- EPex Pro and CanalPro are newer analogues of RootZX II and Raypex 6 respectively

## 3. Methodology
- 30 single-rooted human mandibular premolars, fully formed apices
- AF standardized to 250 µm; RCL measured under 16× magnification + digital caliper
- 3 sequential foramen modifications: RCL1 (normal), RCL2 (cross-sectioned 3mm), RCL3 (retropreparation 4mm = immature divergent)
- EFLs: Root ZX II (RZX), Raypex 6 (RAY), EPex Pro (EPEX), CanalPro (CNP)
- Measurements at 0.0mm (AF) and −1.0mm (1mm short of AF)
- Statistics: Shapiro-Wilk, Kruskal-Wallis, Dunn test; p<0.05

## 4. Key Results
- All 4 EFLs generally accurate in fully formed foramen
- **Divergent AF at 0.0mm**: EPEX vs RAY significant difference (p<0.05); RAY and CNP lower accuracy
- **RZX and EPEX**: no significant morphology effect (p>0.05)
- **Apical penetration level**: did not significantly influence accuracy across devices
- Null hypothesis partially rejected: divergent morphology does affect some devices at foraminal level

## 5. Limitations
- In vitro / alginate model — no periodontal ligament, blood, or tissue fluid
- n=30; single-rooted premolars only
- Immature morphology simulated by cross-sectioning/retropreparation (artificial)

## 6. Related Work
- Versiani 2022 — in vivo micro-CT EAL accuracy (RootZX II vs Wirele-X)
- Pisano 2024 — umbrella review on EAL accuracy across generations
- Shacham 2020 — EAL accuracy with wide apical foramen (file-size matching)

## 7. Glossary
- **EFL**: Electronic Foramen Locator (same as EAL)
- **AF**: Apical Foramen
- **RCL**: Root Canal Length
- **Fully formed foramen**: Normal, mature apex
- **Immature divergent**: Open apex simulated by retropreparation
- **RZX**: Root ZX II; **RAY**: Raypex 6; **EPEX**: EPex Pro; **CNP**: CanalPro
