## Why Ingested

이 endotoxin 배치에서 disinfection *protocol* 축을 담당한다. [[endodontics/irrigation/boutsioukis-2022-irrigants-irrigation-methods-status-directions]]가 정리한 irrigation 활성화 방법(PUI 등)이 세균뿐 아니라 LTA·LPS 같은 virulence factor 제거에 얼마나 효과적인지를 dual-species 모델로 직접 비교한 첫 연구로, 배치의 [[endodontics/irrigation/bedran-2020-calcium-hydroxide-endotoxin-reduction-sr-ma]](CH가 endotoxin 완전 제거 불가)와 짝을 이뤄 "어느 disinfection 단계도 endotoxin을 완전 제거 못 한다"는 thesis를 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In-vitro dual-species (*E. faecalis* + GFP *E. coli*) biofilm model in 70 single-rooted premolars: PUI, XP-endo Finisher, and GentleWave all significantly reduced bacteria, LTA, and LPS, with GentleWave + minimally invasive technique the most effective; XP-EF ≈ PUI; none fully eliminated LTA/LPS.

## 한줄요약

70개 단근 소구치, dual-species(*E. faecalis* + GFP *E. coli*) 바이오필름 in-vitro 모델: PUI·XP-endo Finisher·GentleWave 모두 세균·LTA·LPS를 유의하게 감소시켰고 GentleWave+최소침습기법이 가장 효과적이었으며(XP-EF ≈ PUI), 어느 프로토콜도 LTA/LPS를 완전 제거하지 못했다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Title**: Comparison of conventional and contemporary root canal disinfection protocols against bacteria, lipoteichoic acid (LTA), and lipopolysaccharide (LPS)
- **Authors**: Theeb A. Alquria, ... Frederico C. Martinho (University of Maryland; UNESP Brazil; et al.)
- **Journal**: Scientific Reports 2023;13:1206
- **DOI**: 10.1038/s41598-022-26855-y
- **Study type**: In-vitro (ex-vivo human premolar dual-species biofilm model)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First study to compare XP-endo Finisher (XP-EF), GentleWave system (GWS), and passive ultrasonic irrigation (PUI) simultaneously against bacteria, LTA, *and* LPS.
- Introduces a novel **dual-species "Live/Live" biofilm model** (*E. faecalis* stained blue + GFP *E. coli* green) enabling simultaneous quantification of both major virulence factors (LTA from G+, LPS from G−).
- Uses cryogenic grinding to quantify intraradicular (intratubular) residual infection, not just main-canal paper-point samples.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 70 single-rooted mandibular premolars: 14 negative control, 56 infected with 3-week *E. faecalis* + GFP *E. coli* biofilm (14 positive control, 42 experimental).
- Three protocols (n=14 each): **GWS + MIT** (minimally invasive technique), **PUI + CIT** (conventional instrumentation), **XP-EF + CIT**.
- Sampling: s1 (before, paper point), s2 (after disinfection, paper point), s3 (after cryogenic grinding = intraradicular residual).
- Outcomes: CFU counts; LTA by ELISA; LPS by LAL (Limulus amebocyte lysate) assay.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- All three protocols significantly reduced bacteria, LTA, and LPS at s2 (p<0.05).
- **XP-EF was as effective as PUI** (p>0.05).
- **GWS + MIT was the most effective** against bacteria, LTA, and LPS (p<0.05), and the most effective for intraradicular (s3) disinfection.
- LTA and LPS remained detectable in 100% of samples at s2 and s3 — no protocol achieved complete elimination.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro ex-vivo model; *E. coli* not a typical endodontic species (used as LPS gold standard).
- No clinical periapical healing outcome; single 3-week biofilm age.
- Cost/availability of GentleWave limits direct clinical generalizability.

## 6. Related Work

- Pairs with calcium hydroxide endotoxin reviews ([[endodontics/irrigation/bedran-2020-calcium-hydroxide-endotoxin-reduction-sr-ma]]) and the SR-of-SRs overview ([[endodontics/irrigation/ahmad-2022-endotoxins-endodontic-infections-overview-sr]]) supporting incomplete endotoxin clearance.
- Extends irrigation-activation literature ([[endodontics/irrigation/boutsioukis-2022-irrigants-irrigation-methods-status-directions]]) into virulence-factor (LTA/LPS) outcomes.

## 7. Glossary

- **LTA / LPS**: lipoteichoic acid (G+) and lipopolysaccharide (G−) — two major endodontic virulence factors; TLR-2 / TLR-4 ligands respectively.
- **PUI**: passive ultrasonic irrigation.
- **XP-EF**: XP-endo Finisher, a non-tapered shape-memory (MaxWire) finishing file.
- **GWS / MIT**: GentleWave multisonic system / minimally invasive technique.
- **LAL assay**: Limulus amebocyte lysate assay for LPS quantification.
