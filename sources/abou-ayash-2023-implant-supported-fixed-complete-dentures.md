## Why Ingested

This SR+MA directly quantifies patient-reported outcome (PRO) treatment effects for both implant-supported fixed complete dentures (IFCDs) and implant overdentures (IODs), filling a gap in [[complete-denture/srinivasan-2021-cad-cam-removable-complete-dentures]] which focuses on CAD/CAM fabrication rather than prosthesis-type PRO comparisons. Key evidence base for advising edentulous patients on fixed vs removable implant options.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (28 studies, n=1457) found large treatment-effect sizes for both IFCDs (g=1.68) and IODs (g=1.26) on patient-reported outcomes, with no significant difference between prosthesis types except for denture stability favoring IFCDs.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰 및 메타분석(28편, n=1457): 임플란트 지지 고정 총의치(IFCD)와 임플란트 피개의치(IOD) 모두 환자보고결과에 대한 큰 치료 효과 크기를 보였으며(g=1.68 vs 1.26), 의치 안정성에서만 IFCD가 유의하게 우수했다.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Quantified large-magnitude treatment effects for both IFCDs (Hedges' g = 1.68) and IODs (g = 1.26) on dental patient-reported outcomes (dPROs) in edentulous patients
2. Demonstrated no statistically significant overall difference between IFCD and IOD in dPROs (p = .165)
3. Identified denture stability as the only dPRO dimension where IFCDs significantly outperform IODs (ES difference: 2.37; p = .032)
4. Showed that mandibular IODs on two implants produce moderately higher dPROs than single-implant IODs (ES difference: 0.73; p < .001)
5. Demonstrated that follow-up time had no significant moderating effect on treatment outcomes

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**PICO framework**:
- Population: Fully edentulous patients
- Intervention: IFCDs (≥4 implants)
- Comparison: IODs (1–2+ implants, various attachments)
- Outcome: Pre- and post-treatment dPROs using validated measures

**Inclusion criteria**:
- Prospective clinical investigations
- Pre- and post-treatment dPRO reporting using validated instruments
- Completely edentulous participants
- Minimum sample size ≥10 per arm
- Mean follow-up ≥1 year from restoration delivery
- English-language articles

**Outcome measures used across studies**:
- Oral Health Impact Profile (OHIP) — multiple versions (OHIP-49, OHIP-20, OHIP-EDENT)
- Visual Analogue Scales (VAS) — domain-specific satisfaction items

**Statistical approach**:
- Hedges' g effect size calculation with 95% CIs
- Random-effects meta-analyses
- Subgroup analyses by: prosthesis type, attachment system (balls vs bars), number of implants, follow-up duration
- Heterogeneity assessment (I²)

**Risk of bias**: Assessed using Cochrane Risk of Bias tool for RCTs and ROBINS-I for non-randomized prospective studies

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Prosthesis Type | Hedges' g | 95% CI | Magnitude |
|---|---|---|---|
| IFCD overall | 1.68 | 1.15–2.20 | Large |
| IOD overall | 1.26 | 0.99–1.52 | Large |
| IFCD vs IOD (difference) | NS | — | p = .165 |

**Denture stability subgroup** (only significant domain difference):
- IFCD vs IOD ES difference: 2.37 (95% CI: 0.21–4.54; p = .032)

**IOD implant number subgroup** (mandibular):
- 2 implants vs 1 implant ES difference: 0.73 (95% CI: 0.34–1.12; p < .001)

**Attachment type (IODs)**:
- Balls vs bars: No significant difference in overall dPROs

**Follow-up duration**:
- Regression analysis: Follow-up time not a significant moderator

**Study inclusion**: 28 studies from 1608 initially identified records; n = 1457 patients total

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

1. High heterogeneity across included studies due to variation in dPROMs, follow-up periods, implant number, and patient characteristics
2. No control groups with conventional complete dentures in many included studies (pre-post design), limiting causal inference
3. Most studies lacked reporting of potentially confounding sociodemographic factors
4. Maxillary IOD data were sparse; conclusions primarily apply to mandibular reconstructions
5. Exclusion of non-English publications may introduce language bias
6. Future RCTs needed with standardized dPROMs, longer follow-up, and head-to-head IFCD vs IOD comparison with pre-treatment baseline

## 7. Glossary

- **IFCD** (Implant-Supported Fixed Complete Denture, 임플란트 지지 고정 총의치): Non-removable implant prosthesis replacing complete arch; requires ≥4 implants per jaw
- **IOD** (Implant Overdenture, 임플란트 피개의치): Removable prosthesis retained/supported by implants; can use 1–4+ implants
- **dPRO** (Dental Patient-Reported Outcome, 치과 환자보고결과): Patient-centered outcome reflecting self-perceived oral health, function, or satisfaction
- **dPROM** (Dental Patient-Reported Outcome Measure, 치과 환자보고결과 측정도구): Validated instrument capturing dPROs (e.g., OHIP)
- **OHIP** (Oral Health Impact Profile, 구강건강영향지수): Validated multi-domain questionnaire measuring OHRQoL
- **OHRQoL** (Oral Health-Related Quality of Life, 구강건강관련 삶의 질): Patient-perceived impact of oral health on daily functioning
- **Hedges' g**: Standardized effect size corrected for small-sample bias; g ≥ 0.8 = large effect
- **PRISMA**: Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses — reporting guideline
