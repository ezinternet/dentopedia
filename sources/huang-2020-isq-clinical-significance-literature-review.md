## One-line Summary
Narrative review identifying 17 ISQ-influencing factors; only 6 have clinically predictive power; only 2 have well-documented scientific basis (local bone quality and implant macro-design).

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Journal of Oral Biology and Craniofacial Research 10(4):629-638 (2020)
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jobcr.2020.07.004
- Design: Literature review (systematic + exhaustive database search)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Comprehensive taxonomy of 17 ISQ-influencing factors
- Critical filtering: only 6 clinically predictive; only 2 well-documented
- Framework for interpreting ISQ values in clinical decision-making

## 3. Methodology
- Exhaustive search of major databases
- Reports classified: experimental (preclinical) vs clinical
- 17 factors identified by 13 basic factors + 4 bone quality subfactors

## 4. Key Results
- 17 total factors influence ISQ: include bone quality, implant design, site preparation, resonance frequency device, measurement direction, SmartPeg type, etc.
- 6 factors with clinically predictive power: bone quality, macro-design, insertion torque, site preparation, healing time, measurement direction
- 2 well-documented: local bone quality (dominant), implant macro-design (secondary)
- ISQ alone insufficient without clinical context — must interpret alongside bone type and site

## 5. Limitations
- Narrative/literature review (not SR+MA — cannot quantify effect sizes)
- Factor weighting is qualitative
- Technology (devices, implant systems) evolving faster than review cadence

## 6. Related Work
- Sennerby 2008: RFA principles (foundational)
- Stoilov 2023: experimental confirmation of macro-design and bone quality effects
- Chatvaratthana 2017: cortical bone thickness as key ISQ predictor (CBCT)
