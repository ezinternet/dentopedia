## One-line Summary
J Adv Prosthodont 2021;13:237: different cleaning techniques for CAD/CAM zirconia after contamination — tested multiple protocols with dual-cure resin cement; cleaning improves µSBS significantly.

## 1. Key Results
- CAD/CAM Ivoclar Vivadent zirconia discs, 50 specimens
- Multiple cleaning techniques compared with µSBS endpoint
- Dual-cure resin cement used
- Cleaning restored or improved µSBS over contaminated control
