## Why Ingested

균열치 증후군 (Cracked Tooth Syndrome, CTS) 치료법의 **umbrella review (SR of SRs)** — 분야 최고 evidence-level. [[cracked-tooth/jkda-2024-62-1-003·004·005]] 진단·치료 review와 결합해 cracked-tooth cluster를 완성 (한국 review + 국제 SR).

## One-line Summary
Umbrella review (Manipal College of Dental Sciences, JCDE 2026): 4 SR (30 primary studies, 45 entries) 통합 — **endodontic therapy + full cuspal coverage가 최고 prognosis**. PROSPERO registered, AMSTAR-2 평가, CCA 17% (높은 overlap).

## 1. Document Information
저자: Manipal College of Dental Sciences (인도). PROSPERO CRD420250648720. PRISMA 2020. JCDE = Journal of Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 첫 CTS umbrella review — SR-level evidence 종합
- AMSTAR-2 quality 평가
- CCA (Corrected Covered Area) overlap 분석 → 17% 높은 overlap (primary study 부족)
- **Endo + full cuspal coverage**가 최고 prognosis 결론

## 3. Methodology
- Umbrella review (SR of SRs)
- DB: PubMed, Cochrane, Scopus, EMBASE (until 2025-06-15)
- 2 reviewers independent screening
- AMSTAR-2 methodological quality
- Citation matrix + CCA for overlap

## 4. Key Results
- 10 SR identified, 4 met criteria
- 30 primary studies (45 entries)
- CCA 17% — 높은 overlap
- **Endodontic therapy 후 full cuspal coverage** 최고 prognosis
- Endo 없이 crown only 또는 endo 없이 partial coverage는 prognosis 열등

## 5. Limitations
- 4 SR — 작은 evidence base
- 17% CCA = primary study 중복 사용
- Primary RCT 부족 (분야 일반)
