숙주-미생물 상호작용을 재현하는 organotypic gingiva 모델 — 항균제·바이오필름 연구의 in vitro 플랫폼. [[periodontics/cyris-2024-guided-biofilm-therapy-versus-conventional]] 등 임상 바이오필름 관리 근거의 기초기전 layer를 보강.

## One-line Summary
In vitro methods paper — an organotypic reconstructed human gingiva model colonized with saliva-derived commensal vs pathogenic multispecies biofilms produces distinct host inflammatory responses, serving as an antimicrobial screening platform.

## 한줄요약
In vitro 방법론 논문: 재구성 인간 치은 모델에 타액유래 공생/병원성 다종 바이오필름을 정착시켜 서로 다른 숙주 염증반응을 유도 — 항균제 스크리닝 플랫폼.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dental Research 2018;97(2):201–208. ACTA Amsterdam. Research Reports: Biological.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Develops organotypic gingiva (epithelium + fibroblast-populated lamina propria) host-microbiome model.
- Commensal vs pathogenic saliva-derived biofilms elicit distinct host cytokine responses.
- Provides a physiologically relevant antimicrobial/drug-discovery screening tool.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- In vitro reconstructed human gingiva (RHG) on collagen hydrogel.
- Saliva-derived multispecies commensal and pathogenic biofilms co-cultured; host inflammatory readouts compared.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pathogenic biofilms drive a stronger/distinct inflammatory (cytokine) response vs commensal biofilms.
- Model reproduces aspects of in vivo host-microbiome interaction better than single-species systems.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; lacks immune-cell/vascular components and salivary flow; biofilm composition simplified vs in vivo. Validation against clinical responses needed.

## 6. Related Work
- Multispecies biofilm models (Guggenheim, Peyyala); organotypic mucosa models.

## 7. Glossary
- RHG: reconstructed human gingiva. Organotypic: 3D tissue-equivalent culture. Commensal/pathogenic biofilm: health- vs disease-associated microbial community.
