## Why Ingested

심미 치과(라미네이트·스마일디자인·전치부 보철) 환자 중 신체이형장애(Body Dysmorphic Disorder, BDD) 환자는 술 후 불만족·반복 시술·분쟁으로 이어진다. 이 SR+MA는 가장 유사한 심미수술군(rhinoplasty)에서 BDD 유병률을 정량화해, 심미 진료 전 심리 스크리닝 필요성의 근거가 된다. 방금 ingest한 의료분쟁 클러스터 [[complaint-management/park-2016-analysis-of-malpractice-claims-associated]]·[[complaint-management/kwon-2017-analysis-of-the-legal-effect]]의 상류(분쟁 유발 환자 요인)를 설명하는 patient-selection 근거.

## One-line Summary
Systematic review + meta-analysis of 18 studies (n=2,132) finding a pooled body dysmorphic disorder prevalence of 32.7% (95% CI 23.6–41.7) among rhinoplasty candidates, varying by diagnostic tool and region.

## 한줄요약
연구 18편(n=2,132) SR+MA — 코성형 지원자의 신체이형장애(BDD) 통합 유병률 32.7%(95% CI 23.6–41.7); 진단도구·지역에 따라 편차.

## 1. Document Information
- Health Science Reports 2023;6:e1495. Narrative Review labeled, but methodologically a systematic review + meta-analysis (PRISMA/MOOSE).
- Shiraz University of Medical Sciences, Iran. Open Access (CC BY-NC-ND).
- Received 18 May 2023; accepted 2 Aug 2023.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First meta-analytic pooled BDD prevalence estimate in rhinoplasty candidates (32.7%).
- Quantifies the diagnostic-tool effect: BDD-YBOCS yields higher prevalence than questionnaire screening.
- Subgroup/meta-regression mapping of heterogeneity sources (region, sample size, tool, quality).
- Reinforces preoperative psychological screening for esthetic-procedure candidates.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Databases: Web of Science, PubMed, Cochrane, ProQuest, Research Square, Scopus, Google Scholar (to Aug 2022).
- PRISMA + MOOSE; STATA v16; heterogeneity via I².
- 12,999 records screened → 18 studies (n=2,132) included.
- Subgroup analyses: study year, age, gender, WHO region, sociodemographic index, country, sample size, diagnostic tool. Meta-regression for heterogeneity.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pooled BDD prevalence: 32.7% (95% CI 23.6–41.7; I²=96.45%, very high heterogeneity).
- European region 42.8%; sample ≥100 → 40.2%; BDD-YBOCS tool → 47.3%; quality score ≥6 → 34.2%.
- Meta-regression: BDD-YBOCS vs BDD questionnaire β=0.23 (95% CI 0.04–0.43; p=0.022).
- Literature range across primary studies: 7%–53%.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Very high heterogeneity (I²≈96%) — pooled point estimate must be read cautiously.
- Rhinoplasty-specific; transfer to esthetic dentistry is by analogy of esthetic-motivation overlap, not direct measurement.
- Diagnostic-tool dependence means prevalence is partly an artifact of instrument choice.
- Mostly screening instruments, not full psychiatric diagnostic interviews.

## 6. Related Work
- BDD literature in cosmetic/plastic surgery populations (elevated vs general population).
- BDD-YBOCS and BDD questionnaire validation studies.

## 7. Glossary
- Body dysmorphic disorder (BDD): preoccupation with a minor or imagined physical defect causing significant distress/impairment.
- BDD-YBOCS: BDD modification of the Yale-Brown Obsessive Compulsive Scale, a structured BDD severity/screening tool.
- I²: statistic quantifying between-study heterogeneity in a meta-analysis.
