## Why Ingested

Robotic vs dynamic navigation 근거군에서 가장 높은 근거수준(RCT). [[digital-workflow/yu-2025-autonomous-robotic-versus-dynamic-navigation]] 전향 비교연구를 RCT로 확증하되, 동일 optical tracking 통제 하에서는 각도만 유의차라는 더 보수적 결과를 제시 — robotic 우위의 scope를 한정.

## One-line Summary
Open-label RCT (40 patients, single tooth) — autonomous robot lowered angular deviation (1.01° vs 1.78°) vs dynamic navigation, but coronal/apical deviation comparable; robot took longer.

## 한줄요약
공개라벨 RCT(환자 40명·단일치): 동일 추적장치 조건에서 자율로봇이 각도 편차만 유의하게 낮추고(1.01° vs 1.78°) 관상·첨부는 동등, 시술시간은 더 김.

## 1. Document Information
- Clinical Oral Implants Research 2025;36(11):1486–1497. First published 20 Aug 2025.
- Funding: Clinical Research Fund of Fourth Military Medical University.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First RCT controlling for the optical tracking system (same tracker both arms), isolating the robot vs hand-guided variable.
- Under that control, only angular deviation favored the robot; linear deviations were equivalent.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Prospective open-label RCT, two parallel arms. 40 single-tooth patients, AR n=20 vs DN n=20. Same optical tracking technology.
- Primary: angular, coronal, apical deviation. Secondary: surgical time, pain, edema, complications, early success.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Angular: AR 1.01° ± 0.65° vs DN 1.78° ± 1.15° (p < 0.01).
- Coronal and apical deviation: no significant difference.
- Surgical time: AR 21.52 ± 9.81 vs DN 14.27 ± 3.57 min (p < 0.01).
- Pain, edema, complications, early implant success: comparable.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small n, single tooth, open-label. Same-tracker design narrows robot advantage to angular control; generalization to complex cases limited.
