## Why Ingested

전방 상악 GBR 결과를 예측하는 골형태학적 변수(골오목 깊이·각도)를 정량화한 후향적 연구 — 술전 CBCT 기반 케이스 선택 도구를 제공하며, [[bone-regeneration/buser-2023-gbr-implant-35years-basic-principle-review]]에서 제시한 GBR 원칙을 임상 예측 모델로 구체화.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective study (n=28) in anterior maxilla showing bone concavity depth ≥1.03 mm and angulation <155.3° significantly reduce grafted bone resorption rate after simultaneous GBR with DBBM + collagen membrane.

## 한줄요약

전방 상악 지연 임플란트+GBR 28명 후향적 연구 — 골오목 깊이 ≥1.03 mm, 각도 <155.3°에서 이식재 흡수율이 유의하게 낮아 GBR 예후 예측 변수로 확인.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: International Dental Journal (Fudan/Zhongshan)
- **Volume/Issue**: 74: 102–109 (2024)
- **Publication date**: Available online 14 September 2023
- **Study type**: Retrospective study
- **Ethics**: Ethics Committee of Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University (B2020-102R2)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Identified two quantitative bone morphology predictors (BCD and BCA) for GBR outcome in anterior maxilla
- Established cut-off values: BCD ≥1.03 mm and BCA <155.30° → significantly lower resorption rate of grafted bone (RRGB)
- Mean RRGB at 6 months = 49.78% — approximately half of grafted volume resorbs regardless of material type or implant brand
- RRGB not correlated with gender, age, bone graft material type, implant angulation, or implant brand

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Single-center retrospective study (Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University)
- **Period**: September 2015 – August 2021
- **Subjects**: 28 patients with single missing maxillary anterior tooth ≥6 months, horizontal bone defect only (no vertical)
- **Procedure**: Delayed implant placement + simultaneous GBR (collagen membrane + DBBM; Bio-Oss + Bio-Gide, Geistlich)
- **Imaging**: CBCT at T0 (preop), T1 (immediate postop), T2 (6 months postop)
- **3D analysis**: Mimics 21.0 — 3D virtual model superimposition, manual sagittal slice segmentation (0.2 mm thickness)
- **Measured variables**:
  - RRGB = (volume at T1 − volume at T2) / volume at T1 × 100%
  - Labial bone width (LBW) at 0, 2, 4 mm apical to implant platform
  - Implant angulation (IA), max bone graft thickness (MBGT), bone graft volume (BGV)
  - Bone concavity depth (BCD) and bone concavity angulation (BCA) — morphological predictors
- **Statistics**: Pearson correlation, ANOVA, optimal binning method

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Variable | Correlation with RRGB | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| BCD | r = −0.872 | <0.001 |
| BCA | r = 0.686 | <0.001 |
| Gender, age, material, IA, MBGT, BGV, site, brand | Not significant | >0.05 |

- BCD ≥1.03 mm: significantly lower RRGB (p<0.001) — deeper concavity provides better "tent effect" space maintenance
- BCA <155.30°: significantly lower RRGB (p<0.001) — narrower angulation (more contained defect) retains graft better
- Labial bone width decreased significantly at 6 months (LBW0, LBW2, LBW4 all reduced)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample size (n=28), single center, single surgeon — limits generalizability
- Short follow-up (6 months); long-term volumetric stability not assessed
- No control group (unassisted healing)
- Only horizontal defects included; vertical/combined defects excluded
- DBBM + collagen membrane only — cannot generalize to other graft/membrane combinations

## 6. Related Work

- Terheyden classification — alveolar bone height vs. implant length
- Buser et al. 2023 — GBR 35-year review (framework reference)
- Geistlich Bio-Oss + Bio-Gide — standard collagen membrane + DBBM combination used in this study

## 7. Glossary

- **RRGB (Resorption Rate of Grafted Bone)**: Volume percentage of graft lost between immediate postop (T1) and 6-month (T2) CBCT
- **BCD (Bone Concavity Depth)**: Depth of labial bone concavity on sagittal CBCT section (mm)
- **BCA (Bone Concavity Angulation)**: Angular measurement of the labial bone concavity (degrees)
- **LBW (Labial Bone Width)**: Horizontal bone thickness on labial aspect at defined levels below implant platform
- **DBBM**: Deproteinized bovine bone mineral (Bio-Oss)
- **Delayed implant**: Implant placed ≥6 months after extraction
