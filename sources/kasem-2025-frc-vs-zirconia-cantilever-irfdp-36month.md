## 2. Key Contributions
- Overall survival 97.5%, success 96.6% at 36 months
- No statistically significant difference between zirconia and FRC (p>0.05)
- One zirconia OC fracture at connector (30mo), one debond (10mo), one endo complication in FRC
- Both lingual and occlusal coverage designs showed satisfactory results
- Conservative option for mandibular second premolar replacement

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
RCT (n=40 IR-FDPs, 32 patients) comparing monolithic zirconia (Z) vs fiber-reinforced composite (FRC) single-retainer cantilever IR-FDPs with lingual (LC) or occlusal (OC) coverage. 36-month follow-up. Survival 97.5%, success 96.6%. No significant clinical differences between Z and FRC (p>0.05). One OC-Z fractured at connector (30mo), one LC-Z debonded (10mo, re-bonded), one LC-FRC required endodontic treatment (12mo). Modified FDI criteria.
