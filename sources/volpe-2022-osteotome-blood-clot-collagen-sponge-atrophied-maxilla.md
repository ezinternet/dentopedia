## Why Ingested

Graftless OSFE의 장기 결과(5–13년)를 보고한 연구가 wiki에 부족. [[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/volpe-2022-osteotome-blood-clot-collagen-sponge-atrophied-maxilla]]는 collagen sponge만 사용한 no-graft approach의 가장 긴 추적 데이터 중 하나로, Sansupakorn 2024 RCT(1년) 및 Shahood 2024 비교의 장기 맥락 제공.

## One-line Summary
Retrospective (n=36 implants, 2006–2014): OSFE without graft using collagen sponge only achieves 97.2% survival and ISQ 73–76 at 5–13 years mean 8.5 yr follow-up.

## 한줄요약
후향적 연구(36임플란트, 2006–2014): 골이식 없이 collagen sponge만 사용한 OSFE에서 5–13년(평균 8.5년) 추적 생존율 97.2%, ISQ 73–76.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Dentistry 2022; Article ID 6594279
- **DOI**: 10.1155/2022/6594279
- **Study type**: Retrospective cohort
- **n**: 36 implants, 36 patients (1 implant/patient)
- **Implant system**: Neoss Ltd. (SBL surface)
- **Follow-up**: 5–13 years (mean 8.5±2.8 years prosthetic loading)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Graftless transcrestal OSFE (collagen sponge only) 최장 추적 데이터 중 하나
- ISQ 측정: placement(73.2±6.2) → abutment surgery 4–6mo (75.8±3.9)
- Vertical bone height (VBH) 방사선학적 추적: pre-op → 4–6mo → long-term

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Technique**: Transcrestal osteotome SFE, no bone graft, collagen sponge placed below sinus membrane
- **RBH eligibility**: ≥4mm below maxillary sinus floor
- **Implant**: Neoss (sandblasted surface), single tooth replacement
- **Healing**: Submerged 4–6 months, then single crown
- **Outcomes**: Survival/success, ISQ (Osstell), VBH on intraoral radiographs, MBL

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **Survival**: 35/36 (97.2%); 1 failure at 4 years prosthetic loading
- **Success**: 35 implants at mean 8.5±2.8 yr — no complications
- **ISQ**: 73.2±6.2 at placement → 75.8±3.9 at abutment (4–6mo)
- **VBH**: 5.9±1.4mm pre-op → 9.7±1.1mm at 4–6mo → 8.3±1.8mm at long-term follow-up
  - Net long-term bone gain vs pre-op: ~2.4mm retained
- **MBL**: within acceptable range (not numerically specified in abstract)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Retrospective single-center; no control group
- 36 implants = small n; single-operator study (Volpe)
- Radiographic measurement on 2D periapical (not CBCT)
- Collagen sponge brand/protocol variation not detailed

## 6. Related Work
- Sansupakorn 2024: OSFE with BCP vs no-graft (1-year RCT — BCP no benefit)
- Shahood 2024: Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF vs no-graft (1-year prospective)
- Lai 2008: RFA tracking in OSFE without graft (5-month prospective)
- Sennerby: co-author; Osstell ISQ methodology originator

## 7. Glossary
- **VBH** (Vertical Bone Height): 방사선 계측 수직 골 높이
- **Collagen sponge**: bovine collagen scaffold placed sub-membrane; provides provisional scaffold for blood clot organization (no osteoconductive/inductive graft)
- **MBL** (Marginal Bone Loss): 임플란트 주위 변연골 소실
