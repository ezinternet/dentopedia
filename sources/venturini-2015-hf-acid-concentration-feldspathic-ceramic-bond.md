## Why Ingested

HF 산 농도가 장석계 세라믹의 레진 접착 내구성에 미치는 영향을 정량적으로 비교한 in vitro 연구로, [[resin-bonding/dimitriadi-2019-silane-reactivity-lithium-disilicate-bond-strength]]의 표면 처리 프로토콜(HF 에칭 후 실란)에 대한 농도 선택 근거를 보강한다. 1%~10% HF 비교 데이터가 임상 프로토콜 수립에 필요하다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro RCT (n=40 blocks, Vita Mark II): HF ≥3% provides durable resin bond to feldspathic ceramic after 230-day aging + 12,000 thermocycles, while 1% HF shows significant bond strength degradation.

## 한줄요약

In vitro (n=40, Vita Mark II): HF 농도 3% 이상에서 장기 열순환 후에도 레진 접착력 유지; 1% HF는 접착력 유의하게 감소.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Compared 4 HF concentrations (1%, 3%, 5%, 10%) on Vita Mark II feldspathic ceramic bond durability
- Demonstrated that ≥3% HF provides stable resin adhesion after long-term aging
- 1% HF produced bond degradation after 230-day storage + 12,000 thermocycles
- Surface wettability (contact angle) decreased with increasing HF concentration (UC: 61.4°; HF10: 17.5°)
- All etched groups showed similar immediate bond strengths (14.2–15.7 MPa, p > 0.05)
- Morphological surface changes confirmed by AFM and SEM

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Design**: In vitro, randomized, controlled study.

**Material**: VitaBlocks Mark II (feldspathic CAD/CAM ceramic blocks), 12×10 mm specimens.

**Groups (n=5 for contact angle; n=10/group for μTBS)**:
- UC: Unconditioned control
- HF1: 1% HF, 60 s etching
- HF3: 3% HF, 60 s etching
- HF5: 5% HF, 60 s etching
- HF10: 10% HF, 60 s etching

**Protocol**: All etched groups → silane application → resin cement → sectioned to bar specimens. Two subgroups: non-aged (immediate) and aged (230 days storage + 12,000 thermocycles at 5°C/55°C).

**Testing**: Microtensile bond strength (μTBS). Surface characterization by atomic force microscopy (AFM) and scanning electron microscopy (SEM).

**Statistics**: One-way ANOVA, Tukey's post-hoc test.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Group | Contact Angle | Immediate μTBS (MPa) | Aged μTBS (MPa) |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| UC | 61.4° | — | — |
| HF1 | — | 14.5 | 10.2* |
| HF3 | — | 14.2 | ~14 (stable) |
| HF5 | — | 15.7 | ~15 (stable) |
| HF10 | 17.5° | ~15 | ~14 (stable) |

*Statistically significant decrease after aging (p < 0.05)

- Non-aged: all concentrations produced similar bond strengths (p > 0.05)
- After aging: only HF1 showed significant bond strength decrease
- Higher HF concentration = lower contact angle = better wettability

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract-level data only (publisher paywall; full methodology from abstract extraction)
- Single ceramic material tested (Vita Mark II); may not generalize to other feldspathic or pressed ceramics
- Simulated aging (thermocycling) may not fully replicate clinical intraoral conditions
- Silane type/brand not specified in abstract; could influence results

## 7. Glossary

- **HF (Hydrofluoric acid, 불화수소산)**: 세라믹 표면 처리 산; 유리상을 선택적으로 용해시켜 미세기계적 유지력 형성
- **μTBS (Microtensile Bond Strength, 마이크로인장접착강도)**: 접착 강도 측정 표준법; 소형 bar 시편에 인장력 적용
- **Contact angle (접촉각)**: 표면 친수성 지표; 낮을수록 wettability 우수
- **Thermocycling (열순환)**: 냉온 반복 노출로 구강 내 온도 변화 시뮬레이션
- **Vita Mark II**: Vita Zahnfabrik의 CAD/CAM용 장석계 세라믹 블록
- **AFM (Atomic Force Microscopy, 원자력현미경)**: 나노스케일 표면 형태 분석
