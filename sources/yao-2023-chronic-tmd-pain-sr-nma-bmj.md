## Why Ingested
[[tmj/bmj-2023-tmd-chronic-pain-management-guideline]] (076227) 기반이 된 SR+NMA (076226). 233 RCT·59 개입을 직접·간접 비교한 가장 크고 강력한 TMD 통증 치료 NMA. [[tmj/haggman-henrikson-2017-pharmacological-orofacial-pain-sr-ma]]의 약물 중심 NMA(2017)를 훨씬 광범위한 59 개입으로 확장.

## One-line Summary
SR+NMA (233 RCTs, n=8,713, 59 interventions, BMJ 2023): CBT+biofeedback (RD 36%), jaw mobilization (36%), manual trigger point therapy (32%) top 3 for chronic TMD pain; movement/coping-promoting therapies consistently best.

## 한줄요약
SR+NMA (233 RCT, 8,713명, 59 개입): CBT+바이오피드백·하악 가동화·수기 트리거포인트 치료가 만성 TMD 통증 TOP 3; 운동·대처 촉진 치료 일관 최선.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: BMJ 383:e076226 (2023)
- **Study type**: SR + network meta-analysis (PROSPERO CRD42021258567)
- **Databases**: MEDLINE, EMBASE, CINAHL, CENTRAL, SCOPUS (to May 2021 + Jan 2023 update)
- **Eligible**: 233 RCTs; **NMA-included**: 153 trials, n=8,713, 59 interventions
- **Companion paper**: BMJ 2023;383:e076227 (clinical practice guideline)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Largest TMD treatment NMA; 59 interventions in direct/indirect comparison
- High/moderate certainty evidence for 8 interventions
- "Coping + movement" therapies consistently superior
- Pharmacological interventions: low/very low certainty evidence
- Adverse events evidence: low/very low certainty across all interventions

## 3. Key Results (NMA — pain outcomes)

### High-to-moderate certainty evidence (top 8 interventions):
| Intervention | RD vs placebo | Certainty |
|---|---|---|
| CBT + biofeedback/relaxation | 36% (33–39%) | High-moderate |
| Therapist-assisted jaw mobilization | 36% (31–40%) | High-moderate |
| Manual trigger point therapy | 32% (29–34%) | High-moderate |
| CBT (alone) | 23–30% | Moderate |
| Supervised postural exercise | 23–30% | Moderate |
| Supervised jaw exercise + stretching | 23–30% | Moderate |
| Jaw exercise + stretching + MTrP therapy | 23–30% | Moderate |
| Usual care (home exercises, stretching, reassurance) | 23–30% | Moderate |

### Physical functioning (moderate certainty):
| Intervention | RD for MID (SF-36 PCS) |
|---|---|
| Supervised jaw exercise + stretching | 43% (33–51%) |
| Manipulation | 43% (25–56%) |
| Acupuncture | 42% (33–50%) |
| Supervised jaw exercise + mobilization | 36% (19–51%) |

## 4. Limitations
- Chronic TMD pain only (≥3 months) — acute TMD not covered
- Search limited to May 2021 + Jan 2023 update
- Adverse events poorly reported in most included trials

## 5. Clinical Implication
만성 TMD 통증 1차 치료 (2023 최고 근거):
1. **CBT + 바이오피드백/이완**: 가장 강한 근거 → 만성 통증·심리 동반 시
2. **치료사 보조 하악 가동화**: 동일 효과, 관절 위주 TMD에 적합
3. **수기 트리거포인트 치료**: 근육 위주 TMD에
4. **일상적 치료 (home exercise + reassurance)**: 단순 관리로도 moderate certainty 효과

약물 치료는 low certainty → [[haggman-henrikson-2017-pharmacological-orofacial-pain-sr-ma]] 참조.

## 6. Glossary
- **RD (Risk Difference)**: 치료군-대조군 MID 달성 차이 (%)
- **MID**: Minimally important difference (최소 임상적 의미 차이) — 1 cm on 10 cm VAS
- **CBT**: Cognitive behavioural therapy (인지행동치료)
- **MTrP**: Manual trigger point therapy (수기 트리거포인트 치료)
- **NMA**: Network meta-analysis (네트워크 메타분석)
