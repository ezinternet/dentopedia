## Why Ingested

기존 wiki의 골내결손/치주재생 근거는 술식·이식재 중심이고 (예: [[periodontics/stavropoulos-2021-periodontal-regeneration-intrabony-nma]]), 재생을 구동하는 osteoinductive factor(BMP-2 대안)의 분자생물학적 근거는 비어 있었다. 본 in-vitro PDLSC + in-vivo rat alveolar bone defect 연구는 천연 oxysterol (SS) 조합이 LXR↔Hedgehog cross-talk를 통해 PDLSC osteogenesis와 발치와 골재생을 BMP-2 수준으로 유도함을 보여, BMP-2 부작용(이소성 골형성·염증·발암 우려) 없는 osteoinductive 분자 후보로 [[bone-biology/tokavanich-2025-control-alveolar-bone-development]]·[[bone-regeneration/suarez-lopez-del-amo-2022-efficacy-biologics-alveolar-ridge]]의 biologics 논의를 분자기전 측면에서 보강한다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Experimental — in-vitro (human PDLSC) + in-vivo (rat alveolar bone defect)
- **Journal**: Stem Cell Research & Therapy 2017;8:276
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s13287-017-0725-9
- **Affiliation**: Kyung Hee University School of Dentistry, Seoul, South Korea
- **Funding**: NRF (Korea) grant 2015R1A2A2A01006490
- **Test agent**: SS = equimolar combination of 22(S)-hydroxycholesterol + 20(S)-hydroxycholesterol (oxysterols)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First study to test oxysterols for **periodontal** regeneration (prior oxysterol work was in ESCs/MSCs and orthopedic/spinal/cranial models).
- Establishes that the SS oxysterol combination drives **osteogenic differentiation of human PDLSCs** and **rat alveolar bone defect healing** with efficacy comparable to rhBMP-2.
- Mechanistically demonstrates a **reciprocal LXR ↔ Hedgehog (Hh) signaling cross-talk** mediating the oxysterol-induced osteogenesis.
- Positions oxysterols as a naturally occurring, cost-effective **BMP-2 alternative** lacking BMP-2's safety concerns.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**In-vitro (PDLSC):**
- Human PDLSCs isolated from extracted molars via STRO-1(+) magnetic selection; passage 4–7.
- Osteogenic medium + SS at 0.5, 1, 5 μM; DMSO 0.1% as control.
- Outcomes: ALP activity, intracellular calcium [Ca²⁺]i, Alizarin Red mineralization; RT-qPCR for OCN/OSX/OPN/RUNX2; Western blot + immunofluorescence for OCN/OSX/RUNX2.
- Mechanism probes: Western/RT-qPCR for LXRα, LXRβ, target gene ABCA1; Smo and Gli1 (Hh pathway); LXRα/β siRNA knockdown; Smo inhibitor 22-NHC (22-azacholesterol).

**In-vivo (rat alveolar bone defect):**
- 18 male Sprague–Dawley rats (8 wk, 250–300 g), 3 groups (control / BMP-2 / SS), n=6 each (3 for Western, 3 for μCT).
- β-APN diet 5 days pre-extraction to ease extraction; bilateral maxillary first molar extraction.
- SS injected (10 μl, 100 μM each oxysterol mix) into sockets on days 3 and 5; rhBMP-2 (1.5 mg/ml, 10 μl) injected on day 3; control left for natural healing.
- Sacrifice day 15; μCT (Skyscan 1173, 9.94 μm) for new bone volume %; Western blot of socket specimens (COLIA, ALP, RUNX2, OCN); H&E + IHC (OCN, ALP) at 10/15 days.
- Stats: ANOVA (in-vitro); Kruskal–Wallis + Tukey HSD (animal); p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **PDLSC osteogenesis**: SS (0.5/1/5 μM) significantly increased ALP, [Ca²⁺]i, and mineralization vs control; raised OCN/OSX/OPN/RUNX2 mRNA and protein (not strictly dose-dependent).
- **LXR pathway**: SS increased LXRα/LXRβ protein and mRNA, plus target gene ABCA1.
- **Hh pathway**: SS increased Smo and Gli1 protein.
- **Cross-talk**: LXRα/β siRNA knockdown blocked SS-induced Smo/Gli1 increase; conversely Smo inhibitor 22-NHC suppressed SS-induced LXRα/β — demonstrating mutual LXR↔Hh interaction. Either LXR siRNA or 22-NHC abolished SS-induced ALP and OCN/OSX/RUNX2.
- **In-vivo bone**: μCT — BMP-2 and SS sockets filled to alveolar crest; control sockets had unfilled centers. New bone volume showed **no statistically significant difference between the 3 groups**, but SS appeared (visually/qualitatively) higher than BMP-2 on μCT. Socket Western blot: COLIA/ALP/RUNX2/OCN higher in both BMP-2 and SS vs control. H&E/IHC confirmed more new bone and higher OCN/ALP in BMP-2 and SS groups.
- **Bottom line**: SS oxysterol combination promotes alveolar bone regeneration **equivalent to rhBMP-2**.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small in-vivo sample (n=6/group) and short follow-up (15 days); new-bone volume difference vs control/BMP-2 was **not statistically significant** — qualitative claims of "higher than BMP-2" not statistically supported.
- No cementum/PDL fiber regeneration outcomes despite "periodontal regeneration" framing; the in-vivo model is an extraction socket bone-fill, not a true periodontal (intrabony/furcation) defect.
- Mechanism dissection is in-vitro only; in-vivo signaling (LXR/Hh) not directly verified.
- No dose optimization or carrier/scaffold delivery for clinical translation; rat model, no clinical data.

## 6. Related Work

- Oxysterols pro-osteogenic / anti-adipogenic in MSCs and ESCs (Kha 2004; Aghaloo 2007; Dwyer 2007; Johnson 2011; Kim 2010; Kwon/Heo 2015).
- Semi-synthetic oxysterols Oxy133 (spinal fusion) and Oxy49 (cranial defect) match rhBMP-2 efficacy (Scott 2015; Hokugo 2016).
- PDLSC multipotency and dental tissue engineering (Seo 2004).
- BMP-2 safety/cost concerns (Glassman 2011; Garrett 2010; Epstein 2011).

## 7. Glossary

- **Oxysterol (SS)**: oxygenated cholesterol by-products; here 22(S)- + 20(S)-hydroxycholesterol combination.
- **PDLSC**: periodontal ligament stem cell (STRO-1+).
- **LXR (α/β)**: liver X receptor — nuclear receptor for oxysterols; target gene ABCA1.
- **Hh / Smo / Gli1**: Hedgehog pathway — receptor Smoothened and transcription factor Gli1.
- **22-NHC**: 22-azacholesterol, a Smo inhibitor.
- **rhBMP-2**: recombinant human bone morphogenetic protein-2 (osteoinductive comparator).
- **OCN/OSX/OPN/RUNX2/COLIA/ALP**: osteogenic markers (osteocalcin, osterix, osteopontin, Runt-related TF2, collagen type IA, alkaline phosphatase).
