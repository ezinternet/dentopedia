## Why Ingested

SmartPeg의 재사용 가능 여부와 소독 후 ISQ 측정값 안정성은 임상에서 자주 제기되는 실용적 질문이다. 제조사(Osstell)는 1회용 사용을 권장하지만 실제 임상에서는 재사용하는 경우가 많다. 이 2008년 한국산 in-vitro 연구는 400회 반복 측정·탈부착 및 6종 소독법의 ISQ 영향을 체계적으로 검증한 유일한 초기 문헌으로, [[implants/isq/kastel-2019-smartpeg-torque-isq-rfa]] 및 [[implants/isq/herrero-climent-2013-osstell-isq-reliability-icc]]의 SmartPeg 신뢰성 데이터와 대조하여 소독·재사용 맥락을 보완한다.

## One-line Summary

Korean in-vitro study (n=8 SmartPegs per experiment, Dankook University 2008) showing that ISQ values remain stable after up to 400 repeated measurements, 400 attach-detach cycles, and six clinical sterilization methods, but SmartPegs lose magnetism and become non-functional at 150°C/10 min dry-heat sterilization.

## 한줄요약

단국대 2008년 체외 연구 (실험당 SmartPeg 8개): SmartPeg은 400회 반복 측정·탈부착 및 6종 임상 소독법 후 ISQ값이 유의하게 변하지 않으나, 150°C/10분 진공건열멸균 시 자성 소실로 측정 불가.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Original article (in-vitro experimental study)
- **Journal**: 대한치과보철학회지 (Journal of Korean Academy of Prosthodontics)
- **Year / Volume / Pages**: 2008, Vol. 46 No. 1, pp. 42-52
- **Authors**: Won Ho-yeon, Cho In-ho, Lee Jun-seok — Dept. of Prosthodontics, Dankook University Dental College, Cheonan, Korea
- **Language**: Korean (with English tables and figure captions)
- **Device**: Osstell™ Mentor (ISQ probe) + SmartPeg™ Type IV (Integration Diagnostics AB, Gothenburg, Sweden)
- **Implant substrate**: Osstem fixture Ø4.1×10 mm in D3 artificial bone block

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **400-measurement ceiling test**: SmartPeg fixed in place — ISQ unchanged across all 400 repetitions (mean 75 for all 8 units; p>0.01).
2. **400 attach-detach cycles**: ISQ mean range 73.2–74.1 across 8 SmartPegs, no significant change (p>0.05).
3. **Six sterilization methods tested**:
   - Autoclave (고압증기멸균, 132°C/15 min standard)
   - UV sterilizer
   - High-temperature oil sterilizer (Dentistar)
   - Boiling water (10 min)
   - H₂O₂ + Alcohol (35% H₂O₂ + 83% ethanol)
   - Vacuum dry heat (진공건열멸균, 138°C/5 min)
   → All 6 methods: ISQ unchanged (p>0.01)
4. **10× repeated autoclave sterilization**: ISQ mean across 8 SmartPegs 73.4–74.8, no significant change (p>0.05).
5. **Curie-point (magnetic loss) test**:
   - 138°C/10 min: no change (p>0.05)
   - 150°C/5 min: 2/8 SmartPegs non-functional (ISQ=0)
   - 150°C/10 min: 8/8 SmartPegs non-functional (ISQ=0)
   → Magnetic loss threshold lies between 138°C and 150°C (Curie temperature of the permanent magnet component)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Bench-top in-vitro; n=8 SmartPegs per sub-experiment (color-coded for tracking)
- **Fixture setup**: Osstell™ Mentor + D3 block bone rigidly fixed on plastic board to eliminate positional measurement error
- **Statistics**: Paired t-test; significance threshold p<0.01 (experiments 1, 3, 5) or p>0.05 (experiments 2, 4)
- **Sterilization equipment**: Autoclave 1321 (Hanshin Medical), UV sterilizer (Bubang Techron), Dentistar hot-oil unit (BIG LINE), boiling water bath, Vacuum Dry Unit (Sejong Dental)
- **Limitation**: Single implant system (Osstem Ø4.1×10 mm, D3 bone block only); no clinical (in-vivo) validation; no thread-wear imaging; no quantification of how many uses before thread damage

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Experiment | n per group | Max trials | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Repeated measurement (fixed SmartPeg) | 8 | 400 | ISQ mean=75; no change (p>0.01) |
| Attach-detach cycles | 8 | 400 | ISQ range 73.2–74.1; no change (p>0.05) |
| 6 sterilization methods (1×) | 8 each | 1 | All unchanged (p>0.01) |
| Repeated autoclave (×10) | 8 | 10 | ISQ mean 73.4–74.8; no change (p>0.05) |
| Magnetic loss (138°C/10 min) | 8 | 1 | No change (p>0.05) |
| Magnetic loss (150°C/5 min) | 8 | 1 | 2/8 failed |
| Magnetic loss (150°C/10 min) | 8 | 1 | 8/8 failed |

**Clinical upshot**: Within the temperature envelope of standard clinical sterilization (highest = autoclave at 132°C/15 min), SmartPegs retain full magnetic function. Manufacturer's "single-use" recommendation appears conservative — this study found no ISQ drift up to 400 uses or 10× autoclave cycles, as long as thread integrity is preserved.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only one implant type, one bone density (D3 block), one SmartPeg type (Type IV)
- Artificial bone block does not replicate in-vivo bone microstructure
- Thread integrity was not imaged (SEM) after repeated use; "no ISQ change" ≠ "no wear"
- No contamination or biofilm cycle tested (sterilization of clinically used pegs)
- Future work: in-vivo thread-wear study, different SmartPeg types, different bone quality/density blocks
