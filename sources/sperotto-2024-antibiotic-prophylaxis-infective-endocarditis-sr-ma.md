## One-line Summary
JAMA Cardiology 2024 대규모 SR+MA (n=1,152,345 IE cases): 침습적 치과시술 후 항생제 예방 — 고위험군에서만 감염성 심내막염 (IE) 위험 유의 감소; 중간/저위험군 효과 없음 → 2017 AHA·NICE 입장 (고위험군 한정 권고) 지지.

## 1. Document Information
- Authors: F. Sperotto, K. France, M. Gobbo, et al.
- Type: Systematic review + meta-analysis
- Journal: JAMA Cardiol 2024;9(7):599-610
- PMC: PMC10999003

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Largest evidence synthesis to date on dental antibiotic prophylaxis for IE prevention (1.15M cases).
2. Risk-stratified findings: high-risk benefit, moderate/low/unknown no benefit.
3. Settles the AHA 2007/2017 vs older universal-prophylaxis debate empirically.

## 3. Methodology
SR+MA across dental procedure populations stratified by IE risk (high vs moderate vs low/unknown).

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함]
| IE risk group | Antibiotic prophylaxis effect |
|---|---|
| High risk | Significant reduction in IE incidence |
| Moderate risk | No significant effect |
| Low / unknown risk | No significant effect |

## 5. Limitations
- Observational evidence base
- Heterogeneity in defining "invasive dental procedures"
- Population-level data may underestimate individual risk
