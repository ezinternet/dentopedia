## Why Ingested

Provides clinical validation of one of the eight IDRA (Implant Disease Risk Assessment) risk factors — the restoration-margin-to-alveolar-bone-crest (RM-AC) distance — by quantifying how RM-AC ≤1.5 mm raises marginal bone loss risk; extends the risk-factor synthesis in [[wiki/overviews/peri-implantitis-management-overview]].

## One-line Summary

Retrospective study (77 patients, 202 platform-switched bone-level implants): RM-AC distance ≤1.5 mm gave 3.42× higher marginal bone loss risk than >1.5 mm; risk also rose with periodontitis stage (Stage 4 vs 2 = 26.31×) and with greater implant diameter (6.10×) and length (5.02×).

## 한줄요약

후향연구(환자 77명, platform-switched 골수준 임플란트 202개): 수복물 변연–치조정 거리(RM-AC) ≤1.5 mm는 >1.5 mm보다 변연골소실 위험 3.42배. 치주염 단계(4기 vs 2기 26.31배), 임플란트 직경(6.10배)·길이(5.02배) 증가에서도 위험 상승.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Retrospective cross-sectional study
- **Journal**: Journal of Dentistry 2024;144:104935
- **Institution**: Kütahya Health Sciences University, Turkey (ethics #2022/09-12)
- **DOI**: 10.1016/j.jdent.2024.104935

## 2. Key Contributions

- Clinically tests the IDRA-defined RM-AC <1.5 mm threshold as a real MBL risk factor.
- Quantifies effect size (≤1.5 mm → 3.42× MBL) and identifies periodontitis stage, implant diameter and length as additional MBL drivers.
- Reinforces that prosthetic/radiographic risk assessment after functional loading complements surgical-phase IDRA.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 77 partially edentulous patients (39 F / 38 M, 22–76 y); 202 platform-switched conical-connection, cement-retained, bone-level implants placed 2016–2021.
- Follow-up 6–36 months post functional loading.
- Group A: RM-AC ≤1.5 mm (n=69); Group B: RM-AC >1.5 mm (n=133).
- MBL measured radiographically from implant shoulder to alveolar bone; RM-AC from restoration margin to alveolar crest.
- Multinomial logistic regression.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- MBL incidence in Group A (≤1.5 mm) = **3.42×** that of Group B (>1.5 mm), statistically significant.
- Periodontitis Stage 4 MBL rate = **26.31×** Stage 2.
- MBL incidence **6.097×** higher with increasing implant diameter; **5.02×** higher with increasing length.
- Null hypothesis (no difference by RM-AC threshold) rejected.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective, single-centre; cement-retained platform-switched bone-level implants only (limited generalisability).
- Heterogeneous subgroups; authors call for larger samples and short/medium/long-term follow-up.
- 2D radiographic MBL.

## 7. Glossary

- **RM-AC distance**: restoration margin to alveolar bone crest; clinical analogue = supracrestal tissue height / abutment height.
- **IDRA**: Implant Disease Risk Assessment diagram (Heitz-Mayfield et al.), 8 peri-implant-disease risk factors.
- **MBL**: marginal bone loss.
- **Platform switching**: abutment narrower than implant platform, shifting microgap inward.
