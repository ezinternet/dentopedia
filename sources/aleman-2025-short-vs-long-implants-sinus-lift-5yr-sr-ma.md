## Why Ingested

Short vs long implant + sinus lift 비교의 ≥5년 장기 RCT 근거를 2024년까지 갱신하기 위해 인제스트. [[sinus-lift/lateral/mester-2023-short-vs-standard-implants-sinus-floor-elevation-sr-ma]] 의 ≥5년 thesis를 최신 검색(2016–2024, 7 RCT)으로 확장하나, 포함 RCT 다수가 high RoB임을 명시 — 근거 확실성 한계를 함께 기록.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 7 RCTs (≥5-year follow-up, search 2016–2024) on short vs long implants with sinus augmentation; success/survival risk ratio not significant (RR 2.37, 95% CI 0.83–6.78), with 5 of 7 trials at high risk of bias.

## 한줄요약

≥5년 추적 RCT 7편 메타분석(2016–2024 검색) — short vs long implant+상악동거상 성공/생존 RR 2.37(95% CI 0.83–6.78)로 유의차 없음; 7편 중 5편이 high risk-of-bias로 근거 확실성 제한적.

## 1. Document Information
- P R Health Sci J 2025;44(1):54–62. Author manuscript in PMC (available 2025-09-11).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Updates the ≥5-year evidence base through 2024 (4 databases).
- Applies RoB 2 to main outcomes — transparent bias grading.
- Reports success/survival, complications, and marginal bone loss together.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- SR+MA; electronic search of 4 databases, 2016–2024.
- Inclusion: RCTs with ≥5-year loading follow-up comparing short vs long implants with sinus augmentation; 7 studies.
- RoB 2 tool: 5 studies "High", 2 "Some concerns".
- Stata v18 for pooled RR/CI.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Implant success/survival: RR 2.37 (95% CI 0.83–6.78) — not statistically significant.
- Outcomes assessed: success/survival, complications, marginal bone loss (MBL).
- Overall certainty limited by predominantly high RoB.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 5/7 included RCTs at high risk of bias — low certainty.
- Wide confidence interval crossing 1 — imprecise effect estimate.
- Heterogeneous implant systems and augmentation protocols.

## 6. Related Work
- ≥5-year short-implant RCTs (Esposito, Thoma, Felice).

## 7. Glossary
- SA: sinus augmentation. RoB 2: revised Cochrane risk-of-bias tool.
- RR: risk ratio.
