## 2. Key Contributions
- Multicentre RCT with 3-year follow-up in posterior mandible
- 63 patients, 135 implants (66 PM, 69 PS)
- MBL: PS 0.28±0.56 mm vs PM 0.68±0.64 mm — significant (p=0.002)
- Effect size: 0.39 mm (95% CI 0.15–0.64) in favour of PS
- Randomization at surgery; conventional loading with cemented crowns

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Multicentre RCT. Patients with ≥2 missing adjacent posterior mandibular teeth. Randomization after open-flap implant insertion to platform-matched or platform-switched healing abutments. Conventional loading with cemented crowns. Annual clinical follow-up up to 3 years post-loading. Bone level measured on standardized radiographs from one reference point.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 63 patients, 135 implants at 3-year analysis
- PS MBL: 0.28±0.56 mm; PM MBL: 0.68±0.64 mm (p=0.002)
- Mean difference: 0.39 mm (95% CI 0.15–0.64) favouring PS
- PS: significantly better crestal bone preservation
- No significant difference in implant survival rates

## 7. Glossary
- **PS**: Platform switching — use of smaller-diameter abutment on larger implant collar
- **PM**: Platform matching — abutment matches implant platform diameter
- **MBL**: Marginal bone loss — radiographic bone level change around implant
- **TL**: Tissue-level implant — transmucosal component integral to implant
- **BL**: Bone-level implant — implant platform at crestal bone level
