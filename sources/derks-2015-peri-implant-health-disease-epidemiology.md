## One-line Summary
SR+MA of 11 studies (n≥100 subjects each): peri-implant mucositis prevalence 43% (CI 32–54%), peri-implantitis 22% (CI 14–30%); prevalence increases with function time and varies strongly with case definition.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Periodontology 2015; 42(Suppl. 16): S158–S171
- **Study type**: Systematic review with meta-analysis
- **Databases searched**: MEDLINE/PubMed, EMBASE, Cochrane (search March 2014)

## 2. Key Contributions
- First comprehensive SR+MA establishing population-level prevalence of peri-implant mucositis (~43%) and peri-implantitis (~22%)
- Meta-regression demonstrating positive relationship between peri-implantitis prevalence and implant function time
- Negative relationship between peri-implantitis prevalence and bone loss threshold used in case definition
- Highlights heterogeneity of case definitions as major limitation in epidemiological research

## 3. Methodology
- PRISMA-compliant SR
- Inclusion: studies with ≥100 subjects, reporting subject-level prevalence of peri-implant mucositis or peri-implantitis
- 15 articles describing 11 studies included
- Binary random-effects meta-analyses (OpenMeta[Analyst])
- Meta-regression for function time and bone loss threshold effects

## 4. Key Results
- Mucositis prevalence: 19–65% across studies; weighted mean 43% (CI 32–54%), I²=96%
- Peri-implantitis prevalence: 1–47% across studies; weighted mean 22% (CI 14–30%), I²=98%
- Peri-implantitis prevalence increases significantly with function time
- Higher bone loss threshold → lower apparent prevalence (case definition artifact)
- Extent and severity data rarely reported

## 5. Limitations
- Extreme heterogeneity (I²=96–98%) due to inconsistent case definitions
- Most studies not designed as epidemiological surveys (convenience samples)
- Limited incidence data

## 6. Related Work
- Berglundh 2018 consensus formalised case definitions partly addressing issues raised here
- Galarraga-Vinueza 2025 updated prevalence estimates (46% mucositis, 21% peri-implantitis)

## 7. Glossary
- **Peri-implant mucositis**: plaque-related soft tissue inflammation without bone loss
- **Peri-implantitis**: inflammation + progressive bone loss around implant
