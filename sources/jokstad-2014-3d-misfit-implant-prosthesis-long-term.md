## Why Ingested

19년 장기 추적으로 전악 고정성 보철의 미스피트(평균 150 μm)와 나사 합병증·골 소실 관계를 3D 스캐너로 정량화. [[implants/katsoulis-2017-misfit-implant-prosthesis-clinical-outcomes-sr]] 에서 인용된 핵심 장기 임상 데이터.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective (n=30 edentulous patients, avg 19yr follow-up): average framework misfit 150 μm; weak correlation with bone loss (R²=0.04); but prostheses with screw complications had significantly higher misfit (169 vs 134 μm, P=.005).

## 한줄요약

후향 코호트 (n=30 무치악 환자, 평균 19년): 프레임워크 미스피트 평균 150 μm; 골 소실과 약한 상관(R²=0.04); 나사 합병증 보철이 없는 보철보다 미스피트 유의하게 높음(169 vs 134 μm, P=.005).

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Implants Research 2014;26(10):1129-34
- **Study type**: Retrospective cohort
- **n**: 30 edentulous mandible patients; 4–6 implants per patient
- **Follow-up**: Average 19 years (range 12–32 years)
- **Method**: Intraoral scanner (STL) + desktop scanner of detached FDP → metrological software matching

## 2. Key Contributions
- Longest available follow-up data on full-jaw prosthesis misfit
- First study using 3D digital scanning to quantify misfit clinically
- Establishes screw complication threshold: >134 μm misfit

## 3. Methodology
- 30 patients, 4–6 implants each; 3 different alloys (Ag-Pd, Pd-Ag, Au type IV)
- Implant positions scanned with intraoral scanner; prosthesis intaglio surface with desktop scanner
- STL files matched with metrological software → misfit quantified
- Outcomes: average misfit, MBL, screw loosening/fractures

## 4. Key Results
- **Mean misfit**: 150 μm (SD 35; range 95–232 μm; CI 138–163)
- **Mean MBL**: 2.2 mm (SD 0.7; range 0.6–5.8 mm per implant)
- **MBL vs misfit correlation**: R² = 0.04 (P=.29) — **weak/non-significant**
- **Screw complications in 14/30 patients**
- **Misfit in complication group**: 169 μm (SD 32)
- **Misfit in no-complication group**: 134 μm (SD 30) → **P=.005**
- Alloy type did not affect complication rate
- Implant survival: 96.7% (5/150 implants lost over 19yr)
- 8/30 prostheses (26.7%) had been remade

## 5. Limitations
- Small n (30 patients)
- Single center
- Mixed alloys; predominantly older prostheses
- Misfit measured after prosthesis removal and remake in some cases

## 7. Glossary
- **Framework misfit / marginal gap**: deviation between prosthesis interface and implant platform when seated
- **Metrological software**: industrial precision measurement software for 3D STL comparison
- **Screw complication**: loosening or fracture of prosthetic or abutment screw
