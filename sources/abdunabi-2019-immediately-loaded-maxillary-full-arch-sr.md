## Why Ingested

무치악 상악 전악(full-arch) 고정성 보철의 즉시 로딩이 환자 만족도·임플란트 성공·보철 합병증 측면에서 조기/통상 로딩과 다른지에 대한 근거 정리. 하악 오버덴처 로딩을 다룬 [[implants/liu-2021-immediate-delayed-loading-overdenture-mbl-sr-ma]]와 짝을 이루어 무치악 로딩 프로토콜 근거를 양악으로 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (4 controlled clinical trials; meta-analysis not possible due to heterogeneity) finding only weak evidence of any difference between immediate loading and early/conventional loading of fully implant-supported maxillary full-arch fixed prostheses, for patient satisfaction or maintenance complications.

## 한줄요약

체계적 고찰(4개 임상시험; 이질성으로 메타분석 불가) — 무치악 상악 전악 고정성 보철에서 즉시 로딩과 조기/통상 로딩 간 환자 만족도·유지관리 합병증 차이는 약한 근거만 존재했다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Focused evidence summary on immediate loading of maxillary full-arch fixed prostheses (a higher-risk loading scenario than mandible).
- Maps the paucity and heterogeneity of controlled trials in this indication.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: systematic review of controlled clinical trials (CCTs); duplicate screening/extraction; Cochrane Risk of Bias tool.
- Heterogeneity prevented quantitative pooling (qualitative synthesis only).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 4 CCTs included (from 1,052 screened).
- Patient satisfaction: one RCT favored immediate over early loading at 12 months; one non-randomized study favored immediate over conventional at 3 months (lost by 12 months).
- Implant success / prosthetic complications: 3 trials reported no significant differences vs other protocols.
- Conclusion: weak evidence overall; well-designed RCTs needed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very few trials; heterogeneous designs; no meta-analysis.
- Short follow-up for satisfaction outcomes.

## 6. Related Work

- Loading-protocol reviews for single crowns and mandibular overdentures.

## 7. Glossary

- **CCT**: controlled clinical trial.
- **Full-arch fixed prosthesis**: screw-retained fixed complete denture supported entirely by implants.
