## One-line Summary
SR+MA (28 studies, 27,927 older adults) - pooled oral frailty prevalence 32%; oral frailty associated with physical frailty (OR 1.67), malnutrition (OR 2.27), low dietary variety (OR 1.98), social withdrawal (OR 1.42).
