## Why Ingested

[[occlusion/didier-2026-occlusal-contacts-ios-vs-carbon-paper-concordance]]는 IOS vs carbon paper 일치도를 자연치열에서 다뤘고, [[occlusion/mao-2024-occlusal-changes-implant-supported-single-crowns]]는 임플란트 단일치관 교합 변화(저교합화)를 SR+MA로 정리했다. 본 prospective in-vivo 연구(Gözen 2025)는 그 둘을 잇는 미싱 링크 — 단일 구치부 임플란트 수복 전후의 교합력 분포 변화를 **두 디지털 분석 장비(Medit i700 IOS vs OccluSense)로 직접 비교**해, 디지털 교합 분석 도구 선택 근거와 임플란트 수복 후 전악 교합력 재분배 evidence를 동시에 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

Prospective in-vivo study (n=18 analyzed, 20 enrolled) comparing Medit i700 IOS (contact area) vs OccluSense (contact area + force) before/after single posterior implant single-crown restoration: occlusal force redistributes across the whole arch (anterior + posterior contact/force decrease, opposing arch + total increase; posterior drop > anterior), and the two digital methods correlate strongly (Kendall's Tau-B, p<0.001).

## 한줄요약

전향 임상연구(분석 18명, 등록 20명) — 단일 구치부 임플란트 단일치관 수복 전후를 Medit i700 IOS(접촉면적)와 OccluSense(접촉면적+교합력)로 비교: 수복 후 교합력이 전악으로 재분배(전·후방 접촉/힘 감소, 대합치·전체 증가; 후방 감소>전방)되고, 두 디지털 방식은 강한 상관(Kendall Tau-B, p<0.001)을 보였다.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First in-vivo head-to-head of two digital occlusal analysers — **Medit i700 intraoral scanner** (IOS, contact-area only) vs **OccluSense** (sensor-integrated articulating paper giving contact area + quantitative force amplitude) — for single posterior implant restorations.
2. Quantifies how a single-tooth posterior implant single crown reshapes **whole-arch** occlusal force distribution pre→post prosthesis, not just the restored site.
3. Establishes that the two methods **correlate strongly** (Kendall's Tau-B, p<0.001) despite measuring different parameters, supporting interchangeability for trend detection while noting OccluSense alone delivers force magnitude.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Sample**: G*Power 3.1 (α=0.05, power 0.90, 10% dropout) → minimum 19. 20 enrolled, 2 excluded (confounding procedures) → **18 analyzed**.
- **Timepoints**: pre-prosthetic and post-prosthetic (after final occlusal adjustment), both same morning to avoid diurnal variation.
- **Devices**: OccluSense (Dr. Jean Bausch, Cologne) at 100 Hz × 6 s, max-force bite (max intercuspation); Medit i700 (MEDIT, Seoul) full-arch STL scans in parallel, same patient position. Restorations all designed by one technician in Exocad DentalCAD v3.1.
- **Processing**: Medit STL → CloudCompare v2.13.1 surface maps (thresholded to 60 µm = OccluSense sensor thickness); OccluSense → ImageJ 1.54f pixel-based contact-area. Groups: M (Medit area), O1 (OccluSense area), O2 (OccluSense force); 4 regions = anterior, posterior, opposing arch, total. Outcome = **% change** (ratio of total occlusal area to contact area), absolute area not assessed.
- **Calibration**: 10 repeated measurements on one patient, ImageJ-confirmed >80% agreement.
- **Stats**: Kruskal–Wallis (non-normal), DSCF post-hoc with Bonferroni for region differences; Kendall's Tau-B for method agreement (p<0.05).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Pre→post % change (region differences p<0.001):

| Region | Medit i700 (area) | OccluSense (area) | OccluSense (force) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Anterior | ↓ 47.8 ± 1.02% | ↓ 16.36 ± 2.2% | ↓ 18.54 ± 1.58% |
| Posterior | ↓ 65.5 ± 1.39% | ↓ 34.39 ± 0.8% | ↓ 30.77 ± 2.66% |
| Opposing arch | ↑ 30.04 ± 1.82% | ↑ 110.02 ± 3.76% | ↑ 30.29 ± 4.82% |
| Total | ↑ 17.66 ± 1.09% | ↑ 36.71 ± 1.92% | ↑ 7.3 ± 3.36% |

- **Direction is consistent across methods**: anterior + posterior segments decrease, opposing arch + total increase; **posterior reduction > anterior** (p<0.001).
- **Method agreement**: strong correlation Medit i700 vs OccluSense (p<0.001) → H2 accepted.
- **H1 rejected**: bite-force distribution DOES change significantly after single-implant restoration.
- Qualitative: IOS shows contact-point locations clearly but no intensity; OccluSense shows force intensity (color + %) but less-distinct contact locations.
- Aligns with Zhou 2021 (anterior force drop), Kim 2021 (contralateral/opposing arch force rise), Roque 2017 (force redistribution over time, patient-variable adaptation).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (18). Single-tooth posterior implants only — no multiple-implant or full-arch.
- Individual chewing habits / unconscious avoidance of the implant side not accounted for.
- The two devices measure different parameters → direct comparison inherently limited.
- Controlled clinic setting; real-life dynamic mastication not captured. Conventional articulating paper deliberately excluded (study scoped to digital-vs-digital reliability).
- Authors call for larger samples and long-term follow-up; OccluSense force-amplitude data proposed as the more practical clinical occlusal-adjustment aid.

## 7. Glossary

- **OccluSense**: digital occlusal analyser (Dr. Jean Bausch) — articulating-paper sensor recording contact location + force amplitude (color-coded: red high, green normal, yellow minimal) and timing.
- **Medit i700**: intraoral scanner; software-based occlusal contact-area analysis (Medit Occlusion Analyzer), no force magnitude.
- **Implant-protected occlusion**: occlusal scheme giving light contact on the implant crown at max intercuspation due to the absent periodontal ligament, to limit biomechanical overload.
- **Max intercuspation (ICP)**: complete-intercuspation bite position used here for max-force recording.
