## Why Ingested

Flapless tissue-level implant placement with Laser-Lok (laser-microtextured collar) neck 을 4년간 추적한 임상 코호트 데이터가 없어 인제스트. 이 연구는 [[implants/solderer-2025-tissue-level-implant-bone-loss-factors]]의 tissue-level MBL 위험인자 분석을 실제 임플란트 디자인(laser-microtextured neck) 수준에서 보강하며, [[implants/cosola-2020-bone-tissue-level-radiological-sr]]의 tissue-level MBL SR 결론에 대한 직접 임상 검증 사례로 활용.

## One-line Summary

Prospective 4-year cohort (41 implants, 36 patients) of tapered tissue-level Laser-Lok implants placed flaplessly showed 100% survival, mean MBL −1.10 ± 0.89 mm at 48 months, and stable peri-implant soft-tissue parameters with no mucositis or peri-implantitis.

## 한줄요약

무판막 술식으로 식립한 Laser-Lok 조직-레벨 임플란트 41개(36명)를 4년 추적한 전향적 연구: 생존율 100%, 48개월 MBL −1.10 ± 0.89 mm로 제한적 골소실, 치주 지표 안정적이며 주위염·점막염 없음.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First clinical data on tapered **tissue-level Laser-Lok** implants placed with a **fully flapless** (transmucosal) technique, with radiographic follow-up at 1, 3, 6, 12, 36, and 48 months.
2. Demonstrates 100% survival rate and no peri-implantitis or mucositis over 4 years in a heterogeneous (immediate/early/delayed) placement timing group.
3. Identifies **implant diameter** (3.8 mm vs. 4.6 mm) as the single statistically significant predictor of MBL at 48 months (multilevel regression, p = 0.027), with wider implants preserving crestal bone better.
4. Shows that **early placement timing** (2–3 months post-extraction) yielded the lowest MBL variation; differences became statistically significant only from T36 onward.
5. Demonstrates stable plaque score (PS ≈ 8%) and bleeding on probing (BoP ≈ 4.7%) throughout 4 years, consistent with healthy peri-implant soft tissue.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

### Patient Selection
- 36 healthy consecutive patients (16 males, 20 females; mean age 60 ± 9 years)
- Inclusion: age 18–75, single implant rehabilitation, ≥4 yr recall compliance, <10 cigarettes/day
- Exclusion: ASA ≥ 3, uncontrolled DM, bisphosphonate therapy, active periodontal disease (PPD >4 mm / BoP >25%), bruxism, insufficient crestal bone for 3.8 × 10 mm implant, sites requiring GBR

### Implant System
- **Tapered Tissue-Level Laser-Lok (Biohorizons, Birmingham, AL)**
- Transmucosal collar: 2.0 mm total (1.8 mm laser-microtextured + 0.2 mm smooth coronal portion)
- Laser-Lok microchannels: precision-engineered 8 µm cell-sized channels created by computer-controlled laser ablation
- Diameters used: 3.8 mm (n = 14) and 4.6 mm (n = 27)

### Surgical Protocol
- Flapless approach for early and delayed timings; atraumatic flapless extraction + socket preparation for immediate timing
- Initial 1.2 mm pilot drill through mucosa and bone; final site prepared at 225 rpm under saline irrigation
- No computer-aided surgical guide used
- Placement timing groups (ITI Third Consensus classification):
  - **Immediate** (n = 12): fresh extraction socket (chronic periapical disease/hopeless teeth)
  - **Early** (n = 12): 8–12 weeks post-extraction (acute periapical lesion/abscess)
  - **Delayed** (n = 17): ≥10–12 months post-extraction (mature alveolar bone)

### Prosthetic Protocol
- Prosthetic loading at 4 months post-insertion
- Polyether impression → customized abutment screwed → acrylic provisional (zinc-oxide cement) → metal-ceramic definitive crown (polycarboxylate cement) after 15 days

### Radiographic Assessment (MBL)
- Periapical radiographs with Rinn paralleling device at T0, T1, T3, T6, T12, T36, T48
- Analog films scanned at 968 dpi, ×20 magnification; calibrated to known implant dimensions
- Mesial and distal measurements from implant shoulder to crestal bone-to-implant contact (0.1 mm increments)
- Single blinded operator, calibrated against reference radiograph set

### Clinical Periodontal Parameters
- Plaque Score (PS) and Bleeding on Probing (BoP): 4-site dichotomous scoring at T3, T6, T12, T48
- Gingival thickness: classified thin (≤2 mm) vs. thick (>2 mm) via endodontic K-file #20 at surgery

### Statistics
- Stata 17.1; GEE-based linear regression (accounts for within-subject correlation of multiple implants)
- Multilevel mixed-effects logistic regression for T48 MBL predictors
- Significance threshold p < 0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### Survival and Complications
- **100% survival** at 48 months (41/41 implants)
- **Zero** early failures, peri-implantitis, mucositis, peri-implant bone necrosis, or prosthetic complications
- Zero dropouts

### Marginal Bone Level (MBL) — Total Cohort
| Timepoint | MBL (mean ± SD, mm) |
|---|---|
| T1 (1 month) | −0.15 ± 0.18 |
| T3 (3 months) | −0.29 ± 0.29 |
| T6 (6 months) | −0.45 ± 0.37 |
| T12 (12 months) | −0.53 ± 0.45 |
| T36 (36 months) | −1.06 ± 1.13 |
| T48 (48 months) | **−1.10 ± 0.89** |

### MBL by Subgroup at T48
- **Diameter**: 3.8 mm: −1.43 ± 1.01 mm vs. 4.6 mm: −0.64 ± 1.12 mm (p = 0.027 at multilevel analysis)
- **Timing**: Early −0.63 ± 0.89 mm < Immediate −1.01 ± 1.01 mm < Delayed −1.45 ± 1.10 mm (significant from T36)
- **Gender**: Males −1.03 ± 0.49 vs. Females −1.16 ± 1.10 (NS)
- **Location**: Maxilla −1.08 ± 1.01 vs. Mandible −1.12 ± 1.16 (NS)
- **Gingival thickness**: Thin −1.27 ± 1.11 vs. Thick −1.01 ± 1.02 (NS at T48; significant only pre-loading)

### Soft-Tissue Parameters at T48
- **BoP**: Mean 4.42% (range 0–6.5%); mesial site most affected; stable from T3 to T48
- **PS**: ~8.1% at T3 declining to similar levels at T48; mesial and buccal sites most affected; oral site 0%

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Small sample**: n = 41 implants / 36 patients; underpowered for subgroup analysis
- **Single center / single surgeon**: reduces generalizability
- **No randomization / control group**: prospective cohort design, no comparison arm with bone-level or non-Laser-Lok tissue-level implants
- **Analog radiography**: digital sensor would improve precision and consistency
- **No probing depth data reported**: PPD not tabulated as an outcome variable despite periodontal baseline assessment
- Authors call for larger RCTs and longer follow-up to validate the flapless Laser-Lok tissue-level protocol
- Future work should incorporate intraoral scanner impressions and digital abutment design workflow

## 7. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Laser-Lok | Computer-controlled laser ablation surface with 8 µm precision microchannels on implant collar; promotes connective tissue attachment and fibroblast growth |
| Tissue-level implant | Implant with transmucosal collar positioned at gingival level; implant-abutment connection supra-crestal |
| Flapless (transmucosal) surgery | Implant placement without flap elevation; only mucosal punch/drill penetration; minimally invasive |
| MBL (Marginal Bone Level) | Distance from implant shoulder to first bone-to-implant contact on periapical radiograph; negative values = bone loss |
| BoP (Bleeding on Probing) | Dichotomous score for peri-implant soft-tissue inflammation; 0 = no bleeding, 1 = bleeding |
| PS (Plaque Score) | Dichotomous score for visible plaque at soft margin; 0 = absent, 1 = present |
| GEE (Generalized Estimating Equations) | Statistical approach accounting for correlated observations from multiple implants per patient |
| Gingival phenotype | Thin (≤2 mm) vs. thick (>2 mm) soft tissue classification by trans-gingival probing |
| Early placement timing | ITI classification: implant placed 8–12 weeks after extraction (soft tissue healed, partial bone fill) |
| Immediate placement timing | ITI classification: implant placed into fresh extraction socket at time of extraction |
| Delayed placement timing | ITI classification: implant placed into fully healed bone ≥3–6 months post-extraction |
