## Why Ingested

임플란트 간격 관련 핵심 케이스: 권고 기준 3 mm보다 훨씬 좁은 1 mm IID에서 10년 관찰. 현대 임플란트 디자인(내부 원추형 연결부, 플랫폼 스위칭, 오목형 지대주, 치조정하 식립)이 골소실 방지에 미치는 영향을 보여줌. [[implants/tarnow-2000-inter-implant-distance-crestal-bone-height]] 의 3 mm 룰 원조 논문에 대한 중요한 임상적 업데이트.

## One-line Summary

Case report + literature review (10yr follow-up, IID=1 mm, platform-switch BL implants): bone maintained 1.40 mm coronal to implant-abutment connection at 10 years; 2 animal studies on 1 mm IID also show no bone loss vs wider distances.

## 한줄요약

케이스보고+문헌고찰 (10년, IID=1 mm, 플랫폼 스위칭 골수준 임플란트): 임플란트-지대주 연결부보다 1.40 mm 상방 골 유지; 1 mm IID 동물실험 2편도 넓은 거리 대비 골소실 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Implant Dentistry 2025;11(1):8
- **Study type**: Case report + literature review
- **Patient**: 57yr female, heavy smoker; maxillary premolar area; 12mm M-D space (need 13.5mm for 3mm IID)
- **Implant**: Ø3.5×13 mm × 2 (titanium grade 23, internal conical 24°, platform-switching 0.25mm, BioEtch surface to collar)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Only clinical case report with 10yr follow-up at IID=1 mm
- Reviews 2 animal studies on 1 mm IID: bone NOT inferior to wider distances
- Identifies design factors enabling bone preservation at narrow IID

## 3. Literature Review Summary
- **Tarnow 2000 IID rule**: 3 mm guideline based on external hex, no PS implants
- **2 mm IID clinical studies**: no bone loss vs 3 mm (all with PS + internal conical)
- **1 mm IID animal studies (n=2)**: bone response EQUAL or SUPERIOR to wider IID
- **1 mm IID clinical cases**: virtually none before this report

## 4. Key Results
| Follow-up | Finding |
|---|---|
| 3 months | Immature bone coronal to IAJ; papilla present |
| 1 year | Dense bone filling concave abutment space |
| 3 years | Papilla complete; bone stable |
| 9 years | Slight papilla recession (hygiene lapse); minor crest resorption |
| 10 years | Bone 1.40 mm coronal to IAC; papilla recovered; IID confirmed 1.02 mm on CBCT |

Design factors credited for bone preservation:
- Internal conical connection (24°) → microgap stability
- Platform-switching (0.25 mm) → shifts microgap inward
- BioEtch surface to collar → osseointegration to top
- Subcrestal placement (~1.5 mm infra-osseous)
- Concave prosthetic abutments → more space for bone filling

## 5. Limitations
- Single case; n=1
- Smoker patient; hygiene non-ideal
- Cannot revise guideline based on one case

## 7. Glossary
- **IID (Inter-Implant Distance, 임플란트 간격)**: distance between adjacent implant platform collars
- **Platform-switching (플랫폼 스위칭)**: abutment diameter < implant platform → shifts biologic width junction inward
- **Internal conical connection (내부 원추형 연결부)**: sealed implant-abutment junction → reduces microleakage and bacterial colonization at junction
