## One-line Summary

SR with meta-analyses of 35 papers (33 RCTs; PRP/PRGF/L-PRF) finding autologous platelet concentrates alone in alveolar ridge preservation generally accelerate soft-tissue healing, reduce post-extraction pain, and lessen ridge resorption versus unassisted healing, though high heterogeneity limits strong conclusions.

## 한줄요약

35편(33 RCT; PRP·PRGF·L-PRF)의 SR+MA로, 치조제보존술에서 자가혈소판농축물(APC) 단독 사용이 미처치 자연치유 대비 연조직 치유 가속·발치 후 통증 감소·치조제 흡수 완화 경향을 보였으나, 높은 이질성으로 강한 결론은 제한적이다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Periodontology 2000, 2025;97:104-130
- DOI: 10.1111/prd.12609
- Design: Systematic review with meta-analyses (RCTs only)
- Databases: PubMed, EMBASE, Web of Science, Scopus (English, up to Dec 2023)
- Yield: 35 papers (33 studies): 1 PRP, 6 PRGF, 28 L-PRF family

## 2. Key Contributions

- Focused SR+MA on APC as a **single biomaterial** (not combined with other grafts) in ARP, separating PRP, PRGF, and the L-PRF family.
- Stratifies by surgical approach (flap vs flapless; APC to fill vs fill-and-cover).
- Synthesizes hard-tissue, soft-tissue, and post-op pain outcomes.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PICO: systemically healthy humans needing ARP after non-third-molar extraction; intervention = APC (PRP/PRGF/L-PRF) alone in socket; comparison = unassisted healing (blood clot) or another biomaterial; outcomes = hard-/soft-tissue healing, pain.
- Inclusion: RCTs ≥10 healthy humans, split-mouth or parallel; short- and long-term.
- Exclusion: third molars, radiotherapy/bone disease/immune disease/uncontrolled diabetes, APC-plus-other-material combinations, <5 sockets/group, in vitro, animal, non-English.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Background reference (cited): pooled post-extraction resorption ~horizontal 2.7 mm (95% CI 2.4–3.1) and mid-facial vertical 1.7 mm (95% CI 1.3–2.1) clinically; ~2/3 occurs in first 3 months.
- APC alone (PRP/PRGF/L-PRF) vs unassisted: faster soft-tissue healing, less post-extraction pain, less ridge resorption, more socket bone fill, higher bone density in most studies.
- Benefit strongly influenced by surgical approach.
- APC vs other biomaterials: limited literature, inconclusive.
- Cited Cochrane (Atieh) ARP benefit: width MD −1.2 mm (95% CI −1.8 to −0.5; p=0.0003), height MD −1.4 mm (95% CI −2.0 to −0.7; p<0.0001).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Large heterogeneity (outcome parameters, timing, surgical approach, inclusion criteria) hindered strong conclusions.
- Few head-to-head APC-vs-other-biomaterial trials.
- L-PRF family dominates; PRP/PRGF underrepresented.

## 6. Related Work

- Extends descriptive post-extraction change quantifications (Couso-Queiruga 2021).
- Complements biologics consensus reviews (Suárez-López del Amo & Monje 2022).

## 7. Glossary

- **ARP** — Alveolar Ridge Preservation (치조제보존술)
- **APC** — Autologous Platelet Concentrate (자가혈소판농축물)
- **PRP** — Platelet-Rich Plasma (혈소판풍부혈장)
- **PRGF** — Plasma Rich in Growth Factors (성장인자풍부혈장)
- **L-PRF** — Leukocyte- and Platelet-Rich Fibrin (백혈구·혈소판풍부피브린)
