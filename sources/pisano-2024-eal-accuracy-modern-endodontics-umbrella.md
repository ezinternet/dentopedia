## One-line Summary
Umbrella review (7 SRs): EAL and radiography comparable for WL adequacy; EAL generations 3–6 not significantly different; EAL acceptable in primary teeth (low evidence); overall low evidence level.

## 1. Document Information
University of Salerno + CHU Besançon. Medicina 2024. Umbrella review including 7 SRs/MAs on EAL accuracy. Covers: EAL vs radiography, EAL generations, EAL in primary teeth.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Highest synthesis level for EAL accuracy: umbrella review of 7 SRs
- EAL vs radiography: no significant difference in postoperative pain; WL adequacy slightly better with EAL (RR 1.10, p=0.02); WL accuracy better with radiography by Kaur 2024 SR
- EAL generations 3–6: no statistically significant differences (Nasiri 2022 SR)
- Primary teeth: EAL acceptable but low-quality evidence (Paradiso 2022, Vitali 2022)
- CBCT vs EAL: comparable, mean discrepancy 0.51mm (Amin 2019)
- Overall conclusion: EAL and radiography "equally valid" (with caveats)

## 3. Methodology
- Umbrella review (UR) of systematic reviews
- 7 SRs included: Kaur 2024, Paradiso 2022, Vitali 2022, Martins 2014, Amin 2019, Nasiri 2022, Tsesis 2015
- Total patient pool across SRs: thousands of canals
- Devices covered: RootZX, RootZX II, Raypex 5/6, iPex/iPex II, ProPex, Apex ID, and others (generations 3–6)

## 4. Key Results

| SR Author | Comparison | Key Finding |
|---|---|---|
| Kaur 2024 | EAL vs radiography (818 pts) | WL adequacy: RR 1.10 (p=0.02) EAL better; WL accuracy: radiography better (SMD 0.55) |
| Martins 2014 | EAL vs radiography (2498 pts) | EAL better than radiography for AC localization (43.9–89.1% vs 14.6–32.7%) |
| Amin 2019 | CBCT vs EAL (82 pts) | Mean discrepancy 0.51mm; comparable |
| Nasiri 2022 | Generations 3–6 EAL | No significant differences between generations |
| Paradiso 2022 | Primary teeth EAL | EAL vs CR: OR 0.73 (NS) |
| Vitali 2022 | Primary teeth EAL | 69.3% within ±0.5mm |
| Tsesis 2015 | EAL influencing factors | NaOCl presence: smaller file–AC distance for RootZX and Justy II (p<0.05) |

## 5. Limitations
- Low evidence levels throughout — most included SRs contain heterogeneous primary studies
- Conflicting results on whether EAL or radiography is more accurate for WL
- Limited high-quality RCTs; primary teeth data especially weak

## 6. Related Work
- Versiani 2022 — in vivo micro-CT: RootZX II 81.8% vs Wirele-X 90.9% at ±0.5mm
- Furlan 2024 — foramen morphology effects on EFL accuracy
- Mousavi 2018 — AC position measurements (foundation for WL endpoints)

## 7. Glossary
- **EAL**: Electronic Apex Locator
- **WL**: Working Length
- **AC**: Apical Constriction
- **RR**: Risk Ratio; **SMD**: Standardized Mean Difference
- **Generations 3–6**: EAL technology generations (impedance-based → multi-frequency)
