## Why Ingested

즉시식립·즉시 임시수복(IIPP) 시 연조직 증대 방식 — 결합조직이식(SCTG) vs 이종 콜라겐 기질(XCM) vs 무이식(NG) — 의 1년 협측 변연·연조직 두께 결과 비교. XCM이 SCTG의 대안이 될 수 있는지를 검증해 [[immediate-implant/fettouh-2024-soft-tissue-volume-midfacial-gingival-immediate-implant]](CTG RCT)와 [[immediate-implant/seyssens-2021-immediate-implant-connective-tissue-graft-sr-ma]]를 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

1-year retrospective study (48 anterior single immediate implants) comparing subepithelial CTG (SCTG), xenogenic collagen matrix (XCM), and no graft (NG); 100% survival, with the no-graft group losing significantly more facial soft-tissue thickness and buccal marginal level, while XCM performed comparably to SCTG.

## 한줄요약

1년 후향연구(전치부 단일 즉시식립 48례) — SCTG·이종 콜라겐 기질(XCM)·무이식(NG) 비교에서 생존율 100%, 무이식군이 협측 연조직 두께·변연 수준 손실이 유의하게 컸고 XCM은 SCTG와 동등한 결과를 보였다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: BioMed Research International, Volume 2021, Article ID 8822804 (Hindawi). Open access.
- Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore / Policlinico Gemelli, Rome, Italy.
- Design: 1-year retrospective comparative study.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Direct clinical comparison of SCTG vs a xenogenic collagen matrix vs no graft at immediate-provisionalized anterior implants.
- Supports XCM as a graft-substitute alternative to autogenous CTG for facial soft-tissue stability.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 48 patients, single implant-supported restoration in the anterior jaw, type 1 (immediate) placement + provisionalization.
- 3 arms: SCTG, XCM, NG (control).
- Outcomes at 1 year: peri-implant soft-tissue marginal change, facial soft-tissue thickness (FSTT), peri-implant health, esthetics, patient satisfaction.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Implant survival: 100% (all groups).
- FSTT: no significant difference SCTG vs XCM (P > 0.05); NG significantly worse (P < 0.05).
- Buccal marginal level: NG lost significantly more than SCTG and XCM (P < 0.05).
- Conclusion: XCM is a viable alternative to SCTG; no graft compromises facial tissue stability.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective design; single center; 1-year follow-up.
- Group sizes and confounder control limited.

## 6. Related Work

- CTG / collagen matrix literature for immediate implants; FSTT and phenotype risk factors.

## 7. Glossary

- **IIPP**: immediate implant placement and provisionalization (type 1, restored within 24 h).
- **SCTG**: subepithelial connective tissue graft.
- **XCM**: xenogenic collagen matrix.
- **FSTT**: facial soft-tissue thickness.
