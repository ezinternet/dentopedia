## One-line Summary

SR (11 studies, n=278, all Invisalign): clear aligners achieve maxillary molar distalization up to ~2 mm with good accuracy; achieved-vs-predicted was 36-75% (M6), 42-72% (M7), derotation 63-78%; moderate-quality, mostly retrospective evidence.

## 한줄요약

SR(11편, n=278, 전부 Invisalign): 투명교정으로 상악 대구치 distalization 최대 약 2 mm 양호한 정확도. 달성/예측 = 제1대구치 36-75%, 제2대구치 42-72%, derotation 63-78%. 근거 moderate, 대부분 후향적.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Provides numeric achieved-vs-predicted distalization efficiency by tooth (M6/M7) and for derotation.
- Confirms ~2 mm practical distalization ceiling with aligners in Class II.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

5-database search to Dec 2023; prospective/retrospective/RCT/CCT in Class II treated with CAT. 547 -> 11 studies (8 retrospective, 3 prospective), n=278. RoB: Newcastle-Ottawa + ROBINS-I. PRISMA. No MA (heterogeneity).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Distalization 0.67 +/- 0.5 mm to 2.54 +/- 0.12 mm.
- Achieved/predicted: M6 36.48-75.5%; M7 41.94-72.2%; molar derotation accuracy 62.7-77.5%.
- Attachments (2 studies) and microimplants + Class I elastics (1 study) used to limit anchorage loss.
- Strength of evidence: moderate (AHRQ).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Mostly retrospective, small samples, no adequate controls; heterogeneous measurement/timing. RCTs needed.

## 7. Glossary

- **M6 / M7**: first / second molar.
- **Derotation**: correcting tooth rotation about its long axis.
- **Achieved/predicted ratio**: realized movement divided by software-planned movement (expression efficiency).
