## Why Ingested

8주 examiner-blind RCT로 5% calcium-sodium phosphosilicate (CSPS, NovaMin — Sensodyne Repair & Protect 기술) 무수 치약의 **단기/시간경과 onset**을 특성화 — Day 3부터 유의한 상아질 과민증 (Dentine Hypersensitivity, DH) 완화가 시작되어 8주까지 증가. 동일 Haleon 그룹의 무수 불소 치약 vs 표준 NaF 치약 RCT인 [[wiki/dentin-hypersensitivity/qiu-2026-stannous-fluoride-toothpaste-dentin-hypersensitivity-rct]]를 같은 Schiff·Yeaple·56일 설계로 보강하며, bioactive glass 치약 효능 SR [[wiki/dentin-hypersensitivity/defreitas-2021-bioactive-toothpastes-dentin-hypersensitivity-sr]]에 임상 time-course 근거를 더한다. (abstract-only — full text not retrieved.)

## One-line Summary

8-week examiner-blind parallel RCT (217 screened / 215 completed): anhydrous 5% CSPS+NaF toothpaste reduced examiner-assessed DH (Schiff + Yeaple tactile) significantly at every timepoint from Day 3 onward and significantly more than conventional NaF-silica toothpaste (all p<0.001), with relief growing over 8 weeks; DHEQ-48 OHRQoL improved but treatment difference NS.

## 한줄요약

8주 examiner-blind 평행 RCT (스크리닝 217명 / 완료 215명): 무수 5% CSPS+NaF 치약이 Schiff·Yeaple 촉각역치 두 지표 모두에서 Day 3부터 모든 시점에 DH를 유의하게 감소시키고 표준 NaF-실리카 치약보다 유의하게 우월 (모두 p<0.001), 완화 효과는 8주까지 증가; DHEQ-48 삶의질은 개선됐으나 군간 차이는 유의하지 않음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Characterizes the **short-term / time-course onset** of anhydrous 5% CSPS (calcium-sodium phosphosilicate; NovaMin) toothpaste against DH — long-term efficacy was already well-established, but the early days were not.
- Demonstrates statistically significant DH relief **as early as Day 3** by two independent clinical measures (Schiff evaporative + Yeaple tactile threshold).
- Shows the effect is **progressive** — DH reduction markedly increased across the 8-week study.
- Pairs examiner-assessed clinical endpoints with a patient-reported OHRQoL instrument (DHEQ-48).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Single-centre, 8-week, randomized, controlled, examiner-blind, 2-arm, stratified, parallel-group.
- **Population**: Orally-healthy adults with dentine hypersensitivity; two non-adjacent DH teeth selected per participant.
- **Arms**:
  - **Test**: 5% w/w CSPS toothpaste containing NaF (sodium fluoride).
  - **Reference**: conventional NaF-silica toothpaste.
- **Regimen**: twice-daily brushing.
- **Endpoints**:
  - DH by **Schiff** sensitivity score (evaporative/air stimulus) and **Tactile threshold** (Yeaple probe) at Baseline, 3d, 7d, 14d, 28d, 56d.
  - **DHEQ-48** (Dentine Hypersensitivity Experience Questionnaire) OHRQoL at 28d and 56d.
- **Sample**: 217 screened, 215 completed.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Test significantly reduced examiner-assessed DH at **all post-baseline timepoints** for **both** measures (p<0.0001 within-arm).
- Test was **significantly greater than Reference** at the assessed timepoints (all p<0.001).
- Significant separation from Reference **after 3 days**, with the magnitude of DH reduction **markedly increasing over the study** (growing to 8 weeks).
- **DHEQ-48** decreased progressively (improving OHRQoL) but **treatment differences were not statistically significant**.
- Test was **well-tolerated**.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Abstract-only ingest** — exact effect sizes, CIs, per-timepoint means, and adverse-event detail not retrieved (no PMC OA).
- **Manufacturer-sponsored** (Haleon, the Sensodyne maker) — interpret efficacy claims with sponsorship in mind.
- Single-centre; 8-week horizon (short-to-medium term; long-term durability addressed elsewhere in the CSPS literature).
- Patient-reported OHRQoL benefit did not reach significance between arms, despite clear examiner-assessed differences.

## 7. Glossary

- **CSPS / CSP** — calcium-sodium phosphosilicate; bioactive glass (NovaMin), the active in Sensodyne Repair & Protect; occludes dentinal tubules via hydroxycarbonate-apatite-like mineral deposition.
- **DH** — dentine hypersensitivity.
- **Schiff score** — 0–3 examiner sensitivity score to an evaporative (air) stimulus.
- **Yeaple / Tactile threshold** — force (g) at which a probe elicits sensitivity; higher = less sensitive.
- **DHEQ-48** — Dentine Hypersensitivity Experience Questionnaire; patient-reported OHRQoL.
- **Anhydrous** — water-free formulation; keeps reactive bioactive glass stable in the tube.
