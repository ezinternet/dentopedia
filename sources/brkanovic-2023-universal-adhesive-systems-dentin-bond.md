## Why Ingested
Quantifies aging + adhesive-choice effects on dentin bond strength; supports [[resin-bonding/brkanovic-2023-universal-adhesive-systems-dentin-bond]].

## One-line Summary
In-vitro study: thermocycling (p=0.018) and adhesive system (p=0.001) significantly affect dentin SBS; comparable at one year.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — thermocycling·adhesive 종류 모두 상아질 SBS에 유의 영향, 1년 후 비교 가능.

## 1. Document Information
Materials 2023; 16: 1530. DOI 10.3390/ma16041530. Open access. University of Zagreb, School of Dental Medicine.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Separates aging vs adhesive-choice contributions to dentin bond strength.
- One-year durability context.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro dentin shear bond strength across universal adhesive systems; thermocycling; one-year storage; factorial statistics.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Thermocycling p=0.018; adhesive-system p=0.001; comparable SBS at one year.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; absolute values do not directly translate clinically.

## 6. Related Work
Hurtado 2023 (long-term universal adhesives to dentin).

## 7. Glossary
- SBS: shear bond strength.
- Universal adhesive: adhesive usable in E&R, SE, or selective-etch modes.
