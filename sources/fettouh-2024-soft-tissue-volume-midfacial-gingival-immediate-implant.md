## Why Ingested

얇은 협측골을 가진 심미부 즉시식립에서 **연조직 증대(CTG) vs 골이식재 vs 맞춤형 치유지대주(CHA) 단독**이 1년 중안면 치은연 변화에 미치는 효과를 직접 비교한 RCT. [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/seyssens-2021-immediate-implant-connective-tissue-graft-sr-ma]](CTG SR+MA)의 결론을 1차 RCT 근거로 보강하고, [[immediate-implant/dutra-2025-customized-healing-abutments-immediate-implants]](CHA)와 대비된다.

## One-line Summary

1-year RCT (39 maxillary esthetic-zone sites, thin labial bone) comparing immediate implant placement with bone graft vs CTG vs customized healing abutment (CHA) alone; CTG produced the least midfacial gingival recession and reduced volumetric soft-tissue loss ~5-fold, while CHA alone failed to maintain contour (recession −0.74 CTG vs −0.98 bone graft vs −1.54 mm CHA).

## 한줄요약

1년 RCT(심미부 39부위, 얇은 협측골) — 즉시식립 시 결합조직이식(CTG)군이 중안면 치은퇴축이 가장 적고(−0.74 mm) 부피 손실을 약 5배 줄인 반면, 맞춤형 치유지대주(CHA) 단독군은 윤곽 유지에 실패했다(−1.54 mm; 골이식재군 −0.98 mm).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: BMC Oral Health (2024) 24:1256. Open access (CC BY 4.0).
- Cairo University / MIU Egypt. Trial: ClinicalTrials.gov NCT05975515.
- Design: parallel 3-arm RCT, clinical + volumetric (digital) + CBCT.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Head-to-head RCT of three soft/hard-tissue management strategies at immediate-implant sites with thin labial bone.
- Adds volumetric (mm³) and CBCT outcomes alongside clinical midfacial margin change.
- Demonstrates CTG superiority and the inadequacy of customized healing abutment alone.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 39 non-restorable maxillary esthetic-zone teeth, randomized 1:1:1 to: IIP + Bonegraft (control), IIP + CTG, IIP + CHA.
- All received a customized healing abutment; flapless immediate placement.
- Primary outcome: midfacial gingival margin change (mm) at 1 year.
- Secondary: labial soft-tissue contour change, interdental tissue height, total volume (mm³), labial bone amount, crestal bone level (CBCT).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Midfacial gingival margin change (1 yr): −0.98 (bone graft), −0.74 (CTG), −1.54 mm (CHA); significant between groups (P ≤ 0.05).
- CTG reduced total volume loss ~5-fold vs other groups.
- Significant positive correlation between volume loss and margin change in bone-graft and CHA groups; none in CTG group (P = 0.63).
- CBCT: significant difference in crestal bone change between groups; no difference in labial bone amount.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small per-arm sample (n = 13); single center; 1-year follow-up.
- Retrospectively registered.

## 6. Related Work

- CTG for immediate implants (Seyssens et al. SR+MA); customized healing abutment literature.

## 7. Glossary

- **IIP**: immediate implant placement.
- **CTG**: connective tissue graft.
- **CHA**: customized (anatomic) healing abutment that supports the emergence profile and socket seal.
