## Why Ingested
Key bond-strength evidence behind TheraCem's zirconia/tooth bonding; supports [[resin/mahrous-2020-mdp-caf-self-adhesive-resin-cement-microshear]] and [[resin/theracem-bisco-product-reference]].

## One-line Summary
In-vitro micro-shear study: MDP/CaF self-adhesive cement (TheraCem) > non-MDP RelyX Unicem on enamel/dentin/zirconia after thermocycling.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro micro-shear — TheraCem이 RelyX Unicem보다 3개 기질 모두 우위(thermocycling 5000).

## 1. Document Information
Int J Periodontics Restorative Dent 2020; 40: 445–455. DOI 10.11607/prd.3992. PMID 32233200.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Quantifies 10-MDP bond-strength benefit across enamel/dentin/zirconia after aging.
- Underpins TheraCem zirconia-bonding claim.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro micro-shear bond strength to enamel, dentin, zirconia; 5,000 thermocycles; TheraCem vs RelyX Unicem.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Enamel 6.46 vs 3.04 MPa (p=.002); dentin 10.67 vs 6.46 (p=.014); zirconia 39.76 vs 27.04 (p<.001) — all favor TheraCem.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; two-cement comparison; clinical survival data needed.

## 6. Related Work
Chen 2018 (BISCO TheraCem characterization); Müller & Özcan 2022 (zirconia cement adhesion).

## 7. Glossary
- 10-MDP: 10-methacryloyloxydecyl dihydrogen phosphate (functional monomer).
- Micro-shear bond strength: small-area shear bond test.
