## Why Ingested
TMD 환자의 삶의 질을 RDC/DC-TMD 진단 그룹별로 정량화한 첫 SR+MA. [[tmj/kapos-2020-temporomandibular-disorders-aetiology-diagnosis-review]]의 역학·삶의 질 서술을 메타분석 수치로 확인; [[tmj/yao-2023-chronic-tmd-pain-sr-nma-bmj]]의 만성 통증 치료 NMA의 QoL 근거를 보강.

## One-line Summary
SR+MA (24 studies, n=3,829, Health Qual Life Outcomes 2021): all TMD axis I groups show significantly worse QoL vs controls; muscle disorders (SMD 0.82) and arthralgia/arthrosis (SMD 0.98) worse than disc displacement (SMD 0.59).

## 한줄요약
SR+MA (24편, n=3,829): 모든 TMD 진단 그룹에서 삶의 질 유의하게 저하; 근육형·관절통/관절염형이 디스크변위형보다 더 낮음.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Health and Quality of Life Outcomes 19:83 (2021)
- **Study type**: SR + meta-analysis (PRISMA, PROSPERO CRD42017072229)
- **Databases**: MEDLINE, EMBASE, LILACS + gray literature
- **Search period**: To August–December 2019
- **Included**: 24 articles in SR; 9 in MA; n=3,829 total

## 2. Key Contributions
- First MA to separate QoL by RDC/TMD axis I diagnostic group
- All three groups: significantly worse QoL than asymptomatic controls
- Group I (muscle disorders): large effect size (SMD 0.82)
- Group III (arthralgia/arthritis/arthrosis): largest effect (SMD 0.98)
- Group II (disc displacement): smallest effect (SMD 0.59)
- Supports biopsychosocial approach: QoL impact is real and measurable

## 3. Results

| Group | N | SMD (95% CI) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Global TMD (all axis I) | 3,829 | 1.06 (0.65–1.51) | <0.001 |
| Group I: muscle disorders | 3,056 | 0.82 (0.45–1.18) | <0.001 |
| Group II: disc displacements | 3,184 | 0.59 (0.26–0.91) | <0.001 |
| Group III: arthralgia/arthritis/arthrosis | 2,781 | 0.98 (0.59–1.36) | <0.001 |

All comparisons: TMD patients vs asymptomatic controls. Effect sizes interpreted as large (>0.8) for global, group I, III.

## 4. Limitations
- Heterogeneous QoL instruments across studies (SF-36, OHIP, generic QoL tools)
- Publication bias possible (funnel plot asymmetry)
- Cross-sectional/cohort studies only; no causal inference

## 5. Clinical Implication
TMD는 단순 저작 장애가 아닌 전반적 삶의 질을 크게 저하시키는 상태:
- 근육형·관절통형: QoL 저하 더 심각 → 조기 적극 개입 필요
- 치료 목표: 통증 감소 + QoL 회복 (VAS만으로 모니터링 불충분)
- 심리사회적 측면 평가 (DC/TMD Axis II) 필수

## 6. Glossary
- **RDC/TMD**: Research Diagnostic Criteria (이전 진단기준; DC/TMD 이전)
- **DC/TMD**: Diagnostic Criteria for Temporomandibular Disorders (2014 현행)
- **SMD**: Standardized mean difference (표준화 평균 차이) — 효과 크기
- **Axis I**: 신체적/구조적 진단 분류 (근육·관절)
- **Axis II**: 심리사회적 평가 (우울·기능 장애)
