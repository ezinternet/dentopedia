## One-line Summary

Review (Periodontology 2000) of behavioral/psychosocial and PRO/PRE assessment in periodontitis, implant-therapy candidate, and peri-implantitis patients — treatment improves quality of life, but diverse patient expectations/perceptions can undermine satisfaction and long-term implant success; a thorough pre-therapy behavioral assessment plus remedial correction of misperceptions is essential.

## 한줄요약

치주·임플란트·peri-implantitis 환자의 행동·심리사회 및 PRO/PRE 평가 review(Periodontology 2000) — 치료는 삶의 질을 개선하나, 환자마다 다른 기대·인식이 만족과 장기 임플란트 성공을 저해할 수 있음. 치료 전 행동평가와 오인식 교정(remedial action)이 필수. peri-implantitis 진단은 불신·분노·distress의 원천.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Periodontology 2000 2024;95:40–50
- DOI: 10.1111/prd.12589
- Affiliation: Chulalongkorn University (+ Tufts, Harvard, Karolinska)
- Study type: Review article (behavioral/psychosocial + PRO/PRE focus)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Extends the PRO/PRE framework from disease treatment into implant therapy candidacy and peri-implantitis.
- Centers pre-therapy behavioral assessment: perceptions, expectations, and risk for behavioral disorders, with remedial correction.
- Frames a peri-implantitis diagnosis as a source of distress, resentment, and loss of trust → relationship/communication consequence.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative review summarizing behavioral and psychosocial assessment evidence across the three patient groups.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Periodontitis adversely affects quality of life; treatment yields significant patient-experienced improvement.
- Implant rehabilitation improves QoL, but heterogeneous expectations/perceptions interfere with satisfaction and long-term success.
- Behavioral assessment of the implant candidate (perceptions, expectations, behavioral-disorder risk) is essential; misperceptions must be corrected before therapy.
- Peri-implantitis carries significant psychological burden affecting trust and long-term outcomes.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Narrative review — no quality grading or pooled estimates.
- PRO/PRE instruments and behavioral-assessment protocols not standardized for implant/peri-implant care.

## 7. Glossary
- **PRO / PRE**: patient-reported outcome / patient-reported experience.
- **Peri-implantitis**: inflammatory disease with progressive bone loss around an implant.
- **Behavioral assessment**: structured evaluation of patient perceptions, expectations, and behavioral-disorder risk.
