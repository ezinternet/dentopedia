## Why Ingested

임플란트-자연치 간격 <1 mm이 골수준 임플란트(BL)에서 주위염·골소실을 증가시키는 반면 조직수준 임플란트(TL)에서는 영향이 없다는 임상적으로 중요한 발견. 임플란트 유형별 차별화된 거리 기준의 근거. [[implants/joshi-2025-implant-tooth-proximity-sr-ma]] SR+MA에 포함된 핵심 연구.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective (n=98 patients, 195 implants, mean 37.8 months): BL implants <1 mm from adjacent tooth → significantly more BOP and interproximal bone loss; TL implants showed no significant impact of proximity.

## 한줄요약

후향 코호트 (n=98명, 195 임플란트, 37.8개월): BL 임플란트 <1 mm 근접 → BOP↑ 및 치간 골소실↑ 유의; TL 임플란트에서는 근접 거리 영향 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Australian Dental Journal 2018;63(4):467-477
- **Study type**: Retrospective cohort (single center, Hong Kong)
- **n**: 98 patients / 195 implants
- **Follow-up**: mean 37.8 months (min 12 months)
- **Outcome**: Bone level, BOP, radiographic assessment

## 2. Key Contributions
- Critical finding: BL vs TL implants differ in sensitivity to tooth proximity
- Defines practical safety zone: ≥1 mm from adjacent tooth root (BL implants)
- Implant survival 99.6% overall

## 3. Methodology
- Radiographic horizontal and vertical position of each implant identified
- Probing depth, BOP, bone level at implant AND adjacent tooth — at placement, delivery, most recent review
- Horizontal distance: implant surface to adjacent tooth root surface

## 4. Key Results
- Horizontal distance <1 mm (BL implants):
  - Significantly more interproximal bone loss
  - Higher BOP prevalence
- Horizontal distance <1 mm (TL implants): NO significant impact
- Corono-apical implant position: no significant impact on MBL
- Survival: 99.6% (1 implant lost to peri-implantitis)

## 5. Clinical Interpretation
The IAJ (implant-abutment junction) position relative to crestal bone determines sensitivity to proximity:
- BL implants: IAJ at bone level → microgap at crest → proximity compounds inflammation risk
- TL implants: IAJ at gingival level → microgap away from bone → proximity less harmful

## 7. Glossary
- **BL (Bone Level, 골수준) implant**: IAJ located at or near bone crest
- **TL (Tissue Level, 조직수준) implant**: IAJ located at gingival level (polished collar)
- **BOP (Bleeding on Probing, 탐침 출혈)**: indicator of peri-implant inflammation
