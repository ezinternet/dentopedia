## Why Ingested

[[radiology/ludlow-2008-patient-risk-dental-radiographic-examinations]]의 2008년 선량 데이터를 최신 장비·조준통으로 업데이트. NCPR Report No. 177(2019) 직사각조준 재권고를 검증하는 실험적 근거. 성인·소아 팬텀 모두 포함해 최신 FMS 선량 기준값 제공.

## One-line Summary

Phantom dosimetry (adult + child, 7 collimator systems): best FMS = Focus-RC 54 µSv adult / 44 µSv child; circular collimation 86–89 µSv; rectangular collimation outperforms thyroid shielding for thyroid dose reduction.

## 한줄요약

팬텀 선량측정(성인·소아, 7개 조준 시스템): 최저 FMS = Focus-RC 54 µSv 성인 / 44 µSv 소아; 원형조준 86–89 µSv; 직사각조준이 갑상선 차폐보다 갑상선 선량 감소에 더 효과적.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Updated FMS effective dose benchmarks with modern rectangular collimation devices (post-2008).
- 7 collimation modalities compared: circular, Focus-RC, JadRad-RC, Rinn-RC, Durr-RC, DEXshield-RC, TruAlign-RC.
- Rectangular collimation reduced FMS dose by 37–56% vs circular (adult); similar trend in children.
- Key recommendation: rectangular collimation (specifically Focus-RC, smallest field) surpasses thyroid shielding as the primary dose-reduction strategy.
- Aligns with NCRP Report No. 177 (2019) recommendation for rectangular collimation as standard of care.

## 3. Methodology

- Adult and child tissue-equivalent phantoms.
- 18 projections (adult FMS) and 12 projections (child FMS) per collimator.
- OSL (optically stimulated luminescence) dosimeters for organ dose measurement.
- Equipment: Focus intraoral X-ray unit, 70 kVp, 5.34 mA (adult), 2.7 mA (child).

## 4. Key Results

| Collimator | Adult E (µSv) | Child E (µSv) |
|---|---|---|
| Focus-RC (best) | **54** | **44** |
| JadRad-RC | 55 | 44 |
| Durr-RC | 58 | 45 |
| Rinn-RC | 62 | 48 |
| DEXshield-RC | 70 | 53 |
| TruAlign-RC | 85 | 85 |
| Circular (reference) | **86** | **89** |

- Thyroid dose reduced by up to 59% with thyroid shielding + circular, but rectangular alone reduces more.
- Best-case FMS (54 µSv adult) ≈ 6.6 days of natural background (US: ~8.2 µSv/day).
- Single periapical in a full FMS series (18 images): approximately 54/18 ≈ **3 µSv per image** (estimated).

## 5. Limitations

- Single X-ray unit (Focus, 70 kVp); different kVp/mA settings would shift absolute values.
- OSL vs TLD measurement technique may affect comparability with Ludlow 2008 TLD data.

## 6. Related Work

## 7. Glossary

- **FMS**: Full-mouth series (same as FMX — full mouth radiographic survey)
- **OSL**: Optically stimulated luminescence dosimeter — alternative to TLD for dose measurement
- **NCRP Report 177**: National Council on Radiation Protection and Measurements 2019 report reinforcing rectangular collimation for intraoral imaging
