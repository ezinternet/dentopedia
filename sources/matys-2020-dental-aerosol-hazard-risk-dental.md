## Why Ingested

사용자 요청: 하이스피드·로우스피드 치과 엔진(핸드피스) 풀텍스트 인제스트. 신규 `dental-handpiece` 카테고리의 controlled bench(manikin) 근거 — 동일 caries removal을 하이스피드 vs 로우스피드 핸드피스 vs Er:YAG 레이저로 비교하고, 7종 석션 시스템별 에어로졸 저감 효율을 정량화. 실제 환자 데이터인 [[dental-handpiece/lahdentausta-2022-aerosol-concentrations-size-distributions-clinical]]와 짝을 이뤄 핸드피스 에어로졸 위험·완화의 manikin 대조군을 제공.

## One-line Summary

In-vitro manikin study measuring aerosol (0.3–10 μm) at manikin/operator/assistant during caries removal, scaling, and polishing — high-speed handpiece + saliva ejector produced the most aerosol, low-speed produced less than the high-speed turbine, Er:YAG laser the least, and wide customized high-volume evacuators removed the most (Zirc evacuator 88% reduction).

## 한줄요약

마네킹 인비트로 실험(0.3–10 μm, 마네킹·술자·보조자 3지점) — 하이스피드 핸드피스+타액흡인기가 에어로졸을 가장 많이, 로우스피드는 하이스피드 터빈보다 적게, Er:YAG 레이저가 가장 적게 생성했고, 넓은 맞춤형 고용량 흡입기(HVE)가 가장 효과적으로 제거(Zirc 88% 감소).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Controlled bench comparison of caries-removal modality (high-speed handpiece vs low-speed handpiece vs Er:YAG laser) by aerosol output.
- Quantified relative aerosol-reduction efficiency of 7 suction modalities, including two author-built wide HVEs.
- Demonstrates low-speed handpiece generates significantly less aerosol than the high-speed turbine for caries removal.
- Identifies silicone-rubber polishing on a low-speed contra-angle as the single highest aerosol-particle generator.
- Shows Er:YAG laser caries removal yields the lowest aerosol of all rotary/optical modalities.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: in-vitro manikin (FDI tooth #35) aerosol experiment, no rubber dam.
- Measurement: PC200 laser particle counter (Trotec), 6 sizes (0.3/0.5/1.0/2.5/5.0/10.0 µm) summed; 3 sites — manikin (2 cm), operator (40 cm), assistant (40 cm); sensor on 60 s post-start; 6 repeats per site.
- Air standardized to 28,000–30,000 baseline particles; NV1050 air purifier (800 m³/h) reset (~5 min, 20 m³ room).
- Procedures: caries removal (high-speed bur, low-speed bur, Er:YAG 300 mJ/20 Hz/6 W), ultrasonic scaling, silicone-rubber polishing (1000 & 10,000 RPM).
- Suction: saliva ejector EM15; HVE EM19 EVO; each ± extraoral vacuum (MAcURAY PRO); Zirc Mr. Thirsty One-step; customized white HVE; customized black HVE.
- Statistics: ANOVA + Tukey post-hoc, Statistica; p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- High-speed handpiece + saliva ejector: highest spray particles at every site; manikin >> operator/assistant (p<0.001).
- Low-speed handpiece caries removal: significantly LOWER aerosol than high-speed turbine; HVE cut aerosol ~4× (manikin) and ~3× (operator) vs saliva ejector.
- Er:YAG + HVE: lowest aerosol (manikin 28.6 ± 1.3; operator 29.2 ± 0.8; assistant 29.0 ± 1.1; p<0.001).
- Silicone-rubber polishing (low-speed): highest particle number overall; raising RPM 1000→10,000 with HVE reduced manikin aerosol (p<0.001).
- Ultrasonic scaling: lower aerosol than rotary caries removal/polishing; HVE reduced manikin (p=0.005) and operator (p=0.003) vs saliva ejector.
- Suction efficiency: wide customized HVE removed 2× (vs HVE) and 8× (vs saliva ejector) more aerosol; extraoral added ~33%; Zirc evacuator 88% reduction (32.1 ± 2.0 vs HVE 60.5 ± 3.0).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro manikin (no real saliva/blood/respiration); no rubber dam used.
- Particle-count surrogate only — no microbial/viral assay or infectivity data.
- Two HVE prototypes are author-designed (not commercially validated).
- Single Er:YAG setting; cost of laser device limits generalizability.
- Future: virus-inactivation potential of Er:YAG caries removal; optimization of HVE tip geometry.

## 6. Related Work

- Real-clinical patient aerosol data (Lahdentausta et al. 2022) — complementary in-vivo confirmation.
- Jacks: HVE removes ~90% of ultrasonic-scaling aerosol vs saliva ejector.
- Harrel et al.: ultrasonic scaler spreads material ≥18 inches.
- Graetz et al.: sonic/ultrasonic devices and splatter risk.

## 7. Glossary

- HVE: high-volume evacuator (wide-bore suction, high flow).
- Saliva ejector: low-volume suction; poor aerosol capture.
- Zirc evacuator (Mr. Thirsty One-step): one-piece intraoral HVE+saliva ejector.
- Extraoral vacuum: external suction unit positioned outside the mouth.
- Er:YAG laser: erbium-doped YAG, 2940 nm, high water absorption; hard/soft tissue ablation.
- Splatter: droplets >50 μm (vs aerosol ≤50 μm).
