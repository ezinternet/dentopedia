## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Prospective cohort (n=20 patients, 439 restorations) evaluating minimally invasive glass-ceramic full-mouth rehabilitation over up to 12 years. Restoration types: lithium disilicate (79.7%), feldspathic porcelain (17.5%), leucite (2.7%). Design: partial coverage (35.3%), full-contour (31.2%), veneers (27.8%), onlays (4.6%), inlays (1.1%). Mean follow-up 4.5 years (max 12.4yr). Kaplan-Meier 5-year survival: 96.6% (restoration), 86.4% (patient). Technical complications 3% (chipping n=5, retention loss n=7). Biological complications 4.3%. Patient satisfaction: esthetics VAS 9.5, function VAS 9.3.
