## Why Ingested
OVD 증가와 교합 상실이 TMJ 연골에 미치는 병리학적 영향을 직접 비교한 동물 연구. 임상에서 구치부 고경 변화 후 TMJ 증상 발생 메커니즘([[tmj/radej-2023-condylar-displacement-malocclusion-cr-mip-sr]])의 조직학적 근거 제공. 사용자가 "구치부 고경 설정 후 TMJ 클리킹"을 질문한 직접적 근거 논문.

## One-line Summary
Animal study (adult rats, 8 wk): OVD elevation causes faster and more severe condylar cartilage damage than occlusal loss; both show cartilage destruction followed by partial repair; subchondral bone repair delayed in OVD-elevated group.

## 한줄요약
성인 쥐 동물 연구 (8주): OVD 증가는 교합 상실보다 더 빠르고 심한 과두 연골 손상; 양군 모두 연골 파괴 후 부분 회복; OVD 증가군에서 연골하골 회복 지연.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Oral Rehabilitation 51(9):1821–1832 (2024)
- **Study type**: Animal (rat, in vivo)
- **Groups**: CON (n), iOVD (occlusal plate = increased OVD), LO (tooth extraction = occlusal loss)
- **Assessment**: micro-CT, H&E, toluidine blue, IHC at 3d/1wk/2wk/4wk/8wk

## 2. Key Contributions
- OVD elevation (iOVD): cartilage destruction faster and more severe than occlusal loss (LO)
- iOVD: subchondral bone repair occurs LATER than in LO
- Both groups: cartilage destruction followed by partial repair (adaptive response)
- LO group: no obvious time-trend change in toluidine blue/IHC staining (more static)
- Mechanism: mandibular malpositioning → abnormal stress concentration → articular cartilage wear, subchondral bone sclerosis, osteophyte formation

## 3. Methodology
- 15-week-old female adult rats; 3 groups: CON, iOVD (occlusal plate), LO (tooth extraction)
- Sequential sacrifice at 3d, 1wk, 2wk, 4wk, 8wk
- Micro-CT: bone morphology; H&E: histomorphology; Toluidine blue: proteoglycan; IHC: cartilage matrix markers

## 4. Key Results
| Time | iOVD (OVD elevation) | LO (occlusal loss) |
|---|---|---|
| Early (3d–1wk) | Rapid cartilage destruction | Slower damage |
| 4–8wk | Partial repair, delayed subchondral repair | Partial repair, more variable |
| Severity | Greater damage | Less severe |
| Time trend | Clear progressive then repair | No obvious time trend |

## 5. Limitations
- Animal model (rat); cannot directly extrapolate to humans
- OVD increase amount not standardized to clinical ranges
- Only female rats; no behavioral outcomes (pain, jaw function)

## 6. Clinical Implication
OVD 증가는 교합 상실보다 TMJ에 더 빠르고 심한 조직 손상을 유발 → 임상적으로 고경을 올리는 것은 내리는 것보다 TMJ에 더 큰 위험 부담이 있을 수 있음. 다만 인간 임상 데이터로의 직접 번역은 주의 필요.

## 7. Glossary
- **OVD**: Occlusal vertical dimension (교합 수직 고경)
- **iOVD**: Increased OVD group (고경 증가군)
- **LO**: Loss of occlusion group (교합 상실군)
- **IHC**: Immunohistochemistry
- **Subchondral bone**: Bone tissue underlying articular cartilage (연골하골)
