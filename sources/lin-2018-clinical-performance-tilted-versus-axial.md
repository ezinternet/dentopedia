## Why Ingested

무치악 환자에서 경사 식립(tilted implant) vs 수직 식립(axial implant) 선택의 핵심 SR. [[implants/implants-clinical-decision-ladder]] 의 식립 경로 결정 근거 보강을 위해 인제스트.

## One-line Summary

SR (42 studies, level I–II): intentionally tilted implants show no difference in survival, marginal bone loss, prosthesis survival, or PROMs versus axially placed implants in edentulous jaws.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰 (42편): 무치악 악궁에서 경사 임플란트(tilted implant)는 수직 임플란트(axial implant)와 생존율·변연골 소실·보철 생존·환자보고결과 모두 유의한 차이 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Implants Research (Suppl 16)
- **Study type**: Systematic review (level I–II evidence)
- **Population**: Edentulous jaws (full arch)
- **Databases**: 5 electronic databases + manual review
- **Included studies**: 42 articles (2 level I, 20 level II + 15 level IV, 1 gray lit, 4 prior SR+MA)

## 2. Key Contributions
- First major SR directly comparing tilted vs axial implants in edentulous jaws
- Descriptive analysis (heterogeneity prevented quantitative synthesis)
- Only one level II study directly compared tilted vs axial implants head-to-head
- Establishes tilted implant as evidence-based alternative to axial placement

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PICO-based search strategy across 5 electronic databases
- Oxford Centre for Evidence-based Medicine levels (March 2009)
- Data extracted from level I and II studies only
- 811 articles identified → 46 full-text review → 42 included

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **Implant survival**: No difference between tilted vs axial
- **Marginal bone loss**: No difference
- **Prosthesis survival**: No difference
- **PROMs**: No difference
- Tilted implants placed with intentional inclination toward the **distal aspect** of edentulous jaws
- Heterogeneity prevented meta-analysis

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneity of included studies prevented quantitative pooling
- Only one level II study directly compared tilted vs axial
- Long-term data limited; majority of evidence from observational studies

## 6. Related Work
- All-on-4 concept (Maló): the clinical origin of distal tilting
- Del Fabbro 2014 SR+MA: marginal bone around axial vs tilted

## 7. Glossary
- **Tilted implant (경사 임플란트)**: implant placed with the coronal aspect inclined toward the distal aspect of the edentulous jaw
- **Axial implant (수직 임플란트)**: implant placed parallel to the long axis of the alveolar ridge
- **PROMs**: Patient-Reported Outcome Measures
