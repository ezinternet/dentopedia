## Why Ingested

연조질 블록형 골대체재 (soft block bone substitute, BBS)를 이용한 GBR 시 L형 성형 후 느슨하게 적용하는 방식(LP)과 비성형 압축 적용 방식(CP)의 효과 차이가 임상적으로 쟁점이었으나 in vivo 비교 데이터가 없었음. 이 개 동물 실험은 해당 gap을 직접 채우며, micro-CT와 조직형태계측 일치도 데이터도 제공해 [[wiki/bone-regeneration/darby-2024-resorbable-membrane-dbbm-gbr-dehiscence]]와 [[wiki/bone-regeneration/friedmann-2022-horizontal-augmentation-gbr-dog]]의 맥락을 보완한다.

## One-line Summary

Dog RCT (n=4 dogs, 32 implants, 8 weeks): L-shaped loosely-packed vs compactly-packed soft BBS in GBR for peri-implant dehiscence showed no significant difference in bone width at any level; both failed to maintain coronal augmentation without membrane fixation; micro-CT overestimated bone width by 0.31 mm but correlated strongly with histomorphometry (r=0.971).

## 한줄요약

개 동물실험 (n=4마리, 32 임플란트, 8주): L형 느슨하게 적용 vs 비성형 압축 적용 연조질 블록형 골대체재(BBS) 간 골폭 차이 없음; 막 고정 없이는 두 군 모두 치관부 골유지 실패; 미세CT(micro-CT)는 조직형태계측보다 0.31mm 과대평가하나 상관성 강함(r=0.971).

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First in vivo comparison of LP (L-shaped, loosely packed) vs CP (non-trimmed, compactly packed) soft BBS application techniques in GBR for peri-implant dehiscence defects.
2. Demonstrated that packing technique (LP vs CP) does not significantly affect bone regeneration outcomes when membrane fixation is absent.
3. Validated strong agreement between micro-CT and histomorphometry for bone width assessment (r=0.971), supporting micro-CT as a reliable surrogate in pre-clinical GBR studies.
4. Quantified micro-CT overestimation bias: mean +0.31 mm (95% LoA −0.63 to +1.25 mm).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Study type**: Pre-clinical in vivo animal experiment (ARRIVE guidelines)
- **Animals**: 4 male mongrel dogs (25–35 kg)
- **Defect model**: Standardized box-shaped buccal defects (5×5 mm apico-coronally × bucco-palatally) created after extraction of P2, P3, distal root P4, M1 bilaterally; 4-week spontaneous healing before implant placement
- **Implants**: 32 Dentis implants (Ø4.0 mm, L11.5 mm); 2 per defect; 8 per jaw
- **Bone substitute**: Porcine deproteinized bone block with 10% collagen (DPBM-C, THE Graft Collagen, Purgo Biologics, 10×10×12 mm); same material in both groups
- **Membrane**: Non-cross-linked porcine resorbable collagen membrane (BioCover, Purgo Biologics, 30×40 mm); no fixation pins or screws in either group
- **LP group**: DPBM-C trimmed to L-shape, soaked in saline, passively placed without compression
- **CP group**: DPBM-C not trimmed, manually compacted to defect contour with gentle pressure
- **Sacrifice**: 8 weeks post-GBR
- **Micro-CT**: Skyscan 1173 (130 kV, 60 μA, 1.0 mm Al filter, 800 cross-sections); analysis in Mimics 26.0 and OnDemand3D
- **Histomorphometry**: Ground sections 40–70 μm, Goldner's trichrome; Pannoramic 250 Flash III scanner; CaseViewer 2.4
- **Measurements**: Augmented tissue width at implant shoulder and 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 mm apical
- **Statistics**: Mann–Whitney U; Bland–Altman analysis; Spearman correlation; ICC for intra-examiner reliability (ICC >0.90)

| Level | LP | CP |
|---|---|---|
| Implant shoulder | 0.00±0.00 | 0.00±0.00 |
| 1 mm apical | 0.14±0.32 | 0.06±0.24 |

No statistically significant differences at any level (P>0.05).

### Micro-CT vs histomorphometry correlation:
- Total: r=0.971 (95% CI 0.962–0.978, P<0.001)
- Mean bias (Bland–Altman): +0.31 mm (micro-CT overestimates)
- 95% limits of agreement: −0.63 to +1.25 mm

### Clinical outcomes:
- No wound dehiscence, membrane exposure, or infection in any site

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (n=4 dogs, n=16 implants per group); pilot study
- Single time point (8 weeks); long-term bone maturation not evaluated
- Box-shaped chronic defect model may not fully reflect clinical presentations
- No membrane fixation in either group — this was intentional to isolate compaction effect, but limits direct clinical translation (most clinicians may use fixation)
- Porcine DPBM-C material specific; results may not generalize to other BBS products
- Intra-examiner design may introduce bias despite high ICC
- Authors suggest future studies with membrane fixation to determine whether LP advantage emerges

## 7. Glossary

- **BBS (Block Bone Substitute, 블록형 골대체재)**: Soft, collagen-containing porcine block used as GBR graft material; retains shape better than particulate
- **LP (Loosely Packed, 느슨하게 적용)**: L-shaped trimmed BBS placed passively without compression
- **CP (Compactly Packed, 압축 적용)**: Non-trimmed BBS manually compressed into defect
- **DPBM-C (Deproteinized Porcine Bone Mineral with Collagen, 탈단백 돼지 골광물질 콜라겐)**: 10% collagen porcine bone block; THE Graft Collagen (Purgo Biologics)
- **NCCM (Non-Cross-linked Collagen Membrane, 비가교 콜라겐 차폐막)**: BioCover (Purgo Biologics); resorbable
- **Micro-CT (미세 컴퓨터 단층촬영)**: High-resolution 3D radiographic analysis for pre-clinical bone quantification
- **Bland–Altman analysis**: Method-comparison statistics showing mean bias and limits of agreement
- **GBR (Guided Bone Regeneration, 골유도재생)**: Barrier membrane technique to promote bone regeneration
