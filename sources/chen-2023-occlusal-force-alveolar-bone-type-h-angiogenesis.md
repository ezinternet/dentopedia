## Why Ingested

기존 [[occlusion/fan-2018-occlusal-trauma-and-excessive-occlusal]]·[[occlusion/di-fiore-2022-periimplant-bone-loss-overload-occlusal-analysis]]는 occlusal *과부하*가 골 손실을 일으키는 측을 다루는데, 그 반대 축 — occlusal *unloading*(저작 기능 상실)이 어떻게 치조골을 잃게 하는가(alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis, ABDO) — 의 분자 기전이 위키에 없었다. 본 논문은 ABDO를 PDL의 type H 혈관(CD31^high/EMCN^high) 감소 → osterix(OSX) osteoprogenitor 동반 감소로 설명하고, 기계감지 회로 PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3를 제시한다. 이는 [[bone-biology/kondo-2022-current-perspectives-residual-ridge]](발치 후 잔존 치조제 흡수)와 [[bone-biology/tokavanich-2025-control-alveolar-bone-development]](치조골 osteoblast 분자생물학)의 "치조골은 별개의 골생물학 단위" framing에 혈관-신생(angiogenesis-osteogenesis coupling) 축을 추가하는 보강 근거다. "occlusion → 치조골 밀도/항상성" 인과의 mechanistic 핵심 페이지.

## One-line Summary

Mouse/in-vitro mechanistic study (J Dent Res 2023) showing that loss of occlusal force triggers alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis (ABDO) by reducing periodontal-ligament type H vessels and coupled OSX osteoprogenitors via a PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 mechanosensory circuit; periodontal recombinant SLIT3 injection partially rescued type H vasculature and bone loss.

## 한줄요약

마우스·in-vitro 기전 연구(J Dent Res 2023) — 교합력 소실은 치주인대(PDL)의 type H 혈관과 동반 OSX 골전구세포를 감소시켜 치조골 폐용성 골다공증(ABDO)을 유발하며, 그 기계감지 회로는 PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3; 재조합 SLIT3 국소 주사가 type H 혈관과 골손실을 부분 회복시킴.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Defines a mechanism for alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis (ABDO) — bone loss after occlusal unloading — which was previously unexplained.
- Identifies type H endothelium (CD31^high / endomucin^high) as enriched in the periodontal ligament (PDL) but rare in alveolar bone (AB) itself; this PDL type H vasculature declines under occlusal hypofunction.
- Couples type H vessel loss to a parallel decline in osterix (OSX)^+ osteoprogenitors — i.e., angiogenesis–osteogenesis coupling operating in the periodontium.
- Establishes the PDL as the mechanotransduction hub translating occlusal force into vascular/osteogenic signaling.
- Validates a mechanosensory signaling circuit: PIEZO1 → intracellular Ca²⁺ → HIF-1α → SLIT3 (angiogenic ligand).
- Therapeutic proof of concept: periodontal injection of recombinant SLIT3 protein partially rescued type H vessel dysfunction and AB loss in ABDO mice — nominating SLIT3 as a candidate target to prevent disuse alveolar bone loss.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- In vivo: mouse model of occlusal hypofunction producing ABDO (occlusal unloading); analysis of PDL type H vessel density (CD31/EMCN immunostaining) and coupled OSX^+ osteoprogenitors in functional vs hypofunctional PDL.
- Molecular: assessment of SLIT3 (Slit guidance ligand 3) expression in disused PDL; rescue by periodontal injection of recombinant SLIT3 protein.
- In vitro: 3-dimensionally cultured PDL cells subjected to cyclic compression using the Flexcell FX-5000 compression system to validate the PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 axis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Type H endothelium (CD31^high/EMCN^high) localized to PDL, rarely to alveolar bone.
- In hypofunctional PDL: type H vessel density and coupled OSX^+ osteoprogenitor numbers both declined significantly.
- SLIT3 downregulated in disused PDL.
- Periodontal recombinant SLIT3 injection partially ameliorated type H vessel dysfunction and alveolar bone loss in ABDO mice.
- Cyclic compression of 3D PDL cells confirmed the PIEZO1/Ca²⁺/HIF-1α/SLIT3 mechanosensory circuit.
- (Numeric magnitudes / microCT BV/TV figures were in the paywalled full text, not in the retrieved abstract.)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Animal (mouse) model + in-vitro PDL cells — no direct human translation.
- SLIT3 rescue was *partial*, not complete restoration of bone.
- Quantitative outcomes not available in the retrieved abstract-only PDF.
- Therapeutic delivery of recombinant SLIT3 in humans is unvalidated.
- Future work: SLIT3-targeted therapy to prevent ABDO bone loss (e.g., after tooth loss, occlusal hypofunction, or in non-functional implant segments).

## 7. Glossary

- ABDO — alveolar bone disuse osteoporosis: net alveolar bone loss following occlusal unloading.
- Type H vessel — capillary subtype (CD31^high / endomucin^high) tightly coupled to osteogenesis.
- PDL — periodontal ligament.
- OSX (osterix) — transcription factor marking committed osteoprogenitors.
- SLIT3 — Slit guidance ligand 3, an angiogenic factor.
- PIEZO1 — mechanically activated cation channel (mechanosensor).
- HIF-1α — hypoxia-inducible factor 1-alpha.
