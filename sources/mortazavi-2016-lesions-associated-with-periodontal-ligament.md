## Why Ingested

PDL 확장(widening)은 교합외상의 대표적 영상 소견이지만, 악성종양·전신질환 등 위험 병변과도 겹친다. 본 review는 PDL widening을 일으키는 병변군을 영상의학적으로 감별 정리해, 교합외상 클러스터([[occlusion/fan-2018-occlusal-trauma-and-excessive-occlusal]])의 "위험 감별" 안전망을 제공한다. 교합 소견으로 단정하기 전 배제해야 할 병변 목록.

## One-line Summary
Radiographic review cataloguing the lesions that cause periodontal ligament (PDL) widening — ranging from benign (occlusal trauma, orthodontic force) to serious (osteosarcoma, non-Hodgkin lymphoma, scleroderma) — to guide differential diagnosis.

## 한줄요약
치주인대(PDL) 확장을 유발하는 병변군을 영상의학적으로 감별 정리한 review — 양성(교합외상·교정력)부터 중대(골육종·비호지킨림프종·경피증)까지 포괄.

## 1. Document Information
- Imaging Science in Dentistry 2016;46(4):229–237. Review. DOI 10.5624/isd.2016.46.4.229.
- Open Access (Korean Academy of Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Compiles a differential-diagnosis list of conditions producing PDL widening.
- Distinguishes benign/functional causes from malignant/systemic ones.
- Provides radiographic cues to avoid misattributing widening to occlusal trauma alone.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative/review search (Google Scholar, PubMed, PubMed Central, ScienceDirect) of lesions associated with PDL widening.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Normal PDL width ~0.15–0.21 mm (radiographic radiolucent space between lamina dura and root); narrows with age.
- PDL widening causes span: occlusal trauma, orthodontic movement, periodontitis, inflammatory periapical lesions, systemic disease (scleroderma), and malignancies (osteosarcoma, non-Hodgkin lymphoma, metastasis).
- Symmetric vs localized widening and associated bone changes help differentiate benign from malignant.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Narrative review; no diagnostic-accuracy data.
- Radiographic overlap means definitive diagnosis often needs biopsy.

## 6. Related Work
- 2017 World Workshop occlusal trauma diagnostic considerations (Fan 2018).
- Oral/maxillofacial malignancy imaging literature.

## 7. Glossary
- PDL widening: increased radiolucent periodontal-ligament space; a nonspecific sign with a broad differential.
- Lamina dura: radiopaque cortical bone lining the tooth socket; its integrity aids differential diagnosis.
