## One-line Summary
Case letter presenting the Five Thread Guideline (5-TG) — engaging ≥5 implant threads in native bone for primary stability prediction — illustrated through 3 immediate implant placement cases using CBCT virtual planning.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Oral Implantology, Vol. XLVI, No. 1, 2020, pp. 81-83
- **DOI**: 10.1563/aaid-joi-D-19-00039
- **Institution**: University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill; Stanley Dentistry, Cary, NC, USA
- **Type**: Case Letter (case series)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Proposes a new clinical guideline: the Five Thread Guideline (5-TG) — minimum 5 implant cylinder threads must engage native bone for primary stability in immediate or healed sites
- Introduces concept of "socket stabilization" — bi-socket (2 walls) or tri-socket (3 walls) contact as supplementary stability criteria
- Demonstrates CBCT virtual planning with bone rendering on/off to count engaging threads pre-surgically

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Design**: Case series (3 cases)
- **Tool**: SIMPLANT 17 (Dentsply Sirona) virtual planning software with CBCT + optical scan alignment
- **Workflow**: Teeth segmented separately; bone rendering toggled off to visualize implant-bone thread engagement
- **Cases**: 
  - Case 1: Tooth #28 (lower premolar), 3 apical threads + bi-socket → good prognosis
  - Case 2: Tooth #19 (lower molar), 5 apical threads + bi-socket → good prognosis
  - Case 3: Tooth #14 (upper molar), 5 apical threads + tri-socket → good prognosis
- All 3 cases: immediate extraction + immediate implant placement + bone grafting of gaps + healing abutment

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- All 3 cases achieved successful osseointegration with no complications
- ISQ measurements taken at 4-5 month follow-ups (specific values not reported in excerpt)
- Screw-retained crowns placed successfully in all cases
- 5-TG allowed pre-surgical prediction and patient communication about surgical success likelihood

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Only 3 cases — no control group, no comparative outcomes
- No long-term follow-up beyond initial crown placement
- ISQ values not specified
- Guideline is clinician-derived, not yet validated in prospective studies

## 7. Glossary
- **Five Thread Guideline (5-TG)**: Recommendation to engage ≥5 implant threads in native bone for primary stability
- **Primary stability**: Initial mechanical fixation of implant immediately after placement
- **Bi-socket stabilization**: Implant engaging bone in 2 socket walls (interradicular bone)
- **Tri-socket stabilization**: Implant engaging bone in 3 socket walls
- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient)**: 1-100 scale from resonance frequency analysis; >70 indicates high stability
