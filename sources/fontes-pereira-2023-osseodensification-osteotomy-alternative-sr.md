## One-line Summary
Systematic review (J Clin Med 2023) of OD vs conventional osteotomy: OD consistently increases primary implant stability (IT and ISQ) in most studies; greatest effect in low-density bone; evidence quality low to moderate.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Medicine 2023; 12(22):7046
- **Study type**: Systematic review

## 2. Key Contributions
- Systematic synthesis of OD clinical evidence vs conventional drilling
- Consistent finding: OD increases primary stability (IT) across studies
- ISQ advantage present but variable across studies
- Low-density bone: greatest benefit from OD
- Evidence quality: low to moderate — randomised studies needed
