## Why Ingested

신규 [[endodontics/shaping]] subcategory 첫 paper 중 하나. TruNatomy(작은 taper, n=20)·ProTaper GOLD(공격적 taper)·WaveOne GOLD(reciprocation)의 canal transportation을 동일 S-shaped block에서 직접 비교 — minimally-invasive endodontics vs conventional shaping 의사결정 근거. [[endodontics/anatomy/song-2008-cross-sectional-morphology-and-minimum-canal]]의 minimum dentin thickness 안전마진 논의와 직접 연결.

## One-line Summary
In vitro S-shaped resin block (n=60): TruNatomy < WaveOne GOLD < ProTaper GOLD 순으로 canal transportation 적음; TruNatomy shaping time 최단; ProTaper GOLD apical curve(2-3 mm) 위치 transportation 최대.

## 1. Document Information
- BMC Oral Health 21:533 (2021)

## 2. Key Contributions
- 세 file 시스템 동일 디자인 비교 (S-shaped)
- TruNatomy의 최소침습 우월성 정량 (3, 5 mm level transportation NS lower)
- Shaping time 비교 (TruNatomy < WaveOne GOLD < ProTaper GOLD, p<0.05)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 60 S-shaped resin blocks (n=20/group)
- Pre/post staining 이미지 superimposition
- 9 levels (1~9 mm from apex) transportation 측정
- Statistics: paired t-test (within-group), one-way ANOVA + Kruskal-Wallis (between-group), α=0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- TruNatomy: 3, 5 mm level transportation 유의 낮음 (p<0.05)
- ProTaper GOLD: 2, 3 mm (apical curve) 최대 transportation
- Resin removal: TruNatomy < others (모든 구간 p<0.05)
- Shaping time: TruNatomy < WaveOne GOLD < ProTaper GOLD (p<0.05)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Resin block — dentin hardness 미반영
- Operator(들) 숙련도 미보고
- S-shape 단일 형상 — 다른 곡률 일반화 한계
- 임상 outcome (success rate, post-op pain) 미측정
