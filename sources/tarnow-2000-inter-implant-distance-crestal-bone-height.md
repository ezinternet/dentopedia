## Why Ingested

임플란트 간격 3 mm 룰의 원조 논문. 인접 임플란트 간 수평 골소실 폭 (측방 골소실)과 임플란트 간격의 상호작용을 처음 정량화. [[implants/morales-schwarz-2025-1mm-interimplant-distance-10year-case]] 의 1 mm IID 케이스가 이 논문의 3 mm 권고를 기준점으로 인용.

## One-line Summary

Radiographic study (n=36 patients, 2 adjacent implants each): inter-implant crestal bone loss 0.45 mm when IID >3 mm vs 1.04 mm when IID ≤3 mm; lateral bone loss ~1.4 mm per side.

## 한줄요약

방사선 연구 (n=36명, 인접 임플란트 2개): 임플란트 간 치조정 골소실 0.45 mm (IID>3 mm) vs 1.04 mm (IID≤3 mm); 측방 골소실 ~1.4 mm.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Periodontology 2000;71(4):546-9
- **Study type**: Radiographic observational (retrospective)
- **n**: 36 patients with 2 adjacent implants each (72 implants total)
- **System**: External hex, bone-level implants (pre-platform-switching era)
- **Note**: Foundational paper that established the "3 mm IID" guideline

## 2. Key Contributions
- Demonstrated lateral component of bone loss (1.34–1.40 mm per implant)
- Quantified IID threshold: ≤3 mm → significantly more crestal bone loss
- Explained mechanism: when two lateral bone loss zones overlap (each ~1.4 mm), the interimplant bone is lost
- Clinical implication: papilla height correlates with bone crest height → IID <3 mm → no papilla

## 3. Methodology
- Radiographic measurements at 36 patients with 2 adjacent implants
- Lateral bone loss: measured from crest to implant surface (mesial/distal to interproximal site)
- Crestal bone loss: measured between adjacent implant tops

## 4. Key Results

| IID | Interimplant crestal bone loss |
|---|---|
| ≤3 mm | 1.04 mm |
| >3 mm | 0.45 mm |

- Lateral bone loss: 1.34 mm (mesial) / 1.40 mm (distal) from each implant shoulder
- **Mechanism**: two lateral loss zones (~1.4 mm each) = total 2.8 mm needed to prevent overlap → guideline of ≥3 mm

## 5. Limitations
- External hex implants only (no platform-switching, no internal conical)
- Single-center, no statistical power calculation reported
- Modern implant designs may perform differently

## 7. Glossary
- **IID (Inter-Implant Distance, 임플란트 간격)**: horizontal distance between adjacent implant platforms
- **Lateral bone loss (측방 골소실)**: horizontal bone resorption extending from implant shoulder outward
- **Platform-switching**: mismatch between implant platform and abutment diameter (shifts microgap inward) — NOT present in this older study
