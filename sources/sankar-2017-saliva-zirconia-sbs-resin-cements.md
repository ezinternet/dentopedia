## One-line Summary
Indian J Dent Res 2017: saliva-contaminated zirconia, 4 cleaning protocols × 2 cements — Ivoclean + air abrasion highest SBS; conventional cement lower than self-adhesive MDP cement.

## 1. Document Information
Journal: Indian Journal of Dental Research 2017
DOI: 10.4103/ijdr.IJDR_274_16

## 2. Key Results
- 4 groups: uncontaminated, water-cleaned, Ivoclean-cleaned, air-abraded
- 2 cement types: conventional resin vs self-adhesive (MDP-containing)
- Air abrasion: highest SBS in both cement groups
- Ivoclean: significantly better than water
- Self-adhesive MDP cement: higher SBS than conventional cement in most groups
