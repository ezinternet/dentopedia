## Why Ingested

발치 전 식립와 형성법(pre-extractive approach)이 하악 대구치 즉시식립 ISQ와 식립 위치에 미치는 영향을 직접 비교한 RCT가 부재했던 간극을 메운다. [[immediate-implant/bleyan-2021-molar-septum-expansion-osseodensification-immediate-implant]]의 골밀도화 프로토콜과 비교해 inter-radicular septum 활용 전략의 근거 폭을 넓히며, 하악 대구치 ISQ 임계값 근거로도 활용 가능.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 4 RCTs (82 implants) found pre-extractive inter-radicular site preparation for mandibular molar immediate implant placement yielded significantly higher ISQ at placement (MD +3.39) and near-perfect central positioning vs postextractive approach; ISQ advantage dissipated by 3–6 months.

## 한줄요약

4개 RCT(임플란트 82개)를 포함한 SR+MA로, 발치 전 식립와 형성(pre-extractive) 기법이 발치 후 형성에 비해 하악 대구치 즉시식립 시 식립 직후 임플란트 안정성 지수(ISQ, Implant Stability Quotient)를 유의하게 높이고(MD +3.39) 중심 위치 달성률이 월등함을 확인했으나, 3–6개월 시 차이는 소실됨.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First SR+MA exclusively comparing pre-extractive vs postextractive implant site preparation in mandibular molar sockets using only RCTs.
2. Demonstrated significantly higher ISQ at implant placement with pre-extractive technique (MD −3.39; 95% CI −4.20 to −2.59; p < .0001; I² = 0%).
3. Showed near-complete central positioning with pre-extractive approach (100% vs ~10% in postextractive group; RR 0.05; p = .002).
4. Marginal bone level advantage of pre-extractive approach was marginally significant only at 12 months.
5. Zero implant failures in both groups across 6–12 months of follow-up.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Study design**: SR+MA following PRISMA and Cochrane guidelines.

**PICOS**:
- Population: Adults ≥18 requiring mandibular molar extraction, immediate implant placement
- Intervention: Pre-extractive implant site preparation (drilling through retained roots before extraction)
- Comparison: Postextractive implant site preparation (conventional drilling after extraction)
- Outcomes: ISQ (primary), marginal bone level, implant position, implant failure rate
- Study design: RCTs only

**Inclusion criteria (patient-level)**:
- Wide bone septa, ≥6 mm bucco-lingual ridge width
- Minimum 5 mm between inferior alveolar nerve and root apices
- Intact buccal/lingual plates (≥1 mm buccal cortical thickness)
- No dehiscence/fenestrations, adequate keratinised tissue

**Exclusion criteria**: Systemic diseases affecting osseointegration, thin/tilted septa, acute infection, bruxism, chemotherapy/radiation history, bisphosphonate use, smoking.

**Surgical protocol (common to both groups)**:
- CBCT for preoperative planning
- Full mucoperiosteal flap, coronectomy prior to drilling
- Minimally traumatic root extraction (piezoelectric/ultrasonic in some studies)
- Implant placed at centre of inter-radicular bone, 1–2 mm apical to buccal crest
- GBR with deproteinised bovine bone mineral to fill residual gaps
- Second-stage surgery at 3–6 months; final restoration metal-ceramic or zirconia screw-retained crown

**Pre-extractive specifics**: Osteotomy drilled through middle of sectioned furcation area (roots intact), using roots as natural guide. Roots extracted minimally traumatically after osteotomy complete.

**ISQ measurement**: Resonance frequency analysis (Osstell Mentor) at placement, 3 months, 6 months.

**Statistical methods**: Random-effects meta-analysis; MD with 95% CI for continuous outcomes; RR for dichotomous; I² for heterogeneity (threshold I² > 60%); leave-one-out sensitivity analysis; GRADE for certainty.

**Risk of bias (RoB2)**: 3/4 studies high risk (mainly lack of outcome assessor blinding); 1/4 some concerns.

| ISQ | Placement | MD −3.39 | −4.20 to −2.59 | < .0001 | 0% |
| ISQ | 3 months | MD −1.90 | −4.63 to 0.83 | .17 | NS |
| ISQ | 6 months | MD −2.72 | −7.72 to 2.29 | .29 | 83% |
| MBL (bucco-lingual) | 6 months | MD −0.05 | −0.32 to 0.21 | .70 | 71% |
| MBL (bucco-lingual) | 12 months | MD −0.62 | −1.62 to −0.01 | .04 | — |
| Central position | — | RR 0.05 | 0.01–0.33 | .002 | 0% |
| Implant failure | — | 0/82 both groups | — | — | — |

**GRADE certainty**: Low (primary stability, positioning); Very low (MBL at 6 months, stability at 6 months).

**Included studies**: 4 RCTs — 3 from Egypt, 1 from Italy. All university-based, self-funded, parallel-group. n=82 total (41 per group).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

1. **Small evidence base**: Only 4 RCTs, mostly from Egypt — geographic and methodological homogeneity limits generalisability.
2. **High risk of bias**: 3/4 studies high risk (outcome assessor blinding absent); none pre-registered.
3. **Short follow-up**: ≤12 months — insufficient for long-term MBL and survival conclusions.
4. **Small sample sizes**: Zero failures likely reflect underpowering, not true absence.
5. **Significant heterogeneity** in 6-month ISQ (I²=83%) and 6-month MBL (I²=71%) — different implant systems and grafting materials likely contributors.
6. **Pre-extractive technique limitations**: Not suitable for periodontally compromised sockets with reduced inter-radicular bone or increased tooth mobility; risk of dentin debris and heat generation during drilling through dentin (use new drills recommended).
7. **Future needs**: Long-term RCTs with blinding, pre-registration, larger samples, CONSORT adherence, patient-reported outcomes.

## 6. Related Work

- Fugazzotto: Original description of inter-radicular bone engagement technique using bur at acute angle.
- Previous SR on molar immediate implant survival (1013 implants): pooled survival rate 99.0% over 12–133 months — consistent with zero failures in this review.
- Bleyan 2021: Osseodensification/molar septum expansion for immediate implant — alternative inter-radicular strategy.
- Studies confirming strong correlation between bone-to-implant contact and ISQ — supports mechanism for pre-extractive advantage.
- Alveolar ridge preservation in periodontally compromised sockets as alternative when pre-extractive not feasible.

## 7. Glossary

- **Pre-extractive site preparation**: Osteotomy drilling performed through the furcation of retained (not yet extracted) tooth roots, using root morphology as natural guide
- **Postextractive site preparation**: Conventional approach — osteotomy drilled into empty socket after root extraction
- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient, 임플란트 안정성 지수)**: 1–100 scale from resonance frequency analysis (RFA) reflecting implant-bone stiffness
- **ITV (Insertion Torque Value, 삽입 토크값)**: Rotational force at implant placement (not directly measured in this review but related concept)
- **RFA (Resonance Frequency Analysis, 공명주파수분석)**: Method of ISQ measurement via Osstell device
- **Inter-radicular bone/septum (치근간 중격)**: Bone between roots of multi-rooted teeth; key engagement zone for molar immediate implants
- **GBR (Guided Bone Regeneration, 골유도재생술)**: Barrier membrane + graft material to fill gap between implant and socket walls
- **MD (Mean Difference)**: Absolute difference in means between groups
- **RR (Relative Risk, 상대위험도)**: Ratio of event probability in two groups
- **GRADE**: System for rating certainty of evidence (high → very low)
