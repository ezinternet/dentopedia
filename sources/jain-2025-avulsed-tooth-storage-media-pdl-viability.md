## Why Ingested

탈구치 (Avulsed Tooth) 보관 배지 선택은 응급 임상 결정이며, HBSS가 부재할 때 대안 배지에 대한 근거가 필요하다. 이 체외 연구는 DNS (Dextrose Normal Saline)와 ORS가 HBSS와 동등한 PDL 세포 생존율을 보인다고 보고하여 [[dental-trauma/debrier-2020-storage-avulsed-tooth-prior]]의 기존 보관 배지 권고를 실용적 관점에서 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (n=62 extracted premolars) demonstrating that DNS (dextrose normal saline) and ORS maintain PDL cell viability equivalent to HBSS for avulsed tooth storage, offering practical alternatives when HBSS is unavailable.

## 한줄요약

발치된 소구치 62개 체외 실험 — 탈구치 (Avulsed Tooth) 보관 배지 비교에서 당포도당생리식염수 (Dextrose Normal Saline, DNS)와 경구수액 (Oral Rehydration Salt, ORS)가 한크평형염용액 (Hanks Balanced Salt Solution, HBSS)과 동등한 치주인대 (Periodontal Ligament, PDL) 세포 생존율을 보임; 과일주스는 가장 저조.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Annals of African Medicine, 2025;24(4):724–728
- **Institution**: People's Dental Academy, Bhopal, India
- **Study type**: In vitro experimental
- **PMCID**: PMC12588408

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Demonstrates that DNS (Dextrose Normal Saline 5%) and ORS are equivalent to HBSS in maintaining PDL cell viability — providing evidence for easily accessible emergency alternatives.
2. Confirms Monster energy drink as acceptable short-term storage (NS difference vs ORS/DNS) but significantly inferior to HBSS.
3. Documents that fruit juice (Frooti) is the poorest performer due to low pH and hypertonic osmolality.
4. Validates the 30-min dry time + 45-min storage model with trypan blue dye exclusion and hemocytometric counting.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Sample**: 62 freshly extracted human premolars, closed apices, normal periodontal health, patients <30 years. Coronal 3 mm of PDL scraped to remove potentially damaged cells.

**Groups**:

| Group | Medium | n |
|---|---|---|
| I | HBSS (Hanks Balanced Salt Solution) | 10 |
| II | DNS (5% Dextrose + 0.9% NaCl) | 10 |
| III | ORS (Oral Rehydration Salt) | 10 |
| IV | Monster energy drink | 10 |
| V | Frooti fruit juice | 10 |
| VI (positive control) | Immediate assay, no dry/storage | 6 |
| VII (negative control) | 8h dry, no storage medium | 6 |

**Protocol**: 30 min dry time → 45 min in storage medium (all experimental groups).

**PDL isolation**: PBS wash → collagenase type III + trypsin (10 min) → FBS to stop → centrifuge 90 rpm × 4 min.

**Viability**: Trypan blue dye exclusion 1:1 ratio; Neubauer's hemocytometer at 10× magnification.

**Statistics**: One-way ANOVA; Tukey's HSD post-hoc. p<0.05 significant.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Mean viable PDL cells (±SD):

| Group | Mean | SD |
|---|---|---|
| Positive control | 521.8 | 22.6 |
| HBSS | 341.8 | 19.9 |
| ORS | 320.0 | 23.4 |
| DNS | 316.2 | 22.7 |
| Energy drink | 298.1 | 15.0 |
| Fruit juice | 287.5 | 19.1 |
| Negative control | 1.0 | 1.7 |

ANOVA: F = 307.4, p < 0.0001.

**Key pairwise comparisons (Tukey's HSD)**:
- HBSS vs ORS: NS | HBSS vs DNS: NS | ORS vs DNS: NS
- HBSS vs Fruit juice: p<0.001 | DNS vs Fruit juice: p=0.023 | ORS vs Fruit juice: p=0.005
- DNS vs Energy drink: NS | ORS vs Energy drink: NS
- Energy drink vs Fruit juice: NS

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro setting; extracted premolars differ from actual clinical avulsion scenario
- No gene expression or histological assessment of cell viability
- Single dry-time protocol (30 min); longer delays not evaluated
- DNS/ORS effectiveness over extended storage periods (>45 min) not studied

## 6. Related Work

- American Association of Endodontists recommends HBSS as standard storage solution
- Hiltz & Trope: HBSS maintains 71.3% viability at 24h, 38% at 48h, 0% at 120h
- Souza: milk and HBSS more effective than other media for cell viability/proliferation
- Rajendran & Subramaniam: ORS equivalent to HBSS — consistent with present study
- Mahesh (conflicting): ORS showed minimum viability vs RL/coconut water/electrol

## 7. Glossary

- **PDL (Periodontal Ligament)**: 치주인대 — 치근과 치조골 사이의 결합조직
- **HBSS**: Hanks Balanced Salt Solution — 한크평형염용액; 세포 보존 표준 배지
- **DNS**: Dextrose Normal Saline — 5% 포도당 + 0.9% NaCl 혼합 수액
- **ORS**: Oral Rehydration Salt — 경구수액; 포도당과 전해질 혼합
- **Avulsion**: 탈구 (완전 탈구) — 치아가 치조와에서 완전히 이탈된 상태
- **Trypan blue dye exclusion**: 트리판 블루 색소 배제법 — 손상된 세포만 색소 흡수
- **Ankylosis**: 골유착 — 치근과 치조골이 직접 유착되는 비정상적 치유
