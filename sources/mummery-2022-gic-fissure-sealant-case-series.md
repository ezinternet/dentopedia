## Why Ingested
Real-world GIC fissure-sealant retention data for the preventive arm of the GIC cluster; supports [[glass-ionomer/mummery-2022-gic-fissure-sealant-case-series]].

## One-line Summary
Retrospective case series reporting ~77% GIC fissure-sealant retention in high-caries-risk paediatric patients awaiting GA.

## 한줄요약
GIC fissure sealant case series — 고우식위험 소아에서 평균 77% 유지율.

## 1. Document Information
BDJ Open 2022; 8: 25. DOI 10.1038/s41405-022-00119-3. Open access. Harrogate and District NHS Foundation Trust Community Dental Service, UK.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pandemic-driven service-evaluation data on GIC-FS (non-aerosol-generating alternative).
- Supports GIC-FS for partially erupted molars under moisture challenge.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Retrospective clinical review / case series, single community dental service. Outcome: clinical retention at GA appointment and follow-up.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Average GIC-FS retention ~77%, consistent with literature.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Single-service, non-comparative, retrospective; GIC-FS retention lower than resin-based sealants; comparative local audit suggested.

## 6. Related Work
Resin-based fissure sealant (RB-FS) guidelines; COVID-19 AGP restrictions.

## 7. Glossary
- GIC-FS: glass ionomer cement fissure sealant.
- RB-FS: resin-based fissure sealant.
- AGP: aerosol-generating procedure.
