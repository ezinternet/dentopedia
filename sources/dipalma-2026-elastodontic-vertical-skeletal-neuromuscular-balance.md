## Why Ingested

This paper adds the **vertical + neuromuscular (sEMG) axis** to the elastodontic/AMCOP® myofunctional cluster: it is the first page we hold pairing digital cephalometry with standardized surface electromyography (Teethan®) to document open-bite closure *and* masticatory-muscle rebalancing in growing patients. It reinforces and extends the sibling airway-functional treatment page [[wiki/orthodontics/myofunctional/inchingolo-2025-elastodontic-pharyngeal-airway-functional-treatment]] (same AMCOP® platform, functional-treatment lens) by quantifying the neuromuscular outcome the airway page only implies, and complements the sagittal-focused evidence in [[wiki/orthodontics/myofunctional/ureni-2024-elastodontic-devices-sagittal-malocclusion-scoping-review]].

## One-line Summary

Retrospective single-arm case series (n=9 cephalometric, n=6 sEMG subgroup; mean age 9.8 yr) of AMCOP® "OPEN" elastodontic therapy for anterior open bite: overbite rose 0.1→2.2 mm, SN-GoGn/PP-MP fell 2.8°±1.9°, and Teethan® neuromuscular efficiency (IMP) increased ~40% — but no control group, so findings are hypothesis-generating.

## 한줄요약

성장기 전치부 개교합(anterior open bite) 환자에서 AMCOP® "OPEN" 탄성교정장치(elastodontic appliance) 치료의 후향적 단일군 증례군(cephalometric n=9, 표면근전도 n=6, 평균 9.8세): 수직피개(overbite)가 0.1→2.2 mm로 증가하고 SN-GoGn/PP-MP 각이 2.8°±1.9° 감소했으며, Teethan® 신경근 효율 지수(IMP)가 약 40% 상승 — 그러나 대조군이 없어 가설 생성 수준.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **First integrated cephalometry + sEMG documentation** of elastodontic (AMCOP®) therapy in growing patients — most prior elastodontic studies report only dental/skeletal outcomes, and sEMG work was mostly in adults or isolated functional protocols.
2. Pairs **digital cephalometry (Deltadent®)** for vertical/sagittal skeletal change with **standardized surface EMG (Teethan®)** of anterior temporalis + masseter, correlating structural correction with functional muscle rebalancing.
3. Documents a **staged clinical protocol**: AMCOP® "OPEN" (~9 months, to recover transverse/vertical corridors) followed by a Class-specific device for sagittal refinement; total ~16 months; worn 1 h/day + passively overnight, no separate myofunctional exercises (swallowing itself supplies activation).
4. Argues for elastodontic therapy as **early interceptive treatment** combining orthopedic guidance with neuromuscular rehabilitation, targeting the underlying neuromuscular imbalance rather than only symptomatic open-bite camouflage (relapse risk).
5. Transparent about its own evidentiary weight — explicitly framed as a **preliminary pilot**, hypothesis-generating, no causal inference.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Sample**: 9 consecutive growing patients (5 F, 4 M; mean age 9.8 ± 1.4 yr) in deciduous/mixed dentition with anterior open bite (overbite ≤ 0 mm). sEMG successfully acquired in **6/9 (66.7%)** — 3 children failed reliable signal acquisition (technical/compliance). Cephalometric outcomes refer to n=9; neuromuscular results to the n=6 subgroup.
- **Inclusion**: anterior open bite documented clinically + on T0 cephalogram; AMCOP® "OPEN" as primary modality; standardized T0/T1 lateral cephalograms; no adjunctive fixed appliances.
- **Exclusion**: prior ortho/orthopedic treatment affecting vertical dimension; craniofacial syndromes/cleft/systemic growth conditions; adeno-tonsillar surgery in interval; poor-quality radiographs.
- **Intervention**: AMCOP® "OPEN" elastodontic bioactivator (Micerium S.p.A.) — posteriorly raised occlusal plane + elastic flanges to promote mandibular counterclockwise rotation and anterior bite closure. Mean treatment duration **14 ± 2 months** (per-case 10–14 mo).
- **Cephalometry**: Deltadent® 2.5.3 digital tracing; T0 (pre) and T1 (post). Primary outcomes: overbite (mm), SN-GoGn (°), PP-MP (ANS-PNS^Go-Gn, °), ANS-Me (mm). Secondary: FMA, interincisal angle, U1-PP, L1-MP, overjet.
- **Reliability**: single calibrated examiner; 20% of radiographs retraced ≥7 days later; ICC (two-way mixed, absolute agreement; threshold ≥0.80 accepted) and Dahlberg's error (≤0.5 mm / ≤0.5° acceptable).
- **sEMG**: portable Teethan® system; bilateral anterior temporalis + masseter per SENIAM; two MVCs on cotton rolls for normalization then two MVCs in intercuspal position (5 s each, 30 s rest). Indices: **POC TA/MM, BAR (barycenter), TORS (torsion), ASIM (asymmetry), IMP (impact/performance)** (plus CL noted).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Pooled cephalometric outcomes (n=9; all completed therapy, mean 14 ± 2 mo, excellent compliance, no adverse events):

- **Overbite**: mean 0.1 mm → **2.2 mm** (anterior open-bite closure).
- **SN-GoGn / PP-MP**: mean reduction **2.8° ± 1.9°** (improved vertical control, slight counterclockwise mandibular rotation).
- **ANS-Me** (lower anterior facial height): **stable** (balanced lower facial height).
- **ANB**: normalization toward Class I (reported as ≈ −3.2° mean change).
- **U1-PP** decreased and **interincisal angle increased** (better incisal guidance); **overjet** reduced in most cases.

Neuromuscular (sEMG, n=6 subgroup):

- **BAR** (barycenter): shifted toward midline / anterior alignment (normalization).
- **TORS** (torsion) and **ASIM** (asymmetry): **decreased**.
- **IMP** (overall performance/impact index): rose by an average of **~40%**.
- Qualitative per-case sEMG: shift from posterior barycenter, unilateral (left) temporalis prevalence, torsional imbalance, low global activity → centered barycenter, balanced temporalis/masseter activation, reduced asymmetry/torsion, higher muscular efficiency.

Illustrative per-case vertical changes (open-bite cases): Case 6 SN-GoGn 42.4°→36.4° and intermaxillary angle 30.5°→21.9° (hyperdivergent→mesofacial, full open-bite closure); Case 9 SN-GoGn 44.7°→38.0° with overbite/incisor normalization. (Individual case tables show heterogeneous, sometimes inconsistent values — see Limitations.)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **No control group** — the dominant limitation; cannot attribute changes exclusively to AMCOP® vs physiological craniofacial growth or spontaneous functional adaptation in a growing sample.
- **Retrospective single-arm pilot, small n** (9 cephalometric / 6 sEMG); findings explicitly hypothesis-generating, no causal inference.
- **sEMG attrition**: only 6/9 yielded reliable recordings.
- Several per-case cephalometric tables contain **internally inconsistent values** (the authors flag some, e.g., Case 6 IS∧AII/Wits post values, as possibly needing re-measurement), and per-case "improvement" direction is not uniform.
- Single calibrated examiner; no inter-examiner reliability.
- Future work: prospective controlled trials, larger samples, long-term follow-up to isolate treatment effect from growth and confirm stability.

## 7. Glossary

- **AMCOP®** — Apparecchi Modulari Customizzati Ortopedici Posturali (Cranio-Occlusal-Postural Multifunctional Harmonizers); removable, modular elastodontic bioactivators (Micerium S.p.A.).
- **Elastodontic appliance** — prefabricated elastomeric removable functional/orthopedic device acting on teeth, bone, and musculature.
- **Anterior open bite** — lack of vertical overlap (overbite ≤ 0 mm) between maxillary and mandibular incisors in occlusion.
- **sEMG (surface electromyography)** — non-invasive recording of muscle electrical activity via skin electrodes.
- **Teethan®** — standardized portable sEMG system computing masticatory-muscle indices.
- **SN-GoGn** — mandibular plane angle relative to the sella-nasion plane (vertical divergence).
- **PP-MP (ANS-PNS^Go-Gn)** — intermaxillary/palatal-to-mandibular plane angle.
- **FMA** — Frankfort-mandibular plane angle.
- **ANS-Me** — lower anterior facial height.
- **BAR / TORS / ASIM / IMP / POC TA-MM** — Teethan® indices for barycenter, torsion, asymmetry, overall impact/efficiency, and percentage overlap coefficient of temporalis/masseter.
- **MVC** — maximum voluntary clench (used for sEMG normalization).
