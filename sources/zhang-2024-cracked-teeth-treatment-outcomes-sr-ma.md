## One-line Summary

Abstract-only SR+MA (27 studies, 26 pooled): for cracked teeth, direct restorations without cuspal coverage on vital teeth raise pulpal-complication risk (RR 3.2) and extraction risk (RR 8.1) vs full crowns, and endodontically treated cracked teeth without full crowns have 11.3× higher extraction risk — full-crown restoration strongly recommended for symptomatic/RCT cracked teeth, while asymptomatic teeth may be monitored.

## 한줄요약

abstract-only SR+MA(27편, 26편 pooled): 균열치에서 생활치 direct 수복(cuspal coverage 없음)은 full-crown 대비 치수합병증 RR 3.2·발치 RR 8.1, 근관치료 균열치는 full-crown 없으면 발치 위험 11.3배 → 증상성·근관치료 균열치엔 full-crown 강력 권고, 무증상은 monitoring 가능.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Largest CTS treatment-outcome SR+MA (27 studies) covering vital (CT-VDP) and root-canal-treated (CT-RCT) cracked teeth.
- Quantifies the cuspal-coverage benefit: full crown vs direct restoration risk ratios.
- Supports monitoring for asymptomatic cracked teeth.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Medline/Embase/PubMed/Cochrane search; tooth survival rate (TSR), pulp survival rate (PSR), success rate (SR) with ≥1-yr follow-up; Newcastle-Ottawa risk of bias. 27 studies qualitative, 26 in meta-analysis. (Abstract-level only.)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Monitoring without restoration: SR 80% at 3 yr (asymptomatic CT).
- **CT-VDP**: TSR 92.8–97.8% (1–6 yr), PSR 85.6–90.4% (1–3 yr), SR 80.6–89.9% (1–3 yr).
- **CT-RCT**: TSR 90.5–91.1% (1–2 yr), SR 83.0–91.2% (1–4 yr).
- Direct restoration **without cuspal coverage** (CT-VDP): pulpal complications **RR 3.2 (95% CI 1.51–6.82, p=0.002)**, extraction **RR 8.1 (1.05–62.5, p=0.045)** vs full crown.
- CT-RCT without full crown: **11.3× higher extraction risk** (p<0.001).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Observational evidence (Newcastle-Ottawa); heterogeneity across protocols.
- Abstract-only: per-study weights and full forest plots not extractable.

## 7. Glossary

- **CT-VDP / CT-RCT**: cracked tooth with vital dental pulp / cracked tooth undergoing root canal treatment.
- **Cuspal coverage**: restoration that overlays cusps (onlay/crown) to splint the crack.
- **TSR / PSR / SR**: tooth survival / pulp survival / success rate.
