## One-line Summary
SR+MA (11 RCT, n=701: IIP 353 vs ARP 348; PROSPERO CRD42024503989): 즉시식립(IIP) vs 치조제 보존 후 지연식립(ARP) — IIP가 MBL 유의하게 더 큰 감소 (non-molar MD −0.36 mm, molar MD −0.41 mm); PES·implant failure·HW·FGM·만족도 동등; IIP 술후 합병증 더 많음.

## 2. Key Contributions
SR+MA per PRISMA. PubMed/Embase/Cochrane/WoS/Scopus search of RCTs comparing IIP vs ARP. Primary: MBL, PES, implant failure. Secondary: postoperative complications, HW change, FGM change, patient satisfaction. Cochrane RoB2 used.

## 3. Methodology
PROSPERO CRD42024503989. RoB2 for risk-of-bias. Mean differences (MD), odds ratios (OR) with 95% CI. Subgrouping by molar vs non-molar site.

## 4. Key Results
- 11 RCTs included; 353 IIP vs 348 ARP patients.
- MBL: IIP significantly greater reduction — non-molar MD −0.36 mm (95% CI −0.64 to 0.07), molar MD −0.41 mm (95% CI −0.49 to −0.32).
- PES: no difference (MD −0.05, 95% CI −0.52 to 0.43).
- Implant failure: no difference (OR 2.09, 95% CI 0.98–4.44).
- HW change, FGM change, patient satisfaction: no difference.
- Postoperative complications: significantly higher in IIP (p<0.05).

## 5. Clinical Relevance
IIP는 ARP 대비 심미 결과와 implant success 동등하나 MBL 감소 더 크고 합병증 비율 높음. 결정은 case-specific (시간·비용·환자 선호 vs MBL/합병증 trade-off). 추가 장기 RCT 필요.
