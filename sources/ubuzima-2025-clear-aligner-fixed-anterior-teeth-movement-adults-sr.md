## One-line Summary

Qualitative SR (GRADE, no meta) of 12 studies (2 low-RoB RCTs, 10 moderate-RoB non-RCTs) on anterior tooth movement in adults: vertical movement (extrusion/intrusion) inconsistent between CA and FA, while FAs appear superior for rotation and torque control; limited evidence from few studies.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Decomposes CA-vs-FA anterior tooth movement by movement type (translation, extrusion, intrusion, tipping, rotation, torque) rather than as a single efficacy verdict.
- Substantiates the umbrella-level claim of CA inferiority for rotation and torque control with a study-by-study tally.
- Identifies vertical movement (extrusion/intrusion) as the inconsistent / unresolved domain.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PubMed, Cochrane Library, Science Direct, Web of Science, Scopus, Google Scholar to September 2024. Randomized and non-randomized clinical trials eligible. RoB via Cochrane RCT and non-RCT tools; evidence quality via GRADE. 12 studies included (2 RCTs low RoB, 10 non-RCTs moderate RoB). Qualitative synthesis only — no meta-analysis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Buccolingual crown translation**: 2 studies favor FA, 2 favor CA, 2 no significant difference.
- **Mesiodistal translation**: equal between FA and CA (2 studies).
- **Extrusion**: inconsistent — 3 studies favor CA, 2 favor FA.
- **Intrusion**: equally effective — 1 study each.
- **Tipping**: mixed — 1 study each + 2 no significant difference.
- **Torque control**: better with FA in 1 study; 1 study no significant difference.
- **Bottom line**: vertical OMAT inconsistent; FAs appear superior for rotation and torque control; limited evidence from a small number of studies.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small number of studies (12); only 2 RCTs, the other 10 non-RCTs at moderate RoB.
- Qualitative tally only — no meta-analysis, so effect sizes not pooled.
- Movement-type subsets each rest on 1–3 studies (low power per comparison). Abstract-only ingest.

## 7. Glossary

- **OMAT**: orthodontic movement of the anterior teeth.
- **CA / FA**: clear aligners / fixed appliances.
- **GRADE**: Grading of Recommendations, Assessment, Development and Evaluation — evidence-quality tool.
- **Torque**: third-order (buccolingual root) movement; **tipping**: crown tilting without bodily movement; **translation**: bodily movement.
