## Why Ingested

기존 [[oral-medicine/manfredini-2021-recurrent-aphthous-stomatitis-treatment]]가 재발성 아프타성 구내염(Recurrent Aphthous Stomatitis, RAS)의 단계적 치료(국소 우선 → 전신)를 다루지만 탈리도마이드(thalidomide)의 효능·안전성 근거를 정량적으로 다루지 않았다. 본 SR+MA(21 RCT, 1668명)는 국소제 무반응 major RAS에 대한 전신 2차 약제로서 탈리도마이드의 위치를 evidence-weight로 보강하며, 동시에 부작용(말초신경병증·혈전색전) 증가 신호를 정량화한다. [[oral-medicine/belenguer-guallar-2014-recurrent-aphthous-stomatitis-review]]·[[oral-medicine/gasmi-2021-oral-aphthous-pathophysiology-treatment]]의 치료 알고리즘과 짝을 이룸.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 21 RCTs (n=1668): thalidomide significantly raised complete response (vs placebo RR 6.95; vs other drugs RR 2.18), prolonged recurrence interval (MD +26.91 d), accelerated healing and reduced ulcer number/size and TNF-α, but no pain (VAS) benefit and significantly more adverse events (RR 1.87) — best reserved for major or topical-refractory RAS.

## 한줄요약

21개 RCT(1668명) SR+MA: 탈리도마이드는 완전반응률(위약 대비 RR 6.95, 타약물 대비 RR 2.18)을 유의하게 높이고 재발간격(+26.91일)·치유속도·궤양 수/크기·TNF-α를 개선했으나 통증(VAS)은 개선 못 했고 부작용은 유의하게 증가(RR 1.87) — major RAS 또는 국소제 무반응 사례에 한정 권고.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First systematic evaluation of thalidomide efficacy and safety for RAS, pooling 21 RCTs (1668 patients) across English and Chinese databases (15 Chinese / 6 English publications; 17 of 21 trials from China).
- Quantifies a large complete-response benefit but explicitly weighs it against a significant rise in adverse events, supporting thalidomide as a reserve agent (major RAS or topical-refractory cases), not first-line.
- Provides a dosing synthesis converging on 25 mg/d as the efficacy-safety sweet spot.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Databases (inception–Dec 2023): Cochrane, PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Embase, CNKI, CBM, Wanfang, VIP. Search: (thalidomide OR sedoval) AND RAS terms.
- PICOS: RAS patients (including AIDS/Behçet/acute leukaemia comorbidity); oral thalidomide alone or combined; comparator placebo or any non-thalidomide intervention; RCTs/CCTs.
- Primary outcomes: complete response, overall response (= complete + partial). Secondary: recurrence interval (RI), ulcer number, healing time, VAS, ulcer diameter, immunological data, adverse events.
- Three comparison subgroups: (a) thalidomide vs placebo, (b) thalidomide vs other drug, (c) thalidomide + other drug vs other drug alone.
- Cochrane RoB tool; RevMan 5.4; RR (Mantel-Haenszel) for dichotomous, MD for continuous; I² >50% → random-effects.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Complete response (all significant):**
- vs placebo (3 studies): RR 6.95 (95% CI 3.04–15.92), I²=39%, p<0.00001.
- vs other drug (8 trials): RR 2.18 (1.38–3.45), I²=68%, p=0.008.
- combination vs other drug alone (10 trials): RR 2.44 (1.55–3.84), I²=59%, p=0.0001.

**Overall response:**
- vs placebo (1 study): RR 3.59 (1.87–6.89).
- vs other drug (7 studies): RR 1.18 (0.99–1.41), I²=80%, p=0.07 — NOT significant.
- combination vs other drug alone (10 studies): RR 1.32 (1.23–1.42), I²=0%, p<0.00001 — significant.

**Secondary:**
- Recurrence interval (3 studies): MD +26.91 d (2.29–51.54), p=0.03, I²=99% — prolonged.
- Healing time (4 studies): MD −1.31 d (−2.06 to −0.56), p=0.0006, I²=70% — faster.
- Ulcer number (4 studies): MD −0.98 (−1.87 to −0.08), p=0.03, I²=91% — reduced.
- Ulcer size/diameter (2 RCTs): MD −0.85 (−1.02 to −0.68), p<0.00001, I²=4% — reduced.
- VAS pain (3 studies): MD −0.64 (−1.75 to 0.47), p=0.26, I²=91% — NO benefit.
- Immunology: 3 of 4 TNF-α studies showed reduction (1 unexpected increase; meta-analysis not feasible); thalidomide+cimetidine lowered IgA/IgG/IgM.

**Adverse events:** thalidomide-containing regimens RR 1.87 (1.17–2.99), p=0.009, I²=36%. Withdrawal due to AEs: 12 (thalidomide) vs 1 (control). AEs: peripheral neurotoxicity, thrombosis/embolism, constipation, somnolence, dizziness, rash. (Severe-comorbidity patients excluded from AE analysis.)

**Dosing:** Early 200 mg/d (Jacobson, David) → high AE incidence. 25 mg/d (Deng) gave long-term RI prolongation with better safety; 50 mg/d not superior to 25 mg/d as maintenance (Wei). Authors favor 25 mg/d; stop immediately on peripheral-neuropathy symptoms.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Low methodological quality of most trials (poor blinding / allocation-concealment reporting → high RoB).
- Considerable clinical heterogeneity (RAS type, underlying disease, dose, comparators, outcomes, duration, follow-up) — several pooled estimates with I² up to 91–99%.
- Most trials (17/21) conducted in China → limited generalizability.
- Thalidomide absolutely contraindicated in pregnancy/women planning conception.
- Future: large multicenter RCTs; explore every-other-day or twice-weekly low-dose regimens.

## 7. Glossary

- **RAS** — Recurrent Aphthous Stomatitis (재발성 아프타성 구내염); minor / major / herpetiform subtypes.
- **Complete response** — full resolution of ulcers/symptoms; **Overall response** = complete + partial response.
- **Recurrence interval (RI)** — ulcer-free time between episodes (days).
- **TNF-α** — Tumor Necrosis Factor alpha; major Th1 inflammatory mediator targeted by thalidomide (via NF-κB blockade, MyD88 downregulation, mRNA degradation).
- **VAS** — Visual Analogue Scale for pain.
- **RR / MD** — Risk Ratio / Mean Difference (meta-analysis effect sizes).
