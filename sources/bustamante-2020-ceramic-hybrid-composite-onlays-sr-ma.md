## One-line Summary
후방부 온레이(Onlay) 재료별 임상성적 SR+MA (IJERPH 2020): 29편 정성·27편 정량 분석; 전체 생존율 94.2%; 복합레진 온레이가 세라믹 대비 생존율 낮음; 추적기간과 재료가 주요 예측인자.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health 2020, 17, 7582
- **DOI**: 10.3390/ijerph17207582
- **Published**: 19 October 2020
- **Study type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis (PRISMA)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Compared clinical behavior of three onlay material categories: (1) ceramic (lithium disilicate, feldspathic, leucite-reinforced), (2) hybrid materials (resin-ceramic/nanoceramic), (3) composite resin
- Databases: PubMed, Scopus, Embase, Cochrane; no publication date or language restrictions
- 29 articles for qualitative analysis; 27 for quantitative meta-analysis
- **Overall weighted mean survival rate: 94.2%**
- Composite resin onlays: significantly lower survival than ceramic onlays
- Follow-up duration and material type are significant predictors of restoration success

## 3. Methodology
- **Databases**: PubMed, Scopus, Embase, Cochrane Library
- **Restrictions**: None (no language/date limit)
- **PICO**: Patients requiring indirect posterior restoration; ceramic/hybrid/composite onlay; clinical survival and complications
- **Outcomes**: Survival rate, material-related complications, factors influencing success
- **Analysis**: Random-effects meta-analysis; subgroup by material type

## 4. Key Results
| Material | Survival Rate | vs Ceramic |
|---|---|---|
| Ceramic (LD, feldspathic, leucite) | Higher | Reference |
| Hybrid (resin-ceramic/nanoceramic) | Intermediate | — |
| Composite resin | Lower (statistically significant) | Inferior |
| **Overall** | **~94.2%** | — |

- Factors associated with lower survival: longer follow-up, composite material, larger cavity size
- Main complications: chipping/fracture (ceramic), secondary caries (composite)

## 5. Limitations
- High heterogeneity between studies in follow-up duration, preparation design, and adhesive protocols
- Older composite generations may inflate composite failure rates
- Hybrid materials have limited long-term data (newer category)
