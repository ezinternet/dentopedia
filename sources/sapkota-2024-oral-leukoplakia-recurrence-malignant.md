## Why Ingested
구강백반증 (Oral Leukoplakia, OL) 수술적 치료 후 재발률과 재발-악성변환 관계를 다루는 최신 SR+MA. 기존 [[oral-medicine/opmd/pimenta-barros-2025-leukoplakia-malignant-transformation-sr-ma]]가 MT 발생률에 초점을 맞춘 반면, 이 논문은 재발률과 수술 방법 비교에 특화되어 의사결정에 상호보완적.

## One-line Summary
SR+MA of 80 studies (n=7,614): pooled OL recurrence after surgery = 22%; laser excision+vaporization combination was the best surgical modality; recurrent OL carries 7.39× higher malignant transformation risk.

## 한줄요약
80개 연구 메타분석(n=7,614): 구강백반증 수술 후 재발률 22%; 레이저 절제+기화 복합술이 최적; 재발 병변은 비재발 대비 악성변환 위험 7.39배.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pooled OL recurrence proportion = 22% (95% CI reported) across all surgical modalities
- Network meta-analysis identified laser excision + vaporization combination as superior to scalpel, cryosurgery, or PDT alone
- Recurrent OL shows 7.39× higher risk of MT than non-recurrent OL
- Nonhomogeneous OL, retromolar/multiple sites, female sex, older age, inadequate margin, and betel quid chewing independently associated with higher recurrence
- Laser-based surgeries overall result in fewer recurrences than conventional scalpel surgery

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Study design: PRISMA-compliant systematic review + network meta-analysis
- Databases: EMBASE, PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science; all languages, inception to April 2024
- PICO: P=OL patients; I=surgical treatment; C=different surgical modalities; O=recurrence
- Surgical modalities analyzed: laser excision, laser vaporization/ablation, scalpel, cryosurgery, PDT, combination approaches
- Additional subgroup analyses: anatomical site, lesion size, clinical type, surgical margin, epithelial dysplasia grade, risk habits
- Meta-regression for age, sex, follow-up duration
- 80 studies included, total n=7,614

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pooled recurrence: 22%
- Laser excision + vaporization: best overall modality by network meta-analysis ranking
- Up to 49% recurrence for conventional scalpel at 5 years (literature range)
- Pooled recurrence for laser-based surgeries: 16.5% (de Pauli Paglioni 2020, cited for context)
- Recurrence risk factors: retromolar/multiple sites, NHOL, age >50, female, inadequate margin, retrospective data, betel quid
- Recurrent OL → MT: RR = 7.39 vs. non-recurrent

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneity across included studies (varying follow-up periods, OL classification criteria across time)
- Majority of studies retrospective cohort design
- Inconsistent reporting of surgical margins and dysplasia grading across studies
- Insufficient data to assess PDT combination protocols
