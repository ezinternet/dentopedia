## One-line Summary
Cureus 2023: Narrative review (Saudi Arabia): AI (ML/DL) in implant dentistry — diagnostics, treatment planning, deep learning image analysis for precision placement; data analytics for patient-specific strategies; AI promises personalized, efficient oral healthcare.

## 1. Key Results
- Narrative review; AI subfields: ML, DL, ANNs, robotics
- AI-driven image analysis and deep learning: enhance precision of implant placement
- AI-driven data analytics: patient-specific treatment strategies, improved success rates
- Key applications: implant site assessment, position planning, complication prediction
- AI promises personalized, efficient oral healthcare transformation

## 2. Clinical Implications
- AI image analysis can support implant position planning from CBCT/panoramic data
- Patient data integration enables risk stratification before treatment
- Limited standardization across AI models — clinical caution warranted
- Continued AI evolution expected to reshape implant dentistry workflow
