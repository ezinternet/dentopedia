## Why Ingested

Provides the highest-evidence anchor for OLP symptomatic treatment, complementing the comparative-cost SR [[oral-medicine/sandhu-2022-oral-lichen-planus-treatment-sr]] and the management review [[oral-medicine/srivastava-2022-oral-lichen-planus-diagnosis-management-review]].

## One-line Summary

Cochrane systematic review + meta-analysis (35 RCTs, 1474 patients): topical corticosteroids may resolve OLP pain better than placebo (RR 1.91, 95% CI 1.08-3.36, low certainty); topical tacrolimus may resolve pain better than clobetasol (very low certainty).

## 한줄요약

코크란 SR+MA(35 RCT, 1474명): 국소 코르티코스테로이드가 위약보다 OLP 통증 해소에 우월할 수 있음(RR 1.91, 95% CI 1.08-3.36, 낮은 근거), 타크롤리무스가 클로베타솔보다 통증 해소에 나을 수 있음(매우 낮은 근거).

## 1. Document Information

- Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews 2020, Issue 2, Art. No. CD001168.pub3. Update of the 2011 review, restricted to corticosteroids.
- Search to 25 Feb 2019. GRADE certainty assessment. No commercial funding.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Definitive Cochrane synthesis isolating corticosteroid evidence for OLP.
- Quantifies the modest, low-certainty benefit of topical corticosteroids over placebo for pain.
- Signals possible superiority of tacrolimus over clobetasol for pain resolution.
- Documents that clinical-resolution and adverse-effect evidence remain inconclusive.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- RCTs of any local/systemic corticosteroid vs placebo, calcineurin inhibitor, another corticosteroid, other drug, or corticosteroid+adjunct.
- Dichotomous outcomes as RR (95% CI); continuous as MD. Unit of analysis = participant. Meta-analysis only for comparable comparisons/outcomes.
- 35 studies / 1474 participants; 7 low, 11 unclear, 17 high risk of bias.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Corticosteroid vs placebo: pain resolution RR 1.91 (95% CI 1.08-3.36; 2 studies, 72 pts; low certainty). Clinical resolution RR 6.00 (95% CI 0.76-47.58; very low). Adverse effects RR 1.48 (very low).
- Corticosteroid vs calcineurin inhibitor (clobetasol vs tacrolimus): very-low-certainty signal favoring tacrolimus for pain resolution.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Few studies per comparison, small samples, high proportion of high-risk-of-bias trials.
- No malignant-transformation or long-term outcomes. Companion review on non-corticosteroid treatments pending.

## 6. Related Work

- Builds on the 2011 Cochrane OLP review.
- References calcineurin-inhibitor (tacrolimus/pimecrolimus) OLP trials.

## 7. Glossary

- OLP: Oral Lichen Planus.
- RR: Risk Ratio; MD: Mean Difference; CI: Confidence Interval.
- GRADE: Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation.
