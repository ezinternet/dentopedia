## Why Ingested
Materials-science basis for GIC mechanical-property improvement; supports [[glass-ionomer/moheet-2019-gic-powder-nanofiller-modifications-review]].

## One-line Summary
Narrative review of GIC-powder nano-filler modifications concluding nanoparticles generally improve physicomechanical properties.

## 한줄요약
GIC 분말 나노필러 개질 review — 나노입자가 물성을 대체로 향상.

## 1. Document Information
European Journal of Dentistry (Thieme) 2019; 13: 470–. DOI 10.1055/s-0039-1693524. Universiti Sains Malaysia; Lincoln University College.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Synthesizes nano-filler strategies addressing GIC's mechanical weaknesses.
- Design heuristic: select nanoparticles with matrix + tooth chemical affinity.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Narrative review of peer-reviewed studies/reviews 2000–2018 (EBSCOhost, PubMed, ScienceDirect); English only.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Nanoparticle GIC-powder modification commonly improves physicomechanical properties (↑surface area, surface energy, particle distribution); benefit not universal across filler types.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Narrative (non-systematic); pre-2019 coverage; in-vitro-dominated evidence; needs clinical validation.

## 6. Related Work
Nano-ceramic and nano-hydroxyapatite GIC modification studies.

## 7. Glossary
- Nano-filler: nanoscale particle added to GIC powder phase.
- Physicomechanical properties: combined physical + mechanical material behavior.
