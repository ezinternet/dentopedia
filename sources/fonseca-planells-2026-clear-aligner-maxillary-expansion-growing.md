## One-line Summary

SR+MA (15 studies, 7 pooled) in growing patients: clear aligners can achieve maxillary expansion but produce significantly LESS transverse gain than conventional expanders — intermolar (6-6) −1.77 mm, palatal volume −460.6 mm³, arch perimeter −1.75 mm in favor of conventional expanders; aligner expansion is mainly dentoalveolar (anterior, deciduous teeth), evidence mostly retrospective / moderate RoB.

## 한줄요약

성장기 환자 SR+MA(15편, 7편 pooled): 투명교정으로 상악 확장은 가능하나 conventional expander 대비 횡적 확장량이 유의하게 적음 — 구치간폭(6-6) −1.77 mm, 구개 용적 −460.6 mm³, 호선 둘레 −1.75 mm 모두 conventional 우위. aligner 확장은 주로 치조성(전치부·유치)이며 근거 대부분 후향적·중등도 RoB.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First wiki evidence on clear-aligner maxillary expansion; quantifies aligner vs conventional expander transverse outcomes in growing patients.
- Separates dentoalveolar from skeletal expansion contributions.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PubMed/Scopus/Embase/WoS/Cochrane search; retrospective + prospective studies + 1 RCT, published Jan 2021–Oct 2025, PICO-driven. Risk of bias: ROBINS-I (non-randomized) + RoB2. Heterogeneity I²; forest plots; Trim-and-Fill for publication bias. 267 screened → 15 included, 7 in meta-analysis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Statistically significant differences (clear aligner vs conventional expander; negative favors conventional):
- Intermolar distance (6-6): **−1.77 mm (95% CI −2.57, −0.97; p<0.0001)**.
- Total palatal volume: **−460.63 mm³ (−738.32, −182.94; p=0.0011)**.
- Arch perimeter: **−1.75 mm (−2.71, −0.80; p=0.0003)**.
- Aligners expand mainly via **dentoalveolar** changes, more anterior and at deciduous teeth.
- Conventional expanders achieve greater skeletal/posterior transverse change, especially in pronounced deficiency.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Most evidence retrospective, moderate risk of bias → cautious interpretation; prospective RCTs needed.
- Only 7 of 15 studies pooled; heterogeneity present.

## 7. Glossary

- **Clear aligner**: removable thermoplastic appliance series moving teeth incrementally.
- **Maxillary (transverse) expansion**: widening the maxillary arch; skeletal (sutural) vs dentoalveolar.
- **Invisalign First**: aligner system for growing/mixed-dentition patients.
- **ROBINS-I / RoB2**: risk-of-bias tools for non-randomized / randomized studies.
