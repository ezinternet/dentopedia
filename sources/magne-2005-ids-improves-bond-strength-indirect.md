## One-line Summary

JPD 94(6):511-519, Dec 2005 — Magne et al. (USC) in-vitro µTBS — 15 human molars, OptiBond FL (3-step E&R): IDS µTBS 58.25 MPa ≈ direct control 55.06 MPa (P>.05) >> DDS 11.58 MPa (P=.0081). SEM에서 hybrid layer 3–5 µm DDS는 disruption 관찰.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. IDS의 bond strength 우위를 정량적으로 처음 증명 — DDS 11.58 MPa → IDS 58.25 MPa (5배 차이).
2. IDS가 direct immediate bonding (control)과 통계적으로 동등함을 보임 — "IDS는 direct bonding과 동일한 substrate quality 회복".
3. SEM으로 hybrid layer integrity 차이 시각화 (IDS·C: well-organized 3–5 µm; DDS: marked disruption).
4. Film thickness 우려 제거 — "IDS sealant thickness가 indirect restoration seating을 방해하지 않음".

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 15 freshly extracted human molars → 3 groups × 5 teeth.
- DBA: OptiBond FL (3-step etch-and-rinse, filled adhesive).
- Group C (control): direct immediate bonding.
- Group DDS: indirect approach, cementation 시점 sealing.
- Group IDS: indirect approach, preparation 직후 sealing.
- µTBS test: 11 beams (0.9×0.9×11 mm) per tooth, 24h water storage.
- 분석: Kruskal-Wallis + Mann-Whitney U (α=.05). SEM 모드 분석.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Group | µTBS (MPa) | SEM hybrid layer |
|---|---|---|
| C (direct) | 55.06 | well-organized 3–5 µm |
| IDS | 58.25 | well-organized 3–5 µm |
| DDS | 11.58 | marked disruption |

- C vs IDS: not different (P>.05).
- DDS vs others: P=.0081.
- Failure mode: C·IDS는 mixed cohesive/interfacial; DDS는 대부분 interfacial (top of hybrid layer).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro, 15 molars만 (under-powered).
- OptiBond FL (3-step E&R) 한 종류만 → universal adhesive 일반화 불가.
- 24h water storage → long-term aging 미반영.
- Clinical outcome (POS·survival) 미평가 → [[resin-bonding/alghauli-2025-clinical-benefits-immediate-dentin-sealing]] · [[resin-bonding/josic-2022-ids-postoperative-sensitivity-sr-ma]] 후속 SR로 보강.

## 7. Glossary

- µTBS (Microtensile Bond Strength, 미세인장결합강도): 작은 단면 시편의 인장 결합력 측정.
- OptiBond FL: Kerr사의 3-step etch-and-rinse filled adhesive.
- Hybrid layer (혼성층): collagen matrix + 침투 resin의 복합층 (Nakabayashi 1982).
