## Why Ingested

"1회 vs 다회 방문" 근관치료(Root Canal Treatment, RCT) 논쟁은 임상에서 가장 자주 묻는 질문; umbrella SR로 기존 SR들의 메타분석을 통합. 기존 wiki에 단일 SR/RCT는 없고 이 umbrella review가 12개 SR을 종합해 현재 근거 수준을 가장 잘 요약한다. See [[overviews/single-vs-multivisit-endodontic-outcomes-overview]].

## One-line Summary

Umbrella systematic review of 12 SRs found no consistent clinically important difference in post-operative pain between single-visit and multiple-visit root canal treatment in permanent teeth, though confidence is limited by heterogeneity and primary-study overlap.

## 한줄요약

12개 체계적 문헌고찰을 종합한 umbrella SR에서 1회 방문과 다회 방문 근관치료 간 술후 통증에 임상적으로 유의한 차이가 없었으나, 이질성과 1차 연구 중복으로 근거 신뢰도가 제한됨.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First umbrella review to synthesize SR-level evidence specifically on post-endodontic pain outcomes for single- vs multiple-visit root canal treatment
- Included 12 SRs (with or without meta-analysis), assessed quality with AMSTAR 2 and risk of bias with ROBIS
- Examined overlap of primary studies across included SRs
- Conclusion: no consistent or clinically important pain difference between visit strategies in higher-confidence reviews

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- **Search**: Major bibliographic databases + grey literature, inception to December 2025
- **Eligibility**: SRs comparing single-visit vs multiple-visit endodontic treatment, reporting post-endodontic pain outcomes
- **Study selection/extraction**: Two independent reviewers
- **Quality appraisal**: AMSTAR 2 (methodological quality), ROBIS (risk of bias)
- **Overlap assessment**: Primary-study overlap across SRs was examined
- **Outcomes**: Post-operative pain intensity (VAS/NRS), pain incidence, flare-up rate

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- **n = 12 SRs** met inclusion criteria
- **Pain intensity**: assessed by VAS or NRS at prespecified intervals in most SRs; pain incidence reported less often
- **Flare-up**: reported inconsistently; definitions varied widely across SRs
- **Main finding**: higher-confidence SRs showed no consistent or clinically important difference in post-operative pain between single- and multiple-visit RCT
- **Confidence limitation**: heterogeneity in outcome definitions, pain measures, follow-up periods, and overlap among primary studies
- **Clinical recommendation**: visit strategy choice should be guided by case complexity, infection control, patient preference, and logistics — not by expected pain difference

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- Heterogeneity in pain outcome definitions and measurement tools across SRs
- Redundancy/overlap in the underlying primary study evidence base
- Future syntheses should standardize pain outcomes and assessment time points, address overlap transparently, apply more rigorous risk-of-bias methods

## 6. Related Work

- Single-visit vs multiple-visit RCT debate has been addressed in numerous individual SRs/RCTs; this umbrella review provides the highest-level synthesis to date
- AMSTAR 2 and ROBIS are standard appraisal tools for SRs in dental evidence synthesis

## 7. Glossary

- **Umbrella review (umbrella systematic review)**: A review of systematic reviews, synthesizing evidence at the SR level
- **AMSTAR 2**: A Critical Appraisal Tool for Systematic Reviews, assessing methodological quality
- **ROBIS**: Risk Of Bias In Systematic reviews tool
- **Flare-up (flare-up)**: Acute exacerbation of pain or swelling after endodontic treatment
- **VAS**: Visual Analogue Scale for pain intensity
- **NRS**: Numeric Rating Scale for pain intensity
- **Primary-study overlap**: The same original RCTs/studies appearing in multiple SRs, inflating apparent evidence volume
