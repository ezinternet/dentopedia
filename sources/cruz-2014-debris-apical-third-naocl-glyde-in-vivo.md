## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/irrigation/wong-2014-root-canal-lubricants-antibacterial-irrigants]]가 Glyde 항균성을 다룬 데 비해, 본 in vivo 연구는 Glyde paste 사용이 apical third debris 축적을 오히려 증가시킴을 보여 윤활제 사용의 trade-off를 제시.

## One-line Summary
In vivo study finding that using Glyde File Prep (EDTA paste) during rotary instrumentation increases debris accumulation in the apical third, whereas NaOCl with a final rinse left little/no apical debris.

## 한줄요약
In vivo: 회전 기구조작 중 Glyde File Prep(EDTA paste) 사용이 apical third debris 축적을 증가시킴 — NaOCl + 최종 세척군은 debris 거의 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Endodontics 2014;40(9):1419–1423.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Direct in vivo comparison of debris with vs without EDTA paste lubricant.
- Clinically warns that EDTA-paste lubrication can worsen apical debris.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- In vivo (teeth scheduled for extraction): Group A no Glyde; Group B Glyde during cleaning/shaping; NaOCl irrigation; histologic/microscopic apical-third debris assessment.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Group A: little or no apical-third debris.
- Group B (Glyde): favored debris accumulation in the apical third.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small in vivo sample; single paste; technique-sensitive.

## 6. Related Work
- In vivo counterpoint to the lubricant antibacterial study.

## 7. Glossary
- Glyde File Prep: EDTA + carbamide peroxide lubricating paste.
- Apical third: hardest canal region to clean.
