## One-line Summary

Case report: earrings produced real + typical ghost + pseudo-ghost (ghost-like real) images on panoramic; rotation center extending beyond the jaw explains the doubling.

## 한줄요약

증례보고: 귀걸이가 파노라마에 실상+전형적 허상+유사허상(ghost-like real)을 동시 형성. 회전중심이 악골 밖으로 확장되어 이중상 발생.

## 1. Document Information

- Study type: case-report

## 2. Key Contributions

- Distinguishes typical ghost vs pseudo-ghost (ghost-like real) images from bilateral earrings.
- Shows Planmeca ProMax center of rotation extending beyond the jaw, creating a double-image zone.
- Explains formation zones from X-ray beam path/angle, with dry-mandible simulation.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Case report: real + two ghost images per side analyzed by beam path/angle; radiopaque marker on dry-mandible condyle to simulate pseudo-ghost vs typical ghost.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Adds the 'pseudo-ghost' category to panoramic interpretation and ties it to scanner rotation geometry — refines chairside differentiation of artefacts from pathology.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Single case; device-specific (Planmeca ProMax); descriptive.
