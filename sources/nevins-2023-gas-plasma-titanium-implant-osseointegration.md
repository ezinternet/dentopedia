## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치(2026-05-31)의 large-animal(foxhound) 골유착 근거이자 "식립 직전 chairside gas plasma"라는 임상 적용 시나리오를 시점별(2·4·6주)로 검증. [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/hui-2020-cold-atmospheric-plasma-implantology-review]]의 chairside 주장을 실험으로 받친다.

## One-line Summary

Preclinical in vivo study: 36 implants in 6 male foxhounds (mandibular sites, sockets healed 42 days; randomized chairside gas-plasma test vs control, sacrificed at 2/4/6 weeks): BIC was higher for the plasma group at all timepoints and significantly higher at 4 weeks (p=0.046); radiographic bone-level difference reached significance at 6 weeks (p=0.016, more bone loss in control).

## 한줄요약

전임상 in vivo: foxhound 6마리에 임플란트 36개(하악, 발치와 42일 치유 후; 식립 직전 gas plasma 무작위배정 vs 대조, 2·4·6주 희생): 골-임플란트 접촉률(BIC)이 모든 시점에서 플라즈마군이 높았고 4주에 유의(p=0.046); 방사선 골수준 차이는 6주에 유의(p=0.016, 대조군 골소실 더 큼).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Bioengineering 2023, 10(10):1181
- DOI: 10.3390/bioengineering10101181
- Study type: preclinical in vivo (foxhound), randomized split-design, time-course (2/4/6 wk)
- Surface: sandblasted large-grit acid-etched (SLA)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Tests clinically realistic chairside gas plasma applied immediately before placement.
- Time-course (2/4/6 wk) histomorphometric + radiographic readout in a large animal.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 6 male mixed foxhounds; extraction of mandibular premolars and first molars; 42-day socket healing.
- 36 implants placed; mandibles randomized to plasma-treated (test) vs untreated (control).
- Sacrifice at 2 wk (2 animals), 4 wk (2), 6 wk (2). BIC (histomorphometry) and radiographic bone level.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- BIC higher for test group at all timepoints; significantly higher at 4 weeks (p=0.046).
- Radiographic bone-level change: NS at 2 and 4 wk; significant at 6 wk (p=0.016) — more bone loss baseline→6wk in control.
- Conclusion: nonthermal gas plasma has potential to enhance osseointegration.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small per-timepoint sample (2 animals each); animal model.
- Significance reached at single timepoints (BIC 4 wk; bone level 6 wk), not uniformly.
- No biomechanical (RTQ) or clinical/loading outcomes.
