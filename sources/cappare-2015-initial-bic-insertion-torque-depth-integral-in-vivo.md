식립 토크/깊이 적분이 초기 BIC를 in vivo로 예측하는지 검증한 연구로, [[implants/di-stefano-2018-undersizing-tapping-bic-primary-stability-bovine-histomorphometric]] 의 in-vitro 결과를 임상(in vivo)으로 확장하기 위해 인제스트. 기존 [[implants/isq/lages-2018-isq-insertion-torque-correlation-sr]] 의 IT-ISQ 상관 논의에 "토크 적분-BIC" 직접 측정 축을 추가.

## One-line Summary

In vivo study testing whether the insertion torque/depth integral recorded by an instantaneous torque-measuring motor correlates with initial bone-to-implant contact, supporting intra-operative bone-quality and primary-stability quantification.

## 한줄요약

in vivo 연구 — 순간 토크측정 모터로 기록한 식립 토크/깊이 적분값이 초기 BIC와 상관되는지 검증, 술중 골질·일차안정성 정량화 근거 제공.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Moves the torque-integral–BIC link from bench to in vivo.
- Validates an intra-operative device measuring bone density and insertion parameters (instantaneous, average, peak torque, torque/depth integral).
- Supports objective, real-time primary-stability assessment at placement.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- In vivo clinical study; implants placed with an instantaneous torque-measuring micromotor.
- Recorded parameters: bone density at site, insertion torque (instantaneous/average/peak), torque/depth integral.
- Correlation with initial bone-to-implant contact.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Torque/depth integral assessed as a correlate of initial BIC (quantitative intra-operative bone-quality proxy).
- Two key parameters framed: intra-operative bone-quality evaluation + post-insertion primary-stability assessment.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Device-specific measurements; generalizability to other systems unclear.
- Initial BIC (placement) only — not healing/secondary stability.
