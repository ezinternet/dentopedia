## Why Ingested

임플란트-자연치 간격(임플란트-치근 간격)에 대한 가장 최신 SR+MA. 1.5 mm 안전거리 기준의 근거를 체계적으로 정리. [[implants/tarnow-2000-inter-implant-distance-crestal-bone-height]] 의 임플란트 간격 3 mm 룰과 쌍을 이루는 자연치 근접 거리 근거.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (11 studies, 1,727 patients, 2,000+ implants): <1.5 mm implant-to-tooth distance — pulpal damage 20–25%, marginal bone loss up to 4% of implants; implant survival consistently >95%; pooled effect size 0.11.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (11편, 1,727명, 임플란트 2,000개+): 임플란트-치근 <1.5 mm → 치수 손상 20–25%, 골소실 최대 4%; 임플란트 생존율 >95% 유지; 풀링 효과크기 0.11.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Cureus 2025;17(12):e99077 (PROSPERO ID: CRD42024499790)
- **Study type**: SR + meta-analysis, PRISMA 2020
- **Included**: 11 studies (8 observational + 3 case series), 1995–2022
- **n**: 1,727 participants / >2,000 implants
- **Follow-up**: 6–75 months

## 2. Key Contributions
- Definitive SR on implant-to-tooth proximity effects
- Establishes ≥1.5 mm as safety margin with evidence base
- Distinguishes: implant survival HIGH vs adjacent tooth damage MODERATE risk

## 3. Key Results Summary

| Complication type | Risk | GRADE certainty |
|---|---|---|
| Pulpal/periapical pathology | 20–25% of teeth in contact cases (e.g., Kucukkurt 2020) | Moderate |
| Marginal bone loss/peri-implantitis | Up to 4% of closely placed implants | Moderate |
| Caries/structural damage | Less consistent; mainly posterior | Low |
| Implant survival | Consistently >95% | High |

Pooled effect size: 0.11 (95% CI 0.03–0.22), I²=95%

## 4. Specific Study Highlights
- **Ng 2018** (HK): <1 mm BL implant → increased BOP + interproximal bone loss
- **Yi (Korea)**: <1% root injury at long-term (86–184 months); no extractions
- **Tasharoie**: <1 mm proximity → pulp vitality PRESERVED (apical third intact)
- **Kucukkurt 2020**: 20.5% RCT; 2.3% extraction; but implant survival 96.4%

## 5. Limitations
- All observational studies; no RCT
- I²=95% — high heterogeneity
- No standardized distance measurement method

## 7. Glossary
- **Implant-to-tooth distance / ITD (임플란트-치근 거리)**: horizontal distance between implant surface and adjacent root surface
- **1.5 mm safety margin**: current minimum recommended ITD
- **Platform considerations**: BL implants more affected by proximity than TL implants (Ng 2018)
