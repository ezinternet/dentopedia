## Why Ingested

기존 [[wiki/overviews/sinus-lift-lateral-2026-synthesis]] 개요가 슈나이더 막 천공(Sinus Membrane Perforation, SMP) 수복에 집중하는 반면, 측방창 상악동거상술(Lateral Window Sinus Floor Augmentation, LWSFA) 후 상악동 자연공(Maxillary Sinus Ostium, MSO) 폐쇄·개통성 변화의 시계열 데이터는 누락되어 있었다. 본 연구(Sakuma 2020)는 72개 상악동에서 T0→T1주→T9개월 CBCT를 통해 MSO 직경 및 누두(Infundibulum) 개통성 변화를 정량화한 첫 번째 후향적 관찰연구로, 상악동거상술 후 부비동염(Sinusitis) 예방 축에 실질적 수치 근거를 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective CBCT study (n=72 sinuses, 59 patients): after lateral-window SFE, MSO diameter narrowed from 1.8 mm to 1.1 mm at 1 week with 19.4% infundibulum occlusion, fully recovering to 1.5 mm / 1.4% occlusion by 9 months with no sinusitis cases.

## 한줄요약

후향적 CBCT 연구 (n=72 상악동, 59명): 측방창 상악동거상술 후 1주에 MSO 직경이 1.8→1.1 mm로 감소하고 누두 폐쇄 19.4%까지 증가하지만, 9개월에 1.5 mm / 1.4%로 완전 회복; 부비동염 발생 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: International Journal of Implant Dentistry (IJID), 2020;6:35
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s40729-020-00233-7
- **Study type**: Retrospective observational CBCT study
- **Sample**: 72 sinuses from 59 patients (39F/20M; mean age 53.3 ± 9.1 years); 216 CBCTs total
- **Follow-up**: T0 (baseline), T1w (1 week), T9m (9 months)
- **Setting**: University Corporation Rafael Núñez, Cartagena de Indias, Colombia, August 2015–March 2018

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Quantified MSO diameter reduction** post-SFE: 1.8 mm (T0) → 1.1 mm (T1w) → 1.5 mm (T9m) — all differences statistically significant (Friedman test, p=0.0001).
2. **Infundibulum patency loss** peaked at 1 week (19.4%) vs pre-op baseline 4.2%, recovering to 1.4% at 9 months with no associated sinusitis.
3. Introduced the term **"virtual" sinus mucosa thickness/area** to acknowledge that post-op CBCT signal includes membrane + edema + clot (not membrane alone).
4. In 8 of 72 sinuses, post-op palatal edema reached the MSO anatomically; all resolved spontaneously.
5. Membrane perforations (n=6) sealed with collagen did NOT cause MSO involvement or sinusitis.
6. No significant predictors of patency loss found (logistic regression across gender, age, smoking, side, type of edentulism, ostium position).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **CBCT device**: 3D Accuitomo 170 Tomograph (J Morita, Japan); 80 kV/8 mA; FOV 77×77×74 mm; voxel 0.125 mm
- **Software**: i-Dixel 2.0 (J. Morita) for all measurements
- **Reference plane**: Nose floor (X-axis for coronal; Z-axis for lateral views); anterior nasal spine + nasal septum as vertical reference
- **Filler materials**: OsteoBiol Gen-Os (porcine cortico-cancellous, 250–1000 µm) or Cerabone (bovine cancellous, 1.0–2.0 mm)
- **Lateral wall access**: Sonic-air surgical instrument with diamond insert
- **Intra-examiner reliability**: κ > 0.9 for all variables; duplicate measurements with mean values
- **Statistics**: Friedman test (three timepoints); Nemenyi post-hoc; Wilcoxon for paired T1w–T9m; logistic regression for patency predictors; significance p<0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Parameter | T0 (Baseline) | T1w (1 week) | T9m (9 months) | p |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| MSO diameter (mm) | 1.83 ± 0.54 | 1.07 ± 0.65 | 1.55 ± 0.77 | 0.0001 |
| Virtual mucosa thickness, coronal (mm) | 2.74 ± 3.97 | 7.70 ± 7.06 | 1.65 ± 1.98 | 0.0001 |
| Virtual mucosa area, coronal (mm²) | 37.2 ± 52.5 | 184.5 ± 153.8 | 34.0 ± 50.7 | 0.0001 |
| Virtual mucosa area, lateral (mm²) | 61.8 ± 77.9 | 289.5 ± 240.3 | 59.2 ± 89.3 | 0.0001 |
| Infundibulum out of patency | 3 (4.2%) | 14 (19.4%) | 1 (1.4%) | 0.0001 |
| Distance edema to MSO (coronal) | — | 10.3 ± 9.5 mm | 22.6 ± 7.1 mm | 0.0001 |

- MSO floor-to-ostium distance: mean 33.3 ± 3.6 mm (min 25.2 mm)
- Infundibulum length: 8.8 ± 1.8 mm
- Accessory maxillary ostia (AMO): 7 sinuses (9.7%), mean diameter 1.9 ± 0.5 mm, positioned posteriorly ~10.9 mm from primary MSO
- Δ Ostium diameter T1w–T0: −0.8 ± 0.7 mm (range −2.1 to +1.4 mm)
- 1-week CBCT: 13 sinuses (18.1%) had MSO diameter = 0; 26 sinuses (36.1%) had diameter < 1 mm
- No clinical sinusitis events throughout the study

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective design: no randomization, no control arm
- Single-center/single-ethnicity cohort (mulatto patients, Colombia)
- No dedicated microbiological or endoscopic sinusitis workup — sinusitis excluded only radiographically and clinically
- "Virtual" mucosa thickness integrates membrane + clot + edema; cannot be disaggregated on CBCT
- No data beyond 9 months; long-term patency unknown for the 1.4% with residual occlusion
- Low n for perforation subgroup (n=6) — underpowered to test perforation vs MSO obstruction association
- Future: prospective design, endoscopic MSO assessment, mucociliary clearance testing

## 7. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| MSO (Maxillary Sinus Ostium, 상악동 자연공) | Natural drainage opening of the maxillary sinus into the middle meatus of the nasal cavity |
| Infundibulum (누두, 깔때기관) | Funnel-shaped channel connecting MSO to the middle meatus; its patency determines sinus drainage |
| Virtual sinus mucosa thickness/area | CBCT-measured signal of membrane + post-op edema + clot — introduced by this paper to acknowledge inability to separate components radiographically |
| AMO (Accessory Maxillary Ostium, 부상악동 자연공) | Accessory openings posterior to the primary MSO, found in ~10% of sinuses |
| Schneiderian membrane (슈나이더 막) | Pseudostratified ciliated columnar epithelium lining the maxillary sinus floor |
| CBCT (Cone-Beam Computed Tomography, 콘빔 전산화단층촬영) | 3D radiographic modality used here for serial volumetric assessment |
