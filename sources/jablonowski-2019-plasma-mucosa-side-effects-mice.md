## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치 2차(2026-05-31)의 **안전성(safety) 축**. 골유착·항균 이득과 별개로 CAP를 구강 점막에 직접 적용했을 때 조직 손상이 있는지 동물에서 검증해, [[wiki/overviews/plasma-surface-treatment-dental-implants]]가 임플란트주위염 chairside 적용을 논할 때 필요한 안전성 전제를 채운다. [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/alqutaibi-2023-cap-bacterial-reduction-implants-sr]] 제염 적용의 안전 근거.

## One-line Summary

In vivo mouse safety study (180 male B6C3F1 mice, 12 groups; buccal mucosa treated with two CAP sources for 10 s or 1 min, vs UV and untreated controls; sacrificed 1 day or 1 week later): assesses short-term mucosal tissue damage before in-human CAP use for peri-implant disease — an explicit pre-clinical safety check.

## 한줄요약

쥐 in vivo 안전성 연구(수컷 B6C3F1 180마리, 12군; 협측 점막에 2종 CAP를 10초·1분 적용, UV·무처리 대조, 1일·1주 후 희생): 사람 대상 임플란트주위염 CAP 적용 전 단기 점막 손상 여부를 평가한 전임상 안전성 검증.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: PLoS ONE 2019, 14(4):e0215099
- DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0215099
- Study type: in vivo animal (mouse) safety study
- Plasma sources: kINPen 09, PS-MWM

## 2. Key Contributions

- Pre-clinical mucosal-safety evaluation underpinning clinical CAP use for peri-implant disease.
- Dose/source comparison (10 s vs 1 min; two devices) with histopathology by two pathologists.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 180 mice, 12 groups (n=15). Buccal mucosa treated 10 s (two sources) or 1 min (kINPen 09); UV (8 s/48 s) and untreated controls.
- Sacrifice 1 day or 1 week; histological tissue-damage assessment, weight monitoring.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Study designed to detect short-term harmful mucosal damage/side effects from oral CAP application.
- (Exact lesion grading per group not extracted here; framed as a safety screen prior to human use.)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Animal (mouse) mucosa; short-term (≤1 week) only.
- Source/dose specific — findings tie to the tested devices/parameters.
