## One-line Summary
J Prosthet Dent 2014;111(1):20-9: in vitro 6 groups (3 CAD/CAM Zr ± staining/glazing + feldspathic), 240,000 cycles — polished Zr: least enamel wear (1.11mm³); glazed Zr (stained+glazed): most among Zr (3.07mm³); feldspathic: most overall.

## 1. Key Results
- 6 groups: polished Zr, Zr+staining, Zr+staining+glazing (3 brands/conditions), feldspathic ceramic
- 240,000 masticatory cycles; human premolar antagonists; FE-SEM before/after
- Zr+staining+glazing: significantly greater roughness than all other groups (p<0.01)
- Enamel wear: polished Zr (1.11±0.51mm³) < stained+glazed Zr (3.07±0.98mm³)
- Feldspathic ceramic: most enamel wear overall
- Smooth polished surface = less antagonist wear; glazing = more wear
