## One-line Summary
Split-mouth prospective (10 patients, 60 implants): ISQ trajectory after sinus lift — BBM (bovine HA) baseline 63.8 → 73.5 (3 mo) → 74.65 (6 mo); PBM (porcine HA) 62.6 → 67.0 (3 mo) → 72.9 (6 mo). Higher crystallinity / lower porosity (BBM) → faster early stability gain.

## 1. Document Information
Materials 2017 open-access. Split-mouth bilateral maxillary sinus augmentation comparing two biological hydroxyapatites at multiple healing timepoints via RFA.

## 2. Key Contributions
- **Quantified ISQ trajectory** over 6-month healing window after sinus lift + bone graft
- Statistically significant ISQ rise from baseline → 3 mo → 6 mo for both grafts
- Physico-chemical properties of HA (crystallinity, porosity) directly influence early stability quotient

## 3. Methodology
- 10 patients, split-mouth bilateral sinus augmentation
- Control side: bovine HA (BBM, high crystallinity, low porosity, high-temperature sintered)
- Test side: porcine HA (PBM)
- 6 months sinus healing → 60 implants placed in posterior maxilla
- RFA at:
  - **T1** = baseline (day of implant placement)
  - **T2** = 3 months post-placement
  - **T3** = 6 months post-placement

## 4. Key Results

| Time | BBM ISQ | PBM ISQ |
|---|---|---|
| T1 (baseline) | 63.8 ± 2.97 | 62.6 ± 2.11 |
| T2 (3 mo) | 73.5 ± 4.21 | 67.0 ± 4.99 |
| T3 (6 mo) | 74.65 ± 2.93 | 72.9 ± 2.63 |

- All within-group increases statistically significant
- BBM reaches loading-ready ISQ (>70) by 3 months
- PBM catches up by 6 months
- Histology: no gaps at bone-particle interface for either graft

## 5. Limitations
- Small sample (10 patients, 60 implants)
- Only two HA types compared — no β-TCP arm
- No T0 (immediate placement) ISQ — sinus first then implant 6 months later
- All implants placed after graft consolidation (two-stage)

## 6. Related Work

- Del Fabbro 2025 — graft material survival equivalence
- Sennerby 2008 — ISQ rise during osseointegration

## 7. Glossary
- **BBM**: Bovine Bone Mineral (bovine HA)
- **PBM**: Porcine Bone Mineral (porcine HA)
- **ISQ**: Implant Stability Quotient
- **T1/T2/T3**: Sequential RFA measurement timepoints
