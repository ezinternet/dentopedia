## One-line Summary
PRISMA-compliant systematic review with meta-analysis (28 articles, 20 pooled; PROSPERO CRD42020178857) of alveolar ridge dimensional changes following unassisted post-extraction socket healing: in non-molar sites mean horizontal ridge reduction is 2.73 mm (clinical) / 2.54 mm (radiographic); molar sites lose 3.61 mm horizontally radiographically; vertical mid-facial loss 1.4–1.7 mm — providing the most cited reference benchmarks for unassisted ridge resorption magnitudes.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Provides the **most rigorous pooled estimate** of unassisted post-extraction ridge resorption magnitude, stratified by **assessment method** (clinical vs radiographic) and **tooth site** (non-molar vs molar).
- Standardises a reference framework for comparing the benefit of alveolar ridge preservation (ARP) interventions against an evidence-based "no treatment" baseline.
- Documents the molar site as the most severely resorbed location after unassisted healing.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PRISMA-compliant systematic review with protocol pre-registration in PROSPERO.
- Eligibility: human studies measuring alveolar ridge dimensional change ≥3 months after tooth extraction with no concomitant grafting or socket management.
- Outcomes: horizontal ridge width change, vertical mid-facial ridge height change, vertical mid-lingual/palatal ridge height change.
- Random-effects pooled meta-analysis where ≥2 comparable studies reported the same outcome.
- Sub-grouping by measurement modality (clinical caliper / cast vs radiographic CBCT or periapical) and by anatomic site (non-molar vs molar).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
**Non-molar sites — clinical measurement:**
- Horizontal ridge reduction: **2.73 mm** (95% CI 2.36–3.11)
- Vertical mid-facial reduction: **1.71 mm** (95% CI 1.30–2.12)
- Vertical mid-lingual reduction: **1.44 mm** (95% CI 0.78–2.10)

**Non-molar sites — radiographic measurement:**
- Horizontal: **2.54 mm** (95% CI 1.97–3.11)
- Vertical mid-facial: **1.65 mm** (95% CI 0.42–2.88)
- Vertical mid-lingual: **0.87 mm** (95% CI 0.36–1.38)

**Molar sites — radiographic measurement:**
- Horizontal: **3.61 mm** (95% CI 3.24–3.98)
- Vertical mid-facial: **1.46 mm** (95% CI 0.73–2.20)
- Vertical mid-lingual: **1.20 mm** (95% CI 0.56–1.83)

- 28 studies included; 20 contributed to quantitative synthesis.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneity in baseline ridge dimensions, follow-up duration, and reference landmarks among included studies.
- Few studies stratified by buccal bone thickness — a known major modifier of resorption magnitude.
- Limited evidence specifically on **mid-lingual** vertical loss at molar sites.
- Most studies measure at 3–6 months; longer-term remodelling is under-characterised.

## 7. Glossary
- **Unassisted socket healing**: spontaneous extraction socket healing with no grafting, membrane, or socket management.
- **Alveolar ridge preservation (ARP)**: grafting and/or barrier procedures at extraction to limit ridge resorption.
- **Horizontal ridge reduction**: bucco-lingual width loss measured at a defined apico-coronal level.
- **Mid-facial vertical reduction**: change in buccal alveolar crest height at the mid-tooth axis.
- **PRISMA**: Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses — reporting standard.
