## Why Ingested

Brings trauma-specific splinting evidence (flexible, short-term for replanted avulsed teeth) into the new `dental-trauma` category, complementing the periodontal/occlusal splinting page [[occlusion/dommisch-2022-efficacy-of-tooth-splinting-occlusal]] which addresses a different indication (mobility/periodontal). It also follows directly from the storage-media decision in [[dental-trauma/debrier-2020-storage-avulsed-tooth-prior]].

## One-line Summary

SR (7 studies; 4 in MA; 708 patients, 975 teeth; GRADE very low): trends favor flexible splints and shorter durations for replanted avulsed permanent teeth in children, tentatively supporting IADT guidance, but most effects were not statistically significant.

## 한줄요약

SR (7편, MA 4편; 환자 708명·치아 975개; GRADE 매우 낮음): 소아 영구치 탈구치 재식 후 탄성(flexible) 스플린트·짧은 고정 기간이 더 나은 경향을 보여 IADT 권고를 잠정 지지하나, 대부분 통계적 유의성에는 도달하지 못함.

## 1. Document Information

- Type: Systematic review (meta-analysis on subset).
- Journal: J Indian Soc Pedod Prev Dent 2025;43(3):307-321.
- Registration: PROSPERO CRD42023414888.
- Abstract-only — full text not retrieved (PMC unavailable).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Focused synthesis on splint TYPE and DURATION specifically for replanted avulsed permanent teeth in pediatric patients.
- Provides an evidence grade behind the long-standing IADT preference for flexible, short-term splinting.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PRISMA-guided; PROSPERO-registered.
- Databases: PubMed, Cochrane Library, Embase, Google Scholar. 734 records → 7 included, 4 pooled.
- Risk of bias: Newcastle-Ottawa Scale. Meta-analysis: RevMan v5.4. Certainty: GRADE.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 708 participants, 975 avulsed teeth.
- Trend: flexible splints + shorter durations → better periodontal and pulp outcomes.
- Most comparisons NOT statistically significant.
- Overall evidence: GRADE very low.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Few studies, small pooled set, observational designs → very low certainty.
- Heterogeneous splint materials/durations; no definitive threshold established.
- High-quality RCTs needed to fix optimal type/duration.

## 6. Related Work

- IADT avulsion guidelines (flexible splint ~2 weeks default).
- [[dental-trauma/debrier-2020-storage-avulsed-tooth-prior]] — preceding storage-media step.
- [[occlusion/dommisch-2022-efficacy-of-tooth-splinting-occlusal]] — splinting for a different (periodontal/mobility) indication.

## 7. Glossary

- Avulsion: complete displacement of a tooth from its socket.
- Flexible (functional) splint: low-rigidity stabilization allowing physiologic micromovement, favored to reduce ankylosis/replacement resorption.
- GRADE: certainty-of-evidence framework.
