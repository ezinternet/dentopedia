## One-line Summary
인도 Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham 비교연구: 단일/이중 항혈소판제 (SAPT/DAPT) 지속하 단순 발치 시 출혈 시간 — DAPT가 SAPT 대비 다소 길지만 모두 임상적 의미 있는 출혈 합병증 없이 국소 지혈로 통제; DAPT 발치 위해 중단 불필요.

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Indian-population data on uninterrupted SAPT vs DAPT through dental extractions.
2. Quantifies bleeding time differences and clinical bleeding outcome.
3. Supports umbrella-review recommendation (cited in paper) that DAPT need not be discontinued.

## 3. Methodology
Comparative study comparing bleeding time after extraction in SAPT vs DAPT patients, with uninterrupted antiplatelet therapy.

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Both SAPT and DAPT groups bleeding controllable with local hemostasis; DAPT longer bleeding time but no clinically significant bleeding complications.

## 5. Limitations
- Single center, comparative observational
- Small sample (typical of single-institution OMFS studies)
- No DOAC arm

## 7. Glossary
- SAPT: Single antiplatelet therapy (e.g., aspirin alone)
- DAPT: Dual antiplatelet therapy (e.g., aspirin + clopidogrel/ticagrelor)
