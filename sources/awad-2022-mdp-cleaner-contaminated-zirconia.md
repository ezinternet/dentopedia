## One-line Summary
Materials 2022;15:1023: MDP-containing cleaner (Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus) applied after contamination significantly improves SBS vs water or alcohol cleaning; combines chemical adhesion with cleaning function.

## 1. Key Results
- Monolithic zirconia contaminated with saliva/blood/provisional cement
- MDP-containing cleaner (Clearfil Ceramic Primer Plus used as cleaner): significantly higher SBS
- Water or alcohol cleaning: inadequate decontamination
- Mechanism: MDP's phosphate group displaces organic contaminants + chemically bonds to Zr
