## One-line Summary
J Funct Biomater 2026: SR (5 RCTs): β-TCP alone variable results (worse than xenograft in some); β-TCP in combination = comparable to DBBM; evidence limited and heterogeneous; role of β-TCP in ARP remains provisional.

## 1. Key Results
- Electronic search up to April 2026; 5 RCTs met inclusion criteria
- β-TCP alone: variable results — ranging from greater resorption vs xenograft to comparable to FDBA
- β-TCP combined with other biomaterials: outcomes generally comparable to DBBM
- Considerable heterogeneity in formulations, socket management, outcome measures
- Overall: limited and heterogeneous evidence; β-TCP may have potential role in ARP

## 2. Clinical Implications
- β-TCP alone not consistently superior to other materials for ARP
- Combination formulations with β-TCP more promising than solo use
- DBBM remains better-supported reference material for socket preservation
- More standardized RCTs needed before β-TCP recommendation
