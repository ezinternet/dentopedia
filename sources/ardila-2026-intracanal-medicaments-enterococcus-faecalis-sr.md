## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/ordinola-zapata-2022-intracanal-medicaments-status-directions]]가 정리한 근관 내 약제의 status를, E. faecalis 표적이라는 특정 병원체 축으로 좁혀 정량 근거를 보강한다. 또한 [[endodontics/irrigation/rossi-fedele-2023-irrigation-dressing-apical-periodontitis-sr-ma]]의 dressing → 치유 흐름에서 "어떤 dressing이 가장 강력한 항균인가"라는 질문에 도메인 기반 합성으로 답한다.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (PRISMA 2020, PROSPERO CRD420261345958; 12 studies, in vitro/ex vivo/clinical) with domain-based interpretive synthesis + exploratory meta-analysis of intracanal medicaments vs Enterococcus faecalis: antibiotic-based pastes (esp. triple antibiotic paste) showed comparatively stronger antimicrobial performance, calcium hydroxide variable/weaker (esp. deep dentin), nanoparticle/bioactive agents promising but heterogeneous; pooling (only 2 studies) showed extreme heterogeneity, certainty low–moderate.

## 한줄요약

근관 내 약제의 Enterococcus faecalis 항균력을 평가한 체계적 고찰(PRISMA 2020, 12편, 도메인 기반 해석적 합성 + 탐색적 메타분석) — 항생제 기반 페이스트(특히 삼중항생제 페이스트)가 상대적으로 가장 강력했고, 수산화칼슘은 특히 심부 상아질에서 효과가 변동·약했으며, 나노입자·생활성 제제는 유망하나 이질성이 컸다. 정량 통합은 2편뿐이라 이질성·불확실성이 높았다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Pathogen-focused SR isolating **E. faecalis** (keystone of post-treatment apical periodontitis) as the sole target microorganism.
- Introduces a **structured domain-based interpretive synthesis** to organize antimicrobial performance and resistance/tolerance patterns across heterogeneous experimental contexts (CFU, log reduction, MIC, MBC).
- Ranks medicament classes by comparative antimicrobial strength: **antibiotic-based (TAP) > combination CH therapies > CH alone**; nanoparticle/bioactive agents promising but inconsistent.
- Documents E. faecalis tolerance to calcium hydroxide within dentinal tubules and biofilm.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Guideline**: PRISMA 2020; a priori protocol (PROSPERO CRD420261345958).
- **Databases**: PubMed/MEDLINE, Scopus, Embase (via Ovid); up to February 2026; no language restriction; reference/citation tracking.
- **PICOS**: P = E. faecalis as primary organism (dentin/biofilm/clinical models); I = CH, CHX-as-medicament, TAP/modified TAP, nanoparticle systems, other antimicrobial formulations; C = different medicaments / untreated / vehicle controls; O = CFU, log reduction, MIC, MBC, persistence/resistance/tolerance; S = in vitro, ex vivo, clinical with quantitative outcomes.
- **Exclusion**: E. faecalis not primary or not reported separately; studies on irrigants/activation/sealers/PDT only; reviews; case reports; no quantitative data.
- **Risk of bias**: Cochrane RoB 2 (clinical); adapted tool for in vitro/ex vivo.
- **Synthesis**: domain-based interpretive synthesis across all 12 studies; exploratory meta-analysis where feasible (only 2 studies poolable).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **12 studies** included.
- **Antibiotic-based medicaments (esp. triple antibiotic paste)**: comparatively strongest antimicrobial performance, particularly for bacterial reduction and biofilm-related outcomes.
- **Calcium hydroxide**: variable and generally lower efficacy, notably in deeper dentinal regions; **combination therapies improved** CH performance.
- **Novel agents** (nanoparticle systems, bioactive compounds): promising but heterogeneous.
- **Meta-analysis**: limited to **2 studies**, revealing extreme heterogeneity and imprecise estimates.
- **Certainty of evidence**: low to moderate overall.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 2 of 12 studies poolable → exploratory, imprecise meta-analytic estimates with extreme heterogeneity.
- Predominantly in vitro/ex vivo; scarce high-quality clinical evidence.
- Marked methodological heterogeneity (inoculation protocols, concentrations, vehicles, outcome metrics) limits direct comparison.
- Antibiotic-paste superiority must be weighed against antimicrobial-resistance and staining concerns — authors call for standardized, clinically robust trials.

## 7. Glossary

- **TAP** — Triple Antibiotic Paste (ciprofloxacin + metronidazole + minocycline).
- **CH** — Calcium hydroxide.
- **CHX** — Chlorhexidine.
- **CFU** — Colony-Forming Units.
- **MIC / MBC** — Minimum Inhibitory / Bactericidal Concentration.
- **Domain-based interpretive synthesis** — structured qualitative organization of findings across predefined domains when meta-analytic pooling is infeasible.
