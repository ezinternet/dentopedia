## Why Ingested

[[resin-bonding/anithakumari-2022-desensitizing-agents-bond-strength-sr]]의 "탈감작제가 결합강도에 미치는 영향" 질문을 self-adhesive resin cement + zirconia 시나리오로 직접 검증. 보철 전 탈감작제 도포가 SBS를 낮추는지에 대한 실무 근거. [[dentin-hypersensitivity/jiang-2022-desensitizing-agents-permeability-cytotoxicity]]와 제제 동일군.

## One-line Summary
In vitro SBS study (80 molars, 8 groups n=10, thermocycled) finding Admira, Smart Protect, and Gluma increased shear bond strength of zirconia to dentin with a self-adhesive cement, supporting their pre-cementation use.

## 한줄요약
In vitro 전단결합강도(SBS) 연구(80치·8군 각 10·열순환): self-adhesive cement 사용 시 Admira·Smart Protect·Gluma가 지르코니아-상아질 SBS를 증가시켜 세멘 전 도포를 지지.

## 1. Document Information
- BMC Oral Health 2024;24:1403. İzmir/İstanbul private practice + Ege University Prosthodontics.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Tests 7 desensitizers' effect on zirconia-to-dentin SBS with Rely-X U200 self-adhesive cement.
- Reports failure modes; identifies agents that help vs hurt bonding.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 80 molars, dentin exposed, 8 groups (n=10): Gluma, Bifluoride, Admira, Smart Protect, Teethmate, Clinpro White, BisBlock, Control.
- Zirconia discs luted with Rely-X U200; 24 h water 37°C; 2500 thermocycles (5–55°C); SBS at 0.5 mm/min; ANOVA + post hoc.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Highest SBS arms ~10.70 / 9.62 / 8.90 MPa (resin-/glutaraldehyde-/HEMA-based: Admira, Smart Protect, Gluma) vs lower for oxalate/fluoride agents (down to ~1.60 MPa).
- Conclusion: Admira, Smart Protect, Gluma increased SBS and can be recommended pre-cementation.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro; single self-adhesive cement; no aging beyond 2500 cycles.

## 6. Related Work
- Practical complement to the bond-strength SR; same desensitizer families as the DH permeability study.

## 7. Glossary
- SBS: shear bond strength. Self-adhesive resin cement: no separate etch/primer (e.g., Rely-X U200).
