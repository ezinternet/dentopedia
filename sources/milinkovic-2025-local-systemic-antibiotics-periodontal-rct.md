## Why Ingested

Fills a direct gap in the wiki's coverage of adjunctive antibiotic strategies during 비외과적 치주치료 (Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy, NSPT): we have papers on antiseptics and ozone adjuncts but no head-to-head RCT comparing 국소 항생제 (local antibiotics, LA) vs 전신 항생제 (systemic antibiotics, SA). This RCT introduces piperacillin + tazobactam as a locally delivered alternative with equivalent 6-month outcomes, directly extending [[periodontics/ramanauskaite-2020-antiseptics-adjuncts-scaling-root-planing]] and contextualizing the systemic-antibiotic restrictiveness now encoded in EFP guidelines.

## One-line Summary

Single-blinded RCT (n=38, stage III periodontitis) found that locally delivered piperacillin + tazobactam produced equivalent 6-month reductions in PPD, CAL, BOP, plaque index, and total bacterial count compared to systemic amoxicillin + metronidazole as NSPT adjuncts, with no significant differences in IL-17 or TNF-α expression between groups.

## 한줄요약

단일 맹검 RCT (n=38, 치주염 (Periodontitis) 3기): 비외과적 치주치료 (Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy, NSPT) 보조 요법으로 국소 피페라실린 + 타조박탐 (Piperacillin + Tazobactam)이 전신 아목시실린 + 메트로니다졸 (Amoxicillin + Metronidazole)과 동등한 탐침 깊이 (Probing Pocket Depth, PPD), 임상 부착 수준 (Clinical Attachment Level, CAL), 탐침 출혈 (Bleeding on Probing, BOP), 총 세균수 감소 효과를 보임.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Single-blinded randomised clinical trial (RCT)
- **Population**: 38 adults (18–40 years) with generalised stage III periodontitis grade B or C, non-smokers/light smokers, penicillin-non-allergic; recruited at University of Belgrade, School of Dental Medicine, January 2023–March 2024
- **Intervention (LA group, n=19)**: Local delivery of piperacillin + tazobactam (Gelcide; Italmed MedTechDental) 24 h post-NSPT, single application in all pockets ≥4 mm (6 injection points per tooth)
- **Comparator (SA group, n=19)**: Systemic amoxicillin 500 mg TID + metronidazole 400 mg TID × 7 days post-NSPT
- **Primary outcome**: PPD reduction at 6 months
- **Secondary outcomes**: CAL, BOP, PI, total bacterial count (16S rRNA qPCR), IL-17 and TNF-α gene expression (GCF paper-point samples)
- **Follow-up**: 6 months
- **Registration**: ClinicalTrials.gov, registered 31/10/2022

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **First head-to-head RCT** comparing local piperacillin + tazobactam against systemic amoxicillin + metronidazole as NSPT adjuncts in stage III periodontitis.
2. **Equivalence confirmed**: No statistically significant differences between LA and SA at 6 months for any clinical, microbiological, or immunological parameter.
3. **Single-delivery local protocol**: One session of local antibiotic delivery achieved outcomes equivalent to a 7-day systemic course, supporting simplified local delivery in settings where systemic antibiotics carry resistance or tolerance concerns.
4. **Cytokine non-response**: Neither IL-17 nor TNF-α changed significantly with treatment in either group, suggesting GCF cytokine levels do not track short-term clinical improvement.
5. **Antimicrobial resistance rationale**: Offers a local-delivery alternative consistent with EFP 2020 guideline restrictions on routine systemic antibiotic use in NSPT.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Study design**: Single-blinded RCT (outcome assessor blinded). Allocation concealment via opaque sealed envelopes. Treating clinician and patients not blinded to arm, but examiner had no access to dental records. Intention-to-treat analysis; no dropouts.

**Sample size**: 17 per arm (80% power, 1.0 mm PPD difference in pockets ≥7 mm, SD 1.0 mm).

**NSPT protocol**: Full-mouth disinfection. LA group received Gelcide (piperacillin + tazobactam) via blunt-needle syringe in six circumferential injection points per tooth for all pockets ≥4 mm, 24 h post-NSPT, single visit covering all quadrants. SA group received amoxicillin 500 mg + metronidazole 400 mg TID × 7 days with compliance logbooks.

**Clinical measurements**: PPD, CAL, BOP (%), PI at 6 points per tooth; calibrated single examiner using UNC-15 probe.

**Microbiological sampling**: Subgingival GCF via paper points (No. 30) ≥30 s from the deepest pocket in the first premolar/molar region (PPD >5 mm). Stored in RNAlater at −80°C.

**Laboratory analysis**:
- DNA → total bacterial count by qPCR (universal 16S rRNA primers, SYBR Green)
- RNA → cDNA → IL-17 and TNF-α relative expression by qPCR (normalized to GAPDH)

**Statistical tests**: Kolmogorov-Smirnov normality check → Wilcoxon signed-rank (within-group) and Mann-Whitney U (between-group). Significance threshold: p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Clinical parameters — 6-month follow-up:**

| Parameter | LA baseline | LA 6m | SA baseline | SA 6m | Between-group p |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| PPD (mm) | 3.93 ± 0.54 | 3.25 ± 0.51 | 4.08 ± 0.58 | 3.29 ± 0.55 | NS |
| CAL (mm) | 4.40 ± 0.85 | 3.83 ± 0.91 | 4.50 ± 0.77 | 4.03 ± 0.80 | NS |
| BOP (%) | 0.631 ± 0.12 | 0.123 ± 0.08 | 0.680 ± 0.19 | 0.124 ± 0.04 | NS |
| PI | 0.654 ± 0.13 | 0.101 ± 0.06 | 0.704 ± 0.25 | 0.115 ± 0.04 | NS |

Both groups: p<0.001 for all parameters (baseline vs 6 months).

**Total bacterial count**: ~10-fold reduction in both groups (LA: p=0.001; SA: p=0.013); no between-group difference at 6 months (p>0.05).

**Cytokines (GCF)**: IL-17 and TNF-α — no significant change from baseline to 6 months in either group; no between-group difference (p>0.05 for all).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample size (n=38, 19 per arm)
- Single-blinded design (patients and therapist not blinded)
- Only 6-month follow-up — long-term durability of local antibiotic effects unknown
- Single local delivery session — reapplication protocols not tested
- Only total bacterial count measured (not species-specific pathogen analysis)
- Only two cytokines (IL-17, TNF-α) evaluated from GCF; protein levels not measured
- Single-centre study (University of Belgrade); generalizability uncertain
- Piperacillin + tazobactam (Gelcide) not widely available globally

**Future directions**: Larger multicentre RCTs; longer follow-up (1–3 years); species-specific microbiological outcomes; protein-level cytokine analysis; repeat local delivery protocols; cost-effectiveness analysis.

## 6. Related Work

- EFP 2020 guidelines: restrict routine systemic antibiotics in NSPT; recommend only for generalised stage III grade B/C in younger patients
- Herrera et al. 2022: adjuvant amoxicillin + metronidazole significant for stage III/IV; benchmark used in SA group
- Sanz et al. (meta-analysis): adjuvant amoxicillin + metronidazole reduced PPD, CAL, BOP and residual pockets
- Prior piperacillin + tazobactam study (Kolb et al.): significant pathogen reduction but no PPD/BOP improvement at 6 months with limited pocket protocol — present study's full-mouth disinfection protocol differs
- Local antimicrobials: previous meta-analyses show limited benefits (tenths of mm gains); present study shows larger equivalent gains possibly due to full-mouth protocol

## 7. Glossary

- **NSPT (Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy)**: 비외과적 치주치료; mechanical debridement (scaling and root planing) as initial periodontal treatment
- **PPD (Periodontal Probing Depth)**: 탐침 깊이; gingival sulcus/pocket depth measured with periodontal probe
- **CAL (Clinical Attachment Level)**: 임상 부착 수준; distance from CEJ to base of periodontal pocket
- **BOP (Bleeding on Probing)**: 탐침 출혈; percentage of sites with bleeding upon probing
- **PI (Plaque Index)**: 치태 지수; proportion of tooth surfaces with detectable plaque
- **GCF (Gingival Crevicular Fluid)**: 치은열구액; fluid from the periodontal sulcus used for biomarker sampling
- **Piperacillin + tazobactam (Gelcide)**: Extended-spectrum ureidopenicillin + beta-lactamase inhibitor; high anaerobic activity
- **Amoxicillin + metronidazole**: Standard systemic adjunct for periodontitis stage III; EFP 2020 guideline-endorsed combination
- **TNF-α (Tumor Necrosis Factor-alpha)**: Key pro-inflammatory cytokine involved in periodontal bone resorption and innate immunity
- **IL-17 (Interleukin-17)**: Pro-inflammatory cytokine driving neutrophil recruitment and osteoclast activation in periodontitis
- **qPCR (Quantitative PCR)**: Quantitative polymerase chain reaction; method for measuring gene copy number or expression
- **Stage III periodontitis**: 3기 치주염; severe periodontitis with ≥2 teeth with interdental CAL ≥5 mm or PPD ≥6 mm and bone loss extending to middle or apical third
