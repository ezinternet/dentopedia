## 2. Key Contributions
1. Indication-by-indication breakdown for dental antibiotic prophylaxis.
2. AMR-aware framework; explicit minimization of routine prophylaxis.
3. Implant placement prophylaxis nuance addressed.

## 3. Methodology
Narrative review across IE prophylaxis, prosthetic joint prophylaxis, immunocompromised patients, implant surgery.

## 4. Key Results
[합의수준] Indication-specific recommendations:
- IE high-risk: prophylaxis indicated (per AHA 2007/2017)
- Prosthetic joint: no longer routinely indicated (ADA 2014 position)
- Implant placement: amoxicillin single preoperative dose acceptable for prophylaxis
- Immunocompromised: case-by-case

## 5. Limitations
- Narrative not systematic
- Regional (Croatian/EU) regulatory context
- Implant prophylaxis evidence still evolving
