## One-line Summary
J Prosthet Dent 2013;109:22-29: in vitro Alabama wear device — polished zirconia: no ceramic loss, least enamel wear; glazed zirconia: significant ceramic + enamel wear; veneering porcelain: most enamel wear; polish > glaze.

## 1. Key Results
- Groups: polished, glazed, polished+reglazed zirconia; veneering porcelain; enamel control
- Surface roughness order (smooth→rough): polished Zr < glazed Zr < polished+reglazed Zr < veneering porcelain < enamel
- Ceramic wear: polished Zr = none; glazed Zr = significant; veneering porcelain = most
- Enamel antagonist wear: polished Zr least; veneering porcelain most; glazed Zr > polished Zr
- Surface roughness predicts antagonist wear
- UAB (University of Alabama at Birmingham)
