## One-line Summary
Materials 2021;14:3920: MDP-based chemical bonding to cubic (5Y) zirconia — MDP primer significantly improves bond strength; tribochemical silica coating (CoJet) + silane + MDP: highest stability at 6 months; cubic zirconia responds similarly to tetragonal.

## 1. Key Results
- Cubic 5Y-PSZ zirconia tested (high-translucency type)
- Chemical treatments: MDP primer, silane, combination with tribochemical silica coating
- CoJet + silane + MDP: highest bond strength stability at 6 months
- MDP primer alone: significant improvement over untreated
- Aging (water storage, thermocycling): bond degradation greater in non-combined treatments
