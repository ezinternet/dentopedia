## One-line Summary
CBCT retrospective (n=1,200 Saudi maxillary molars): MB2 prevalence 1MM 46.7%, 2MM 17.7% — Chilean (69.8/46.9%)·Iranian SR 평균보다 낮은 편; 1MM 여성↑, 2MM sex-NS.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 사우디 1,200 CBCT primary data (1MM 600 + 2MM 600)
- 1MM 46.7%, 2MM 17.7% — 다른 인구 대비 낮은 prevalence
- PMB1-PMB2 거리 정량 (1MM 1.87±0.42 mm, 2MM 1.24±0.76 mm)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Retrospective CBCT chart review
- Standardization: pulpal floor에서 1 mm apical 측정
- Chi-square sex/tooth-type comparison, α=0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 1MM MB2 prevalence: 46.7% (p=0.020, female↑)
- 2MM MB2 prevalence: 17.7% (p=0.457, sex-NS)
- PMB1-PMB2: 1MM 1.87±0.42 mm, 2MM 1.24±0.76 mm
- PMB2-PP: 1MM 0.74±0.21 mm, 2MM 0.43±0.18 mm
- Overall: 386/1,200 (32.2%) maxillary molars positive

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Retrospective single-center
- CBCT resolution (voxel size 미명시) — micro-MB2 누락 가능
- Operator inter-rater reliability 미보고
- 사우디 인구 단일 — 다른 중동/북아프리카 인구 비교 후속 필요
