## Why Ingested

기존 wiki는 Versah Densah bur의 임플란트 안정성(ISQ) 근거를 보강하지만 Magnetic Mallet과의 직접 head-to-head 비교는 없었다. 본 split-mouth RCT는 두 OD 기법의 ISQ·골밀도·순측 골판 두께를 전치부 심미 구역에서 직접 비교해 [[implants/versah-protocols/althobaiti-2023-osseodensification-conventional-drilling-isq-sr]] 의 Densah bur 우위 근거를 esthetic-zone 맥락에서 심화한다.

## One-line Summary

Split-mouth RCT (n=7 patients, 14 sites, maxillary anterior zone): rotary Densah bur osseodensification yielded significantly higher primary ISQ (70.1 vs 49.0) and labial plate thickness vs Magnetic Mallet, while bone density and crestal width were comparable at 6 months.

## 한줄요약

분할구 RCT (7명, 14부위, 상악 전치부): Densah bur 골밀화(osseodensification)가 Magnetic Mallet 대비 1차 ISQ(70.1 vs 49.0)와 순측 골판 두께에서 유의하게 우수했으며, 골밀도·치조정 폭경은 6개월 시점에 동등했다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Split-mouth randomized clinical trial (CONSORT-compliant)
- **Authors**: Ali M, Elaskary A, Elsayed Y, Sherif A, Hassan H (Faculty of Dentistry, Alexandria University, Egypt)
- **Year**: 2026
- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s12903-026-08802-9
- **PMID**: 42249424 / **PMCID**: PMC13270663
- **Trial Registration**: PACTR202412717492416 (retrospective)
- **Sample**: 7 patients, 14 implant sites (maxillary anterior esthetic zone, April 2024–April 2025)
- **Implant**: IS-III-CMI (Neobiotech, Seoul, Korea), 1 mm sub-crestal placement

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First split-mouth RCT directly comparing rotary Densah bur OD vs pulse-based Magnetic Mallet OD in the maxillary anterior esthetic zone
2. Demonstrates Densah bur superiority in primary ISQ (>70 threshold achieved) and labial plate preservation
3. Reports two Magnetic Mallet complications (1 labial plate fracture → implant failure; 1 palatal crack) pointing to steep learning curve and thin-plate risk
4. Both OD techniques showed equivalent peri-implant bone density and crestal width outcomes at 6 months
5. LMM analysis with clustering adjustment validates the split-mouth design rigorously

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Design**: Split-mouth RCT, blinded assessors (ISQ: Y.E.; radiographic: A.S.)

**Groups**:
- DB-Group (n=7 sites): Densah Pilot Drill G3 (1.6 mm) clockwise 800 RPM → progressive Densah Burs in reverse counter-clockwise densifying mode 800 RPM
- MM-Group (n=7 sites, 1 failed → n=6 completed): Pilot osteotome (1.8 mm) Mode 1 ~75 daN / 80 µs → 100P-Osteotome Mode 2 ~90 daN / 80 µs

**Outcomes**:
- Primary: ISQ at placement (Osstell, 3rd gen SmartPeg) and 6 months
- Secondary: EWH score (days 7/14/21), VAS pain (days 7/14/21), peri-implant bone density (Hu, CBCT), labio-palatal crestal width (mm), labial plate thickness (mm)

**Statistics**: 2-level hierarchical Linear Mixed-Effects Model (LMM), REML, Bonferroni correction, Kenward-Roger df, AIC/BIC model selection

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | DB (n=7) | MM (n=6) | p (between) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary ISQ | 70.14 ± 14.99 | 49.00 ± 7.13 | <0.001 |
| Secondary ISQ | 74.71 ± 8.10 | 59.83 ± 4.07 | 0.003 |
| Bone density immediate (Hu) | 901.86 ± 131.65 | 835.67 ± 102.14 | 0.216 (NS) |
| Bone density 6 mo (Hu) | 749.00 ± 134.76 | 700.17 ± 106.19 | 0.342 (NS) |
| Crestal width immediate (mm) | 5.42 ± 0.38 | 5.95 ± 0.57 | 0.034 |
| Crestal width 6 mo (mm) | 5.30 ± 0.31 | 5.42 ± 0.40 | 0.625 (NS) |
| Labial plate immediate (mm) | 1.85 ± 0.36 | 1.17 ± 0.23 | 0.002 |
| Labial plate 6 mo (mm) | 1.68 ± 0.54 | 0.98 ± 0.32 | 0.002 |
| EWH day 14 | 9.14 ± 1.46 | 6.83 ± 2.23 | 0.009 |

- DB exceeded 70 ISQ threshold at both time points (both primary and secondary)
- MM showed significant ISQ improvement over time (p=0.024), suggesting adequate late osseointegration
- Both groups: significant bone density reduction at 6 months (remodeling woven→lamellar)
- MM group crestal width reduced significantly at 6 months (p=0.040); DB stable

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small sample (n=7, 14 sites) — exploratory, not confirmatory
- Retrospective trial registration
- Short follow-up (6 months) — insufficient for long-term crestal bone maintenance
- Single-operator study (potential learning-curve bias for MM, especially early cases)
- Split-mouth VAS pain reporting susceptible to carry-over effect (central pain processing)
- CBCT densitometry (GSV→Hu conversion) carries inherent variability
- Needs larger multicenter RCTs with longer follow-up

## 6. Related Work

- Lahens et al. (animal model): bur-driven OD → significantly greater BIC and insertion torque vs conventional drilling
- Negidah et al.: increasing ISQ values with MM over time (consistent with MM secondary stability gain)
- Trisi et al.: bone density reduction from immediate to 6 months consistent with woven→lamellar remodeling
- Ghoneim et al.: significantly better crestal bone level preservation at 6 months with Densah bur vs MM in esthetic zone
- Bennardo et al. (SR): low complication rate and high implant survival with MM in general
- Arafat & Elbaz: OD preserved alveolar width following sinus floor elevation; expansion-based techniques associated with crestal resorption

## 7. Glossary

- **OD (Osseodensification, 골밀화)**: Implant osteotomy preparation via compaction rather than excavation; preserves bone chips as autograft along osteotomy walls
- **DB (Densah Bur, Densah 버)**: Rotary OD drill operating in reverse counter-clockwise densifying mode
- **MM (Magnetic Mallet, 마그네틱 말렛)**: Pulse-based OD device; magneto-dynamic technology delivering controlled force impulses (75–260 daN at 80 µs)
- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient, 임플란트 안정성 지수)**: RFA-derived 1–100 scale; ≥70 = high stability threshold
- **RFA (Radio Frequency Analysis, 공명 주파수 분석)**: Non-invasive ISQ measurement via Osstell device
- **EWH (Early Wound Healing) score**: Quantitative wound healing scale 0–10 based on re-epithelialization, hemostasis, inflammation signs
- **LMM (Linear Mixed-Effects Model)**: Statistical method accounting for clustering (multiple sites within same patient)
- **BIC (Bone-to-Implant Contact, 골-임플란트 접촉률)**: Histomorphometric measure of osseointegration quality
- **GSV (Grey Scale Value)**: CBCT output converted to Hounsfield units via Y = 0.682x – 161 formula
- **Sub-crestal placement**: Implant positioned 1 mm below the crestal bone level
