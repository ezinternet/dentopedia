## One-line Summary

Abstract-only SR+MA: diode-laser-assisted pulpotomy showed clinical/radiographic success comparable to other methods at all follow-ups (p>0.05) but significantly reduced pathologic periodontal-space widening (p<0.05), especially with ZOE capping; wavelength did not matter.

## 한줄요약

abstract-only SR+MA: diode laser 보조 펄포토미는 임상·방사선 성공률이 타 방법과 동등(p>0.05)하나 치주공간 병적 확대를 유의하게 감소(p<0.05, 특히 ZOE 복조 시); 레이저 파장은 무관. 유치 펄포토미 대안으로 가능.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Pools diode-laser vs conventional pulpotomy outcomes in primary teeth.
- Identifies a specific safety signal: reduced pathologic periodontal-space widening.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PubMed/Embase/Cochrane to Aug 31 2025; Cochrane RoB; RevMan 5.4; I². (Abstract-level only.)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Clinical/radiographic success: no significant difference vs other methods at any follow-up (p>0.05).
- Pathologic periodontal-space widening: significantly lower with diode laser (p<0.05); further lowered with ZOE capping (p<0.05).
- Laser wavelength: no significant effect (p>0.05). No publication bias.

## 7. Glossary

- **Diode laser pulpotomy**: laser used for pulp-stump hemostasis/disinfection during pulpotomy.
- **Pathologic periodontal-space widening**: radiographic sign of failure (inflammation/internal resorption).
- **ZOE**: zinc oxide eugenol, a pulpotomy capping material.
