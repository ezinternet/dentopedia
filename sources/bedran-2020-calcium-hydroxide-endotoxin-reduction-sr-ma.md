## Why Ingested

이 endotoxin 배치에서 calcium hydroxide(CH) intracanal medication의 LPS 감소 효과를 정량화하는 핵심 SR+MA. [[endodontics/ordinola-zapata-2022-intracanal-medicaments-status-directions]]의 CH 한계 서술과 [[endodontics/irrigation/rossi-fedele-2023-irrigation-dressing-apical-periodontitis-sr-ma]]의 medicament 효과 평가를 endotoxin endpoint로 확장하며, "CH는 endotoxin을 감소시키나 완전 제거는 못 한다"는 배치 thesis의 정량적 근거를 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (9 studies qualitative, 7 meta-analyzed): calcium hydroxide intracanal medication significantly reduced LPS before and after chemomechanical preparation (SMD ≈ −0.9 to −1.1, p<0.001) but did not eliminate endotoxin completely; certainty of evidence very low.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰+메타분석(정성 9편, 메타 7편): 수산화칼슘 근관내 첩약은 화학기계적 형성 전후 모두 LPS를 유의하게 감소(SMD 약 −0.9~−1.1, p<0.001)시켰으나 endotoxin을 완전히 제거하지는 못했으며 근거 확실성은 매우 낮음.

- **Study type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis

## 2. Key Contributions

- Quantifies the LPS-reduction effect of calcium hydroxide (Ca[OH]₂) as intracanal medication (ICM), with and without an added antimicrobial substance (AS).
- Subgroup analysis by Ca(OH)₂ formulation, presence of AS, irrigant solution during chemomechanical preparation (CMP), and incidence of LPS reduction.
- GRADE certainty assessment.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Searches up to June 2020. Included clinical and experimental studies comparing LPS amount before vs after Ca(OH)₂ ICM in infected root canals.
- Risk of bias: RoB 2 / ROBINS-I / NIH pre-post tool. 9 studies qualitative, 7 in meta-analysis (3 low RB, 1 moderate, 2 high, 3 "some concerns").
- Random-effects meta-analysis of standardized mean difference (SMD); GRADE for certainty.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Ca(OH)₂ (±AS) reduced mean LPS **before CMP**: SMD = −1.087 (CI −1.453 to −0.721), p<0.001, I²=58.7%.
- **After CMP**: SMD = −0.919 (CI −1.156 to −0.682), p<0.001, I²=24.7%.
- By irrigant: reduction before CMP SMD = −1.053 (CI −1.311 to −0.795); after CMP SMD = −0.938 (CI −1.147 to −0.729).
- Incidence of LPS reduction: 98.9% with AS vs 61.7% without AS.
- Conclusion: Ca(OH)₂ reduces endotoxin but cannot eliminate LPS completely, independent of irrigant; **very low** certainty of evidence.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very low GRADE certainty; heterogeneity in formulations, irrigants, and LPS quantification.
- Few low-RB studies; mostly pre-post designs without control group.
- Cannot isolate Ca(OH)₂ effect from concurrent CMP/irrigation.

## 7. Glossary

- **ICM**: intracanal medication.
- **CMP**: chemomechanical preparation.
- **AS**: antimicrobial substance added to Ca(OH)₂ (e.g., chlorhexidine).
- **SMD**: standardized mean difference.
- **LPS / endotoxin**: lipopolysaccharide, Gram-negative virulence factor; LAL-assay quantified.
