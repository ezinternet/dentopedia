## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치(2026-05-31)에서 티타늄·지르코니아를 함께, 골세포와 연조직세포를 함께 본 in vitro 기전 연구. CAP가 원소조성은 안 바꾸면서 처리시간 의존적으로 세포반응을 조절한다는 점을 보여, 동물 골유착 결과([[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/zheng-2020-non-thermal-atmospheric-plasma-titanium]])의 세포 메커니즘 근거.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study on titanium and zirconia discs (various surface modifications): cold atmospheric plasma (CAP) did not change the elemental composition of the materials, but regulated proliferation, extracellular-matrix, and adhesion markers in a treatment-time-dependent manner in osteoblast-like MG-63 cells and HGF-1 gingival fibroblasts — beneficial for faster cell proliferation and adhesion on both materials.

## 한줄요약

티타늄·지르코니아 디스크 in vitro 연구(다양한 표면개질): 저온대기압플라즈마(CAP)는 재료의 원소조성은 바꾸지 않으면서, 골모세포양 MG-63과 치은 섬유아세포 HGF-1에서 증식·세포외기질·부착 마커를 처리시간 의존적으로 조절 — 두 재료 모두에서 세포 증식·부착을 빠르게 함.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Clinical Oral Investigations 2022, 26:2949–2963
- DOI: 10.1007/s00784-021-04277-w (Received 15 Jun 2021 / Accepted 1 Nov 2021)
- Study type: in vitro (material + cell)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Covers both titanium AND zirconia, and both osteoblasts (MG-63) AND gingival fibroblasts (HGF-1).
- Shows CAP works without altering elemental composition; effect is time-dependent.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Ti and zirconia discs (different surface modifications) treated with CAP at various durations.
- Cells: MG-63 osteoblast-like, HGF-1 gingival fibroblasts (SEM, fluorescence, adhesion).
- Surface chemistry by EDS; cell adhesion/proliferation/ECM by real-time PCR.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- CAP did not affect elemental composition of the materials (EDS).
- Proliferation, ECM-formation, and adhesion markers differently regulated by CAP application time in MG-63 and HGF-1.
- Conclusion: CAP is beneficial for faster proliferation and adhesion of surrounding-tissue cells on both titanium and zirconia surfaces; healing capacity could enhance osseointegration.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro only; durability of activation and in vivo/clinical effect not tested.
- Optimal application time material/cell-dependent (no single best protocol).
