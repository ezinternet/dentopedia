## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/irrigation/boutsioukis-2022-irrigants-irrigation-methods-status-directions]]는 NaOCl/EDTA + 활성화의 현재 표준을 정리하지만, 2015–2025 신흥 약제(나노입자·허브·광활성)와 멀티소닉/레이저 활성화 시스템을 한 자리에 모은 최신 스냅샷이 없었다. 본 SR(Gupta 2025)은 그 공백을 메워 차세대 세정제·활성화 기술의 임상 함의를 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (PROSPERO CRD420250655029, 2015–2025) of emerging endodontic irrigants (nanoparticle-based, herbal, photoactivated) and activation systems (ultrasonic, laser-activated, multisonic, negative-pressure), concluding they enhance antimicrobial action, biofilm disruption, and smear-layer removal over conventional NaOCl/EDTA but lack long-term clinical validation.

## 한줄요약

근관 세정제·활성화 시스템 최신 발전(2015–2025)을 정리한 체계적 고찰 — 나노입자·허브·광활성 세정제와 초음파·레이저·멀티소닉·음압 활성화가 기존 NaOCl/EDTA보다 항균력·바이오필름 제거·스미어층 제거를 향상시키나, 장기 임상 근거는 아직 부족하다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Consolidates 2015–2025 advances in endodontic irrigation across two axes: (1) novel **irrigants** and (2) **activation systems**.
- **Novel irrigants** covered:
  - **Nanoparticle-based** (silver/AgNP, chitosan, copper oxide) — enhanced biofilm penetration, prolonged antimicrobial action vs NaOCl.
  - **Herbal/natural** (green tea polyphenols, neem, curcumin, Punica granatum, Emblica officinalis) — antibacterial with reduced cytotoxicity; some show better smear-layer removal and penetration than NaOCl with less microhardness reduction.
  - **Photoactivated disinfection** (methylene blue, indocyanine green + laser) — boosts kill of resistant biofilms.
  - **Ozonated water / electrolyzed saline** — potent antimicrobial, improved biocompatibility, stability needs validation.
- **Activation systems** covered: passive ultrasonic irrigation (PUI, piezoelectric), laser-activated irrigation (Er:YAG, Er,Cr:YSGG; SWEEPS shockwaves), multisonic (GentleWave), negative-pressure (EndoVac), manual dynamic activation (MDA).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Databases**: PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Google Scholar.
- **Search window**: 2015–2025.
- **PICO**: P = human patients requiring endodontic treatment; I = novel irrigants and/or activation systems; C = traditional irrigants (NaOCl, EDTA) / syringe irrigation; O = antimicrobial efficacy, biofilm removal, smear-layer removal, cytotoxicity/biocompatibility.
- **Inclusion**: peer-reviewed clinical trials, in vitro, and in vivo studies. **Excluded**: non-English, review/opinion papers, insufficient methodology/results.
- **Quality assessment**: Cochrane Collaboration Risk of Bias tool (for RCTs); 6 studies tabulated in the RoB table.
- **Synthesis**: narrative comparative effectiveness (Table 1 data extraction across 6 representative studies 2018–2024).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **GentleWave (multisonic)** > passive ultrasonic for E. faecalis biofilm removal (Choi 2019, in vitro).
- **Laser activation** achieved greatest AgNP penetration depth > ultrasonic > conventional syringe (Kumari 2024, in vitro).
- **Herbal (P. granatum)** least microhardness reduction; herbal irrigants better smear-layer removal/penetration than NaOCl (Lakshmaiah 2023).
- **Positive-pressure needle irrigation** caused more intense postop pain at 6/24/48 h vs EndoVac negative pressure; no difference later (Topçuoğlu 2018, RCT).
- **Periapical healing**: LAI-SWEEPS and PUI showed greater percentage healing vs control but **no statistically significant difference at 12 months** (Doğan 2024, RCT).
- Conventional irrigants (NaOCl, CHX, ozonated water) all significantly reduced bacterial counts vs control with no significant inter-group difference (Pinheiro 2018).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small evidence base synthesized in the extraction table (6 studies), heavily in vitro; clinical/healing endpoints sparse and underpowered.
- No meta-analysis; narrative synthesis only. Heterogeneous agents/outcomes preclude pooling.
- Authors stress long-term clinical efficacy and routine-practice integration of novel irrigants/activation remain unestablished.
- Stability of ozonated water/electrolyzed saline needs validation.
