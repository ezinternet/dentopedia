## Why Ingested

치주인대(PDL) 재생은 [[periodontics/stavropoulos-2021-periodontal-regeneration-intrabony-nma]]의 GTR/EMD 임상 재생 근거를 생물학적으로 지탱하는 기저 메커니즘이나, 위키에는 bone-PDL-cementum 복합체를 동시 재생하는 공학적 접근 전체를 조망하는 페이지가 없었다. 본 SR은 줄기세포·3D 바이오프린팅·유전자치료·층별 생체모방 재료를 망라하여 [[periodontics/lee-2017-oxysterol-periodontal-regeneration-pdlsc-alveolar-bone]]의 PDLSC 분자기전 연구를 임상적 재생공학 맥락으로 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review of stem cell- and scaffold-based strategies (3D bioprinting, gene therapy, layered biomimetics) for simultaneous bone–PDL–cementum complex regeneration in periodontitis.

## 한줄요약

치주염에서 뼈–치주인대–백악질 복합체의 동시 재생을 위한 줄기세포·3D 바이오프린팅·유전자치료·층별 생체모방 전략을 망라한 체계적 고찰.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First systematic review comprehensively covering regeneration of all three periodontal tissues (bone, PDL, cementum) and their simultaneous complex regeneration
- Demonstrates that PDLSCs seeded on biphasic calcium phosphate (BCP) scaffolds yield new bone and PDL fibers with Sharpey's fiber insertion at correct angles in dog models
- Establishes 3D bioprinting (PCL/PGA fiber-guiding scaffolds) as the most promising approach for generating oriented, perpendicular PDL fiber insertion
- Identifies endogenous cell-homing via SDF-1α/BMP7 delivery as an ethical alternative to exogenous stem cell transplantation
- Shows layered biomimetic (LBL) constructs achieve complete periodontal reconstruction (cementum + PDL at 45° + cribriform bone) within 20 days in dog models

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Systematic review (narrative synthesis)
- **Databases searched**: Not explicitly stated; broad literature review of cutting-edge studies
- **Focus areas**: (1) Bone regeneration, (2) PDL regeneration, (3) Cementum regeneration, (4) Endogenous cell-homing, (5) 3D bioprinting, (6) Gene therapy, (7) Layered materials
- **In vitro + in vivo** animal studies (rat, dog, rabbit, mouse xenograft models) reviewed
- Key cell types reviewed: PDLSCs, BMSCs, DPSCs, hUCMSCs, iPSC-MSCs, GMSCs, DFSCs, ADSCs
- Key scaffold materials: CPC (calcium phosphate cement), PCL, PGA, PLGA, chitosan, collagen, bioactive glass, HA

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Bone regeneration:**
- hBMSC-encapsulated alginate-MB/CPC in rat bone defect: new bone area fraction 42.1% ± 7.8% at 12 weeks (3× control)
- Tri-culture (iPSC-MSC + HUVEC + pericyte) on CPC in rat cranial defect: new bone 45% (4.5×), blood vessel density 50% (2.5×) vs. CPC control at 12 weeks

**PDL regeneration:**
- PDLSC-seeded BCP in dogs: effective PDL with reorganized collagen fibers inserting into cementum and bone at correct angle + abundant blood vessels at 12 weeks
- Fiber-guiding 3D-printed PCL/PGA scaffold: achieved parallel and obliquely oriented Sharpey's fibers with PDL-bone tissue integration
- Absorbable GTR barrier (long-term): CAL-V gain 3.5 ± 2.5 mm at 120 months (vs. 3.3 ± 1.6 mm for non-resorbable) — absorbable superior long-term

**Cementum regeneration:**
- Cell sheet technique (PDL cell sheets + β-TCP): complete periodontal regeneration with well-oriented collagen fibers at 6 weeks in dogs
- CEMP1 addition to 3D PDLC cultures: ALP activity increased 2-fold

**Layered (LBL) constructs:**
- LBL complex (tissue-engineered periodontal membrane + mineralized membrane) in dogs: complete periodontal reconstruction (new bone + cementum + 45° PDL + Sharpey's fiber insertion) at 20 days

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Most evidence is from animal (dog, rat, rabbit) studies; clinical translation still limited — only FDBA/DFBA/Bio-Oss/collagen membrane are commercially approved
- No PRISMA checklist or formal search strategy described (limits methodological rigor)
- Exogenous stem cell therapies in early clinical trial stage; cell-homing, gene therapy, and LBL materials still laboratory-only
- PDL fiber directionality and cementum quality (AEFC vs. CIFC) remain major unresolved challenges in clinical settings
- Vascularization is critical but often overlooked in scaffold design

## 7. Glossary

- **PDL (Periodontal Ligament)**: 치주인대 — collagen fiber bundles connecting root cementum to alveolar bone; transmits occlusal forces
- **PDLSC**: periodontal ligament stem cell — 치주인대줄기세포
- **Sharpey's fibers**: PDL collagen fiber ends embedded in mineralized cementum/bone
- **GTR (Guided Tissue Regeneration)**: 조직유도재생술 — barrier membrane prevents epithelial downgrowth
- **AEFC**: acellular extrinsic fiber cementum — desired regeneration target (vs. inferior CIFC)
- **CPC (Calcium Phosphate Cement)**: 인산칼슘시멘트 — injectable scaffold material
- **LBL (Layer-by-Layer)**: 층별 구조 — biomimetic stratified construct mimicking PDL anatomy
- **SDF-1α**: stromal-derived factor-1α — chemokine for endogenous stem cell homing
- **BMP7**: bone morphogenetic protein-7 — growth factor for bone/PDL induction
- **CEMP1**: cementum protein-1 — cementum-specific marker; promotes cementoblast differentiation
