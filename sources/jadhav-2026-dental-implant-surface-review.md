Narrative review — comprehensive survey of dental implant surface modification (physical/chemical/mechanical), bio-functionalized surfaces, and emerging responsive coatings / 'omics / AI directions.

## 한줄요약
내러티브 리뷰: 임플란트 표면개질(물리·화학·기계적) 전반과 생기능화 표면, 그리고 반응성 코팅·오믹스·AI 등 신경향을 종합 정리.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Materials Science: Materials in Medicine 2026;37:21 (online Dec 2025). Agharkar Research Institute, Pune.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Structured survey of failure causes and surface-modification strategies.
- Highlights bio-functionalized surfaces improving osseointegration and reducing peri-implantitis in healthy and compromised patients.
- Flags under-addressed factors: immune response, in vivo validation.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative review of last-decade literature on implant materials, failure causes, surface modification techniques.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Surface modification considered critical for long-term success; strategies target antibacterial activity, texture, and geometry.
- Bio-functionalized surfaces → better osseointegration, less peri-implantitis.
- Emerging: responsive (stimuli-reactive) coatings, 'omics-guided design, AI translation.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Narrative (non-systematic); many strategies lack clinical validation; immune response and in vivo testing often overlooked.

## 6. Related Work
- Smeets 2016; nanoparticle coating reviews (Rehner 2025).

## 7. Glossary
- Bio-functionalization: biological coating/agent applied to enhance surface response.
