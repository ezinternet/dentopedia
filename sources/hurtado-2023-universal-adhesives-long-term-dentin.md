## Why Ingested
Long-term aging data (µTBS + nanoleakage) for universal adhesives to dentin; supports [[resin-bonding/hurtado-2023-universal-adhesives-long-term-dentin]] and the CSE reference page.

## One-line Summary
In-vitro 6-month aging: CSE and Scotchbond Universal > Xeno Select (higher µTBS, lower nanoleakage).

## 한줄요약
in-vitro 6개월 노화 — CSE·Scotchbond Universal이 Xeno Select보다 µTBS↑·nanoleakage↓.

## 1. Document Information
Materials 2023; 16: 3458. DOI 10.3390/ma16093458. Open access. Universidad Rey Juan Carlos / CSIC, Spain.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Combines µTBS and nanoleakage over 6-month aging.
- Reinforces 10-MDP system durability vs weaker comparator.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro µTBS + nanoleakage of universal adhesives to dentin after 6-month water storage.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
CSE & Scotchbond Universal > Xeno Select: higher µTBS, lower nanoleakage after aging.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; two-adhesive scope; clinical correlation needed.

## 6. Related Work
Brkanović 2023 (universal-adhesive dentin SBS).

## 7. Glossary
- µTBS: microtensile bond strength.
- Nanoleakage: nanoscale silver/dye penetration indicating hybrid-layer degradation.
