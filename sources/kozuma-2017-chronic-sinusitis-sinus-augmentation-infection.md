## Why Ingested

측방 접근 상악동거상술 (Lateral Sinus Augmentation, LSA) 후 수술 후 감염 및 임플란트 실패의 원인 규명이 임상적으로 중요하며, 기존 [[wiki/sinus-lift/lateral/diaz-olivares-2021-schneiderian-membrane-perforation-sinus-lift]]가 슈나이더 막 천공 중심의 합병증을 다루나, 술 전 만성 부비동염 (Chronic Sinusitis, CS)이 독립적 위험인자임을 다변량 분석으로 제시한 연구가 없었음. 이 논문은 OMS 예방 축 (술 전 부비동 건강 스크리닝 필요성)의 근거로 활용됨. [[wiki/overviews/odontogenic-maxillary-sinusitis-overview]]의 "preop screening → sinus clearance before augmentation" 논거를 보강함.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective cohort (109 patients, 121 sinuses, 252 implants) found preoperative chronic sinusitis was the strongest predictor of postoperative infection (p=0.007) and implant loss (p=0.007) after lateral sinus augmentation, mandating preoperative ENT evaluation and treatment.

## 한줄요약

후향적 코호트 연구 (109명, 121개 상악동, 252개 임플란트): 술 전 만성 부비동염이 측방 상악동거상술 후 감염(p=0.007) 및 임플란트 실패(p=0.007)의 가장 강력한 예측인자였으며, 수술 전 이비인후과적 평가 및 치료가 필수임을 시사.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Identified preoperative chronic sinusitis as the single strongest independent risk factor for both postoperative infection AND implant loss following lateral sinus augmentation.
2. Established a multivariable risk hierarchy: chronic sinusitis > timing of implant insertion (infection axis); chronic sinusitis > sex > diabetes > denture use > sinus membrane perforation (implant loss axis).
3. Proposed clinical protocol: mandatory preoperative ENT/rhinologic evaluation; treat active chronic sinusitis (macrolide therapy, FESS if needed) before proceeding to augmentation.
4. Quantified overall complication rate: 8/121 sinuses (6.6%) experienced postoperative infection/implant loss — comparable to published literature (1.6–8.5%).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Single-center retrospective cohort, Kyushu University / Kyushu Central Hospital
- **Sample**: 109 patients, 121 sinuses, 252 implants; follow-up until osseointegration assessment
- **Outcome variables**:
  - Primary: postoperative infection (purulent discharge, fever, pain requiring reoperation)
  - Secondary: implant loss (any implant in augmented sinus)
- **Predictor variables assessed** (logistic regression):
  - Preoperative chronic sinusitis (ICD/EPOS 2012 criteria: ≥12 weeks symptoms + endoscopic/CT evidence)
  - Timing of implant placement (simultaneous vs. staged)
  - Sex, age, smoking, diabetes
  - Postoperative denture use
  - Intraoperative sinus membrane perforation
  - Graft material type
- **Statistical analysis**: Univariate + multivariate logistic regression; p < 0.05 significance threshold
- **Chronic sinusitis diagnosis**: Endoscopy + CBCT/CT + SNOT-20 symptom scoring pre-surgery

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### Overall Complications
- Postoperative infection + implant loss: **8/121 sinuses (6.6%)**
- All 8 complicated cases involved at least partial implant loss

### Postoperative Infection — Logistic Regression
| Predictor | OR (multivariate) | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| Preoperative chronic sinusitis | Strongest | **0.007** |
| Timing of implant insertion | Second | — |
| Sinus membrane perforation | NS | — |
| Diabetes | NS | — |

### Implant Loss — Logistic Regression
| Predictor | OR (multivariate) | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| Preoperative chronic sinusitis | Strongest | **0.007** |
| Sex (male) | Second | — |
| Diabetes | Third | — |
| Postoperative denture use | Fourth | — |
| Sinus membrane perforation | Fifth | — |

### Chronic Sinusitis Prevalence
- Chronic sinusitis identified preoperatively in a subset of the 109-patient cohort
- All 8 complication cases occurred in patients with preoperative chronic sinusitis or suboptimally treated sinuses

### Management Protocol Proposed
1. Preoperative: SNOT-20 + endoscopy + CT; ENT referral if chronic sinusitis confirmed
2. Medical treatment: Macrolide antibiotic therapy (clarithromycin) for ≥12 weeks or FESS
3. Confirm sinus clearance before proceeding to augmentation
4. Perioperative: careful Schneiderian membrane management; avoid simultaneous implant if high-risk

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Single-center retrospective design; selection bias possible
- Relatively small sample (121 sinuses); insufficient power for subgroup OR with confidence intervals
- Chronic sinusitis definition relied on clinical criteria; no standardized histopathologic confirmation
- Follow-up endpoint was osseointegration, not long-term implant survival (5y+)
- The paper is a Springer Link web-rendered page — full statistical tables may not have been fully captured by text extraction; some OR values were not numerically reported in the abstract
- Authors suggest prospective study with standardized sinusitis workup and longer follow-up

## 7. Glossary

- **만성 부비동염 (Chronic Sinusitis, CS)**: ≥12주 지속되는 부비동 점막 염증; 내시경·CT·증상 기준으로 진단 (EPOS 2012 criteria)
- **슈나이더 막 천공 (Schneiderian Membrane Perforation, SMP)**: 측방 상악동거상술 시 상악동 내막 파열; 골 이식재 누출 및 감염 위험 증가
- **SNOT-20**: 20-item Sino-Nasal Outcome Test; 만성 비부비동염 증상·삶의 질 평가 설문
- **FESS (Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery)**: 기능적 내시경 부비동 수술; 만성 부비동염 수술적 치료 표준
- **클라리스로마이신 (Clarithromycin)**: 마크로라이드 계열 항생제; 만성 부비동염 점액 분비 억제 및 항염 효과
- **측방 접근 상악동거상술 (Lateral Sinus Augmentation, LSA)**: 측벽 골창을 통해 상악동저를 거상하여 골이식하는 술식
