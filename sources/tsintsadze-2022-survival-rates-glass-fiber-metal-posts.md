## Why Ingested

Founding paper for the new `post-and-core` category (previously a gap — only tangential fiber-post bonding mentions in [[resin-bonding/dhawan-2026-bonding-total-self-etch-fiberpost]]). This SR+MA of RCTs answers the core material-selection question — glass-fiber vs metal post survival — and provides the quantitative anchor (92.8% vs 78.1% survival, no statistically significant difference) for the field. Extends the bonding-focused fiber-post material into a clinical-outcome framing.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 7 RCTs (≥2-yr follow-up): endodontically treated teeth restored with glass-fiber posts had 92.8% survival vs 78.1% for metal posts, but the difference was NOT statistically significant; both are reliable when a post is indicated.

## 한줄요약

RCT 7편 SR+MA(≥2년 추적): 근관치료치아를 유리섬유 포스트로 수복 시 생존율 92.8%, 금속 포스트 78.1%였으나 통계적 차이는 없었음 — 포스트가 필요한 상황에서 둘 다 신뢰할 만한 재료.

## 1. Document Information

Systematic review and meta-analysis (PRISMA/PICOS), published in *The Journal of Prosthetic Dentistry* (2022;131(4):567-578). Abstract-only ingest (no PMC full text). Searched Medline, Embase, Scopus + manual; RCTs with ≥2-year follow-up; random-effects meta-analyses (RevMan), odds ratios with 95% CI, publication-bias assessment (funnel plots, Begg, Egger).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Direct head-to-head clinical comparison of glass-fiber-reinforced vs metal post survival/failure in ETT, restricted to RCTs.
- Quantified pooled survival: 92.8% (glass-fiber) vs 78.1% (metal).
- Concluded both materials are clinically reliable when significant coronal structure is lost and a post is indicated.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PICOS-structured search of three databases plus hand search → 184 + 4 records → 23 full-text → 7 RCTs extracted. Homogeneity by chi-square / I-square; random-effects model; OR + 95% CI (alpha=.05).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Overall survival: glass-fiber 92.8% vs metal 78.1%.
- No statistically significant difference in survival, success, or failure rates (P > .05).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Only 7 RCTs met inclusion (small evidence base). Abstract-only ingest limits extraction of subgroup data (tooth type, ferrule, follow-up stratification). Numerical 14.7-percentage-point survival gap favoring fiber posts did not reach significance — likely underpowered.

## 7. Glossary

- **ETT** — endodontically treated tooth.
- **Fiber-reinforced post** — glass/quartz fiber post with elastic modulus close to dentin.
- **Ferrule** — circumferential band of sound coronal dentin (≥1.5-2 mm) encircled by the crown.
