## Why Ingested

literature-surveillance `primary-pulpotomy-ssc` sweep(2026-06-20) 적립 SR+MA. SSC의 심미적 대안인 prefabricated zirconia crown(PZC)을 정량 비교해 위키 Hall/SSC 클러스터([[wiki/caries/konukman-turker-2026-hall-vs-modified-hall-primary-molars-rct]])를 crown-material 축으로 확장. abstract-only(no PMC OA).

## One-line Summary

Abstract-only SR+MA (7 RCTs, 700 crowns; 3 pooled): prefabricated zirconia crowns and stainless steel crowns had comparable retention (OR 0.96, 95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93) in primary molars, with PZCs showing better gingival health and less plaque but possibly more antagonist wear and strongly higher parental esthetic satisfaction.

## 한줄요약

abstract-only SR+MA(RCT 7편, 크라운 700개; 3편 pooled): 유구치에서 기성 지르코니아 크라운(PZC)과 SSC의 유지력 동등(OR 0.96, 95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93), PZC가 치은건강·플라크에서 우수하나 대합치 마모 가능성↑·비용↑·부모 심미 만족도 강하게 우위.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Pools PZC vs SSC outcomes in primary molars across retention, gingival health, plaque, antagonist wear, satisfaction.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PRISMA 2020; RCTs 2018–2024; Cochrane RoB2; 7 RCTs (n=700 crowns), 3 in meta-analysis. (Abstract-level only.)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Retention: no significant difference (OR 0.96; 95% CI 0.40–2.32; p=0.93).
- PZCs: better gingival health, less plaque accumulation.
- Possible increased antagonist wear with PZCs (greater hardness).
- Parental satisfaction strongly favored PZCs (esthetics).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 3 of 7 RCTs pooled; cost and antagonist wear flagged; abstract-only.

## 7. Glossary

- **Prefabricated zirconia crown (PZC)**: tooth-colored preformed crown for primary molars; esthetic but technique-sensitive.
- **Antagonist wear**: wear of opposing teeth from a harder crown material.
