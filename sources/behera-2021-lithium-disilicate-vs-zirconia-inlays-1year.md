- Class II inlay restorations evaluated clinically
- Both materials showed satisfactory 1-year clinical performance

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
One-year prospective RCT (n=30 patients, 15 per group) comparing lithium disilicate (IPS e.max Press) vs zirconium dioxide (Dentcare Zirconia) Class II inlay restorations. Internal surfaces of ZrO₂ inlays treated differently. Clinical evaluation using standard criteria. Both materials showed satisfactory 1-year clinical performance.
