## Why Ingested

구강암 수술 중 생리식염수 세정(Saline Irrigation)의 효과에 대한 임상 근거가 부족한 상황에서, 이 전향적 연구는 설득력 있는 세포학적 증거를 제공한다. 수술 직후 상처 오염을 55%에서 7.6%로 줄였다는 정량적 결과는 [[oral-surgery/al-aroomi-2025-cgf-third-molar-postop-sr]]의 창상 치유 맥락과 연결되며, 세정 프로토콜의 근거 기반을 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

Prospective cohort (n=132) of oral cavity SCC surgery demonstrating that normal saline irrigation significantly reduced wound contamination by exfoliated epithelial cells from 55% to 7.6% (p<0.001).

## 한줄요약

구강 편평세포암 (Squamous Cell Carcinoma, SCC) 수술 132명 전향적 코호트 — 생리식염수 (Normal Saline) 세정으로 박리 상피세포 오염률을 55%에서 7.6%로 유의하게 감소 (p<0.001).

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Asian Pacific Journal of Cancer Prevention, 2025;26(2):657–661
- **Institution**: Mahavir Cancer Sansthan (MCS), Patna, India — Head and Neck Oncology Department
- **Study type**: Prospective consecutive cohort
- **Ethics**: RMRI/EC/53/2022

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First study from an Indian head and neck oncology unit to quantify wound contamination with exfoliated epithelial cells during oral cavity SCC resection.
2. Demonstrates that 55% of wound sites contain epithelial cells or cellular debris after radical resection — raising concern about implantation metastasis.
3. Provides cytological evidence that mechanical cleansing with isotonic normal saline reduces contamination to 7.6% (p<0.001) without cytotoxic agents.
4. Identifies perineural invasion (PNI) as the only clinico-pathological predictor significantly associated with lavage positivity (p=0.037).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Population**: 132 consecutive treatment-naïve oral cavity SCC patients undergoing surgical excision with neck dissection. 28 excluded for incomplete data; 104 analysed.

**Procedure**:
- Tumour bed and neck incisions washed with 500–1500 ml of isotonic normal saline after extirpation
- Wash effluent collected → cell block cytology (H&E stain; two Onco-pathologist consensus)
- Smears taken from tumour bed and neck incision edges → Giemsa stain

**Classifications**: Positive (epithelial/abnormal cells), Suspicious (cellular debris), Negative. Combined positive+suspicious = "positive" for analysis.

**Statistics**: Chi-square tests (categorical), Mann-Whitney/Student T (continuous). Microsoft Excel + Jamovi.

**Limitation**: No immunohistochemical staining; cell block cytology accuracy 85–90%.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Timepoint | Positivity rate |
|---|---|
| Before saline wash (cell block) | 55% (57/104) |
| After saline wash (smear cytology) | 7.6% (8/104) |
| Reduction | 47.4 percentage points |
| Statistical significance | chi² = 29.8, p < 0.001 |

- Epithelial cells in wash effluent: 24%; cellular debris: 21%
- Perineural invasion: only significant predictor (p=0.037)
- Site, T-stage, N-stage, grade, LVI, margin — all NS

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Cell block cytology accuracy ~85–90%; IHC (e.g. pancytokeratin) would improve epithelial cell identification
- No cytotoxic comparison arm (povidone-iodine, hypotonic saline) — cannot compare efficacy against other agents
- Retrospective contamination before collection cannot be fully excluded
- Whether the residual 7.6% contamination translates to implantation metastasis risk is not studied

## 6. Related Work

- Other head and neck cancer series found 7.5–26% malignant cells and 13–19% suspicious in wound washings (present: 24% + 21%)
- Lung cancer pleural washings: 33% malignant cells
- Povidone-iodine achieves near-total cell kill; hypotonic water (15 min) causes cell lysis
- Present study establishes isotonic normal saline as effective through mechanical (not cytotoxic) action

## 7. Glossary

- **SCC (Squamous Cell Carcinoma)**: 편평세포암
- **Implantation metastasis**: 종양 세포의 수술 창상 이식 전이
- **Cell block cytology**: 세포 블록 세포학 — 세정액을 원심분리 후 파라핀 포매하여 조직 절편처럼 검사
- **Perineural invasion (PNI)**: 신경주위 침윤 — 악성 세포가 신경 주위 공간으로 침투
- **LVI (Lymphovascular invasion)**: 림프혈관 침윤
- **WPOI (Worst pattern of invasion)**: 가장 나쁜 침윤 양상 — 조직학적 예후 지표
