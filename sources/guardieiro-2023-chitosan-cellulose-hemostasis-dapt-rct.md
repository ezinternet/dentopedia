## One-line Summary
Within-person, single-blind RCT (J Evid Based Dent Pract 2023, n=DAPT 환자): 이중 항혈소판 (DAPT) 환자 발치에서 chitosan-기반 국소 지혈제 vs cellulose oxidized gauze — chitosan이 출혈시간 단축 + 치유 개선; DAPT 환자 발치 시 chitosan 지혈제 우위.

## 1. Document Information
- Authors: Guardieiro B, Santos-Paul MA, Furtado RHM, Dalçóquio T, Salsoso R, Neves IL, Neves RS, Cavalheiro Filho C, Baracioli LM, Nicolau JC
- Type: Original article (RCT), J Evid Based Dent Pract, Sep 2023
- Design: within-person, single-blind, randomized

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Head-to-head RCT comparing two local hemostatic methods in DAPT patients undergoing dental extraction.
2. Chitosan-based hemostat reduces bleeding time vs cellulose oxidized gauze.
3. Chitosan improves wound healing.

## 3. Methodology
- Within-person design (each patient = own control)
- Single-blind randomization
- Population: DAPT patients undergoing dental extraction
- Outcomes: bleeding time, healing
- Interventions: chitosan-based hemostat vs cellulose oxidized gauze

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Chitosan-based hemostat:
- Reduced bleeding time vs cellulose oxidized gauze
- Improved healing

## 5. Limitations
- Single-blind (operator not blinded)
- Sample size constrained by within-person design
- Single center
