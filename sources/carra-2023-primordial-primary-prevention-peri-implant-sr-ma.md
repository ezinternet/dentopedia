## One-line Summary
JCP 2023 EFP/AAP Workshop SR+MA (Univ Paris): peri-implant 질환의 primordial (식립 전 위험인자 통제) + primary (식립 후 건강 유지) 예방 — observational+interventional studies ≥6 months follow-up; 흡연·치주염 history·잘못된 oral hygiene 등 risk factor control이 peri-implant mucositis·peri-implantitis 예방 효과.

## 2. Key Contributions
- EFP S3 가이드라인 supporting SR+MA.
- Primordial (pre-placement) + primary (post-placement) prevention 구분.
- Modifiable risk factors quantified.

## 3. Methodology
SR+MA up to Aug 2022; interventional + observational studies; ≥6 mo follow-up; outcomes peri-implant mucositis and/or peri-implantitis.

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Risk factor control (smoking cessation, periodontal management before implant, oral hygiene reinforcement) reduces peri-implant mucositis and peri-implantitis incidence.

## 5. Limitations
- Mixed observational/interventional
- Heterogeneity in risk factor definitions
