## Why Ingested

재건적 임플란트주위염 수술의 부가가치(질환 해소 vs 골충전)를 비수술치료 후 supportive care와 직접 비교. [[implants/peri-implantitis/monje-2025-surface-decontamination-reconstructive-peri-implantitis]](재건 술식 내부 비교)와 짝을 이뤄, "재건을 추가할 가치가 있는가"라는 상위 결정 질문을 다룸.

## One-line Summary
RCT (34 patients) — after non-surgical therapy, reconstructive surgery vs supportive care alone gave equal disease resolution (~44%), but ~3× greater radiographic bone fill (1.21 vs 0.36 mm).

## 한줄요약
RCT(환자 34명): 비수술치료 후 재건수술과 지지치료만 비교 — 질환 해소율은 동등(~44%)이나 방사선상 골충전은 재건군이 약 3배(1.21 vs 0.36mm).

## 1. Document Information
- Clinical Implant Dentistry and Related Research 2025;27(2):e70024 (with later correction). UIC Barcelona. NCT05168891 (registered after recruitment).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Isolates the added value of reconstructive surgery over supportive care, both after standardized non-surgical therapy.
- Disease resolution did not differ; bone fill did.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- RCT. 36 enrolled, 34 completed. All received standardized non-surgical therapy; non-responders randomized to reconstructive group (RG) vs non-reconstructive/supportive (NRG, SPT q3mo).
- Intrabony defects ≥3 mm. Primary: mean radiographic bone change at 12 mo (Mann–Whitney). PROs assessed.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Disease resolution: NRG 8/18 (44.4%) vs RG 7/16 (43.8%), no significant difference.
- Radiographic bone fill ~3× greater in RG: 1.21 ± 0.92 mm vs 0.36 ± 0.59 mm (significant).
- No significant difference in clinical variables.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small n; 12-month; trial registered after enrollment; resolution endpoint may undervalue radiographic gain. Longer follow-up on whether bone fill translates to survival/stability.

## 6. Related Work
- Monje 2025 decontamination RCT; EFP S3 peri-implant guideline (Herrera 2023).

## 7. Glossary
- RG: reconstructive group. NRG: non-reconstructive group. SPT: supportive peri-implant therapy.
