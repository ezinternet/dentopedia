## One-line Summary
Narrative review of bone density classification (Misch D1–D4, Lekholm-Zarb), measurement methods (CBCT HU), and clinical implications for implant treatment planning, surgical technique, and loading protocols.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Research in Medical Sciences 2025; 13(9):3904–3907
- **Study type**: Narrative review

## 2. Key Contributions
- Concise review of Misch D1–D4 and Lekholm-Zarb bone quality classification systems
- CBCT Hounsfield unit (HU) thresholds for bone density assessment
- Clinical protocol adaptations: undersized drilling in D3/D4, modified loading in poor bone
- Consolidates practical guidelines for implant planning based on bone density
