## Why Ingested

[[wiki/implants/vertical-ridge-augmentation/cucchi-2024-ptfe-mesh-ti-mesh-vertical-ridge-rct]] (primary RCT)는 골증대량·합병증을 보고했으나 PROMs, 비용, 수술시간을 다루지 않았다. 이 2차 분석이 그 공백을 채운다 — 실제 환자 경험(통증·불안·NSAID 사용량), 비용(~€1200–1300), 수술 계획 시간(PTFE 26분 vs Ti 84분)이 임상 선택의 핵심 데이터임.

## One-line Summary

RCT secondary analysis (n=48): CAD/CAM Ti mesh non-inferior to PTFE mesh in complications (8.3% vs 12.5%); PROMs, pain, costs statistically equivalent; Ti mesh requires 3× longer pre-op planning (84 vs 27 min).

## 한줄요약

RCT 2차분석 (n=48): CAD/CAM 티타늄 메쉬가 PTFE 메쉬 대비 합병증 비열등(8.3% vs 12.5%); 통증·비용 동등; 단, 수술 전 계획 시간은 Ti 메쉬가 3배 이상 소요(84 vs 27분).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First RCT-based comparison of patient-reported outcomes (PROMs) for two digital VRA approaches
- Non-inferiority confirmed: Ti mesh ≥ PTFE mesh in complications
- Documents the true time/cost burden of digital GBR (both expensive and time-intensive)
- Highlights clinician anxiety (~6.4/10) even for experienced operators — normalization data
- Sedation protocol effectiveness demonstrated (anxiety 4.5 → 1.5 post-op)
- 90% patients symptom-free by 2 weeks regardless of mesh type

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Design:** Non-inferiority, parallel-group, double-blind RCT; secondary analysis
**Groups:**
- CONTROL: Ti-reinforced PTFE mesh (RPM, Osteogenics) + collagen membrane
- TEST: CAD/CAM Ti mesh (YxossCBR, Reoss) + collagen membrane
**Graft:** 50:50 autogenous + DBBM (Bio-Oss) + venous blood
**Healing:** 6 mo (mandible) / 9 mo (maxilla) → re-entry + computer-guided implant placement
**Primary outcome:** Healing complication rate (non-inferiority margin Δ=0.1)
**Secondary outcomes:** Operative times (pre- + intra-op), costs (material itemization), PROMs (anxiety/VAS, pain/VAS, NSAIDs, function/5-pt, OHIP-14, PoSSe, willingness to repeat), ClinROs

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | PTFE mesh | Ti mesh | Significance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Complications | 12.5% (3/24) | 8.3% (2/24) | Non-inferior (p=0.645) |
| Pre-op planning time | 26.6 ± 11.9 min | 84.4 ± 15.5 min | p<0.0001 |
| Intra-op surgical time | 121.7 ± 37.2 min | 118.2 ± 31.3 min | NS |
| Total operative time | 148.3 ± 41.0 min | 221.1 ± 30.0 min | p<0.0001 |
| Total material cost | €1200 ± 177 | €1272 ± 148 | NS (p=0.132) |
| Pre-op anxiety (VAS) | 5.3 | 3.9 | NS |
| Post-op pain day 1 | 2.7 | 2.2 | NS (p=0.565) |
| NSAIDs day 1 | 2.3 doses | 2.2 doses | NS (p=0.599) |
| OHIP-14 | ~12 | ~12 | NS (p=0.845) |
| PoSSe discomfort | 35.7 | 33.2 | NS (p=0.343) |
| Willingness to repeat | ~66% "no problem" | ~66% "no problem" | NS |

- Clinician anxiety pre-op: 6.3–6.4/10; satisfaction post-op: 8.6–8.7/10
- ~90% symptom resolution by 2 weeks in both groups
- Note: Ti mesh vs CONVENTIONAL PTFE membrane (no digital) = ~€400 cost savings for Ti mesh

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Single-center (COVID forced abandonment of university center)
- No conventional PTFE membrane arm (cannot evaluate full digital benefit)
- Sample powered for non-inferiority only — larger studies needed
- Short-term follow-up for implant outcomes

## 7. Glossary

- **PROMs**: patient-reported outcome measures
- **ClinROs**: clinician-reported outcome measures
- **OHIP-14**: Oral Health Impact Profile (0–56; <25 = good)
- **PoSSe**: Post-operative Symptom Severity Scale
- **Non-inferiority margin Δ=0.1**: Ti mesh accepted as non-inferior if difference in complications ≤10%
- **VBG**: vertical bone gain; mean >5.5 mm in this study (severe defect cohort)
