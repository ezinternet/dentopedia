## Why Ingested

기존 [[dentin-hypersensitivity/jkda-2022-60-4-005]] 및 [[overviews/dentin-hypersensitivity-overview]]의 in-office 탈감작 옵션 비교를 임상 RCT 수치로 보강. Er,Cr:YSGG 레이저 단독/병용이 NaF varnish 대비 우월한지에 대한 직접 근거.

## One-line Summary
Single-session clinical trial (55 patients / 165 teeth, 5 groups n=33) finding Er,Cr:YSGG laser alone or with Gluma reduced DH more than 5% NaF varnish over 6 months, though all treatments significantly reduced sensitivity.

## 한줄요약
임상시험(55명·165치, 5군 각 33치): Er,Cr:YSGG 레이저 단독 또는 Gluma 병용이 6개월간 5% 불화나트륨 바니시보다 상아질 과민증(Dentin Hypersensitivity, DH)을 더 줄였고, 모든 처치가 유의하게 통증을 감소시킴.

## 1. Document Information
- Lasers in Medical Science, 2022. Received 4 Mar 2022 / Accepted 24 May 2022.
- Springer-Verlag London. Funding/COI not flagged in abstract.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Head-to-head single-session comparison of NaF varnish (F), Gluma (G), Er,Cr:YSGG laser (L), Gluma+laser (GL), and varnish+Gluma (VL) with 6-month VAS follow-up.
- Er,Cr:YSGG laser (2780 nm, 20 Hz, 0.25 W, 44.3 J/cm², 150 µs, 1 mm, 30 s) protocol fully specified.
- Demonstrates durability ordering: laser-containing arms > chemical-only arms at 1 week–6 months.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Design: clinical trial, 55 patients, 165 hypersensitive teeth, 5 parallel groups (n=33).
- Outcome: VAS to air-spray stimulus at baseline, 15 min, 1 week, 1 month, 6 months.
- Stats: one-way ANOVA (between groups per timepoint), RM-ANOVA (within/between over time), Tukey post hoc. SPSS v21.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- No significant between-group difference at baseline and 15 min (P=0.063).
- All groups significantly reduced DH after treatment, sustained to 6 months.
- Er,Cr:YSGG laser alone or + Gluma significantly outperformed NaF varnish at 1 week, 1 month, 6 months.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Single session only; no sham/placebo arm; air-spray VAS subjective.
- "Control groups" definition (varnish, Gluma) conflates active comparators with control.

## 6. Related Work
- Compares chemical (Gluma, fluoride) vs photonic (laser) tubule-occluding mechanisms; complements LLLT meta-analysis [[dentin-hypersensitivity/shan-2021-low-level-light-therapy-dentin-hypersensitivity-sr-ma]].

## 7. Glossary
- Er,Cr:YSGG: erbium, chromium-doped yttrium-scandium-gallium-garnet laser (2780 nm).
- Gluma: HEMA + glutaraldehyde desensitizer.
- VAS: visual analogue scale.
