## Why Ingested

기존 [[tmj/zhang-2024-arthrocentesis-injectable-agent-tmd-sr]]는 관절천자(Arthrocentesis) 병용 주입제를 HA·PRP·corticosteroid 중심으로 비교하며 i-PRF는 다루지 않는다. 본 2026 SR+MA(9 RCT, n=320)는 i-PRF 단일 주입제로 범위를 좁혀, pain 1차 outcome에 **Trial Sequential Analysis(TSA)**까지 적용해 근거가 conclusive·sufficiently powered임을 확인한다 — zhang의 injectable-agent 프레임워크를 i-PRF로 확장·정량화하는 근거. (PubMed full text; DOI https://doi.org/10.7759/cureus.110871)

## One-line Summary
Updated SR+MA with TSA (Cureus 2026; 9 RCTs, 320 patients / 374 joints): arthrocentesis + injectable platelet-rich fibrin (i-PRF) significantly reduces TMD pain (MD -1.62 VAS, 95% CI -2.28 to -0.96, p<0.001) and improves MMO (+5.03 mm), contralateral excursion, and protrusive movement vs control; TSA confirms the pain result is conclusive and sufficiently powered; GRADE moderate.

## 한줄요약
업데이트 SR+MA + TSA (Cureus 2026; 9 RCT, 환자 320명/관절 374개): 관절천자(Arthrocentesis)에 주입형 혈소판농축섬유소(i-PRF) 병용 시 악관절증(TMD) 통증 유의 감소(VAS MD -1.62, 95% CI -2.28~-0.96, p<0.001), 최대개구량(MMO) +5.03 mm·반대측 측방운동·전방운동 개선; TSA로 통증 결과가 결정적·충분 검정력 확인; GRADE moderate.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First i-PRF-specific SR+MA of arthrocentesis adjunct to add **Trial Sequential Analysis (TSA)** to the pain primary outcome — moving beyond "significant" to "conclusive and sufficiently powered."
- 9 RCTs / 320 patients (374 joints), 2021-2026, five countries; RCTs only.
- Pooled pain reduction MD -1.62 VAS (95% CI -2.28 to -0.96, p<0.001), significant at 3, 6, and 9-12 mo subgroups and across all pain types (chewing, jaw movement, palpation).
- TSA Z-curve crossed both conventional and sequential monitoring boundaries → no further trials needed for the pain endpoint.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PRISMA + Cochrane Handbook. Search PubMed, Scopus, WoS, Cochrane CENTRAL inception → May 1, 2026; English filter.
- PICO: symptomatic TMD internal derangement; arthrocentesis (Ringer's/saline lavage) + intra-articular i-PRF; vs arthrocentesis alone or conservative/no active Rx; ≥1 predefined outcome.
- Primary: VAS pain change. Secondary: MMO, contralateral/right/left lateral excursion, protrusive movement, QoL (OHIP-14).
- Random-effects MD with 95% CI; I² >50% = substantial heterogeneity. Leave-one-out sensitivity, DOI plot + LFK index, follow-up subgroups (3/6/9-12 mo), pain-type subgroup, meta-regression (pain vs baseline MMO).
- TSA alpha 0.05, power 95%. ROB-2 + GRADE. Stata/MP 19.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample sizes; short-to-intermediate follow-up; substantial heterogeneity (Wilkes stage, lavage/centrifugation/injection protocol variation).
- QoL from only 2 studies (imprecise); possible small-study effects / publication bias; no patient-level data → no severity subgrouping.
- Comparator pooling is mixed (AC alone, conservative, and some i-PRF-only-vs-Ringer's protocols), which loosens the "AC + i-PRF vs AC alone" interpretation; larger standardized RCTs with longer follow-up requested.

## 7. Glossary

- **i-PRF (Injectable Platelet-Rich Fibrin)**: 주입형 혈소판농축섬유소 — second-generation autologous platelet concentrate, prepared without anticoagulant; liquid fibrin scaffold with sustained growth-factor release.
- **Arthrocentesis (관절천자)**: minimally invasive upper-joint-space lavage (Ringer's lactate/saline) to flush inflammatory mediators and reduce intra-articular pressure.
- **TSA (Trial Sequential Analysis)**: meta-analytic method adding sequential monitoring boundaries + required information size to test whether the cumulative evidence is conclusive or needs more trials.
- **MMO (Maximum Mouth Opening, 최대개구량)** / **MIO (Maximum Interincisal Opening)**: vertical jaw-opening range in mm.
- **Wilkes stage**: I-V staging of TMJ internal derangement severity.
- **OHIP-14**: Oral Health Impact Profile-14, QoL instrument (0-56).
- **LFK index**: Luis Furuya-Kanamori index for DOI-plot asymmetry (small-study/publication bias).
