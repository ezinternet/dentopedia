## Why Ingested

OMS 상악동거상술 (Sinus Floor Elevation, SFE) 개요의 "예방 축(prevention axis)" 보강을 위해 수집. 기존 [[wiki/sinus-lift/lateral/diaz-olivares-2021-schneiderian-membrane-perforation-sinus-lift]]는 천공 후 수복 결과에 집중했으나, 본 SR+NMA (85연구, 5,551술식)는 술식 유형별(측방창 vs 경치조골) 및 기구별 슈나이더 막 천공 (Sinus Membrane Perforation, SMP) 위험을 NMA로 직접 비교하고 천공이 임플란트 생존율에 미치는 영향을 정량화한 첫 네트워크 메타분석.

## One-line Summary

SR + NMA (85 studies, 5,551 SFE procedures): rotary burs in LSFE carry the highest SMP risk (SUCRA 0.0745) while reamers in TSFE have the lowest (SUCRA 0.9444); each 10% increase in SMP rate reduces implant survival (OR = 0.45, 95% CrI: 0.21–0.71).

## 한줄요약

SR+NMA (85연구, 5,551술식): 측방창에서 회전 버 (Rotary Bur)가 SMP 위험 최고(SUCRA 0.0745), 경치조골에서 리머 (Reamer)가 최저(SUCRA 0.9444); 천공률 10% 증가마다 임플란트 생존율 유의하게 감소(OR 0.45).

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **First NMA** comparing SMP risk across both lateral sinus floor elevation (LSFE) and transcrestal sinus floor elevation (TSFE) instrument/technique subtypes simultaneously.
2. Ranked all SFE techniques by SMP risk using SUCRA (Surface Under the Cumulative Ranking curve) values.
3. Quantified the **dose-response** between SMP rate and implant survival (OR = 0.45 per 10% SMP rate increase).
4. Identified reamer drilling in TSFE as the safest instrument class for SMP avoidance.
5. Identified rotary burs in LSFE as carrying the highest SMP risk among tested approaches.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Bayesian network meta-analysis allowing indirect comparisons across instrument/technique nodes.
- **Outcome 1**: SMP incidence (primary).
- **Outcome 2**: Vertical ridge gain after SFE.
- **Outcome 3**: Implant survival rate stratified by SMP occurrence.
- **Ranking metric**: SUCRA — higher = lower SMP risk (safer technique).
- **Statistical link to survival**: OR with 95% credible intervals (CrI); OR < 1 indicates increased SMP rate predicts reduced survival.
- **Limitations acknowledged**: Heterogeneity across included studies; residual confounders (operator skill, sinus anatomy, membrane thickness); call for RCTs with standardized SMP assessment.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### LSFE (Lateral Sinus Floor Elevation) Technique Ranking by SMP Risk
| Technique | SUCRA | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Rotary bur | 0.0745 | **Highest SMP risk** (worst rank) |
| Piezoelectric / other | intermediate | Moderate risk |

### TSFE (Transcrestal Sinus Floor Elevation) Technique Ranking by SMP Risk
| Technique | SUCRA | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Reamer | 0.9444 | **Lowest SMP risk** (best rank) |
| Osteotome / balloon / other | lower SUCRA | Higher risk than reamer |

### SMP Impact on Implant Survival
- OR = 0.45 (95% CrI: 0.21–0.71) per 10% increase in SMP rate
- P < .001 → statistically significant inverse relationship
- Clinical interpretation: higher perforation prevalence in a procedure set predicts meaningfully lower implant survival

### Vertical Ridge Gain
- Impact of SMP on vertical ridge gain also evaluated (exact values not in abstract).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Heterogeneous study designs (prospective + retrospective pooled).
- SMP assessment not standardized across studies (detection bias likely).
- Indirect comparisons inherent in NMA when head-to-head RCTs are absent.
- Authors call for: RCTs directly comparing LSFE vs TSFE and standardized SMP reporting protocols.
- Cannot disentangle operator experience from instrument type.

## 7. Glossary

- **SFE (Sinus Floor Elevation, 상악동저거상술)**: Surgical augmentation of the maxillary sinus floor to gain bone volume for implant placement.
- **LSFE (Lateral Sinus Floor Elevation, 측방창 상악동거상술)**: Classic lateral window approach; higher SMP risk than TSFE.
- **TSFE (Transcrestal Sinus Floor Elevation, 경치조골 상악동거상술)**: Crestal approach via the implant osteotomy site; generally lower SMP rates.
- **SMP (Sinus Membrane Perforation, 슈나이더 막 천공)**: Intraoperative tear of the Schneiderian membrane; primary complication of SFE.
- **SUCRA (Surface Under the Cumulative Ranking area)**: NMA ranking metric; higher SUCRA = safer (lower risk outcome).
- **NMA (Network Meta-analysis, 네트워크 메타분석)**: Allows simultaneous comparison of multiple treatments using both direct and indirect evidence.
- **OR (Odds Ratio, 오즈비)**: Here: OR < 1 means increased SMP rate associates with reduced implant survival.
- **CrI (Credible Interval, 신용구간)**: Bayesian equivalent of CI; 95% CrI does not cross 1.0 → statistically significant.
