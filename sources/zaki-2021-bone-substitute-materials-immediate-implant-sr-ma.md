## Why Ingested

즉시식립 fixture–socket gap에 골대체재(Bone-Substitute Material, BSM)를 채우는 것이 협측골 흡수·심미·합병증에 미치는 효과의 대규모 RCT 근거(20 RCT). gap grafting을 다룬 [[immediate-implant/sanz-2017-bone-graft-gap-immediate-implant-rct]]·[[immediate-implant/seyssens-2022-immediate-implant-socket-grafting-sr-ma]]를 보강하고, 콜라겐 변수를 더한 [[immediate-implant/liu-2023-collagen-immediate-implant-soft-hard-tissue-sr-ma]]의 모논문 격이다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 20 RCTs (848 patients, 916 sites) showing bone-substitute grafting of the immediate-implant gap reduces horizontal buccal bone resorption (MD −0.52 mm) and improves esthetic score (MD +1.49) but increases complications (RR 3.50), with no effect on implant failure (RR 0.92); GRADE certainty mostly moderate.

## 한줄요약

20개 RCT(848명·916부위) SR+MA — 즉시식립 gap에 골대체재를 채우면 수평 협측골 흡수가 줄고(MD −0.52 mm) 심미 점수가 높아지나(MD +1.49) 합병증은 증가하며(RR 3.50), 임플란트 실패율에는 차이가 없었다(RR 0.92).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Clinical Implant Dentistry and Related Research 2021;23:506–519. Open access.
- Cochrane Netherlands / Julius Center, UMC Utrecht; Cairo University.
- Search of MEDLINE, EMBASE, CENTRAL; ROB 2.0; GRADE; random-effects (generic inverse variance).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Largest RCT-based meta-analysis of bone-substitute use in the immediate-implant socket gap.
- Separates the hard-tissue/esthetic benefit from a previously under-appreciated complication signal.
- GRADE-graded certainty for each outcome.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- SR+MA of RCTs only; two-author screening/extraction.
- Primary outcomes: implant failure, overall complications, soft-tissue esthetics.
- Secondary: vertical buccal, vertical interproximal, horizontal buccal bone resorption, mid-buccal recession.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 20 RCTs, 848 patients (916 sites) from 19,544 references.
- Implant failure: RR 0.92 (95% CI 0.34–2.46) — no difference.
- Horizontal buccal bone resorption: MD −0.52 mm (95% CI −0.74 to −0.30), favoring BSM.
- Esthetic score: MD +1.49 (95% CI 0.46–2.53), favoring BSM.
- Overall complications: RR 3.50 (95% CI 1.11–11.1), higher with BSM.
- Too few head-to-head comparisons of BSM types to pool.
- Certainty: moderate for most; low (failure), very low (complications, vertical interproximal).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Complication outcome at high ROB / very low certainty.
- Unable to rank specific bone-substitute materials.

## 6. Related Work

- Sanz et al. (gap grafting RCT); Seyssens et al. (socket grafting SR+MA).

## 7. Glossary

- **BSM**: bone-substitute material placed in the fixture–socket gap.
- **PES**: pink esthetic score.
- **GRADE**: certainty-of-evidence grading framework.
