## Why Ingested

수술부위감염(SSI)이 분쟁·소송으로 이어지는 메커니즘과, 법원이 의사 책임을 인정하는 구체적 사유를 한국 판례로 보여줌. 발치·임플란트 등 외과적 치과 술식의 술후 감염 분쟁에 직접 대응하는 medico-legal 근거로, 같은 연구팀의 합의서 연구 [[complaint-management/kwon-2017-analysis-of-the-legal-effect]]와 짝을 이루고 기존 medico-legal 적용층 [[complaint-management/JISPCD-14-295]]을 "감염 책임" 축으로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary
Retrospective review of 40 Korean plastic-surgery SSI malpractice precedents (2010–2015): 34/40 won by plaintiffs, mean award 2.83M KRW, with liability driven by failure to culture/treat, failure to refer, poor infection control, and absent preoperative infection consent.

## 한줄요약
한국 성형외과 SSI 의료소송 판례 40건(2010–2015) 후향 분석 — 34건 원고 승소, 평균 배상 283만원; 배상 사유는 배양·항생제 미시행, 상급병원 미전원, 부적절한 감염관리, 술전 감염 설명 부재.

## 1. Document Information
- J Korean Med Sci 2016;31(12):1963–1968. Original Article (Surgery).
- Department of Plastic Surgery, Ewha Womans University Mokdong Hospital, Seoul.
- Open Access (CC BY-NC). Cases obtained from Supreme Court of Korea and lower courts.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Quantifies plaintiff success rate (34/40, 85%) in SSI-related plastic-surgery litigation in Korea.
- Identifies four recurrent grounds on which courts ruled against surgeons.
- Establishes preoperative explanation about infection risk as a liability threshold.
- Documents claim characteristics (most claimants female, breast surgery most frequent).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Precedents collected from Supreme Court Legal Information Service (glaw.scourt.go.kr), LAWnB, and Google Scholar using terms "nosocomial infection," "plastic surgery," "malpractice litigation," "surgical site infection."
- 40 cases identified 2010–2015; full judgment texts obtained by application to the Supreme Court of Korea, raw data from lower courts.
- Cases split into facial vs body surgery, then subcategorized; analyzed for accused parties, institution, complications, monetary amounts, judgment reasons, and breach of care standards.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 40 cases: 35 female, 5 male; mean age 36 (range 24–57).
- 34/40 (85%) won by plaintiff; mean settlement/award 2,832,654 KRW (~USD 2,636.6).
- Most claims related to breast surgery; common complications were deformity, scar, asymmetry.
- Four judgment grounds against surgeons: (1) no immediate bacterial culture / inappropriate antibiotics; (2) no transfer to higher-level hospital or infection-control consult; (3) inadequate SSI control measures; (4) no preoperative explanation about SSI risk.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small n (40) and plastic-surgery–specific.
- Litigated cases only — non-litigated SSI disputes unobserved.
- Descriptive; monetary amounts not adjusted or modeled.

## 6. Related Work
- CDC SSI definitions and epidemiology (2.7% rate; 14–16% of nosocomial infections).
- SSI risk-factor literature (diabetes, steroids, smoking, obesity; operative time, LOS).

## 7. Glossary
- Surgical site infection (SSI): infection at/near the surgical incision within 30 days (or 1 year if prosthetic material implanted).
- Care-standard breach: deviation from accepted practice used by courts to assign liability.
