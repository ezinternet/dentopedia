## Why Ingested

[[wiki/endodontics/vpt/fasoulas-2023-pulpotomy-direct-pulp-capping-sr-ma]]의 재료·modality 비교를 독립 표본(21 RCT, 1,816 참가자, 1,948 치아)으로 재확인하기 위해 ingest. 본 J Oral Sci (Pusa 2025) SR+MA는 DPC에서 MTA가 Ca(OH)2보다 유의 우월(RR 1.21)함을 보여 anchor 결론을 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 21 RCTs (1,816 participants, 1,948 permanent teeth) finding direct pulp capping with MTA significantly more successful than calcium hydroxide (RR 1.21, 95% CI 1.02–1.42, P=0.03), with no significant MTA-vs-Biodentine or partial-vs-full-pulpotomy difference.

## 한줄요약

21편 RCT(참가자 1,816명·치아 1,948개)의 SR+MA로, 직접치수복조에서 MTA가 수산화칼슘보다 유의하게 높은 성공률(RR 1.21, 95% CI 1.02–1.42, P=0.03)을 보였고 MTA vs Biodentine, 부분 vs 전체 치수절단술 간에는 유의차가 없었다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Oral Science, 2025;67(3):107-115
- DOI: 10.2334/josnusd.24-0452
- Design: Systematic review and meta-analysis (PRISMA), RCTs/quasi-RCTs/cluster-RCTs
- Search: CENTRAL/MEDLINE/SCOPUS/CINAHL/ClinicalTrials.gov/J-STAGE/ThaiJo/OpenGrey to Dec 2022
- Included: 21 studies, 1,816 participants, 1,948 treated permanent teeth; 17 comparison types, meta-analysis on 6

## 2. Key Contributions

- Broad multi-database (incl. grey literature) SR+MA spanning DPC, partial and full pulpotomy with Ca(OH)2, MTA, Biodentine.
- Confirms MTA superiority over Ca(OH)2 specifically in direct pulp capping.
- Finds no significant difference between pulpotomy modalities for a given capping material.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Inclusion: RCTs/quasi-/cluster-RCTs; permanent teeth with pulp exposure (caries/mechanical/trauma); normal pulp, reversible or irreversible pulpitis; follow-up ≥6 months.
- Interventions: DPC, partial pulpotomy, full pulpotomy with Ca(OH)2 / MTA / Biodentine.
- Outcome: clinical success (clinical + radiographic criteria).
- RoB: Cochrane 7-domain tool; effect measure RR (95% CI); RevMan 5.4; I² heterogeneity; fixed/random-effects per heterogeneity.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **DPC, MTA vs Ca(OH)2**: RR 1.21 (95% CI 1.02–1.42, P=0.03) — MTA significantly more successful.
- **DPC, MTA vs Biodentine**: no significant difference.
- **Partial vs full pulpotomy** (either capping material): no significant difference.
- Of 17 comparison types, meta-analysis feasible for only 6.
- Bias: all 21 studies low risk for random sequence generation; 100% high performance-bias risk (materials easily recognizable, blinding impractical).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 6 of 17 comparisons poolable; many sparse comparisons.
- Universal high performance-bias risk due to non-blindable materials.
- Heterogeneous diagnoses, follow-up windows.

## 6. Related Work

- Reinforces pulpotomy/DPC material SR+MA (Fasoulas 2023).
- Consistent with network meta-analyses (Komora 2024, Elmsmari 2026): MTA > Ca(OH)2, MTA ≈ Biodentine.

## 7. Glossary

- **VPT** — Vital Pulp Therapy (치수보존치료)
- **DPC** — Direct Pulp Capping (직접치수복조)
