## One-line Summary

Systematic review (5 studies, 364 patients, mean age 7.6 y) of primary herpetic gingivostomatitis (PHGS) management — only weak evidence that early aciclovir shortens lesion healing and symptom duration; most regimens are empiric symptomatic care.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰(5편, 364명, 평균 7.6세) — 1차 헤르페스 치은구내염(PHGS)에서 조기 아시클로버가 치유·증상기간을 줄인다는 약한 근거만 존재, 대부분은 경험적 대증요법.

## 1. Document Information

- Clinical Oral Investigations 2023;27(11):6333-6344. Published online 2023-09-21.
- PROSPERO CRD42023391386. PRISMA-compliant. No external funding declared.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR to consolidate the fragmented PHGS treatment literature under PRISMA/PICO.
- Classifies regimens into antiviral, symptomatic, and combination groups.
- Identifies the ~72 h diagnostic delay as the key barrier limiting antiviral efficacy.
- Highlights absence of RCTs due to rapid onset/remission of disease.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Databases: PubMed, Scopus, Embase. Risk of bias via RoB 2 (RCTs) and ROBINS-I (non-randomised).
- Population: immunocompetent patients with primary HSV-1 gingivostomatitis.
- Interventions catalogued: aciclovir; aciclovir+honey; fluids/analgesics; maalox+diphenhydramine; lidocaine; chlorhexidine ± hyaluronic acid; antimicrobial photodynamic therapy (aPDT); topical antivirals.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 5 studies / 364 patients included. Only one study provided weak evidence that aciclovir improves oral-lesion healing and reduces symptom duration.
- No consensus regimen identified; most interventions are symptomatic and do not affect viral replication.
- Effectiveness of any antiviral is undermined once diagnosis is delayed beyond ~72 h.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small evidence base, heterogeneous outcomes, mostly pediatric cohorts.
- RCTs impractical given rapid self-limiting course; need for early-diagnosis protocols and standardized outcomes.

## 6. Related Work

- WHO HSV-1 seroprevalence estimates (67% under age 50).
- Prior PHGS case series and pediatric antiviral trials.

## 7. Glossary

- PHGS: Primary Herpetic Gingivostomatitis.
- aPDT: antimicrobial Photodynamic Therapy.
- HSV-1: Herpes Simplex Virus type 1.
