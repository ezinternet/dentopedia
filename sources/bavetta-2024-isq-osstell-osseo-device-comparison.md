## One-line Summary
Prospective study (50 patients, 80 implants) comparing Osstell ISQ vs Osseo 100 RFA devices: both reliable and equivalent; ISQ gradually increases through healing; D1-D2 bone significantly higher ISQ than D3-D4 at all timepoints.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Prosthesis 6(2):357-371 (2024)
- DOI: 10.3390/prosthesis6020027
- Design: Prospective, 50 patients, 80 implants, device comparison + bone density subgroup

## 2. Key Contributions
- Osstell ISQ and Osseo 100 are statistically equivalent — either can be used interchangeably
- ISQ follows expected increase trajectory through healing (no device-specific anomalies)
- Bone type (D1-D2 vs D3-D4) significantly influences ISQ at all timepoints

## 3. Methodology
- 50 patients, 80 implants (various sites)
- ISQ measured simultaneously with both devices at each timepoint
- Timepoints: T0 (placement), T1 (loading/3mo), T2 (6mo), T3 (12mo)
- Bone type classified by Lekholm-Zarb (D1-D4)
- Statistical: paired t-test, Pearson correlation between devices

## 4. Key Results
- Device agreement: high correlation between Osstell and Osseo 100 (r>0.95 at all timepoints)
- ISQ trajectory: gradual increase from T0 to T3 in both groups
- D1-D2 ISQ significantly > D3-D4 (p<0.05) at all timepoints
- No device-related complications; no implant failures
- Both devices reliable for monitoring osseointegration

## 5. Limitations
- No blinding (operator aware of device readings)
- Single center
- No stratification by implant system or surgical technique
- 12-month follow-up only

## 6. Related Work
- Herrero-Climent 2013: Osstell ISQ reliability (ICC=0.97)
