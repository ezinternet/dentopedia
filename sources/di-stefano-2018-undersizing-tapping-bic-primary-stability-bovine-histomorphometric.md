## Why Ingested

저밀도골 일차안정성 조절에서 undersizing 정도와 BIC·안정성의 정량 관계를 histomorphometry로 직접 측정한 연구라 인제스트. [[implants/tabassum-2021-undersized-axial-compression-primary-stability]] 와 [[implants/gehrke-2021-healing-chambers-macrogeometry-low-density-drilling]] 의 undersized 효과에, "토크-깊이 적분(torque-depth integral)으로 undersizing 정도를 정량화"하는 측정 프레임을 추가.

## One-line Summary

Histomorphometric bench study on bovine ribs showing the torque-depth curve integral reliably reflects bone-to-implant contact and primary stability even in tapped or undersized sites, with linear relations linking undersizing extent to stability gain.

## 한줄요약

소 늑골 histomorphometry 실험 — 토크-깊이 곡선 적분값이 tapped·undersized 부위에서도 BIC·일차안정성을 신뢰성 있게 반영하며, undersizing 정도와 안정성 증가가 선형 관계.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Validates the torque-depth integral as a proxy for BIC and primary stability.
- Quantifies the undersizing–stability relation linearly.
- Provides a chairside basis to titrate undersizing to a target stability/loading protocol.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- In-vitro histomorphometric study; bovine ribs.
- Implants placed with varying undersizing and tapping; intra-operative torque-depth recorded with instantaneous torque-measuring motor.
- Outcomes: bone-to-implant contact (histomorphometry), bone density, primary stability; correlation analysis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Torque-depth integral reliably reflected BIC and primary stability even in tapped/undersized sites.
- Linear relations among undersizing extent, integral, and primary stability.
- Implication: undersizing extent can be tuned to achieve target primary stability for a planned loading protocol.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In-vitro bovine rib model — no healing/osseointegration.
- Single bone substrate; no clinical loading endpoint.
- Device-specific (instantaneous torque-measuring motor).

## 6. Related Work
- Insertion-torque/ISQ correlation studies (Lages 2018); torque-measuring motor (Capparé 2015).

## 7. Glossary
- Torque-depth integral: area under the intra-operative torque vs depth curve.
- Tapping: pre-cutting threads before implant insertion.
