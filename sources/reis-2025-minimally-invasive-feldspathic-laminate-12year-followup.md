## One-line Summary

Case report (J Esthet Restor Dent 2025) of maxillary anterior diastema closure / morphology correction with thin (<0.5 mm) feldspathic laminates on enamel-limited (~0.2 mm cervical) minimally invasive preparations — HF + phosphoric-acid clean + silane on ceramic, 3-step adhesive + light-cured veneer cement, cotton-roll isolation — surviving 12+ years with no fracture, caries, sensitivity, or repair.

## 한줄요약

상악 전치 치간이개 폐쇄·형태 수정을 thin (<0.5 mm) feldspathic 라미네이트로 시행한 case report (J Esthet Restor Dent 2025). 법랑질 한정 최소삭제(치경부 ~0.2 mm) + 세라믹 HF/인산 세척·실란, 3-step 접착제·광중합 비니어 시멘트, 코튼롤 격리로 접착하여 **12년+ 추적 동안 파절·우식·과민·재수복 없이 생존**.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Documented 12+ year survival** of enamel-limited minimally invasive feldspathic laminates with zero biological/mechanical complication (no fracture, secondary caries, pulpitis/necrosis, or postoperative sensitivity).
2. **Reproducible step-by-step protocol**: additive diagnostic wax-up → PVS index → bis-acrylic mock-up → enamel-limited prep (~0.2 mm cervical knife-edge) → refractory-die feldspathic layering → HF (10%, 90 s) + phosphoric clean + silane → 3-step adhesive + light-cured veneer cement → cotton-roll isolation.
3. **Adhesion threshold restated**: ideal when ≥50% bonding substrate is enamel AND ≥70% of margin remains in enamel; minimizing reduction prioritizes enamel preservation.
4. **Material rationale**: feldspathic on refractory die for optical mimicry/translucency despite low pre-cementation flexural strength (~70–90 MPa).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Single illustrative clinical case (23-yr-old male, well-aligned short rounded maxillary anteriors with diastemas). Minimally invasive, enamel-confined preparation; thin feldspathic laminates fabricated on refractory dies (Creation CC, shade A1). Adhesive cementation under cotton-roll isolation (cited as noninferior to rubber dam for longevity). Evidence weight is **case-report** — illustrative, not comparative; no control or sample.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **>12 years** follow-up, no repairs required; documented at baseline, 3 yr, 12+ yr.
- Only finding: subtle marginal staining attributed to age-related gingival apical migration (minimal cervical exposure) — no esthetic/functional impact.
- Cervical preparation ~**0.2 mm** enamel; laminate thickness **<0.5 mm** ("contact lens").
- Cement light-curing 1000 mW/cm² × 40 s per surface; HF etch 90 s + 37% phosphoric 30 s clean + silane 60 s.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **n = 1 case report** — lowest comparative evidence weight; outcome is anecdotal, not generalizable.
- Single ideal candidate (well-aligned, no discoloration) — does not address cases needing masking/alignment correction (where conventional prep is favored).

## 7. Glossary

- **Feldspathic porcelain** — high-vitreous-matrix ceramic, layered on refractory die; superior optics, low flexural strength pre-bonding
- **Refractory die** — heat-stable die on which porcelain is built and fired
- **IDS** — Immediate Dentin Sealing; improves adhesion/fracture resistance when >50% prep is dentin
- **Knife-edge / featheredge finish line** — minimal-reduction margin that preserves the laminate insertion axis
