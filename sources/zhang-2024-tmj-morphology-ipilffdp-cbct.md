## Why Ingested
즉시식립·즉시부하 전악 보철(IPILFFDP) 후 TMJ 형태 변화를 CBCT로 정량 평가한 희소 임상 연구. [[tmj/zhang-2024-arthrocentesis-injectable-agent-tmd-sr]] 및 [[immediate-implant/]] 카테고리의 TMD risk 정보 보강.

## One-line Summary
Retrospective CBCT study (n=12): IPILFFDP does not significantly alter TMJ morphology at 1 year; ~2 mm VD increase correlates with increased medial joint space.

## 한줄요약
후향적 CBCT 연구 (n=12): 즉시식립·즉시부하 전악 보철 1년 후 TMJ 형태에 유의한 변화 없음; VD 약 2 mm 증가 시 내측 관절강 증가와 상관.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health (2024) 24:1392
- **Study type**: Retrospective observational study
- **n**: 12 participants (18 TMJs)
- **Follow-up**: 1-year post-IPILFFDP
- **Imaging**: CBCT (Mimics 21.0 software)

## 2. Key Contributions
- First study to quantitatively evaluate TMJ morphology before/after IPILFFDP using CBCT
- No significant differences in bilateral TMJ morphology pre vs post (null hypothesis not rejected)
- Posterior joint space significantly increased post-treatment
- ~2 mm VD increase correlated with medial joint space increase (Pearson/Spearman)
- Neuromuscular memory / adapted centric posture (ACP) preservation may explain TMJ stability

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 12 participants without TMJ symptoms (DC/TMD criteria); Jan 2021–Dec 2023
- CBCT at initial visit and 1-year after loading; 18 TMJ/VD measurement items
- VD increase controlled within 5 mm (Abduo threshold)
- Statistical: Shapiro-Wilk normality; t-test / Wilcoxon; Pearson / Spearman correlation

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- No significant pre/post differences in condyle volume, surface area, or joint space bilaterally (overall)
- **Posterior joint space**: significantly increased post-IPILFFDP
- **VD increase**: ~2 mm average → correlated with medial joint space increase
- No TMJ-related adverse events reported at 1-year

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small sample (n=12); single-center retrospective; no control group
- Only patients without pre-existing TMJ symptoms included — cannot generalize to TMD patients
- 1-year follow-up only; longer-term TMJ changes unknown

## 6. Related Work
- Abduo (2012): VD increase ≤5 mm considered safe threshold
- DC/TMD criteria used for patient selection
- Prior studies on conventional implant placement and TMD risk

## 7. Glossary
- **IPILFFDP**: Immediate implant placement with immediate loaded full-arch fixed dental prostheses
- **ACP**: Adapted centric posture — existing comfortable jaw position used as prosthetic guide
- **VD**: Vertical dimension
- **Mimics**: 3D reconstruction/measurement software (Materialise)
