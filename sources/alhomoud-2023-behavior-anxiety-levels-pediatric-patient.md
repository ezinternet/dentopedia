## One-line Summary

Cross-sectional study (n=150 children aged 2–14, Sharjah) measuring behavior/anxiety with Frankl, Venham, and categorical rating scales — no sex difference, but significant age-group differences (the 11–14y group showed the most notable crying/cooperativeness/apprehensiveness effects); children showed heightened anxiety at dental visits.

## 한줄요약

소아 단면연구(2–14세 150명, 샤르자) — Frankl·Venham·categorical scale로 행동·불안 측정. 성별 차이 없음, 연령군 차이는 유의(11–14세군에서 울음 p=.034·협조도 p=.002·불안감 p=.003 가장 두드러짐). 소아는 치과 내원 시 불안이 고조됨.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Clinical and Experimental Dental Research 2023;9:1223–1231
- DOI: 10.1002/cre2.795
- Affiliation: Gulf Medical University; Sharjah Thumbay Dental Hospital
- Study type: Cross-sectional (n=150, age 2–14), 23-week recruitment (Feb–Jul 2022)
- Instruments: Frankl behavior rating, Venham anxiety/behavior, categorical rating; single trained rater post-visit

## 2. Key Contributions
- Provides primary measured pediatric anxiety/behavior data across three validated scales in one cohort.
- Tests age and sex associations: age matters, sex does not (in this sample).
- Reinforces communication with both child and guardian as a management lever.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Guardian consent; exclusions: >14y, guardian refusal, medically compromised.
- Analysis: Mann–Whitney U (sex), Kruskal–Wallis (age groups).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- No significant sex difference in behavior/anxiety across all scales (Mann–Whitney U).
- Significant age-group differences across nearly all scales (Kruskal–Wallis).
- Categorical scale, 11–14y group most notable: crying p=.034, cooperativeness p=.002, apprehensiveness p=.003.
- Overall heightened anxiety at dental attendance.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Single-center, convenience sample; single rater (no inter-rater reliability reported).
- End-of-visit rating only (no pre/post change); no intervention tested.
- Calls for population-tailored behavior-management strategies.

## 7. Glossary
- **Frankl behavior rating scale**: 4-point scale (definitely negative → definitely positive) of child cooperation.
- **Venham scale**: clinical anxiety/behavior rating for children.
- **Categorical rating scale**: ordinal rating of discrete behaviors (crying, cooperativeness, apprehensiveness).
