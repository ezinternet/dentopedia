## Why Ingested
Universal adhesive의 ER vs SEE 적용 모드 비교 3년 RCT. 기존 [[resin-bonding/oza-2022-universal-adhesive-nccl-rct-2year]]·[[resin-bonding/hong-2021-universal-adhesive-etching-modes-sr-ma]]의 "모드 무차이" 근거를 long-term primary data로 보강.

## One-line Summary
Split-design RCT (51 patients, 251 NCCLs) showing Clearfil Universal Bond Quick performs equivalently in etch-and-rinse vs self-etch-with-selective-enamel-etching modes at 3 years (success 82.6% vs 83.8%; retention 87.2% vs 86.3%).

## 한줄요약
NCCL 251개 3년 RCT — Clearfil Universal Bond Quick의 ER 모드와 SEE 모드가 성공률(82.6% vs 83.8%)·유지율(87.2% vs 86.3%)에서 유의차 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- J Adhes Dent 2023;25:133-146. DOI 10.3290/j.jad.b4186751. CC BY 4.0. KU Leuven (BIOMAT, Van Meerbeek group).

## 2. Key Contributions
- 3-year head-to-head of two application modes of a "no-wait" universal adhesive (CUBQ) on NCCLs.
- Confirms no significant difference between ER and SEE modes for any FDI parameter (retention, marginal staining, marginal adaptation, fracture, post-op sensitivity, caries).
- High 3-year recall rate (90%) strengthens evidence quality.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- RCT, n=51 patients, 251 NCCLs: CUBQ-ER (n=122) vs CUBQ-SEE (n=129, 35% phosphoric acid selective enamel etch). Single restorative composite (Clearfil Majesty ES-2). FDI criteria at baseline/1/3 yr. 2-way GEE logistic regression. Excluded severe bruxism, high caries risk, severe/chronic periodontitis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Overall success: 82.6% (ER) vs 83.8% (SEE). Retention: 87.2% vs 86.3%.
- 38 failures total (19 ER, 19 SEE) from loss of retention/fracture/severe marginal defect/discoloration.
- Increase in small clinically acceptable marginal defects (ER 67%, SEE 63.2%) and marginal staining (ER 32.6%, SEE 31.7%).
- No significant between-mode difference for any parameter.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Single adhesive/composite system; 3-year horizon; excluded high-risk patients (limits generalizability to bruxers). Operator effect not isolated.

## 6. Related Work
- Prior 24-month CUBQ trial (SE failures 13%, ER/SEE 100% success), Peumans 2020 review, universal-adhesive mode SR/MAs.

## 7. Glossary
- CUBQ: Clearfil Universal Bond Quick (HEMA 2.5%, 10-MDP, hydrophilic amide monomer, "no-wait" cure).
- SEE: self-etch with prior selective enamel etching.
- ER: etch-and-rinse. FDI: Federation Dentaire Internationale clinical criteria.
