## One-line Summary
J Prosthodont Res 2025;69(4):595-602: cleaning methods after 72h delay between APA and bonding — Ivoclean (SFE 68.4 mN/m) and alcohol (61.8 mN/m) highest SFE; Katana Cleaner reduces SFE; TBS: no significant difference between groups at 3 days; at 150 days (37500 thermocycles), cleaning methods significantly affect durability.

## 1. Key Results
- 140 zirconia discs; 72h delay between APA and bonding (simulates clinical delay)
- 5 cleaning groups: alcohol, Ivoclean, Katana Cleaner, Clearfil directly after APA, Clearfil after 72h delay
- SFE: Ivoclean (68.4) > alcohol (61.8) >> Katana Cleaner (33.1) (P≤0.05)
- TBS 3 days: 60.7 MPa (alcohol) to 51.6 MPa (Clearfil P2) — no significant difference
- 150 days / 37,500 thermocycles: cleaning method significantly affects durability
- Katana Cleaner: reduces SFE — contra-indicated for this application
