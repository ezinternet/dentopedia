## One-line Summary
Prospective 1-year study of 48 Neoss ProActive Tapered implants: IT>40 Ncm in 96% allowed 6–8 week loading; stable ISQ trajectory, no dip observed, MBL −0.47mm, 0 failures.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: International Journal of Dentistry 2020, Article ID 8832500
- DOI: 10.1155/2020/8832500
- Design: Prospective cohort, 48 implants, 1-year follow-up

## 2. Key Contributions
- IT>40 Ncm = safe loading criterion for 6–8 week simplified loading
- ISQ trajectory stable (no classic stability dip in tapered implant design)
- MBL at 1 year: −0.47 ± 0.38 mm (within acceptable range)

## 3. Methodology
- 48 Neoss ProActive Tapered implants, single-tooth replacements
- Loading criterion: IT ≥40 Ncm → provisional at 6–8 weeks
- ISQ measured at placement (T0), 6 weeks (T1), 3 months (T2), 6 months (T3), 1 year (T4)
- MBL measured on periapical radiographs

## 4. Key Results
- IT>40 Ncm achieved in 46/48 implants (96%)
- ISQ stable throughout observation period (no dip)
- MBL: −0.47 ± 0.38 mm at 1 year
- Survival rate: 100% at 1 year
- No complications, no peri-implantitis

## 5. Limitations
- 1-year follow-up only
- No control group (non-simplified loading)
- Single implant system and design
- No randomization
