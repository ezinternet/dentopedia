radiology 카테고리 파노라마 기하·초점골 이해의 기기 특이 근거. [[radiology/devlin-2013-object-position-magnification-panoramic-radiography]]의 확대·왜곡 이론을 다층(multilayer) 장비로 실측 확장, 판독 시 층 선택 함의.

## One-line Summary

Technical mapping of an OP300 Maxio multilayer panoramic device: all 5 image layers curved (39–51°), maxilla anteriorly displaced vs mandible, layers thicker at mandible.

## 한줄요약

OP300 Maxio 다층 파노라마 초점골(focal trough) 매핑: 5개 영상층 모두 곡선(39~51°), 상악이 하악보다 전방 변위, 하악측 층이 더 두꺼움.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Maps shape/location/thickness of focal troughs across 5 image layers of OP300 Maxio.
- All layers curved (39–51°) for both arches; maxillary anterior region anteriorly displaced vs mandibular.
- Layers thicker at mandible than maxilla; inner layers thinner, outer thicker.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Acrylic plate (1156 holes, 34×34) with 20 metal spheres in 15 arrangements at maxilla/mandible levels; panoramic acquisition (66 kV, 8 mA, 16 s); magnification/distortion measured (ImageJ); ≤30% magnification & ≤10% distortion defines the trough.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Quantifies how multilayer (tomosynthesis-like) panoramic image layers differ geometrically — relevant to choosing the right layer and avoiding positioning artefacts.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Single device (OP300 Maxio); phantom geometry; not a diagnostic-accuracy study.
