ARP 봉합 술식이 KT 폭에 미치는 영향을 다룬 [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/park-2016-hidden-x-suture-ridge-preservation]], open-healing 노출막 컨셉의 [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/ionescu-2022-open-healing-flapless-ridge-preservation]]와 짝 — 노출된 d-PTFE 막 자체가 KT를 증가시킬 수 있음을 RCT로 제시.

## One-line Summary
RCT showing intentionally exposed nonexpanded d-PTFE membranes predictably increase the zone of keratinized tissue prior to implant placement (test +6.6–7.1 mm vs control +1.4–2.5 mm at 60/90 days).

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Implant Dentistry 2014;23(3):289–294
- First author: Eliane Porto Barboza
- Type: randomized controlled clinical trial
- DOI: 10.1097/ID.0000000000000060

## 2. Key Contributions
- Quantifies KT gain from an intentionally exposed (open-healing) d-PTFE membrane
- Positions a barrier membrane as a soft-tissue (KT) augmentation tool, not only GBR
- RCT-level evidence for a low-morbidity alternative to grafting for KT

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Design: RCT, test (d-PTFE membrane) vs control.
- Outcome: increase in zone of keratinized tissue at 60 and 90 days.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Test group KT increase: 7.06 ± 2.63 mm (60 d), 6.6 ± 2.84 mm (90 d).
- Control group KT increase: 2.46 ± 1.59 mm (60 d), 1.40 ± 1.40 mm (90 d).
- Conclusion: nonexpanded d-PTFE can predictably increase KT zone in preparation for implant placement.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small clinical trial; short follow-up (90 d).
- KT measured short-term; long-term stability not reported.
