## 2. Key Contributions

- 87 commercially available CBCT devices from 34 manufacturers (11 countries) catalogued; FOV and voxel size most consistently reported.
- Decision-support charts per specialty: prosthodontics/implant, periodontology, OMFS, paediatric/orthodontics, endodontics.
- Documents the large parameter spread driving dose: tube voltage 50–120 kV, current 1–17 mA, DAP 10–5600 mGy·cm², effective dose ~3–500 µSv.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Literature review plus manufacturer websites, manuals, and direct inquiries (through Feb 2025); descriptive + quantitative categorization of acquisition/reconstruction parameters to derive per-specialty device requirements.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Parameter variability across — and within — models markedly changes dose. Authors map indication → required spatial resolution / FOV / exposure, e.g. high spatial resolution + small FOV for endodontics, larger FOV for orthodontics/OMFS, with optimization left adjustable by the operator.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Manufacturer-reported data confirmed for only 10 companies; missing data common; narrative (not systematic) synthesis; rapid technology turnover means charts need periodic updating.
