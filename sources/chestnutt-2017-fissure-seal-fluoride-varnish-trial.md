## Why Ingested

우식 예방 1차술식(실란트 vs 불소바니시)의 상대적 효과를 직접 비교한 대규모 RCT. 기존 [[caries/meyer-lueckel-2019-intervene-root-caries-process-sr-ma]] 등 비복원 예방 근거를 1차 영구대구치 점열구 예방 맥락으로 확장하고, [[caries/urquhart-2019-nonrestorative-treatments-caries-network-meta-analysis]] NMA와 짝.

## One-line Summary
RCT (1016 children, 36 mo) — pit-and-fissure sealant vs fluoride varnish on first permanent molars: no significant difference in dentine caries (FS 19.6% vs FV 17.5%); FV applied in a community program may be more cost-effective.

## 한줄요약
RCT(아동 1016명·36개월): 1차 영구대구치에 점열구실란트 vs 불소바니시 — 상아질우식 발생에 유의차 없음(실란트 19.6% vs 바니시 17.5%); 지역사회 프로그램에선 바니시가 더 비용효과적일 수 있음.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dental Research 2017;96(7):754–761. Cardiff University, "Designed to Smile" Welsh program. Targeted high-deprivation population.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Head-to-head pragmatic RCT of FS vs FV (most prior evidence was each vs no-treatment).
- Clinically equivalent caries prevention at 36 months.
- FV (fewer visits, lower cost) competitive with FS in a school program.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- RCT, 1016 children (FS 514, FV 501), high-deprivation; 6-monthly applications over 36 mo. 835 (82.2%) completed.
- Outcome: proportion developing dentine caries (D4-6MFT) on ≥1 first permanent molar.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Dentine caries on ≥1 FPM at 36 mo: FS 19.6% vs FV 17.5% (no significant difference).
- Sealant retention at 36 mo: maxillary FPM 74.5% intact; mandibular 91.4% intact.
- No adverse effects; high trial fidelity (95% received ≥5/6 treatments).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 36-month horizon; community (not chairside) setting; deprivation-targeted sample limits generalization. Cost-effectiveness context-dependent.

## 6. Related Work
- Ahovuo-Saloranta 2013 (sealant Cochrane); Marinho 2013 (FV Cochrane); Urquhart 2019 NMA.

## 7. Glossary
- FS: fissure sealant. FV: fluoride varnish. FPM: first permanent molar. D4-6MFT: dentine-level caries index.
