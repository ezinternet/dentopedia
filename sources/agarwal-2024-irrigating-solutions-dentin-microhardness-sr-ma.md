## One-line Summary
SR+MA (44 studies assessed) of how endodontic irrigating solutions affect root-canal dentin microhardness, with method/load/dwell-time and sectioning influencing measured values.

## 한줄요약
SR+MA(44편 평가): 근관세정제가 상아질 미세경도에 미치는 영향과 측정법(하중·접촉시간·절단 방향)의 영향을 정량 분석.

## 1. Document Information
- J Funct Biomater 2024;15:132. MDPI. Siksha 'O' Anusandhan / Medical University of Lodz.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Meta-analyzes irrigant effects on dentin microhardness.
- Highlights methodological moderators (sample prep, load, dwell time, sectioning).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PRISMA SR+MA; 44 studies assessed with consistent quality/RoB; microhardness (Vickers/Knoop) outcomes pooled with moderator discussion.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Irrigating solutions (esp. NaOCl, EDTA, chelators/acids) reduce dentin microhardness; magnitude depends on concentration and on testing methodology.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneous indentation methods/loads; in vitro; standardization needed.

## 6. Related Work
- Mechanical-property counterpart to FTIR chemistry review and chelator SR.

## 7. Glossary
- Microhardness: Vickers/Knoop indentation resistance, proxy for dentin mineral integrity.
