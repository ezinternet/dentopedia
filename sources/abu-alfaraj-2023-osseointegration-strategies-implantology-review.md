## One-line Summary
Cross-disciplinary narrative review (Cureus 2023) of osseointegration optimisation strategies: implant design, surface modifications (SLA/SLActive/nanotopography), systemic factors, biologics (PRF, growth factors), and digital workflow integration.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Cureus 2023; 15(10): e47943
- **Study type**: Narrative review

## 2. Key Contributions
- Comprehensive cross-disciplinary overview of factors affecting osseointegration
- Covers implant geometry, surface chemistry, bone biology, systemic factors, and emerging biologics
- Reviews PRF, PRP, BMP-2, and PDGF as biological adjuncts
- Addresses digital workflow for optimised implant positioning
