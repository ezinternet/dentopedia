## One-line Summary
지르코니아(Zirconia, ZrO₂) 단관 vs FPD 5년 후향적 비교 (Medicina 2025): 단관과 FPD 생존율 비교; 임상성적 및 합병증 분석.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Medicina 2025, 61, 210
- **DOI**: 10.3390/medicina61020210
- **Published**: 24 January 2025
- **Study type**: Retrospective cohort; 5-year follow-up

## 2. Key Contributions
- Compared clinical performance and survival of zirconia single crowns vs zirconia fixed dental prostheses (FDPs) over 5 years
- Real-world retrospective data from clinical practice
- Addresses whether zirconia FPDs perform comparably to single crowns in mid-term follow-up
- Assesses chipping, fracture, marginal adaptation, and biological complications by restoration type

## 3. Methodology
- **Design**: Retrospective cohort
- **Follow-up**: 5 years
- **Groups**: ZrO₂ single crowns vs ZrO₂ FDPs
- **Outcomes**: Survival rate, technical complications (chipping, fracture), biological complications (secondary caries, peri-implant/periodontal issues), marginal adaptation
- **Authors**: University of Medicine and Pharmacy Timișoara, Romania + University of Arad, Romania

## 4. Key Results
- ZrO₂ single crowns: high 5-year survival rate
- ZrO₂ FPDs: comparable survival but higher rate of chipping/veneering fracture vs single crowns
- Marginal adaptation acceptable in both groups over 5 years
- Biological complications low for both restoration types

## 5. Limitations
- Retrospective design; no randomization
- Single-center or limited-center data; operator variability not fully controlled
- 5-year follow-up; long-term (>10 year) data lacking
- Zirconia generation (monolithic vs veneered) may not be uniformly reported
