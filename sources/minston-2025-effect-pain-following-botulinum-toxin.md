## One-line Summary
Swedish multicentre double-blind RCT pilot (n=45) — single 100-U Botox session into masseter and temporalis showed no significant change in days with functional jaw pain at 2 months (p=0.585) vs. saline in DC/TMD-diagnosed jaw myalgia.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Oral Rehabilitation 2025;52:587-596
- **DOI**: 10.1111/joor.13915
- **Published**: 2025 (received Sep 2024; accepted Nov 2024)
- **Design**: Randomised, double-blind, parallel-group, controlled, multicentre hypothesis-generation pilot
- **Lead institution**: Postgraduate Dental Education Center / Public Dental Health Service, Örebro and 5 other Swedish specialist clinics
- **Sponsor**: Folktandvården, Regionala Forskningsrådet Uppsala/Örebro, Landstinget Västmanland

## 2. Key Contributions
- First study to use "days with functional jaw pain" (diary-based) rather than pain-intensity VAS as the primary BTX-A outcome — explicitly designed for hypothesis generation about responsive endpoints.
- Conducted across 6 Swedish orofacial-pain specialist clinics with calibrated examiners.
- Strictly DC/TMD-defined myalgia inclusion.
- Negative primary outcome reinforces the Swedish National Board of Health and Welfare's stance that BTX-A is not recommended for myofascial pain.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 45 randomised subjects (23 BTX-A, 22 saline placebo) with DC/TMD jaw myalgia (≥18 y, ≥3 months symptoms).
- Originally planned for 48 due to COVID-19 delays; recruitment expanded across 6 clinics.
- Intervention: 100 Allergan-units of Botox (Clostridium botulinum neurotoxin Type A hemagglutinin complex, AbbVie) in 2.0 mL 0.9% NaCl, distributed across 14 intramuscular injection sites in masseter and temporalis bilaterally.
- Placebo: identical-appearing 0.9% NaCl in same 14 sites.
- Randomisation: computer-generated, sealed envelopes managed by independent personnel at Centre for Clinical Research, Västerås.
- Blinding: double-blind.
- Primary outcome: number of days with jaw pain on function (diary, 14 days pre and at 2-month follow-up).
- Secondary outcomes: clinical examination items, adverse events.
- Follow-up: visit 1 (enrolment), visit 2 (treatment), telephone at 1 month, visit 3 at 2 months.
- Exclusion: pre-existing polyarthritis, fibromyalgia, neuro disorders, prior BTX in jaw/face <6 mo, hypersensitivity, pregnancy.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Final analysis: 23 BTX-A vs. 22 saline.
- Days with jaw pain at function:
  - BTX-A: median 14 (IQR 4) → 10.5 (IQR 6) days at 2 months.
  - Placebo: median 14 (IQR 1.5) → 14 (IQR 5) days at 2 months.
  - Between-group p = 0.585 (not significant).
- Adverse events: mild and transient in both groups; no serious events; similar AE rate.
- Authors conclude one session of BTX-A in masseter + temporalis was not effective in this pilot, but acknowledge underpowering.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Pilot/hypothesis-generation design — n=45 underpowered to detect modest effect sizes.
- Primary outcome ("days with pain at function") is novel and not directly comparable to VAS-based meta-analyses.
- Single dose, single session — no dose-response or repeat-dosing data.
- 2-month follow-up — does not capture longer BTX-A pharmacodynamics or AE evolution.
- Authors suggest an adequately powered RCT could still support efficacy.

## 6. Related Work
- De la Torre Canales 2024 (Drugs umbrella review) — consistent with non-superiority over standard care.
- Saini 2024 (PLoS ONE SR+MA) — null primary findings.
- Sahin 2024 — 4-arm RCT also negative for BoNT-A superiority.
- Swedish National Board of Health and Welfare position: BTX-A not recommended for myofascial pain.

## 7. Glossary
- **BTX-A** (보툴리눔독소 A형, Botulinum Toxin Type A).
- **DC/TMD** (측두하악장애 진단기준, Diagnostic Criteria for TMD; Schiffman 2014).
- **Jaw myalgia** (저작근 통증): TMD pain modified by jaw movement, function, or parafunction with familiar pain on palpation or maximum opening.
- **Days with functional jaw pain**: Diary-recorded count of days with pain triggered by chewing/talking/yawning — novel primary outcome here.
- **Allergan unit (U)**: BoNT-A potency unit per AbbVie Botox.
- **Hypothesis-generation pilot**: Trial sized to estimate effect/feasibility, not to confirm efficacy.
