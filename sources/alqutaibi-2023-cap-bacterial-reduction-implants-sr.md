## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치(2026-05-31)의 임플란트 표면 제염 축. [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/jungbauer-2021-cold-atmospheric-plasma-antimicrobial-sr]]가 일반 구강 병원균이라면, 이 SR은 dental implant 표면 제염(치주염 보조)에 한정해 CAP 살균효과를 종합.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (MEDLINE/PubMed + EMBASE, search to May 2023; 15 studies included): cold atmospheric plasma (CAP) effectively reduced bacterial loads on dental implant surfaces (e.g., Streptococcus mitis, Staphylococcus aureus) and combated biofilm/plaque relevant to peri-implantitis — a promising decontamination option, though evidence remains largely pre-clinical.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰(MEDLINE/PubMed+EMBASE, 2023년 5월까지; 포함 15편): 저온대기압플라즈마(CAP)는 임플란트 표면 세균(예: Streptococcus mitis, Staphylococcus aureus)을 효과적으로 감소시키고 임플란트주위염 관련 바이오필름·플라크를 억제 — 제염 옵션으로 유망하나 근거는 대체로 전임상.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Biomolecules 2023, 13(10):1528
- DOI: 10.3390/biom13101528
- Study type: systematic review (control-group studies on CAP bactericidal effect on implant surfaces)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Implant-surface-specific synthesis of CAP bactericidal efficacy (15 studies).
- Positions CAP as a peri-implantitis decontamination candidate.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- MEDLINE/PubMed, EMBASE (to May 2023). Included studies comparing CAP vs control with bactericidal endpoints; studies lacking a control or bactericidal measure excluded.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 15 studies included.
- CAP effectively reduced bacterial loads on implant surfaces (incl. S. mitis, S. aureus).
- CAP combated biofilms and plaque contributing to peri-implantitis.
- Conclusion: significant bactericidal activity; promising for improved implant outcomes.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Largely in-vitro/pre-clinical; no pooled estimate; no clinical peri-implantitis outcomes.
