## Why Ingested

교정적 치아 이동(OTM) 중 발생한 치조골 결손에서 BMP2 기능화 생체모방 칼슘인산염(BioCaP) 이식재의 효과를 탐색 — 성장인자 강화 재생 이식재가 기계적으로 역동적인 교정 환경에서도 골재생을 촉진하고 OTM을 방해하지 않음을 대형 동물 모델에서 검증. [[bone-regeneration/chakar-2014-dbbm-bcp-platelet-lysate-rabbit]]

## One-line Summary

Randomized beagle dog study (n=9, 3 groups) showing BMP2-functionalized biomimetic calcium phosphate (BioCaP) graft produces 1.61× more new bone than control and 1.25× more than DBB during orthodontic tooth movement without obstructing tooth movement.

## 한줄요약

개 9마리 3군 무작위 연구 — BMP2 기능화 칼슘인산염(BioCaP) 이식재가 교정적 치아 이동 중 대조군 대비 신생골 형성 1.61배, DBB 대비 1.25배 증가; OTM 속도에는 영향 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Frontiers in Bioengineering and Biotechnology
- **Volume/Issue**: 8: 517
- **Publication date**: 29 May 2020
- **Study type**: Randomized animal study (beagle dog)
- **Institutions**: Zhejiang University School of Medicine (China), ACTA Amsterdam, Guangzhou Medical University

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrated BioCaP (BMP2-functionalized biomimetic CaP) promotes alveolar bone healing during orthodontic tooth movement (OTM) without impeding tooth movement
- BioCaP causes less root resorption than DBB (DBB root resorption 1.87× control; BioCaP similar to control)
- BioCaP achieves 1.92× higher bone mineral density than control and 1.36× higher than DBB
- BioCaP reduces periodontal inflammation (lower IL-1β in GCF at weeks 4 and 8)
- BioCaP reduces alveolar bone recession compared to DBB and control
- BMP2 sustained-release strategy reduces required dose by ~10-fold vs conventional rhBMP2 delivery

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Animals**: 9 beagle dogs (randomly assigned to 3 groups, n=3 each)
- **Groups**:
  - Control: no graft (extraction socket only)
  - DBB: deproteinized bovine bone graft
  - BioCaP: BMP2-functionalized biomimetic calcium phosphate
- **Procedure**: Maxillary first premolar extraction → alveolar defect → graft placement → immediate orthodontic protraction of maxillary second premolar into defect site over 8 weeks
- **BioCaP fabrication**: Biomimetic mineralization of CaP granules (250–1000 μm) → BMP2 co-precipitated into CaP coating at 1 μg/mL; 1 mL loads ~140 μg BMP2
- **Outcome measures**:
  - Rate of OTM (distance)
  - Histology and histomorphometry: new bone formation percentage
  - CBCT: bone mineral density
  - Root resorption index
  - Alveolar crest level (enamel-cementum junction to crestal bone distance)
  - Periodontal probing depth (week 8)
  - GCF IL-1β (ELISA at weeks 4 and 8)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | BioCaP vs Control | BioCaP vs DBB |
|---|---|---|
| New bone formation % | 1.61× higher | 1.25× higher |
| Bone mineral density | 1.92× higher | 1.36× higher |
| Root resorption index | Similar to control | 1.39-fold less than DBB |
| Alveolar recession | 1.45× less than control | 1.69× less than DBB |
| OTM rate | No significant difference | No significant difference |
| GCF IL-1β (week 4 & 8) | Significantly lower | — |
| BioCaP resorption rate | 1.42× higher than DBB | (faster resorption, bone replacement) |

- OTM was not obstructed by BioCaP graft — clinically important for orthodontic application
- DBB caused significantly more root resorption (rigid, slow-resorbing particles under orthodontic force)
- BioCaP's faster resorption (vs DBB) allows progressive bone ingrowth during OTM

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small groups (n=3 dogs each) — limited statistical power
- Animal model (beagle); direct translation to human orthodontic patients requires clinical trials
- Only 8-week OTM period — long-term bone quality and stability not assessed
- BioCaP not commercially available — fabrication complexity limits immediate clinical uptake
- Single BMP2 dose (1 μg/mL); dose-response not evaluated in this study

## 7. Glossary

- **BioCaP**: BMP2-functionalized biomimetic calcium phosphate — granules with sustained BMP2 release via CaP co-precipitation coating
- **BMP2 (Bone Morphogenetic Protein 2)**: Potent osteogenic growth factor; promotes osteoblast differentiation and bone formation
- **DBB (Deproteinized Bovine Bone)**: Xenograft; slow-resorbing bone substitute (similar to DBBM/Bio-Oss)
- **OTM (Orthodontic Tooth Movement)**: Tooth movement under orthodontic force; dependent on alveolar bone remodeling
- **GCF (Gingival Crevicular Fluid)**: Fluid sampled from gingival sulcus — IL-1β reflects local inflammation
- **IL-1β**: Interleukin-1 beta — pro-inflammatory cytokine elevated in peri-implant/periodontal inflammation
