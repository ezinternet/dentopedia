- All-ceramic use increased due to esthetic demand and improved materials
- Zirconia: high strength for posterior use; translucency improving with new generations

- CAD/CAM fabrication improved predictability

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Narrative review of dental ceramic classification, construction methods, and clinically relevant aspects for material selection. PubMed search January 2010–December 2019 (2173 articles). Keywords: ceramics, all-ceramics, dental porcelain, polycrystalline, PFM, e.max, zirconia, In-Ceram, inlays, onlays, overlays, endocrown. Covers: PFM vs all-ceramic comparison, classification by microstructure (glass-based vs polycrystalline), clinical success rates for crowns/FDPs, zirconia properties and applications.
