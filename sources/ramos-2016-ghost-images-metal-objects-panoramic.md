## One-line Summary

Pictorial essay (dry skull + clinical case) demonstrating panoramic ghost-image formation from metal objects in the head/neck to prevent misdiagnosis.

## 한줄요약

파노라마 허상(ghost image) 형성을 두개골+임상증례로 보여주는 화보형 논문. 두경부 금속에 의한 허상 오진 방지가 목적.

- Year: 2016 | Journal: Imaging Science in Dentistry | DOI: 10.5624/isd.2016.46.1.63
- Study type: case-report

## 2. Key Contributions

- Visualizes how metal objects on the face/head-neck create ghost images on panoramic radiographs.
- Explains the geometric mechanism (contralateral, higher, magnified projection).
- Clinical case + dry-skull simulation to raise awareness and prevent incorrect radiographic diagnosis.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Pictorial essay: ghost-image formation studied on a dry skull with metal objects in head/neck region, plus a reported clinical case.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Provides a reference atlas linking metal-object location to ghost-image appearance — directly useful for chairside panoramic interpretation.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Pictorial/case (lowest evidence tier); descriptive; no quantitative metrics.
