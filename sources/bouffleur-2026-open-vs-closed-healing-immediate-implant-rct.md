## One-line Summary
Heidelberg Univ RCT (Int J Implant Dent 2026, n=46 patients / 48 implants): 즉시 임플란트에서 open vs closed healing 술기 비교 — Pink Esthetic Score (PES) open group 유의하게 우수 (MD -1.49, 95% CI -2.36 to -0.62, p=0.0014).

## 2. Key Contributions
- 즉시 식립 open vs closed healing 직접 RCT 비교.
- Open healing이 PES 유의하게 우수 (1.49 points higher).
- 12개월 임플란트 생존 + 부피 변화 secondary endpoints.

## 3. Methodology
Prospective RCT; n=46 patients / 48 implants (open n=25, closed n=23); 3 timepoints (T1 pre-extraction, T2 3mo post, T3 12mo post); primary PES (7 parameters); secondary survival + volumetric change; linear mixed-effects model.

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함]
- PES: open > closed (MD -1.49, 95% CI -2.36 to -0.62, p=0.0014)
- Higher baseline (specific outcome metric in paper)
- 12-month implant survival data

## 5. Limitations
- Single-center
- 12 month follow-up
- Heidelberg context

## 7. Glossary
- PES: Pink Esthetic Score (Fürhauser et al. 2005, 7 soft tissue parameters, max 14)
- Open healing: gingival former / healing abutment exposed
- Closed healing: gingiva sutured over implant for submerged healing
