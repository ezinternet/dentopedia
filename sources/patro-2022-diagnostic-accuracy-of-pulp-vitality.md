## Why Ingested

Donnermeyer SR [[endodontics/diagnosis/donnermeyer-2023-effectiveness-of-diagnosing-pulpitis-a]]가 "정확도 낮음"이라 정성 결론을 냈다면, 본 SR+MA는 개별 검사(pulse oximeter·EPT·cold·heat)의 진단정확도를 정량(pooled DOR)으로 줄세워 임상 선택 근거를 준다. 신설 endodontics/diagnosis 카테고리의 정량 축.

## One-line Summary
Systematic review + meta-analysis (10 qualitative / 5 meta-analyzed) ranking pulp-test accuracy by pooled diagnostic odds ratio: pulse oximeter 628.5 ≫ cold 17.24 > EPT 10.75 > heat 3.47 — pulse oximetry most accurate, though no study rated high quality.

## 한줄요약
SR+MA(정성 10편·MA 5편) — 치수검사 정확도를 통합 진단오즈비로 줄세우면 맥박산소계측 628.5 ≫ cold 17.24 > 전기치수검사 10.75 > heat 3.47; 맥박산소계측이 최정확하나 고품질 연구는 없음.

## 1. Document Information

## 2. Key Contributions
- First meta-analytic ranking of pulp-test diagnostic accuracy by pooled diagnostic odds ratio (DOR).
- Quantifies pulse oximeter (a vitality/blood-flow test) as markedly superior to sensibility tests.
- Applies QUADAS-2 quality appraisal; flags that no included study was rated good quality.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Databases: PubMed/MEDLINE, Cochrane CENTRAL, Web of Science, Google Scholar, OpenGrey.
- Extracted/calculated TP, FP, TN, FN, sensitivity, specificity. Quality via QUADAS-2.
- Meta-analysis in MetaDTA v2.0 and RevMan 5.3. 10 studies qualitative, 5 meta-analyzed.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pooled DOR: pulse oximeter (PO) 628.5; cold test (CT) 17.24; electric pulp tester (EPT) 10.75; heat test (HT) 3.47.
- Pairwise: PO higher pooled mean sensitivity and specificity than EPT, CT, and HT.
- SROC curves confirm PO best at correctly screening negatives.
- No study rated "good" on QUADAS-2 (quality caveat on all estimates).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small meta-analytic base (5 studies); all studies low/moderate quality.
- Permanent teeth only.
- PO requires specific equipment/probes not universal in practice.

## 6. Related Work
- Donnermeyer 2023 ESE SR on pulpitis diagnosis effectiveness.
- Laser Doppler flowmetry vs pulse oximetry comparative studies.

## 7. Glossary
- Diagnostic odds ratio (DOR): single summary of test discrimination = (sens/(1−sens))/((1−spec)/spec); higher = better.
- Pulse oximeter (vitality test): measures pulpal blood oxygenation (blood flow), not nerve response.
- QUADAS-2: tool for risk-of-bias appraisal of diagnostic-accuracy studies.
