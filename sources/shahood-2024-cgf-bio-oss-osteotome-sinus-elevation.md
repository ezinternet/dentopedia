## Why Ingested

[[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/guo-2020-non-grafted-platelet-concentrations-grafted-transcrestal-sinus]]에서 PRP·PRF와 Bio-Oss 비교는 있으나 CGF(concentrated growth factor) 조합은 없음. 본 연구는 RBH ≤5mm에서 Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF vs Bio-Oss alone vs no-graft 3군 직접 비교로 이식재 선택 근거를 보강.

## One-line Summary
Prospective study (n=126 implants, 123 patients) showing Bio-Oss Collagen + CGF outperforms no-graft and Bio-Oss alone in OSFE for RBH ≤5 mm: ISR 96%, less MBL, lower pain.

## 한줄요약
전향적 연구(126임플란트, 123환자): RBH ≤5mm OSFE에서 Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF 조합이 무이식·Bio-Oss 단독 대비 골이득·환자 만족도 우수, 생존율 96%.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health 2024;24:1515
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s12903-024-05320-4
- **Study type**: Prospective cohort, 3-arm comparative
- **n**: 126 implants / 123 patients
- **Follow-up**: 1 year functional loading
- **Setting**: Single center (affil. Nanjing Medical University)

## 2. Key Contributions
- RBH ≤5mm에서 CGF+Bio-Oss Collagen 조합의 방사선학적 efficacy 최초 3군 비교
- Graft size(D)가 ISR의 독립 예측인자임을 OR로 정량화
- Group C (CGF+Bio-Oss Collagen)에서 VAS pain 유의미하게 낮음

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Groups**: A=no graft; B=Bio-Oss; C=Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF
- **Inclusion**: RBH ≤5 mm, simultaneous implant placement
- **Primary outcome**: Implant Survival Rate (ISR)
- **Secondary**: Radiographic endo-sinus bone gain (ESBG), Total Bone Resorption (TBR1/TBR2), ISQ, bone density, VAS pain
- Radiographic measurements at 4 timepoints (T0, T1, T2, T3)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **ISR**: 96% overall. Positive correlation with RBH [r=0.359, p<0.001]
- **Endo-sinus bone gain**: both grafted groups > non-grafted
- **TBR1/TBR2**: significantly lower in grafted groups (p=0.004 / p<0.001)
- **Graft size (D) → ISR** (OR): D0=8.06 (95%CI 1.59–38.24, p=0.010); D1=96.58; D2=4.97
- **VAS pain**: Group B >> Group C ≈ Group A (p<0.001) — Bio-Oss alone most painful
- ISQ and bone density: no significant between-group difference reported

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Single center; no randomization (prospective cohort, not RCT)
- Short follow-up (1 year); long-term MBL needed
- Small Group A (no-graft) sample limits subgroup power
- CGF preparation variability not standardized

## 6. Related Work
- Guo 2020: PRP/PRF+graft vs no-graft transcrestal SFE
- Volpe 2022: OSFE without graft + collagen sponge only, 5–13yr follow-up
- Sansupakorn 2024: OSFE with BCP vs no-graft RCT (BCP no benefit)

## 7. Glossary
- **CGF** (Concentrated Growth Factor): 3rd-gen autologous platelet concentrate, higher growth factor concentration than PRP/PRF
- **OSFE** (Osteotome Sinus Floor Elevation): transcrestal approach using osteotomes
- **TBR** (Total Bone Resorption): radiographic measurement of bone loss post-loading
- **ESBG** (Endo-Sinus Bone Gain): new bone height gained inside sinus
