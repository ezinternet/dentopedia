## Why Ingested

기존 [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/samet-2023-immediate-placement-loading-esthetic-zone]]와 [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/hamilton-2023-type1a-immediate-implant-immediate-loading-esthetic-sr-ma]]는 즉시식립·심미의 일반 프로토콜과 SR/MA 근거를 다루지만, 특정 임플란트 매크로디자인(thread 형상·connection 각도)이 1년 연조직·골 결과에 어떻게 기여하는지 단일군 전향 데이터로 보여주는 페이지가 없었다. 본 prospective study (Han 2025, n=20)는 dual-thread(상부 U자 + 하부 V자) + 11° internal hex 디자인의 IU implant를 maxillary esthetic zone IIP에 적용해 ISQ·HW·CBCT·MBL·midfacial recession을 V8(1년)까지 추적한 macrodesign-specific 근거로, [[immediate-implant/stanley-2020-five-thread-guideline-predicting-primary]]의 thread 기반 1차 안정성 framework를 임상으로 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

Single-arm prospective study (n=20, maxillary esthetic zone IIP with dual-thread [upper U-shaped + lower V-shaped] 11° internal-hex IU implant): 85% success / 100% survival post-loading, ISQ >70 by 70 days, minimal 1-year buccal-bone (−0.34 to −0.48 mm) and soft-tissue loss, midfacial recession improved (1.86→1.21 mm), MBL stable above implant shoulder.

## 한줄요약

단일군 전향연구(n=20, 상악 심미부 즉시식립 + 듀얼스레드[상부 U자·하부 V자] 11° 내부육각 IU 임플란트): 성공률 85%·기능부하 후 생존율 100%, 70일째 ISQ >70, 1년 협측골 손실 −0.34~−0.48 mm로 미미, 중안면 치은퇴축은 오히려 개선(1.86→1.21 mm), 변연골 수준은 임플란트 숄더 위로 안정 유지.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First prospective clinical evaluation of a specific dual-thread implant macrodesign (IU implant, Warantec) in maxillary-esthetic-zone IIP, tracking soft + hard tissue to 1 year (V8).
2. Quantifies a thread-design-specific 1° stability trajectory: ISQ rises from ~60 (10 d) to >70 (70 d), supporting the lower V-shaped thread's role in initial fixation.
3. Documents minimal dimensional loss with platform-switching + 11° internal hex: buccal bone −0.34 to −0.48 mm, MBL retained above implant shoulder, and midfacial recession that *improves* rather than worsens over the year.
4. Reports a realistic 85% success / 100% survival split, with a candid multifactorial failure analysis (periodontitis, periapical lesions, narrow ridge near incisive canal).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Population**: 20 patients, single anterior maxillary tooth requiring extraction + implant; socket ≥2 intact walls; ≥20 remaining teeth; plaque/bleeding index <20%; non-smokers; controlled systemic status. Sample size powered (95% 1-sided CI, α=5%, power 70%) on alveolar bone volume change.
- **Implant**: IU implant (Warantec, Seongnam, Korea) — bone-level internal, SLA surface, **dual thread (upper U-shaped + lower V-shaped)**, **11° internal hexagonal connection**, combined straight + tapered geometry positioning stress point 3.0 mm below bone level, platform switching.
- **Protocol**: IIP after atraumatic extraction + socket debridement; 3D optimal positioning. ISQ >50 → 1-stage; ISQ ≤50 or GBR needed → 2-stage. Buccal/gap defects grafted with bovine bone mineral (Cerabone) + absorbable collagen membrane (Jason). Prosthesis at 90 days (V5).
- **Visit schedule**: V0 (registration/pre-extraction), V1 (IIP), V2 (10 d, suture-out), V3 (40 d), V4 (70 d), V5 (90 d, prosthesis), V6 (180 d), V7 (270 d), V8 (450 d / 1 year post-prosthesis).
- **Outcomes**: 3D intraoral-scan soft tissue (Medit; HW-1/3/5, midfacial recession); CBCT horizontal ridge (OnDemand superimposition; buccal bone thickness + buccolingual width at 1/3/5 mm); 2D radiographic MBL (mesial/distal); ISQ (Osstell RFA, buccal + palatal); ICOI Pisa success/survival criteria; AE monitoring.
- **Statistics**: SPSS 21, paired-sample t-tests, 95% CI, significance 5%.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Success/survival**: 85% success (3/20 early osseointegration failures before prosthesis), **100% survival after functional loading** through follow-up.
- **ISQ (RFA)**: Buccal 60.0 (V2) → 65.0 (V3) → 74.0 (V4); Palatal 59.4 → 64.9 → 76.4. Average >70 by V4 (70 d).
- **Soft tissue (HW)**: HW-1 8.68 (V0) → 7.43 (V5, sig) → 7.76 (V8, sig); HW-3 10.62 → 9.74 (V8, sig); change magnitude smaller than the 1.3 mm reported elsewhere.
- **Midfacial recession**: 1.86 (V0) → 1.82 (V1) → 1.25 (V6) → 1.21 (V8) — recession *decreased* (improved) at V6/V8 vs V0; trend toward reduced distance between implant-mucosa zenith and adjacent-teeth zenith line.
- **CBCT V1→V8 (all sig)**: Buccal bone thickness −0.43 (1 mm) / −0.34 (3 mm) / −0.48 (5 mm); buccolingual width −0.76 (1 mm) / −0.63 (3 mm) / −0.78 (5 mm). Largest change at 5 mm.
- **MBL V6→V8**: Mesial −2.13 → −1.56 (p=0.053, NS); Distal −1.37 → −1.78 (p=0.58, NS). Bone retained above implant shoulder (negative = above reference).
- **Safety**: No AEs besides the 3 failed-implant removals; 0 SAE, 0 device deficiencies.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short follow-up (1 year post-prosthesis); long-term thread-design/connection effect on esthetics unproven.
- Small sample (n=20 → 17), limiting power and generalizability.
- No control group — no direct comparison vs delayed placement or other implant systems.
- No stratification by bone-defect morphology (2/3/4-wall); grafting/membrane decisions by surgeon judgment.
- Single-manufacturer device; manufacturer-described mechanisms (von Mises stress reduction, stress point 3.0 mm below bone) not independently tested here.

## 6. Related Work

- IU implant prior data: cited 99% survival over 6 years; MBL ~0.04–0.09 mm at 1–6 years.
- Immediate vs delayed survival: 94.6% vs 98.3% (cited).
- Gap filling with slowly resorbing biomaterial + soft tissue augmentation for thin biotype (cited review).
- Midfacial recession literature: ~0.29 mm over 2–5 years; ~13% advanced recession; ~7% >1 mm progression after 1 year (cited).

## 7. Glossary

- **IIP** — immediate implant placement (Type 1, fixture placed at extraction).
- **Dual thread design** — upper U-shaped thread (easy fixation, lower cortical von Mises stress) + lower V-shaped thread (narrow, higher fixation force, easier installation).
- **11° internal hexagonal connection** — conical/angled internal implant-abutment interface distributing cortical stress; paired with platform switching.
- **ISQ** — implant stability quotient (resonance frequency analysis, Osstell).
- **HW-1/3/5** — horizontal alveolar ridge width measured 1/3/5 mm below the gingival reference line on intraoral scan.
- **MBL** — marginal bone level (radiographic; negative = bone above implant shoulder reference).
- **SLA** — sandblasted, large-grit, acid-etched implant surface.
- **Platform switching** — abutment narrower than implant platform; increases peri-implant soft tissue and limits crestal bone loss.
