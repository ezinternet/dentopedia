## One-line Summary
MDPI Dentistry 2026;14(2):112 (Zagreb/Osijek): Narrative review of peri-implantitis treatments + 3 case reports using resorbable magnesium membrane for GBR in peri-implantitis defects — novel biodegradable membrane option; illustrative cases only.

## 1. Key Results
- Narrative review of therapeutic interventions for peri-implantitis
- Novel approach: magnesium membrane (biodegradable/resorbable) for GBR in peri-implantitis defects
- Three case presentations illustrate the clinical use of magnesium membrane
- Magnesium = biocompatible, resorbable, osteoconductive membrane material
- Still in early clinical exploration phase; no controlled trial data

## 2. Clinical Implications
- Magnesium membrane represents a new biodegradable barrier option for peri-implantitis GBR
- Case reports only — clinical evidence level is preliminary
- Combined nonsurgical + surgical + GBR approach for peri-implantitis defect management
