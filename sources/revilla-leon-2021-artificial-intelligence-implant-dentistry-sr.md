## One-line Summary
J Prosthet Dent 2023;129:293-300 (ePub 2021): SR (17 studies): AI for implant type recognition 93.8–98% accuracy; osseointegration success prediction 62.4–80.5%; implant design optimization; all models in development — additional clinical validation needed.

## 1. Key Results
- SR of 17 studies (search to Feb 2021); 5 databases
- Three AI application domains:
  1. Implant type recognition (periapical/panoramic): accuracy 93.8–98%
  2. Implant success prediction: accuracy 62.4–80.5%
  3. Implant design optimization: stress at implant-bone interface reduced 36.6% vs FEA
- All AI models in development phase; no clinical RCT validation
- JBI critical appraisal applied; quasi-experimental study quality assessment

## 2. Clinical Implications
- AI for implant type recognition from radiograph holds clinical promise
- Success prediction models still insufficiently accurate for clinical decision-making
- AI-driven design optimization theoretically promising but not clinically deployed
- Gap: lack of multicenter, prospective validation data
