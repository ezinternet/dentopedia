## One-line Summary
In vitro (n=15/group): EdgeEvolve이 single·double curve canal 모두 cyclic fatigue·bending flexibility·surface smoothness 모두 PTG 대비 유의 우월 (p<0.05); Ni/Ti 함량은 동등.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Single vs double curvature 양 조건에서 cyclic fatigue 비교
- Bending resistance (45°까지) + surface roughness (AFM Ra) 추가 측정
- EdgeEvolve의 alloy 우월성 (heat-treatment) 근거

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 15 files/group, 4 조건:
  - Single curve: 60°, 5-mm radius
  - Double curve: coronal 60° 5-mm + apical 30° 2-mm
- Cyclic fatigue: NCF 측정 (universal testing machine)
- Bending resistance: 45° 굴곡까지 force
- SEM + EDX (Ni/Ti 함량) + AFM (Ra)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Cyclic fatigue: EdgeEvolve > PTG (single·double curve, p<0.05)
- Double curve < single curve (양 시스템, p<0.05) — double curvature stress 가중
- Flexibility (bending): EdgeEvolve > PTG (p<0.05)
- Surface Ra: EdgeEvolve < PTG (p<0.05, smoother)
- Ni/Ti 함량: 동등 (p>0.05)
