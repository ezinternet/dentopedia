## One-line Summary

2019 narrative review by Kim (Seoul National University) summarizing the pathophysiology, microbiology, diagnosis, and consolidated surgical management (CLP/FESS/MESS) of odontogenic maxillary sinusitis (OMS), which accounts for 30–40% of all chronic maxillary sinusitis cases.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Comprehensive definition distinguishing OMS from rhinogenic chronic rhinosinusitis (CRS) and acute bacterial rhinosinusitis (ABRS)
- Clarification that OMS comprises 30–40% of all chronic maxillary sinusitis (CMS) cases — much higher than the previously cited 10–12%
- Systematic coverage of OMS etiology: tooth extraction (most common), oroantral fistula (OAF), periapical infection, periodontal disease, iatrogenic (implant displacement, endodontic overfill, sinus perforation)
- Microbiology of OMS: polymicrobial (aerobic S. aureus, S. pneumoniae; anaerobic Peptostreptococcus, Prevotella spp. in >75%); bacterial biofilm (BB) hypothesis discussed
- Surgical approach comparison: Caldwell-Luc Procedure (CLP), Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS), Modified Endoscopy-Assisted Maxillary Sinus Surgery (MESS)
- Management of oroantral fistula (OAF) as the most common OMS complication (>80% from maxillary posterior tooth extraction)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

Narrative review of MEDLINE (PubMed), Embase, Cochrane Library using keyword combinations covering OMS, rhinosinusitis, FESS, MESS, Caldwell-Luc procedure, and paranasal sinus. Results summarized based on clinical experience of the author. IRB-approved clinical radiographic images (Seoul National University IRB S-D20170005) included for illustrative cases.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- OMS accounts for **30–40%** of all chronic maxillary sinusitis cases (vs previously cited 10–12%)
- Most common OMS cause: tooth extraction; molar region involved in 47.68% (first molar 22.51%, third molar 17.21%)
- OAF accounts for >80% of OMS complications related to posterior tooth extraction
- Prevalence of OMS with secondary periapical lesions: 16–65%
- Bacterial biofilm (BB) detected in 70% of CRS samples; S. aureus, H. influenzae, P. aeruginosa major OMS-BB pathogens
- MRSA found in 10–12% of OMS patients
- Schneiderian membrane (SM) / osteo-meatal unit (OMU) anatomy: mean root-to-sinus floor distance 1.97 mm
- Surgical options: CLP (easy access, high complication rate — POMC, paresthesia), FESS (flexible, replaces CLP but incomplete for OMS), MESS (new, preserves SM, no POMC)
- Endodontic overfill and displaced implants are iatrogenic OMS causes; do not always produce sinusitis signs
- Actinomyces-related OMS requires apical surgery; orthograde endodontic treatment alone insufficient
- Fungal sinusitis (aspergillosis, mucormycosis) in immunocompromised patients can extend to orbit/brain

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Narrative review only; no meta-analysis or systematic quality assessment
- Limited randomized controlled trial data on OMS management (noted by author)
- Consolidated management outcomes for MESS not quantified
- No standardized classification of OMS severity

## 7. Glossary

- **OMS (Odontogenic Maxillary Sinusitis, 치성 상악동염):** Maxillary sinusitis arising from dental/dentoalveolar source
- **SM (Schneiderian Membrane, 슈나이데리안 막):** Pseudostratified ciliated columnar epithelium lining the maxillary sinus
- **OMU (Osteo-Meatal Unit, 골-비공 복합체):** Drainage pathway of maxillary, anterior ethmoidal, and frontal sinuses through the middle nasal meatus
- **MCC (Mucociliary Clearance, 점액섬모청소능):** Ciliary-driven mucus transport maintaining sinus patency
- **OAF (Oroantral Fistula, 구강-상악동 누공):** Epithelialized communication between oral cavity and maxillary sinus
- **CLP (Caldwell-Luc Procedure, 칼드웰-루크 수술):** Classic anterior wall maxillary sinus surgery
- **FESS (Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery, 기능적 내시경 부비동 수술):** Endoscopic sinus surgery restoring OMU drainage
- **MESS (Modified Endoscopy-Assisted Maxillary Sinus Surgery, 변형 내시경 보조 상악동 수술):** Newer combined intraoral + endoscopic approach preserving SM without POMC
- **BB (Bacterial Biofilm, 세균 생물막):** Polymicrobial community embedded in extracellular polymeric matrix; major factor in recalcitrant OMS
- **POMC (Postoperative Maxillary Cyst, 술후 상악 낭종):** Cyst complication of CLP
- **CRS (Chronic Rhinosinusitis, 만성 비부비동염):** Sinusitis lasting >12 weeks
- **ABRS (Acute Bacterial Rhinosinusitis, 급성 세균성 비부비동염):** Bacterial sinusitis <4 weeks
