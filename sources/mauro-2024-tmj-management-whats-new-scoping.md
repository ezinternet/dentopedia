## Why Ingested
TMD 치료 전체 스펙트럼(비침습·최소침습·수술)을 109개 SR을 바탕으로 망라한 최신 scoping review (2024). [[tmj/alowaimer-2024-noninvasive-therapies-tmj-sr]]과 [[tmj/gonzalez-sanchez-2023-tmj-physiotherapy-treatment-sr]]의 비침습 근거를 확장하며, PRP·오존·BTX·관절경 등 새로운 치료 옵션 근거 추가.

## One-line Summary
Scoping review of SRs (109 studies, Dent J 2024): non-invasive (CBT/PT) → minimally invasive (splints/injections/BTX/PRP/acupuncture/laser) → surgery — tiered approach; emerging therapies (PRP, ozone, stem cells) show promise.

## 한줄요약
SR 109편 대상 scoping review: 비침습(CBT·PT)→최소침습(스플린트·주사·BTX·PRP·침·레이저)→수술 단계별 접근; PRP·오존·줄기세포 등 신치료 주목.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Dentistry Journal 12(6):157 (2024)
- **Study type**: Scoping review (PRISMA-ScR) of SRs and MAs
- **Databases**: Scopus, PubMed, Embase, Web of Science (2019–2024)
- **Screened**: 3,288 → 2,183 after dedup → 106 included + 3 citation search = 109
- **Categories**: Non-invasive (39), Minimally invasive (120 references/23+23+8+13+13+18+22), Surgical (15)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Most comprehensive TMD treatment scoping review 2019–2024
- Non-invasive: CBT+PT effective; O2O3 therapy emerging
- Minimally invasive: arthrocentesis, IA injections, BTX (especially for myalgia), PRP/PRF, splints — all supported
- Acupuncture: moderate evidence for pain reduction
- Emerging: PRP/PRF/stem cells show promise; needs more RCTs
- Surgery reserved for severe cases; arthroscopy preferred over open surgery

## 3. Treatment Categories

### Non-invasive (39 articles)
- CBT + counseling (8): effective for chronic/biopsychosocial TMD
- Physical therapy + MT + exercise (25): strongest non-invasive evidence
- O2O3 oxygen-ozone therapy (5): anti-inflammatory, analgesic
- Ultrasonic therapy (1)

### Minimally invasive (120 references)
- Arthrocentesis + IA injections (23): corticosteroid, hyaluronate
- PRP/PRF/PRGF/PDGF + stem cells (23): regenerative; promising
- Acupuncture (8): pain reduction, moderate evidence
- Botulinum toxin (13): myofascial/bruxism component
- Drugs (13): NSAIDs, muscle relaxants, clonazepam
- Laser + TENS (18): as adjuncts
- Oral splints (22): stabilization, protection

### Surgical (15 articles)
- Minimally invasive surgery / arthroscopy (7): preferred over open
- Open surgery (8): last resort

## 4. Limitations
- Scoping review of SRs — does not re-analyze primary study data
- Heterogeneous outcomes; not registered
- Unregistered

## 5. Clinical Implication
최신 TMD 치료 evidence map (2024):
1. **비침습 1차**: PT + MT + 운동 (가장 강한 근거) + CBT (만성 환자)
2. **최소침습**: 스플린트, IA 히알루론산/코르티코스테로이드, BTX (근육형)
3. **신흥**: PRP/PRF — 관절 내 재생 가능성; 아직 RCT 부족
4. **오존 (O2O3)**: 항염·진통 효과; 보조적 사용 가능
5. **수술**: 관절경 > 개방술; 보존치료 실패 후 고려

## 6. Glossary
- **O2O3 therapy**: Oxygen-ozone 혼합 주사 — 항염·항균·진통
- **PRP/PRF**: Platelet-rich plasma/fibrin — 성장인자 농축 혈소판 농축물
- **PRGF**: Plasma rich in growth factors
- **PDGF**: Platelet-derived growth factor
- **BTX**: Botulinum toxin (보툴리눔 독소)
- **Arthroscopy**: 관절경 수술 (최소침습)
