## One-line Summary
Literature review on Zirconia-Reinforced Lithium Silicate (ZLS) ceramics (Vita Suprinity, Celtra Duo/Press) — higher fracture toughness than lithium disilicate but lower than zirconia; bonding requires acid etching, sandblasting, or laser etching.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Medicina 2023, 59, 2135 (MDPI)
- **DOI**: 10.3390/medicina59122135
- **Institution**: Iuliu Hatieganu University of Medicine and Pharmacy, Cluj-Napoca, Romania
- **Search**: PubMed, Web of Science, Cochrane Library
- **Inclusion**: English clinical + in vitro studies on ZLS color, mechanical behavior, cementation

## 2. Key Contributions
- Consolidated reference on ZLS ceramic system — chemical composition (Li₂O-ZrO₂-SiO₂), microstructure, properties, cementation
- Maps commercial products to fabrication mode: Vita Suprinity PC, Celtra Duo (CAD/CAM milling); Vita Ambria, Celtra Press (heat-pressing)
- Mechanical hierarchy: ZLS > feldspathic > lithium disilicate > hybrid ceramic > nanoceramic; but ZLS < translucent/high-translucency zirconia
- Translucency reduced by tetragonal zirconia content vs pure lithium disilicate
- Bonding strategies: HF etching, air abrasion, laser etching (Er:YAG/Er,Cr:YSGG) followed by silanization + resin cement

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Design**: Comprehensive narrative literature review
- **Eligibility**: English clinical and in vitro studies on ZLS color, mechanical, cementation
- **Exclusion**: abstracts, questionnaires, case reports, narrative reviews, non-English
- **Structure**: chemical composition → microstructure → optical properties → mechanical properties → cementation procedures → clinical outcomes

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
**Composition**:
- Lithium metasilicate (Li₂SiO₃) glass-ceramic matrix + 8–12% ZrO₂ grains
- Fine-grained microstructure after crystallization (Li₂O-ZrO₂-SiO₂)

**Mechanical properties (relative ranking)**:
- ZLS fracture toughness, flexural strength, elastic modulus, hardness HIGHER than:
  - Feldspathic ceramic
  - Lithium disilicate
  - Hybrid ceramic
  - Resin nanoceramic
- ZLS LOWER than translucent / high-translucency zirconia

**Optical properties**:
- Reduced translucency vs lithium disilicate (zirconia content)
- Final shade affected by: material thickness, underlying tooth color, resin cement shade

**Bonding/cementation**:
- Surface preparation: HF etching (most common), air abrasion, or laser etching
- Silane coupling agent application before resin cement
- Adhesive resin cementation preferred

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- No consensus on optimal fabrication protocol
- Long-term clinical data still limited
- Mechanical behavior literature inconsistent across studies
- Comparative RCTs vs lithium disilicate and zirconia needed

## 7. Glossary
- **ZLS (Zirconia-Reinforced Lithium Silicate, 지르코니아 강화 리튬실리케이트)**: Glass-ceramic with 8–12% ZrO₂ in lithium metasilicate matrix
- **Li₂SiO₃ (Lithium metasilicate)**: Crystalline phase
- **Li₂Si₂O₅ (Lithium disilicate)**: Crystalline phase in IPS e.max
- **HF etching**: Hydrofluoric acid etching for glass-ceramic micromechanical bonding
- **Pre-crystallized**: Easier to mill before full crystallization; subsequent heat treatment completes crystallization
