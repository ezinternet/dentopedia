## Why Ingested

미니스크류 일차안정성의 cortical thickness 의존성을 SR+MA로 확정하기 위해 인제스트. 동일 그룹의 BMD 연구([[orthodontics/marquezan-2012-bmd-primary-stability-miniscrews]])를 thickness 축으로 확장하고, 임플란트 측 [[implants/isq/al-juboori-2024-cortical-thickness-implant-stability-sr]] 와 cross-domain으로 일관된 cortical thickness 메커니즘을 형성.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review + meta-analysis (12 studies) finding a positive association between orthodontic miniscrew primary stability and cortical thickness (pooled r=0.409 for primary-stability studies; r=0.338 in human studies).

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰+메타분석(12편) — 미니스크류 일차안정성과 cortical thickness 간 양의 상관(일차안정성 연구 묶음 r=0.409, 인간 연구 r=0.338).

## 1. Document Information
- Angle Orthodontist 2014;84(6):1093–1103.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pools cortical-thickness–stability evidence for miniscrews into a meta-analytic estimate.
- Separates primary-stability vs other endpoints and human vs non-human subgroups.
- Confirms cortical thickness as a modifiable site-selection criterion.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Systematic review + meta-analysis; title/abstract screening → 15 candidates → 12 included (1 hand-searched, exclusions for overlap).
- Pooled correlation between cortical thickness (CtTh) and miniscrew primary stability.
- Subgroups: primary-stability measurement type; human studies.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pooled r=0.409 when grouping primary-stability (PS) studies.
- Pooled r=0.338 in human studies.
- Positive association between miniscrew primary stability and cortical thickness.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Lack of well-designed clinical trials (authors' explicit caveat).
- Moderate correlation magnitudes; heterogeneity across measurement methods.
- Mix of human and experimental data.

## 6. Related Work
- Marquezan 2012 (BMD); implant cortical-thickness–stability literature (Al-Juboori 2024).

## 7. Glossary
- CtTh: cortical thickness. MI: mini-implant/miniscrew.
- PS: primary stability.
