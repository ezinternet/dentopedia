## Why Ingested

Adds a cost-aware comparative-efficacy lens to OLP therapy that the efficacy-only Cochrane review [[oral-medicine/lodi-2020-oral-lichen-planus-corticosteroids-cochrane]] omits, and complements [[oral-medicine/srivastava-2022-oral-lichen-planus-diagnosis-management-review]].

## One-line Summary

Systematic review comparing reported efficacy and treatment costs of OLP interventions — corticosteroids remain the efficacy benchmark while highlighting wide cost variation across topical/systemic options to inform value-based selection.

## 한줄요약

OLP 치료 옵션의 보고된 효능과 치료비용을 비교한 체계적 문헌고찰 — 코르티코스테로이드가 효능 기준이며, 국소/전신 옵션 간 비용 편차가 커서 가치기반 선택에 정보 제공.

## 1. Document Information

- BMC Oral Health 2022;22:161. Published 2022. PRISMA-based systematic review.

## 2. Key Contributions

- One of the few OLP reviews to jointly weigh efficacy and direct treatment cost.
- Provides a comparative framework across topical corticosteroids, calcineurin inhibitors, and systemic agents.
- Surfaces cost as an under-reported decision variable in OLP management.
- Confirms corticosteroids as the practical first-line on efficacy-cost grounds.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Systematic literature search of OLP intervention studies reporting efficacy and/or cost; qualitative synthesis of comparative efficacy and cost data.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Topical corticosteroids deliver favorable efficacy at relatively low cost; calcineurin inhibitors (tacrolimus) effective but more expensive.
- Cost reporting across the literature is inconsistent, limiting formal cost-effectiveness conclusions.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Heterogeneous, often incomplete cost reporting; no formal cost-effectiveness modeling; efficacy evidence limited by underlying trial quality.

## 6. Related Work

- Cochrane OLP corticosteroid review (Lodi 2020).
- Calcineurin-inhibitor OLP trials.
