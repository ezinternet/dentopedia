## Why Ingested

기존 [[oral-surgery/li-2022-ian-position-nerve-injury-sr-ma]]는 파노라마 방사선 기반 IAN canal 위치 분류에 의존하는 반면, 본 SR+MA (Thereza-Bussolaro 2026)는 CBCT 단층 영상에서 검출 가능한 8가지 해부학적 특징의 Odds Ratio를 정량화해 임상적 위험 예측을 한 단계 진전시킨다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 18 CBCT-based studies identifying 8 tomographic predictors of inferior alveolar nerve (IAN) injury during mandibular third molar extraction, with absent cortical bone (OR 9.87), dumbbell-shaped canal (OR 8.25), and lingual canal position (OR 3.82) as highest-risk features.

## 한줄요약

18개 CBCT 연구 SR+MA — 하치조신경관(IAN) 피질골 소실(OR 9.87), 아령형 관(OR 8.25), 설측 위치(OR 3.82)가 하치조신경 손상(IAN injury)의 주요 CBCT 예측 인자임을 정량화.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First SR+MA to systematically quantify CBCT-specific tomographic predictors of IAN injury with OR values
- Identified 8 distinct tomographic features predictive of IAN injury
- Three features with OR >3 provide actionable thresholds for high-risk case identification
- Combination of all 3 high-risk features yields OR 5.06 (95% CI 2.23–11.39)
- Evidence certainty rated moderate to high (GRADE framework)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Design**: Systematic review + meta-analysis; PRISMA-guided
- **Databases**: Not specified in abstract; PROSPERO registered
- **Inclusion**: Retrospective/prospective cohorts, RCTs, nested case-controls using tomographic (CBCT) imaging pre- and post-surgery
- **Exclusion**: Studies without CBCT data, subjective assessments only, unrelated procedures
- **Studies included**: 18
- **Bias assessment**: 16 of 18 studies low risk of bias
- **Statistical model**: Odds ratios with 95% CI; GRADE certainty assessment

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| CBCT Feature | OR | 95% CI | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Absence of cortical bone (MC) | 9.87 | 4.10–23.83 | <0.001 |
| Dumbbell-shaped canal | 8.25 | 3.19–21.35 | <0.001 |
| Lingual canal position | 3.82 | 1.95–7.39 | <0.001 |
| **All 3 features combined** | **5.06** | **2.23–11.39** | <0.001 |

- Total of 8 tomographic predictors identified across 18 studies
- Moderate to high certainty of evidence (GRADE) for the top 3 predictors
- Recommendation: oral/maxillofacial radiologists should explicitly report these high-risk features to inform surgeons and patients

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Abstract-only; specific details on all 8 features and heterogeneity statistics not fully reported here
- Variation in imaging protocols and IAN injury definitions across studies
- Need for prospective multi-center studies with standardized CBCT acquisition and neurosensory testing protocols

## 7. Glossary
- **IAN**: Inferior Alveolar Nerve — 하치조신경
- **MC**: Mandibular Canal — 하악관
- **CBCT**: Cone Beam Computed Tomography — 콘빔 CT
- **Dumbbell-shaped canal**: 아령형 하악관 — MC가 치근 주변을 감싸 아령 모양으로 보이는 고위험 형태
- **Lingual position**: 하악관이 치근 설측에 위치한 경우
- **Cortical bone absence**: 하악관 피질골 소실 — CBCT에서 MC 주변 피질골이 확인되지 않는 경우
