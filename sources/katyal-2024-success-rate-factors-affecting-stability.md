## Why Ingested

[[wiki/orthodontics/alharbi-2018-miniscrew-failure-rate-orthodontics-sr-ma]] pooled mostly inter-radicular miniscrews and left the extra-radicular infrazygomatic crest (IZC) site largely unexplored. This 2024 SR+MA isolates IZC-MSI (14 studies, 1683 screws, 92% success) and reinforces the broader miniscrew-stability evidence base by adding a dedicated extra-alveolar success estimate.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (14 studies, n=1683 infrazygomatic crest miniscrews): pooled success 92% (95% CI 87–95%; I²=80%), rising to 94% in studies with >100 screws; no significant age–success relation and only limited correlation for other host/implant factors.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (14편·관골하능 미니스크루 1683개): 통합 성공률 92% (95% CI 87–95%, I²=80%), 표본 >100 연구에서는 94%; 연령–성공 유의 관계 없음, 기타 숙주·임플란트 인자 상관성은 제한적.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis (single-arm proportion), PRISMA, PROSPERO CRD42024469048
- **Journal**: European Journal of Orthodontics 2025;47(1):cjae074 (e-pub 2024)
- **Search**: PubMed, Scopus, EMBASE, Cochrane (7 Feb 2024), no language/date limit
- **Designs included**: RCTs, non-randomized trials, prospective/retrospective cohorts (single-arm, no comparator)
- **Risk of bias**: RoB 2.0 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (non-randomized) — high in 5, moderate in 5, low in 4

## 2. Key Contributions

- First dedicated SR+MA of infrazygomatic crest miniscrew (IZC-MSI) success, an extra-radicular site previously underrepresented.
- Pooled success 92%; subgroup/meta-regression on age, sex, implant position, loading.
- Documents that most candidate stability factors lack robust correlation given current low evidence quality.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 196 records → 14 studies (after 76 duplicates, 104 exclusions); n=1683 IZC-MSI.
- Random-effects model; success extracted as proportion of total per study; I², prediction interval reported.
- Subgroup analysis + meta-regression for host-, implant-, and loading-related factors.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Pooled success: 92%** (95% CI 87–95%; prediction interval 66–99%; **I²=80%**).
- Studies with **n>100**: **94%** success (95% CI 91–96%; I²=63%, moderate heterogeneity).
- Meta-regression: **no significant age–success relation**.
- Host/implant/other risk factors: significant heterogeneity, limited correlation.
- Per-study examples: Chang 2019 (RCT, 772 screws) 93.6%; Chang 2022 (RC, 200) 89%; Bungau 2022 (RC, 84) 82.1%.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- High heterogeneity (I²=80%) and largely low-quality evidence (9/14 high-or-moderate RoB).
- Could not analyze operator experience, insertion torque, or sinus penetration (insufficient data).
- High-quality prospective trials needed to confirm factor effects.

## 6. Related Work

- Reinforces [[wiki/orthodontics/alharbi-2018-miniscrew-failure-rate-orthodontics-sr-ma]] by extending the success/failure evidence to the extra-radicular IZC site.
- Complements [[wiki/orthodontics/mohammed-2018-miniscrew-anatomical-site-failure-sr-ma]] (anatomical-site effects) and [[wiki/orthodontics/jahanbin-2025-reused-miniscrew-stability-sr-ma]].

## 7. Glossary

- **IZC-MSI**: infrazygomatic crest miniscrew implant; extra-radicular TAD placed near the maxillary first-molar root.
- **TAD**: temporary anchorage device.
- **Extra-radicular / extra-alveolar**: placement outside inter-root alveolar bone, avoiding root contact.
- **I² / prediction interval**: heterogeneity statistic / range for a future study's true effect.
