## Why Ingested

즉시 로딩(immediate loading) 프로토콜이 무치악 하악 비연결형(unsplinted) 임플란트 유지 오버덴처에서 변연골소실(Marginal Bone Loss, MBL)을 증가시키는지에 대한 정량 근거. 단일치 즉시 로딩을 다룬 [[immediate-implant/esposito-2015-immediate-loading-vs-delayed-anterior-rct]]을 무치악 오버덴처 맥락으로 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 5 RCTs + 2 cohorts (191 participants, 400 implants) finding no significant difference in peri-implant MBL between immediate and delayed loading of unsplinted mandibular overdentures (WMD 0.04 mm, 95% CI −0.13 to 0.21), though immediate loading carried a numerically higher implant failure rate (5.03% vs 1.00%).

## 한줄요약

5 RCT + 2 코호트(191명·400 임플란트) SR+MA — 무치악 하악 비연결형 오버덴처에서 즉시 로딩과 지연 로딩 간 임플란트 변연골소실(MBL) 차이는 없었으나(WMD 0.04 mm), 즉시 로딩군 임플란트 실패율이 수치상 더 높았다(5.03% vs 1.00%).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: BMC Oral Health (2021) 21:122
- Tianjin Medical University, China. Open access (CC BY 4.0).
- Search to Dec 2, 2020; ROB by Cochrane tool, Newcastle–Ottawa scale, Egger's test.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First meta-analysis of MBL comparing immediate vs delayed loading specifically in **unsplinted mandibular overdentures**.
- Subgroup analysis by attachment type (Locator vs ball anchor).
- Quantifies the early implant failure trade-off of immediate loading.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: SR+MA of RCTs, CCTs, and cohort studies.
- Databases: PubMed, EMBASE, CENTRAL + grey literature.
- Outcome: pooled MBL via weighted mean differences (WMDs) with 95% CIs.
- Subgroup: Locator attachment vs ball anchor.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Included: 5 RCTs + 2 cohort studies; 191 participants, 400 implants (from 328 records).
- MBL: WMD 0.04 mm (95% CI −0.13 to 0.21, P > .05) — no difference.
- Subgroup (Locator vs ball anchor): no difference (P > .05).
- Implant failure: immediate loading 5.03% (10/199) vs delayed 1.00% (2/201).
- ROB: 4 RCTs moderate, 1 RCT high; 2 cohorts acceptable.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small total sample; few RCTs; one high-ROB trial.
- Failure-rate difference not formally pooled — descriptive only.
- Heterogeneous attachment systems and follow-up durations.

## 6. Related Work

- Loading-protocol literature for single implants and full-arch prostheses.

## 7. Glossary

- **Unsplinted overdenture**: implant-retained removable denture where implants are not connected by a bar.
- **MBL**: marginal (crestal) bone loss around the implant neck.
- **ILP / DLP**: immediate / delayed loading protocol.
