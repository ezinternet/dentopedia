## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 7 RCTs (393 patients, 474 implants) showing short implants (<8 mm) reduce marginal bone loss (MD −0.26 mm) and biological complications versus long implants with sinus floor elevation in the atrophic posterior maxilla, with equivalent survival.

## 한줄요약

무작위대조시험(RCT) 7편(환자 393명, 임플란트 474개) 체계적 고찰·메타분석. 위축된 구치부 상악에서 짧은 임플란트(<8mm)는 상악동거상술(sinus floor elevation)을 동반한 긴 임플란트보다 변연골소실(MBL −0.26mm)과 생물학적 합병증이 적었고 생존율은 동등했다.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Cureus 17(7): e89103, published July 31, 2025
- Search: PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Cochrane (inception–June 2025)
- RoB: Cochrane RoB-2; random-effects (STATA MP 18)

## 2. Key Contributions
- 최신(2025) RCT-only 합성으로 short vs long+SFE 근거를 갱신.
- Primary outcome를 MBL로 설정 — 생존 일변도가 아닌 골보존 관점.
- 생물학적 합병증 감소를 정량화(OR 0.39).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PICO: 위축 구치부 상악, short (<8 mm) vs long (8–15 mm)+SFE.
- Only RCTs; intention-to-treat outcomes.
- 7 RCTs, 393 patients, 474 implants.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- MBL: short lower (MD = −0.26 mm; 95% CI −0.43 to −0.09; p<0.001; I²=56.3%).
- Biological complications: short lower (OR 0.39; 95% CI 0.18–0.85; p=0.02; I²=0%).
- Survival: no difference (OR 0.96; 95% CI 0.74–1.25; p=0.76; I²=0%).
- Prosthetic complications: no difference (OR 2.18; 95% CI 0.82–5.82; p=0.12).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- MBL 이질성 중등도(I²=56%).
- 추적 기간 제한 — 장기 생존 결론 유보(저자 명시).
- short implant 정의(<8mm) — ultrashort(≤6mm) 별도 구분 미흡.
