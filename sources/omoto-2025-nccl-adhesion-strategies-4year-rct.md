## Why Ingested
4년 RCT로 universal adhesive ± selective enamel etching vs RMGIC ± EDTA를 직접 비교. [[resin-bonding/peumans-2023-universal-adhesive-nccl-3year-rct]]의 selective enamel etching 효과와 GIC 대 composite 논쟁을 long-term data로 보강.

## One-line Summary
Split-mouth RCT (50 patients, 200 NCCLs) showing selective enamel etching reduced retention loss for universal adhesive, RMGIC lost more surface luster than composite, and survival curves did not differ among 4 strategies at 4 years (p=0.315).

## 한줄요약
NCCL 200개 4년 split-mouth RCT — selective enamel etching이 universal adhesive 유지력 손실 감소, RMGIC는 광택 손실 더 큼, 4개 전략 간 생존곡선 차이 없음(p=0.315).

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dentistry 2025;153:105529. DOI 10.1016/j.jdent.2024.105529. Sao Paulo State Univ (UNESP). FAPESP/CAPES funded. Trial reg RBR-655c3z.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 4-year comparison of four adhesion strategies in NCCLs: SBU (Scotchbond Universal, no etch), E-SBU (selective enamel etch), RMGIC (Vitremer), E-RMGIC (EDTA pretreatment).
- Selective enamel etching reduced retention loss for the universal adhesive (E-SBU > SBU at retention).
- RMGIC/E-RMGIC showed more surface-texture/luster degradation than composite groups.
- EDTA pretreatment of RMGIC did not yield a clear clinical advantage.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Prospective, randomized, double-blind, split-mouth; 200 restorations in 50 patients (mean age 61, range 38-92); 40 returned at 4 yr. Modified USPHS criteria at baseline/1/2/3/4 yr. Kruskal-Wallis, Friedman, Wilcoxon (p<0.05).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Survival (retention) curves: no significant difference among groups, p=0.315.
- SBU had lower retention at 4 yr than baseline; E-SBU mitigated this.
- All groups: decreased alpha scores for marginal integrity and marginal discoloration vs baseline; initial marginal defects increased for all strategies.
- RMGIC and E-RMGIC: more surface-texture alteration than SBU/E-SBU.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 20% dropout (40/50) by 4 yr; elderly cohort (mean 61); single-center; no intention-to-treat. Material-specific (Scotchbond Universal, Vitremer) — limited extrapolation.

## 6. Related Work
- Naupari-Villasante 2023 (5-yr universal adhesive), de Oliveira 2023 (HEMA/10-MDP SE 2-yr), Luque-Martinez 2015 (EDTA conditioning RCT), Bezerra 2020 SR+MA (GIC vs composite).

## 7. Glossary
- SBU: Scotchbond Universal Adhesive. RMGIC: resin-modified glass-ionomer cement. EDTA: ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid (mild dentin conditioner / MMP inhibitor). USPHS: United States Public Health Service criteria.
