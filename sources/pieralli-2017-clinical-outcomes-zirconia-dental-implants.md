## Why Ingested

지르코니아 임플란트 생존·MBL 근거의 초기 high-quality SR(JDR). 최신 [[implants/mohseni-2024-clinical-outcomes-zirconia-implants]] SR+MA의 historical anchor로서, 12개월 생존율과 MBL covariate 분석을 제공.

## One-line Summary
SR (9 trials — 2 RCT + 7 prospective, 398 implants) — zirconia implant 12-month survival 95.6%, mean MBL 0.79 mm at 12 mo; design/material/loading mode did not significantly affect MBL.

## 한줄요약
체계적문헌고찰(9편: RCT 2 + 전향 7, 임플란트 398개): 지르코니아 임플란트 12개월 생존율 95.6%, 12개월 평균 변연골소실 0.79mm; 설계·재료·하중방식은 MBL에 유의 영향 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dental Research 2017. Search to November 2015 (MEDLINE/Cochrane/EMBASE). PRISMA.
- Inclusion: RCT and prospective trials, >15 patients.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Early pooled survival and MBL estimates for zirconia implants restored with crowns/FDPs.
- Separate meta-analyses for first year vs subsequent years (failures cluster early).
- Tested covariate influence on MBL.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Systematic review; 4196 titles screened → 2 RCT + 7 prospective trials (326 patients, 398 implants). Follow-up 12–60 mo.
- Meta-analysis for 12-mo survival and 12-mo MBL; covariate assessment.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 12-mo survival 95.6% (95% CI 93.3–97.9); thereafter ~0.05%/year expected decrease (0.25% at 5y).
- 12-mo mean MBL 0.79 mm (95% CI 0.73–0.86).
- Implant bulk material/design, restoration type, minor augmentation, temporization, loading mode — no significant MBL influence.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Short follow-up (≤60 mo), few studies, mostly prospective single-arm; early healing-period loss dominant.

## 6. Related Work
- Mohseni 2024 update; titanium implant survival.

## 7. Glossary
- MBL: marginal bone loss. FDP: fixed dental prosthesis.
