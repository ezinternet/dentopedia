## Why Ingested

[[wiki/endodontics/vpt/fasoulas-2023-pulpotomy-direct-pulp-capping-sr-ma]]가 pulpotomy/DPC 재료를 비교했으나, 본 Scientific Reports (Komora 2024) network meta-analysis는 21편 RCT를 시점별(6/12/24개월)·modality별로 계층화해 MTA를 기준으로 Ca(OH)2·Biodentine·Totalfill을 직접 순위화함으로써 anchor의 재료 비교에 시점별 정량 OR 근거를 보강.

## One-line Summary

Network meta-analysis of 21 RCTs (mature permanent teeth) ranking VPT bioactive materials against MTA: calcium hydroxide had significantly higher failure (OR ~2.3–2.5 at 6/12/24 mo) while Biodentine and Totalfill did not differ from MTA.

## 한줄요약

21편 RCT(성숙영구치)의 네트워크 메타분석으로, VPT 생체활성재료를 MTA 기준 비교 시 수산화칼슘은 6·12·24개월에서 유의하게 높은 실패율(OR 약 2.3–2.5)을 보였고 Biodentine·Totalfill은 MTA와 차이가 없었다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Scientific Reports, 2024;14:18421
- DOI: 10.1038/s41598-024-69367-7
- Design: Systematic review and network meta-analysis (PRISMA-NMA), RCTs only; PROSPERO CRD42022375573
- Search: MEDLINE/EMBASE/Cochrane/Web of Science to Jan 20, 2024
- Included: 21 RCTs; primary outcome = odds ratio of failure (95% CI) at 6/12/24 months

## 2. Key Contributions

- First stratified network meta-analysis ranking individual contemporary calcium-silicate materials (not lumped) against MTA in mature permanent teeth.
- Stratification by follow-up (6/12/24 mo) AND by modality (DPC, partial/full pulpotomy).
- Provides clear chairside ranking: MTA, Biodentine, Totalfill efficient; calcium hydroxide not recommended.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PICOS: P = mature permanent teeth undergoing VPT; I = Biodentine/Ca(OH)2/CEM/calcium-silicate materials; C = MTA; O = failure rate; S = RCTs.
- Inclusion: RCTs, reversible/irreversible pulpitis (or Wolters classification), follow-up ≥6 months.
- Exclusion: case reports, cohort/case-control, reviews, animal, non-English, primary/immature/trauma teeth.
- RoB: Cochrane RoB2; effect measure OR (>1 = higher failure than MTA).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Calcium hydroxide vs MTA** (significantly higher failure): OR 2.26 (1.52–3.36) at 6 mo; OR 2.53 (1.76–3.62) at 12 mo; OR 2.46 (1.60–3.79) at 24 mo.
- **Totalfill vs MTA**: OR 1.19 (0.55–2.58) at 6 mo; OR 1.43 (0.71–2.92) at 12 mo — NS.
- **Biodentine vs MTA**: OR 1.09 (0.66–1.78) at 6 mo; OR 1.21 (0.74–1.96) at 12 mo; OR 1.47 (0.81–2.68) at 24 mo — NS.
- Direct-pulp-capping subgroup mirrored overall results; partial/full pulpotomy subgroup had insufficient evidence for significant differences.
- Conclusion: MTA, Biodentine, Totalfill most efficient; Ca(OH)2 not recommended.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Pulpotomy subgroups underpowered (no significant differences achievable).
- Heterogeneity in diagnosis and follow-up handling of lost cases (authors imputed lost unsuccessful cases forward).
- TheraCal LC and CEM sparsely represented.

## 6. Related Work

- Reinforces pulpotomy/pulp-capping material comparisons (Fasoulas 2023).
- Converges with later VPT network meta-analyses (Pusa 2025, Elmsmari 2026).
