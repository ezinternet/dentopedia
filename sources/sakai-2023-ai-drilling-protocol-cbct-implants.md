## One-line Summary
J Prosthodont Res 2023;67(3):360-365 (Osaka U): LeNet-5 AI model on CBCT images predicts drilling protocol A/B/C — overall accuracy 93.8%; AUC 98.6–99.4%; potential decision-support tool for primary stability planning.

## 1. Key Results
- n=60 patients; 1200 CBCT slices (20 slices/patient); 3 drilling protocols A/B/C
- Training/validation 80% (n=960); test 20% (n=240)
- AI model (LeNet-5): accuracy 93.8%; sensitivity 85–97.5% by protocol
- AUC values: 98.6% (A), 98.6% (B), 99.4% (C)
- Gray values from CBCT (not HU) used as bone quality proxy

## 2. Clinical Implications
- CBCT-based AI can predict appropriate drilling protocol pre-surgically
- Decision-support potential particularly for less experienced clinicians
- Gray values (CBCT) can serve as bone quality metric for protocol selection
- Prospective clinical validation and multi-site testing still needed
