## Why Ingested

지대주 나사 조임 반복 시 예압(preload) 감소 현상은 임상적으로 나사 풀림 합병증의 핵심 원인이다. 탄소 코팅(carbon-coated) 나사의 마찰계수 변화가 예압에 미치는 영향을 정량화한 기초 연구로, [[prosthetic-materials/implant-abutment-screw-loosening]] 관련 임상 결정(재조임 횟수 제한, 나사 교체 정책)의 근거로 활용.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (n=25 implant-abutment-screw complexes, Nobel Biocare) showed repeated tightening to 25 Ncm (×10) significantly decreased preload from 329.9 N to 253.7 N and increased the coefficient of friction from 0.33 to 0.44, suggesting carbon-coated screws should not be reused.

## 한줄요약

탄소 코팅 지대주 나사를 25 Ncm으로 10회 반복 조임 시 예압(preload)이 329.9 N → 253.7 N으로 유의하게 감소하고 마찰계수가 0.33 → 0.44로 증가하여, 코팅 나사 재사용은 최적 예압 달성에 실패함을 시험관 내 실험으로 확인.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: International Journal of Implant Dentistry (2023) 9:8
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s40729-023-00473-3
- **Study type**: In vitro mechanical test
- **Institution**: University Medical Center Mainz, Germany

## 2. Key Contributions

- First study to directly measure both preload force AND coefficient of friction (COF) in a carbon-coated abutment screw system using a custom load frame
- Demonstrates that carbon coating does NOT reduce friction as intended; instead, repeated use increases COF significantly
- Provides quantitative COF values (0.33–0.44) for this specific implant-abutment interface using VDI 2230 calculation standard
- Challenges the assumption that coated screws maintain stable preload across multiple tightening sequences

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Sample**: 25 unused Nobel Biocare implant-abutment-screw complexes (Replace Select Tapered implant + Temporary Abutment Non-engaging + carbon-coated Abutment Screw Nobel Replace)
- **Custom load frame**: Two load cells (750 N top / 150 N bottom) to simultaneously measure preload and thread-friction torque component; gap of 0.10 mm maintained between abutment and implant throughout
- **Protocol**: 10 tightening-untightening cycles at 25 Ncm per unit; 60 s hold per cycle; dry conditions (no lubrication)
- **COF calculation**: VDI 2230 Part 1 formula; screw geometry measured by SEM (50× magnification)
- **Statistics**: Linear mixed model (SPSS 23.0); Tukey–Kramer post-hoc; α = 0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Parameter | 1st tightening | 10th tightening | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Preload (N) | 329.9 ± 33.3 | 253.7 ± 36.8 | < 0.0001 |
| COF | 0.33 ± 0.04 | 0.44 ± 0.07 | — |

- Preload decreased significantly from 1st to 2nd tightening (Δ17.4 N, p<0.0001) and from 2nd to 3rd (Δ13.8 N, p=0.001); subsequent steps not significantly different from the one directly preceding
- Thread-friction component showed no consistent trend with repetitions — loss is attributed to screw head friction, not thread friction
- Overall monotone decreasing trend in preload across all 10 cycles

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro only: no masticatory load cycling, no simulated clinical use (saliva, temperature, occlusal forces)
- Single implant system (Nobel Biocare); results not generalizable to other implant-abutment geometries or coating types
- Dry conditions — lubricated conditions (e.g., saliva) may behave differently
- No histological/surface analysis of coating wear after repeated tightening
- Future studies: in vivo/clinical follow-up; comparison with uncoated Ti screws or gold-plated screws; different torque protocols

## 6. Related Work

- VDI 2230 Part 1 guideline: mathematical framework for torque-preload relationship in bolted joints
- Prior literature on abutment screw loosening (references 1–6 in paper) establishes clinical importance
- Comparable studies on uncoated and gold-plated abutment screws (not directly cited; context comparison)

## 7. Glossary

- **예압 (Preload, FVM)**: 나사 조임으로 발생하는 축방향 인장력; 임플란트-지대주 결합부를 유지하는 힘
- **마찰계수 (Coefficient of Friction, COF)**: 접촉면 간 마찰 정도를 나타내는 무차원 수
- **탄소 코팅 (Carbon-coated)**: DLC(Diamond-Like Carbon) 계열 박막 코팅; 마찰 감소 목적으로 나사 표면에 적용
- **VDI 2230**: 독일 엔지니어협회 볼트 이음 계산 표준; 토크-예압-마찰계수 관계식 제공
- **Thread-friction**: 나사산 간 마찰; 조임 토크의 약 40% 소비
- **Screw head friction**: 나사 머리 접촉면 마찰; 조임 토크의 약 50% 소비 (전체 마찰의 주된 요소)
