## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Prospective 5-year study (n=6 patients) evaluating enamel wear of teeth opposing polished monolithic 3Y-TZP resin-bonded FPDs (inlay- or wing-retained) vs contralateral enamel-enamel controls. Indirect wear measurement via annual polyvinylsiloxane impressions and digitized gypsum models. After 5 years: mean enamel wear depth opposing 3Y-TZP = 77 μm vs 54 μm (enamel control). Difference significant after 2 years. Wear remained within clinically acceptable limits. Small sample size limits generalizability.
