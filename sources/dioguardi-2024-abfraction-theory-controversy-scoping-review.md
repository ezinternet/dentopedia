## Why Ingested
Abfraction 논쟁을 PRISMA-ScR + ROBINS-I로 정식 분석한 최신 scoping review. [[nccl/duangthip-2017-occlusal-stress-nccl-abfraction-sr]]의 "81% 연관" 결론을 직접 재비판하며 abfraction 논쟁 축의 최신 근거. overview의 gap 섹션 핵심.

## One-line Summary
Scoping review (PRISMA-ScR, ROBINS-I; only 6/1449 studies included) concluding the evidence is insufficient to confirm or refute the aetiological role of occlusal loads in abfraction; prospective longitudinal studies are needed.

## 한줄요약
PRISMA-ScR scoping review(1449편 중 6편) — 교합부하의 abfraction 병인 역할을 확정도 반박도 못함, 전향적 종단연구 필요.

## 1. Document Information
- Current Oral Health Reports 2024;11:237-247. DOI 10.1007/s40496-024-00378-z. Published 2024-05-17. Open access (CC BY). Univ of Foggia. PRISMA-ScR; protocol on INPLASY; ROBINS-I risk of bias.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Most rigorous (registered, PRISMA-ScR) appraisal of abfraction to date; included only 6 of 1449 articles correlating NCCL progression to applied forces.
- Directly critiques Duangthip 2017: that SR did not separate true abfraction from pure erosion/abrasion lesions, lab-weighted, and acknowledged no clinical studies link occlusal stress to NCCLs.
- Concludes evidence is insufficient to confirm OR refute occlusal-load aetiology; "clues" exist but need prospective longitudinal confirmation considering coexisting erosion/abrasion.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PRISMA-ScR scoping review; PubMed + Scopus, keywords "abfraction" AND "NCCL"; 1449 identified → 6 included. ROBINS-I for risk of bias.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 6 included studies; none provided sufficient evidence to confirm/refute occlusal-load role in abfraction.
- Prevalence framing: NCCLs ~10-40% of adults >30, premolars predominant.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Scoping (not full SR+MA); very small included set; heterogeneous lesion definitions. Calls for prospective longitudinal studies isolating abfraction from erosion/abrasion.

## 6. Related Work
- Duangthip 2017 (re-analyzed), Senna 2012, Silva 2013, Dioguardi 2023 (same group review).

## 7. Glossary
- PRISMA-ScR: PRISMA extension for Scoping Reviews. ROBINS-I: Risk Of Bias In Non-randomized Studies of Interventions. INPLASY: review protocol registry.
