## Why Ingested

Extends [[wiki/implants/mortazavi-2021-bone-loss-tissue-bone-level-implants]] from the implant-type axis (tissue- vs bone-level) to the full set of prosthetic design factors driving peri-implant marginal bone loss, with a current (search through Dec 2023) 93-study AO/AAP joint SR+MA that quantifies each factor's effect on MBL and peri-implantitis risk.

## One-line Summary

AO/AAP SR+MA of 93 studies: nonsplinted (8 articles, p=0.04), platform-switched (20 articles, p<0.0001), conical internal connections (20 articles, p<0.0001), abutment height ≥2 mm (12 articles, p<0.0001), and one-abutment-one-time (10 articles, p<0.0001) each reduce peri-implant MBL; screw- vs cement-retention (p=0.51) and crown-to-implant ratio (p=0.32) showed no significant MBL difference.

## 한줄요약

93편 AO/AAP SR+MA: 비연결(nonsplinted, p=0.04), 플랫폼스위칭(p<0.0001), 원추형 내부연결(p<0.0001), 어버트먼트 높이 ≥2 mm(p<0.0001), one-abutment-one-time(p<0.0001)은 임플란트주위 변연골소실(MBL)을 줄였고, 나사 vs 시멘트 유지(p=0.51)·치관-임플란트 비(p=0.32)는 차이 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Periodontology 2025;96:634–651 (joint AO/Academy of Osseointegration & American Academy of Periodontology project)
- Design: Systematic review + meta-analysis (PROSPERO CRD42023484513), PRISMA-guided
- Search: Ovid MEDLINE, EMBASE, Dentistry & Oral Sciences Source, Jan 1980 – Dec 2023
- 93 studies included; 85 in meta-analyses (8 qualitative only)
- Inclusion: ≥12 mo post-restoration follow-up, ≥10 implants/group, comparative design; RCTs + non-RCTs
- Risk of bias: RoB 2 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (non-RCTs)

## 2. Key Contributions

Comprehensive single-source quantification of how each prosthetic design lever affects MBL (inverse-variance WMD, implant unit) and peri-implantitis risk (risk ratio for emergence factors):

- **Splinting**: nonsplinted < splinted MBL (8 articles, p=0.04).
- **Platform switching**: switched < matched MBL (20 articles, p<0.0001).
- **Connection**: internal — especially conical — < external MBL (20 articles, p<0.0001).
- **Abutment height**: ≥2 mm < <2 mm MBL (12 articles, p<0.0001).
- **One-abutment-one-time** < repeated disconnections MBL (10 articles, p<0.0001).
- **Emergence angle <30°** and **concave/straight profile** → lower peri-implantitis risk (2 articles each; p=0.05 and p=0.03).
- **No significant MBL effect**: screw- vs cement-retention (6 articles, p=0.51); crown-to-implant ratio (5 articles, p=0.32).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PECOS-framed focused question on prosthetic design and peri-implant bone level. Pooled weighted mean difference via inverse-variance meta-analysis (RevMan), implant as unit of analysis, forest plots per factor. Subgroup analyses for crown-to-implant ratio (>2 vs <2; >1 vs <1) and internal-connection type (straight vs conical). For emergence angle/profile, risk ratio + 95% CI for peri-implantitis prevalence used (limited MBL data). Random-effects when I²>50%, fixed-effects otherwise. Funnel plots for publication bias; significance α=0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

See Key Contributions — the actionable hierarchy: nonsplinted, platform-switched, conical internal connection, abutment height ≥2 mm, one-abutment-one-time, and shallow/concave emergence are the bone-protective choices; retention mode and crown-to-implant ratio are neutral for MBL.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Emergence-angle/profile conclusions rest on only 2 articles each (low evidence). Mixed RCT + non-RCT pool; heterogeneity high for several factors. Eight studies excluded from quantitative pooling for insufficient data. AO/AAP framing aims at consensus guidance rather than novel primary data.

## 6. Related Work

Extends prior single-factor reviews (platform switching, abutment height, one-abutment-one-time) into one consolidated appraisal. Reinforces the abutment-height and platform-switching literature (Tajti 2024; Strietzel 2015) and complements implant-type MBL work (Mortazavi 2021).

## 7. Glossary

- **MBL**: marginal bone loss.
- **Platform switching**: narrower abutment on a wider implant platform; horizontal offset of the implant-abutment junction.
- **One-abutment-one-time**: definitive abutment placed once at surgery, never disconnected.
- **Emergence angle/profile**: restoration contour angle and shape at the implant junction.
- **WMD**: weighted mean difference. **RoB 2 / ROBINS-I**: Cochrane risk-of-bias tools.
