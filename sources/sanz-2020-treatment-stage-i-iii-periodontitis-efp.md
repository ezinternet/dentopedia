## One-line Summary
EFP S3 level clinical practice guideline for stage I–III periodontitis treatment using stepwise therapy; based on 15 commissioned SRs and GRADE methodology.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Periodontology 2020; 47(Suppl. 22): 4–60
- **Study type**: Clinical practice guideline (S3 level, EFP)

## 2. Key Contributions
- First S3-level EFP CPG for periodontitis treatment based on 2018 classification (stages/grades)
- Stepwise treatment approach: Step 1 (behaviour/supragingival) → Step 2 (subgingival instrumentation ± adjuncts) → Step 3 (surgery) → Step 4 (SPT)
- Systematic synthesis of 15 commissioned systematic reviews
- GRADE evidence quality ratings for all recommendations

## 3. Methodology
- S3 level: developed per AWMF (Association of Scientific Medical Societies in Germany) and GRADE
- 15 specifically commissioned SRs covering all treatment modalities
- Structured consensus process with leading experts and stakeholders
- Covers stages I, II, III periodontitis (stage IV addressed separately)

## 4. Key Recommendations
- **Step 1**: Behavioural change, supragingival biofilm control, risk factor management (smoking, diabetes)
- **Step 2**: Supra- and subgingival instrumentation (SRP); adjunctive local/systemic antibiotics in selected cases
- **Step 3**: Periodontal surgery (resective or regenerative) when Step 2 goals not achieved
- **Step 4**: Supportive periodontal therapy (SPT) — individualised recall intervals (3–12 months)
- Systemic antibiotics: adjunct to SRP in stage III, grade C/D; not recommended for stages I/II
- Photodynamic therapy and lasers: insufficient evidence for routine use

## 5. Limitations
- Guideline based on evidence available at time of publication (2020)
- Implementation challenges in resource-limited settings
