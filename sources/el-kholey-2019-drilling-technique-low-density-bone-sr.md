## Why Ingested

저밀도골 drilling 프로토콜의 임상적 가치를 비판적으로 한정하기 위해 인제스트. 개별 실험연구([[implants/tabassum-2021-undersized-axial-compression-primary-stability]])와 osseodensification SR([[implants/fontes-pereira-2023-osseodensification-osteotomy-alternative-sr]])이 일차안정성 향상을 보고하지만, 본 SR은 그 향상이 장기 생존으로 이어진다는 근거가 약함을 명시 — overview의 "일차안정성 ≠ 장기성공" thesis를 강화.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (15 studies: 7 experimental + 8 clinical; no meta-analysis) concluding that undersized, osteotome, Piezosurgery, and osseodensification drilling all raise primary stability in low-density bone, but evidence that they improve long-term osseointegration/survival vs conventional drilling is weak.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰(15편: 실험 7 + 임상 8, 메타분석 없음) — undersized·osteotome·Piezosurgery·osseodensification 4개 술식 모두 저밀도골 일차안정성을 높이지만, 장기 생존이 전통 드릴링보다 우수하다는 근거는 약하다는 결론.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Maps the four main drilling alternatives for low-density bone into one comparative frame: undersized, osteotome, Piezosurgery, osseodensification.
- Explicitly separates primary-stability gain (consistent) from long-term success (unproven).
- Highlights heterogeneity preventing meta-analysis.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Systematic review of animal and clinical studies on drilling technique vs implant integration/survival in low-density bone.
- 15 studies met inclusion (7 experimental, 8 clinical).
- Methodological variation precluded meta-analysis (narrative synthesis).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- All four protocols effective at increasing primary stability (insertion torque indicator).
- Long-term outcome comparable to conventional drilling.
- Overall: weak evidence that any technique enhances successful osseointegration/survival in low-density bone.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- No meta-analysis; high heterogeneity in technique, bone classification, outcomes.
- Search ends April 2018 — predates much recent osseodensification RCT evidence.
- Subjective bone-density classification (Lekholm-Zarb) in many included studies.

## 6. Related Work
- Lekholm & Zarb bone classification; Schwarz CT-based density assessment.
- Huwais osseodensification concept; osteotome (summers) technique.

## 7. Glossary
- Undersized drilling: final drill diameter smaller than implant to densify the wall.
- Osteotome: hand instrument laterally/apically compacting bone from a pilot hole.
- Osseodensification: counter-clockwise densifying burs (Densah).
