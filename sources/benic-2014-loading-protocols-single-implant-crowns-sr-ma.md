## Why Ingested

단일 임플란트 단관(single-implant crown)에서 즉시 로딩 vs 통상 로딩의 생존·변연골소실 동등성과, 그 결론이 성립하는 **1차 안정성 임계값(삽입토크 ≥20–45 Ncm, ISQ ≥60–65)** 조건을 정립한 기준 SR+MA. 무치악 로딩을 다룬 [[implants/abdunabi-2019-immediately-loaded-maxillary-full-arch-sr]]·[[implants/liu-2021-immediate-delayed-loading-overdenture-mbl-sr-ma]]의 단일치 대응 근거.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 11 RCTs finding immediately and conventionally loaded single-implant crowns equally successful for implant survival (1-yr OR 0.75, 95% CI 0.32–1.76) and marginal bone loss (SMD −0.05 mm) through 5 years, valid for implants placed at ≥20–45 Ncm torque or ISQ ≥60–65 without simultaneous augmentation.

## 한줄요약

11개 RCT SR+MA — 단일 임플란트 단관에서 즉시 로딩과 통상 로딩은 5년까지 생존율(1년 OR 0.75)·변연골소실(SMD −0.05 mm)이 동등하며, 이는 삽입토크 ≥20–45 Ncm 또는 ISQ ≥60–65·동시 골증대 불필요 조건에서 성립한다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: International Journal of Oral & Maxillofacial Implants 2014;29(Suppl):222–238. PMID 24660200.
- University of Zurich (Hämmerle group).
- Search of Medline/Embase to Aug 1, 2012 + manual; RCTs with ≥1-year follow-up; meta-analysis (OR, SMD).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Establishes equivalence of immediate vs conventional loading for single-implant crowns.
- Defines the primary-stability envelope (torque ≥20–45 Ncm or ISQ ≥60–65) under which immediate loading is safe.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- SR+MA of RCTs comparing loading protocols for single-implant crowns.
- Outcomes: implant survival, MBL, peri-implant soft-tissue stability, esthetics, patient satisfaction at 1/2/3/5 years.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 10 RCTs immediate vs conventional + 1 RCT immediate vs early.
- 1-yr survival: OR 0.75 (95% CI 0.32–1.76) — no difference (10 studies).
- 1-yr MBL: SMD −0.05 mm (95% CI −0.41 to 0.31) — no difference (7 RCTs).
- No differences in survival/MBL at 2, 3, 5 years; no papilla-level difference (3 RCTs).
- Buccal mucosa recession: contradictory (1 RCT favored immediate; 2 RCTs no difference) — inconclusive.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Heterogeneous baseline timepoints; few studies for esthetics/satisfaction.
- Conclusions conditional on adequate primary stability and no augmentation.

## 6. Related Work

- ITI loading-protocol consensus; single-crown immediate-loading RCTs.

## 7. Glossary

- **ISQ**: implant stability quotient (resonance frequency analysis).
- **SMD / OR**: standardized mean difference / odds ratio.
