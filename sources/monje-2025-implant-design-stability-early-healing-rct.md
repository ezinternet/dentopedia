## Why Ingested

ISQ 추적 RCT로 early healing 중 안정성 감소(안정성 감소 구간, Stability Dip) 패턴 분석; 테이퍼형(BLX) vs 원통형(BL) 임플란트 디자인이 초기 치유 기간 ISQ 궤적에 미치는 영향을 직접 비교. [[implants/isq/]] stability ladder 보강 및 디자인 변수 이해에 기여.

## One-line Summary

RCT (n=85 implants, 66 patients) comparing tapered (BLX) vs cylindrical (BL) Straumann implants over 6-week healing: BLX showed higher insertion torque but BL achieved higher final ISQ (75.2 vs 72.2) at 6 weeks, though both designs maintained high stability throughout.

## 한줄요약

RCT (85개 임플란트, 66명): 테이퍼형(BLX)은 초기 삽입 토크(42.4 N·cm)가 높으나, 6주 후 최종 ISQ는 원통형(BL, 75.2)이 유의하게 높았고(p=0.02), 두 디자인 모두 초기 치유 중 높은 안정성을 유지함.

## 2. Key Contributions

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- First direct RCT comparing ISQ/IST stability trajectories of tapered (BLX) vs cylindrical (BL) Straumann SLA/Roxolid implants over a dense 6-week measurement schedule (8 time points).
- Demonstrates that higher initial insertion torque (BLX) does not translate to higher final ISQ at 6 weeks — cylindrical BL implants converge to higher ISQ by T8.
- Validates IST (implant stability test) as a surrogate for ISQ with moderate correlation (r=0.56), supporting its use as a non-ISQ stability monitor.
- Establishes both designs as suitable for various prosthetic loading protocols based on stability data.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- **Design**: RCT, parallel-arm
- **Population**: Patients with edentulous sites, extraction ≥2 months prior
- **Implants**: Tapered BLX (n=45) vs cylindrical BL (n=40), both SLA surface / Roxolid alloy (Straumann, Switzerland)
- **Stability measurements**:
  - Maximum insertion torque (MIT) at placement
  - Implant Stability Quotient (ISQ, via RFA/Osstell) at T1 (immediate) and T8 (6 weeks)
  - Implant Stability Test (IST) at T1 (immediate), T2 (Day 2), and T3–T8 (weekly for 6 weeks)
- **Sample**: 85 implants in 66 patients

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

| Parameter | BLX (Tapered) | BL (Cylindrical) | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mean MIT (N·cm) | 42.35 ± 13.43 | 30.63 ± 6.01 | <0.001 |
| ISQ at T8 (6 wks) | 72.23 ± 0.88 | 75.21 ± 0.93 | 0.02 |
| IST at T8 (6 wks) | 76.06 ± 1.04 | 79.69 ± 1.11 | 0.02 |
| ISQ vs IST correlation | r = 0.56 | — | <0.001 |

- Both designs showed **similar stability trends** at intermediate time points (T2–T7); difference emerged only at T8.
- BLX higher MIT likely reflects tapered geometry compressing bone during placement.
- BL higher final ISQ at 6 weeks may reflect different osseointegration dynamics for cylindrical geometry.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- Abstract does not report ISQ values at T1 (baseline) separately for each group — cannot assess magnitude of stability dip from abstract alone.
- Short 6-week follow-up; longer-term ISQ trajectory and clinical outcomes (survival, MBL) not reported in this abstract.
- Single implant system (Straumann SLA/Roxolid) — generalizability to other surfaces/alloys unclear.
- Study conducted at a single university center (Chulalongkorn); multicenter validation warranted.

## 6. Related Work

abstract-only — full text not retrieved

- Tapered vs cylindrical implant stability comparisons historically mixed; this RCT contributes dense time-series data.
- IST as ISQ surrogate: moderate correlation (r=0.56) consistent with prior IST validation studies.
- SLA surface and Roxolid alloy are well-characterized in osseointegration literature (see Straumann BLX/BL clinical series).

## 7. Glossary

- **ISQ (임플란트 안정성 지수, Implant Stability Quotient)**: Resonance frequency analysis (RFA) output, scale 1–100; higher = more stable
- **IST (임플란트 안정성 검사, Implant Stability Test)**: Alternative stability measurement device/method correlated with ISQ
- **MIT (최대 삽입 토크, Maximum Insertion Torque)**: Peak torque recorded during implant placement (N·cm); reflects initial mechanical engagement with bone
- **BLX**: Straumann Bone Level Tapered implant (tapered body)
- **BL**: Straumann Bone Level implant (cylindrical body)
- **SLA (모래 분사-산 부식 표면, Sandblasted Large-grit Acid-etched)**: Straumann standard implant surface treatment
- **Roxolid**: Straumann titanium-zirconium alloy (TiZr, ~15% Zr) with enhanced mechanical properties vs cp-Ti
- **T1–T8**: Time points: T1=placement, T2=Day 2, T3=Week 1, T4=Week 2, T5=Week 3, T6=Week 4, T7=Week 5, T8=Week 6
