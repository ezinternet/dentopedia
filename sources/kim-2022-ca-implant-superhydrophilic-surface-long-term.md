## One-line Summary
Retrospective study (258 Osstem CA implants, 120 patients, avg 62 months): 97.3% survival, 94.2% success, MBL 0.074mm — super hydrophilic calcium-modified SA surface demonstrates excellent long-term outcomes.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: BMC Oral Health 22:97 (2022)
- DOI: 10.1186/s12903-022-02142-0
- Design: Retrospective clinical study; January 2013–December 2015 placement; ≥5yr follow-up

## 2. Key Results
- Implants: 258, Patients: 120 (61M/59F), Mean age: 63.7yr, Mean follow-up: 62 months
- Survival rate: **97.3%**
- Success rate (Albrektsson criteria): **94.2%**
- Average final MBL: **0.074 mm**
- Implant dimensions: diameter 4.63mm avg (3.5–5.5mm), length 9.94mm avg (7.0–13.0mm)

## 3. CA Surface Technology
- CA = calcium-modified surface treatment on SLA (sandblasted + acid-etched) substrate
- Super hydrophilic (contact angle ≈ 0°) → enhanced protein adsorption → faster osseointegration
- Calcium ion modification adds bioactive layer

## 4. Limitations
- Retrospective; single center (Seoul National University Bundang Hospital)
- No control group (CA vs SA comparison)
- Primarily reports outcomes without isolating surface effect from other variables
