## Why Ingested

기존 [[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/cobo-vazquez-2025-crestal-sinus-lift-osteotome-vs-osseodensification]]은 osteotome vs osseodensification 비교이나, osteotome 기술 내부의 modified drill-based TSFE(non-cutting drill)과의 직접 비교 RCT protocol이 wiki에 없음. Zhao 2018은 이 gap을 채우는 4-arm RCT 설계 문서.

## One-line Summary
RCT protocol (n=120, NCT03445039) comparing osteotome-TSFE vs modified drill-TSFE ± bone graft in posterior maxilla; RFA tracked 6× over 26 weeks.

## 한줄요약
RCT 프로토콜(120환자, NCT03445039): 후방 상악에서 osteotome-TSFE vs modified drill-TSFE ±골이식 4군 비교, RFA로 26주간 6회 안정성 추적.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Trials 2018;19:489
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s13063-018-2879-x
- **Study type**: RCT protocol / study design paper
- **n**: 120 patients planned (30/group)
- **Institution**: Peking University Hospital of Stomatology First Clinical Division
- **Registration**: NCT03445039 (registered 2018-02-26)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Osteotome-TSFE vs modified TSFE(crestal non-cutting drill) 간 첫 4-arm RCT 설계
- Graft 필요성(±) 동시 평가 가능한 factorial 구조
- RFA 6시점(baseline, 6, 8, 12, 16, 26주) — stability dip 관찰에 충분한 granularity

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **4 groups**: (1) osteotome+graft, (2) osteotome-only, (3) modified TSFE+graft, (4) modified TSFE-only
- **Modified TSFE**: crestal approach with non-cutting/modified drills instead of osteotomes
- **Eligibility**: single edentulous site, posterior maxilla; RBH ≥4mm; no active sinus pathology
- **Primary outcomes**: implant survival, ISQ stability (RFA ×6), bone remodeling (radiograph)
- **Secondary**: complications, VAS intraoperative discomfort/postoperative pain, PRO
- **Blinding**: surgeon unblinded; outcome assessor blinded where feasible
- **Follow-up**: 1 year

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Study protocol only — no efficacy results reported in this paper
- Hypothesis: modified TSFE achieves equivalent outcomes with less trauma/discomfort than traditional osteotome technique
- RFA monitoring design allows stability dip characterization across technique groups

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Protocol paper only; results depend on trial completion and publication
- Single center limits generalizability
- Lack of CBCT assessment (only 2D radiographs) for bone volume measurement

## 6. Related Work
- Cobo-Vazquez 2025: osteotome vs osseodensification comparison
- Lai 2008: RFA stability in osteotome OSFE without grafting (prospective)
- Farina 2018: morbidity comparison transcrestal vs lateral SFE

## 7. Glossary
- **TSFE** (Transalveolar Sinus Floor Elevation): osteotome 또는 drill 기반 경치조골 접근
- **Modified TSFE**: crestal non-cutting drill 사용, osteotome의 물리적 충격(말레팅) 없음
- **RFA** (Resonance Frequency Analysis): ISQ 측정 도구
