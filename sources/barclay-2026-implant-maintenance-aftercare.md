## One-line Summary

Narrative/clinical article (British Dental Journal 2026) arguing that as implants spread from the edentulous to the wider general population, a patient-focused peri-implant maintenance protocol is essential, framed by the 2018 periodontal classification which for the first time included peri-implant diseases and conditions.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Argues for a **patient-focused** (not one-size-fits-all) peri-implant maintenance protocol, driven by the demographic broadening of implant recipients.
- Links maintenance practice to the **2018 periodontal classification**, the first to formally include peri-implant diseases and conditions.
- General-dental-practice framing of biological complications and implant survival under maintenance.
