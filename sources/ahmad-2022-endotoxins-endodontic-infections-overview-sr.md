## Why Ingested

이 endotoxin 배치의 최상위 종합(overview of SRs) 논문으로, 배치 내 [[endodontics/irrigation/bedran-2020-calcium-hydroxide-endotoxin-reduction-sr-ma]]를 포함한 5개 SR을 AMSTAR2로 평가해 "화학기계적 형성과 첩약 모두 endotoxin을 완전 제거 못 한다"는 배치 thesis의 근거 천장(evidence ceiling)을 명시한다. [[endodontics/irrigation/ordinola-zapata-2022-intracanal-medicaments-status-directions]]의 medicament 한계 서술을 SR 수준 근거로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

Overview of 5 systematic reviews (2 high-quality by AMSTAR2): higher endotoxin levels correlate with symptomatic teeth (exudation, prior pain, pain on percussion); chemomechanical preparation and calcium hydroxide ICM significantly reduce but cannot eliminate endotoxins; multi-session RCT outperforms single-session for endotoxin removal in symptomatic apical periodontitis.

## 한줄요약

5개 체계적 문헌고찰을 종합한 overview(AMSTAR2 고품질 2편): endotoxin 수치는 증상(삼출·기왕통·타진통)과 상관하며, 화학기계적 형성과 수산화칼슘 첩약은 endotoxin을 유의하게 감소시키나 완전 제거는 못 하고, 증상성 근단치주염에서 다회 내원이 단회보다 endotoxin 제거에 우수함.

- **Journal**: Evidence-Based Dentistry 2022
- **DOI**: 10.1038/s41432-022-0826-x (published 08 Dec 2022)
- **Study type**: Systematic review / overview of systematic reviews (umbrella review)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Synthesizes the endodontic-endotoxin SR literature at the top of the evidence pyramid (overview of SRs / umbrella review).
- Maps endotoxin level to clinical signs/symptoms and to disinfection-procedure efficacy across 5 SRs.
- AMSTAR2 quality appraisal of the included reviews (2 of 5 high quality).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Databases: Medline, Scopus, Embase, Cochrane Library, Google Scholar; reviews up to July 2021.
- Inclusion: SRs on endotoxins based on clinical and/or observational studies.
- Quality: AMSTAR2 tool. 5 SRs selected, 2 high quality.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Significantly higher endotoxin in teeth with exudation, previous pain episode, and pain on percussion.
- Chemomechanical preparation significantly reduces endotoxin levels.
- Calcium hydroxide ICM in symptomatic teeth significantly reduced endotoxin: SMD −1.051 (95% CI −2.039 to −0.063), p<0.05, I²=83.3%, certainty very low.
- Multiple-session RCT was significantly more effective than single-session for endotoxin/LPS removal in symptomatic apical periodontitis.
- Conclusion: conventional chemomechanical preparation and ICM were **unable to eliminate** endotoxins; better disinfection therapies needed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 5 SRs, mixed AMSTAR2 quality; very low certainty for the Ca(OH)₂ effect (high heterogeneity, I²=83.3%).
- Overlap of primary studies across included SRs; observational/pre-post designs dominate.
- Calls for development of more effective disinfection therapies.

## 7. Glossary

- **Overview of SRs / umbrella review**: systematic review whose units of analysis are systematic reviews.
- **AMSTAR2**: appraisal tool for the methodological quality of systematic reviews.
- **ICM**: intracanal medication.
- **Endotoxin / LPS**: lipopolysaccharide, Gram-negative virulence factor.
