## Why Ingested
Biocompatibility evidence (CGIC vs RMGIC) for the GIC cluster; supports [[glass-ionomer/singh-2024-gic-rmgic-biological-response-sr]] and the liner/base + VPT decision points.

## One-line Summary
SR of 9 studies finding conventional GIC less cytotoxic than RMGIC, relevant to vital pulp therapy.

## 한줄요약
9편 SR — 전통 GIC가 RMGIC보다 세포독성 낮음, VPT 재료 선택에 시사.

## 1. Document Information
Restor Dent Endod 2024; 49(4): e41. DOI 10.5395/rde.2024.49.e41. Published 2024-11-01. PROSPERO CRD42023426021. Terna Dental College, Navi Mumbai, India.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Structured CGIC-vs-RMGIC biological-response comparison.
- Cytotoxicity rationale favoring CGIC for pulp-adjacent use.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
SR. Databases: MEDLINE/PubMed, EBSCO, Cochrane CENTRAL, Google Scholar. 9 studies from 552 records. Bias: MINORS, modified ARRIVE/CONSORT. Endpoints: odontoblastic changes, inflammation, tertiary dentin, microorganisms, morphology, cell viability/number/metabolism.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Conventional GIC shows lower cytotoxicity than RMGIC, particularly in vital pulp therapy contexts.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Small/heterogeneous evidence; mix of in-vitro and clinical designs; need in-vivo and long-term clinical trials.

## 6. Related Work
Dental cement biocompatibility literature; VPT material selection.

## 7. Glossary
- VPT: vital pulp therapy.
- MINORS: methodological index for non-randomized studies.
- Cytotoxicity: adverse effect on cell viability/metabolism.
