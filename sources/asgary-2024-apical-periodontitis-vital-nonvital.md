## Why Ingested

기존 [[wiki/endodontics/diagnosis/karamifar-2020-endodontic-periapical-lesion-an-overview]]가 치근단 병소의 병인·분류를 다루지만, 생활치아의 치근단 병소(ALEO in vital teeth)와 새로운 8-범주 진단 분류 체계 및 생활치수요법 적응 확장 근거가 없음. 본 리뷰는 vital/nonvital 치아 모두의 AP 임상·방사선 소견을 종합하고 VPT 우선 치료 원칙을 명문화한다.

## One-line Summary

Narrative review (IEJ 2024) proposing a new 8-diagnosis taxonomy for apical periodontitis (AP) in vital and nonvital teeth, advocating vital pulp therapy (VPT) with 92–99% long-term success as first-line treatment for vital teeth with AP, and summarizing global AP prevalence at ~52%.

## 한줄요약

서사 리뷰(IEJ 2024) — 생활·비생활 치아 모두의 치근단 주위염(AP) 임상·방사선 소견을 정리하고, 새 8범주 진단 분류를 제안하며, 생활치수요법(VPT)의 장기 성공률 92–99%를 근거로 생활치 AP의 1차 치료로 VPT를 권고함.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **New 8-category diagnostic taxonomy**: Integrates pulp vitality status, percussion test result, and radiographic PDL/lesion finding into a 4×2 matrix yielding 8 distinct diagnoses (NP+NPDL, P+AAP, P+SAP, PN+NPDL, PN+AAP, PN+SAP and intermediate combinations).
2. **AP in vital teeth (ALEO)**: Emphasizes that AP can occur in vital, inflamed pulps — a pathway distinct from conventional necrosis-first model; calls for a paradigm shift in classification and treatment.
3. **Recommends retiring "reversible/irreversible pulpitis" terminology**: Proposes replacement with more accurate descriptors reflecting pulp's healing potential.
4. **VPT as first-line for vital teeth with AP**: Cites HTA evidence and long-term cohort data (92% success for pulpitis+AP; 99% survival overall) supporting VPT (DPC, mini/full pulpotomy) over immediate RCT.
5. **Global AP epidemiology**: Summarizes two meta-analyses finding ~52% global prevalence in adults; 16–86% range across populations.
6. **Systemic associations**: Reviews links between AP and cardiovascular disease, diabetes mellitus (higher HbA1c, larger lesions), adverse pregnancy outcomes, autoimmune disorders, and smoking.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Narrative review design; no systematic search protocol or PRISMA.
- Reviews: nomenclature/classification (WHO, Nair histopathological), bacterial pathogenesis and biofilms, cellular/molecular mechanisms (PMNs, T/B cells, cytokines IL-1/IL-6/TNF-α, MMPs), epidemiology (global/regional), systemic associations, diagnostic procedures (conventional and advanced imaging including CBCT), and treatment modalities.
- Diagnostic approach: history + clinical exam + pulp sensibility/percussion tests + periapical radiograph ± CBCT → integrated diagnosis (Figure 2).
- Treatment taxonomy: 4 clinical scenarios based on pulp status × AP presence.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Scenario | Recommended Treatment |
|---|---|
| Vital tooth, pulpitis, no AP | VPT (DPC, indirect PC, pulpotomy); RCT alternative |
| Vital tooth, pulpitis + AP | VPT preferred (HTA-supported); full pulpotomy for symptomatic AP |
| Nonvital, necrosis, no AP | RCT |
| Nonvital, necrosis + AP | RCT |

- VPT long-term success rate for pulpitis ± AP: **92% (pulpitis+AP)** and **99% overall survival** (10-year retrospective cohort, n=large).
- Global AP prevalence: ~**52%** in adults (meta-analysis, 2021).
- AP in endodontically treated teeth (Iran cross-sectional): **52%** prevalence; acceptable RCT → lower AP rate.
- CBCT: highest sensitivity/specificity for AP detection, particularly for subtle lesions and multirooted teeth.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Narrative review — no systematic search methodology; potential for selection bias.
- Pathogenesis of AP in vital teeth is described as "immature/ongoing" — limited evidence base.
- Causal links between AP and systemic diseases not firmly established.
- Future: longitudinal studies on VPT outcomes in AP cases; biomarker discovery for AP–systemic links; regenerative endodontics (stem cells, nanotechnology); standardized diagnostic taxonomy validation.

## 7. Glossary

- **AP (Apical Periodontitis, 치근단 주위염)**: Inflammatory condition of periapical tissues, primarily microbially driven.
- **ALEO (Asymptomatic Lesion of Endodontic Origin, 무증상 근관 기원 병소)**: AP without clinical symptoms; detected radiographically.
- **VPT (Vital Pulp Therapy, 생활치수요법)**: DPC, indirect pulp cap, mini/partial/full pulpotomy — preserves pulp vitality.
- **DPC (Direct Pulp Capping, 직접 치수 복조)**: Placement of bioactive material directly on exposed pulp.
- **NP (Normal Pulp, 정상 치수)**, **PN (Pulp Necrosis, 치수 괴사)**, **P (Pulpitis, 치수염)**
- **AAP (Asymptomatic Apical Periodontitis, 무증상 치근단 주위염)**, **SAP (Symptomatic Apical Periodontitis, 유증상 치근단 주위염)**
- **NPDL (Normal PDL, 정상 치주인대)**: No radiographic periapical change.
- **HTA (Health Technology Assessment, 보건기술평가)**: Evidence-based policy appraisal of treatment cost-effectiveness.
