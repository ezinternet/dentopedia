## One-line Summary
리튬 디실리케이트(Lithium Disilicate, LDS) 고정성 보철물 생존율 SR (Medicina 2023): 2006–2022 문헌 검색; LDS vs PFM 비교; LDS 단관 생존율 양호, 장경간 FPD는 한계.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Medicina 2023, 59, 95
- **DOI**: 10.3390/medicina59010095
- **Published**: 31 December 2022
- **Study type**: Systematic review (no meta-analysis)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Focused question: survival and complication rates of LDS complete-coverage fixed prostheses vs non-LDS (primarily PFM)
- Literature search: PubMed/MEDLINE, EMBASE, Google Scholar, ClinicalTrials.gov; January 2006–August 2022
- LDS single crowns: survival rates generally favorable; comparable to or exceeding PFM for single-unit restorations
- LDS bridges (FPDs): higher fracture risk for longer spans vs single crowns; limited evidence
- PFM remains reference standard; LDS advantageous in esthetics and avoidance of metal framework

## 3. Methodology
- **Inclusion**: Prospective clinical studies; LDS fixed bridges and crowns; natural teeth as abutments; English language
- **Exclusion**: In vitro studies; case reports; single-tooth restorations without comparison group
- **Outcomes**: Survival rate, complications (fracture, debonding, endodontic complications)

## 4. Key Results
- LDS single crowns: 5-year survival rates comparable to PFM (>90%)
- LDS FPDs: greater fracture risk than metal-ceramic FPDs for multi-unit spans
- Main complications: chipping/fracture of LDS material; secondary caries at margins
- No meta-analysis performed due to study heterogeneity

## 5. Limitations
- No meta-analysis; qualitative synthesis only
- Heterogeneous follow-up periods (range not specified)
- Variations in cementation protocols and tooth preparation designs not standardized
