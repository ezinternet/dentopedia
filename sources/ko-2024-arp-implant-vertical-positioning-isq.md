## Why Ingested

치조제보존술 (Alveolar Ridge Preservation, ARP) 후 임플란트의 수직적 위치(within vs. beyond ARP 경계)가 ISQ에 미치는 영향을 직접 비교한 임상 자료가 부족하여 인제스트. 기존 [[implants/isq/bavetta-2019-insertion-torque-isq-immediate-loading-extraction-socket]]이 즉시 발치 소켓에서의 삽입 토크-ISQ 관계를 다루는 반면, 본 연구는 ARP 치유 후 10주 시점의 ISQ를 결과 지표로 삼아 수직 위치 결정의 임상적 의미를 분석한다.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective case series (n=30 sites, 28 patients) comparing implants placed within vs. beyond ARP boundaries: beyond-ARP group showed higher insertion torque (33.33 vs. 17.08 Ncm) but equivalent ISQ at 10 weeks (80.66 vs. 76.44).

## 한줄요약

후향적 증례연구(30부위, 28명): ARP 경계 초과 임플란트는 삽입 토크가 높았으나(33.33 대 17.08 Ncm), 10주 ISQ는 두 군 간 유의한 차이 없음(80.66 대 76.44).

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Journal of Periodontal & Implant Science (JPIS)
- **Published online**: July 10, 2024 (print: Feb 2025, Vol 55(1):50–61)
- **Study design**: Retrospective case series (STROBE guidelines)
- **Institution**: Seoul National University School of Dentistry; Kim & Lee Dental Clinic, Seoul
- **Ethics**: Public Institutional Review Board (PIRB), Ministry of Health and Welfare Korea (P01-202009-21-004)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First study to directly compare ISQ outcomes based on vertical implant positioning relative to ARP graft boundary
- Demonstrates that extending osteotomy 3 mm into pristine bone significantly increases insertion torque but does NOT produce superior ISQ at 10 weeks
- Establishes that ARP sites (DBBM-C + collagen membrane) achieve clinically acceptable ISQ ≥70 even when implant is confined entirely within the graft
- Provides moderate positive correlation (r=0.415, p=0.023) between insertion torque and 10-week ISQ

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Patients**: 28 patients (15M/13F), 30 ARP sites, posterior area, Nov 2015–Jul 2019

**ARP protocol**: DBBM-C (Bio-Oss Collagen, Geistlich) + porcine collagen membrane (Bio-Gide), open membrane technique; healing ≥16 weeks before implant placement

**Groups**:
- Within-ARP (n=12 sites): osteotomy and implant entirely within ARP graft boundary
- Beyond-ARP (n=18 sites): osteotomy extended up to 3 mm beyond ARP into pristine bone

**Implants**: Astra Osseospeed and Dentium Superline; ≥5 mm diameter (80%+); 8–10 mm length (most); placed 0.5–1 mm subcrestal; 1-stage protocol

**Bone quality**: assessed by tactile sense (Lekholm & Zarb classification)

**Outcomes**:
- Insertion torque (Ncm) at time of surgery
- ISQ via RFA (Osstell) at 10 weeks post-placement
- Threshold for loading: ISQ ≥70

**Statistics**: Multiple linear regression, Pearson correlation; GraphPad Prism v10 + R v4.1.2

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Parameter | Within-ARP | Beyond-ARP |
|---|---|---|
| n (sites) | 12 | 18 |
| Insertion torque (Ncm) | 17.08 ± 11.17 | 33.33 ± 13.39 |
| ISQ at 10 weeks | 76.44 ± 6.62 | 80.66 ± 6.27 |

- **Insertion torque**: beyond-ARP significantly higher (β=14.14, p=0.018 implant level; p=0.027 patient level)
- **ISQ**: NO significant difference between groups at 10 weeks
- **Bone quality**: both groups predominantly type 3 (75–72.2%) and type 4 (25–27.8%); no type 1 or 2
- **Insertion torque–ISQ correlation**: r=0.415, R²=0.172, 95%CI 0.028–0.345, p=0.023 (moderate positive)
- **Failure**: 1 implant failure in within-ARP group (insertion torque 10 Ncm, ISQ 72.5 at 10w; failed at 2 years after loading)
- **Survival**: 29/30 implants survived through prosthetic delivery

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (n=30 sites, retrospective, single-center private clinic)
- No randomization; surgeon made real-time decision on implant positioning
- ISQ measured at only one timepoint (10 weeks); no baseline or serial measurements
- No radiographic bone-level data reported
- Bone quality by tactile sense is subjective (no CBCT-based objective density)
- No long-term follow-up beyond prosthetic delivery for most cases
- ARP performed only in periodontally compromised sites; results may not generalize to other extraction indications

## 6. Related Work

- Lekholm & Zarb bone quality classification (Type 1–4) used for bone density assessment
- Bio-Oss Collagen (DBBM-C) + Bio-Gide ARP protocol consistent with published SR/MA on ARP in compromised sites
- ISQ ≥70 loading threshold used (matches commonly reported clinical decision point)
- Bavetta 2019 (ISQ–insertion torque in extraction sockets): corroborates moderate correlation observed here

## 7. Glossary

- **ARP (Alveolar Ridge Preservation, 치조제보존술)**: Socket grafting procedure at extraction to minimize ridge resorption
- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient, 임플란트 안정성 지수)**: RFA-derived score 1–100 indicating implant stability; ≥70 = adequate for loading in this study
- **DBBM-C**: Deproteinized bovine bone mineral with collagen (Bio-Oss Collagen); barrier membrane = Bio-Gide (porcine collagen)
- **Within-ARP**: Implant apex and entire body within graft boundary
- **Beyond-ARP**: Implant apex extends ≤3 mm beyond graft boundary into pristine bone
- **Insertion torque (삽입 토크)**: Resistance encountered during implant placement; reflects primary stability
- **RFA (Resonance Frequency Analysis, 공명주파수분석)**: Non-invasive measurement of implant stability via Osstell device
