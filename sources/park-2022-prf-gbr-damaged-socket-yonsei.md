## Why Ingested

연세대 치주과학교실(Research Institute for Periodontal Regeneration) 팀의 PRF vs GBR 직접 비교 동물 실험 — qRT-PCR(분자) + micro-CT + 조직학의 3중 분석으로 sticky bone 기법의 전임상 근거를 제공. [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/alavi-2024-regenerative-grafts-socket-preservation-sr-ma]]의 L-PRF SR 결과에 대한 기초과학 보완 역할.

## One-line Summary

Beagle dog 2-wall defect model: sticky bone (PRF + DPBM) ± solid-PRF membrane performs comparably to GBR (collagen membrane + DPBM) in alveolar ridge area and new bone formation at 8 weeks, though PRF modestly upregulates osteocalcin, VEGF, and IL-6.

## 한줄요약

비글견 2벽성 결손 모델에서 sticky bone(PRF+DPBM) ± solid-PRF 멤브레인은 8주 신생골 형성 면적에서 GBR(콜라겐 멤브레인+DPBM)과 유의한 차이가 없었으나, PRF는 osteocalcin·VEGF·IL-6를 소폭 상향 조절하였다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First direct head-to-head comparison of sticky bone (liquid PRF + DPBM) versus GBR (DPBM + collagen membrane) in a surgically created 2-wall alveolar defect model in beagle dogs.
- Demonstrates that PRF-based approaches achieve comparable ridge area augmentation and graft consolidation to GBR at 8 weeks.
- Provides molecular evidence (qRT-PCR) that PRF modestly modulates bone turnover (osteocalcin, calcitonin receptor) and angiogenesis/inflammation markers (VEGF, IL-6) compared to GBR.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Animal model**: Beagle dogs; surgically created 2-wall alveolar defects
- **Three treatment groups**:
  - (i) Sticky bone (SB): liquid PRF + deproteinized porcine bone mineral (DPBM)
  - (ii) SB + solid-PRF compressed membrane covering
  - (iii) GBR: DPBM + collagen membrane (positive control)
- **Endpoints**:
  - 1 week: qRT-PCR for osteoblast/osteoclast markers (osteocalcin, calcitonin receptor), angiogenesis (VEGF), inflammation (IL-6)
  - 8 weeks: micro-CT (ridge area, bone volume) + histology (new bone formation, graft consolidation)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Micro-CT & histology**: All three groups showed increased alveolar ridge area; no statistically significant differences between groups in ridge area or new bone formation at defect site.
- **qRT-PCR (1-week)**: PRF groups showed moderate increases in osteocalcin and calcitonin receptor (bone turnover markers) vs GBR; VEGF and IL-6 were modestly elevated in PRF groups (angiogenesis + inflammation signals).
- **Bottom line**: PRF-based sticky bone approaches are non-inferior to established GBR for DPBM consolidation in 2-wall defects.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract-only; sample size per group and statistical power not retrievable.
- Animal model (beagle dog 2-wall defect) may not fully translate to human clinical settings.
- Follow-up limited to 8 weeks; longer-term remodeling not assessed.
- Does not address patient-relevant clinical endpoints (implant survival, prosthetic success).

## 6. Related Work

- Alavi 2024 SR+MA on regenerative grafts in socket preservation (L-PRF evidence review) — this paper provides mechanistic animal data supporting PRF use.
- GBR with collagen membranes and DPBM — established ridge preservation technique serving as the gold-standard comparator.

## 7. Glossary

- **PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin)**: Autologous platelet concentrate containing growth factors (PDGF, TGF-β, VEGF) in a fibrin matrix; prepared by centrifugation of autologous blood.
- **Sticky bone**: Mixture of liquid PRF (i-PRF) with particulate bone graft, forming an injectable/moldable composite.
- **DPBM (Deproteinized Porcine Bone Mineral)**: Xenograft particulate; structurally similar to DBBM (bovine) but porcine origin.
- **GBR (Guided Bone Regeneration)**: Barrier membrane technique to exclude non-osteogenic cells and allow bone fill of a defect.
- **qRT-PCR**: Quantitative real-time PCR; used here to measure gene expression of bone and vascular markers at 1 week.
- **2-wall defect**: Surgically created alveolar defect with two bony walls remaining; models "damaged" extraction socket scenarios.
