## Why Ingested

위축 상악 구치부에서 "short implant(graftless) vs long implant + sinus graft" 의사결정 근거를 보강하기 위해 인제스트. 기존 [[sinus-lift/lateral/toledano-2022-short-versus-standard-implants-sinus]] 및 [[implants/zhang-2024-short-vs-long-implants-sinus]] 와 짝을 이뤄, short implant가 생존율·변연골흡수에서 비열등함을 더 큰 RCT 풀(22 RCT)로 재확인.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review + meta-analysis (22 RCTs, 667 patients, 1595 implants) finding no significant difference in implant survival or marginal bone resorption between short implants (graftless) and long implants with sinus graft in the posterior atrophic maxilla.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰+메타분석(22 RCT, 환자 667명, 임플란트 1595개) — 위축 상악 구치부에서 short implant(무이식)와 long implant+상악동이식 간 생존율·변연골흡수에 유의차 없음; short implant가 적절한 대안.

## 1. Document Information
- J Indian Prosthodont Soc 2021;21(1):28–. 
- DOI: 10.4103/jips.jips_400_20
- Meta-analysis of RCTs only.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Large RCT pool (22 trials) for the graftless-short vs grafted-long question.
- Patient-level and implant-level survival both reported.
- Quantifies complication trade-off direction (biological vs prosthetic).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- SR+MA restricted to RCTs; 667 patients, 1595 implants (short:767, long:835).
- Outcomes: implant survival rate (patient + implant level), marginal bone resorption, biological and prosthetic complications.
- Random-effects meta-analysis; heterogeneity reported (I²).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Survival, patient level: RR 1.01 (95% CI 0.52–2.0), P=0.87, I²=0%.
- Survival, implant level: RR 1.09 (95% CI 0.6–2.0), P=0.7, I²=0%.
- Marginal bone resorption: MD 0.16 (95% CI −0.23 to −0.08), P=0.00, I²=74.83%.
- Biological complications: marginally higher for long implants (RR 0.48, 95% CI 0.23–0.8, P=0.13).
- Prosthetic complications: marginally higher for short implants (RR 1.56, 95% CI 0.85–3.15, P=0.43).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- High heterogeneity for marginal bone resorption (I²≈75%).
- Complication comparisons not statistically significant (wide CIs).
- Follow-up durations vary across included RCTs.

## 6. Related Work
- Short-implant vs sinus-graft RCT literature; Esposito et al. trials.

## 7. Glossary
- Graftless option: short implant placed without sinus augmentation.
- RR: risk ratio; MD: mean difference.
