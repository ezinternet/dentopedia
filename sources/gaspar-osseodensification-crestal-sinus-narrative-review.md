## One-line Summary
Narrative review of osseodensification for crestal maxillary sinus elevation: mechanism, clinical evidence, advantages over osteotome; OD achieves higher primary stability and reduced membrane perforation risk in RBH 3–8mm cases.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Implant Dentistry and Related Research (CID); doi: 10.1111/cid.13399
- **Study type**: Narrative review

## 2. Key Contributions
- Comprehensive review of OD technique for CSFE: mechanism, instrumentation, evidence
- Compares OD vs osteotome: higher ISQ, lower perforation risk, lower post-op morbidity with OD
- Reviews clinical evidence for RBH 3–8mm cases
- Identifies OD as preferred CSFE technique based on available evidence
