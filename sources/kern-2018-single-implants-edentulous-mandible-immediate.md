단일 정중부 하악 임플란트의 즉시하중 안전성을 처음으로 RCT로 검증. 즉시식립/즉시하중 의사결정 근거를 보강하며 immediate loading의 적응증 한정 시나리오를 제시 — early-failure clustering 측면에서 [[implants/mohseni-2024-clinical-outcomes-zirconia-implants]]의 조기실패 패턴과 연결.

## One-line Summary
Multicenter RCT (24 mo) — immediate loading of a single median mandibular implant for overdenture retention could NOT be proven noninferior to delayed loading: 9 early failures in the immediate group vs 1 in the delayed group.

## 한줄요약
다기관 RCT(24개월): 하악 정중부 단일 임플란트의 오버덴처 즉시하중은 지연하중에 대해 비열등성이 입증되지 않음 — 즉시군 조기실패 9개 vs 지연군 1개.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Dental Research 2018;97(2):163–170. German multicenter (Kiel et al.). DRKS registered. 24-mo RCT.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First RCT on loading protocol for a single mandibular midline implant retaining a complete denture.
- Immediate loading failed the noninferiority test due to clustered early failures.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Multicenter RCT; immediate loading vs delayed (second-stage) loading. Outcomes at 1, 4, 12, 24 mo after loading.
- Primary: implant survival (noninferiority). Secondary: prosthetic complications, maintenance.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 9 implants failed in the immediate loading group, all within the first 3 months of loading.
- 1 implant failed in the delayed group (before loading).
- Noninferiority of immediate loading survival was NOT confirmed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 24-month horizon; specific indication (single midline mandibular implant + overdenture). Early failure mechanism (insufficient primary stability under immediate load) needs mitigation before recommending.

## 6. Related Work
- Gallucci 2014 loading consensus; single mandibular implant overdenture series.

## 7. Glossary
- Immediate loading: functional loading at/near placement. Delayed loading: loading after a healing/second-stage phase.
