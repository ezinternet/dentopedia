## One-line Summary
SR+MA (25 studies, 4017 implants) — zirconia implants 10-year cumulative survival 95.1%, low short-term MBL (0.63–2.06 mm); two-piece and drill-prepared implants survived worse.

## 한줄요약
체계적문헌고찰+메타분석(25편·임플란트 4017개): 지르코니아 임플란트 10년 누적생존율 95.1%, 단기 변연골소실 낮음; 2-piece·드릴가공형은 생존율 낮음.

## 1. Document Information
- Clinical Oral Investigations 2024;28:15 (online 23 Dec 2023). PROSPERO CRD42022342055.
- Search updated June 2023, three databases + hand search.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Updated, larger evidence base (25 studies, 4017 implants, 2083 patients) vs prior zirconia SRs.
- 10-year cumulative survival rate (CSR) 95.1%.
- Identifies survival-reducing factors: coronal drilling preparation, two-piece design, discontinued products, narrow diameter (fracture).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Systematic review + meta-analysis of clinical studies on zirconia implants.
- CSR computation; MBL meta-analysis across follow-up windows; meta-regression MBL vs follow-up time.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- 172/4017 implants failed at mean 12.0 ± 16.1 months; 47 early failures; 26 fractures (mostly narrow-diameter).
- 10-year CSR 95.1%.
- Drill-prepared coronal part → lower survival (p<0.001); two-piece < one-piece (p=0.017); discontinued products lower (p<0.001); maxilla vs mandible NS (p=0.637).
- Mean MBL 0.632–2.060 mm up to 132 months; +0.005 mm per additional month.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Few studies >60 months; heterogeneity; many single-arm series; product heterogeneity (incl. discontinued systems).

## 6. Related Work
- Pieralli 2017 SR; titanium implant survival benchmarks.

## 7. Glossary
- CSR: cumulative survival rate. MBL: marginal bone loss.
