## Why Ingested

기존 [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/hamilton-2023-type1a-immediate-implant-immediate-loading-esthetic-sr-ma]]의 Type 1A 즉시식립·즉시임시수복 심미 결과를, 1차안정성(ITV/ISQ) 임계값을 기준으로 즉시 vs 지연식립을 직접 대조한 단일기관 retrospective cohort로 보강한다. 즉시식립군이 식립시 1차안정성은 유의하게 낮아도(ISQ 63.1 vs 71.5) 충분한 1차안정성(ITV ≥20 N·cm, ISQ ≥50) 충족 시 12개월 성공률·골유착이 동등하고 심미·환자만족이 더 우수하다는 결과로, [[immediate-implant/esthetic-soft-tissue/samet-2023-immediate-placement-loading-esthetic-zone]]의 즉시식립 적응증 논의를 1차안정성 관점에서 contextualize.

## One-line Summary

Single-center retrospective cohort (n=120; 60 immediate vs 60 delayed, single maxillary anterior teeth): despite lower primary stability at placement (ISQ 63.1 vs 71.5, P<.001), immediate placement achieved equivalent 12-month success (98.3% vs 96.7%) and final ISQ (P=.589) once primary-stability thresholds (ITV ≥20 N·cm, ISQ ≥50) were met, with less MBL (0.48 vs 0.85 mm) and higher PES (11.47 vs 10.05).

## 한줄요약

단일기관 후향 코호트(n=120; 즉시 60 vs 지연 60, 단일 상악 전치): 식립 시 1차안정성은 즉시군이 더 낮았지만(ISQ 63.1 vs 71.5), ITV ≥20 N·cm·ISQ ≥50 임계값을 충족하면 12개월 성공률(98.3% vs 96.7%)과 최종 ISQ는 동등했고, 변연골소실은 더 적고(0.48 vs 0.85 mm) PES는 더 높았다(11.47 vs 10.05).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Directly contrasts immediate vs delayed implant placement in single maxillary anterior teeth (incisor/canine, n=120), framing the comparison around **primary stability** (ITV + ISQ) as the determinant of success.
- Demonstrates that **lower primary stability at placement does not compromise 12-month outcomes** when minimum thresholds (ITV ≥20 N·cm, ISQ ≥50) are met — the immediate vs delayed ISQ gap closes by 12 months.
- Quantifies an **esthetic/MBL advantage for immediate placement** (lower MBL, higher PES, higher patient satisfaction) attributed to reduced post-extraction remodeling + immediate provisionalization supporting the soft-tissue envelope.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Retrospective cohort, Wuhan University Stomatological Hospital, Jan 2022–Dec 2024. No a priori sample size calculation; consecutive eligible patients.
- **Groups**: Observation (immediate placement + immediate provisional, n=60) vs Control (delayed placement after 6-mo socket preservation, n=60).
- **Immediate protocol**: Atraumatic extraction, palatal-initiated osteotomy, WeGo threaded implant placed crestal or 1.0–3.0 mm subcrestal; ≥1.5 mm jumping gap grafted with Bio-Oss + Bio-Gide collagen membrane; immediate provisional; definitive restoration at 6 mo.
- **Delayed protocol**: Atraumatic extraction → socket preservation (Bio-Oss + collagen membrane) → 6-mo healing → implant + immediate provisional → definitive at 6 mo.
- **Inclusion**: ages 18–60, single maxillary anterior loss, **ITV ≥20 N·cm and ISQ ≥50**, intact labial plate, thick gingival biotype (>1 mm), good hygiene, stable occlusion, no bruxism.
- **Outcomes**: primary/final ITV & ISQ (Osstell IDx, mean of mesial+distal), implant success/survival, MBL (parallel-technique periapical radiographs, mesial+distal mean), peri-implant soft-tissue indices, PES (7-item, 0–14, mean of 2 examiners), VAS patient satisfaction, adverse events. Follow-up at placement and 12 months.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Success (12 mo)**: 98.33% (59/60) immediate vs 96.67% (58/60) delayed, P=.559. 3 failures (1 immediate, 2 delayed), all from peri-implantitis with conservative-therapy failure → removed.
- **Primary stability (placement)**: ITV 31.53 vs 36.27 N·cm (P=.003); ISQ 63.10 vs 71.46 (P<.001) — immediate significantly lower.
- **Final stability (12 mo)**: ITV 36.49 vs 37.15 N·cm (P=.588); ISQ 69.74 vs 70.62 (P=.589) — no difference (gap resolved).
- **MBL (12 mo)**: 0.48 ± 0.07 vs 0.85 ± 0.18 mm (P<.001), favoring immediate (Δ0.37 mm).
- **PES (12 mo)**: 11.47 ± 0.96 vs 10.05 ± 1.36 (P<.001). Significant per-item: labial mucosa curvature (P=.006), labial mucosa height (P=.032), soft-tissue texture (P<.001); papillae/color/root convexity NS.
- **Soft-tissue indices**: plaque, recession, BoP, PD all NS.
- **Patient satisfaction (VAS)**: higher for immediate in keratinized mucosa height (P=.024), overall aesthetics (P=.004), treatment-duration perception (P<.001), surgical-procedure-count perception (P<.001), total (54.47 vs 46.24, P<.001); color/masticatory function NS.
- **Discussion threshold note**: text cites ITV ≥30 N·cm as the adequate primary-stability target for osseointegration despite the ≥20 N·cm inclusion floor.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Applicable only to **selected cases with adequate intraoperative primary stability** — not generalizable to all immediate cases.
- Single-center, strict inclusion (intact labial plate, thick biotype, good hygiene) → limited external validity.
- Modest sample, short 12-month follow-up, no a priori power calculation.
- Inter-examiner reliability for PES not formally assessed (potential measurement variability).
- Authors call for prospective multicenter trials with larger cohorts, longer follow-up, standardized esthetic assessment, and multivariable analyses.

## 6. Related Work

- Patel et al SR (2014–2022): pooled survival 97.4% immediate vs 97.5% delayed.
- Granić et al: ISQ ~61.4 immediate vs 64.2 healed at placement, gap resolving over time.
- Prior PES literature for immediate placement typically 10.3–11.3 on the 14-point scale (this cohort 11.47).

## 7. Glossary

- **ITV (insertion torque value)**: peak torque at implant seating (N·cm); a primary-stability indicator.
- **ISQ (implant stability quotient)**: resonance-frequency-analysis stability index (1–100; Osstell).
- **MBL (marginal bone loss)**: crestal bone change at implant neck on radiographs.
- **PES (Pink Esthetic Score)**: 7-item peri-implant soft-tissue esthetic index (0–14).
- **Type 1 / immediate placement**: implant inserted into fresh extraction socket at time of extraction.
