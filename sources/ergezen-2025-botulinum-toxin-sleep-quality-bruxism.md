## Why Ingested

수면 브럭시즘(SB)과 각성 브럭시즘(AB)을 **동시에 진단받은** 환자에서 BoNT-A가 수면 질에 미치는 효과를 평가한 드문 연구. 기존 논문들은 SB 또는 AB 중 하나만 대상으로 하는 경향. [[botulinum-toxin/hira-2026-masseter-botulinum-bruxism-severity-pain-sleep]]과 함께 "BoNT-A → 수면 질(PSQI) 개선" 임상 근거를 구성하는 핵심 쌍.

## One-line Summary

Prospective observational study (n=40 women with awake + sleep bruxism) of standardized BTX-A injection (30U masseter + 20U temporalis) showed good sleep quality (PSQI ≤5) rising from 17.5% to 60% post-treatment, with significant improvements in all PSQI subscales. Abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

각성 및 수면 브럭시즘을 동시 진단받은 여성 40명을 대상으로 표준화 BTX-A 주사(교근 30U + 측두근 20U) 후 PSQI 기준 수면의 질 양호 비율이 17.5%→60%로 증가하고 모든 수면 하위척도가 유의하게 개선된 전향 관찰 연구. 초록만 수집.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Rare study including both **awake bruxism (AB) and sleep bruxism (SB)** in the same cohort — most BTX-A studies focus on one type
- Standardized injection protocol: 30U masseter + 20U temporalis (clearly reported)
- PSQI used as primary systematic sleep outcome
- Good sleep quality rate (PSQI ≤5) doubled post-treatment: 17.5% → 60%
- All PSQI subscales (latency, efficiency, duration, disturbance, daytime function) significantly improved
- Results interpreted cautiously: no control group; placebo effect contribution unknown

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Prospective observational study
- **Sample**: n=40 female patients; diagnosed with probable sleep bruxism AND awake bruxism
- **Intervention**: BTX-A injection (standardized): 30U bilateral masseter + 20U bilateral temporalis
- **Primary outcome**: PSQI (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index) pre- and post-treatment
- **Subgroup analyses**: PSQI subscales (subjective quality, latency, duration, efficiency, disturbance, daytime function)
- **Statistics**: Pre- vs post-treatment comparisons; p-values reported per subscale

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract only — timing of post-treatment assessment not clear (1mo? 3mo? 6mo?)
- All-female cohort (n=40) — limits generalizability to males
- No control / sham injection group
- "Probable" bruxism diagnosis (not polysomnography-confirmed)
- Confounders: stress, sleep hygiene, concurrent treatments not controlled
- Future: RCT with sham-injection control; polysomnography-confirmed SB diagnosis; male inclusion

## 7. Glossary

- **PSQI** (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index, 피츠버그수면질지수): validated 19-item self-report questionnaire; global score ≤5 = good sleep quality
- **Awake bruxism (AB)** (각성 브럭시즘): jaw-clenching/grinding during wakefulness (distinct from sleep bruxism)
- **Sleep bruxism (SB)** (수면 브럭시즘): repetitive jaw muscle activity during sleep
- **Probable bruxism**: diagnosis by clinical criteria and questionnaire without polysomnographic confirmation
