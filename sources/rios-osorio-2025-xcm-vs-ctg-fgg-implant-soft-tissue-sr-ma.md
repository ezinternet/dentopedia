## Why Ingested

기존 [[implants/soft-tissue/dadlani-2024-efficacy-xenogeneic-collagen-matrices-augmenting]]은 9편 연구(6편 MA)로 XCM vs 자가이식 전체를 분석했으나, 비교가교(non-crosslinked) vs 교가교(crosslinked) XCM 아형을 구분하지 않았다. 본 SR+MA(Ríos-Osorio 2025)는 17 RCT(14편 MA)를 포함하고, XCM* (비가교) vs VCMX (가교형) 서브그룹 분석을 최초로 통합해 VCMX가 CTG와 동등하다는 새 임상 시사점을 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 17 RCTs: non-crosslinked XCM inferior to CTG for mucosal thickness gain (MD 0.27 mm, P=0.01) but crosslinked XCM (VCMX) equals CTG; FGG superior to XCM* for keratinized mucosa width (MD 1.47 mm, P=0.0006) at implant sites.

## 한줄요약

17편 RCT SR+MA: 임플란트 부위 연조직 증대에서 비가교 XCM은 CTG 대비 점막두께 열위(MD 0.27 mm)이나 가교형 VCMX는 CTG와 동등; FGG는 XCM* 대비 각화점막폭 1.47 mm 우위.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Subgroup analysis by XCM type**: First SR+MA to separately analyse non-crosslinked XCM (XCM*) and crosslinked VCMX vs CTG, revealing that VCMX (e.g., Geistlich Fibro-Gide) performs equivalently to CTG for mucosal thickness augmentation.
2. **Largest RCT pool**: 17 RCTs in qualitative synthesis (12 XCM vs CTG; 5 XCM vs FGG); 14 in meta-analysis — larger than any prior SR on this question.
3. **Clinical decision framework**: Non-crosslinked XCM is still inferior for MT increase; crosslinked XCM may be clinically acceptable as a morbidity-sparing alternative to CTG.
4. **KMW gap confirmed**: FGG remains significantly superior to XCM* for keratinized mucosa width gain at implant sites.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **PICO**: Systemically healthy patients with peri-implant mucosal deficiencies (MT <2 mm and/or KMW <2 mm) undergoing soft tissue augmentation; XCM vs CTG (MT) or XCM* vs FGG (KMW); follow-up ≥3 months.
- **Inclusion**: RCTs only, parallel-group design with autogenous graft control.
- **Risk of bias**: Cochrane RoB 2 tool. XCM vs CTG studies — moderate RoB; XCM* vs FGG studies — low RoB.
- **Statistical model**: Random-effects meta-analysis; pooled mean difference (MD) with 95% CI.
- **Subgroups**: XCM* vs CTG; VCMX vs CTG; XCM* vs FGG.
- **Outcomes**: Primary — mucosal thickness (MT) gain, keratinized mucosa width (KMW) gain. Secondary — aesthetic outcomes, patient-reported outcomes, peri-implant health indices.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### Mucosal Thickness (MT) — XCM vs CTG

| Comparison | n RCTs | Pooled MD (mm) | 95% CI | P-value | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| XCM* vs CTG | 9 | −0.27 | −0.49 to −0.05 | 0.01 | CTG significantly superior |
| VCMX vs CTG | subset | −0.02 | −0.17 to +0.22 | 0.83 | No significant difference |

### Keratinized Mucosa Width (KMW) — XCM* vs FGG

| Comparison | n RCTs | Pooled MD (mm) | 95% CI | P-value | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| XCM* vs FGG | 5 | −1.47 | −2.30 to −0.63 | 0.0006 | FGG significantly superior |

### Summary conclusions
- **Non-crosslinked XCM (XCM*)** < CTG for MT augmentation at implant buccal sites.
- **Crosslinked XCM (VCMX)** = CTG for MT augmentation — no statistically significant difference.
- **FGG** > XCM* for KMW augmentation — FGG maintains a clinically meaningful advantage (~1.5 mm).
- Risk of bias: XCM vs CTG studies moderate (mostly single-blinded, heterogeneous follow-up); XCM* vs FGG studies low.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Heterogeneity in surgical techniques (tunnel vs flap), XCM products (multiple brands), and follow-up periods (3–24+ months).
- Moderate RoB in the majority of included studies limits certainty of MT conclusions.
- Limited long-term data (>12 months) for VCMX specifically.
- Small number of studies for KMW meta-analysis (n=5).
- No data on implant survival, marginal bone level, or peri-implantitis incidence as function of augmentation type.
- Future RCTs should standardize VCMX products, follow-up ≥12 months, and report peri-implant health outcomes longitudinally.

## 7. Glossary

- **XCM (Xenogeneic Collagen Matrix, 이종 콜라겐 매트릭스)**: Porcine-derived collagen scaffold used as autograft substitute for soft tissue augmentation.
- **XCM* (Non-crosslinked XCM, 비가교 이종 콜라겐 매트릭스)**: Standard (non-chemically crosslinked) porcine collagen matrix (e.g., Geistlich Mucograft).
- **VCMX (Crosslinked XCM, 가교형 이종 콜라겐 매트릭스)**: Chemically crosslinked porcine collagen matrix (e.g., Geistlich Fibro-Gide), designed for volumetric stability.
- **CTG (Connective Tissue Graft, 결합조직이식)**: Subepithelial autogenous graft harvested from palatal mucosa; gold standard for MT augmentation.
- **FGG (Free Gingival Graft, 유리치은이식)**: Epithelialized autogenous graft from palate; gold standard for KMW augmentation.
- **MT (Mucosal Thickness, 점막두께)**: Buccal peri-implant mucosal thickness; adequate ≥2 mm.
- **KMW (Keratinized Mucosa Width, 각화점막폭)**: Width of keratinized tissue around implant; adequate ≥2 mm.
- **MD (Mean Difference, 평균차이)**: Pooled continuous outcome measure in meta-analysis.
- **IPD (Inflammatory Peri-implant Disease, 임플란트 주위 염증성 질환)**: Includes peri-implant mucositis and peri-implantitis.
- **RoB (Risk of Bias, 비뚤림 위험)**: Quality assessment using Cochrane RoB 2 tool.
