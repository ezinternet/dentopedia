[[overviews/short-implant-vs-sinus-augmentation-decision]] thesis의 최상위 근거(umbrella review)로 인제스트. 개별 SR/MA들([[sinus-lift/lateral/chaware-2021-short-vs-long-implant-sinus-graft-sr-ma]], [[implants/zhang-2024-short-vs-long-implants-sinus]])을 60개 SR 수준에서 통합해 "생존 동등, MBL·생물학적 합병증은 short 우위" 결론을 high-confidence로 확정.

## One-line Summary

Umbrella review of 60 systematic reviews finding no significant differences in survival, implant failure, or prosthetic complications between short and standard implants, while short implants showed less marginal bone loss and fewer biological complications.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰 60편 umbrella review — short vs standard 임플란트의 생존·실패·보철 합병증 유의차 없음, short가 MBL·생물학적 합병증 적음; high-confidence 근거로 short는 침습 적은 유효 대안.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Top-of-pyramid evidence (umbrella review of SRs) for the short-vs-standard question.
- Confidence-graded synthesis (high-confidence SRs drive the conclusion).
- Confirms the recurring split: survival equal, MBL/biological complications favor short.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Umbrella review; 790 records retrieved → 60 systematic reviews included.
- Outcomes: survival rate, implant failure, prosthetic complications, marginal bone loss, biological complications.
- Confidence of included SRs assessed; conclusion based on high-confidence reviews.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Survival, implant failure, prosthetic complications: no significant difference (short vs standard).
- Marginal bone loss: less with short implants.
- Biological complications: fewer with short implants.
- Conclusion: short implants provide comparable outcomes; viable less-invasive alternative for reduced vertical bone height.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Umbrella reviews inherit overlap and bias of constituent SRs.
- Heterogeneous definitions of "short" across included reviews.
- Edentulous/partially-edentulous mixing across SRs.

## 6. Related Work
- Component SRs on short vs standard implants (Toledano, Chaware, etc.).

## 7. Glossary
- Umbrella review: systematic review of systematic reviews.
- Standard implant: conventional-length (>6–8 mm) implant.
