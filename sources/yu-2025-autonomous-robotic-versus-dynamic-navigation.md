## Why Ingested

기존 [[digital-workflow/saeed-2023-robotic-artificial-intelligence-implant-dentistry]] narrative review가 robotic CAIS의 정량 정확도를 직접 비교하지 못했음. 본 prospective comparative study는 r-CAIS vs d-CAIS의 deviation/시간을 head-to-head로 제공해 robotic 정확도 근거를 보강. 자매 RCT [[digital-workflow/wei-2025-autonomous-robotic-surgery-dynamic-navigation]]와 짝.

## One-line Summary

Prospective comparative study (83 patients, 135 implants) showing autonomous robotic CAIS halves angular/platform/apex deviation vs dynamic navigation and is faster.

## 한줄요약

전향 비교연구(환자 83명·임플란트 135개): 자율로봇 임플란트수술(r-CAIS)이 동적내비게이션(d-CAIS)보다 각도·플랫폼·첨부 편차를 절반으로 줄이고 시술시간도 단축.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Clinical Periodontology 2025;52(2):280–288. First published 2 Dec 2024.
- Funding: Sichuan Province Science & Technology Support Program; National Natural Science Foundation of China (82271015).
- West China School of Stomatology, Sichuan University.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Direct clinical head-to-head of autonomous robot (r-CAIS) vs dynamic navigation (d-CAIS).
- Robot roughly halved all three deviation metrics.
- Robot also reduced combined drilling + placement time despite robotic overhead.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Prospective comparative (non-randomized allocation by period/operator).
- 83 patients, 135 implants, Dec 2022–Mar 2024. r-CAIS: 43 patients/71 implants; d-CAIS: 40 patients/64 implants.
- Pre/post CBCT superimposition; linear (platform, apex) and angular deviations in 2D/3D. Surgical time logged by phase.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Angular: d-CAIS 3.61° ± 1.65° vs r-CAIS 1.62° ± 0.93° (p < 0.001).
- Platform: 1.12 ± 0.51 mm vs 0.50 ± 0.19 mm (p < 0.001).
- Apex: 1.36 ± 0.57 mm vs 0.58 ± 0.21 mm (p < 0.001).
- Drilling + placement time: 10.6 ± 3.8 vs 8.3 ± 3.4 min (p < 0.01); preparation time NS.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Non-randomized; selection/operator confounding. Single center. No osseointegration/MBL follow-up. Learning-curve effect not isolated.

## 6. Related Work
- Tahmaseb 2018 (CAIS accuracy meta-analysis); robotic CAIS first-in-human reports.

## 7. Glossary
- r-CAIS: robotic computer-assisted implant surgery. d-CAIS: dynamic CAIS (navigation).
