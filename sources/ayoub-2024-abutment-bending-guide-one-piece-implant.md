## 2. Key Contributions
- Novel ABVG concept: digitally-guided abutment bending for one-piece implants
- RealGuide.5 digital planning software integration
- NeoBiotech NEO NAVIGUIDE KIT: simultaneous surgical + bending guide
- Predictable and reproducible abutment angulation
- Reduces trial-and-error in one-piece implant abutment adjustment

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Case study. One-piece compressive implant placement with need for abutment bending. RealGuide.5 used for digital planning. ABVG fabricated alongside surgical placement guide. NeoBiotech NEO NAVIGUIDE KIT for guided surgery. Clinical verification of abutment angulation accuracy against digital plan.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- ABVG provided accurate, predictable abutment bending
- Path of insertion matched pre-designed crown angulation
- Digital workflow reduced intraoperative adjustment time
- Reproducible technique applicable to one-piece implant cases
- No complications reported

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Case-level evidence; limited generalizability. No control group. Single operator/center.

## 7. Glossary
- **One-piece implant**: Implant with integrated abutment (no separate abutment screw)
- **Sticky bone**: Autologous fibrin + allograft mixture with PRF
- **PRF**: Platelet-rich fibrin — autologous growth factor membrane
- **Piezosurgery**: Ultrasonic bone cutting with selective hard-tissue cutting
