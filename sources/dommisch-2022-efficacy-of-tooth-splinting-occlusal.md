## Why Ingested

Fan 2018 [[occlusion/fan-2018-occlusal-trauma-and-excessive-occlusal]]이 정의·논쟁을 정리했다면, 본 SR은 실제 개입(스플린팅·교합조정)이 치주염 환자의 치아 생존·임상지표를 바꾸는지 직접 검증한다. 동요치 스플린팅의 효용에 관한 임상결정 근거.

## One-line Summary
Systematic review finding that, on low-level evidence, tooth splinting does NOT improve survival of mobile teeth in advanced periodontitis, while occlusal adjustment of mobile/prematurely-contacting teeth may improve clinical attachment level (other parameters unclear).

## 한줄요약
SR — 낮은 근거수준에서 치아 스플린팅은 진행성 치주염의 동요치 생존을 개선하지 못하고, 동요·조기접촉 치아의 교합조정은 임상부착수준(CAL)을 개선할 수 있음(나머지 지표는 불명확).

## 1. Document Information
- J Clin Periodontol 2022;49(Suppl 24). Systematic Review. DOI 10.1111/jcpe.13563.
- Background work for EFP S3-level periodontitis treatment guideline.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Separates the evidence for tooth splinting (TS) vs occlusal adjustment (OA) on hard outcomes.
- Quantifies tooth-loss incidence with vs without splinting.
- Finds OA may yield CAL gain in mobile/premature-contact teeth.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 1515 publications screened → 51 full-text → included: 2 retrospective case series on TS (low risk of bias); 1 RCT + 2 prospective studies on OA (unclear risk of bias).
- Outcomes: tooth loss (TL), clinical attachment level (CAL), probing pocket depth (PPD), tooth mobility (TM), over ~2 years after non-surgical periodontal therapy.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Tooth splinting: 26/311 teeth lost with TS (weighted mean TL incidence 8.4%) vs 156/1541 teeth without TS (10.1%) over 2 years — no meaningful survival benefit.
- Occlusal adjustment: RCT indicated CAL gain for teeth with OA vs no OA.
- Effects of OA on TL, PPD, TM: heterogeneous/unclear.
- Overall evidence level: low.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Very few eligible studies; mostly non-randomized with bias concerns.
- Short follow-up (~2 years); no long-term survival data.
- Heterogeneous OA outcomes preclude pooling.

## 6. Related Work
- 2017 World Workshop occlusal trauma case definitions (Fan 2018).
- EFP S3 periodontitis treatment guideline.

## 7. Glossary
- Tooth splinting (TS): mechanically joining mobile teeth to distribute force.
- Occlusal adjustment (OA): selective grinding to remove premature/traumatic contacts.
- Secondary occlusal trauma: occlusal injury to a periodontitis-reduced periodontium.
