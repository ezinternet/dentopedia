## 2. Key Contributions
- Umbrella-level GIC longevity synthesis across dentitions.
- Quantifies weak underlying SR quality (AMSTAR-2/ROBIS).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Umbrella review. Databases: MedLine/PubMed, Web of Science, Scopus. 13 SRs (from 24 eligible). Follow-up 6 mo–6 yr. Quality: AMSTAR-2; bias: ROBIS.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
GIC types: RMGIC, compomers, low-/high-viscosity GIC. 7/13 SRs low risk of bias (ROBIS); no SR met all AMSTAR-2 criteria; none a priori designed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Underlying-review quality low; heterogeneous follow-up; need high-quality, a-priori-designed reviews and longer RCTs.

## 6. Related Work
GIC-vs-amalgam and GIC-vs-composite longevity SRs.

## 7. Glossary
- Umbrella review: review of systematic reviews.
- AMSTAR-2: SR methodological-quality tool.
- ROBIS: risk-of-bias-in-systematic-reviews tool.
