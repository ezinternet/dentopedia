## One-line Summary
J Adv Prosthodont 2015;7:85-92: 7 cleaning methods for saliva-contaminated zirconia — Ivoclean + air abrasion highest SBS; water alone insufficient; methanol and phosphoric acid intermediate.

## 1. Document Information
Journal: J Adv Prosthodont 2015;7:85-92
DOI: 10.4047/jap.2015.7.2.85
Authors: Kim DH et al., Kyungpook National University

## 2. Key Results
- 7 cleaning methods tested: no cleaning, water, methanol, 35% phosphoric acid, Ivoclean, air abrasion, air abrasion + Ivoclean
- Air abrasion alone or combined with Ivoclean: highest bond strength
- Ivoclean alone: significantly better than water, methanol, or phosphoric acid
- Water alone: minimal improvement over uncontaminated control
