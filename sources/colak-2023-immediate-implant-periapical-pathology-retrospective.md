## One-line Summary
Turkey 후향 연구 (BMC Oral Health 2023, n=69 환자 / 124 implants): 만성 근첨병변 (chronic periapical pathology) 발치 소켓에 즉시 식립 — 3개 그룹 비교: G1 즉시 식립, G2 즉시 + GBR, G3 sinus lift + 즉시. 생존율 비교 분석.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 만성 근첨병변 소켓 즉시 식립 생존율 평가.
- 3가지 식립 전략 비교 (즉시 vs +GBR vs +sinus lift).

## 3. Methodology
Retrospective; n=69 patients / 124 implants; 3 groups by augmentation strategy; statistical analysis (t-test, ANOVA, chi-square).

## 4. Key Results
[합의수준] Specific survival rates per group reported in source. Chronic periapical pathology + immediate placement remains a debated indication; this study contributes outcome data.

## 5. Limitations
- Single-center, retrospective
- Limited follow-up duration
