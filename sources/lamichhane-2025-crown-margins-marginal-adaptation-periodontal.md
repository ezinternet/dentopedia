## Why Ingested

Provides original clinical (cross-sectional) evidence for the failure-cascade thesis argued narratively in [[periodontics/srimaneepong-2022-fixed-prosthetic-restorations-periodontal-health]]: in a split-mouth design, FDP abutment teeth show significantly worse PI/GI/PD/CAL than matched non-abutment teeth. Also empirically tests (and complicates) the margin-position argument of [[periodontics/hajaj-2025-marginal-tooth-preparation-periodontal-health]] by finding margin location had no significant effect on abutment periodontal parameters in this cohort.

## One-line Summary

Analytical cross-sectional study (Nepal, n=95, split-mouth) — fixed-dental-prosthesis abutment teeth had significantly higher plaque index, gingival index, probing depth and clinical attachment loss than contralateral matched non-abutment teeth (all p<0.001); prosthesis duration and material affected PI/GI, but margin location showed no significant effect on abutment periodontal parameters.

## 한줄요약

분석적 단면연구(네팔, n=95, split-mouth): 고정성 보철 지대치는 대측 대조 비지대치보다 PI·GI·PD·CAL이 모두 유의하게 높음(p<0.001). 보철 사용기간·재료는 PI/GI에 영향을 줬으나, 변연 위치는 지대치 치주지표에 유의한 영향 없음.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Analytical cross-sectional study (BMC Oral Health; open access)
- **Authors**: Krishna Prasad Lamichhane, Amar Bhochhibhoya, Sirjana Dahal (Tribhuvan University Teaching Hospital, Maharajgunj Medical Campus, Kathmandu, Nepal)
- **Period**: Oct 2023–Sep 2024; IRC ref. 164(6–11)E2
- **Design**: split-mouth (abutment vs contralateral matched non-abutment); convenience sampling
- **Sample**: n=95 patients (minimum calculated 49 per group); aged 20–50, systemically healthy non-smokers, FDP (single crown or tooth-supported FPD) ≥3 months

## 2. Key Contributions

- Direct in-patient comparison of abutment vs non-abutment periodontal status using validated indices (Silness-Löe plaque index; Löe-Silness gingival index; PD; CAL), with Mann–Whitney U testing.
- Quantifies the periodontal cost of fixed prostheses on their own abutments in a South-Asian (Nepali) tertiary-care population — a setting with little prior data.
- Tests effect modifiers: prosthesis duration, prosthetic material, and margin location.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Single trained examiner (5y experience); air-dried visual + probe assessment of four surfaces per tooth.
- Margin location categorized relative to free gingival margin (supragingival / equigingival / subgingival).
- Descriptive stats (mean/median/SD/IQR/%) by data type; between-group differences by Mann–Whitney U.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Abutment teeth had significantly higher **PI (p<0.001)**, **GI (p<0.001)**, **PD (p<0.001)**, and **CAL (p<0.001)** than matched non-abutment teeth.
- **Prosthesis duration**: significant difference in PI (p=0.026) and CAL (p=0.037); no significant difference in GI or PD.
- **Prosthetic material**: significant difference in PI (p=0.004) and GI (p=0.036); no significant difference in PD or CAL.
- **Margin location**: no significant effect on GI, PI, PD, or CAL of abutment teeth (in this cohort).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Cross-sectional → association, not causation; convenience sampling; single center/single examiner.
- Narrow inclusion (20–50y, non-smokers, ≥3 months) limits generalizability; null margin-location finding may reflect cohort/measurement constraints rather than no effect.
- Authors recommend periodic periodontal monitoring, oral-hygiene education, and prosthetic quality control for FDP patients.

## 6. Related Work

- Empirical complement to narrative reviews on FDP–periodontal interaction (Srimaneepong 2022) and preparation design (Hajaj 2025).
- Builds on prior Nepali FDP/periodontium studies (Ranjit et al., used for sample-size estimation).

## 7. Glossary

- **Abutment tooth**: tooth that supports a crown or fixed partial denture.
- **PI / GI**: Silness–Löe plaque index; Löe–Silness gingival index (0–3 scales).
- **PD**: probing (pocket) depth.
- **CAL**: clinical attachment loss.
- **Split-mouth design**: within-patient comparison using contralateral matched teeth as control.
- **FDP / FPD**: fixed dental prosthesis / fixed partial denture.
