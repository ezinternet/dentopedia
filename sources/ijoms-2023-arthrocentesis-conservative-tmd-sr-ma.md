## One-line Summary
SR+MA of 7 RCTs (448 patients) comparing arthrocentesis vs. conservative management for painful TMD; arthrocentesis significantly improved MMO (+1.12 mm, p=0.001) with borderline pain benefit.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery 2023;52(8):889–896
- **DOI**: 10.1016/j.ijom.2022.10.008 (PII S0901-5027(22)00488-X)
- **Study type**: Systematic review and meta-analysis of RCTs

## 2. Key Contributions
- First SR+MA specifically comparing 관절천자 (arthrocentesis) to conservative management in RCT designs
- Quantifies MMO benefit: +1.12 mm (95% CI: 0.46–1.78, p=0.001) favoring arthrocentesis
- Pain reduction difference was statistically borderline — clinically modest
- Identifies 7 eligible RCTs totaling 448 patients
- Informs shared decision-making between conservative and procedural approaches

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Search: MEDLINE, Embase, Cochrane CENTRAL, Web of Science
- Inclusion: RCTs comparing arthrocentesis (with or without lavage) vs. conservative management (splint, physical therapy, medications)
- Primary outcomes: MMO (mm), pain (VAS/NRS)
- Random-effects meta-analysis
- Risk of bias: Cochrane RoB 2.0
- GRADE certainty assessment

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **MMO**: Weighted mean difference +1.12 mm (95% CI 0.46–1.78, p=0.001) favoring arthrocentesis
- **Pain (VAS)**: Statistically significant but borderline clinical relevance; heterogeneity I² moderate-to-high
- 7 RCTs included; 448 patients total
- Most studies: moderate risk of bias (blinding inherently limited for procedural arms)
- Conclusion: arthrocentesis modestly superior to conservative management; clinical significance is debatable

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small number of eligible RCTs (n=7); publication bias possible
- High procedural variability (number of lavages, injectate volume, anesthetic use)
- Short follow-up (most ≤6 months)
- No comparison with arthroscopy or open surgery
- Future trials should use DC/TMD subtype stratification

## 6. Related Work
- Zhang et al. (2024) arthrocentesis with injectable agents SR
- BMJ 2023 guideline recommends conservative-first approach
- Singh et al. (2024) Cochrane occlusal interventions review

## 7. Glossary
- **Arthrocentesis** (관절천자): Minimally invasive TMJ lavage via needle puncture of the upper joint compartment
- **Conservative management**: Non-invasive treatments — splints, physiotherapy, NSAIDs, behavioral modification
- **MMO** (하악 최대개구량): Maximum mouth opening; key functional outcome
- **WMD**: Weighted mean difference; meta-analytic effect size for continuous outcomes
- **Joint lavage**: Irrigation of the TMJ space to remove inflammatory mediators and adhesions
