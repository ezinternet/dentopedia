## Why Ingested

`clear-aligner` 발치·복잡/vs고정식 축에서 **치료기간** 쟁점 전담. 발치 케이스에서 FA가 더 짧다는 [[wiki/orthodontics/clear-aligner/jaber-2023-clear-aligners-complex-extraction-vs-fixed-sr]] 결과와 대비 — 경중등도 crowding에선 차이 없음을 보여 case-class 의존성을 드러냄. paywall, abstract-only.

## One-line Summary

SR (10 studies, 6 RCTs; GRADE low): in mild-to-moderate crowding, treatment duration with clear aligners is similar to fixed appliances; no meta-analysis (high inconsistency); duration also driven by patient/treatment factors.

## 한줄요약

SR(10편, RCT 6편; GRADE low): 경중등도 crowding에서 CA와 FA의 치료기간은 유사. 이질성 커서 MA 미시행. 기간은 환자·치료 요인에도 좌우.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Isolates treatment-duration outcome by case complexity (mild-moderate crowding).
- Shows duration parity in mild-moderate, contrasting with extraction-case FA advantage.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

9 databases to Jun 2023; RCTs + matched non-randomized; RoB 2 / ROBINS-I; GRADE. 3537 -> 10 included (6 RCTs); 9 non-extraction, 1 extraction. No MA (inconsistency).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Low-certainty evidence: no significant CA vs FA difference in duration for mild-to-moderate crowding.
- Duration influenced by patient and treatment-related factors beyond appliance type.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- High inconsistency precluded MA; severe cases under-studied. Abstract-only ingest.
