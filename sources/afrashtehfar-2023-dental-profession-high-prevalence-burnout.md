## Why Ingested

Adds the evidence-based peer-reviewer's lens on the dentist-burnout literature — this JEBDP commentary critically appraises and endorses Moro 2022's headline emotional-exhaustion finding (~28%), anchoring the appraisal layer of [[overviews/dentist-burnout-mental-health-overview]].

## One-line Summary
Critical appraisal/commentary on Moro 2022 — endorses the SR+MA's headline finding (EE prevalence ~28%) and provides JEBDP's commentary frame; useful as a peer-reviewer's lens but does not add new primary data.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Journal of Evidence-Based Dental Practice, 23(3), Sep 2023, 101886

## 2. Key Contributions
- Independent critical appraisal of Moro 2022 published in the same journal (JEBDP)
- Reinforces that MBI is the gold standard tool and EE is the dominant subscale signal
- Adds editorial weight to the prevalence findings without re-analyzing data

## 3. Methodology
- Commentary format — reviewers summarize the original methodology and provide commentary/analysis
- No new search or pooling
- Standard JEBDP critical appraisal structure: study design, selection criteria, commentary and analysis

## 4. Key Results (Restated from Moro 2022)
- 37 studies narrative / 31 meta-analyzed
- MBI subscales (EE, DP, PA) used as the diagnostic framework
- Affirms the high prevalence finding and the emotional exhaustion-dominant pattern

## 5. Limitations
- Adds no new primary evidence
- Reviewer perspective only — same conclusions as the underlying SR
