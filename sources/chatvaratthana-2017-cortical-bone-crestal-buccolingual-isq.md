## Why Ingested

ISQ에 영향을 미치는 골 파라미터 중 *어느 깊이*의 피질골이 결정적인지가 불분명했음. 본 논문은 치조정에서 3·6·9 mm 깊이의 협설측 피질골 두께 및 cortical/cancellous 비율과 ISQ의 상관성을 CBCT 기반으로 분석, **치조정 직하 3 mm**만이 유의 구간임을 보여준다. 기존 [[wiki/implants/isq/chatvaratthana-2017-cortical-bone-thickness-isq-cbct]] 문서의 치조정 피질골-ISQ 상관 발견을 깊이별 분해 분석으로 확장·보완한다.

## One-line Summary

Prospective clinical study (n=19 implants, 16 patients, Mahidol University) showing that crestal cortical bone thickness (r=0.885, p<0.001) and buccolingual cortical bone at 3 mm (r=0.535, p=0.018) strongly correlate with ISQ, while bone parameters at 6 and 9 mm do not.

## 한줄요약

전향적 임상연구 (n=19 임플란트, 16명, Mahidol 대학): 치조정 피질골 두께(r=0.885, p<0.001)와 3 mm 깊이 협설측 피질골(r=0.535, p=0.018)이 ISQ와 강한 상관을 보였으며, 6·9 mm 깊이 파라미터는 ISQ와 무관.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: PLOS ONE 12(12): e0190293
- **Published**: December 27, 2017
- **Institution**: Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University, Bangkok, Thailand
- **Ethics**: MU-DT/PY-IRB 2016/019.2103
- **Implant system**: Conelog screw-line Promote plus (Camlog), 5 mm × 9 mm, posterior regions

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Establishes a strong correlation (r=0.885) between crestal cortical bone thickness and ISQ at implant placement.
2. Demonstrates that only bone within 3 mm of the crestal level (both crestal cortical and buccolingual cortical thickness) is significantly related to ISQ.
3. Shows that the cortical-to-cancellous bone ratio at 3 mm also correlates with ISQ (r=0.488, p=0.034), providing an objective CBCT-based bone quality classifier.
4. Confirms that bone at 6 and 9 mm depths has no significant ISQ correlation, suggesting RFA reflects primarily marginal bone characteristics.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Non-randomized prospective clinical study
- **Sample**: 16 patients, 19 implants (17 posterior mandible, 2 posterior maxilla); mean age 51.13 years
- **Sample size rationale**: Power calculation based on r=0.65, α=0.05, power=85% → minimum 19 implants
- **CBCT machine**: 3D Accuitomo 170 (J.Morita); 90 kVp, 5 mA, 17.5 s; FOV 6×6 cm; voxel 0.125 mm; slice 0.5 mm
- **Bone measurements**:
  - Crestal cortical bone thickness (buccal + lingual sides)
  - Buccolingual cortical bone thickness at 3, 6, 9 mm below crestal level
  - Cancellous bone thickness at 3, 6, 9 mm
  - Cortical/cancellous ratio at 3, 6, 9 mm (classified into 4 groups simplified from Leckholm & Zarb)
- **ISQ measurement**: Osstell (Osstell AB, Gothenburg); 4 orientations (buccal/lingual/mesial/distal) at time of placement
- **Statistics**: Spearman's rank correlation; Kolmogorov-Smirnov normality test; ICC for intra-observer reliability; p<0.05 significant

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Variable | r | p-value |
|---|---|---|
| Crestal cortical bone thickness | 0.885 | <0.001 |
| Buccolingual cortical at 3 mm | 0.535 | 0.018 |
| Buccolingual cortical at 6 mm | 0.167 | 0.495 |
| Buccolingual cortical at 9 mm | 0.217 | 0.373 |
| Cancellous bone at 3/6/9 mm | ns | all >0.05 |
| Cortical/cancellous ratio at 3 mm | 0.488 | 0.034 |
| Cortical/cancellous ratio at 6/9 mm | ns | >0.05 |

- Mean ISQ: 75.29 ± 8.76 overall; mandible 74.37 ± 8.79, maxilla 83.12 ± 2.3 (11.76% higher)
- Mean crestal cortical bone: 1.41 ± 0.48 mm (males wider: 1.52 mm vs females: 1.31 mm)
- Group 1 (cortical ratio >0.75 at 3 mm): mean ISQ 80.54 ± 7.11 — highest among groups
- Intra-observer ICC >0.986 for all bone measurements

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample size (n=19); predominantly posterior mandible (89.5%)
- Only one implant dimension (5×9 mm) and manufacturer used — limits generalizability
- Single-surgeon placement; non-randomized design
- No secondary stability follow-up — only primary stability at placement
- Posterior maxilla underrepresented (n=2)

## 6. Related Work

- Leckholm & Zarb bone classification (1985) — subjective radiographic/tactile bone quality grading this study partially replaces with objective CBCT ratios
- Chatvaratthana 2017 (other paper, different co-authors) — crestal cortical bone thickness and ISQ with CBCT in similar design
- Studies correlating D1–D4 bone types with ISQ — this study provides quantitative CBCT validation

## 7. Glossary

- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient)**: Score 1–100 from resonance frequency analysis; higher = greater primary stability
- **RFA (Resonance Frequency Analysis)**: Non-invasive ISQ measurement; Osstell device
- **Crestal cortical bone thickness**: Width of compact bone at the alveolar crest (buccal + lingual sides)
- **Buccolingual cortical bone thickness**: Thickness of the outer cortical plate measured perpendicular to the ridge at a given depth
- **Cortical/cancellous ratio**: Cortical bone thickness ÷ total bone thickness at a given depth; proxy for bone type (L&Z Groups 1–4)
- **Conelog**: Conical connection implant system (Camlog Biotechnologies)
