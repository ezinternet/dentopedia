## One-line Summary
Review (Journal of Advanced Research) classifying DH desensitization strategies into physical, chemical, and synergistic tubule-sealing approaches, with emphasis on biomimetic mineralization and biomaterials.

## 한줄요약
리뷰: DH 탈감작 전략을 물리·화학·시너지 세관 폐쇄 접근으로 분류하고 생체모방 광화·생체재료를 강조.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Advanced Research 2026;84:509–523. Received 23 May 2025 / accepted 21 Sep 2025.
- Affiliations incl. State Key Laboratory of Polymer Science and Technology, CAS.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Organizes DH therapy around tubule-sealing as the unifying mechanism.
- Reviews physical (laser), chemical (mineralizing agents), and synergistic strategies.
- Highlights biomimetic mineralization and advanced biomaterials.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative/mechanistic review (materials-science oriented).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Sealing exposed tubules identified as the key desensitization strategy; physical, chemical, and combined approaches each profiled with mechanism and limitations.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Review without pooled clinical estimates; translational gap between bench biomaterials and clinic.

## 6. Related Work
- Materials-depth complement to clinical narrative and trial-based evidence.

## 7. Glossary
- Biomimetic mineralization: lab-induced apatite formation mimicking natural mineral to occlude tubules.
