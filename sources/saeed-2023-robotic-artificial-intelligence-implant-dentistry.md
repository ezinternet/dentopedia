## One-line Summary
Cureus 2023: Narrative review — robotics + AI in implant dentistry; robotic accuracy in placement, AI for diagnosis/treatment planning; ethical considerations; integration with digital workflows; case studies show improved survival rates and patient satisfaction.

## 1. Key Results
- Narrative review (Ministry of Health, Saudi Arabia)
- Robotic systems: unparalleled accuracy in implant placement; reduces human error
- AI algorithms: analyze patient data for diagnosis, treatment planning, implant design
- Case studies demonstrate improved implant survival rates and patient satisfaction
- Challenges: ethical considerations, human expertise balance, cost, technology reliability
- Future: integration with digital workflows; collaborative development across disciplines

## 2. Clinical Implications
- Robotic guidance reduces surgeon-dependent variability in implant placement
- AI diagnosis tools can flag high-risk cases for enhanced planning
- Implementation barriers: cost, learning curve, regulatory framework
- Responsible implementation requires interdisciplinary collaboration
