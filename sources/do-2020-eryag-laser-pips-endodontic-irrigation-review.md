## One-line Summary
Literature review of 59 studies: Er:YAG LAI and PIPS outperformed other irrigation activation methods in 33/59 studies — promising for canal disinfection and smear layer removal, but large method variability limits conclusions; no in vivo studies found.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Lasers in Medical Sciences 2020;11(3):316–331
- **DOI**: 10.34172/jlms.2020.53
- **Institution**: CHU Nantes / University of Nantes, France

## 2. Key Contributions
- First comprehensive literature review of Er:YAG 레이저 활성 관개 (Laser Activated Irrigation, LAI) and 광유도 광음향 스트리밍 (Photon-Induced Photoacoustic Streaming, PIPS) for endodontic irrigation
- PIPS parameters: sub-ablative energy (20 mJ, 15 Hz), ultra-short pulses (50 µs)
- 33 of 59 studies showed Er:YAG LAI/PIPS superior to other methods
- No in vivo studies identified

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Literature review via PubMed keyword search
- 59 articles selected by inclusion/exclusion criteria
- Outcomes: bacterial reduction, smear layer removal, debris removal, irrigant penetration
- Comparison methods: syringe irrigation, ultrasonic passive irrigation (PUI), sonic activation (EndoActivator)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Er:YAG LAI/PIPS creates cavitation + shockwaves via photoacoustic/photomechanical effects
- Superior in 33/59 articles vs other activation methods
- Strong evidence for smear layer removal and debris reduction
- Antimicrobial efficacy: promising but study heterogeneity is large
- No clinical (in vivo) studies available to confirm bench results

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- No in vivo studies: all evidence from in vitro / ex vivo models
- Large variability: bacterial incubation time, laser parameters, irrigation protocols, irrigants used
- Lack of standardized protocols
- Future: need for standardized protocols and in vivo RCTs

## 6. Related Work
- Complements PUI/sonic irrigation literature (ANP, EndoActivator studies)
- NaOCl remains gold standard irrigant regardless of activation method

## 7. Glossary
- **Er:YAG**: Erbium-doped Yttrium Aluminum Garnet laser (2940 nm wavelength)
- **LAI**: Laser Activated Irrigation
- **PIPS**: Photon-Induced Photoacoustic Streaming
- **PUI**: Passive Ultrasonic Irrigation
- **NaOCl**: Sodium hypochlorite
- **EDTA**: Ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid (smear layer removal)
