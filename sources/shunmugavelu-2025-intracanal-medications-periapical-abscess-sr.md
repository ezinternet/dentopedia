## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/ordinola-zapata-2022-intracanal-medicaments-status-directions]]의 약제 status 리뷰를, "치근단 농양(periapical abscess) 감염 제어 + 통증 + 치유"라는 임상 결과 축으로 적용·보강한다. 또 [[endodontics/irrigation/rossi-fedele-2023-irrigation-dressing-apical-periodontitis-sr-ma]]의 dressing→치유 결과 흐름을, 통증 완화 속도(48–72h)와 신제형(TAPC·나노·Ledermix)까지 확장해 재확인한다.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (GMS Hyg Infect Control 2025; PubMed/Scopus/Cochrane, 2020–2024 RCT/cohort/case-control; no meta-analysis due to heterogeneity) of intracanal medications for periapical-abscess infection control and healing: calcium hydroxide gives strong microbial reduction (~99.5%) and lowers IL-1β/TNF-α, triple antibiotic paste + diclofenac (TAPC) and nano-formulations (nano-Ag, nano-CH) give superior 48-h pain relief, Ledermix is fastest for acute apical periodontitis pain, and CBCT-monitored nonsurgical treatment of large lesions reaches ~76% healing.

## 한줄요약

치근단 농양 감염 제어·치유를 위한 근관 내 약제 체계적 고찰(2020–2024 RCT/코호트/환자대조, 이질성으로 메타분석 미시행) — 수산화칼슘은 미생물 ~99.5% 감소 및 IL-1β/TNF-α 저하, 삼중항생제+디클로페낙(TAPC)과 나노 제형(나노은·나노CH)은 48시간 통증 완화에서 우수, Ledermix는 급성 치근단 치주염 통증에 가장 빠르며, CBCT로 모니터링한 큰 병소의 비수술 치료는 ~76% 치유율을 보였다.

- **Type**: Review article (systematic search; no meta-analysis)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Evaluates four medication classes for periapical-abscess control: **calcium hydroxide (CH)**, **triple antibiotic paste + diclofenac potassium (TAPC)**, **nano-formulations (nano-silver, nano-CH)**, and **Ledermix paste** (corticosteroid + antibiotic).
- Couples three clinical endpoints — microbial reduction, inflammation/pain, and radiographic healing — rather than antimicrobial kill alone.
- Highlights CBCT's role in diagnosing and monitoring periapical-lesion healing.
- Adds an explicit antimicrobial-stewardship caveat: local antibiotic application should be avoided unless no equally effective antiseptic exists, given resistance risk.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Databases**: PubMed, Scopus, Cochrane Library.
- **Search window**: January 2020 – December 2024.
- **Keywords**: intracanal medication duration, periapical abscess, calcium hydroxide, chlorhexidine, healing outcomes.
- **Inclusion**: studies on intracanal-medication duration vs periapical-abscess healing; clinical/radiographic/microbiological endpoints; RCT, cohort, or case-control; English.
- **Exclusion**: incomplete data; non-comparative studies or reviews; animal or in-vitro experiments.
- **Risk of bias**: Cochrane RoB tool (RCTs) + Newcastle-Ottawa Scale (observational).
- **Synthesis**: narrative; **meta-analysis NOT performed** due to heterogeneity in study designs and outcomes.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Calcium hydroxide**: microbial reduction up to **99.5%**; reduced inflammatory mediators (IL-1β, TNF-α); enhanced tissue healing — reliable for apical periodontitis but slow-acting with limited immediate pain relief.
- **TAPC (TAP + diclofenac potassium)**: superior pain relief vs CH from dual antibacterial + anti-inflammatory action; significant pain reduction within **48 h**.
- **Nano-formulations (nano-Ag, nano-CH)**: enhanced pain relief and effective microbial control with maintained safety; superior pain reduction in first **48–72 h**.
- **Ledermix paste**: most effective for **rapid pain reduction in acute apical periodontitis** — underscores corticosteroid + antibiotic role.
- **CBCT-monitored nonsurgical treatment**: high healing rate (**76%**) for large periapical lesions.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- No quantitative pooling (heterogeneous designs/outcomes) — conclusions are interpretive.
- Search restricted to English and to 2020–2024; excludes animal/in-vitro mechanistic data.
- Antibiotic-containing pastes (TAPC, Ledermix) carry resistance and (for TAP) discoloration concerns; authors urge antiseptic alternatives where efficacy is comparable.
- Calls for optimized medication protocols and long-term outcome data for emerging formulations.

## 7. Glossary

- **TAPC** — Triple Antibiotic Paste with diclofenac potassium (anti-inflammatory adjunct).
- **TAP** — Triple Antibiotic Paste.
- **CH** — Calcium hydroxide; **nano-CH** — nanoparticle calcium hydroxide.
- **nano-Ag** — nano-silver.
- **Ledermix** — corticosteroid (triamcinolone) + antibiotic (demeclocycline) paste.
- **CBCT** — Cone-Beam Computed Tomography.
- **IL-1β / TNF-α** — pro-inflammatory cytokines used as inflammation markers.
