## One-line Summary
후방부 아말감 vs 레진 수명 SR (2025, Cureus): 아말감 중앙생존 16년 vs 레진 11년; 최신 연구에서 격차 축소; 재2차 우식이 주요 실패 원인.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Cureus 2025, 17(7): e88836
- **DOI**: 10.7759/cureus.88836
- **Study type**: Systematic review (no meta-analysis; heterogeneity)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Amalgam median survival time >16 years; composite ~11 years historically
- Recent studies (post-2015) show narrowing gap as composite technique improved
- Secondary caries and marginal discoloration: primary failure causes for composite
- Fracture: primary failure cause for amalgam
- High-caries-risk patients: composite significantly higher failure risk

## 3. Methodology
- **Databases**: PubMed, Cochrane, Google Scholar (1990–2024)
- **Inclusion**: Clinical trials and observational studies ≥12 months follow-up; posterior permanent teeth; both materials compared
- **Quality**: CASP; no meta-analysis due to study heterogeneity

## 4. Key Results
| Material | Median Survival | Primary Failure Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Amalgam | >16 years | Fracture |
| Composite | ~11 years (older studies); improving in recent cohorts | Secondary caries, marginal discoloration |
| High caries risk | Composite significantly worse | — |
| Single-surface | Comparable | — |

## 5. Limitations
- SR without meta-analysis; qualitative synthesis only
- Studies span different composite generations (older materials may inflate composite failure)
- No standardized failure criteria across studies
