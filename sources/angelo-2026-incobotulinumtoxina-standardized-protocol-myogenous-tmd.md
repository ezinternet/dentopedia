## Why Ingested

기존 보유 BoNT-A 논문은 onabotulinumtoxinA(Botox) 위주이며 incobotulinumtoxinA(Xeomin)의 표준화 프로토콜 근거가 부재함. [[botulinum-toxin/manfredini-2019-botulinum-toxin-bruxism-tmj-rct]]를 포함한 기존 연구들이 이형성 프로토콜로 비교를 어렵게 하는 문제를 해소하기 위해, DC/TMD 기준으로 진단 확정하고 표준화된 Xeomin 프로토콜(Ângelo Protocol)을 적용한 12개월 후향 코호트를 추가.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective cohort (n=98) of DC/TMD-confirmed myogenous TMD patients treated with a standardized incobotulinumtoxinA (Xeomin) protocol showed significant reduction in myalgia severity (r=0.86) and VAS pain at 12 months, with preserved MMO and a 79.6% favorable outcome rate.

## 한줄요약

DC/TMD로 진단된 근육성 악관절장애 환자(n=98)에게 표준화된 incobotulinumtoxinA(Xeomin) 프로토콜을 적용한 12개월 후향 코호트로, 근통 중증도(r=0.86) 및 VAS 통증이 유의하게 감소하고 최대 개구량이 유지되며 79.6% 호전율을 달성.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First study reporting long-term (12-month) outcomes with the **Ângelo Botulinum Toxin Protocol** for incobotulinumtoxinA in myogenous TMD
- DC/TMD diagnostic criteria applied — strengthens internal validity over many prior studies with inconsistent diagnostic standards
- Protocol details: ~200U incobotulinumtoxinA; bilateral masseter (40U extra-oral + 30U intra-oral), temporalis (35U), sternocleidomastoid (25U) per side
- Highlights lower immunogenicity advantage of incobotulinumtoxinA (pure 150 kDa neurotoxin) for long-term treatment plans
- Introduces concept of "Preoperative Unload Joint with Botulinum Toxin (PUB-TT)" as a preparatory step before TMJ surgery

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Single-center retrospective observational study (Portugal)
- **Period**: June 2022 – June 2025
- **Screened**: 930 patients; eligible: 252; analyzed: 98 (complete 12-month follow-up)
- **Inclusion**: Age ≥18, myogenous TMD by DC/TMD, ≥3 months conservative treatment failure, ≥12 months follow-up
- **Exclusion**: TMJ surgery, arthrogenous TMD, prior BoNT-A within 12 months
- **Demographics**: Mean age 45.55 ± 16.27 years; 85.71% female
- **Primary outcome**: Myalgia severity (0–3 scale, bilateral digital palpation at 1 kg/5s)
- **Secondary outcomes**: VAS orofacial pain, MMO (interincisal distance), arthrogenous progression
- **Adjunctive**: 5 physiotherapy sessions + behavioral counseling post-injection
- **Statistics**: Wilcoxon signed-rank (myalgia), paired t-test (VAS, MMO), Cohen's d / r effect sizes

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Baseline | 12-month | p-value | Effect size |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Myalgia severity (median, 0–3) | 3.0 (IQR 2–3) | 0.0 (IQR 0–0) | <0.001 | r=0.86 (large) |
| VAS orofacial pain (mean) | 2.44 ± 2.54 | 0.37 ± 1.15 | <0.001 | Cohen's d=0.76 (moderate-large) |
| MMO (mm, mean) | 42.55 ± 16.72 | 43.10 ± ~16 | 0.35 (NS) | d=0.10 (negligible) |

- **Favorable outcome** (myalgia 0–1 AND VAS ≤2 AND MMO ≥35mm at 12mo): **79.6%** (n=78)
- **Treatment failures** (20.4%): 12 required reintervention (7 repeat BoNT-A, 5 TMJ arthrocentesis)
- **Arthrogenous progression during follow-up**: 5 patients (5.1%)
- **Adverse events**: Transient local discomfort and mild muscle fatigue only; no major adverse events

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective design; no control group — cannot establish causation
- Adjunctive physiotherapy/behavioral counseling prevent isolating BoNT-A-specific effect
- No systematic intermediate follow-up (3mo, 6mo data incomplete)
- Adverse events not systematically recorded
- Single-center, highly selected (refractory) cohort limits generalizability
- Future: multicenter RCT isolating incobotulinumtoxinA effect; imaging-based structural safety data

## 7. Glossary

- **IncobotulinumtoxinA (Xeomin)**: pure 150 kDa neurotoxin formulation without complexing proteins; lower immunogenicity than onabotulinumtoxinA (Botox)
- **DC/TMD** (Diagnostic Criteria for Temporomandibular Disorders): standardized, validated clinical diagnostic framework
- **Myogenous TMD** (근육성 악관절장애): TMD originating from masticatory muscles
- **Myalgia severity scale 0–3**: 0=no pain/pressure, 1=mild, 2=moderate, 3=severe
- **MMO** (Maximum Mouth Opening, 최대 개구량): interincisal distance in mm; ≥35mm = functional threshold
- **PUB-TT** (Preoperative Unload Joint with Botulinum Toxin): proposed concept of BoNT-A as surgical preparation
