## Why Ingested

무치악 전악 고정성 보철에서 경사·수직 임플란트 생존율·변연골 손실 비교 최신(2022) SR+MA. [[implants/implants-clinical-decision-ladder]] 의 경사 식립 임상결정 근거 갱신 목적.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (24 studies, n=2,637 patients, 5,594 tilted + 5,611 axial implants, 3–18yr): cumulative implant survival 93.91%; marginal bone loss significantly LOWER around axial vs tilted (P<.0001); prosthesis survival 99.31%.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (24편, n=2,637명, 추적 3~18년): 임플란트 누적 생존율 93.91%·보철 생존율 99.31%; 변연골 소실은 수직(axial) < 경사(tilted) (P<.0001) - 무치악 악궁에서 tilted+axial 혼합 전악 고정 보철은 예측 가능한 방법.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: International Journal of Oral & Maxillofacial Implants
- **Study type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis
- **Population**: Edentulous jaws, fixed full-arch rehabilitation
- **Follow-up**: ≥3 years (range 3–18 years)
- **Included studies**: 24 studies

## 2. Key Contributions
- Largest SR+MA on full-arch tilted+axial implants with ≥3yr follow-up
- First to show statistically significant (P<.0001) marginal bone loss difference: axial < tilted
- Differentiates maxilla vs mandible outcomes

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Electronic + hand search through April 2021
- Specific inclusion criteria independent of study design
- Main outcomes: cumulative implant survival rate, marginal bone level changes, complications at ≥3yr
- Mantel-Cox test and meta-analysis for axial vs tilted and maxilla vs mandible comparisons

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **Total patients**: 2,637 rehabilitated with 2,735 full prostheses (1,464 maxillary, 1,271 mandibular)
- **Implants**: 5,594 tilted + 5,611 axial
- **Failed implants**: 274 total
- **Cumulative implant survival**: 93.91%
- **Prosthesis survival**: 99.31%
- **Marginal bone loss**: Mean moderate; exceeded 2mm in only 2 studies
- **Axial vs tilted MBL**: Axial significantly LOWER (P<.0001)
- **Maxilla vs mandible**: MBL NOT significantly affected by arch (P=.17)
- Follow-up range: 3–18 years

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneity among included studies
- Need for randomized trials on efficacy and long-term bone remodeling
- Most included studies are observational

## 6. Related Work
- Del Fabbro & Ceresoli 2014: earlier SR+MA on same topic (shorter follow-up)
- Lin & Eckert 2018: no MBL difference found (different search period/inclusion criteria)

## 7. Glossary
- **Tilted implant**: coronal aspect intentionally inclined toward distal
- **Full-arch rehabilitation**: ≥6-unit fixed prosthesis on 4–6 implants per arch
- **Mantel-Cox test**: log-rank survival analysis method
