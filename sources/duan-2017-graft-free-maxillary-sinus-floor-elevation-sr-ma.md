## One-line Summary
Systematic review + meta-analysis (22 studies, 864 implants placed simultaneously with graft-free sinus floor elevation, lateral or transcrestal): mean implant survival 97.9%, mean vertical bone gain 3.8 mm.

## 한줄요약
체계적 문헌고찰+메타분석(22편, 무이식 상악동거상 동시식립 864 임플란트): 평균 임플란트 생존율 97.9%, 평균 수직 골증대 3.8mm.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Journal of Periodontology 2017;88(6):550–564
- DOI: 10.1902/jop.2017.160665; First published 01 June 2017
- Authors incl. Hom-Lay Wang (University of Michigan).
- Random-effects model + mixed-effect meta-regression.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pooled quantitative evidence that graft-free sinus floor elevation (GFSFE) with simultaneous implant placement achieves high survival (97.9%) without any graft material.
- Identifies determinants of vertical bone gain via meta-regression: surgical approach, implant length, and protruded implant length immediately post-op (PILi).
- Establishes benchmark values: mean VBG 3.8 mm, PILf 1.26 mm, MBL 0.91 mm (follow-up–dependent).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Design: systematic review + meta-analysis. Electronic + manual search, English, Jan 1976–Mar 2016.
- Inclusion: human clinical studies, simultaneous implant placement + GFSFE via lateral window or transcrestal approach.
- 740 studies screened → 22 included; 864 implants; mean RBH 5.7 ± 1.7 mm.
- Statistics: random-effects pooling of weighted means; mixed-effect meta-regression for VBG determinants.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Implant survival rate: 97.9% ± 0.02% (range 93.5%–100%).
- Weighted mean vertical bone gain (VBG): 3.8 ± 0.34 mm; significantly affected by surgical approach, implant length, PILi (R² = 19.1%, 39.3%, 83.9% respectively).
- Protruded implant length at follow-up (PILf): 1.26 ± 0.33 mm (range 0.3–2.1 mm) — i.e., implant apex re-osseointegrates / bone forms around protruded portion.
- Weighted mean MBL: 0.91 ± 0.11 mm, significantly associated with follow-up period (R² = 43.75%).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Author-stated: limited number of controlled studies, heterogeneity in follow-up and measurement, "within limitations" caveat on conclusions.
- Additional: search ends 2016 (pre-osseodensification era); pooled weighted means rather than comparative effect vs grafted controls; mixed lateral + transcrestal pooling; ingested copy is the Wiley web-print (abstract + references), full methods body not in PDF.

## 7. Glossary
- GFSFE: graft-free (graftless) sinus floor elevation — elevation without biomaterial, relying on blood clot/space maintenance by the implant.
- VBG: vertical bone gain (endo-sinus bone height increase).
- PILi / PILf: protruded implant length into sinus immediately post-op / at follow-up.
- MBL: marginal (crestal) bone loss.
- RBH: residual bone height.
