## Why Ingested

DBBM의 느린 turnover와 잔존 입자 문제를 다룬 [[bone-regeneration/manfro-2013-bovine-bone-substitutes-comparative-histomorphometric]]의 histomorphometric 비교를 보강·확장하는 head-to-head RCT. 부분탈회 동종골 plug(ACBP)가 DBBM 대비 6개월 차원 안정성은 동등하면서 잔존 graft 비율은 더 낮다는 직접 임상 근거를 추가한다.

## One-line Summary

Pilot RCT (n=25; ACBP 12 vs DBBM 13, sinus + socket GBR): at 6 months, partially demineralized allogeneic cancellous bone plug matched DBBM on CBCT dimensional stability and vital bone (38.84% vs 32.18%, P=0.105) but had significantly lower residual graft (11.18% vs 19.80%, P=0.006).

## 한줄요약

소규모 RCT (총 25명; ACBP 12명 vs DBBM 13명, 상악동거상 + 발치와 보존 GBR): 6개월 시점 CBCT 차원 안정성과 생활골 비율은 동등(38.84% vs 32.18%, P=0.105)했으나, 부분탈회 동종골 plug가 잔존 graft 비율은 유의하게 낮았다(11.18% vs 19.80%, P=0.006).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First direct head-to-head clinical comparison of a commercial **partially demineralized allogeneic cancellous bone plug (ACBP)** against the xenograft benchmark **deproteinized bovine bone mineral (DBBM, Bio-Oss)** in GBR, combining 3D CBCT dimensional analysis with histomorphometry.
- Demonstrates that the principal between-material difference at 6 months lies in **graft turnover (residual graft fraction)**, not in the proportion of newly formed vital bone — challenging the assumption that lower residual graft means more bone formation.
- Confirms that early dimensional/contour stability in membrane-protected GBR depends more on the integrity of the space-maintaining system (membrane, graft immobilization, flap closure) than on intrinsic graft material differences.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Participants**: 25 adults (18–65 y) needing GBR before implant placement, recruited Nov 2023–Nov 2024. Randomized via random number table → **ACBP n=12, DBBM n=13**. Allocation concealed; outcome assessors (CBCT, histomorphometry) and statistician blinded; surgeon not blinded (distinguishable handling). Not prospectively registered. CONSORT-reported.
- **Two indications under one membrane-based protocol**:
  - **Posterior maxillary sinus floor elevation** (lateral window; residual subantral height < 5 mm): ACBP 5 sites, DBBM 5 sites.
  - **Mandibular posterior extraction socket preservation** (horizontal defect > 5 mm, 3–4 wall): ACBP 7 sites, DBBM 8 sites.
- **Materials**: DBBM = Bio-Oss (Geistlich). ACBP = allogeneic bone graft 9×9×12 mm (SDBM-GK-P/S/H; Hubei Osteolink Biomaterials, Wuhan), saline-soaked 10 min. Both covered with absorbable collagen membrane (Heal All; Yantai Zhenghai). Single senior implantologist; tension-free closure with 3-0 braided polyester.
- **Primary outcome**: histomorphometric **residual graft material (RG, %)** on 3-mm trephine core at implant placement (6 mo).
- **Secondary**: **vital bone (VB, %)**; CBCT dimensional change — sinus height gain; socket ridge height + ridge width at 8/10/12 mm above mandibular canal.
- **Analysis**: independent-samples / Welch t tests, two-sided P<0.05; indication-specific subgroups exploratory; ANCOVA for sinus height adjusting baseline subantral height. No missing data. No a priori sample-size calculation.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **All 25 completed 6-month follow-up; no intervention-related adverse events.**
- **Histomorphometry (primary)**:
  - Vital bone: **ACBP 38.84 ± 8.37% vs DBBM 32.18 ± 11.04%, P=0.105** (NS).
  - Residual graft: **ACBP 11.18 ± 5.48% vs DBBM 19.80 ± 8.23%, P=0.006** (ACBP significantly lower).
- **Sinus floor elevation subgroup**: baseline subantral height 2.82 ± 0.63 mm (ACBP) vs 1.47 ± 0.87 mm (DBBM) — imbalanced. 6-mo augmented height 10.64 ± 1.98 vs 9.68 ± 4.00 mm; **height gain 7.82 ± 1.52 vs 8.21 ± 4.30 mm, P=0.852** (ANCOVA-adjusted P=0.801).
- **Socket preservation subgroup**: ridge height change −0.30 ± 0.22 (ACBP) vs +0.51 ± 1.24 mm (DBBM), P=0.079. Ridge width change at 8/10/12 mm all NS (P=0.437 / 0.957 / 0.945).
- **Bottom line**: ACBP ≈ DBBM on CBCT dimensional stability and vital bone, but with **lower residual graft fraction** → difference reflects graft turnover, not greater osteogenesis.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Single-center, small sample (n=25), **no a priori power calculation** → underpowered for small-to-moderate effects; two indications further dilute indication-specific power.
- Only **6-month** follow-up — captures intermediate remodeling, not long-term dimensional stability, implant survival, or peri-implant outcomes.
- Histomorphometry from **localized 3-mm trephine cores** may not reflect spatial heterogeneity of the whole augmented compartment.
- Baseline subantral height imbalance in sinus subgroup; not prospectively registered. Authors call for larger, stratified, multicenter trials with extended follow-up.

## 7. Glossary

- **ACBP** — partially demineralized allogeneic cancellous bone plug; controlled-demineralization allograft exposing matrix-bound osteoinductive proteins (e.g., BMPs) while retaining a trabecular scaffold.
- **DBBM** — deproteinized bovine bone mineral (Bio-Oss); slowly resorbing xenograft favoring space maintenance.
- **VB (vital bone, %)** — mineralized bone with osteocyte lacunae, continuous with regenerated trabeculae/marrow.
- **RG (residual graft, %)** — acellular graft remnants, sharp borders, no osteocyte lacunae; marker of remodeling/turnover.
- **GBR** — guided bone regeneration: barrier membrane excludes soft tissue to allow protected bone formation.
