## One-line Summary
SR+MA of 61 studies providing site-specific failure rates for orthodontic miniscrews, showing midpalatal sites have the lowest failure rate (1.3%) and zygomatic buttress the highest (16.4%), with root contact conferring an 8.7× increased risk of failure.

- **Databases**: Cochrane CENTRAL, Web of Knowledge, Scopus, MEDLINE, PubMed; comprehensive manual search

## 2. Key Contributions
- First systematic review to report failure rates stratified by each independent insertion site (palatal, maxillary buccal, mandibular buccal)
- Identifies root contact as a major modifiable risk factor (RR 8.7)
- Provides site-specific guidance for clinicians choosing miniscrew placement locations
- Protocol pre-registered as dissertation thesis

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Eligibility**: RCTs and prospective non-randomised studies; minimum 20 miniscrews in a specific insertion site; failure rate reported per site
- **Included**: 61 studies in quantitative synthesis
- **Sub-grouping**: Studies grouped by insertion site (midpalatal, paramedian palatal, parapalatal, maxillary buccal interradicular [M1-P2; C-L], zygomatic buttress, mandibular buccal interradicular [M1-P2; C-P1])
- **Statistical model**: Random-effects model, 95% confidence intervals
- **Sensitivity analyses** for robustness
- **Quality assessment**: Independent dual reviewers; GRADE-like evidence quality rating

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
**Palatal sites:**
- Midpalatal: 1.3% (95% CI 0.3–6.0) — lowest failure rate
- Paramedian: 4.8% (95% CI 1.6–13.4)
- Parapalatal: 5.5% (95% CI 2.8–10.7)

**Maxillary buccal sites:**
- Interradicular M1–P2: 9.2% (95% CI 7.4–11.4)
- Interradicular C–LI: 9.7% (95% CI 5.1–17.6)
- Zygomatic buttress: 16.4% (95% CI 4.9–42.5) — highest reported failure

**Mandibular buccal sites:**
- Interradicular M1–P2: 13.5% (95% CI 7.3–23.6)
- Interradicular C–P1: 9.9% (95% CI 4.9–19.1)

**Root contact risk:**
- Root contact → Risk Ratio 8.7 (95% CI 5.1–14.7) — statistically significant, moderate quality evidence

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Evidence quality: Very low to low for palatal sites; moderate for root contact findings
- Methodological drawbacks in some included studies
- Protocol not registered in a publicly accessible online registry
- Results must be interpreted with caution due to study heterogeneity

## 7. Glossary
- **OMI**: Orthodontic Miniscrew Implant
- **Interradicular**: Between adjacent tooth roots; common miniscrew insertion site
- **Zygomatic buttress**: Lateral maxillary bone region near zygoma; used for larger skeletal anchorage needs
- **Paramedian palate**: Region lateral to midpalatal suture; alternative to midpalate
- **RR (Risk Ratio)**: Relative likelihood of failure in exposed vs non-exposed group
- **CENTRAL**: Cochrane Central Register of Controlled Trials
