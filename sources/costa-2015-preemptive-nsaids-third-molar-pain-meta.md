## One-line Summary
Anesth Prog 2015 메타분석 (n=420, 6 RCTs, 4 분석포함): 제3대구치 외과 발치 시 preemptive (사전) NSAID 투여 — 술후 통증 감소 효과 **유의하지 않음** (P=0.2227); 임상 protocol에서 pre-emptive 처방 routine 정당화 부족.

## 1. Document Information
- Authors: Costa FWG, Esses DFS, Silva PGB, Carvalho FSR, Sá CDL, Albuquerque AFM, Bezerra TP, Ribeiro TR, Fonteles CSR, Soares ECS
- Type: Meta-analysis of RCTs
- Journal: Anesth Prog 2015;62(2):57-63

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Tests common clinical hypothesis (pre-emptive analgesia for third molar surgery).
2. Negative finding: preemptive NSAID does NOT show significant pain reduction.
3. Challenges the practice of pre-op NSAID dosing in this indication.

## 3. Methodology
- PubMed search; 704 articles → 6 selected (n=420 subjects)
- 4 studies included in meta-analysis (2 excluded for inadequate numeric data)
- Low risk of bias (Cochrane criteria); high methodological heterogeneity

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Preemptive NSAID vs placebo/no preemptive: NS (n=298, P=0.2227, OR not significant)

## 5. Limitations
- Small included study sample (4 for meta-analysis)
- High methodological heterogeneity
- Mostly older RCTs
