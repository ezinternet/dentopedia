## Why Ingested
[[gonzalez-sanchez-2023-tmj-physiotherapy-treatment-sr]]를 보완하는 비침습적 TMD 치료 SR. 스플린트, 관절천자세정술, 레이저, 전기자극 등 보다 넓은 non-invasive 스펙트럼을 포함; 병용 치료의 우월성 확인.

## One-line Summary
SR (20 studies, Cureus 2024, PRISMA): combination of manual therapy + counseling + splint shows best outcomes for TMD; TENS and LLLT alone show limited efficacy; >80% managed non-surgically.

## 한줄요약
SR (20 연구): 수기치료+상담+스플린트 병용이 최선; TENS·LLLT 단독 효과 제한; TMD 환자 80% 이상 비수술 관리 가능.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Cureus 16(3):e56713 (2024)
- **Study type**: Systematic review (PRISMA)
- **Databases**: PubMed, Google Scholar, ScienceDirect, Medline
- **Screened**: 1,437 → 611 → 229 full-text → 20 included
- **Treatments reviewed**: Physical therapy, laser, ultrasound, electrical stimulation, splint, injections, arthrocentesis

## 2. Key Contributions
- Combination (manual therapy + counseling + splint) → substantial reduction in pain, depression, anxiety
- TENS and LLLT alone: limited efficacy
- Precise diagnosis (surface EMG recommended) before treatment
- >80% TMD patients manageable non-surgically; ~5% ultimately require surgery
- Arthrocentesis effective as minimally invasive option when conservative fails

## 3. Methodology
- PRISMA SR; 20 RCTs/clinical trials included
- Outcomes: mandibular mobility, pain-free mouth opening, pain levels (p-values from paired t-test/ANOVA)
- Focus: comparative efficacy of non-invasive vs minimally invasive approaches

## 4. Key Results
| Treatment | Efficacy | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Manual therapy + counseling + splint | Best outcomes | Substantial ↓ pain, depression, anxiety |
| Physical therapy alone | Effective | Consistent evidence |
| TENS alone | Limited | Not superior to placebo in some studies |
| LLLT (low-level laser) alone | Limited | Inconsistent evidence |
| Arthrocentesis | Effective (minimally invasive) | Reserve for conservative failures |
| Injections | Effective (minimally invasive) | After exhausting non-invasive options |

## 5. Limitations
- SR without meta-analysis; heterogeneous study designs
- Varied outcome measures across studies
- Limited longitudinal follow-up in most studies
- Need for standardized evidence-based protocol

## 6. Clinical Implication
TMD 단계별 치료 접근:
1. 정밀 진단 (표면근전도 포함)
2. 비침습적 1차: 수기치료 + 상담 + 스플린트 병용 (최상 근거)
3. 물리치료 모달리티: TENS·LLLT는 병용 시 보조로만 사용
4. 1차 실패 시: 관절천자세정술 또는 주사 (최소침습)
5. 수술: 마지막 수단 (~5%)

## 7. Glossary
- **TENS**: Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (경피전기신경자극)
- **LLLT**: Low-level laser therapy (저강도 레이저치료)
- **Arthrocentesis**: 관절천자세정술 (관절강 세척)
- **Surface EMG**: 표면근전도 — 저작근 활성도 측정
