## Why Ingested

[[endodontics/shaping/aflaki-2020-glide-path-pathfile-scoutrace-cbct]] 의 PathFile/Scout RaCe 2-arm 비교를 5-arm으로 확장 (Scout RaCe + One G + PathFile + GPS + manual K). S-shape에서 rotary NiTi 동질성 evidence 강화 + K-file 열위 재확인.

## One-line Summary
S-shaped resin blocks (n=100, 5 groups × 20): manual K-file이 apical·middle·coronal third 모두 transportation 유의 증가; 4 rotary 시스템 (Scout RaCe / One G / PathFile / GPS) 간 NS 차이.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 5-system head-to-head S-shape transportation
- Manual K-file이 모든 third에서 통계적으로 열위 확인
- 4 rotary 시스템 동질성 (개별 design 차이 미미)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 100 S-shaped resin block simulators
- 5 그룹: (1) Scout RaCe (#10·#15 .02), (2) One G (#14 .03), (3) PathFile (#13·#16 .02), (4) GPS (#15 .03), (5) K-file (#15 .02) Control
- Pre/post photo superimposition (Adobe PhotoShop CC 2019)
- 10 cross-section transportation 측정 (Digimizer)
- One-way + repeated-measures ANOVA, α=0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Manual K-file: apical·middle third transportation 유의 증가 (p<0.001)
- Coronal third: K-file > Scout RaCe (p<0.05) 등
- Rotary 4 시스템 간 NS

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Resin block — dentin substrate 차이
- 단일 operator (vs multi)
- 임상 시간·debris extrusion 미측정
