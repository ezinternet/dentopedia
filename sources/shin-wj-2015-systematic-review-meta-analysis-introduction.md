## One-line Summary
Korean-language methodological introduction to systematic review and meta-analysis (Hanyang Med Rev 2015;35:9-17) covering search/selection/quality assessment, effect size pooling, heterogeneity (I², Cochran Q), fixed vs random effects, and publication bias (funnel plot, Egger's test).

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Hanyang Med Rev 2015;35(1):9-17
- DOI: 10.7599/hmr.2015.35.1.9
- Affiliation: Hanyang University Guri Hospital, Department of Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine

## 2. Key Contributions
- Comprehensive Korean-language SR/MA methodology in one paper — covers everything a clinician needs to read a Cochrane-style review.
- Distinguishes systematic review (qualitative synthesis) from meta-analysis (statistical synthesis of effect sizes).
- Operationalizes effect size pooling: MD/SMD for continuous outcomes, OR/RR for binary outcomes, log-transformed pooling weights inversely proportional to within-study variance.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative methodological review.
- Walks through the canonical pipeline: question (PICOS) → search → selection → data extraction → quality assessment (Cochrane RoB) → effect size calculation → pooling (fixed vs random effects via I²) → heterogeneity assessment → publication bias detection (funnel plot, Egger's test) → interpretation.
- Discusses model choice rules: I² < 25% low heterogeneity → fixed effects; I² > 50% substantial → random effects; I² > 75% high → caution about pooling.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Educational; no original empirical results.
- Provides I² heuristic cutoffs (25/50/75 low/moderate/high) consistent with Cochrane Handbook conventions.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Pre-PRISMA 2020 (uses PRISMA 2009 reporting items).
- Does not cover network meta-analysis, GRADE certainty assessment, or living systematic reviews.

## 6. Related Work
- Korean-language follow-up to Shin IH 2009 in this wiki (different author, same surname).
- Conceptually identical to the SR appraisal halves of Kiriakou 2014 (dentistry) and the anonymous worksheet.

## 7. Glossary
- I²: 이질성(heterogeneity) 정량 지표. 100 × (Q − df) / Q.
- Cochran Q: 이질성 카이제곱 검정.
- Fixed effects model: 모든 연구가 동일한 진참값을 추정한다고 가정.
- Random effects model: 연구마다 진참값이 다르고 분포를 이룬다고 가정.
- Funnel plot: publication bias 시각화. 비대칭 → 출판편향 의심.
