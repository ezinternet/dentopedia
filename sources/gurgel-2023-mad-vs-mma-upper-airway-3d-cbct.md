## Why Ingested
MAD와 MMA 수술의 상기도 확장 효과를 CBCT로 3D 비교한 최초 연구. [[tmj/pereira-2024-condylar-mandibular-movements-mad-osa-cbct]]의 MAD 연구를 보강하며, 하악 회전 방향 차이(시계방향 vs 반시계방향)가 상기도에 미치는 임상적 함의 제공.

## One-line Summary
Retrospective CBCT cohort (n=17 MAD, n=17 MMA, BMI-matched): MAD causes clockwise mandibular rotation and increases superior oropharynx; MMA causes counterclockwise rotation with greater gains across all UA regions.

## 한줄요약
후향적 CBCT 코호트 (MAD 17명, MMA 17명, BMI 매칭): MAD는 시계방향 하악 회전으로 상부 구인두 확장; MMA는 반시계방향으로 전 상기도 영역에서 더 큰 확장.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health (2023) 23:436
- **Study type**: Retrospective cohort
- **n**: 17 MAD + 17 MMA (BMI/height/weight matched)
- **Imaging**: CBCT (T0 baseline, T1 at 6–12 months post-treatment)

## 2. Key Contributions
- MAD: clockwise mandibular rotation → superior oropharynx volume increase
- MMA: counterclockwise rotation → greater increase in ALL UA regions (superior + inferior)
- Both groups: significant superior oropharynx volume increase; MMA group greater
- MMA: additionally significant inferior oropharynx volume increase; MAD: no significant inferior change
- Mandibular advancement amount (MAD): greater advancement → ↓ superior oropharynx, ↑ inferior oropharynx

## 3. Methodology
- BMI/height/weight matched retrospective cohort; CBCT before and after
- 3D Slicer + ITK-SNAP for UA segmentation and measurement
- MAD: Brazilian dental appliance (BRD), gradual advancement to therapeutic protrusion
- MMA: bilateral sagittal split osteotomy + Le Fort I

## 4. Key Results
| Outcome | MAD | MMA |
|---|---|---|
| Mandibular rotation | Clockwise (−3.97°) | Counterclockwise (+2.40°) |
| Superior oropharynx ↑ | Significant | Significant (greater) |
| Inferior oropharynx ↑ | Not significant | Significant |
| Overall UA enlargement | Moderate | Greater |

## 5. Limitations
- Retrospective; no randomization; MMA group no PSG confirmation of OSA
- Different follow-up periods across subjects
- Small n per group

## 6. Glossary
- **MAD**: Mandibular advancement device
- **MMA**: Maxillomandibular advancement (orthognathic surgery)
- **BRD**: Brazilian dental appliance (individualized MAD)
- **Clockwise rotation**: Mandible rotates such that chin moves downward/backward
- **Counterclockwise rotation**: Chin moves upward/forward
