## One-line Summary
SR (5 phantom studies) - scattered radiation dose to the eye lens from dental CBCT ranges 0.103-8.3 mSv, varying with FOV, exposure, dosimeter and scanner; calls for dose reference levels and optimization.

## 한줄요약
