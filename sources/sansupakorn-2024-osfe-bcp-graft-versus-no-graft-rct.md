## Why Ingested

[[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/guo-2020-non-grafted-platelet-concentrations-grafted-transcrestal-sinus]]와 같은 graft vs no-graft 비교이나, Sansupakorn은 BCP(HA30:TCP70)를 특정하고 ISQ·CBCT·2D 복합 평가한 RCT로 wiki의 graft-benefit 논쟁에 직접 근거 추가. Shahood 2024(Bio-Oss+CGF)와 결론이 다름 — 이식재 유형에 따라 결과가 갈리는지 검토 필요.

## One-line Summary
RCT (n=30, 1 yr): OSFE with BCP (HA30:TCP70) shows no significant advantage over OSFE alone in ISQ (p=0.56), survival, or 3D bone gain; BCP group had higher marginal bone change.

## 한줄요약
RCT(30임플란트, 1년): OSFE+BCP(HA30:TCP70)는 무이식 OSFE 대비 ISQ(p=0.56)·생존율·CBCT 골이득에서 유의미한 이점 없음; BCP 군에서 오히려 MMBC 높음.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Investigations 2024;28:342
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s00784-024-05736-w
- **Published**: 27 May 2024
- **Study type**: RCT
- **n**: 30 implants / 30 patients (15/group)
- **Registration**: TCTR20210517008 (registered May 17, 2021)
- **Follow-up**: 12 months functional loading
- **Note**: File is web-captured PDF (Springer Nature); abstract and methods complete

## 2. Key Contributions
- BCP(HA30:TCP70) 이식재의 transcrestal SFE 추가 이익 없음을 RCT로 규명
- ISQ, ESBG-CT, ESBG-2D, MMBC, survival rate 종합 비교
- OSFE without grafting이 BCP 대비 MMBC 유의미하게 낮음 (6, 9, 12mo; p<0.05)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Group 1**: OSFE + BCP (30%HA:70%βTCP), n=15
- **Group 2**: OSFE without graft, n=15
- **Site**: upper premolar or molar, single edentulous
- **Assessments at 3, 6, 9, 12 months post-loading**:
  - ISQ (resonance frequency analysis)
  - ESBG (2D periapical)
  - ESBG-CT (CBCT)
  - MMBC (mean marginal bone change)
  - Survival/failure, surgical complications

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **ISQ mean** at 1 yr: 79.18±3.43 overall (BCP: 78.72±3.46; no-graft: 79.65±3.52); p=0.56 (NS)
- **Survival**: BCP=13/15 (2 failures); no-graft=14/15 (1 failure); no statistical difference
- **MMBC**: no-graft group significantly lower at 6, 9, 12mo (p<0.05) — BCP group showed MORE marginal bone change
- **ESBG-CT / ESBG-2D**: between-group differences not significant
- **Conclusion**: BCP provides no extraordinary benefit; OSFE without grafting preferred clinically

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small n=30 (15/group); underpowered for failure rate comparison
- Web-captured PDF — full tables/figures only accessible behind paywall
- Single center (Thailand); follow-up 1 year only
- BCP composition fixed (HA30:TCP70); other ratios not tested

## 6. Related Work
- Guo 2020: PRP/PRF vs graft vs no-graft transcrestal SFE (ISQ comparison)
- Shahood 2024: Bio-Oss Collagen+CGF shows benefit over no-graft (contradicts Sansupakorn's conclusion for BCP)
- Volpe 2022: graftless OSFE long-term (97.2% survival at 8.5yr)

## 7. Glossary
- **BCP** (Biphasic Calcium Phosphate, 이상인산칼슘): HA+βTCP 혼합 alloplastic bone graft
- **ESBG** (Endo-Sinus Bone Gain): 상악동 내 신생골 이득량
- **MMBC** (Mean Marginal Bone Change): 임플란트 변연골 변화량
