## Why Ingested

리튬 디실리케이트 세라믹 접착에서 실란의 화학적 상태(실란올 단량체 vs 실록산 중합체)와 표면 처리(광택 vs HF 에칭)가 접착강도에 미치는 영향을 13C-NMR·ATR-FTIR·SBS로 체계적으로 분석한 논문. 유니버설 접착제 내 실란 활성도 저하 문제를 근거 있게 설명하여 [[resin-bonding/kheirkhah-2021-universal-adhesive-silane-porcelain-repair-bond]]의 임상 관찰(별도 실란 필요성)에 분자적 근거를 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro (5 silane products × 2 ceramic surface conditions, 13C-NMR + ATR-FTIR + SBS Weibull analysis): Prehydrolyzed silanol monomer (Calibra Silane) achieved highest bond strength; universal adhesives/primers containing siloxane adducts showed reduced chemical bonding, with HF etching compensating by micromechanical retention.

## 한줄요약

In vitro (5종 실란 제품, 광택 vs HF 에칭 리튬 디실리케이트, NMR+FTIR+SBS): 사전가수분해 실란올 단량체(Calibra Silane)가 최고 접착강도; 유니버설 접착제의 실록산 중합체는 화학결합력 저하, HF 에칭이 기계적 유지력으로 보상.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Characterized silane chemical status in 5 commercially available products using 13C-NMR
- Demonstrated that prehydrolyzed silanol monomers (Calibra Silane) provide superior chemical bonding to ceramic surface
- Universal adhesive/primer products showed degraded silane forms (siloxane adducts/polymers) with reduced reactivity
- HF acid etching significantly increased surface roughness parameters (Sa, Sz, Sdr, Sc, Sv; p < 0.001)
- ATR-FTIR evidence of chemical bonding (Si-O) only for prehydrolyzed silanol primer (CS) with substrate
- Weibull analysis showed HF etching partially compensated for reduced silane reactivity in multicomponent products
- Siloxane polymers in KS (Kerr Silane Primer) showed no differential reactivity between polished and HF-etched surfaces

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Products tested**:
- CS: Calibra Silane Coupling Agent (prehydrolyzed silanol monomers — reference silane)
- GM: G-Multi Primer (methoxylated-siloxane adducts)
- KS: Kerr Silane Primer (siloxane polymers)
- MB: Monobond Plus (silanol–siloxane adducts)
- SB: Scotchbond Universal Adhesive (silanol–siloxane adducts)

**Ceramic surface conditions**:
- Group A: Polished lithium disilicate (IPS e.max CAD/Press)
- Group B: HF acid-etched lithium disilicate

**Characterization methods**:
- **13C-NMR**: Silane chemical status in product vials (silanol vs siloxane forms)
- **Optical profilometry**: Surface roughness parameters (Sa, Sz, Sdr, Sc, Sv), n=5/group
- **ATR-FTIR**: Interaction of silane products with ceramic surfaces (chemical bonding assessment)
- **SBS**: Shear bond strength of flowable composite to ceramic surfaces (n=20/group/product), Weibull analysis

**Statistics**: Weibull distribution for SBS; statistical significance at p < 0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**NMR silane status**:
- CS: Silanol monomers only (most reactive)
- GM: Methoxylated-siloxane adducts
- MB, SB: Silanol-siloxane adducts
- KS: Siloxane polymers (least reactive)

**Surface roughness (Group B > Group A; p < 0.001)**: HF etching significantly increased all roughness parameters.

**SBS Weibull characteristic life rankings**:
- Group A (polished): CS > SB, KS, MB > GM > NS
- Group B (HF-etched): CS > GM > SB, KS, MB, NS

- CS (silanol monomer) ranked highest in both surface conditions
- Group B SBS significantly higher than Group A for same silane treatment
- For products with silanol-siloxane adducts (MB, SB), HF etching elevated bond strength to match or exceed CS on polished surface — acid-etching neutralized the silane chemistry advantage differential

**ATR-FTIR**: Chemical bonding evidence (Si-O peaks) identified only for CS. Carboxylate salt formation detected for SB adhesive; more pronounced on polished surfaces.

**Failure mode**: Mainly adhesive; some partial resin cohesive failures in Group B (HF-etched).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short-term bond strength (no aging or thermocycling for durability)
- SBS geometry (less precise spatial control than μTBS)
- Single ceramic material (lithium disilicate); results may differ for feldspathic or leucite ceramics
- NMR performed on product vials, not after application to ceramic — in situ reactivity may differ
- Clinical relevance of differences between groups remains to be established in long-term studies

## 7. Glossary

- **13C-NMR (탄소-13 핵자기공명분광법)**: 실란 화학 구조(실란올 vs 실록산) 분석
- **ATR-FTIR (감쇠전반사-푸리에변환적외선분광법)**: 세라믹 표면과 실란 간 화학결합 확인
- **Silanol monomer (실란올 단량체)**: Si-OH 그룹이 활성; 세라믹 표면 Si-OH와 직접 축합반응 → 최적 결합
- **Siloxane polymer (실록산 중합체)**: Si-O-Si 중합; 표면과 반응성 저하
- **Weibull analysis (와이불 분석)**: 접착강도 신뢰도 통계; 특성 수명(characteristic life)과 형상 계수(shape factor)로 재료 신뢰성 평가
- **Sa (Arithmetic mean height, 평균면거칠기)**: 표면 거칠기 3D 파라미터
- **MPTMS (γ-methacryloxypropyltrimethoxysilane)**: 치과 실란의 주 성분; 세라믹-레진 커플링제
- **Calibra Silane (CS)**: Dentsply Sirona 단일병 사전가수분해 실란; 본 연구에서 최고 성능
