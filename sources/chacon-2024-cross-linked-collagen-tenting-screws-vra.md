## Why Ingested

Ti mesh 대안 탐색의 핵심 논문. **가교화 콜라겐막 + 텐팅 스크류(tenting screws)** 조합으로 수직골증대 6.2 mm를 달성 — Ti mesh 없이도 공간 유지 가능함을 Wang HL 그룹이 심미 구역에서 증명. [[wiki/implants/vertical-ridge-augmentation/sabri-2024-titanium-mesh-bone-augmentation-sr-ma]] Ti mesh 풀드 3.36 mm보다 높은 수직골증대를 보여 XL 콜라겐+텐팅 접근법의 임상적 경쟁력을 시사.

## One-line Summary

Case series (n=10, esthetic zone): Cross-linked collagen membrane + tenting screws for VRA → mean VBG 6.2 ± 1.61 mm (80% defect resolution); low complication rate; eliminates need for Ti mesh.

## 한줄요약

증례시리즈 (n=10, 심미 구역): 가교화 콜라겐막 + 텐팅 스크류 수직골증대 → 평균 수직 6.2 ± 1.61 mm(결손 해소율 80%); 합병증 낮음; Ti 메쉬 불필요.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrates Ti mesh can be replaced by tenting screws + XL collagen for VRA in esthetic zone
- 6.2 mm VBG — HIGHEST reported in case series with resorbable membranes
- 80% defect resolution (better than most Ti mesh series)
- Eliminates second surgery for Ti mesh removal
- Eliminates Ti mesh exposure complication (resorbable membrane)
- Avoids neurological risk during Ti mesh handling
- Novel technique: tenting screws placed strategically to define augmentation shape, then XL collagen tented over them

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Tenting screw technique: osteosynthesis mini-screws placed into bone at planned augmentation height
- Cross-linked collagen membrane draped over screws (space maintained by screws, not by membrane stiffness)
- Bone graft placed under membrane in tented space
- No Ti mesh used
- Healing: 9.3 months mean before re-entry
- Outcomes: VBG (mm), defect resolution (%)
- Esthetic zone specific (anterior maxilla/mandible)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Value |
|---|---|
| N | 10 patients |
| Mean healing time | 9.3 months |
| Mean defect resolution | 80% |
| Mean VBG | 6.2 ± 1.61 mm |
| Minimum VBG | 6 mm (in the 50% resolution case) |
| Complication rate | Low (no quantified number in abstract) |

Context: Sabri 2024 SR+MA pooled VBG for Ti mesh = 3.36 mm; this case series achieved 6.2 mm without Ti mesh.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small n=10; no control group
- Case series (low evidence level)
- Single technique operator (Wang HL group)
- Abstract-only available here — exact complication rates and full methodology in full text only
- Esthetic zone only — not validated for posterior mandible large defects

## 7. Glossary

- **Tenting screws**: titanium osteosynthesis mini-screws placed into bone to mechanically support (tent) a membrane over a defect, providing space maintenance without rigid metal mesh
- **XL collagen / cross-linked collagen**: collagen membrane with chemical cross-links (ribose or glutaraldehyde) for extended resorption time (6–12+ months)
- **Defect resolution**: % of initial vertical defect repaired with new bone
- **VBG**: vertical bone gain in mm
