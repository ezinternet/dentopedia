## Why Ingested

기존 [[endodontics/regenerative/]] 논문들은 scaffold/barrier 위주; 약제 선택(TAP vs DAP vs Ca(OH)₂)이 REP 결과에 미치는 영향을 SR+MA로 정량화한 첫 메타분석. TAP·CH 동등, 두 약제 모두 DAP 대비 우월함을 pooled success rate으로 직접 수치화.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 24 studies (n=791) in permanent necrotic immature teeth finds TAP (96.7%) and Ca(OH)₂ (97.4%) equivalent for 1-year REP success, both superior to DAP (84.2%; GRADE: high for TAP/CH, low for DAP).

## 한줄요약

영구 미성숙 치아 REP에서 TAP(96.7%)과 수산화칼슘(97.4%)의 1년 성공률이 동등하며, 두 약제 모두 DAP(84.2%)보다 우월함을 24개 연구 SR+MA로 확인.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR+MA to quantitatively compare TAP, DAP, and Ca(OH)₂ as intracanal medicaments specifically within REP protocols
- Demonstrates TAP–CH equivalence (pooled difference −0.7%, 95% CI −0.9% to +2.5%, within ±2.5% equivalence margin)
- Demonstrates both TAP and CH superiority over DAP (TAP−DAP: +12.5%; CH−DAP: +13.2%)
- Applies GRADE: high evidence for TAP and CH; low evidence for DAP due to few small studies
- Covers literature through December 2025 (MEDLINE, Scopus, Embase, Web of Science, Google Scholar)

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

*abstract-only — full text not retrieved*

- **Databases searched**: MEDLINE, Scopus, Embase, Web of Science, Google Scholar (last update December 2025)
- **Inclusion**: Trials investigating REP success in permanent necrotic immature teeth
- **Risk of bias**: Revised Cochrane RoB2 tool for randomised trials
- **Statistical model**: Random-effects model preferred when I² > 50%
- **Heterogeneity**: Cochran's Q + I² quantification
- **Equivalence margin**: ±2.5% for success-rate differences
- **Sensitivity analyses**: study quality, publication bias, study inclusion
- **Subgroup analyses**: scaffold type, world region
- **Evidence grading**: GRADE system
- **Design**: Naïve indirect comparison (no common comparator available across all arms; no network meta-analysis)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Medicament | n (patients) | Studies | Pooled 1-yr Success Rate | 95% CI | GRADE |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| TAP (triple antibiotic paste) | 544 | — | 96.7% | 94.8%–98.0% | High |
| DAP (double antibiotic paste) | 64 | — | 84.2% | 73.2%–92.0% | Low |
| CH (calcium hydroxide) | 183 | — | 97.4% | 93.9%–99.1% | High |

**Pairwise comparisons (naïve indirect)**:

| Comparison | Pooled Difference | 95% CI | Conclusion |
|---|---|---|---|
| TAP vs DAP | +12.5% | 2.8%–22.1% | TAP superior |
| TAP vs CH | −0.7% | −0.9%–2.5% | Equivalent |
| CH vs DAP | +13.2% | 3.3%–23.0% | CH superior |

- Total included studies: 24 (average quality per RoB2)
- Sensitivity and subgroup analyses corroborated primary results
- Statistical power limited by small DAP sample

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

*abstract-only — full text not retrieved*

- Lack of direct head-to-head comparisons between all three protocols
- No common comparator available across all arms, preventing more robust network meta-analysis
- Low statistical power due to small DAP sample (n=64, few studies)
- GRADE evidence for DAP rated low due to substantial imprecision
- Future RCTs with direct comparisons and larger DAP arms needed

## 6. Related Work

- REP outcomes research has historically focused on scaffold (blood clot vs PRP vs PRF) and barrier (MTA vs Biodentine) materials; this SR+MA fills the intracanal medicament gap
- TAP = ciprofloxacin + metronidazole + minocycline (classic Hoshino formulation)
- DAP = ciprofloxacin + metronidazole (minocycline omitted to reduce crown discoloration)
- CH = calcium hydroxide paste — alternative for practitioners avoiding antibiotic resistance concerns

## 7. Glossary

- **REP** (Regenerative Endodontic Procedure, 재생근관치료): Protocol aiming to restore pulp–dentin complex vitality in necrotic immature permanent teeth via disinfection + scaffold + biological seal
- **TAP** (Triple Antibiotic Paste, 삼제항생제 페이스트): Ciprofloxacin + metronidazole + minocycline intracanal paste
- **DAP** (Double Antibiotic Paste, 이제항생제 페이스트): Ciprofloxacin + metronidazole (no minocycline)
- **CH** (Calcium Hydroxide, 수산화칼슘): Classic intracanal medicament used as alternative to antibiotic pastes
- **Naïve indirect comparison** (단순 간접 비교): Meta-analytic comparison of arms not directly randomized against each other; less robust than network MA
- **GRADE**: Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation — evidence quality framework
- **RoB2**: Revised Cochrane Risk of Bias tool for randomised trials
- **Periapical healing** (근첨부 치유): Radiographic and/or clinical resolution of periapical pathology — primary REP success criterion
