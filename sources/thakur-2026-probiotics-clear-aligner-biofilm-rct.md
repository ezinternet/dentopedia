## Why Ingested

`clear-aligner` 위생관리 축의 **개입(intervention) 데이터**. SR들이 일관되게 보고하는 "aligner는 치주 우위지만 plaque/biofilm 누적은 여전히 관리 대상"이라는 문제( [[wiki/orthodontics/clear-aligner/alhuwaizi-2026-clear-aligner-fixed-oral-hygiene-periodontal-grade-sr]] 가 위생/치주 등급으로 정립)에 대해, 소비자용 probiotic 4종(드링크·린스·구미·캡슐)이 aligner biofilm(CFU·EPS)을 단기적으로 줄일 수 있는지 직접 시험한 4-period crossover RCT. abstract-only, preliminary.

## One-line Summary

4-period single-blind crossover RCT (n=20 Invisalign users): all four consumer probiotic forms (drink/rinse/gummy/capsule) significantly reduced aligner biofilm viable counts (CFU/mL) and EPS matrix fluorescence vs pre-intervention (P<0.001), CFU reductions 0.62–1.06 log10 with the probiotic drink (Yakult) largest (1.055 log10); preliminary — short duration, label-only verification, no comparison vs mechanical cleaning.

## 한줄요약

4기간 단일맹검 교차 RCT(n=20 Invisalign 사용자): 소비자용 프로바이오틱 4종(드링크·린스·구미·캡슐) 모두 aligner biofilm 생균수(CFU/mL)와 EPS 기질 형광을 개입 전 대비 유의하게 감소(P<0.001), CFU 감소 0.62–1.06 log10로 드링크(야쿠르트)가 최대(1.055 log10). 단기간·라벨만 확인·기계적 세정 비교 없음 → 예비 결과.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First crossover RCT comparing four delivery forms of consumer probiotics for reducing clear aligner biofilm.
- Quantifies both viable bacterial load (CFU/mL, log10) and biofilm matrix (EPS) via CLSM with Concanavalin A staining.
- Ranks delivery forms: probiotic drink > others on CFU reduction.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 20 Invisalign users, four 7-day intervention periods, 14-day washouts between periods.
- Interventions (used as labelled; no independent strain-identity/viability verification):
  1. Probiotic gummy (Bonatona; *Bacillus coagulans*, 1.5×10^9 CFU/dose)
  2. Probiotic rinse (Perfora; proprietary multi-strain, 1.5×10^9 CFU/10 mL)
  3. Probiotic capsule (HealthKart; multi-strain, 3.0×10^10 CFU/dose)
  4. Probiotic drink (Yakult Original; *Lacticaseibacillus paracasei* Shirota, ~6.5×10^9 CFU/65 mL)
- After each period: 5-mm discs from standardized aligner sites analysed for total viable counts (CFU/mL) and for EPS matrix + viability by confocal laser scanning microscopy (CLSM, Concanavalin A staining).
- Analysis: linear mixed-effects models (treatment, period, sequence fixed; subject random intercept); least-squares means (95% CI) with Bonferroni-adjusted contrasts.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- All four probiotic delivery forms significantly reduced total viable counts and EPS fluorescence vs pre-intervention controls (P<0.001).
- CFU reductions 0.62–1.06 log10 (mean reduction 3.8–4.3 ×10^3 CFU/mL).
- Probiotic **drink** largest decrease: LS-mean difference vs baseline = 1.055 log10 CFU (95% CI 0.93–1.18; P<0.001).
- CLSM fluorescence mean differences 93–145 AU (all P<0.001).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short intervention duration (7-day periods) — no durability data.
- Label-only verification of probiotic content (no strain ID / viability check).
- No comparison against routine mechanical cleaning (the current standard) → cannot establish added value over brushing/soaking.
- Small n=20; findings are **preliminary**. Larger, species-resolved studies with longer follow-up and a mechanical-cleaning comparator are needed. Abstract-only ingest.

## 7. Glossary

- **EPS**: extracellular polymeric substances — the biofilm matrix.
- **CLSM**: confocal laser scanning microscopy; Concanavalin A stains glucan-rich EPS.
- **CFU/mL**: colony-forming units per mL (viable bacterial load); log10 reductions reported.
- **Crossover RCT**: each subject receives all interventions in sequence, separated by washout.
- **LS-mean**: least-squares (model-adjusted) mean. **AU**: arbitrary (fluorescence) units.
