## One-line Summary
전향적 RCT 7년 (n=50, 42명): HA 코팅 임플란트 (Zimmer Tapered Screw-Vent) 즉시부하 vs 조기(3주) 부하 — 누적 생존율 98.0% (즉시 100%/조기 95.5%); 2yr↔7yr MBL 차이 없음; 두 프로토콜 동등.

## 2. Key Contributions
The study evaluated 7-year outcomes of single-tooth restorations on hydrothermally treated, plasma-sprayed, partially HA-coated Zimmer Tapered Screw-Vent implants under immediate (Group A) vs early (3-week, Group B) loading. 42 patients received 50 implants; 34 patients (42 implants) completed 7-year follow-up. One implant failed at 3 weeks.

## 3. Methodology
Postgraduate Periodontics Clinic, LSU. Randomization to immediate vs early (3-week) loading. Continuous follow-up with periodic maintenance and radiographic evaluation. Primary outcome: implant survival (Kaplan-Meier). Secondary: peri-implant crestal bone level changes. Multiple regression for bone-loss predictors (age, sex, bone quality, location, length/diameter, prior augmentation).

## 4. Key Results
- Survival 7yr: Group A 100%, Group B 95.5%, overall 98.0%.
- One implant lost at 3 weeks (failed osseointegration).
- No significant difference in MBL between 2-year and 7-year follow-ups.
- No group differences in implant location, bone type, length, diameter, or augmentation status.

## 5. Clinical Relevance
플라즈마 분사 + 열수처리 부분 HA 코팅 임플란트는 단일치 치환에서 즉시부하·조기(3주)부하 모두 7년 생존율 95% 이상으로 임상 예측 가능. HA 코팅 장기 안정성을 뒷받침하는 드문 7년 데이터.
