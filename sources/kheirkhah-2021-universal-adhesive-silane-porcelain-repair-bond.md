## Why Ingested

유니버설 접착제가 실란을 포함하고 있어도 별도의 실란 적용이 장석계 도재 수리 접착강도를 향상시키는지 검증한 논문으로, [[resin-bonding/dimitriadi-2019-silane-reactivity-lithium-disilicate-bond-strength]]의 실란 화학적 반응성 분석을 임상적 수리 시나리오에서 실용적으로 확장한다. 유니버설 접착제 사용 시 별도 실란 적용 여부에 대한 임상 의사결정에 직접 기여한다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro (n=70 feldspathic porcelain disks, 7 groups, shear bond strength after thermocycling): Separate silane application after HF etching significantly increased repair bond strength for Single Bond Universal and All Bond Universal, but not for FuturaBond; FuturaBond with or without silane showed comparable strength.

## 한줄요약

In vitro (n=70, 7그룹, SBS, 5,000 열순환): Single Bond와 All Bond 유니버설 접착제 사용 시 별도 실란 추가로 접착강도 유의하게 향상; FuturaBond는 실란 추가 효과 없음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrated that separate silane application enhances repair bond strength even when using silane-containing universal adhesives (for Single Bond Universal and All Bond Universal)
- FuturaBond Universal showed no significant benefit from additional separate silane (silane in FuturaBond may be more effectively incorporated)
- Challenges the assumption that silane-containing universal adhesives eliminate the need for separate silane priming in ceramic repair
- All groups received HF etching (9.5%), ensuring mechanical pretreatment as baseline
- Mixed failure mode was predominant across all groups

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Design**: Experimental in vitro study.

**Material**: 70 feldspathic porcelain disks (Ceramco 3, Dentsply), 6 mm × 2 mm, polished with SiC disks (400, 600 grit). All specimens etched with 9.5% HF (Porcelain Etchant, Bisco) for 2 min.

**Groups (n=10 each)**:
- **C (Control)**: HF + Bis-Silane (Bisco) + Porcelain Bond (Bisco)
- **HSB**: HF + Single Bond Universal (3M ESPE) [silane-containing adhesive without separate silane]
- **HSSB**: HF + Bis-Silane + Single Bond Universal
- **HAB**: HF + All Bond Universal (Bisco) [silane-containing adhesive without separate silane]
- **HSAB**: HF + Bis-Silane + All Bond Universal
- **HFB**: HF + FuturaBond Universal (Voco) [silane-containing adhesive without separate silane]
- **HSFB**: HF + Bis-Silane + FuturaBond Universal

**Composite**: Filtek Z250 (3M ESPE) in Tygon tubes (3 mm × 4 mm, 2 mm thick), light-cured 40 s.

**Aging**: Distilled water, 37°C, 24 h → 5,000 thermocycles (5–55°C, 30 s dwell, 10 s transfer).

**Testing**: Shear bond strength (SBS) in universal testing machine (ZwickRoell Z050), 1 mm/min crosshead speed.

**Failure mode**: Stereomicroscope (×20); SEM for representative specimens.

**Statistics**: One-way ANOVA, Tukey's post-hoc test (IBM SPSS v. 21.0).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Significant overall difference between groups (p < 0.0001)
- **HSSB > HSB** (p < 0.001): Separate silane + Single Bond > Single Bond alone
- **HSAB > HAB** (p < 0.001): Separate silane + All Bond > All Bond alone
- **HSFB vs HFB**: No significant difference — FuturaBond's silane incorporation appears sufficient
- Mixed failure mode predominant in all groups
- FuturaBond groups (HFB, HSFB) showed comparable results regardless of silane

**Clinical implication**: For most universal adhesives (Single Bond Universal, All Bond Universal), a separate silane step after HF etching remains beneficial and should not be skipped, even though these adhesives contain silane.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short-term aging only (5,000 thermocycles; clinical simulation limited)
- Shear bond strength (SBS) geometry less standardized than μTBS for ceramic substrates
- Single feldspathic ceramic material tested (Ceramco 3)
- Results limited to repair scenario (aged, polished ceramic surface); may differ from new ceramic bonding
- Product-specific findings may not generalize to other universal adhesive brands

## 7. Glossary

- **Universal adhesive (유니버설 접착제)**: 실란 포함 범용 접착 시스템; 지르코니아·세라믹·금속·수복물 전 기질에 적용 가능
- **Bis-Silane (비스-실란)**: 2-병 실란 커플링제 (Part A+B 혼합 활성화); 도자 표면 화학결합 형성
- **SBS (Shear Bond Strength, 전단접착강도)**: 전단력 적용하는 접착강도 측정법
- **HF etching (불화수소산 에칭)**: 도자 표면의 유리상 선택적 용해; 미세기계적 유지력 제공
- **Mixed failure (혼합파괴)**: 접착면(adhesive)과 재료 내부(cohesive) 동시 파괴; 최적 접착력 지표
- **FuturaBond (Voco)**: 실란 포함 유니버설 접착제; 단독 적용 시 효율적 실란 통합으로 별도 실란 불필요 가능
- **Thermocycling (열순환)**: 접착 내구성 시험 표준; 5°C/55°C 온도 반복 노출
