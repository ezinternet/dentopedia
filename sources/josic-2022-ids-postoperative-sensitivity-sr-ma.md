## One-line Summary

JERD 34(1):55-64, 2022 — Josic·Breschi et al. (Bologna·Belgrade·Bristol) SR+MA — PRISMA·PICOS, 4 clinical trials 정성, 2 정량 분석. RoB-2 "some concerns", GRADE very-low/low. IDS vs DDS의 POS 차이 P>.05 (baseline·2년). 결론: "IDS does NOT reduce POS" — clinical significance: no evidence to favor IDS over DDS.

## 1. Document Information

저자: Uros Josic (Bologna·Belgrade), Maicon Sebold·Rodrigo B.E. Lins (Campinas·Paraiba), Jelena Savovic (Bristol), Claudia Mazzitelli·Tatjana Maravic·Annalisa Mazzoni·Lorenzo Breschi (Bologna).
저널: Journal of Esthetic and Restorative Dentistry 34(1):55-64, 2022.
DOI: 10.1111/jerd.12841.
유형: Systematic review + meta-analysis (PRISMA·PICOS).

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **IDS POS 효과의 임상 evidence 부족 polemic** — Magne 2005 등 narrative claim ("reduced sensitivity")이 in-vivo RCT로 재현되지 않음.
2. **GRADE downgrading**: very-low (baseline) → low (2y follow-up) — RoB + heterogeneity + small sample 문제.
3. **임상 메시지의 신중성** — "literature reports higher bond strengths with IDS" (in-vitro) ≠ "lower POS" (clinical).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 검색·screening: PRISMA + PICOS.
- 포함기준: 성인 환자, IDS vs DDS clinical trial (각 환자당 ≥1 indirect restoration).
- Primary outcome: POS.
- Secondary outcome: survival, retention, marginal adaptation/discoloration, surface roughness 등.
- 4 studies → qualitative, 2 studies → quantitative meta-analysis.
- RoB-2 risk of bias tool, GRADE certainty assessment.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- POS at baseline: IDS vs DDS, P>.05, GRADE very-low.
- POS at 2-year follow-up: IDS vs DDS, P>.05, GRADE low.
- RoB-2: 2 studies "some concerns" for POS outcome.
- Quantitative pooling: 2 studies만 → power 낮음, 광범위 CI.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- 포함 clinical trial 4편만, 정량 2편 — under-powered.
- 다양한 indirect restoration material (ceramic·composite) heterogeneity.
- POS 측정 timing·도구 (VAS·Schiff scale) 표준화 부재.
- Long-term RCT (5y+) 부재 — alghauli-2025도 동일 한계 보고.

## 7. Glossary

- PICOS (Population·Intervention·Comparison·Outcome·Study design): 체계적 문헌고찰 검색 framework.
- RoB-2 (Risk of Bias 2, Cochrane): RCT 비뚤림 위험 평가 도구.
- GRADE (Grading of Recommendations, Assessment, Development, and Evaluation): 근거 등급 평가 framework — very-low/low/moderate/high.
- POS (Postoperative Sensitivity, 술후 민감).
