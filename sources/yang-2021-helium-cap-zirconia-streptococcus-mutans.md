## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치 2차(2026-05-31)에서 **항균효과의 시간감쇠(time-decay)**를 정량적으로 보여주는 지르코니아 근거. [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/lee-2022-plasma-contaminated-zirconia-gingivalis-osteoblast]]의 비단조 용량반응과 함께, 플라즈마 제염 이득이 일시적임을 보강해 [[wiki/overviews/plasma-surface-treatment-dental-implants]]의 한정 시나리오를 강화.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study on zirconia: helium cold atmospheric plasma (CAP) increased O/C ratio and hydrophilicity with good biocompatibility, and inhibited early S. mutans adhesion/biofilm via reactive oxygen species — but the antibacterial effect lasted only ~24 h, was attenuated at 48 h, and disappeared by 72 h after treatment.

## 한줄요약

지르코니아 in vitro: 헬륨 저온대기압 플라즈마(CAP)는 O/C 비·친수성을 높이고(생체적합성 양호) 반응성 산소종으로 S. mutans 초기 부착·바이오필름을 억제했으나, 항균효과는 약 24시간만 지속되고 48시간에 약화, 72시간에 소실되었다.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Materials Science & Engineering C 2021, 120:111633
- DOI: 10.1016/j.msec.2020.111633
- Study type: in vitro (surface + antibacterial, time-course)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Quantifies the time-decay of plasma's antibacterial effect (24 h → gone by 72 h).
- Links effect to reactive oxygen species; confirms biocompatibility and O/C↑.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Helium CAP on zirconia; surface chemistry/microstructure, cytotoxicity; S. mutans cultured at intervals after CAP.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- ↑ O/C ratio and hydrophilicity; good biocompatibility.
- Inhibited early S. mutans adhesion and biofilm formation.
- Antibacterial effect lasted ~24 h, attenuated at 48 h, disappeared at 72 h.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro; antibacterial benefit transient → timing of placement relative to treatment matters.
- Single species (S. mutans); no in vivo/clinical data.
