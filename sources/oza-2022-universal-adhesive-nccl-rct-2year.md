## One-line Summary
RCT (234 NCCLs, 34 patients, 24 months): Clearfil Universal Bond Quick in SE mode — clinically unacceptable (6/234 lost); SLE and E&R modes acceptable; Clearfil SE Bond 4/234 lost; pure SE mode inferior for universal adhesive.

## 1. Document Information
Hacettepe University, Ankara, Turkey. Journal of Adhesive Dentistry 2022. 2-year 5-arm RCT on universal adhesive application modes in NCCLs. Largest arm count among bonding RCTs.

## 2. Key Contributions
- "No wait" universal adhesive (Clearfil Universal Bond Quick) vs conventional SE Bond and E&R adhesive
- Key finding: CUQ in **SE mode is clinically unacceptable** at 24 months
- CUQ in **SLE (selective enamel etch) and E&R mode: acceptable**
- Confirms that SE mode of universal adhesives is insufficient for enamel-rich NCCL margins
- MDP monomer in SE mode: insufficient enamel etching → marginal deterioration

## 3. Methodology
- 234 NCCLs in 34 patients; 5-arm parallel RCT
- Groups: CUQ-SE / CUQ-SLE / CUQ-ER / Clearfil SE Bond (CSEB) / Tetric N-Bond Universal E&R (TBU-ER)
- Composite: Tetric N-Ceram (nanohybrid)
- Evaluation: baseline, 6, 12, 24 months
- Criteria: modified USPHS (retention, marginal adaptation, discoloration, caries, sensitivity, color, texture)
- Recall rate at 24 months: 73.5%

## 4. Key Results
| Group | Retention loss at 24mo |
|---|---|
| CUQ-SE | **6 restorations lost** — unacceptable |
| CSEB (SE Bond) | 4 restorations lost |
| TBU-ER | 1 restoration lost |
| CUQ-SLE | Acceptable |
| CUQ-ER | Acceptable |

- Marginal adaptation/discoloration: CUQ-SE and CSEB more bravo scores (p<0.05)
- Secondary caries, sensitivity, color, texture: NS among all groups

## 5. Limitations
- 73.5% recall rate at 24mo — some dropout
- Single-center Turkey
- Short 24-month follow-up for definitive durability conclusions
- NCCL only — cannot extrapolate to Class I/II restorations

## 6. Related Work
- Mortazavi 2012 — 12-month RCT: SE Bond vs E&R vs ethanol wet bonding in NCCLs
- Tay & Pashley 2003 — theoretical basis: SE adhesives insufficient enamel etching
- Sharifian 2023 — in vitro: universal E&R mode > SE mode for µTBS (consistent with clinical finding)

## 7. Glossary
- **NCCL**: Noncarious cervical lesion
- **CUQ**: Clearfil Universal Bond Quick (Kuraray) — "no wait" (no 20-sec active application required)
- **SLE**: Selective enamel etch — phosphoric acid on enamel only, SE on dentin
- **MDP**: 10-Methacryloyloxydecyl dihydrogen phosphate (acidic monomer in Kuraray adhesives)
- **USPHS**: United States Public Health Service clinical evaluation criteria
