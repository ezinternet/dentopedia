## Why Ingested

Supplies the pharmacology backbone (mechanisms, resistance, pipeline) behind the clinical HSV regimens summarized in [[oral-medicine/mancini-2025-oral-herpes-treatment-sr]] and [[oral-medicine/coppola-2023-herpetic-gingivostomatitis-sr]].

## One-line Summary

Narrative pharmacology review of anti-HSV-1 agents over ~60 years — nucleoside/nucleotide analogs (aciclovir family), helicase-primase inhibitors, immunomodulators — framing the drug-resistance problem and emerging therapeutic directions.

## 1. Document Information

- Frontiers in Pharmacology 2025;15:1451083. Received 2024-06-18, accepted 2024-11-27, published 2025-01-21.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Updates the mechanistic landscape of approved and investigational anti-HSV-1 agents.
- Centers the clinical problem of nucleoside-analog resistance (thymidine-kinase/DNA-polymerase mutations).
- Surveys helicase-primase inhibitors (e.g., pritelivir/amenamevir) and immunomodulatory/natural-product candidates.
- Notes the absence of an approved HSV vaccine as the rationale for continued drug development.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Narrative review of antiviral pharmacology and HSV-1 replication mechanism (binding/entry via gB, gC, gD, heparan sulfate; latency in sensory ganglia).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Global HSV-1 seroprevalence ~64% in under-50s. Nucleoside analogs remain first-line despite resistance.
- Helicase-primase inhibitors offer a non-nucleoside mechanism with activity against resistant strains.
- Emerging strategies: immunomodulation, natural compounds, gene-editing/latency-targeting approaches.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Narrative scope; not oral-disease-specific; clinical efficacy of novel agents largely preclinical/early-phase.

## 6. Related Work

- Looker et al. global HSV seroprevalence estimates.
- Mechanistic HSV entry and latency literature.

## 7. Glossary

- HSV-1: Herpes Simplex Virus type 1.
- HPI: Helicase-Primase Inhibitor.
- gB/gC/gD: viral glycoproteins B/C/D; HS: Heparan Sulfate.
