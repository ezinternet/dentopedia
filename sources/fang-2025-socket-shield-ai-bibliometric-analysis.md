## Why Ingested

소켓 쉴드(Socket Shield Technique, SST) 연구의 15년(2010–2024) 학술 지형을 AI 보조 계량서지학으로 지도화 — 어느 임상 근거 페이지가 분야에서 차지하는 위치를 파악하는 메타 맥락. 임상 결과를 다룬 [[immediate-implant/gharpure-2022-socket-shield-conventional-implant-sr-ma]]·[[immediate-implant/blaschke-2020-socket-shield-technique-critical-review]] 위에 얹는 연구 동향 레이어.

## One-line Summary

AI-assisted bibliometric analysis of 157 socket-shield-technique articles (Scopus, 2010–2024) mapping a steadily rising output (especially post-2015), with the USA leading total and international collaborative publications, China most single-country publications, and Germany the highest total and average citations.

## 한줄요약

소켓 쉴드 논문 157편(Scopus, 2010–2024)을 AI 보조 계량서지학으로 분석 — 2015년 이후 출판이 꾸준히 증가, 미국이 총·국제공동 출판 1위, 중국이 단일국가 출판 최다, 독일이 총·평균 피인용 최고.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: The Journal of Prosthetic Dentistry (in press; PII S0022-3913(25)00456-1).
- University of Illinois Chicago / Kansas State / Sakarya / Loma Linda / University of Pittsburgh.
- Systematic-review-format bibliometric study (Scopus 2010–2024, R bibliometrix, VOSviewer, Python; AI-assisted screening + manual expert verification).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First dedicated bibliometric map of the socket shield technique literature.
- Demonstrates an AI-assisted (Scopus API) screening + expert-verification workflow for evidence mapping.
- Identifies leading countries, authors, and the durable influence of early conceptual work.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Metadata query of Scopus (2010–2024); AI-assisted screening and automated metadata extraction via Scopus API; manual verification by domain experts.
- Bibliometric analysis with R bibliometrix; visualization with VOSviewer and Python.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 157 articles identified; output rising steadily, especially after 2015.
- USA: highest overall and collaborative international publication volume.
- China: most single-country publications.
- Germany: highest total and average citations.
- Early conceptual work (Hürzeler 2010) retains lasting impact.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Bibliometric (not clinical-outcome) evidence; Scopus-only; citation metrics ≠ clinical validity.
- AI screening requires manual verification; English-language/indexing bias.

## 6. Related Work

- Hürzeler et al. (2010) original socket shield; socket-shield outcome SRs and critical reviews.

## 7. Glossary

- **SST**: socket shield technique (partial root retention to preserve buccal bone).
- **Bibliometric analysis**: quantitative study of publication and citation patterns.
