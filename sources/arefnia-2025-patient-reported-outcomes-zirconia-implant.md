## Why Ingested

지르코니아 임플란트 근거가 생존율·MBL([[implants/mohseni-2024-clinical-outcomes-zirconia-implants]], [[implants/pieralli-2017-clinical-outcomes-zirconia-dental-implants]]) 중심이라 환자 관점이 비어 있었음. 본 SR은 PRO/PROM 축을 채워 지르코니아 임플란트 evidence를 환자보고결과 카테고리로 확장.

## One-line Summary
Systematic review (12 controlled studies) — patients report high satisfaction and improved aesthetics/comfort/chewing with zirconia implants, but PROM instruments are highly heterogeneous.

## 한줄요약
체계적문헌고찰(대조연구 12편): 지르코니아 임플란트 환자는 심미·편안함·저작 등에서 높은 만족도를 보고하나, 사용된 환자보고측정도구(PROM)는 매우 이질적.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal of Patient-Reported Outcomes 2025;9:7. Medical University of Graz. PRISMA, 6 databases.
- MINORS bias scoring for nonrandomized studies.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First SR focused specifically on patient-reported outcomes for zirconia implants.
- Documents consistent PRO improvement from pretreatment to prosthetic delivery.
- Flags lack of standardized PROM instruments as the main evidence weakness.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PRISMA systematic review; 596 records → 189 screened → 12 controlled clinical trials included.
- Extracted PROMs, follow-up, survival, implant system, MINORS bias score, evidence level. English-only, controlled trials.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Most PROMs (aesthetics, speaking, comfort, chewing ability, general satisfaction) significantly improved at prosthetic delivery vs pretreatment.
- High patient satisfaction overall.
- High heterogeneity across PROM instruments precluded pooling.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Few controlled studies; heterogeneous, non-validated PROM tools; possible publication/selection bias; no meta-analysis. Need standardized zirconia-implant PROMs.

## 6. Related Work
- Zirconia implant survival SRs (Mohseni 2024, Pieralli 2017); OHRQoL/PROM methodology.

## 7. Glossary
- PRO: patient-reported outcome. PROM: patient-reported outcome measure. MINORS: Methodological Index for Non-Randomized Studies.
