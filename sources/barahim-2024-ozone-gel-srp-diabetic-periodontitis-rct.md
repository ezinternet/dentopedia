## Why Ingested

기존 [[wiki/periodontics/deepthi-2020-ozone-therapy-periodontics-meta-analysis]]는 일반(비당뇨) 환자에서 SRP(scaling and root planing) 부가 오존이 PPD·CAL에 소폭·대부분 비유의한 효과만 있다고 보고했다. 본 Barahim 2024 RCT는 그 결론을 **controlled type 2 DM (HbA1c<7) + stage III periodontitis**라는 고위험 하위집단에 적용·검증하며, 임상 파라미터의 intergroup 무차이는 재확인하되 방사선학적 골결손(RLDD·DA·PDL space)에서 오존군에서만 유의한 intra-group 개선을 보여 deepthi-2020을 당뇨 맥락으로 확장한다. 또한 [[wiki/periodontics/simpson-2022-treatment-periodontitis-glycaemic-control-people]] 및 [[wiki/periodontics/baeza-2020-effect-periodontal-treatment-patients-periodontitis]]의 "NSPT가 당뇨 대사조절(HbA1c)을 개선한다"는 결론과 짝을 이뤄, 오존 부가 여부와 무관하게 양 군 모두 6개월에 HbA1c가 유의하게 감소한 점을 추가 근거로 제공한다.

## One-line Summary

Egyptian RCT (n=24; 12 SRP+ozone gel vs 12 SRP alone) in controlled T2DM + stage III grade B periodontitis: both groups improved CAL/PPD/BOP/PI/HbA1c with NO significant intergroup difference, but the SRP+Ozone group uniquely showed significant intra-group radiographic improvement (RLDD, defect angle, PDL space) and significantly greater PPD reduction at 3 months and lower postoperative VAS pain.

## 한줄요약

조절된 제2형 당뇨 + stage III periodontitis 환자 24명(SRP+오존젤 12 vs SRP 단독 12) RCT — 양 군 모두 CAL·PPD·BOP·PI·HbA1c가 개선됐으나 군간 차이는 없었고, 다만 오존군에서만 방사선학적 지표(RLDD·결손각·치주인대공간)가 유의하게 개선되고 3개월 PPD 감소와 술후 통증(VAS)이 유의하게 더 좋았다.

## 1. Document Information

- **Title**: Clinical and radiographic evaluation of non-surgical therapy with and without ozone gel application in controlled type 2 diabetic patients with periodontitis: a randomized controlled clinical trial
- **Authors**: Abeer Abubaker Barahim, Nesma Shemais, Arwa Mousa, Mona Darhous (Faculty of Dentistry, Cairo University, Egypt)
- **Journal**: BMC Oral Health 2024;24:1435
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s12903-024-05212-7
- **Trial registration**: NCT05538078 (registered 09/09/2022)
- **Study type**: Two-arm parallel randomized controlled clinical trial, assessor- and statistician-blinded (single-center)

## 2. Key Contributions

- First RCT to evaluate subgingival **ozonated gel** (oily/oil-based ozone, Geli O3, bio-ozonized olive oil 20 mEq O2/Kg) as an adjunct to SRP in **controlled type 2 diabetic** patients with stage III grade B periodontitis, including both clinical AND radiographic intrabony-defect outcomes.
- Uses a **customized acrylic stent** to hold the gel in the pocket ≥30 min (vs the ~2 min contact in prior studies), and an **oily ozone** vehicle (vs unstable gaseous ozone), proposed as the reason for the favorable PPD/radiographic signal.
- Adds VAS dentinal-hypersensitivity (pain) and HbA1c outcomes to the ozone-SRP evidence base in a diabetic cohort.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Population**: 24 patients (18 F, 6 M), controlled T2DM (HbA1c < 7), stage III grade B periodontitis (interdental CAL ≥ 5 mm, radiographic bone loss to middle/apical third, PD ≥ 6 mm). Recruited March–October 2023, Cairo University. Excluded: smokers, pregnant, recent perio/antibiotic/anti-inflammatory therapy, mobility > grade I, prosthetic crowns.
- **Groups (1:1, n=12 each)**: Group I = SRP + ozone gel (test); Group II = SRP alone (control).
- **Intervention**: One-visit full-mouth SRP (ultrasonic + Gracey/universal curettes). Test group: 1–1.5 mL ozonated gel applied subgingivally/interproximally via syringe + acrylic stent, held ≥30 min. No antibiotics, antiplaque agents, or anti-inflammatories given.
- **Sample size**: G*Power, SD 1.09 mm, effect size d=1.5, α=0.05, power 0.8 → 9/group, inflated to 12/group for 15% dropout.
- **Blinding**: outcome assessors (NS, AM) and biostatistician blinded; PI and participants not blinded.
- **Outcomes**: Primary = CAL. Secondary = PPD, full-mouth plaque index (PI%), full-mouth bleeding (BOP%), dentinal hypersensitivity (VAS), radiographic linear defect depth (RLDD, CEJ–BD), radiographic defect angle (DA), PDL space width. Clinical at baseline/3/6 mo; radiographic (periapical, standardized bite-plate, ImageJ) at baseline/6 mo; HbA1c at baseline/6 mo; VAS at baseline/1 week.
- **Statistics**: parametric (CAL, PD, age, HbA1c) repeated-measures ANOVA + Bonferroni; non-parametric (BOP%, PI%, VAS, RLDD, DA, PDL) Mann–Whitney/Friedman/Dunn/Wilcoxon. P ≤ 0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Baseline**: no significant intergroup differences. Mean age 46.7±4.7 (SRP) vs 44.3±5.5 (SRP+Ozone).

**Clinical (intra-group all p<0.001; intergroup p in parentheses):**

| Variable | SRP alone | SRP + Ozone | Intergroup p |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAL 0 m (mm) | 6.75 ± 1.22 | 7.5 ± 1.78 | 0.141 |
| CAL 6 m (mm) | 3.7 ± 1.06 | 4.11 ± 1.76 | 0.541 |
| CAL change 6 m | 3.2 ± 1.14 | 3.89 ± 1.76 | 0.287 |
| PPD 0 m (mm) | 6.1 ± 0.32 | 6.44 ± 0.53 | 0.098 |
| PPD 6 m (mm) | 3.1 ± 1.1 | 3.11 ± 0.78 | 0.980 |
| **PPD reduction 3 m** | 2.25 ± 1.29 | 3.3 ± 0.67 | **0.045\*** |
| BOP% 6 m | 10.6 ± 6.8 | 9.3 ± 7.6 | 0.869 |
| PI% 6 m | 16.2 ± 6.6 | 19 ± 8.8 | 0.485 |
| **VAS 1 week** | 2.92 ± 1.62 | 1.42 ± 1 | **0.017\*** |
| HbA1c% 0 m | 6.81 ± 0.13 | 6.79 ± 0.14 | 0.733 |
| HbA1c% 6 m | 6.56 ± 0.2 | 6.52 ± 0.16 | 0.656 |

**Radiographic (baseline → 6 mo; within-group p):**

| Variable | SRP alone (within p) | SRP + Ozone (within p) | Intergroup p (6 m) |
|---|---|---|---|
| RLDD (mm) | 3.63→3.42 (p=0.888, NS) | 3.44→2.66 (**p=0.008**) | 0.220 |
| Defect angle (°) | 68.3→66.8 (p=0.092, NS) | 62.8→70 (**p=0.007**) | 0.870 |
| PDL space (mm) | 0.92→1.0 (p=0.593, NS) | 0.28→0.2 (**p=0.008**) | 0.014\* (intergroup) |

- **Key pattern**: significant intra-group improvement over time in BOTH groups for clinical/glycemic outcomes, but **no significant intergroup difference** at any time point — except PPD reduction at 3 months (p=0.045) and VAS pain at 1 week (p=0.017) favoring ozone.
- **Radiographic divergence**: only the SRP+Ozone group achieved significant within-group improvement in RLDD, DA, and PDL space; SRP alone showed none. SRP-alone RLDD change −0.21±0.42 mm (NS).
- **HbA1c** fell significantly in both groups at 6 months, no intergroup difference.
- Regression: no variable was a significant CAL predictor at 6 months. No adverse effects reported.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small sample (n=12/group); underpowered for intergroup contrasts → most "no difference" findings may be type II error.
- Single center, single operator (unblinded PI/participants), short 6-month follow-up; radiographs only at baseline/6 mo.
- Only controlled T2DM (HbA1c < 7); results may not generalize to poorly controlled diabetics.
- Authors note scarce comparable literature (ozone in diabetic periodontitis) and call for studies on ozone mechanism, optimal dosage, frequency, application method, and bone-density changes.

## 7. Glossary

- **SRP**: Scaling and root planing — mechanical non-surgical debridement.
- **NSPT**: Non-surgical periodontal therapy.
- **CAL**: Clinical attachment level (CEJ to base of sulcus).
- **PPD/PD**: Probing pocket depth.
- **RLDD**: Radiographic linear defect depth (CEJ to bottom of defect).
- **DA**: Radiographic defect angle of the intrabony defect.
- **PDL space**: Periodontal ligament space width (radiographic).
- **HbA1c**: Glycated hemoglobin (glycemic control marker).
- **VAS**: Visual analogue scale (dentinal hypersensitivity / pain).
- **Ozonated gel**: Oil-based (bio-ozonized olive oil) ozone delivery vehicle.
