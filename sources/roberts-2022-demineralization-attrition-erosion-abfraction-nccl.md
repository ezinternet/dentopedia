## Why Ingested
NCCL을 골다공증·우식과 같은 "demineralization disease" 스펙트럼에 위치시키는 병태생리 review. [[nccl/goodacre-2022-nccl-morphology-etiology-restoration-guidelines]](공저자 Roberts 중복)의 병인 섹션에 생체역학·percolation 기전의 기초를 제공.

## One-line Summary
Narrative review reframing NCCLs, caries, and osteoporosis as a unified demineralization disease driven by acid, biomechanics, and intercrystalline fluid percolation.

## 한줄요약
NCCL·우식·골다공증을 acid·biomechanics·percolation 3요인의 통합 demineralization 질환으로 재개념화한 병태생리 review.

## 1. Document Information
- Current Osteoporosis Reports 2022;20:90-105. Published online 2022-02-07. DOI 10.1007/s11914-022-00722-1.
- Part I of a two-part series (Part II covers infectious demineralization / caries). Open access.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Unifies skeletal and dental demineralization under shared physiology: cell-mediated apposition, protein matrix, fluid logistics, intercrystalline ion percolation, cyclic demin/remin, acid-based degradation.
- Frames NCCL formation as cervical catabolic modeling (clastic-cell mediated) analogous to bone resorption, plus acid + mechanical flexure.
- Emphasizes fluoride percolation and intercrystalline fluid flow as regulators of demineralization resistance.
- Contextualizes prevalence: dental demineralization disorders 85-100% lifetime; osteopenia ~55%, OP >10%.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Narrative review (Topical Collection on Craniofacial Skeleton). No systematic protocol.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Mandibular remodeling rate ~44%/yr (3x femur) due to high bending/torsion loads.
- Attrition/abrasion/erosion/abfraction diagnosed visually; subsurface caries <400 µm undetectable clinically.
- Dental erosion, abrasion, abfraction described as dual-etiology (acidic demineralization + mechanical flexure).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Conceptual/biomechanical framing; limited direct clinical NCCL outcome data.
- Piezoelectric/percolation contributions to NCCLs remain mechanistically argued rather than clinically quantified.

## 6. Related Work
- Grippo 2012 (biocorrosion), Goodacre 2022 (NCCL clinical guidelines, shared author), osteoporosis demineralization literature.

## 7. Glossary
- Percolation: intercrystalline fluid flow through hard-tissue microporosity mediating ion exchange.
- Catabolic modeling: clastic-cell-mediated resorptive change (cervical / root resorption).
- HA: hydroxyapatite.
