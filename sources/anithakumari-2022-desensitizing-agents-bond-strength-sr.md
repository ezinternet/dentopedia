## Why Ingested

탈감작제 도포 후 dentin bonding agent의 결합강도가 떨어지는지에 대한 controversial 질문을 SR로 정리. [[resin-bonding/cotert-2024-desensitizing-agents-zirconia-bond-strength]] 단일 실험을 상위 근거로 묶고, [[overviews/adhesive-bonding-strategies-overview]]의 표면처리 단락에 연결.

## One-line Summary
Systematic review (23 in vitro studies, human teeth, 2010–2020) of how various desensitizing agents affect the bond strength of dentin bonding agents, where effects remain controversial and agent/timing-dependent.

## 한줄요약
SR(in vitro 23편·사람치아·2010–2020): 탈감작제가 dentin bonding agent의 결합강도에 미치는 영향을 정리 — 제제·도포시점에 따라 결과 상충.

## 1. Document Information
- J Conserv Dent 2022;25(6):580–587. PMC9795680 / PMID 36591577.

## 2. Key Contributions
- PICO-structured SR isolating desensitizer effect on dentin-adhesive bond strength.
- Modified Cochrane RoB assessment of 23 in vitro studies.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PubMed, Cochrane, Google Scholar; 2010–2020; in vitro human-tooth bond-strength studies after desensitizer application.
- 146 identified → 23 included; customized extraction; modified Cochrane Collaboration tool.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Effect is agent- and protocol-dependent; some desensitizers reduce, some preserve or increase bond strength — no uniform direction.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- In vitro only; heterogeneous adhesives/desensitizers; controversial net effect.

## 6. Related Work
- Umbrella for single-study SBS experiments such as the zirconia desensitizer study.

## 7. Glossary
- Dentin bonding agent: adhesive resin coupling restorative to dentin.
- PICO: Population-Intervention-Comparison-Outcome.
