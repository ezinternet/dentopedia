## Why Ingested

처음으로 초음파 탄성영상(USE)을 이용해 BoNT-A 후 교근의 미세구조 변화(두께 vs 탄성)를 시계열로 정량화. 탄성 회복이 6개월에 기저선으로 돌아오므로 재주사 타이밍 결정에 직접 임상 적용 가능. 두께 회복과 탄성 회복이 비상관적이라는 발견은 서로 다른 회복 기전을 시사하며, [[botulinum-toxin/eberlikose-2026-botulinum-toxin-mandibular-bone-bruxism]]의 골 리모델링 연구와 보완 관계.

## One-line Summary

Prospective self-controlled study (n=14, 28 masseter muscles) using ultrasound elastography showed BoNT-A caused 28.9–31.5% thickness reduction at 1–3 months (partial recovery 47.4% at 6mo) and decreased elasticity (shear wave velocity) at 1–3 months that fully returned to baseline by 6 months; thickness and elasticity recovery were uncorrelated. Abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

초음파 탄성영상을 이용한 전향 자기대조 연구(n=14, 28개 교근)에서 BoNT-A 후 교근 두께가 1–3개월에 28.9–31.5% 감소(6개월에 47.4% 부분 회복)하고, 전단파 속도로 측정한 탄성도는 1–3개월 감소 후 6개월에 기저선으로 완전 회복; 두께와 탄성 회복은 비상관. 초록만 수집.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First human study using **ultrasound elastography (USE)** to track BoNT-A-induced masseter microstructural changes over time
- Key finding: elasticity (shear wave velocity) **fully recovers to baseline by 6 months** despite only partial volume recovery (47.4%)
- Thickness and elasticity recovery are **uncorrelated** — supports two distinct mechanisms (atrophy vs fibrosis/stiffness)
- Clinical implication: USE elasticity can serve as objective readiness marker for re-injection timing
- No evidence of fibrosis (elasticity at 6 months ≥ baseline); supports long-term safety narrative

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Prospective self-controlled trial (each subject = own control)
- **Sample**: n=14 subjects (28 masseter muscles), aged 21–27 years
- **Intervention**: BoNT-A injection into masseter (dose not specified in abstract)
- **Imaging method**: Ultrasound elastography (USE) — shear wave velocity (V median, m/s)
- **Timepoints**: Baseline, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months post-injection
- **Measurements**: Masseter thickness (mm) + V median (shear wave velocity, m/s)
- **Statistics**: Paired t-tests, Spearman correlation

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Masseter Thickness:**
- Decrease: **28.9–31.5%** at 1–3 months (p ≤ 0.05)
- Recovery at 6 months: **47.4% partial recovery** only (not back to baseline)

**Elasticity (Shear Wave Velocity, V median):**
- Baseline: 2.53 ± 0.46 m/s
- 1–3 months: 2.10 ± 0.45 m/s (decreased, p ≤ 0.05)
- **6 months: 2.70 ± 0.68 m/s (p > 0.05 vs baseline — FULL RECOVERY)**

**Key correlation:**
- Thickness and elasticity changes: **uncorrelated** (Spearman correlation NS) → distinct recovery mechanisms

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract only — dose, number of injection sites, injection landmarks not detailed
- Very small sample (n=14); young adults (21–27 yr) — may not generalize to older patients or bruxism patients
- Self-controlled design: no independent control arm (seasonal variation, measurement variability)
- Shear wave velocity units vary by machine; cross-study comparison limited without standardization
- Future: bruxism patients specifically; dose-response elasticity analysis; >6 months tracking

## 7. Glossary

- **USE** (Ultrasound Elastography, 초음파탄성영상): imaging technique measuring tissue stiffness/elasticity non-invasively
- **Shear wave velocity** (전단파 속도, V median, m/s): surrogate for tissue elastic modulus; higher = stiffer tissue
- **Masseter thickness** (교근 두께): B-mode ultrasound measure of muscle volume proxy
- **Atrophy** (위축): reduction in muscle fiber size/number → decreased thickness
- **Fibrosis** (섬유화): replacement of muscle tissue by collagen → increased stiffness; NOT detected in this study (elasticity returned to baseline)
- **Self-controlled design** (자기대조 설계): each participant serves as their own control; eliminates between-subject variability
