## Why Ingested

The wiki's TMD splint evidence ([[tmj/zhang-2020-occlusal-splint-tmd-rct-sr]], [[tmj/zhang-2021-exercise-vs-splint-tmd-sr-ma]]) centres on stabilization splints and pain endpoints; it lacks a head-to-head RCT on anterior repositioning splint (ARS) *designs* for anterior disc displacement with reduction (ADDwR) specifically. This RCT (Liu 2026, n=118) directly compares a novel one-piece Mono-Block ARS against the Twin-Block ARS, extending the splint evidence from "splint vs no treatment" to "which ARS design" and reinforcing the recurring finding that splints relieve symptoms without durably repositioning the disc.

## One-line Summary
Open-label RCT (n=118, ADDwR) — a novel one-piece Mono-Block (nMB) anterior repositioning splint beat the Twin-Block (TB) splint on joint-noise reduction (T6 82.1% vs 54.4%, p=0.001) and patient comfort/stability, while pain relief, mouth opening, and CBCT disc-condyle position were equivalent; neither splint durably repositioned the disc.

## 한줄요약
공개표지 RCT (n=118, 정복성 전방관절원판변위 ADDwR) — 신규 일체형 단일블록 전방재위치장치(novel Mono-Block, nMB)가 트윈블록(Twin-Block, TB)보다 관절잡음 감소(6개월 82.1% vs 54.4%, p=0.001)와 편안함·안정성에서 우월; 통증·최대개구량(MMO)·CBCT 과두-와 위치는 동등하고 두 장치 모두 관절원판을 지속적으로 정복시키지는 못함.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal of Oral Rehabilitation 2026;53(7):1275-1285. DOI 10.1111/joor.70179; PMID 41793287; PMCID PMC13261787.
- Open-label randomised controlled trial; single centre (Affiliated Stomatology Hospital, Guangzhou Medical University), Dec 2023 – Dec 2024.
- Source: PubMed Central full text (PMC13261787).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First RCT to directly compare a redesigned one-piece **novel Mono-Block (nMB)** ARS against the **Twin-Block (TB)** ARS for ADDwR.
- Demonstrates that ARS *design* (integrated vs two-piece occlusal-incline) measurably affects joint-noise control and patient comfort, even when symptomatic outcomes (pain, MMO) converge.
- Reaffirms with CBCT that conservative ARS therapy relieves symptoms without producing a durable disc-condyle repositioning.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **n=118** ADDwR patients, randomised 1:1 (computer-generated, variable block sizes 4/6, SNOSE concealment): **nMB n=59, TB n=59**. No stratification.
- Diagnosis by DC/TMD + MRI-confirmed ADDwR. Excluded MMO < 20 mm, significant tooth loss, prior TMD/orthognathic/trauma, severe systemic/psychological disease.
- **nMB** = one-piece full-arch ARS with an anterior breathing space (anti-airway-obstruction). **TB** = modified Twin-Block ARS without arch-expansion elements. Both built by one technician, one clinician; mandible set to minimal protruded position eliminating joint noise. Worn **nighttime only**.
- Outcomes at T0/T1(1mo)/T3(3mo)/T6(6mo): joint noise (palpation, dichotomous), joint pain (0–5 VAS), MMO (interincisal mm). CBCT ln(P/A) (Kamelchuk/Pullinger) at T0/T6. Patient satisfaction (5 domains, 0–5 VAS) at T6.
- **Open-label** (apparent design differences): only the statistician was blinded. SPSS 27, Shapiro–Wilk + appropriate parametric/non-parametric tests; p<0.05.
- 118/118 completed, no loss to follow-up, no adverse events. Baseline groups balanced (gender p=0.679, age p=0.937).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Joint noise** — nMB improvement T1 66.1% / T3 71.4% / T6 82.1%; TB 47.4% / 54.4% / 54.4% (all p<0.001 vs baseline). Inter-group: nMB superior at **T1 (p=0.040)** and **T6 (p=0.001)**; T3 p=0.055 (ns).
- **Joint pain (VAS)** — both fell sharply (nMB 2.69→0.27; TB 2.71→0.41). Inter-group difference only at **T3 (p=0.009, nMB lower)**; ns at T0/T1/T6.
- **MMO** — both improved (nMB 35.83→42.38 mm; TB 35.56→41.99 mm). Inter-group significant at T0 (0.022) and **T1 (0.035)**; ns at T3/T6.
- **CBCT ln(P/A)** — no significant intra- or inter-group change (nMB -0.45→-0.38, p=0.737; TB -0.54→-0.56, p=0.601). Condyle-posterior positive rate: nMB 75%→56% (p=0.264), TB 78%→78% (p=1.000) — no durable disc repositioning by either splint.
- **Satisfaction (T6)** — nMB superior in general satisfaction (p<0.001), comfort (p=0.005), stability (p<0.001); ns for cleaning (0.771) and retention (0.195).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Open-label: no blinding of patients/clinicians/outcome assessors (joint noise & MMO scored unblinded); only statistician blinded → observer/performance bias possible.
- Single centre, homogeneous population (limited generalisability); only 6-month follow-up (TMD chronic — relapse/durability unassessed).
- CBCT (not MRI) used; disc morphology/position unknown — MRI is gold standard for TMJ soft tissue.
- No placebo / conservative-care control → cannot separate splint effect from natural course or placebo.
- No orthodontic follow-up: prolonged ARS advancement may cause occlusal change / unwanted tooth movement (unassessed). Authors plan MRI + longer follow-up + EMG.

## 7. Glossary

- **ADDwR** — anterior disc displacement with reduction: disc displaced in closed-mouth, recaptured on opening (reciprocal clicking).
- **ARS** — anterior repositioning splint: advances mandible to recapture displaced disc.
- **nMB / TB** — novel Mono-Block (one-piece ARS) / Twin-Block (two-piece occlusal-incline ARS).
- **MMO** — maximum mouth opening (interincisal mm).
- **ln(P/A)** — log ratio of posterior to anterior joint space (Pullinger): < -0.25 posterior, > 0.25 anterior condylar position.
- **DC/TMD** — Diagnostic Criteria for Temporomandibular Disorders.
