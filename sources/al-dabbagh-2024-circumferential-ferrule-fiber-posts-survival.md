## Why Ingested

The ferrule effect is the single most cited predictor of restored-ETT longevity, so the new `post-and-core` category needs a dedicated clinical-evidence page on it. This SR+MA isolates the ferrule variable in fiber-post restorations (clinical RCTs/cohorts), complementing the material-choice papers [[post-and-core/tsintsadze-2022-survival-rates-glass-fiber-metal-posts]] and reinforcing the ferrule-dominance theme seen biomechanically in [[post-and-core/mously-2024-anterior-endocrowns-alternative-core-crown]].

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (8 clinical studies, 407 teeth): a uniform circumferential ferrule significantly increased SUCCESS of fiber-post-restored ETT in direct analysis (RR=1.28, 95% CI 1.06-1.54), but indirect analysis across all studies showed no significant survival/success difference.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA(임상연구 8편, 치아 407개): 균일한 원주형 페룰(circumferential ferrule)이 직접 분석에서 섬유포스트 수복 근관치료치아의 성공률을 유의하게 높였으나(RR=1.28, 95% CI 1.06-1.54), 전체 연구 간접분석에서는 생존·성공률 차이가 유의하지 않았음.

## 1. Document Information

Systematic review and meta-analysis (PRISMA), *The Journal of Prosthetic Dentistry* (2024;132(6):1251-1259). Abstract-only ingest. Included RCTs and prospective cohorts with ≥6-month follow-up of ETT restored with prefabricated fiber posts, with vs without a uniform circumferential ferrule. RR + 95% CI; chi-square for heterogeneity (alpha=.05).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR+MA to isolate the circumferential-ferrule variable specifically in fiber-post-restored ETT (clinical data).
- Direct analysis: significant success benefit of a uniform circumferential ferrule (RR=1.28).
- Tempered by indirect (all-study) analysis showing no significant overall difference — flags confounding and limited high-quality evidence.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

3731 records screened → 8 eligible. Two strata: direct success-rate comparison (2 studies, 123 teeth) and indirect pooled survival/success (8 studies, 407 teeth).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Direct success analysis: RR=1.28 (95% CI 1.06-1.54), P<.05 favoring ferrule.
- Indirect analysis: no significant difference between ferrule and no-ferrule groups (P>.05).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

Only 8 studies; direct analysis rests on 2 studies/123 teeth. Heterogeneity and confounders (post length, crown type, follow-up) limit certainty. Authors call for higher-quality RCTs. Abstract-only ingest.

## 6. Related Work

Clinical counterpart to the in-vitro partial-vs-complete-ferrule evidence and the ferrule-as-dominant-factor finding in [[post-and-core/mously-2024-anterior-endocrowns-alternative-core-crown]]. Ferrule preservation also underpins fracture-resistance logic in [[resin/susita-2026-comparative-analysis-stress-distribution-teeth]] and [[overviews/cracked-tooth-syndrome-overview]].

## 7. Glossary

- **Circumferential (360°) ferrule** — continuous band of sound coronal dentin encircled by the crown, distributing stress and resisting lever forces.
- **Direct vs indirect meta-analysis** — direct = head-to-head ferrule vs no-ferrule within studies; indirect = pooled comparison across all studies.
- **RR (relative risk)** — ratio of outcome probability between groups; RR>1 here = higher success with ferrule.
