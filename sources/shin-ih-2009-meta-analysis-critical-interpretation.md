## One-line Summary
A Korean-language invited lecture (2009 KASL Autumn Conference) that walks clinicians through the rationale, prerequisites, statistical machinery, and common pitfalls of meta-analysis for critical appraisal.

## 1. Document Information
- Format: Invited lecture handout (proceedings S22–S32, Korean), Catholic University of Daegu, Department of Medical Statistics
- Venue: 2009 대한간학회 추계학술대회 (Korean Association for the Study of the Liver, Autumn Meeting)
- Language: Korean
- No DOI (conference proceeding)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Frames meta-analysis as the synthesis layer above systematic review (SR), not a substitute for it.
- Lays out PICO as the entry point to evidence-based decision making.
- Catalogues statistical tools clinicians need to interpret a forest plot: pooled p-value, effect size for continuous (mean difference) and categorical (OR/RR/rate difference) outcomes, publication bias, and homogeneity test.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Walks through worked examples: combining 17 trials with mixed significance directions; combining mean ± SD of continuous outcomes via Cohen's d-style effect sizes; combining categorical outcomes via odds/relative risk/rate difference.
- Discusses how many studies are needed, what bias sources (publication, language, database) threaten validity, and how heterogeneity (test for homogeneity / heterogeneity) is detected.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Educational document — no original empirical results.
- Reproduces the canonical forest plot and funnel plot interpretation framework.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Korean-language conference handout; not peer reviewed.
- Pre-2010 (does not cover PRISMA 2020, GRADE, network meta-analysis).
- Examples are hepatology-flavored but methodology is generic.

## 6. Related Work
- Conceptually adjacent to Shin WJ 2015 (SR/MA introduction, also Korean) and Monaghan 2021 (OR/RR/NNT tutorial).

## 7. Glossary
- 메타분석 (Meta-analysis): 여러 연구 결과를 통계적으로 통합해 단일 효과 추정치를 산출하는 방법.
- 비뚤림 (Bias): 체계적 오류. publication / language / database bias 등.
- 동질성 검정 (Test for homogeneity): 연구 간 효과 크기 일관성 검정.
- 효과크기 (Effect size): 연속변수 → d 계열, 범주변수 → OR / RR / rate difference.
