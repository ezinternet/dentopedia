## Why Ingested

Open-healing ARP에서 콜라겐 막 적층(단층 vs 이중층)이 치조제 치수 보존에 차이를 주는지 검증. Park 그룹 open-healing 연작 [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/park-2016-hidden-x-suture-ridge-preservation]]·[[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/cho-2017-compressive-force-graft-ridge-preservation]]의 변수 분해 — 막 층수는 결과에 영향 없음을 보여 [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/adams-2022-clinical-evidence-alveolar-ridge-preservation]]의 "막 디테일보다 graft·노출관리가 중요" 논지에 부합.

## One-line Summary
Randomized pilot (n=11 molars): single-layer vs double-layer collagen membrane coverage in open-healing ARP gave comparable horizontal/vertical ridge preservation and clinical healing — both suitable.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: J Periodontal Implant Sci 2017 Dec;47(6):372–380
- First author: Ho-Keun Choi (corresponding: Jung-Chul Park)
- Type: prospective randomized pilot study
- DOI: 10.5051/jpis.2017.47.6.372

## 2. Key Contributions
- Tests whether membrane layering (1 vs 2 layers) matters in open-healing ARP
- Negative/equivalence finding: no substantial dimensional difference
- All sites received implants with acceptable initial stability

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Design: prospective randomized pilot; 11 molars, 9 patients; posterior extractions.
- Groups: single-layer (SL) vs double-layer (DL) collagen membrane, open-healing.
- Outcomes: CBCT dimensional change, clinical healing.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- All sites healed without complications; implants placed at all sites with acceptable initial stability.
- Width reduction 1 mm apical to crest: SL −1.7 ± 0.5 mm, DL −1.8 ± 0.4 mm (similar); other horizontal changes similar; height reductions comparable.
- Conclusion: SL and DL collagen coverage show no substantial difference; both suitable for open-healing ARP.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Small pilot (n=11); short follow-up.
- Equivalence from a small sample — underpowered to exclude small effects.
