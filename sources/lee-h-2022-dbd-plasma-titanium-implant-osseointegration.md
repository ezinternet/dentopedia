## Why Ingested

플라즈마 배치 2차(2026-05-31)에서 **유전체 장벽 방전(DBD) 방식으로 임플란트를 무균 상태로 유지하며 처리하는** 장치 공학 근거. 탄소 60%↓·초친수성·세포반응 향상을 보여 [[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/jeon-2024-vortex-direct-plasma-titanium-surface]]·[[wiki/implants/surface/plasma/zheng-2020-non-thermal-atmospheric-plasma-titanium]]의 표면 재활성화 기전을 장치 측면에서 보강.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study of a cylindrical dielectric barrier discharge (DBD) plasma source (implant as electrode, packaging as dielectric, keeping the implant sterile): plasma reduced surface carbon by ~60% (XPS), made the surface super-hydrophilic with increased calcium elution, and significantly improved cell adhesion, proliferation, and differentiation on SLA implants.

## 한줄요약

원통형 유전체 장벽 방전(DBD) 플라즈마 장치(임플란트를 전극, 포장재를 유전체로 사용해 무균 유지)의 in vitro 연구: 표면 탄소 약 60%↓(XPS), 초친수성·칼슘 용출↑, SLA 임플란트에서 세포 부착·증식·분화 유의 향상.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Biomedical Engineering Letters 2022, 12:421–432
- DOI: 10.1007/s13534-022-00245-9
- Study type: in vitro (device engineering + cell)

## 2. Key Contributions

- DBD design treats the implant while keeping it sterile inside its packaging (chairside-ready concept).
- Quantifies ~60% carbon reduction and super-hydrophilicity with optimized discharge conditions.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Titanium implant as electrode, packaging wall as dielectric barrier → DBD plasma.
- Numerical + experimental optimization; XPS surface chemistry; in vitro cell assays on SLA.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- XPS: ~60% reduction in surface carbon impurities.
- Super-hydrophilic surface; increased calcium elution.
- Significantly improved cell adhesion, proliferation, differentiation.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In vitro only; no in vivo/clinical osseointegration data.

- DBD = dielectric barrier discharge (유전체 장벽 방전). XPS = X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy.
