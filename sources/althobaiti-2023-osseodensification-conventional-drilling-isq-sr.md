## One-line Summary
SR (Cureus 2023, PubMed/Scopus/EMBASE/Cochrane): 다수의 연구에서 골밀도화(OD) 드릴링이 기존 방식 대비 ISQ·골밀도 유의하게 높음; 단, 포함된 RCT들의 비뚤림위험(RoB 2) 높아 해석 주의 필요.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Cureus 2023 (Published October 11, 2023)
- DOI: 10.7759/cureus.49024 [미검증 — Cureus DOI format; exact number from filename]
- Design: Systematic review
- Databases: PubMed, Scopus, EMBASE, Cochrane Oral Health Group, EBSCO (Dentistry and Oral Science Source)
- Search period: January 2013–December 2022
- Inclusion: RCTs and NRSIs comparing OD vs conventional drilling, reporting ISQ, ≥3 months follow-up

## 2. Key Contributions
- Majority of included studies show OD drilling yields higher ISQ (RFA values) and bone density than conventional drilling
- OD = biomechanical bone compaction technique preserving bone volume during osteotomy site preparation
- NRSIs overall risk of bias: LOW across confounding/selection/classification domains
- RCTs quality via RoB 2: HIGH overall risk — important limitation
- Practical implication: OD appears to improve primary stability, but high-quality RCT evidence is lacking

## 3. Methodology
PRISMA-based SR. Electronic search across 5 databases (through Dec 2022). RCTs and NRSIs (non-randomized studies of interventions). Primary outcome: ISQ/RFA values comparing OD vs conventional drilling. Quality: RoB 2 for RCTs, ROBINS-I for NRSIs.

## 4. Key Results
- OD vs conventional drilling: majority of studies → significantly higher ISQ in OD group
- Bone density: significantly higher in OD group in majority of studies
- NRSI quality: low overall RoB
- RCT quality: high RoB overall — limits confidence

## 5. Limitations
- High RoB in available RCTs is the main limitation
- Heterogeneity in OD device protocols and drilling parameters
- Most studies small sample sizes
- Search cutoff Dec 2022 (Konuklu 2026 RCT not included)

## 6. Related Work
- Konuklu 2026 (RCT n=100: OD shows transient mid-healing ISQ gains, not sustained to T4)
- Tisci 2026 (SR+MA: ISQ-IT moderate correlation; OD may explain some IT-ISQ discordance)
- Huang 2020 (17 ISQ factors: preparation technique is one factor)
