## One-line Summary
J Esthet Restor Dent 2025: 20 endocrowns (ZG glazed vs ZP polished), lower first molars, 2yr clinical — polished 0.422mm vs glazed 0.491mm enamel wear (NS, p=0.108); 100% survival; both clinically acceptable.

## 1. Key Results
- 20 patients; zirconia endocrowns on endodontically treated lower first molars; CAD/CAM
- ZG (glazed, n=10) vs ZP (adjusted+polished, n=10)
- 2-year follow-up: 6, 12, 18, 24 months; intraoral scanner + Geomagic software
- Survival: 100% (no replacements)
- Enamel wear ZP: 0.422±0.130mm; ZG: 0.491±0.105mm; NS (p=0.108)
- Clinical significance: both glazing and polishing are acceptable for endocrowns
