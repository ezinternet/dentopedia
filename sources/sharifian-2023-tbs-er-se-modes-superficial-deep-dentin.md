## One-line Summary
In vitro (40 third molars): superficial > deep dentin µTBS for all adhesives (p=0.005); Scotchbond Universal in E&R mode highest µTBS overall; universal adhesive benefits from E&R mode; Clearfil SE Bond and Adper Single Bond 2 also tested.

## 1. Document Information
Babol University of Medical Sciences, Iran. Frontiers in Dentistry 2023. In vitro µTBS study comparing three adhesive systems (E&R, SE, and universal) across two dentin depths.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Dentin depth effect confirmed: superficial > deep dentin µTBS for all systems (p=0.005)
- Scotchbond Universal in E&R mode: **highest µTBS** overall
- Universal adhesive (SBU) benefits significantly from E&R mode vs SE mode
- Adper Single Bond 2 (E&R) and Clearfil SE Bond (SE) tested as comparators
- Mode of failure: no significant difference among groups

## 3. Methodology
- 40 sound third molars divided into superficial and deep dentin groups
- Superficial: beneath deepest occlusal groove; Deep: 2mm below that
- 4 subgroups/dentin level: Adper Single Bond 2 (ASB/E&R), Clearfil SE Bond (CSE/SE), Scotchbond Universal E&R mode (SBU/E&R), Scotchbond Universal SE mode (SBU/SE)
- Composite: Charisma Smart; incubated 37°C/24h in distilled water
- µTBS tested; failure mode under stereomicroscope ×40
- Statistics: one-way ANOVA, α=0.05

## 4. Key Results
| Group | µTBS ranking |
|---|---|
| Superficial/SBU/E&R | **Highest** |
| Superficial > Deep | All adhesives (p=0.005) |
| SBU E&R vs SBU SE | E&R significantly higher |
| Failure mode | NS between groups |

- Deep dentin: lower µTBS due to more tubules, higher moisture, less intertubular dentin

## 5. Limitations
- In vitro; 24h only — no aging/cycling
- Single-center Iran; third molars may not represent clinical posterior teeth
- n=20 per subgroup — adequate but modest
- Only 24h aging — long-term durability not assessed

## 6. Related Work
- Tay & Pashley 2003 — deep dentin hydrophilicity explains lower bond strength
- Daneshkazemi 2013 — SE Bond µTBS after thermal/mechanical cycling
- Oza 2022 — clinical RCT: universal vs SE mode clinical performance at 24 months

## 7. Glossary
- **µTBS**: Microtensile bond strength
- **E&R**: Etch-and-rinse (phosphoric acid, then primer, then adhesive)
- **SE**: Self-etch (acidic primer etches + primes simultaneously)
- **Adper Single Bond 2 (ASB)**: 2-step E&R adhesive (3M)
- **Clearfil SE Bond (CSE)**: 2-step SE adhesive (Kuraray)
- **Scotchbond Universal (SBU)**: Universal adhesive (3M) — usable in E&R, SE, or selective-etch mode
