## One-line Summary
Retrospective study (96 Osstem SA implants, 45 patients, ≥8yr follow-up): 94.8% survival; early complications + MBL >1mm within 1yr both significant failure predictors; ISQ measured at placement and loading.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Maxillofacial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery 42:10 (2020)
- DOI: 10.1186/s40902-020-00255-6
- Design: Retrospective clinical study; 2008–2010 placement; long-term follow-up

## 2. Key Results
- Implants: 96 (TS III SA, SS II SA, SS III SA, U III SA — Osstem), Patients: 45
- Survival rate: **94.8%** (5 implants removed)
- Total complications: 14 (6 early, 8 delayed)
- All 5 failures occurred among early complications
- ISQ measured at placement and at loading

## 3. Failure Predictors (significant)
- Occurrence of complications (p<0.05)
- MBL >1mm within 1 year after prosthetic completion (p<0.05)
- Both factors independently predictive of long-term failure

## 4. SA Surface
- SLA: sandblasted alumina + acid etching → moderately rough surface (Ra ~2–4μm)
- Standard hydrophilic surface (not super-hydrophilic like CA)

## 5. Limitations
- Retrospective; small sample (96 implants, 45 patients)
- Multiple implant subtypes combined
- No control group or surface comparison
