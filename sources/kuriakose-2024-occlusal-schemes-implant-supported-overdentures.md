## Why Ingested

Wiki에 implant-supported overdenture의 occlusal scheme(balanced vs lingualized vs monoplane)을 직접 head-to-head로 기능 결과로 비교한 임상 데이터가 없었다. 기존 [[occlusion/bhambhani-2020-choosing-denture-occlusion-systematic-review]]는 conventional complete denture의 occlusal scheme SR로 결론이 약했고, [[occlusion/zhang-xueyang-2018-occlusion-design-edentulous-implant-prosthesis]]는 무치악 임플란트 보철 교합 설계를 다루지만 overdenture scheme별 정량 결과는 부족했다. 본 연구(Kuriakose 2024)는 mandibular 2-implant overdenture에서 세 scheme의 bite force·masticatory efficiency·satisfaction을 정량 비교해 그 gap을 메운다.

## One-line Summary

Per PubMed, single-center trial of 60 mandibular 2-implant overdenture patients (3 groups × 20) found balanced occlusion gave the highest 6-month bite force (200 N), masticatory efficiency (80%), and OHIP-EDENT satisfaction (9.0), beating lingualized then monoplane.

## 한줄요약

PubMed 기준, 하악 2-임플란트 오버덴처 환자 60명(3군 각 20명)에서 6개월 시점 교합력·저작효율·환자만족 모두 균형교합(Balanced) > 설측교합(Lingualized) > 단일평면교합(Monoplane) 순으로, 균형교합이 가장 우수했다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Direct head-to-head comparison of the three classic prosthodontic occlusal schemes — **balanced, lingualized, monoplane** — in **mandibular 2-implant overdentures**, a comparison the wiki previously lacked for the overdenture context.
- Reports quantitative 6-month functional outcomes (bite force, masticatory efficiency) plus a validated PRO (OHIP-EDENT–based satisfaction) across all three schemes.
- Provides a clinical rank order: **balanced > lingualized > monoplane** on every measured outcome.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Three parallel groups, 60 completely edentulous patients, randomly assigned (20 per group). Reported as RCT in the abstract; reporting depth is that of a brief report.
- **Intervention**: All patients received **two implants in the mandibular canine regions**; mandibular implant-supported overdentures fabricated and adjusted to the assigned scheme.
  - Group A: Balanced occlusion (simultaneous bilateral contact in centric + eccentric movements).
  - Group B: Lingualized occlusion (maxillary palatal cusps into mandibular fossae).
  - Group C: Monoplane occlusion (flat occlusal surfaces, minimized lateral forces).
- **Outcomes & timing**: baseline, 3 months, 6 months.
  - Bite force — bite force transducer (Occlusal Force Meter GM10, Nagano Keiki, Japan), first-molar region bilaterally, mean of 3.
  - Masticatory efficiency — colorimetric two-color chewing-gum color-mixing method.
  - Patient satisfaction — OHIP-EDENT (19-item) at 6 months, 1–10 Likert.
- **Analysis**: SPSS v25.0; between-group differences tested (p < 0.05 reported).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome (6 mo) | A: Balanced | B: Lingualized | C: Monoplane |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bite force (N) | **200 ± 20** | 180 ± 15 | 160 ± 10 |
| Masticatory efficiency (%) | **80 ± 5** | 75 ± 4 | 70 ± 3 |
| Satisfaction (1–10) | **9.0 ± 0.5** | 8.5 ± 0.4 | 8.0 ± 0.3 |

- Bite force baseline → 6 mo (A 100→200, B 95→180, C 90→160 N); all within-group increases significant (p < 0.05).
- Masticatory efficiency baseline → 6 mo (A 50→80%, B 48→75%, C 45→70%).
- All between-group differences reported significant (p < 0.05).
- **Bottom line**: Balanced occlusion gave superior functional outcomes and satisfaction vs lingualized and monoplane.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Brief Supplement report: no CONSORT flow, no randomization/allocation-concealment detail, no inferential test statistics or CIs (only group means ± SD and a blanket "p < 0.05").
- Single center, small n (20/group), short follow-up (6 months) — no implant/MBL or prosthetic-maintenance/biomechanical endpoints despite the "biomechanical outcomes" framing.
- Reported group means are suspiciously round and ordered monotonically; effect-size and variance reporting is thin. Findings should be read as directional, not definitive.
- Mandibular 2-implant overdentures only — does not address maxillary overdentures, bar/locator differences, or fully implant-supported (vs implant-retained) designs.

## 6. Related Work

- Cites prior work favoring balanced occlusion for masticatory performance/satisfaction (Rizzatti-Barbosa CM; Kapur; van Kampen FMC) in the complete/overdenture context.
- Complements complete-denture occlusal-scheme evidence summarized in [[occlusion/bhambhani-2020-choosing-denture-occlusion-systematic-review]] and edentulous implant-prosthesis occlusion design in [[occlusion/zhang-xueyang-2018-occlusion-design-edentulous-implant-prosthesis]].

## 7. Glossary

- **Balanced occlusion (균형교합)**: simultaneous bilateral tooth contacts in centric and eccentric (protrusive/lateral) movements; classic full-denture stabilizing scheme.
- **Lingualized occlusion (설측교합)**: maxillary palatal (lingual) cusps as the primary functional cusps seating into mandibular fossae; compromise between balanced and monoplane.
- **Monoplane occlusion (단일평면교합)**: flat (0° / cuspless) occlusal surfaces minimizing lateral/torquing forces; easiest to adjust.
- **OHIP-EDENT**: Oral Health Impact Profile for edentulous patients — validated PRO instrument.
- **Implant-supported overdenture**: removable denture retained/supported by implants (here, 2 mandibular canine-region implants).
