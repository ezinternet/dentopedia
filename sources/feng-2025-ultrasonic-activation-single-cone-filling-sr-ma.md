## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 20 lab studies (11 meta-analyzed) — ultrasonic activation of sealers during single-cone obturation significantly reduced porosity (micro-CT MD −1.21; apical stereomicroscopy MD −0.50) and increased apical/middle sealer penetration (CLSM SMD 2.28/2.69), but bond strength and other penetration indicators were inconsistent.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Quantifies whether **ultrasonic activation (UA)** of sealers improves single-cone filling quality vs non-activation (NA).
- Consistent **porosity reduction** (micro-CT + apical/coronal stereomicroscopy) and **deeper sealer penetration** (CLSM apical/middle).
- Flags inconsistency in **push-out bond strength** and some penetration metrics — UA benefit is not uniform across endpoints.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 6 databases; 20 included, 11 meta-analyzed; quality assessment performed.
- Comparison: UA vs NA of root canal sealers (CaSi + resin-based) in single-cone obturation.
- Endpoints: porosity (micro-CT, stereomicroscopy by canal third), sealer penetration % (CLSM), push-out bond strength.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Endpoint | Effect (UA vs NA) | 95% CI | I² |
|---|---|---|---|
| Porosity — micro-CT | MD −1.21 | −1.69 to −0.74 | 28% |
| Porosity — stereo coronal | MD −0.05 | −0.82 to −0.18 | 0% |
| Porosity — stereo middle | MD −0.44 | −1.56 to 0.68 | 89% |
| Porosity — stereo apical | MD −0.50 | −0.78 to −0.21 | 0% |
| Penetration — CLSM apical | SMD 2.28 | 1.17 to 3.40 | 71% |
| Penetration — CLSM middle | SMD 2.69 | 1.16 to 4.22 | 87% |

- Other penetration indicators + push-out bond strength: inconsistent.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Lab-only (no clinical outcomes); high heterogeneity at middle third and CLSM endpoints.
- Bond-strength variability unexplained; abstract-only ingest (methodological detail not extracted).

## 7. Glossary

- **Ultrasonic activation (UA)**: applying an ultrasonically energized instrument to the sealer to improve flow, reduce voids, and enhance dentinal-tubule penetration.
- **Porosity / void**: unfilled space in the obturation mass; single-cone's main quality weakness.
- **CLSM**: confocal laser scanning microscopy (measures sealer penetration depth).
- **Push-out bond strength**: interfacial sealer-to-dentin resistance test.
