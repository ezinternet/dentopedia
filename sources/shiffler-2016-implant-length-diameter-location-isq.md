## One-line Summary
후향적 연구(n=200 NobelReplace Tapered, UCLA): 구강 내 위치가 ISQ에 가장 유의한 영향(하악>상악, T1·T2 모두); 임플란트 길이는 임상적으로 의미 있는 효과 경향(p=0.08/0.09); 직경은 유의하지 않음; 생존율 98%.

## 1. Document Information
- Journal: Oral Surgery, Oral Medicine, Oral Pathology and Oral Radiology 2016;122(6):e193–e198
- DOI: 10.1016/j.oooo.2016.06.016
- Design: Retrospective observational study
- n = 200 consecutive NobelReplace Tapered Groovy implants; 143 patients
- Age range: 32–93 years (mean 63.7, SD ±14.3)
- Site: UCLA oral and maxillofacial surgery center, May 2007–October 2011
- IRB: UCLA Office of Human Research Protection Program #13-002253

## 2. Key Contributions
- Intraoral location is the most significant factor in ISQ: mandible > maxilla at both placement and follow-up
- Implant length trends toward clinical relevance (p=0.08 at T1, p=0.09 at T2) — may influence stability
- Implant diameter: not statistically significant for ISQ
- Gender and age: no effect on ISQ or survival
- 98% survival rate — high baseline performance of the implant system tested

## 3. Methodology
Retrospective chart review. 200 NobelReplace Tapered Groovy implants. ISQ measured at T1 (placement) and T2 (follow-up, timing not specified). Implant dimensions: 4.3×10, 4.3×13, 5×10 mm (roughly equal distribution). 49% received sinus graft (maxillary posterior). Analysis: ANOVA and simple linear regression.

## 4. Key Results
| Variable | Effect on ISQ | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| Intraoral location | Mandible > maxilla | Significant (T1 and T2) |
| Implant length | Longer → higher ISQ trend | p=0.08 (T1), p=0.09 (T2) — clinically relevant |
| Implant diameter | No effect | NS |
| Gender | No effect | NS |
| Age | No effect | NS |
| Overall survival rate | 98% | — |

## 5. Limitations
- Single institution, single implant system (NobelReplace Tapered)
- Retrospective; confounders (bone density, grafting) not fully controlled
- T2 timing not standardized across patients
- Small subgroup sizes for some dimension combinations
