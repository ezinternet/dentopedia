## Why Ingested

치아미백 (Tooth Whitening, 미백)은 위키에 전용 페이지가 0개인 GAP였다. 본 2025 갱신 SR+MA (32 studies)는 자가미백 (at-home) vs 진료실미백 (in-office)의 효능·민감도를 직접 비교하는 anchor 근거로, 새 `tooth-whitening` 카테고리의 기준점이 된다. 미백 후 민감도는 [[overviews/dentin-hypersensitivity-mechanism-diagnosis-treatment-synthesis]]의 상아질 과민증 기전과 직결된다. abstract-only — full text not retrieved.

## One-line Summary

Updated SR+MA (32 RCTs, 23 in meta-analysis) — at-home and in-office bleaching achieve equivalent shade change (ΔSGU, no difference); at-home shows significantly lower sensitivity intensity and slightly greater ΔE, but evidence quality is low.

## 한줄요약

업데이트 SR+MA (RCT 32편) — 자가미백과 진료실미백의 색조 단위 변화(ΔSGU)는 동등하고, 자가미백이 민감도 강도는 더 낮고 ΔE는 약간 더 큰 경향이나 근거 수준은 낮음.

## 1. Document Information

- Study type: Systematic review + meta-analysis (update of a 2016 SR), GRADE-assessed.
- Population: Adult patients receiving vital tooth bleaching.
- Comparison: At-home (tray, carbamide/hydrogen peroxide) vs in-office (high-concentration hydrogen peroxide).
- Included: 32 studies qualitative; 23 in meta-analysis. Seven databases + grey literature.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Largest/most current head-to-head comparison of the two delivery modes for color change AND sensitivity.
- Separates the two outcomes that drive technique choice: efficacy (ΔSGU, ΔE) vs adverse effect (tooth sensitivity risk & intensity).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- RCTs comparing in-office vs at-home in adults; risk-of-bias and GRADE applied.
- Effect measures: SMD (continuous, ΔE / sensitivity intensity), RR (sensitivity risk), ΔSGU.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Sensitivity intensity**: significantly lower for at-home (SMD −0.78; 95% CI −1.53 to −0.03; p=0.04).
- **Sensitivity risk**: no significant difference (RR 0.82; 95% CI 0.61–1.10; p=0.19).
- **Efficacy ΔSGU**: no significant difference (SMD 0.04; 95% CI −0.17–0.25; p=0.68).
- **Efficacy ΔE**: significantly higher for at-home (SMD 0.49; 95% CI 0.14–0.84; p=0.006).
- Overall certainty (GRADE): **low**.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Low quality of evidence; protocol heterogeneity (concentrations, exposure time, number of sessions).
- ΔE vs ΔSGU disagreement reflects measurement-method sensitivity, not necessarily clinical visibility.
- Abstract-only ingest — full-text subgroup detail not extracted.

## 6. Related Work

- Internal/non-vital bleaching: Frank 2021 (J Endod SR+MA).
- OTC bleaching: de Oliveira 2024 (Clin Oral Investig NMA).

## 7. Glossary

- **ΔSGU**: change in shade guide units (e.g., Vita classical) — clinical visual metric.
- **ΔE / ΔEab***: instrumental color difference (CIELAB) measured by spectrophotometer.
- **In-office bleaching**: chairside, high-concentration HP (25–40%), clinician-applied.
- **At-home bleaching**: patient-applied tray, lower-concentration CP (10–22%) / HP.
