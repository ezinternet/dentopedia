## Why Ingested

기존 [[implants/peri-implantitis/baima-2022-surface-decontamination-protocols-surgical-periimplantitis]]가 표면 오염제거 프로토콜 우열을 결론짓지 못했음. 본 multicenter RCT는 electrolytic(GalvoSurge) vs hydrogen peroxide를 powered sample로 직접 비교해 그 공백을 겨냥.

## One-line Summary
Multicenter RCT (58 patients) — adjunctive electrolytic cleaning (GalvoSurge) vs hydrogen peroxide for surface decontamination in reconstructive peri-implantitis therapy: both effective, no significant superiority of either.

## 한줄요약
다기관 RCT(환자 58명): 재건적 임플란트주위염 치료에서 보조 표면오염제거로 전해법(GalvoSurge)과 과산화수소를 비교 — 둘 다 효과적이며 유의한 우열 없음.

## 1. Document Information
- Clinical Implant Dentistry and Related Research 2025;27:e70075. Funded by Nobel Biocare (2021-1716). Multicenter (Michigan, UIC Barcelona, Bern, private practices).

## 2. Key Contributions
- First powered multicenter RCT comparing electrolytic (GS) vs hydrogen peroxide (HP) adjuncts in reconstructive peri-implantitis.
- Both adjuncts yielded comparable clinical/radiographic improvement and disease resolution.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Multicenter, prospective, randomized, two-arm. 58 patients completed (HP 29 pt/31 impl; GS 29 pt/32 impl). Crater-like/circumferential intrabony defects, reconstructive therapy + SPT.
- T0/T1(6mo)/T2(12mo); radiographic at T0/T2. Primary: disease resolution. GEE models; post-hoc power.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Mean PPD reduction T0→T2: GS 3.15 mm, HP 3.31 mm (both significant).
- Groups balanced at baseline; mean intrabony defect depth 3.98 ± 0.91 mm.
- No significant superiority between GS and HP for the primary disease-resolution outcome.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 12-month follow-up; heterogeneous implant surfaces (mostly TiUnite); industry funding; resolution definitions sensitive.

## 6. Related Work
- Baima 2022 decontamination protocols SR; in vitro GalvoSurge data.

## 7. Glossary
- GS: electrolytic cleaning (GalvoSurge). HP: hydrogen peroxide. PPD: probing pocket depth. GEE: generalized estimating equations.
