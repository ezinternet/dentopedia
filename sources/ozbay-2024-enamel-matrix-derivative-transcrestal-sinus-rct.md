## Why Ingested

PubMed 감시 인제스트(transcrestal sinus lift)로 발굴한 신규 RCT — transcrestal 거상에 **법랑기질유도체(Enamel Matrix Derivative, EMD)** 생물학적 첨가의 가치를 묻는다. 기존 transcrestal 페이지들이 술식(osteotome vs OD)·이식재 유무에 집중한 반면, 본 RCT는 biologic adjunct 축을 추가해 [[sinus-lift/transcrestal/guo-2020-non-grafted-platelet-concentrations-grafted-transcrestal-sinus]]의 혈소판농축물 adjunct 논의와 짝을 이룬다. 결론은 "EMD가 조기(T3) endo-sinus 골증가는 늘리지만 12개월엔 차이 소실" — adjunct의 한계를 보여 [[sinus-lift/transcrestal/kadkhodazadeh-2024-transcrestal-maxillary-sinus-elevation-sr-ma]]의 높은 기저 생존율과 함께 "추가 술식의 한계효용"을 시사한다. PMC 전문 미제공 — abstract-only.

## One-line Summary

RCT (24 patients, 40 implants) of transcrestal sinus floor elevation with vs without enamel matrix derivative: EMD significantly increased endo-sinus bone gain at 3 months (3.72 vs 3.10 mm) but the difference vanished by 12 months, with no inter-group ISQ difference.

## 한줄요약

경치조골 상악동거상에 법랑기질유도체(EMD) 첨가 RCT(24명·40 임플란트) — EMD군이 3개월 endo-sinus 골증가 유의하게 큼(3.72 vs 3.10 mm)이나 12개월엔 차이 소실, 군간 ISQ 차이 없음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Tests EMD as a biologic adjunct specifically in transcrestal (closed) sinus floor elevation with simultaneous implant placement.
- Early benefit: EMD significantly increased endo-sinus bone gain (ESBG) at 3 months.
- No durable benefit: the ESBG advantage was not significant at 12 months.
- Stability: no inter-group ISQ difference at placement or 3 months (only an intragroup increase within the EMD arm), suggesting EMD does not improve osseointegration endpoints clinically.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: randomized, parallel-arm, CBCT-based.
- Population: 24 patients, 40 implants — EMD+TSFE (13 patients, 20 implants) vs TSFE alone (11 patients, 20 implants).
- Recalls: 3 months (T3) and 12 months (T12).
- Outcomes: residual bone height (RBH), implant protrusion length (IPL), peri-implant sinus bone level (SBL), endo-sinus bone gain (ESBG), implant stability (ISQ); multivariate regression by group.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- ESBG at T3: 3.72 ± 0.85 mm (EMD+TSFE) vs 3.10 ± 0.05 mm (TSFE) — statistically significant (P < .05).
- ESBG at T12: no significant inter-group difference (P > .05).
- ISQ: no significant inter-group difference at T1 and T3; a significant intragroup increase within the EMD+TSFE arm at T3.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (24 patients) and 12-month horizon; no survival/MBL endpoint.
- Abstract-only ingest — graft material co-use, randomization detail, and regression covariates not extractable here.
- The transient (T3-only) ESBG benefit questions the cost-effectiveness of EMD when 12-month bone and stability outcomes converge.

## 7. Glossary

- **EMD (enamel matrix derivative)**: porcine enamel-matrix protein preparation (e.g., Emdogain) used to promote periodontal/peri-implant regeneration.
- **TSFE**: transcrestal sinus floor elevation.
- **ESBG (endo-sinus bone gain)**: new bone formed apical to the original sinus floor around the protruding implant.
- **IPL (implant protrusion length)**: length of implant protruding above the original sinus floor into the elevated space.
- **SBL (sinus bone level)** · **ISQ**: peri-implant sinus bone level; resonance frequency stability index.
