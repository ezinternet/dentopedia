## One-line Summary
Meta-analysis of 61 in vitro studies identifies 10 strategies that improve universal adhesive bond strength to dentin (MMP inhibitors, prolonged application, scrubbing, selective enamel etching, plasma, ethanol-wet bonding, prolonged blowing, multiple layers, prolonged curing, hydrophobic overlay) and 2 that impair it (shortened application time, dentin desensitizers).

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Polymers 2021, 13, 814 (MDPI)
- **DOI**: 10.3390/polym13050814
- **Search**: PubMed, ISI Web of Science, Scopus, EMBASE (through September 2020)
- **Included**: 74 in SR; 61 in meta-analysis (5,671 initial hits)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Most comprehensive quantitative synthesis of strategies to improve universal adhesive dentin bond strength
- Provides clinically actionable list of "do this" vs "avoid this"
- Confirms MMP inhibition (chlorhexidine, EDC) consistently improves bond strength — mechanistic support for biomodification approaches
- Confirms scrubbing technique (active application) improves bond — endorses manufacturer instructions
- Identifies dentin desensitizers as bond-strength-impairing — important clinical note

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Design**: SR + meta-analysis
- **Inclusion**: In vitro studies reporting dentin bond strength of universal adhesives with additional strategies
- **Software**: RevMan v5.3.5
- **Quality assessment**: Modified parameters from prior systematic review

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
**Strategies IMPROVING universal adhesive bond to dentin**:
| Strategy | p-value |
|---|---|
| Prior MMP inhibitor application (chlorhexidine, EDC) | <0.001 |
| Prolonged application time | 0.007 |
| Scrubbing (active) application technique | <0.001 |
| Selective dentin etching (selective enamel etch + SE on dentin) | <0.001 |
| Non-atmospheric (cold) plasma | 0.01 |
| Ethanol-wet bonding | <0.01 |
| Prolonged air-blowing time | 0.02 |
| Multiple layer application | 0.005 |
| Prolonged light-curing time | 0.006 |
| Hydrophobic layer coating | <0.001 |

**Strategies IMPAIRING bond**:
| Strategy | p-value |
|---|---|
| Shortened application time | 0.006 |
| Dentin desensitizers (before bonding) | 0.01 |

- High heterogeneity in most analyses

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- All in vitro — no clinical translation
- High heterogeneity in adhesive brands, substrates, test methods
- Many strategies require clinical RCT validation
- Aging conditions inconsistent across studies
- Different products lumped within "universal adhesive" category

## 7. Glossary
- **Universal adhesive**: Multi-mode adhesive with 10-MDP; used in EAR, selective-etch, or SE
- **MMP (Matrix Metalloproteinase)**: Endogenous dentin enzyme degrading collagen
- **EDC (1-Ethyl-3-(3-Dimethylaminopropyl)Carbodiimide)**: Collagen cross-linker; MMP inhibitor
- **Scrubbing technique**: Active rubbing of adhesive against dentin surface during application
- **Selective dentin etching**: Etch enamel with phosphoric acid, leave dentin unetched (self-etch on dentin)
- **Non-atmospheric plasma**: Cold plasma surface treatment to enhance wettability
- **Ethanol-wet bonding**: Substitute ethanol for water on dentin surface to improve resin infiltration
- **Hydrophobic layer coating**: Application of separate hydrophobic resin layer over adhesive
