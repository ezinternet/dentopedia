## Why Ingested

소비자 직접구매(OTC) 미백제품(화이트닝 스트립·페인트온·트레이)은 환자가 가장 흔히 묻는 미백 경로지만 전문가 감독이 없다. 새 `tooth-whitening` 카테고리에서 "OTC/화이트닝 스트립" 축을 담당하며, [[tooth-whitening/de-geus-2025-at-home-vs-in-office-bleaching]]의 전문가 처방 미백과 대비된다. abstract-only — full text not retrieved.

## One-line Summary

SR + frequentist NMA (37 studies qualitative, 10 meta-analyzed, n=1932) — OTC bleaching (6% HP strips ≥14 h best on ΔEab*; 10% CP ≥14 h best on ΔSGU) gives satisfactory short-term whitening vs placebo with little-to-no sensitivity or gingival irritation (low certainty).

## 한줄요약

SR + 빈도주의 NMA (정성 37편, 메타 10편, n=1932) — OTC 미백(ΔEab*는 6% HP 스트립 ≥14h, ΔSGU는 10% CP ≥14h가 최고)은 위약 대비 단기 미백 효과가 만족스럽고 민감도·치은 자극은 거의 없음(근거 낮음).

## 1. Document Information

- Study type: SR + frequentist network meta-analysis. RoB2; GRADE.
- Intervention: Over-the-counter (OTC) bleaching — strips, at-home CP, etc.
- Outcomes: ΔEab*, ΔSGU, tooth sensitivity.
- Included: 37 qualitative, 10 in meta-analysis; n=1,932; nine databases + grey literature.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First NMA ranking OTC product/protocol formats (strip concentration × wear time × CP tray) on both color and adverse effects.
- Addresses the unsupervised-consumer route directly.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Frequentist NMA via common comparators (ΔEab*, ΔSGU, sensitivity); mean/risk differences with 95% CI.
- RoB2 for individual studies; GRADE certainty.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **ΔEab***: significantly higher with **6% HP strips (≥14 h)**.
- **ΔSGU**: highest with **at-home 10% CP (≥14 h)**, then 6% HP strips (≥14 h), then 3% HP strips (≥14 h).
- **Sensitivity**: at-home 10% CP (7–13 h) and placebo had lower risk, with no significant difference between them.
- OTC products: satisfactory short-term whitening vs placebo; little-to-no dentin hypersensitivity or gingival irritation. Certainty: **low**.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Short-term outcomes only; low certainty; only 10 studies meta-analyzed.
- No long-term color stability; unsupervised-use safety caveats noted by authors.
- Abstract-only ingest.

## 6. Related Work

- de Geus 2025 — professionally delivered at-home vs in-office.
- Terra 2025 — concentration → sensitivity gradient (consistent with low sensitivity for low-strength OTC).

## 7. Glossary

- **OTC bleaching**: consumer products bought without a prescription — whitening strips, paint-on gels, prefilled trays.
- **ΔEab***: CIELAB color difference (instrumental).
- **NMA (frequentist)**: network meta-analysis ranking multiple treatments through shared comparators.
