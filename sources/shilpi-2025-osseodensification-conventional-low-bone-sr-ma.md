## One-line Summary

SR+MA (6 human RCT/NRCT studies) of OD vs conventional drilling in low bone density: ISQ non-significant at both time points; bone density significantly higher immediately post-placement but not at 3–7 months.

## 한줄요약

저밀도 골 SR+MA(인간 대상 RCT/NRCT 6편): OD는 ISQ에서 유의하지 않았으나 임플란트 식립 직후 골밀도는 유의하게 높았고(p=0.004), 3–7개월 후에는 비유의였다.

## 2. Key Contributions
- ISQ non-significant at both measurement points: OD not statistically superior to CD for stability by ISQ
- Bone density immediately post-placement: OD significantly higher (SMD 2.14, p=0.004) — OD's mechanical compaction has a density signal
- Bone density at 3–7 months: non-significant — early density advantage resolves over time
- Human-only studies (RCTs/NRCTs): stricter than some OD SR+MAs that include animal data
- Consistent with Mohammadi 2025 (7 studies, similar ISQ NS conclusion) — mutual reinforcement

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Search:** Electronic + manual; 1,454 publications identified, 6 met inclusion criteria
- **Inclusion:** Human-based RCTs and NRCTs comparing OD vs CD in low-density bone
- **Risk of bias:** RoB 2.0 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (NRCTs)
- **Analysis:** RevMan 5.4; random-fixed effects model; Q test + I² heterogeneity
- **Outcomes:** ISQ (immediately, follow-up); bone density (immediately, 3–7 months); SMD as effect measure

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
| Outcome | Time | SMD | 95% CI | p |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ISQ | Immediately | 2.13 | -0.08, 4.35 | 0.06 (NS) |
| ISQ | Follow-up | 1.81 | -0.41, 4.03 | 0.11 (NS) |
| Bone density | Immediately | 2.14 | 0.68, 3.59 | 0.004 ✓ |
| Bone density | 3–7 months | 1.54 | -0.34, 3.43 | 0.11 (NS) |

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Only 6 studies met inclusion — very narrow evidence base
- Abstract-only; heterogeneity values, study characteristics, and subgroup analyses not accessible
- Human-only restriction may exclude relevant animal mechanistic data
- Need: more standardized RCTs with long-term follow-up in specifically defined low-density sites

## 6. Related Work
- Mohammadi 2025: SR+MA (7 studies) of OD vs conventional in low-density bone — ISQ NS, bone density advantage limited; highly consistent with present findings
- Gaspar 2022: SR+MA of OD vs conventional implant stability — broader scope, includes multiple bone types
- Gaikwad 2022: SR+MA animal/biomechanics OD data — supports BIC and stability advantages

## 7. Glossary
- **OD:** Osseodensification (Densah burs, Versah); bone compaction technique
- **CD:** Conventional drilling — standard cutting osteotomy
- **ISQ:** Implant Stability Quotient (RFA-based 0–100 scale)
- **SMD:** Standardized Mean Difference — pooled effect size across studies
- **RoB 2.0 / ROBINS-I:** Cochrane tools for assessing risk of bias in RCTs / NRCTs
