## Why Ingested

기존 접착시멘트 페이지들은 거의 전부 **in-vitro 결합강도** 근거다 — [[resin-bonding/borouziniat-2024-self-adhesive-vs-etch-resin-cement-zirconia-sr-ma]] (지르코니아 SBS), [[dental-materials/zirconia/kwon-2026-self-adhesive-resin-cement-zirconia]] 등. 정작 임상의가 알고 싶은 "자가접착 레진시멘트로 합착해도 수복물이 더 자주 떨어지지 않나?"라는 **임상 실패율** 질문은 비어 있었다. 본 SR+MA(RCT 9편)는 그 공백을 채우며, in-vitro에서 self-adhesive ≈ self-etch였던 borouziniat 소견이 **임상 생존율 수준에서도 self-adhesive ≈ conventional**로 이어짐을 보강한다. de Oliveira 2021이 보인 "in-vitro 우위가 임상 이점으로 직결되지 않는다"는 패턴과도 결을 같이한다 ([[resin-bonding/de-oliveira-2021-10-mdp-self-etch-adhesive-dentin-sr]]).

## One-line Summary

Systematic review + meta-analysis of 9 RCTs finding no significant difference in clinical failure rate of indirect single-tooth restorations cemented with self-adhesive resin cement versus conventional cements; self-adhesive resin cement is a recommendable, technically simpler alternative.

## 한줄요약

RCT 9편의 체계적 문헌고찰 + 메타분석 — 간접 단일치 수복물을 자가접착 레진시멘트(self-adhesive resin cement)로 합착해도 기존 시멘트(conventional cement) 대비 임상 실패율에 유의차가 없어, 술식이 단순한 자가접착 레진시멘트를 권장할 수 있다.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR+MA restricted to **randomized clinical trials** comparing self-adhesive resin cement vs conventional cements on the **clinical failure rate** of indirect single-tooth restorations (not bench bond-strength).
- Pooled hazard ratios across 9 RCTs show **no significant difference** in failure between cement classes.
- Provides a clinical-evidence basis to recommend self-adhesive resin cement as a **technique-simplifying** alternative without a survival penalty.
- Reinforces, at the clinical-outcome level, the in-vitro "self-adhesive is non-inferior" signal from zirconia bond-strength meta-analyses.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: SR + MA of RCTs; PRISMA-guided; registered (PROSPERO CRD42020215577).
- Databases: PubMed, Cochrane Library, EMBASE, Web of Science, LILACS + clinicaltrials.gov (grey literature).
- PICO: P = adults indicated for indirect restorations; I = self-adhesive resin cement; C = conventional cement; O = failure rates.
- Risk of bias: Cochrane RoB2 tool.
- Synthesis: pooled hazard ratios (HR) and standard errors (available or estimated); certainty of evidence by GRADE.
- Included studies: 9 RCTs in both qualitative and quantitative analysis.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 8 of 9 RCTs: nonsignificant difference in failure rate between self-adhesive resin cement and conventional cements.
- 1 RCT: significantly **higher** failure rate for **single-tooth ceramic crowns** luted with self-adhesive resin cement (the lone discordant signal — material-/indication-specific).
- Pooled result: **nonsignificant difference** across all studies combined.
- Conclusion: self-adhesive resin cement recommendable for indirect single-tooth restorations with failure risk similar to conventional cement.
- (Exact pooled HR/95% CI and GRADE certainty rating not recoverable from the abstract; would require full text.)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Author-acknowledged: limited number of RCTs (n=9); heterogeneity in restoration/material types pooled together.
- The single discordant trial (ceramic crowns) hints that the "no difference" conclusion may **not generalize uniformly** across all restorative materials — high-strength vs low-strength ceramics may behave differently.
- Identified here: abstract reports no numeric pooled effect, so magnitude/precision of the equivalence cannot be judged from this record; certainty (GRADE) likely moderate-to-low given few trials.
- "Conventional cements" is a heterogeneous comparator (glass ionomer, RMGI, zinc phosphate, conventional resin cement) — the abstract does not stratify by comparator.

## 6. Related Work

- Borouziniat 2024 — in-vitro SR+MA: self-adhesive ≈ self-etch bond strength to zirconia; total-etch inferior.
- de Oliveira 2021 — 11-RCT SR: 10-MDP self-etch adhesives show no clinical advantage over non-MDP for NCCL, echoing the in-vitro-vs-clinical gap.
- Ghodsi 2023 — narrative SR on resin cement selection per restoration type (self-/dual-cure for metallic PCRs; light-cure conventional for thin translucent ceramics).

## 7. Glossary

- **Self-adhesive resin cement (SARC)**: resin cement with acidic functional monomers (e.g., 10-MDP, phosphoric esters) enabling one-step luting without separate etch/prime — fewer technique-sensitive steps.
- **Conventional cement**: comparator class including glass ionomer, resin-modified glass ionomer, zinc phosphate, and multi-step (etch-and-rinse / self-etch) resin cements.
- **Indirect single-tooth restoration**: lab-/CAD-CAM-fabricated inlay, onlay, crown, or partial-coverage restoration on one tooth.
- **Hazard ratio (HR)**: ratio of instantaneous failure rates between groups over follow-up time.
- **RoB2 / GRADE**: Cochrane risk-of-bias tool for RCTs / framework rating certainty of a body of evidence.
