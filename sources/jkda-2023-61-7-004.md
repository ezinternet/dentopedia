## 4. Key Points
- 표준: 5% NaF (sodium fluoride) in rosin + ethanol
- 새로운 옵션:
  - 7700 µg F/g NH4F in 합성고분자 base + ethanol
  - Calcium-phosphate 첨가 (Ca·PO4 ion 공급)
  - RMGI (Resin-Modified Glass Ionomer) light-curable
