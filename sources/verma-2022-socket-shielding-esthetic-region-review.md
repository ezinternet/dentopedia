## Why Ingested

소켓 쉴드(socket shielding / root membrane) 기법이 심미 구역에서 협측골·윤곽 보존 옵션으로 타당한지 정리한 서술적 리뷰. 술기 입문서인 [[immediate-implant/socket-shield/ahamed-2022-partial-extraction-therapy-implant-placement]]와 임상 결과 SR인 [[immediate-implant/socket-shield/oliva-2023-socket-shield-buccal-bone-preservation-sr]]를 잇는 짧은 개관.

## One-line Summary

Narrative review concluding that socket shielding (intentional retention of the buccal root fragment) effectively minimizes buccal bone resorption and contour change as a minimally invasive, graft-optional approach in the esthetic zone, but that supporting evidence remains limited and larger studies are needed.

## 한줄요약

서술적 리뷰 — 소켓 쉴드(협측 치근편 의도적 보존)는 심미부에서 협측골 흡수·윤곽 변화를 효과적으로 줄이는 최소침습·골이식 선택적 기법이나, 근거가 제한적이어 대규모 연구가 필요하다는 결론.

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Journal of Oral Biology and Craniofacial Research 12 (2022) 662–665. Elsevier.
- People's Dental Academy (Prosthodontics), India. Type: narrative review (PubMed, Google Scholar, Cochrane).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Concise appraisal of socket shielding as an esthetic-zone hard/soft-tissue preservation option.
- Frames buccal-plate resorption physiology and the rationale for root-fragment retention.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Narrative review of literature from PubMed, Google Scholar, Cochrane.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Post-extraction ridge loss is buccal-dominant (~56% of residual ridge in cited data) due to bundle-bone/PDL loss.
- Socket shielding minimizes buccal bone resorption and contour change; bone graft optional → lower cost; minimally invasive.
- Evidence base limited; large-population studies needed for routine clinical adoption.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Narrative (non-systematic); no pooled outcomes.
- Limited long-term and comparative data.

## 6. Related Work

- Hürzeler socket shield / root membrane technique; partial extraction therapy literature.

## 7. Glossary

- **Socket shielding / root membrane technique**: retaining the buccal root segment in the socket at implant placement.
- **Bundle bone**: tooth-dependent inner buccal plate that resorbs after extraction.
