## Why Ingested
TMD 물리치료(physiotherapy) 접근 방법을 SR로 정리. [[tmj/haggman-henrikson-2017-pharmacological-orofacial-pain-sr-ma]]의 약물치료 근거를 보완하여 비약물 보존적 치료 옵션의 근거 기반 제공.

## One-line Summary
SR (15 RCTs, PubMed/ScienceDirect/Dialnet/PEDro): physiotherapy — especially combination of manual therapy + therapeutic exercise — is effective for TMD pain, function, and QoL.

## 한줄요약
SR (15 RCT): 물리치료, 특히 수기치료+운동치료 병용이 TMD 통증·기능·삶의 질에 효과적.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Medicine 12(12):4156 (2023)
- **Study type**: Systematic review (PRISMA, PROSPERO CRD42022362936)
- **Databases**: PubMed, ScienceDirect, Dialnet, PEDro (search 2010–June 2022)
- **Screened**: 656 articles → 103 after DB search → 65 after dedup → 15 included
- **Quality assessment**: PEDro scale

## 2. Key Contributions
- Physiotherapy (manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, electrotherapy) effective for TMD
- Combination therapy (manual therapy + therapeutic exercise) gives best results
- All major physiotherapy categories (MT, exercise, photobiomodulation, cervical mobilization) reduce TMD pain
- Supports physiotherapy as first-line conservative management

## 3. Methodology
- SR; 15 clinical trials included (n not totaled in abstract)
- Interventions: manual therapy (MT), therapeutic exercise, photobiomodulation, cervico-mandibular mobilization, electrotherapy, occlusal splint comparators
- Outcomes: pain (VAS), mandibular range of motion, QoL, psychosocial measures

## 4. Key Results
- Manual therapy alone: effective for pain and ROM
- Therapeutic exercise alone: effective for pain and muscle strength
- Combination (MT + exercise): superior outcomes — best available results
- Photobiomodulation + MT combination: effective (Brochado 2018)
- Cervical mobilization + exercise (Calixtre, Abboud): effective
- No single intervention clearly superior to all others

## 5. Limitations
- Only 15 studies; heterogeneous interventions and outcomes
- PEDro quality varied; some studies moderate quality
- No meta-analysis (SR only)
- Search limited to clinical trials (excluded observational designs)

## 6. Clinical Implication
TMD 보존적 1차 치료로 물리치료 권장. 근육 위주 TMD: 수기치료 + 치료적 운동 병용. 관절 위주 TMD: 경부-하악 가동화 + 운동. 약물치료([[haggman-henrikson-2017-pharmacological-orofacial-pain-sr-ma]])와 병행 가능.

## 7. Glossary
- **Manual therapy (MT)**: 수기치료 — 관절 가동화·조작술, 연부조직 마사지
- **Photobiomodulation**: 광생체조절 — 저강도 레이저 / LED 치료
- **Cervico-mandibular mobilization**: 경부-하악 가동화 — TMD와 경부 증상 동시 접근
- **PEDro scale**: 물리치료 RCT 방법론적 질 평가 척도 (0–10점)
