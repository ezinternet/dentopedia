## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Narrative short communication review. Literature overview of platform switching mechanism and clinical evidence available up to 2013.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Platform switching: use of smaller-diameter abutment on larger implant collar
- Mechanism: displaces microgap away from crestal bone
- Effect: reduces circumferential bone resorption
- Clinical evidence to 2013: consistently supports PS for bone preservation
- Simple, effective technique for long-term peri-implant bone maintenance

## 7. Glossary
- **PS**: Platform switching — use of smaller-diameter abutment on larger implant collar
- **PM**: Platform matching — abutment matches implant platform diameter
- **MBL**: Marginal bone loss — radiographic bone level change around implant
- **TL**: Tissue-level implant — transmucosal component integral to implant
- **BL**: Bone-level implant — implant platform at crestal bone level
