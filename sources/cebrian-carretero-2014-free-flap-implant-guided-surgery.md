## One-line Summary
Narrative review on guided implant surgery for dental rehabilitation in patients reconstructed with microvascular free flaps after maxillomandibular oncologic resection.

## 1. Document Information
- **Title:** Predictable dental rehabilitation in maxillomandibular reconstruction with free flaps. The role of implant guided surgery

## 2. Key Contributions
Articulates why guided surgery is essential (not optional) in free-flap-reconstructed jaws: bone variability + osteosynthesis hardware + biomechanical demands require pre-planning.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Narrative review summarizing free flap options (fibula, iliac crest, scapula) and the planning workflow for implant-guided surgery in this context.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Guided surgery enables (1) implant positioning in the highest-quality bone regions, (2) avoidance of osteosynthesis hardware, (3) prosthetically-driven planning despite anatomic constraints.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Narrative review; case-illustration based; no quantitative outcome synthesis.
