## Why Ingested

미백 후 치아 민감도(가장 흔한 부작용)가 농도·제제별로 어떻게 달라지는지 정량화하는 핵심 근거. 새 `tooth-whitening` 카테고리에서 [[tooth-whitening/de-geus-2025-at-home-vs-in-office-bleaching]]의 "자가미백 민감도 강도 더 낮음" 결론을 농도 단위로 분해한다. 미백 민감도 관리는 [[overviews/dentin-hypersensitivity-mechanism-diagnosis-treatment-synthesis]]의 탈민감 전략과 연결. abstract-only — full text not retrieved.

## One-line Summary

Bayesian network meta-analysis (77 studies, 50 meta-analyzed) — higher at-home peroxide concentrations (CP 20–22%, HP 14–16%) raise tooth-sensitivity risk ~2.8–2.9× vs low (HP 2–4%/CP 10–11%); on average sensitivity is mild for all concentrations (low QoE).

## 한줄요약

베이지안 NMA (77편, 50편 메타분석) — 고농도 자가미백(CP 20–22%, HP 14–16%)은 저농도(HP 2–4%/CP 10–11%) 대비 민감도 위험 약 2.8–2.9배. 다만 전반적으로 민감도는 경미(근거 수준 낮음).

## 1. Document Information

- Study type: SR + Bayesian network meta-analysis. PROSPERO CRD42021265220.
- Population: Permanent dentition, at-home bleaching only.
- Outcomes: Tooth sensitivity (TS) risk and intensity (VAS 0–10, NRS 0–4).
- Included: 77 studies (50 in meta-analysis). RoB 2.0; GRADE.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First NMA ranking at-home **concentration tiers** by sensitivity (not just bleach vs placebo).
- Provides a concentration-stratified safety gradient clinicians can use to titrate prescriptions.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Bayesian NMA pooling HP and CP concentration nodes via common comparators.
- Dual pain scales (VAS, NRS) analyzed separately — results diverged (NRS significant, VAS not).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **TS risk** higher for: CP 20–22% vs HP 2–4% (RR 2.8, 95% CrI 1.15–8.16); HP 14–16% vs HP 2–4% (RR 2.9, 1.06–9.21); CP 20–22% vs CP 10–11% (RR 2.0, 1.07–4.31).
- **TS intensity (NRS)** higher for CP 20–22% (MD 0.51, 0.14–0.91) and HP 7–8% (MD 0.50, 0.10–0.84) vs CP 10–11%.
- **VAS scale**: no significant differences.
- Average TS is **mild** across all agents/concentrations. QoE: **low**; most studies high RoB.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Low certainty; high RoB; protocol heterogeneity.
- VAS vs NRS discordance complicates threshold setting.
- Abstract-only ingest.

## 6. Related Work

- de Geus 2025 — at-home vs in-office (technique-level sensitivity).
- Dentin-hypersensitivity desensitizer literature (potassium nitrate, fluoride) in `wiki/dentin-hypersensitivity/`.

## 7. Glossary

- **CP / HP**: carbamide peroxide / hydrogen peroxide (10% CP ≈ 3.5% HP).
- **NMA**: network meta-analysis — ranks ≥3 treatments via direct + indirect comparisons.
- **CrI**: credible interval (Bayesian analogue of CI).
- **TS**: tooth sensitivity (bleaching-induced).
