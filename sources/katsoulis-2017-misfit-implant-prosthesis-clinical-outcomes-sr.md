## Why Ingested

무치악에서 인접 임플란트를 평행하게 심어야 하는 이유 = 비평행 식립 → 프레임워크 미스피트(misfit) → 임상 합병증. 이 SR이 misfit의 정의·측정법·임상 영향을 가장 체계적으로 정리한 Foundation for Oral Rehabilitation 2016 합의 기준 논문. [[implants/del-fabbro-2022-full-arch-tilted-axial-implants-sr-ma]] 의 경사 임플란트 보철 설계 결정에 보완 근거.

## One-line Summary

SR (10 in vivo studies: 5 human + 5 animal): marginal misfit of screw-retained implant-supported fixed dentures is associated with screw-related complications; biological sequelae (bone loss) could not be confirmed; tolerable misfit limit remains debated.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰 (생체내 10편): 나사 유지 임플란트 고정성 보철의 변연 미스피트는 나사 관련 합병증과 연관; 골 소실 등 생물학적 영향은 확인 불가; 허용 가능 미스피트 한계는 논쟁 중.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: European Journal of Oral Implantology (Suppl 1) — 2016 FOR Consensus Conference
- **Study type**: Systematic review + consensus
- **PICO**: For edentulous subjects with screw-retained IFDs, does marginal misfit affect clinical outcomes?
- **Search**: PubMed/MEDLINE + hand search, English in vivo studies to August 2016
- **Included**: 10 studies (5 human + 5 animal)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Definitive reference for framework misfit definition, assessment methods, and clinical relevance
- Establishes that misfit causes SCREW complications more than biological bone loss
- Foundation for Oral Rehabilitation 2016 consensus position paper

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Initial search: 2,626 records → 10 included
- Misfit assessed by: linear distortion, vertical gap, strains, screw torque
- Human studies: 2 prospective (6 and 12 months) + 4 retrospective (3–32 years)
- Animal studies: <12 weeks observation

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **Screw-related complications**: observed in human studies with higher misfit
- **Biological sequelae (bone loss)**: could NOT be confirmed
- **Animal studies**: bone adaptation and implant displacement found histologically
- **Meta-analysis**: not possible (heterogeneous designs and misfit assessment methods)
- **Conclusion**: insufficient evidence to define maximum tolerable misfit
- **Clinical guidance**: aim for least misfit possible; gaps and static strains do not appear to negatively affect initial osseointegration or peri-implant bone stability long-term, but increase screw complication risk

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Only 10 studies; heterogeneous designs
- No standardized misfit measurement method
- Short observation periods in most studies
- Could not perform meta-analysis

## 7. Glossary
- **Marginal misfit (변연 미스피트)**: gap at the implant-prosthesis interface when framework is seated
- **Passive fit (수동 적합)**: ideal state where framework seats without inducing stress
- **IFD**: implant-supported fixed denture
- **Static strain**: prestress in peri-implant bone and implant components from a non-passively seated framework
