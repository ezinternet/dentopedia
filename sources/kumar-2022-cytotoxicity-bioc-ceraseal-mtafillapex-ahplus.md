## Why Ingested

Seeds the wiki's first CeraSeal / premixed bioceramic-sealer cytotoxicity anchor, giving the cluster a head-to-head L929 MTT baseline that later CeraSeal pages (clinical and bench) can lean on. Reinforces [[endodontics/spinelli-2024-three-year-single-cone-ceraseal-cohort]] by supplying the in-vitro biocompatibility rationale behind CeraSeal's clinical outcomes.

Source: PubMed Central full text (PMCID PMC10003288), DOI 10.4103/jcd.jcd_320_22.

## One-line Summary

In-vitro L929 fibroblast MTT/microscopy study (n=36, 4 sealers x 0 h/24 h/7 d) showing calcium-silicate sealers CeraSeal and Bio-C drop from severe cytotoxicity at 0 h to noncytotoxic (>90% viability) by 24 h, while AH Plus stays severely cytotoxic at all time points and MTA-Fillapex remains moderate.

## 1. Document Information

- **Title:** Cytotoxicity evaluation of Bio-C, CeraSeal, MTA-Fillapex, and AH Plus root canal sealers by microscopic and MTT assay
- **Journal:** Journal of Conservative Dentistry (J Conserv Dent)
- **Year:** 2022 (date 2022-12-08)
- **PMID:** 36908731 | **PMCID:** PMC10003288 | **DOI:** 10.4103/jcd.jcd_320_22
- **Study type:** in-vitro cytotoxicity (L929 mouse fibroblast, MTT assay + phase-contrast microscopy)
- **Source:** PubMed Central full text

## 2. Key Contributions

- Direct head-to-head cytotoxicity comparison of two premixed calcium-silicate bioceramic sealers (CeraSeal [Meta Biomed, Korea]; Bio-C [Angelus, Brazil]) against MTA-Fillapex (salicylate resin + MTA) and the epoxy-resin gold standard AH Plus.
- Time-resolved design (0 h freshly mixed, 24 h, 7 days) showing cytotoxicity is set-state dependent: calcium-silicate sealers are severely toxic when fresh but recover to noncytotoxic after setting.
- CeraSeal posted the highest viability of all four sealers at 24 h and 7 days; AH Plus was the only sealer that never escaped the "severe" band even fully set.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Cells:** L929 mouse fibroblasts (ATCC), seeded 5 x 10^4 per well in 96-well plates, subconfluent monolayer.
- **Samples:** all sealers mixed per manufacturer; Teflon molds 4 mm x 2 mm. Eluted per ISO-10993-12:2021 at extraction ratio 0.1 g/ml. Three discs per material per time period (0 h / 24 h / 7 d) = 9 per material, total n=36.
- **Exposure:** 100 uL elute to 6 wells per material per time period; 48 h incubation. Positive control 130 mg% phenol; negative control untreated cells.
- **Readouts:** MTT assay (1 mg/mL in PBS, absorbance 550 nm) for % viability; phase-contrast microscopy at x20 graded per US Pharmacopeia elution-test reactivity.
- **Cytotoxicity bands (% viability vs control):** severe <30%, moderate 30-60%, slight 61-90%, noncytotoxic >90%.
- **Stats:** one-way ANOVA + Tukey's post hoc (between groups), repeated-measures ANOVA + Bonferroni (within group over time); P < 0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Mean % cell viability (+/- SD), n=6 per cell:

| Sealer | 0 h | 24 h | 7 days |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CeraSeal** | 29.94 +/- 0.90 (severe) | 96.39 +/- 1.45 (noncytotoxic) | 97.19 +/- 1.51 (noncytotoxic) |
| **Bio-C** | 29.80 +/- 2.34 (severe) | 91.43 +/- 1.34 (noncytotoxic) | 93.58 +/- 2.60 (noncytotoxic) |
| **MTA-Fillapex** | 14.94 +/- 1.49 (severe) | 51.47 +/- 2.99 (moderate) | 59.65 +/- 2.19 (moderate) |
| **AH Plus** | 12.49 +/- 2.57 (severe) | 22.73 +/- 2.73 (severe) | 28.89 +/- 2.00 (severe) |

- CeraSeal vs Bio-C: not significant at 0 h (P=0.99); CeraSeal slightly higher at 24 h (+4.96, P=0.006) and 7 d (+3.62, P=0.04).
- All calcium-silicate vs resin comparisons significant at 24 h and 7 d (P < 0.001); CeraSeal vs AH Plus +73.66 at 24 h.
- Microscopy corroborated MTT: CeraSeal/Bio-C normal subconfluent monolayers with no lysis at 24 h–7 d; AH Plus severe morphologic damage throughout.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro only; does not model periapical washout by circulating blood (overstates real-tissue exposure).
- Small per-cell n (6); single fibroblast line (L929); single extraction ratio.
- Authors call for in-vivo subcutaneous implantation / endodontic usage-test animal models to confirm clinical relevance.

## 7. Glossary

- **MTT assay** — colorimetric test of mitochondrial dehydrogenase activity quantifying viable cells (absorbance at 550 nm).
- **L929** — standard mouse fibroblast cell line for ISO 10993-5 cytotoxicity screening.
- **Calcium-silicate / bioceramic sealer** — root canal sealer (e.g., CeraSeal, Bio-C) that hydrates to calcium silicate gel + calcium hydroxide, releasing Ca/OH/Si ions.
- **Elution / extraction (ISO 10993-12)** — incubating set material in medium to leach extractables, then dosing cells with the extract.
- **Cytotoxicity bands** — severe <30%, moderate 30-60%, slight 61-90%, noncytotoxic >90% viability vs control.
