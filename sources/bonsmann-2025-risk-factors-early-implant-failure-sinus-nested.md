## One-line Summary
JCP 2025 다기관 nested case-control study (8 독일 센터, 2016-2021, n=129 case + 273 control): sinus augmentation 후 조기 임플란트 실패 위험인자 — multivariable logistic regression OR 분석.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 첫 sinus augmentation-specific 다기관 nested case-control 디자인.
- 12개월 내 조기 실패 risk factors 정량.
- Multivariable confounder-adjusted OR.

## 3. Methodology
Nested case-control across 8 German centers (2016-2021); cases n=129 (early implant failure within 12 months); controls n=273; multivariable logistic regression.

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Specific OR for risk factors (smoking, periodontitis history, simultaneous vs delayed placement, sinus floor anatomy) reported in paper.

## 5. Limitations
- Single-country (Germany) 8 centers
- Early failure only (no long-term)
- Nested case-control selection bias risk
