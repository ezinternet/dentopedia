## One-line Summary
Controlled clinical trial (n=36, D3/D4 bone, 60 implants): bioactive nano-superhydrophilic surface maintains ISQ stability (no dip) vs traditional surface which shows significant ISQ decrease at 30/45 days; accelerated loading enabled.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Investigations 2024; 28:372
- **Study type**: Prospective controlled clinical trial

## 2. Key Contributions
- Demonstrates bioactive surface prevents ISQ stability dip in D3/D4 bone
- Traditional surface: significant ISQ decrease at T30 (p=0.01) and T45 (p=0.03) vs baseline
- Bioactive surface: no significant ISQ change at any timepoint — smoother primary→secondary stability transition
- Torque density (Ncm/sec) more predictive than IT or ISQ for early bone density

## 3. Key Results
- Test (bioactive): ISQ 74.57→74.78→74.97 (T0→T30→T45); no significant change
- Control (traditional): ISQ 77.12→73.33→73.44; significant decrease T30/T45 vs baseline
- ISQ ≥70 threshold: 26/30 test vs 23/30 control → earlier loading protocol in test group
- 6 control implants required 180-day delayed loading due to low ISQ
