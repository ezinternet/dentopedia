## One-line Summary
ESE-commissioned systematic review (28 studies) concluding that the effectiveness of clinically diagnosing pulpitis is low because no valid reference standard exists; a multi-test "diagnostic package" is the best current approach.

## 한줄요약
ESE 위촉 SR(28편) — 임상에서 치수염을 진단하는 정확도는 낮고(신뢰할 reference standard 부재), 여러 검사·증상을 묶은 "diagnostic package"가 현재 최선이라는 결론.

## 1. Document Information
- Int Endod J 2023;56(Suppl. 3):296–325. Review Article. DOI 10.1111/iej.13762.
- Basis for European Society of Endodontology (ESE) S3-level guideline.
- PROSPERO CRD42021265366. Münster / Szczecin.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Synthesizes diagnostic-accuracy evidence for pulp sensibility/vitality, biomarkers, and pulp bleeding against true pulp status.
- Exposes the central methodological problem: no clinical reference standard for "true" pulp condition.
- Proposes a combined "diagnostic package" (tests + symptoms + future molecular markers).
- Forms the evidentiary basis for ESE S3 guidelines on pulpitis diagnosis.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Three a priori PICO questions agreed by ESE (sensibility testing, biomarkers, pulp bleeding).
- Searched PubMed, Web of Science, Scopus, Google Scholar.
- 28 studies from 29 publications eligible: 12 on pulp vitality accuracy, 10 on pulpal-condition accuracy, 6 on biomarkers, 3 on prognostic/therapeutic factors.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Effectiveness of diagnosing pulpitis judged LOW overall.
- No reliable preoperative indicator of the true pulp status or of vital-pulp-treatment prognosis.
- Best available strategy: combination of clinical tests + symptoms, with molecular diagnosis as a future addition.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Limited number of well-designed clinical trials; reference-standard problem caps achievable accuracy.
- Heterogeneous tests/outcomes preclude meta-analysis.
- Calls for molecular/biomarker diagnostics and standardized reference definitions.

## 6. Related Work
- ESE position statements; histological vs clinical pulp-status correlation studies.
- Pulp sensibility vs vitality diagnostic-accuracy meta-analyses (Patro 2022).

## 7. Glossary
- Sensibility test: assesses neural response (EPT, cold, heat) — a proxy, not blood flow.
- Vitality test: assesses blood supply (pulse oximetry, laser Doppler).
- Reference standard: an independent gold standard against which a test's accuracy is judged; clinically absent for pulp status.
