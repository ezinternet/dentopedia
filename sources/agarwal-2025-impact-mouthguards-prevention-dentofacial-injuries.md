## Why Ingested

Supplies the PREVENTION arm of the new `dental-trauma` category — the only intervention that stops avulsions/fractures before they happen — with an AMSTAR-2-appraised umbrella review (top of the evidence pyramid here). Closes the loop with the downstream emergency-management pages [[dental-trauma/debrier-2020-storage-avulsed-tooth-prior]] and [[dental-trauma/kumari-2025-effect-splinting-outcomes-replantation-avulsed]].

## One-line Summary

Umbrella review (9 SRs, AMSTAR-2; variable/low underlying quality): mouthguards significantly reduce dentofacial injuries — especially avulsions and fractures — with custom-made designs superior in protection and comfort; performance effects mixed.

## 한줄요약

엄브렐러 리뷰 (SR 9편, AMSTAR-2; 근거질 가변·낮음): 마우스가드(MG)는 치아안면 손상, 특히 탈구·파절을 유의하게 감소시키며 맞춤형(custom-made)이 보호·착용감에서 우수하다. 운동수행 영향은 혼재.

## 1. Document Information

- Type: Umbrella review (overview of systematic reviews).
- Journal: J Indian Soc Pedod Prev Dent 2025;43(2):163-172.
- Appraisal tools: AMSTAR 2 (quality), Corrected Covered Area (overlap).
- Abstract-only — full text not retrieved (PMC unavailable).

## 2. Key Contributions

- Synthesizes 9 SRs into a single prevention statement at the umbrella level.
- Establishes custom-made > stock/boil-and-bite on protection and comfort.
- Flags methodological weakness across the underlying SR base (AMSTAR-2 low for several).

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- PRIOR-guided overview-of-reviews; databases: MEDLINE, EMBASE, CINAHL, Scopus, Web of Science, Cochrane + grey literature.
- 466 records → 9 SRs included.
- Dual-reviewer extraction; AMSTAR 2 quality; CCA overlap index.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Mouthguards significantly reduce dentofacial injuries (notably avulsions, fractures).
- Custom-made: superior protection and comfort vs other types.
- Athletic-performance effect: mixed; some signal of improved occlusion/neuromuscular alignment.
- Strength of conclusions limited by methodological variability and low quality of several SRs.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Underlying SRs heterogeneous and often low quality (AMSTAR-2).
- English-only inclusion; observational underpinnings.
- Recommends mandatory MG-use policies + athlete education; higher-quality MG-design trials needed.

## 7. Glossary

- Mouthguard (MG): intraoral protective appliance; stock, boil-and-bite, or custom-made (lab-fabricated).
- Umbrella review: systematic review of systematic reviews.
- AMSTAR 2: tool for appraising methodological quality of systematic reviews.
- CCA: Corrected Covered Area — measures primary-study overlap across included SRs.
