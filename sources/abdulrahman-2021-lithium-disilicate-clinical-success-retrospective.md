## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Retrospective analysis of 87,203 lithium disilicate (IPS e.max CAD, CEREC) restorations in 4 types: inlay/onlay, veneers, single crowns, FPDs. Data from B&R Dental Center March 2015–June 2020. Kaplan-Meier overall cumulative survival: 85.08% at 5 years. Inlay/onlay: 99.4%, veneer: 98.6% (highest). FPD: 52.9% (significantly lowest, p<0.00001). Mean time to failure: 52.4 months. Failure causes: ceramic fracture, debonding, marginal adaptation, secondary caries.
