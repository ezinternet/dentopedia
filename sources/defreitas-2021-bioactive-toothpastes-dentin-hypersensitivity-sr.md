## Why Ingested

[[dentin-hypersensitivity/joshi-2013-novamin-gluma-dentinal-tubule-occlusion-sem]]의 NovaMin(bioactive glass) 기전을 임상 RCT 수준으로 검증. [[dentin-hypersensitivity/martins-2022-desensitizing-toothpastes-formulations-scoping-review]]와 함께 OTC 탈감작 치약 근거 묶음 구성.

## One-line Summary
SR (15 RCTs; 7 low / 4 high / 4 moderate risk of bias) concluding low-concentration bioactive compounds (2.5–7.5%) are effective for DH at-home and in-office.

## 한줄요약
SR(RCT 15편): 저농도(2.5–7.5%) 생활성(bioactive) 화합물 함유 치약이 가정용·진료실용 모두 DH에 효과적.

## 1. Document Information
- Saudi Dental Journal 2021. PROSPERO-registered, PRISMA; 4 databases Feb–Mar 2020 + gray literature.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Synthesizes RCT evidence for bioactive-glass dentifrices specifically.
- Maps risk of bias across 15 included studies.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- PROSPERO + PRISMA; PubMed/Medline, CENTRAL, Web of Science, LILACS; hand-search + gray literature; no language/date limits.
- 15 studies evaluated (7 low, 4 high, 4 moderate RoB).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Bioactive compounds at low concentrations (2.5–7.5%) reduce DH; usable at-home and in-office.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Mixed risk of bias; heterogeneous bioactive formulations/concentrations.

## 6. Related Work
- Clinical confirmation of bioactive-glass mechanism shown by tubule-occlusion SEM work.

## 7. Glossary
- Bioactive glass: e.g., calcium sodium phosphosilicate (NovaMin), bioglass 45S5.
