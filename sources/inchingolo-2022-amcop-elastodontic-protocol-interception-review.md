## Why Ingested

AMCOP (Cranium-Occluded-Postural Multifunctional Harmonizers) Bio-activators are a core EF-Line / elastodontic interceptive-orthodontics protocol, and this paper is the foundational device-and-protocol description: it catalogs the full AMCOP device family (D/DC, BASIC, INTEGRAL, SC, TC, INTEGRAL PLUS, OPEN, ELASTO-OSAS) and the standard active/retention wear schedule. It reinforces and gives the device-level grounding for the broader scoping review in [[wiki/orthodontics/myofunctional/ureni-2024-elastodontic-devices-sagittal-malocclusion-scoping-review]] and the children-focused review in [[wiki/orthodontics/myofunctional/ronsivalle-2023-elastodontic-appliances-malocclusion-interception-children-review]], anchoring the new `orthodontics/myofunctional` subcategory.

## One-line Summary

Case series (4 of 38 developing-dentition children, ages 7–10) plus literature review showing AMCOP elastodontic Bio-activators corrected Class I/II/III, deep-bite, open-bite and atypical-swallowing malocclusions with no follow-up fixed appliances, over ~12–20-month active+retention protocols.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Retrospective case series (n = 4 illustrative cases drawn from 38 treated children) + narrative literature review
- **Setting**: Dental Unit, University of Bari, Italy
- **Journal**: Int J Environ Res Public Health 2022;19(2):988
- **DOI**: 10.3390/ijerph19020988 · PMID 35055811 · PMCID PMC8775806
- **Device under study**: AMCOP Bio-activators (Micerium spa patent) — preformed silicone-elastomer/polymer elastodontic appliances
- **Ethics**: University of Bari classified the study exempt (negligible risk, non-identifiable existing data); written informed consent obtained.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **First consolidated description of the AMCOP Bio-activator family** as an elastodontic interceptive protocol, with device-by-device indications.
2. **Defines the "elastodontic space" concept** — AMCOP devices have NO indentations; they create the ideal space between tongue and lips, so dental alignment results from restored neuromuscular function rather than device-induced tooth movement (claimed to give stable, long-lasting results).
3. **Demonstrates single-phase resolution** of malocclusions in 4 illustrative cases (Class I deep-bite/atypical swallowing, Class III, Class II deep-bite, skeletal open-bite) requiring no subsequent fixed appliances.
4. **Positions AMCOP within the historical evolution of functional appliances** (Pierre-Robin monobloc 1902 → Andresen activator 1909 → Kesling positioner 1945 → Soulet-Besombes rubber activator 1950s → Bergersen Occlus-O-Guide/EGA 1975 → AMCOP).
5. **Notes AMCOP is, uniquely, applied to Class III** (TC device), whereas prior elastomeric literature excluded Class III a priori.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**AMCOP Bio-activator device family** (two flanges — vestibular + lingual — delimiting a free central area; lingual ramp + tongue button for posture/function; thermoactive superelastic material chairside-adjustable; four arch shapes):

- **D / DC** — deciduous dentition, very young children; DC has a "pacifier" grip. Functional/orthopedic-osteopathic action; for transverse deficit and open bite from prolonged pacifier use; replaces the pacifier.
- **BASIC** — First Class; occlusal plane thicker anteriorly to raise vertical dimension in deep-bite patients.
- **INTEGRAL** — First Class; flat occlusal plane; orthopedic action, correction of occlusal-curve alterations (anterior/lateral open bite), arch balancing.
- **SC (Second Class)** — mandibular anterior sliding plane promoting mandibular advancement; corrects overjet via upper-incisor retroinclination + lower-incisor proinclination; improves TMJ dysfunction.
- **TC (Third Class)** — positions upper teeth on an anterior sliding plane while exerting posterior pressure on the lower arch to brake mandibular growth.
- **INTEGRAL PLUS** — internal groove for use with multibracket appliances.
- **OPEN** — posteriorly raised occlusal plane; for skeletal open bite.
- **ELASTO-OSAS** — front sliding plane + accentuated lingual shield for snoring / sleep apnea, promoting correct tongue-on-palate posture.

**Standard wear protocol** (across cases): active phase — device worn every night + 1 h during the day for ~6 months, then overnight only for a further 6–12 months; followed by a retention/restraint phase (e.g. every other night). Speech therapy adjunct in atypical-swallowing cases. Cephalometric analysis (DeltaDent / Delta software) pre- and post-treatment.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Case 1** — 10-y female, atypical swallowing, mixed dentition, mild molar Class II (right)/Class I (left), deep bite, vestibular inclination of frontal teeth; skeletal Class I (SNA 82°, SNB 78.6°, ANB 3.4°, Wits 1.5 mm). Treated with INTEGRAL S + speech therapy (every night + 1 h/day × 6 mo, then overnight × 6 mo, then restraint every other night). Outcome: dentoskeletal Class I, corrected overjet/overbite, tongue reeducation, atypical-swallowing correction. 3-year follow-up photos reported.

**Case 2** — 7-y Caucasian female, prognathic mandible/concave profile, molar+canine Class III, anterior crossbite; skeletal Class III (SNA 82.4°, SNB 83.2°, ANB −0.8°, Wits −8.5 mm). Treated with AMCOP TC (every night + 1 h/day × 6 mo, then overnight × 6 mo + restraint). Outcome: molar+canine Class I, proper overjet/overbite, improved skeletal class — but strict ongoing control of occlusion and mandibular growth advised.

**Case 3** — 7-y male, convex profile, deep bite, augmented overjet, vestibular inclination of upper incisors; skeletal Class II (ANB 7.7°, Wits 4.2), esoinclined teeth (IS^II 103°, IS^NS 119°, II^GoGn 104°). Treated with INTEGRAL OS × 8 mo then SC × 12 mo (night + 1 h/day × 6 mo, then night only). Outcome: ANB 7.7°→3.2°, Wits 4.2→−0.8 mm, IS^NS 119°→103.4°, IS^II 103°→122°, adequate overjet/overbite.

**Case 4** — 8-y female, long face, incompetent lips, anterior open bite, upper-arch contraction, lack of eruption space, atypical swallowing, prolonged pacifier history; mild skeletal Class II (SNA 74.5°, SNB 70.3°, ANB 4.2°, Wits 0.1 mm) + skeletal open bite (AnsPns^GoGn 37°, SN^GoGn 42.1°). Treated with speech therapy + AMCOP OPEN + elastodontic restraint (night + 1 h/day × 6 mo, then night only × 12 mo). Outcome: open bite corrected (AnsPns^GoGn 37°→23.3°, SN^GoGn 42.1°→36.1°), upper-arch expansion enabling correct permanent-tooth eruption, restored lip/tongue muscle function. 3-year follow-up photos reported.

**Literature-review benchmarks cited:**
- Interceptive treatment corrects developing mixed-dentition problems in ~15% of cases and improves them in ~49%.
- Twin Block failure rates up to **34%** (compliance); a 2021 study reported high compliance with AMCOP despite augmented salivation.
- Occlus-O-Guide series (167 children): overjet 3.1→1.9 mm, overbite 3.2→2.1 mm, good incisor alignment in **98%**.
- CVM: mandibular growth peak between the 2nd and 3rd cervical-vertebral maturational stages (5-stage Baccetti/Franchi/McNamara method).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only 4 illustrative cases reported (of 38 treated); retrospective, uncontrolled, single-center.
- **No long-term follow-up for all cases** — only cases #1 and #4 have 3-year follow-up photos.
- Authors explicitly state the **lack of RCTs** in the literature makes a systematic-review / meta-analysis approach inapplicable, reflecting the novelty of the topic.
- AMCOP/elastodontics evidence base is sparse; more clinical studies with adequate follow-up needed to confirm long-term effectiveness.
- Bio-activators cannot correct tooth rotations or severe skeletal dysmorphosis.

## 7. Glossary

- **AMCOP** — Armonizzatori Multifunzionali Cranio-Occluso-Posturali (Cranium-Occluded-Postural Multifunctional Harmonizers); Micerium-patented elastodontic Bio-activators.
- **Elastodontics** — interceptive orthodontic treatment using removable preformed elastomeric devices that apply light biological forces.
- **Bio-activator** — AMCOP elastodontic appliance with vestibular + lingual flanges, free central area, lingual ramp and tongue button; no indentations.
- **Elastodontic space** — the ideal space between tongue and lips created by an indentation-free device, allowing teeth to align via restored neuromuscular function.
- **Interceptive orthodontics** — elimination of existing interferences in dentition development (vs preventive = prevention of potential interferences; Ackerman & Proffit).
- **CVM** — Cervical Vertebral Maturation; skeletal-maturity indicator for the adolescent growth spurt.
- **EGA / Occlus-O-Guide** — Eruption Guidance Appliance (Bergersen 1975), an elastomeric precursor.
- **OSAS** — Obstructive Sleep Apnea Syndrome (ELASTO-OSAS device indication).
