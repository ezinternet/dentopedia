## Why Ingested

[[wiki/inlay/behera-2021-lithium-disilicate-vs-zirconia-inlays-1year]]는 1년 임상에서 지르코니아 인레이의 변연 적합성(marginal fit)을 다뤘지만, 어떤 레진 시멘트와 어떤 지르코니아 세대(3Y vs 4Y/5Y)가 적합성에 영향을 주는지, 그리고 thermomechanical aging이 변연 간격을 얼마나 악화시키는지는 미해결 과제였다. 본 in-vitro 연구(Al Fodeh 2026)는 64개 발거 소구치 표본에서 지르코니아 재료 × 레진 시멘트 × 노화의 3-way 설계로 이 격차를 직접 메운다. 또한 [[wiki/inlay/bomicke-2025-3ytzp-inlay-enamel-wear-5year]]의 3Y-TZP 인레이 임상 데이터를 bench-level 적합성 근거로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In-vitro study (n=64 extracted premolars, MOD zirconia inlays): both 3Y and 4Y/5Y monolithic zirconia achieved clinically acceptable marginal fit (50-120 μm); RelyX Unicem outperformed Panavia F 2.0 on marginal gap, and 1.2M-cycle thermomechanical aging significantly widened marginal gaps, while internal fit (111.95-163.16 μm) was unaffected by material, cement, or aging.

## 한줄요약

In-vitro 연구 (발거 소구치 64개, MOD 지르코니아 인레이): 3Y와 4Y/5Y 단일구조 지르코니아 모두 임상적으로 허용 가능한 변연 적합도(50-120 μm)를 달성했고, RelyX Unicem이 Panavia F 2.0보다 변연 간격이 작았으며, 120만 회 열기계적 노화는 변연 간격을 유의하게 넓혔으나 내면 적합도(111.95-163.16 μm)는 재료·시멘트·노화에 영향받지 않았다.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Directly compares two contemporary monolithic zirconia generations for inlay fit — Zolid Gen-X (4Y-TZP, uniform color-gradient, flexural strength 700-1000 MPa) vs IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime (3Y-TZP cervical / 5Y-TZP incisal gradient, ~1200 MPa cervical) — finding no significant marginal-fit difference between them (occlusal P = 0.417; proximal P = 0.108).
2. Isolates resin cement as an independent variable: self-adhesive RelyX Unicem produced significantly smaller marginal gaps than MDP-containing Panavia F 2.0 at both occlusal (P = 0.031) and proximal (P = 0.006) surfaces.
3. Quantifies the cost of thermomechanical aging (1.2 million dynamic loading cycles at 50 N/1.6 Hz + thermocycling 5/55 °C, representing ~5 years of mastication): occlusal marginal gaps increased from 57.4 ± 20.1 μm to 74.3 ± 21.3 μm (P < 0.001); proximal gaps from 74.6 ± 19.9 μm to 93.2 ± 16.0 μm (P < 0.001).
4. Identifies a significant Cement × Aging interaction for occlusal marginal fit (P = 0.008): Panavia showed a 21.1 μm / 37.9% increase vs RelyX's 12.3 μm / 20.8% increase after aging, indicating differential durability.
5. Separates marginal from internal fit — internal fit (range 111.95-163.16 μm across subgroups) was stable across all factors (material P = 0.089; cement P = 0.183; aging P = 0.403) but exceeded the 50-100 μm ideal target; adjusting for the 50 μm design spacer yields actual cement thicknesses of 77.4 μm (Zolid Gen-X) and 98.1 μm (IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime), both within the acceptable range.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Specimen preparation**
- 64 caries-free extracted maxillary premolars (pooled biobank, irreversibly anonymized, no additional ethics approval required) screened under optical microscope at 12.5×; measured mesio-distally and bucco-palatally with digital caliper.
- Roots embedded in epoxy resin molds 2 mm below CEJ; roots coated with two layers of duplication silicone (Elite Double 32, Zhermack) to simulate 0.2 mm periodontal ligament space.
- Standardized MOD inlay preparations by a single operator: 2 mm isthmus width, 2 mm occlusal reduction, proximal box 2-2.5 mm wide, gingival floor 1 mm above CEJ, 6-10° convergence; verified with silicone indices and digital calipers.
- Sample size: G*Power 3.1.9.2, effect size 0.666 (from Attia & Kern 2004), α = 0.05, power 0.80 → 64 teeth / 8 per subgroup.

**CAD/CAM fabrication**
- Trios 3 intra-oral scanner (3Shape) → Ceramill design software (Amann Girrbach) with 50 μm virtual spacer layer → 5-axis milling (Ceramill Motion 2) → sintered per manufacturer instructions.
- Group 1: Ceramill Zolid Gen-X (4Y-TZP; ≥99% ZrO₂, 6-7% Y₂O₃, ≤5% HfO₂, ≤0.5% Al₂O₃; flexural strength 700-1000 MPa).
- Group 2: IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime (3Y-TZP/5Y-TZP gradient; 88-95.5% ZrO₂, 4.5-7% Y₂O₃, ≤5% HfO₂, ≤1% Al₂O₃; ~1200 MPa cervical layer flexural strength).

**Cementation**
- All inlays sandblasted with 50 μm Al₂O₃ at 0.25 bar for 10 s, ultrasonic cleaning 3 min, double-coated Z-PRIME Plus primer.
- Selective enamel etching 15 s before cement application.
- Subgroup A (n=16/material): RelyX Unicem (3M ESPE; self-adhesive resin cement, no separate primer/etch).
- Subgroup B (n=16/material): Panavia F 2.0 (Kuraray; MDP-containing self-etching universal adhesive cement).
- Constant load: 10 N for 5 min via custom gripping device; excess cement partial-cure 2-3 s → removal → final light-cure 20 s per surface (Woodpecker LED D).

**Aging protocol**
- Control (n=8/subgroup): 37 °C distilled water for 24 h.
- Aged (n=8/subgroup): thermomechanical aging — 1,200,000 cycles at 50 N, 1.6 Hz, stainless-steel spherical indenter (6 mm) on central occlusal fossa, + thermocycling 5/55 °C (representing ~5 years mastication).

**Measurement**
- Marginal fit: stereomicroscope (Kruss MSZ5600) at 20× — 20 locations per restoration (6 occlusal, 7 mesial, 7 distal). Pre-aging and post-aging measurements at marked reference points. Single trained examiner.
- Internal fit: specimens sectioned (IsoMet Low Speed Precision Cutter; 15HC Diamond Wafering Blades) → gold-palladium sputter-coated → SEM at 200× at 7 internal reference points (N-T: gingival floor midpoint, axiogingival, axiopulpal for mesial/distal boxes; Q: pulpal floor midpoint).

**Statistics**
- Kolmogorov-Smirnov and Shapiro-Wilk normality tests → data normally distributed.
- Three-way ANOVA; post-hoc Tukey; α = 0.05; SPSS v29.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Marginal fit — occlusal surface (μm, mean ± SD)**

| Subgroup | Control | Aged |
|---|---|---|
| Zolid Gen-X + Panavia | 59.31 ± 26.51 | 84.3 ± 20.96 |
| Zolid Gen-X + RelyX | 58.23 ± 16.37 | 53.90 ± 7.38 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + Panavia | 53.03 ± 22.18 | 88.17 ± 20.94 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + RelyX | 59.23 ± 17.13 | 70.85 ± 16.26 |

Group means (occlusal): Zolid Gen-X 59.1 ± 18.2 μm vs IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime 63.2 ± 21.5 μm (NS, P = 0.417). RelyX 60.5 ± 15.47 μm vs Panavia 71.16 ± 26.59 μm (P = 0.031). Non-aged 57.4 ± 20.1 μm vs aged 74.3 ± 21.3 μm (P < 0.001).

**Marginal fit — proximal surfaces (μm, mean ± SD)**

| Subgroup | Control | Aged |
|---|---|---|
| Zolid Gen-X + Panavia | 85.89 ± 11.63 | 92.67 ± 12.99 |
| Zolid Gen-X + RelyX | 61.72 ± 9.54 | 81.60 ± 8.60 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + Panavia | 75.42 ± 29.52 | 105.22 ± 22.72 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + RelyX | 75.39 ± 17.69 | 93.11 ± 7.70 |

Group means (proximal): Zolid Gen-X 85.4 ± 17.6 μm vs IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime 83.9 ± 20.4 μm (NS, P = 0.108). RelyX 77.96 ± 15.93 μm vs Panavia 89.80 ± 22.42 μm (P = 0.006). Non-aged 74.6 ± 19.9 μm vs aged 93.2 ± 16.0 μm (P < 0.001).

**Interaction — Cement × Aging (occlusal, P = 0.008)**
- Panavia: +21.1 μm (37.9%) increase after aging.
- RelyX: +12.3 μm (20.8%) increase after aging.
- No significant three-way or other two-way interactions (all P > 0.05).

**Internal fit (μm, mean ± SD)**

| Subgroup | Control | Aged |
|---|---|---|
| Zolid Gen-X + Panavia | 132.00 ± 62.26 | 143.74 ± 62.21 |
| Zolid Gen-X + RelyX | 121.79 ± 17.76 | 111.95 ± 52.20 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + Panavia | 157.11 ± 73.56 | 127.33 ± 82.07 |
| IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime + RelyX | 163.16 ± 25.89 | 144.88 ± 21.04 |

Overall: Zolid Gen-X mean 127.4 ± 50.8 μm; IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime 148.1 ± 56.5 μm (P = 0.089). Non-aged 136.2 ± 53.1 μm; aged 139.3 ± 55.2 μm (P = 0.403). Significant regional variation by anatomical location (P < 0.001): axiogingival angles highest (~175 ± 42 μm), axiopulpal lowest (~121 ± 38 μm).

After subtracting the designed 50 μm digital spacer: actual cement thickness ≈ 77.4 μm (Zolid Gen-X) and 98.1 μm (IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime) — both within the recommended 50-100 μm range.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro design: single laboratory operator for both preparation and measurement; results may not generalize across operators or to clinical conditions (saliva, soft-tissue, variable occlusal forces).
- Limited sampling density: 20 marginal and 7 internal measurement points — 40% of the ≥50 points recommended by recent SR (Nawafleh 2013; Boitelle 2014); may miss localized discrepancies, especially for internal fit.
- Only two cements and two zirconia products; results may not generalize to other MDP-based or low-shrinkage cement systems.
- Internal fit values exceeded ideal 50-100 μm range across all groups; clinical significance depends on biomechanical context beyond this study.
- No fracture-strength, microleakage, or wear endpoints — fit only.
- Aging simulates 5 years of mastication; longer-term degradation not evaluated.
- Future work: next-generation low-shrinkage resin cements; enzymatic aging protocols; CAD/CAM strategies for improved internal adaptation; >5-year clinical studies.

## 7. Glossary

- **Monolithic zirconia**: full-contour zirconia restoration with no veneering porcelain, avoiding chipping risk.
- **3Y-TZP / 4Y-TZP / 5Y-TZP**: yttria-stabilized tetragonal zirconia polycrystal with 3, 4, or 5 mol% yttria; higher yttria → more translucency, lower flexural strength.
- **Zolid Gen-X**: Amann Girrbach 4Y-TZP monolithic zirconia with uniform color-gradient composition; flexural strength 700-1000 MPa.
- **IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime**: Ivoclar Vivadent dual-layer (3Y-TZP cervical / 5Y-TZP incisal) strength-gradient zirconia; ~1200 MPa cervical.
- **Marginal fit / marginal gap**: vertical discrepancy at the restoration margin; clinically acceptable ≤120 μm.
- **Internal fit**: cement-space discrepancy between the intaglio surface of the restoration and the preparation walls/floor; ideal 50-100 μm.
- **CAD/CAM spacer**: programmed digital offset (here 50 μm) applied to internal surfaces during CAD design to ensure cement flow.
- **RelyX Unicem**: 3M ESPE self-adhesive resin cement; no separate primer/etch step required.
- **Panavia F 2.0**: Kuraray self-etching universal adhesive resin cement; contains MDP monomer for chemical adhesion to zirconia; higher polymerization shrinkage than RelyX.
- **MDP (10-methacryloyloxydecyl dihydrogen phosphate)**: functional monomer that bonds chemically to zirconia oxide surfaces.
- **Thermomechanical aging**: combined thermocycling (5/55 °C) + dynamic cyclic loading (1.2M cycles at 50 N) simulating ~5 years of intraoral function.
- **MOD inlay**: mesial-occlusal-distal inlay preparation covering the proximal contacts and occlusal surface.
