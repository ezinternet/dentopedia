## Why Ingested

[[wiki/inlay/behera-2021-lithium-disilicate-vs-zirconia-inlays-1year]]는 1년 임상에서 지르코니아 인레이의 변연 적합성(marginal fit)을 다뤘지만, 어떤 레진 시멘트와 어떤 지르코니아 세대(3Y vs 4Y/5Y)가 적합성에 영향을 주는지, 그리고 thermomechanical aging이 변연 간격을 얼마나 악화시키는지는 미해결 과제였다. 본 in-vitro 연구(Al Fodeh 2026)는 64개 발거 소구치 표본에서 지르코니아 재료 × 레진 시멘트 × 노화의 3-way 설계로 이 격차를 직접 메운다. 또한 [[wiki/inlay/bomicke-2025-3ytzp-inlay-enamel-wear-5year]]의 3Y-TZP 인레이 임상 데이터를 bench-level 적합성 근거로 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In-vitro study (n=64 extracted premolars, MOD zirconia inlays): both 3Y and 4Y/5Y monolithic zirconia achieved clinically acceptable marginal fit (50-120 μm); RelyX Unicem outperformed Panavia F 2.0 on marginal gap, and 1.2M-cycle thermomechanical aging significantly widened marginal gaps, while internal fit (111.95-163.16 μm) was unaffected by material, cement, or aging.

## 한줄요약

In-vitro 연구 (발거 소구치 64개, MOD 지르코니아 인레이): 3Y와 4Y/5Y 단일구조 지르코니아 모두 임상적으로 허용 가능한 변연 적합도(50-120 μm)를 달성했고, RelyX Unicem이 Panavia F 2.0보다 변연 간격이 작았으며, 120만 회 열기계적 노화는 변연 간격을 유의하게 넓혔으나 내면 적합도(111.95-163.16 μm)는 재료·시멘트·노화에 영향받지 않았다.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. Directly compares two contemporary monolithic zirconia generations for inlay fit — Zolid Gen-X (4Y-TZP) vs IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime (3Y-TZP cervical / 5Y-TZP incisal gradient) — finding no significant marginal-fit difference between them.
2. Isolates resin cement as an independent variable: self-adhesive RelyX Unicem produced significantly smaller marginal gaps than the MDP-containing Panavia F 2.0 at both occlusal and proximal margins.
3. Quantifies the cost of thermomechanical aging (1.2 million dynamic loading cycles + thermocycling 5/55 °C): marginal gaps significantly increased, demonstrating a long-term seal-degradation effect.
4. Separates marginal from internal fit — internal fit (111.95-163.16 μm) was stable across all factors but exceeded ideal targets, while marginal fit was the sensitive endpoint.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Specimens**: 64 caries-free extracted maxillary premolars embedded in acrylic resin molds, MOD inlay preparations by a single operator.
- **Factor 1 — material**: Zolid Gen-X (4Y-TZP, n=32) vs IPS e.max ZirCAD Prime (3Y/5Y gradient, n=32).
- **Factor 2 — cement**: RelyX Unicem (self-adhesive, n=16/group) vs Panavia F 2.0 (n=16/group).
- **Factor 3 — aging**: 37 °C / 24 hr storage (control) vs thermomechanical aging (1,200,000 dynamic loading cycles, thermocycling 5 & 55 °C).
- **Measurements**: marginal fit at 20 occlusal/proximal locations via stereomicroscope (20×); internal fit at 7 locations via SEM (200×).
- **Statistics**: three-way ANOVA + multivariate tests, α = 0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Marginal gap**: within clinically acceptable 50-120 μm range for both zirconia materials (no significant material effect).
- **Cement effect**: RelyX Unicem < Panavia F 2.0 marginal gap at both occlusal and proximal surfaces (P < 0.05).
- **Aging effect**: thermocycling + dynamic loading significantly increased marginal gaps (P < 0.05).
- **Internal fit**: 111.95-163.16 μm; no significant differences by material, cement, or aging (P > 0.05) — but above ideal targets.
- **Bottom line**: cement choice and aging are the decisive factors for marginal seal longevity; zirconia generation is not.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- In-vitro design on extracted teeth — no clinical/biological validation; abstract-only retrieval means full method/figure detail is unavailable.
- Only two cements and two zirconia products tested; generalizability to other self-adhesive vs MDP cement systems is uncertain.
- Internal fit values exceeded ideal ranges across all groups; clinical significance of this baseline elevation is not resolved.
- No fracture-strength or wear endpoints — fit only.

## 7. Glossary

- **Monolithic zirconia**: full-contour zirconia restoration with no veneering porcelain.
- **3Y-TZP / 4Y-TZP / 5Y-TZP**: yttria-stabilized tetragonal zirconia polycrystal with 3, 4, or 5 mol% yttria; higher yttria → more translucency, lower strength.
- **Marginal fit / gap**: vertical/horizontal discrepancy between restoration margin and tooth preparation; clinically acceptable ~50-120 μm.
- **Internal fit**: cement-space discrepancy between the intaglio surface and the preparation walls/floor.
- **Thermomechanical aging**: combined thermocycling (temperature stress) + dynamic cyclic loading simulating intraoral function over time.
- **Self-adhesive resin cement (RelyX Unicem)**: cement requiring no separate primer/etch step.
- **MDP (Panavia)**: 10-methacryloyloxydecyl dihydrogen phosphate monomer providing chemical bond to zirconia.
