## Why Ingested

접착 시스템의 hybridization quality (나노누출)와 즉각 결합강도 간 상관관계를 실험적으로 입증한 초기 근거 논문으로, [[wiki/resin-bonding/breschi-2025-adhesive-dentistry-evolution-review]]의 SE Bond 우위 서술을 구체적 수치로 뒷받침하고, [[wiki/resin-bonding/hardan-2021-universal-adhesive-dentin-bond-sr-ma]]의 2-step SE 우위 메타분석의 메커니즘적 기반으로 활용.

## One-line Summary

In vitro microtensile study (10 molars, 5 adhesives) demonstrating that two-step self-etch adhesives (especially Clearfil SE Bond) achieve higher bond strength and superior hybridization quality vs single-step self-etch and etch-and-rinse systems.

## 한줄요약

10개 대구치, 5종 접착제 비교 in vitro 연구: 2-step 자가산부식(특히 Clearfil SE Bond)이 single-step 자가산부식 및 산부식-수세 방식 대비 높은 접착강도와 우수한 혼성층 질을 보임.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: European Journal of Dentistry, Vol 7 / Issue 3 / Jul-Sep 2013, pp. 315–326
- **DOI**: 10.4103/1305-7456.115416
- **Institutions**: Federal University of Juiz de Fora; Bandeirante University of São Paulo (Anhanguera-Uniban); University of Campinas (UNICAMP), Piracicaba School of Dentistry
- **Study type**: In vitro (microtensile bond strength + SEM nanoleakage)
- **n**: 10 teeth (bond strength), 30 additional teeth (SEM)
- **Groups**: 5 adhesive systems

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrates that **hybridization quality directly correlates with immediate µ-TBS** — refuting the common assumption that nanoleakage only affects long-term durability
- Establishes that the **interaction depth/acidity** of self-etch adhesives critically governs both hybrid layer integrity and bond strength
- Provides SEM evidence (silver methenamine + ammoniacal silver nitrate) that single-step SE adhesives produce more extensive nanoleakage than two-step SE systems despite similar application simplicity

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

### Materials tested

| Group | System | Type | pH |
|---|---|---|---|
| ADP | Adper Prompt (3M ESPE) | Single-step SE | ~0.8 (strong) |
| XE | Xeno III (Dentsply) | Single-step SE | ~1.0 (strong) |
| SE | Clearfil SE Bond (Kuraray) | Two-step SE | primer 1.9 / adhesive 2.8 |
| ADSE | Adhe SE (Ivoclar Vivadent) | Two-step SE | primer 1.7 |
| SB | Adper Single Bond (3M ESPE) | Single-step E&R | 4.7 |

### Bond strength protocol
- 40 caries-free human third molars; roots severed at CEJ; occlusal enamel ground to expose flat dentin (#180-grit SiC)
- 10 teeth sectioned into quarters; each quarter randomly assigned to one group (n = 8 quarters/group)
- Smear layer standardized with #600-grit SiC wet polishing (60 s)
- Resin composite (Filtek Z250) built up in 3 × 2 mm increments; stored 37°C water 24 h
- Sectioned into beams ~0.8 mm² cross-section; µ-TBS at 0.5 mm/min (Geraldeli device)
- One-way ANOVA + Tukey's test (α = 0.05); n = 25 sticks/group

### Nanoleakage (SEM)
- 30 additional teeth; specimens prepared per group
- Two silver-labeling techniques: silver methenamine (SM) and ammoniacal silver nitrate (AgNO₃)
- SEM examination of resin-dentin interfaces for silver deposit distribution and intensity

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### Microtensile bond strength ranking

| Group | µ-TBS (MPa) | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| SE (Clearfil SE Bond) | Highest | Significantly > all others |
| ADSE, XE | Intermediate | No difference between them; > SB and ADP |
| SB, ADP | Lowest | No difference between them |

- Statistical outcome: SE > ADSE = XE > SB = ADP (P < 0.05 between groups except noted equalities)

### Nanoleakage findings

- **SB (E&R)**: Most intense and extensive silver uptake at resin-dentin interface — porous zone at bottom of hybrid layer consistent with incomplete resin infiltration
- **ADP and XE** (single-step SE, strong pH): Significant nanoleakage; areas of exposed collagen without resin encapsulation; incomplete resin permeation
- **SE and ADSE** (two-step SE, mild pH): Minimal silver uptake; better-defined hybrid layer; most favorable hybridization quality

### Hybrid layer characteristics
- Mild self-etch (pH ≈ 1.7–1.9): thin but well-sealed hybrid layer (~1 µm); collagen fibrils protected
- Strong self-etch (pH ≤ 1): deeper demineralization resembling E&R but with retained hydroxyapatite crystals partially protecting collagen; however residual water from ionization medium creates incomplete polymerization zones
- E&R (SB): deepest demineralization; collagen fully exposed; natural monomer concentration gradient → porous bottom zone

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- 24-hour storage only — no aging, thermocycling, or long-term follow-up; nanoleakage at baseline does not capture dynamic degradation trajectories
- Only one E&R system (single-step "1-bottle" SB) — no 3-step E&R for full hierarchy
- Small sample per group (n = 25 sticks from 8 tooth quarters)
- All on mid-coronal dentin; results may differ at cervical or deep dentin
- Authors recommend future studies on durability and correlation with aging-phase nanoleakage

## 7. Glossary

- **µ-TBS** (microtensile bond strength, 미세인장결합강도): bond strength measured on cross-sections ≤ 1 mm², reducing stress concentration
- **Hybrid layer** (혼성층): zone of resin-infiltrated demineralized dentin; key to adhesion
- **Nanoleakage** (나노누출): silver permeation into nanoporosities within polymerized resin matrix, indicating incomplete sealing
- **Self-etch (SE)** (자가산부식): adhesive strategy where acidic monomers simultaneously etch and prime without separate rinse step
- **Etch-and-rinse (E&R)** (산부식-수세): classic approach using separate phosphoric acid etch, rinse, then adhesive application
- **Silver methenamine / ammoniacal silver nitrate**: SEM labeling agents that deposit in water-filled nanoporosities for visualization
- **MDP** (10-methacryloyloxydecyl dihydrogen phosphate): functional monomer in Clearfil SE Bond; chemically bonds to hydroxyapatite
