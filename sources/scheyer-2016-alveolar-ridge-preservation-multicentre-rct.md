## Why Ingested

Part of a recent GBR / ridge-preservation collection. This multicentre RCT directly compares two graft+membrane systems (demineralized allograft + cross-linked membrane vs DBBM-collagen + native bilayer membrane) for ridge preservation of dehisced sockets — a head-to-head that strengthens the ridge-preservation evidence in [[bone-regeneration/avila-ortiz-2019-alveolar-ridge-preservation-interventions]] and [[bone-regeneration/adams-2022-clinical-evidence-alveolar-ridge-preservation]].

## One-line Summary

Multicentre RCT (40 subjects, 10 centres) of ridge preservation in posterior sockets with substantial buccal dehiscence: DBBM-collagen + native bilayer collagen membrane preserved ~1.76 mm more horizontal ridge at 6 months (p=0.0256) and gave better early soft-tissue closure than demineralized allograft + cross-linked membrane.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Head-to-head RCT of two contrasting GBR/ridge-preservation philosophies in dehisced posterior sockets:
  - **Control (DFDBA + RECXC):** demineralized freeze-dried bone allograft + reconstituted, cross-linked bovine collagen membrane (longer-lasting, stiffer).
  - **Test (DBBMC + NBCM):** deproteinized bovine bone mineral with collagen + native bilayer porcine collagen membrane (Bio-Oss Collagen + Bio-Gide; shorter-lasting, more flexible).
- Provides histomorphometric and morphology-based predictors of ridge-preservation success.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 40 subjects (18–70 y) intended for extraction + implant, enrolled across 10 standardized centres.
- Inclusion: posterior sockets (first premolar to first molar) with substantial buccal dehiscence ≥1/3 socket height and width (Elian Class II/III) and intact adjacent teeth/implants for stent indexing.
- Exclusion: tobacco use within 6 months, diabetes/cancer/HIV/bone metabolic disease, recent systemic steroids/immunosuppressants/radiation/chemo, IM/IV bisphosphonates, relevant allergies, pregnancy.
- 1:1 block randomization, balanced within each centre (1–3 test/control per investigator).
- Socket dimensions measured with indexed stents at baseline and 6 months; wound closure and soft-tissue inflammation followed post-op; biopsies retrieved at 6 months for histomorphometry.
- Primary outcome: horizontal ridge dimension change (baseline → 6 months).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Primary endpoint:** at 6 months, horizontal socket measures significantly greater (better preserved) for **DBBMC + NBCM — average 1.76 mm greater, p=0.0256**.
- Lingual and buccal vertical bone changes: not significantly different between treatments.
- Histomorphometry: % new bone and % new bone + graft not significantly different, but significantly more **graft remnants remained for DBBMC**.
- At 1 month, incision-line gaps were significantly greater and more incision lines remained open for **DFDBA + RECXC** (worse early soft-tissue closure).
- Higher inflammation at 1 week tended to correlate with lower ridge-preservation results.
- Deeper socket morphologies with thinner bony walls correlated with **better** ridge preservation.
- 37/40 sites had sufficient ridge dimension for implant placement at 6 months; the 3 insufficient sites were all DFDBA + RECXC.
- Conclusion: DBBMC + NBCM gave better soft-tissue healing and ridge preservation for implant placement; deeper sockets with higher, more intact bony walls responded most favorably.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Modest sample (n=40) across many centres; 6-month endpoint (no long-term implant survival reported here).
- Industry educational grant (Geistlich) — the test arm uses Geistlich products; note potential sponsorship influence.
- Confined to dehisced posterior sockets; not generalizable to intact sockets or anterior esthetic zone.

## 7. Glossary

- **DFDBA** — Demineralized freeze-dried bone allograft.
- **RECXC** — Reconstituted, cross-linked bovine collagen membrane.
- **DBBMC** — Deproteinized bovine bone mineral with collagen (Bio-Oss Collagen).
- **NBCM** — Native bilayer collagen membrane (Bio-Gide).
- **Elian Class II/III** — Extraction socket classification by degree of buccal plate loss.
- **Histomorphometry** — Quantitative microscopic measurement of new bone / graft remnants in biopsy.
