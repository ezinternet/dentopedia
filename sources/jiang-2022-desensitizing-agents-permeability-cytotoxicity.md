## Why Ingested

[[dentin-hypersensitivity/joshi-2013-novamin-gluma-dentinal-tubule-occlusion-sem]]의 tubule-occlusion 비교에 permeability + cytotoxicity(생체적합성) 축을 추가. Gluma(글루타르알데히드) 안전성 우려는 [[dentin-hypersensitivity/scheffel-2015-transdentinal-cytotoxicity-glutaraldehyde-odontoblast]]와 직접 연결.

## One-line Summary
In vitro study (n=6/group) showing GLUMA occludes tubules and cuts permeability only after simulated dentinal fluid (2% BSA) but is cytotoxic to pulp-like cells, whereas an NaF/fumed-silica gel occludes tubules and is biocompatible but rebounds in permeability after artificial-saliva immersion.

## 한줄요약
In vitro(군당 6): GLUMA는 모의 치수액(2% BSA) 존재 시에만 세관 폐쇄·투과도 감소하나 세포독성 있고, NaF/흄드실리카 겔은 세관 폐쇄·생체적합성 우수하나 인공타액 침지 후 투과도 반등.

## 1. Document Information
- BMC Oral Health 2022;22:391. Open Access.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Couples dentin-permeability (hydraulic conductance) and dentin-barrier cytotoxicity (MDPC-like via MTT through dentin disc) in one model.
- Shows GLUMA tubule occlusion is dentinal-fluid (protein)-dependent.
- Separates efficacy from biocompatibility: GLUMA cytotoxic, RD gel non-cytotoxic.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Human dentin specimens, 3 groups (n=6): G1/G2 GLUMA (G2 with 2% BSA perfusate), G3 remineralizing/desensitizing (RD) gel + 24 h artificial saliva.
- Permeability via hydraulic device at 20 cm H2O; SEM of representative discs; cytotoxicity via MTT on 3D cell scaffolds beneath dentin discs after 24 h.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- GLUMA significantly reduced permeability and occluded tubules only with 2% BSA perfusate.
- RD gel reduced permeability and occluded tubules, but permeability rebounded after artificial-saliva immersion.
- GLUMA significantly decreased cell viability; RD non-cytotoxic.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Short immersion underestimates RD remineralization; small n; in vitro only.

## 6. Related Work
- Mechanistic complement to clinical Gluma/fluoride/laser trials; safety links to glutaraldehyde transdentinal cytotoxicity work.

## 7. Glossary
- GLUMA: glutaraldehyde + HEMA desensitizer.
- RD gel: remineralizing/desensitizing gel (NaF + fumed silica).
- BSA: bovine serum albumin (simulated dentinal-fluid protein).
