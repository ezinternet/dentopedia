## Why Ingested

하악 제3대구치 매복 형태(연조직/골매복, Pell & Gregory 위치, Winter 경사도)와 세 가지 주요 병변—치관주위염 (Pericoronitis), 하악 제2대구치 (M2M) 원심 우식, M2M 원심 치주병변—의 연관성을 동시 분석한 후향 연구로, 발치 적응증 결정 근거를 직접 제공한다. [[oral-surgery/damico-2025-third-molar-surgical-indications-guidelines]]의 가이드라인 권고를 실증적 데이터로 보강하며, 연령별 병변 패턴 차이(청년층 → 치관주위염 우세, 중년 이상 → 우식·치주병변 증가)를 수치로 제시한다.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective chart review (n=262 patients, 432 M3Ms, Shanghai 2019–2020): soft-tissue impaction and vertical angulation were associated with pericoronitis (p=0.0097, p<0.0001); mesio-angular shallow (position A) impaction was associated with M2M distal caries (p<0.0001); deeper mesio-angular/horizontal impaction was associated with M2M distal periodontal pathology (p<0.0001).

## 한줄요약

후향 연구 (262명, 432개 매복 제3대구치, 상하이 2019–2020): 연조직 매복·수직 경사 → 치관주위염 (p<0.0001); 근심경사 위치 A → 제2대구치 원심 우식 (p<0.0001); 근심·수평 심부 매복 → 제2대구치 원심 치주병변 (p<0.0001).

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First study to simultaneously examine all three major M3M-associated pathologies (pericoronitis, M2M distal caries, M2M distal periodontal pathology) within a single cohort, using the same classification system.
2. Demonstrates that different impaction features predict different pathologies — soft-tissue/vertical → pericoronitis; shallow mesio-angular → caries; deep mesio-angular/horizontal → periodontal pathology — supporting a tiered, feature-based extraction decision.
3. Provides age-stratified data: pericoronitis dominates in all groups, but caries and periodontal lesions accumulate with age (p<0.0001), supporting delayed but eventual extraction for deeper impactions.
4. Worst outcomes (uncurable root caries or degree III mobility of M2M requiring M2M extraction) clustered in soft-tissue, position A&B, mesio-angular/horizontal impacted teeth with mean patient age 44 years, underscoring the long-term cost of retention.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Inclusion criteria**: Fully or partially impacted mandibular third molars; clinical data + OPG available; surgery performed after examination.

**Sample**: 262 patients (129 female / 133 male), ages 18–63 (mean 31); 432 M3M teeth total.

**Classification systems**:
- Impaction depth: Soft tissue impacted vs. bony impacted
- Pell & Gregory: Position A (occlusal plane or above), B (between occlusal and cervical line of M2M), C (below cervical line)
- Winter's angulation: Vertical, mesio-angular, horizontal, transverse

**Pathology definitions**:
- Pericoronitis: Pain and swelling at third molar area
- M2M distal caries: Caries (including pulp or periapical disease) in contact with M3M
- M2M distal periodontal pathology: Distal bony absorption and/or external root resorption (ERR) of M2M, confirmed by probing and OPG

**Outcome measurement**: Surgical success rate; mucosa healing (1 week, 1 month, 3 months post-op); M2M mobility (Miller classification — degree III = extraction indicated); M2M endodontic complications.

**Statistics**: SAS 8.2; χ² test and Fisher exact test; significance threshold p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

### Sample composition
- Soft tissue impacted: 91.67% (396/432); bony impacted: 8.33% (36/432)
- Pell & Gregory position: A 72.22%, B 20.83%, C 6.94%
- Winter angulation: Vertical 55.56%, mesio-angular 24.54%, horizontal 19.91%

### Pathology prevalence
- Pericoronitis: 82.41% of all M3Ms
- M2M distal caries: 18.75%
- M2M distal periodontal pathology: 14.81%
- 64 teeth had two or three simultaneous pathologies

### Table 2 findings (impaction pattern vs. pathology):

**Pericoronitis**
- Soft tissue impacted: 332 (+) vs. 64 (−); bony impacted 24 (+) vs. 12 (−); p=0.0097 (soft-tissue association)
- Winter vertical: 219 (+) vs. 21 (−); mesio-angular 72 (+) vs. 34 (−); horizontal 65 (+) vs. 21 (−); p<0.0001 (vertical highest rate)
- Pell & Gregory: no significant difference (p=0.7366)

**M2M distal caries**
- Soft tissue vs. bony impacted: p=0.9113 (no significant difference)
- Pell & Gregory position A: highest caries frequency; p=0.0206 (shallower = more caries)
- Winter angulation: vertical 20 (+) vs. 220 (−); mesio-angular 40 (+) vs. 66 (−); horizontal 20 (+) vs. 66 (−); p<0.0001 (mesio-angular highest)

**M2M distal periodontal pathology**
- Soft tissue vs. bony impacted: p=0.0727 (trend, not significant)
- Pell & Gregory: position A 35 (+) vs. 277 (−); B 15 (+) vs. 75 (−); C 14 (+) vs. 16 (−); p<0.0001 (deeper = more periodontal pathology)
- Winter angulation: vertical 8 (+) vs. 232 (−); mesio-angular 29 (+) vs. 77 (−); horizontal 27 (+) vs. 59 (−); p<0.0001 (mesio-angular/horizontal highest)

### Age-stratified pathology (Table 1)
- <30 years: pericoronitis 208, carious/endodontic 34, loosened M2M 17
- ≥30, <50: pericoronitis 139, carious/endodontic 40, loosened M2M 40
- ≥50: pericoronitis 9, carious/endodontic 7, loosened M2M 7; p<0.0001

### Surgical outcomes
- Extraction success rate: 100%
- All pericoronitis resolved after surgery
- 5 M2Ms extracted due to degree III mobility; 5 M2Ms extracted due to uncurable root caries
- Worst-outcome patients: mean age 44 years; predominantly soft-tissue impacted, position A&B, mesio-angular/horizontal angulation

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective design with selection bias: patients referred to OMS had symptoms or pathologies; asymptomatic impacted M3Ms are underrepresented.
- Single-center, hospital-based cohort (Shanghai); generalizability to other populations uncertain.
- No community-based prevalence data; cannot directly inform prophylactic extraction decisions in asymptomatic patients.
- Authors recommend future large-scale community surveys or prospective studies.
- Relatively small sample in some subgroups (bony impacted n=36, position C n=30), limiting statistical power for those cells.
- Transverse impaction not reported in results (likely n too small).

## 7. Glossary

- **M3M**: Mandibular third molar (하악 제3대구치, 하악 사랑니)
- **M2M**: Mandibular second molar (하악 제2대구치)
- **Pericoronitis** (치관주위염): Inflammation of soft tissue surrounding a partially erupted tooth
- **Pell & Gregory classification** (펠 & 그레고리 분류): Classifies M3M by depth relative to occlusal plane of M2M (A, B, C) and relation to ramus (I, II, III)
- **Winter's classification** (Winter 분류): Classifies M3M by angulation to long axis of M2M: vertical, mesio-angular, horizontal, transverse, disto-angular
- **ERR (External Root Resorption)** (외부 치근흡수): Resorption of M2M root surface caused by pressure from adjacent impacted M3M
- **Soft tissue impacted** (연조직 매복): Crown covered by soft tissue but not bone
- **Bony impacted** (골매복): Crown partially or fully covered by alveolar bone
- **CEJ**: Cementoenamel junction (백악법랑경계)
- **OPG**: Orthopantomogram (파노라마 방사선 사진)
- **Miller classification** (Miller 분류): Tooth mobility grading system; degree III = multi-directional mobility indicating extraction
