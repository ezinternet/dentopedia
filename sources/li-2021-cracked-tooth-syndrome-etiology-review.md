## 2. Key Contributions
- 4축 framework (etiology, diagnosis, management, prevention)
- Iatrogenic vs non-iatrogenic etiology 분류
- Endo 부적절·restoration 부적절 등 iatrogenic factor 명시

## 3. Methodology
- Narrative review

## 4. Key Points
- **Iatrogenic etiology**: 부적절 endo·deep restoration·pre-existing crown 부적절
- **Non-iatrogenic etiology**: 교합력·이갈이·외상·치아 구조 약화
- **Diagnosis**: 종합 framework (history + clinical + imaging)
- **Management**: 단계적 (conservative → cuspal coverage → endo)
- **Prevention**: 보존적 술식·정기 check·이갈이 보호

## 5. Limitations
- Narrative review — primary RCT 없음
- 중국·한국 협력 narrative
