## Why Ingested

PubMed surveillance(2025–2026 IIP SR/MA) 중 dedup 게이트를 통과한 최신·최대 SST 메타분석. 기존 [[wiki/overviews/socket-shield-technique-overview]]의 thesis(SST가 협측골·PES·ISQ 우월, 생존 동등)가 더 큰 풀(27편/1307임플란트)에서도 유지되는지 검증 → **supersession이 아니라 reinforces**로 판명(결론 동일, 근거강도 동급 sr+ma지만 풀 최대). 또한 shield 높이(crest 상/동일/하)·두께(≤/>1.5mm)·gap 이식 유무와 **무관하게** SST 우월이라는 subgroup 입도를 추가해 [[wiki/immediate-implant/socket-shield/ji-2025-socket-shield-conventional-network-ma]]·[[wiki/immediate-implant/socket-shield/lin-2022-socket-shield-technique-systematic-review-meta]]를 extends.

## One-line Summary

Meta-analysis (27 studies = 22 RCT + 5 NRSI, 1307 implants) finding the socket shield technique (SST) significantly reduces horizontal (MD −0.50 mm) and vertical (MD −0.56 mm) buccal bone loss and improves PES (+1.25) and ISQ (+5.83) vs conventional immediate implant placement (CIIP), with comparable implant success (RR 1.00); SST superiority held across shield height, thickness, and grafting subgroups, but high heterogeneity, short follow-up, and absent standardized protocols temper the evidence.

## 한줄요약

소켓실드 기법(Socket Shield Technique, SST) vs 전통 즉시식립(CIIP) 메타분석(27편·1307임플란트). SST가 협측 수평 골소실 −0.50 mm·수직 −0.56 mm 감소, 핑크심미점수(PES) +1.25, 임플란트 안정성지수(ISQ) +5.83 우월, 생존율은 동등(RR 1.00). 실드 높이·두께·골이식 무관하게 SST 우월. 현존 최대 풀이나 이질성 높고 장기 데이터 부족.

## 1. Document Information

- **Title**: Clinical efficacy of socket shield technique compared to conventional immediate implant placement in the aesthetic zone: a meta-analysis
- **Authors**: Lu Wei, Du Shanshan, Su Jingtong, Wu Yang, Yao Xinyu, Zhang Chao, Yu Hedong (Taihe Hospital, Hubei University of Medicine, Shiyan, China)
- **Journal**: International Journal of Implant Dentistry, 2025;11(1):72
- **DOI**: 10.1186/s40729-025-00657-z · **PMID**: 41417433 · **PMCID**: PMC12717328
- **Type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis (PRISMA; RoB2 for RCT, ROBINS-I for NRSI)
- **Source**: PubMed Central full text (according to PubMed)

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Largest SST-vs-CIIP pool to date** — 27 studies / 1307 implants (647 SST, 660 CIIP), incorporating 13 Chinese-language studies that prior English-only MAs missed.
2. **Confirms (not overturns) the established direction**: SST superior on buccal bone loss (H −0.50, V −0.56 mm), PES (+1.25), ISQ (+5.83); success comparable (RR 1.00, I²=0%).
3. **Subgroup robustness**: SST advantage on horizontal buccal bone loss persisted regardless of shield height (above/at/below crest), thickness (≤ or >1.5 mm), and whether the gap was grafted — a granularity earlier MAs lacked.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **PICOS**: esthetic zone (to 2nd premolar) IIP; SST vs CIIP; outcomes = horizontal/vertical buccal bone loss (primary: horizontal), PES, ISQ, success rate; RCT + NRSI; ≥3-month follow-up.
- **Search**: 7 databases (incl. CNKI/VIP/Wanfang) to 2024-10-09. 1043 records → 27 included.
- **Analysis**: RevMan 5.4; random-effects when I²>50%; subgroup by shield height/thickness, grafting, language; sensitivity (exclude NRSI; exclude inconsistent-grafting studies); funnel plots for outcomes with >10 studies (low publication-bias signal).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Studies / implants | Effect (SST vs CIIP) | Heterogeneity |
|---|---|---|---|
| Horizontal buccal bone loss | 22 / 1155 | MD −0.50 [−0.60, −0.41] | I²=97% |
| Vertical buccal bone loss | 8 / 252 | MD −0.56 [−0.64, −0.48] | I²=78% |
| ISQ | 8 / 456 | MD +5.83 [4.08, 7.57] | I²=69% |
| PES | 21 / 1001 | MD +1.25 [0.93, 1.57] | I²=90% |
| Implant success | 18 / 883 | RR 1.00 [0.98, 1.02] | I²=0% |

- Horizontal subgroups: shield above crest −0.59, at crest −0.46, below −0.28; thickness ≤1.5mm −0.55, >1.5mm −0.66; grafted −0.59, non-grafted −0.22 — all favor SST.
- Only outlier: Abdullah 2022 (implant in direct contact with shield, grafting only in CIIP) — confounded, did not show SST advantage.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very high heterogeneity (I²=90–97%) on bone-loss/PES outcomes; NRSI lower quality (2 rated "High risk").
- Operator blinding impossible (technique-specific); randomization often under-described ("Some concerns" for most RCTs).
- Long-term data thin — no high-quality RCT beyond 5 years; ISQ all ≤12 months.
- No consensus on ideal shield height/thickness or gap management → standardized-protocol RCTs needed.

## 6. Related Work

- Prior SST-vs-CIIP MAs (Lin 2022, Gharpure 2022, Velasco-Bohorquez 2021, Ji 2025 network MA) — same direction, smaller pools.
- Complication frame from Ogawa 2022 SR (internal shield exposure 46% most common).
- Originator Hürzeler 2010; design refinements Gluckman, Mitsias, Botticelli (gap/shield positioning).

## 7. Glossary

- **SST (Socket Shield Technique)** — retain buccal root fragment at extraction to preserve PDL vascular supply and buccal plate.
- **CIIP** — conventional immediate implant placement (whole-root extraction, immediate fixture).
- **PES (Pink Esthetic Score)** — 7-parameter peri-implant soft-tissue esthetic index (Fürhauser).
- **ISQ (Implant Stability Quotient)** — RFA-derived primary stability metric.
- **NRSI** — non-randomized study of intervention.
