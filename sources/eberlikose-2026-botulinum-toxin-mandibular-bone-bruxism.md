## Why Ingested

처음으로 BoNT-A 브럭시즘 치료가 하악골 형태에 방사선학적으로 측정 가능한 변화를 준다는 증거. 기존 보유 BoNT-A 논문들([[botulinum-toxin/manfredini-2019-botulinum-toxin-bruxism-tmj-rct]] 등)은 통증·기능 개선에 집중하며 골 리모델링 효과는 미보고. 본 연구는 피질골 두께 증가 및 G2/G3(비정상) 형태 감소라는 골 구조 개선 근거를 최초로 제공.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective case-control (n=60, 3 groups) using panoramic radiography showed BoNT-A-treated bruxism patients had increased cortical thickness and reduced G2/G3 mandibular morphology vs untreated bruxism, suggesting BoNT-A reduces occlusal overload-driven pathological bone remodeling. Abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

파노라마 방사선 사진을 이용한 후향 케이스-컨트롤 연구(n=60, 3군)에서 BoNT-A 치료 브럭시즘 군이 미치료 브럭시즘 군 대비 피질골 두께 증가 및 비정상 하악골 형태(G2/G3) 감소를 보여, BoNT-A가 교합 과부하로 인한 병적 골 리모델링을 억제함을 제안. 초록만 수집.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First radiographic evidence that BoNT-A treatment for bruxism affects mandibular bone morphology
- Three-group design separates BoNT-A effect from baseline bruxism-induced bone changes
- Panoramic radiography validated as a tool for both bruxism diagnosis and BoNT-A treatment monitoring
- Proposes mechanism: BoNT-A → masticatory muscle activity reduction → decreased occlusal stress → cortical remodeling normalization
- Antegonial notch depth (AND) was NOT significantly different — suggests cortical changes may be more sensitive markers than gross morphological indices

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Retrospective case-control; 3 groups (n=20 each)
  - Group 1: Bruxism + BoNT-A injection
  - Group 2: Bruxism WITHOUT BoNT-A
  - Group 3: Healthy controls (no bruxism)
- **Imaging**: Panoramic radiography at baseline and 6-month follow-up
- **Outcomes assessed**:
  - Morphological classification: G0–G3 (Gonial angle morphology categories)
  - AMD (Angular Mandibular Depth, 하악각심도)
  - AND (Antegonial Notch Depth, 전각절흔심도)
  - CT (Cortical Thickness, 피질골 두께)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **BoNT-A group vs untreated bruxism group** (p<0.05):
  - G2 and G3 categories (pathological bone morphology): significantly decreased
  - Cortical thickness (CT): significantly increased
- **AND (Antegonial notch depth)**: NO significant difference among groups
- **Interpretation**: BoNT-A reduces excessive masticatory stress → reverses/prevents pathological mandibular bone apposition → cortical remodeling toward healthier morphology

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract only — specific BoNT-A dose, injection sites, frequency not detailed
- Small n=20 per group
- Cross-sectional within-group comparison; lack of longitudinal individual tracking
- Panoramic radiography has limited precision for subtle cortical measurements vs CBCT
- Future: CBCT-based volumetric analysis; dose-response bone remodeling; longer follow-up

## 7. Glossary

- **G0–G3 morphology** (하악골 형태 분류): panoramic radiographic classification of mandibular angle; G2/G3 indicate pathological bone apposition associated with occlusal overload
- **AMD** (Angular Mandibular Depth, 하악각심도): radiographic measurement of mandibular angle depth
- **AND** (Antegonial Notch Depth, 전각절흔심도): depth of the antegonial notch on panoramic radiograph
- **CT** (Cortical Thickness, 피질골 두께): cortical bone thickness at mandibular inferior border
- **Occlusal overload** (교합 과부하): excessive masticatory forces from bruxism causing pathological bone remodeling
