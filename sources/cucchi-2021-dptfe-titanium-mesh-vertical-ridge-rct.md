## Why Ingested

GBR 6패널 분류 인터랙티브 도구의 Panel III vs Panel IV–V 근거 논문. Cucchi 계열 RCT 중 **임플란트 주변 연·경조직 1년 결과**를 보고하는 파트. [[wiki/implants/vertical-ridge-augmentation/cucchi-2017-dptfe-membrane-vs-titanium-mesh-collagen-rct]]의 초기 결과 후속 논문으로, 수직골증대 후 임플란트 PBL·연조직 파라미터가 두 술식 간 동등함을 1년에 걸쳐 확인. abstract-only (PMC 미등재).

## One-line Summary

RCT 1-year outcomes (n=30 completed of 40) comparing Ti-reinforced d-PTFE membranes (Group A) vs Ti meshes + collagen membranes (Group B) after VRA in posterior mandible: equivalent peri-implant bone loss (0.67 vs 0.61 mm) and soft-tissue parameters.

## 한줄요약

하악 구치부 수직골증대 RCT 1년 결과(n=30): Ti 강화 d-PTFE막 vs Ti 메시+콜라겐막 — 임플란트 골소실(0.67 vs 0.61mm), 연조직 지표 모두 동등.

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Randomized clinical trial (RCT)
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Implants Research 2021;32(1):1-14
- **Registration**: ClinicalTrials.gov NCT04332679
- **Note**: Abstract-only — not available in PMC

## 2. Key Contributions

1. **Peri-implant bone loss equivalence**: PBL change 0.67 mm (d-PTFE) vs 0.61 mm (Ti mesh+collagen) at 1 year; estimated treatment difference -0.05 mm (95% CI: -0.27 to 0.16), NS (p>0.9337)
2. **Soft tissue equivalence**: No significant differences in PPD, mPI, mGI, tKT, wKT, FD between groups
3. Strong IBP–PBL correlation (p<0.0001) — interproximal bone peaks predict peri-implant bone levels
4. Confirms biomaterial equivalence for maintaining peri-implant health after VRA
5. Part of a research series: pilot-VRA bone gain → 1-year peri-implant → 5-year PROMS

## 3. Methodology

- **Design**: RCT, 40 enrolled, 30 completed; posterior mandible
- **Group A**: Ti-reinforced d-PTFE membranes + simultaneous implants
- **Group B**: Titanium meshes + collagen membranes + simultaneous implants
- **Evaluation**: After prosthetic restoration at baseline and 1 year
- **Outcomes**: PBL (peri-implant bone levels), IBP (interproximal bone peaks), PPD, BoP, mPI, mGI, keratinized tissue thickness/width (tKT/wKT), fornix depth (FD)
- **Statistics**: p=0.05 threshold

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Parameter | Group A (d-PTFE) | Group B (Ti mesh+collagen) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| PBL change | 0.67 mm | 0.61 mm | >0.9337 |
| PPD | NS | NS | NS |
| mPI, mGI | NS | NS | NS |
| tKT, wKT, FD | NS | NS | NS |
| IBP–PBL | r strong | r strong | <0.0001 |

- Overall bone loss <1.0 mm in first year (both groups) — within acceptable thresholds

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract-only; detailed per-parameter values not available
- 25% dropout (30/40 completed) — modified ITT not specified
- Single-center, same surgeon — generalizability limited
- 1-year follow-up; longer-term VRA remodeling unknown

## 7. Glossary

- **d-PTFE**: Dense polytetrafluoroethylene — non-resorbable, allows controlled epithelial exclusion
- **Ti-reinforced d-PTFE**: d-PTFE with titanium reinforcement for space maintenance in VRA
- **PBL**: Peri-implant bone level — radiographic marginal bone height
- **IBP**: Interproximal bone peak — distal/mesial bone level at adjacent teeth
- **tKT/wKT**: Keratinized tissue thickness/width
