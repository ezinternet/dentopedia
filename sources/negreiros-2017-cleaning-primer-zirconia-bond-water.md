## One-line Summary
Eur J Dent 2017;11:6-11: Ivoclean + Monobond Plus combination → highest BS for Katana zirconia at 24h; all groups lost ~50% bond strength after 1 year water storage.

## 1. Document Information
Journal: European Journal of Dentistry 2017;11:6-11
DOI: 10.4103/ejd.ejd_276_16

## 2. Key Results
- Ivoclean + Monobond Plus: highest bond strength at 24h for Katana; benefit less clear for ZirCAD
- Monobond Plus alone: intermediate improvement
- Ivoclean alone: less effective than primer
- 1 year water storage: ~50% reduction across ALL groups — critical aging effect
- Zirconia brand matters: Katana responds differently from ZirCAD to combined treatment
