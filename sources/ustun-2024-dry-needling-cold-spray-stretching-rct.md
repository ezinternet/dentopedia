## One-line Summary

Prospective single-blind RCT (n=60, upper-trapezius MPS) — cold-spray-stretching vs dry needling over 3 weekly sessions; both improved pain, function, sEMG and ultrasound histogram (P<.05), with cold-spray-stretching showing higher algometer (pain-threshold) and NDI scores than dry needling (P<.008 and P<.028). Extra-masticatory (transferable) technique evidence; abstract-only.

## 한줄요약

전향 단일맹검 RCT (n=60, 상부 승모근 근막통) — 분사신장 vs 건침 3주(주 1회); 두 군 모두 통증·기능·sEMG·초음파 호전(P<.05), 분사신장군이 압통역치(algometer)·NDI에서 건침보다 우수(P<.008, P<.028). 교근 외(승모근) 전이가능 근거, 초록 기반.

## 2. Key Contributions

- A modern **head-to-head RCT** of cold-spray-stretching vs dry needling for active-trigger-point myofascial pain — a comparison missing in the masticatory literature.
- Objective instrumented outcomes (**sEMG**, **ultrasound histogram**) on top of pain/function, documenting trigger-point change for both techniques.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- 60 MPS patients (18–65 y; 51 female; mean 39.7±11.6 y) with an active trigger point in upper trapezius, randomized to DN vs cold-spray-stretching; 1 session/week × 3.
- Outcomes: NRS pain, algometer (pressure pain threshold), neck ROM, Neck Disability Index (NDI); sEMG and US histogram; measured at baseline/end plus pre/post sessions 1 and 3.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Within-group**: both groups improved significantly in pain, functionality, sEMG, and US histogram (P<.05).
- **Between-group**: cold-spray-stretching > dry needling for algometer (P<.008) and NDI (P<.028).
- **Conclusion**: both techniques effective; cold-spray-stretching at least non-inferior (favored on pressure-pain-threshold and disability).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Upper trapezius, not masticatory** — direct transfer to jaw-closing muscles (masseter/temporalis) unproven.
- Short course (3 sessions); no long-term follow-up; single-blind; abstract-only ingest (no effect-size magnitudes captured).
- No sham/placebo arm; vapocoolant agent not specified in abstract.

## 7. Glossary

- **Cold-spray-stretching / spray-and-stretch (분사신장)**: vapocoolant over the muscle followed by passive stretch to inactivate the trigger point.
- **Algometer / Pressure Pain Threshold (PPT, 압통역치)**: force at which pressure becomes painful; higher = less tender.
- **NDI (Neck Disability Index)**: neck-specific functional disability score.
- **sEMG (surface Electromyography)**: surface recording of muscle electrical activity.
