## Why Ingested
Fatigue-bond-strength durability evidence for HEMA-free two-step universal adhesives; supports [[resin-bonding/tsujimoto-2022-two-step-hema-free-universal-adhesive-durability]].

## One-line Summary
In-vitro fatigue bond strength + SEM finding two-step HEMA-free G2-Bond Universal highest enamel fatigue strength in E&R mode.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro 피로결합강도 — 2-step HEMA-free G2-Bond이 E&R 모드 법랑질 최고.

## 1. Document Information
J Funct Biomater 2022; 13: 134. DOI 10.3390/jfb13030134. Open access. Univ. of Iowa / Creighton / Univ. of Minnesota (MDRCBB).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Uses fatigue (cyclic) bond strength — more clinically representative than static.
- Demonstrates HEMA-free two-step universal advantage in enamel.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro fatigue bond strength + SEM of enamel/dentin interfaces. Adhesives: OptiBond FL, Prime&Bond NT, Clearfil SE Bond 2, G2-Bond Universal, Scotchbond Universal Plus.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
E&R mode: G2-Bond Universal enamel fatigue bond strength significantly highest; Prime&Bond NT dentin fatigue bond strength lowest.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; lab fatigue model; clinical validation needed.

## 6. Related Work
Tang 2024 (HEMA/BPA-free universals); universal-adhesive durability literature.

## 7. Glossary
- Fatigue bond strength: bond strength under cyclic loading.
- E&R: etch-and-rinse mode.
- SE: self-etch mode.
