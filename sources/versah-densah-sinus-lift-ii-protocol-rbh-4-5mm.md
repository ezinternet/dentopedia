## One-line Summary
Versah Densah® manufacturer-issued clinical protocol card (2-page, REV08) for crestal sinus floor elevation in residual bone height 4–5 mm — eliminates pilot drill step and adds allograft propulsion.

## 2. Key Contributions
Vendor reference protocol distinguishing the Lift II indication (RBH 4–5 mm) from Lift I (RBH ≥6 mm) by: (1) omitting pilot drill, (2) adding final-bur slow-speed allograft propulsion (150 rpm, no irrigation) when additional lift >3 mm is needed.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Step-by-step clinical protocol (4 steps, no pilot drill). Manufacturer-published technique guide.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Protocol outcome: avoids pilot-drill-induced perforation in thin residual bone; propels well-hydrated allograft to extend membrane lift beyond 3 mm when needed.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Manufacturer marketing document — not peer-reviewed. Higher-risk indication (thinner RBH) than Lift I; perforation risk per Mazor 2024 ([[mazor-2024-maxillary-sinus-membrane-perforation-osseodensification]]) elevates at RBH ≤3 mm. Densah®-specific.
