## Why Ingested

First histological evaluation of a species-specific (all-bovine) collagen-enriched DBBM block (C-DBBM, 95% DBBM + 5% bovine collagen) for ARP — tests whether collagen enrichment changes bone formation kinetics vs standard DBBM and whether secondary GBR can be avoided. Fills the gap noted in [[bone-regeneration/fischer-2022-bone-envelope-arp-dbbm-sr-ma]] where only 6–19% avoid augmentation; this case series reports 0% requiring GBR at implant placement, suggesting the block form + species-specific collagen chain may improve outcomes.

## One-line Summary

Prospective case series (n=10 patients, 130–319 days healing): C-DBBM block + bovine cross-linked collagen membrane achieved histological bone formation via appositional membranous osteogenesis and 0% required GBR at implant placement; 2.5–4.5 year follow-up with no biological or technical complications.

## 한줄요약

전향적 증례집(n=10, 130–319일 치유): 우종별 교원소 함유 DBBM 블록 + 소 교원막으로 ARP 시 막상골 형성 확인, 임플란트 식립 시 GBR 불필요(0%); 2.5–4.5년 추적 무합병증.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First histological data for C-DBBM (species-specific, all-bovine collagen-enriched DBBM block form) in ARP.
- Demonstrated histological evidence of bone formation via **appositional membranous osteogenesis** — lattice of cancellous bone trabeculae surrounding C-DBBM granules.
- **0 of 10 patients** required GBR at implant placement (implant placement via static computer-aided implant surgery, s-CAIS, using trephine cores).
- Species-specific material chain (bovine DBBM + bovine collagen membrane): relevant for patients with religious or ideological objections to porcine-derived materials.
- Extended follow-up (2.5–4.5 years): no biological or technical complications reported.
- Wide healing period range (130–319 days): reflects individualized clinical decision-making based on histological/CBCT readiness.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Prospective case series; no control group
- **Patients**: 10 patients scheduled for extraction + future implantation
- **Inclusion period**: September 2018 onward (exact end date not specified in abstract)
- **ARP Protocol**:
  - Tooth extraction → socket filled with C-DBBM block (95% DBBM + 5% species-specific bovine collagen)
  - Covered with double-folded bovine cross-linked collagen membrane (xCM) — openly exposed to oral cavity (open-healing technique)
  - Crisscross suture securing the membrane
- **Implant placement**:
  - Static computer-aided implant surgery (s-CAIS) using digital planning
  - Guided trephine drilling: trephine cores harvested for histological analysis
  - Healing period: 130–319 days (individualized)
- **Histological processing**: Trephine cores → decalcified and non-decalcified histology
- **Clinical follow-up**: 2.5–4.5 years
- **GBR assessment**: Determined at time of implant placement by surgeon based on bone volume adequacy

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **GBR at implant placement**: 0/10 patients required additional GBR
- **Histological pattern**: Cancellous bone trabeculae in lattice pattern via appositional membranous osteogenesis; some compact ossicles observed; minimal residual C-DBBM remnants in several cores
- **Bone formation stages**: Multiple stages of osteogenesis evident simultaneously, indicating ongoing active remodeling
- **Healing period**: 130–319 days (mean not specified); wider range than typical ARP (usually 3–6 months) reflecting case-series pragmatic follow-up
- **Complications**: Zero biological complications (infection, membrane exposure sequelae) or technical complications over 2.5–4.5 year follow-up
- **Membrane behavior**: Open-healing technique with xCM — membrane exposed to oral cavity but no adverse outcomes

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- No control group (no standard DBBM, no spontaneous healing comparison)
- Small sample (n=10)
- Heterogeneous healing periods (130–319 days) complicate histomorphometric comparisons
- No quantitative histomorphometry reported (no %, only descriptive histology)
- Selection bias: private practice patients, surgeon-selected cases
- No randomization; prospective design but case series level of evidence
- GBR avoidance rate needs validation in larger RCT against standard DBBM

## 6. Related Work

- Fischer et al. (2022): SR+MA showing standard DBBM ARP achieves only 6–19% success in avoiding GBR — this case series' 0% GBR rate is dramatically better, though study designs are not comparable
- Open-healing ARP technique: published evidence showing comparable outcomes to primary closure in selected sockets
- Socket seal / open-membrane ARP: cross-linked collagen membrane as space maintainer with open exposure — alternative to primary flap advancement
- Species-specific material chains: growing clinical interest due to religious/ethical considerations (halal-compliant, vegan-adjacent)
- Bilhan H and co-authors: prior work on ARP outcomes and material comparisons in German private practice settings

## 7. Glossary

- **C-DBBM**: 교원소 함유 탈단백 우골 — collagen-enriched deproteinized bovine bone mineral (95% DBBM + 5% bovine collagen); block form
- **xCM (cross-linked collagen membrane)**: 가교 교원막 — bovine cross-linked collagen membrane; slower resorption than non-cross-linked; used for open-healing ARP
- **Open-healing technique**: 개방치유법 — membrane openly exposed to oral cavity rather than achieving primary wound closure; avoids flap advancement complications
- **Appositional membranous osteogenesis**: 막내골화의 층상 증식 — bone formation pattern via sequential layering on scaffold surfaces (as opposed to endochondral)
- **s-CAIS (static Computer-Aided Implant Surgery)**: 정적 컴퓨터보조 임플란트 수술 — guided implant placement using pre-fabricated surgical guide from CBCT + digital planning
- **Trephine drilling**: 트레핀 시추 — cylindrical bone coring at implant osteotomy for histological sampling
- **Species-specific material chain**: 종 특이성 재료 체계 — all materials from same species (here, all bovine: DBBM + collagen); avoids porcine components
- **ARP (Alveolar Ridge Preservation)**: 치조골보존술 — socket grafting to limit post-extraction resorption
