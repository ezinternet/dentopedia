## Why Ingested

리튬 디실리케이트 도재에 대한 실란 종류 및 접착제 병용 여부가 전단결합강도에 미치는 영향을 체계적으로 비교한 인 비트로 연구. [[resin-bonding/unknown-2009-effect-of-silane-treatment-timing]]의 실란 처리 시기 논의를 보강하며, 실험적 순수 실란 + 접착제 조합이 상용 단독 실란보다 우수함을 보여준다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro study (n=70, 7 groups) comparing silane surface treatments on HF-etched lithium disilicate: experimental silane + adhesive achieved highest SBS (45.49 MPa), with bonding method mattering more than silane brand.

## 한줄요약

인 비트로 연구 (n=70, 7그룹): HF 산처리된 리튬 디실리케이트에 대한 실란 처리 비교 — 실험적 순수 실란 + 접착제 조합이 최고 전단결합강도(45.49 MPa)를 보였으며, 실란 종류보다 접착제 추가 여부가 더 중요했다.

- **Journal**: European Journal of Dentistry, Vol. 16, No. 2, 2022 (published online 2021-12-17)

- **Study type**: In vitro experimental
- **Institution**: Chulalongkorn University, Bangkok, Thailand; Thammasat University; Rangsit University

## 2. Key Contributions

- Demonstrated that adding an adhesive layer (SGII or Adper Scotchbond Multi-purpose) after silane significantly increases SBS of LDGC to resin cement
- Showed that pure experimental silane (γ-MPS in water/acetic acid/alcohol) outperforms commercial Kerr Silane Primer alone
- Confirmed that silane alone (without adhesive) provided minimal improvement over no-treatment control when using KS
- Established that SBS values above ~40 MPa are achievable with optimized silane + adhesive protocols

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Material**: Vintage LD Press LDGC (Shofu), pressed ingots, 5 mm diameter × 3 mm discs, n=10 per group (7 groups total)
- **Surface prep**: All specimens polished (320/600/800 grit SiC), then etched with 4.5% HF (IPS Ceramic Etching Gel, Ivoclar Vivadent) for 20 s, ultrasonic cleaning 10 min
- **Groups**:
  1. Control — no silane
  2. KS — Kerr Silane Primer alone (γ-MPS, BisEMA, TEGDMA, ethanol)
  3. SGI — Signum Ceramic Bond I alone
  4. SGI/SGII — Signum I then Signum II
  5. EXP — experimental silane (γ-MPS + distilled water + acetic acid + alcohol)
  6. EXP/SGII — experimental silane + Signum II
  7. EXP/ADP — experimental silane + Adper Scotchbond Multi-purpose Adhesive
- **Cementation**: RelyX U200 dual-cure resin cement (3M ESPE); specimens stored 37°C/24h, 5,000 thermal cycles (5–55°C)
- **Test**: Shear bond strength; failure mode by stereo microscope
- **Statistics**: Welch ANOVA + Games-Howell post hoc (α=0.05)

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Group | Mean SBS (MPa) | SD |
|-------|---------------|-----|
| Control | 18.36 | 0.69 |
| KS | 20.17 | 1.10 |
| SGI | 25.58 | — |
| SGI/SGII | 30.12 | — |
| EXP | 33.47 | — |
| EXP/SGII | 41.38 | 2.17 |
| EXP/ADP | 45.49 | 3.37 |

- EXP/ADP ≈ EXP/SGII (not significantly different, p≥0.05)
- Control ≈ KS (not significantly different, p≥0.05)
- Adding adhesive after silane is the key variable
- Failure modes: predominantly adhesive at ceramic–cement interface in low-SBS groups; mixed/cohesive in high-SBS groups

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Only one LDGC brand (Vintage LD Press, press-ceramic); results may differ for CAD/CAM milled LDGC (IPS e.max CAD)
- Short aging protocol (5,000 cycles); longer aging or water storage needed
- Experimental silane was custom-prepared (σ-Aldrich γ-MPS); not a commercial product
- No assessment of flexural strength changes post-HF etching

## 7. Glossary

- **LDGC**: Lithium disilicate glass-ceramic
- **SBS**: Shear bond strength
- **γ-MPS**: 3-(Trimethoxysilyl)propyl methacrylate — active silane coupling monomer
- **HF**: Hydrofluoric acid — glass-phase selective etchant creating microporosity
- **RC**: Resin cement
- **KS**: Kerr Silane Primer
- **EXP**: Experimental silane (pure γ-MPS formulation)
- **ADP**: Adper Scotchbond Multi-purpose Adhesive (Bis-GMA, HEMA)
