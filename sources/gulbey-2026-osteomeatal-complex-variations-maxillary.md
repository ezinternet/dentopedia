## Why Ingested

기존 상악동 막 천공 위험 예측 연구들 ([[wiki/sinus-lift/lateral/manor-2024-cbct-prognostic-factors-sinus-perforation]], [[wiki/sinus-lift/lateral/munakata-2021-sinus-membrane-thickness-edentulous-cbct]])이 상악동 내부 해부학(격벽, 잔류골 높이)에 집중한 반면, 상악동 배농 경로인 오스티오메아탈 복합체 (Osteomeatal Complex, OMC)의 해부학적 변이가 막 두께에 미치는 영향은 미탐구 상태였다. 본 연구(n=1957 상악동)는 OMC 구조 변이(관절구 세포, 하르 세포, 상비갑개 기포) CBCT 측정값과 점막 두께의 관계를 정량화하여, 시술 전 CBCT 평가 체크리스트의 해부학적 축을 확장한다.

## One-line Summary

Retrospective CBCT study (n=1957 sinuses) found that osteomeatal complex variations — including ostium width, infundibulum length, ethmoidal bulla diameter, and UPSA types 3/4/6 — are significantly associated with maxillary sinus mucosal thickening and ostium obstruction, with implications for pre-surgical sinus floor elevation risk assessment.

## 한줄요약

후향적 CBCT 연구 (n=1957 상악동): 오스티오메아탈 복합체 (Osteomeatal Complex, OMC) 변이 — 골공 폭 (Ostium Width, OW), 누두부 길이 (Infundibulum Length, IL), 사골포 직경 (Ethmoidal Bulla Diameter, EBD), 구개돌기 상부 부착 (Uncinate Process Superior Attachment, UPSA) 유형 3·4·6 — 이 최대상악동 점막 두께 (Mucosal Thickness, MT) 및 골공 폐쇄와 유의하게 연관됨을 입증; 상악동저 거상술 (Sinus Floor Elevation, SFE) 시술 전 위험 평가 축 확장.

## 2. Key Contributions

1. First large-scale CBCT study (n=1957 sinuses) systematically linking OMC anatomical variations to Schneiderian membrane thickness and mucosal appearance classification.
2. Quantified five OMC parameters (OW, IL, EBD, Haller cells [HC], concha bullosa [CB], UPSA type) against four mucosal categories: normal, flat thickening, polypoid thickening, ostium obstruction.
3. Demonstrated that OMC drainage obstruction — not sinus floor anatomy alone — is a significant risk amplifier for sinus membrane pathology.
4. Identified sex differences: MT, IL, OW, and EBD significantly higher in men.
5. UPSA types 3, 4, and 6 significantly associated with polypoid thickening and ostium obstruction.
6. No significant relationship found between concha bullosa (CB) and MT.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **Design**: Retrospective CBCT analysis
- **Sample**: 1957 maxillary sinuses (bilateral in most patients)
- **OMC measurements**: Ostium Width (OW), Infundibulum Length (IL), Ethmoidal Bulla Diameter (EBD)
- **Anatomical variants recorded**: Haller cells (HC) presence/absence, Concha Bullosa (CB) presence/absence, Uncinate Process Superior Attachment (UPSA) type (types 1–6 per Landsberg classification)
- **Mucosal Thickness (MT)**: measured at thickest point; categorized as ≤2 mm (normal), flat thickening, polypoid thickening, ostium obstruction
- **Mucosal Appearance (MA)**: normal, flat thickening, polypoid thickening, ostium obstruction
- **Statistics**: Kruskal–Wallis, Mann–Whitney U, Chi-square tests; Generalized Estimating Equations (GEE) models to account for bilateral/repeated measurements
- **Software**: R version 2024 with gee package

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- **Mucosal thickening prevalence**: 54% of sinuses showed some thickening; flat thickening most common (23.5%)
- **Ostium obstruction**: significantly associated with MT >10 mm (p<0.05)
- **Sex differences**: MT, IL, OW, and EBD significantly higher in men (p<0.05)
- **Haller cells (HC)**: significant positive correlation with MT
- **Concha bullosa (CB)**: NO significant relationship with MT
- **UPSA types 3, 4, 6**: significantly associated with polypoid thickening and ostium obstruction
- **IL, OW, EBD**: all significantly positively correlated with MT
- Conclusion: OMC anatomical variants influence sinus membrane thickness; comprehensive OMC evaluation recommended before SFE planning

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Retrospective design; no longitudinal follow-up linking pre-operative OMC findings to intra-operative perforation rates
- Laterality not analyzed (right vs. left sinus differences)
- No direct correlation with SFE outcomes (perforation, implant survival) — inferential bridge to surgical risk remains indirect
- CBCT voxel size and scanner variability not standardized across included scans
- Future studies needed to correlate OMC variation findings with actual intraoperative perforation events

## 7. Glossary

- **OMC (Osteomeatal Complex, 오스티오메아탈 복합체)**: The functional unit of the middle meatus through which the maxillary, anterior ethmoid, and frontal sinuses drain. Comprises the ostium, infundibulum, hiatus semilunaris, ethmoidal bulla, and uncinate process.
- **OW (Ostium Width, 골공 폭)**: Width of the maxillary sinus ostium at its narrowest
- **IL (Infundibulum Length, 누두부 길이)**: Length of the ethmoid infundibulum
- **EBD (Ethmoidal Bulla Diameter, 사골포 직경)**: Diameter of the ethmoidal bulla
- **HC (Haller Cells, 하르 세포)**: Infraorbital ethmoid air cells in the floor of the orbit, adjacent to the maxillary ostium
- **CB (Concha Bullosa, 비갑개 기포)**: Pneumatized middle turbinate
- **UPSA (Uncinate Process Superior Attachment, 구개돌기 상부 부착)**: Superior attachment of the uncinate process; types 1–6 per Landsberg classification
- **MT (Mucosal Thickness, 점막 두께)**: Thickness of the Schneiderian membrane measured on CBCT
- **MA (Mucosal Appearance, 점막 형태)**: Qualitative classification: normal, flat thickening, polypoid thickening, ostium obstruction
- **GEE (Generalized Estimating Equations)**: Statistical method for correlated data (bilateral sinuses)
- **SFE (Sinus Floor Elevation, 상악동저 거상술)**: Surgical procedure to augment bone in posterior maxilla prior to implant placement
