## One-line Summary
USPSTF 2022 권고 갱신 (JAMA): 40-59세 ≥10% 10년 CVD 위험 성인 — 저용량 aspirin 1차예방 small net benefit (C grade, 개별 결정); ≥60세 — 신규 시작 권고 안 함 (출혈 위험 > 이득, D grade). 2016 권고에서 후퇴.

## 1. Document Information
- Authors: USPSTF (US Preventive Services Task Force)
- Type: Clinical practice recommendation statement (national guideline)
- Date: April 26, 2022 (online); JAMA 327(16):1577-1584

## 2. Key Contributions
1. **Major reversal**: prior (2016) recommendation supported aspirin primary prevention more broadly; 2022 narrows the eligible population significantly.
2. Age-stratified recommendations:
   - **40-59 y, ≥10% 10-year CVD risk**: C grade (small net benefit, individualize)
   - **≥60 y**: D grade (recommend against initiating)
3. Based on new systematic review (commissioned for this update) including effects on CVD events, CV mortality, all-cause mortality, colorectal cancer (CRC), and bleeding harms.

## 3. Methodology
USPSTF commissioned systematic review of:
- Aspirin effectiveness for CVD primary prevention (MI, stroke, CV/all-cause mortality)
- Aspirin effect on CRC incidence/mortality in primary CVD prevention
- Harms (especially bleeding)

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함]
| Age | 2016 USPSTF | 2022 USPSTF |
|---|---|---|
| 50-59 y, ≥10% CVD risk | B grade (recommend) | merged into 40-59 C |
| 60-69 y, ≥10% CVD risk | C grade | D grade (recommend against) |
| 40-59 y, ≥10% CVD risk | I (insufficient) | C grade (small benefit, individualize) |
| ≥60 y | (not covered) | D grade |

Key insight: bleeding harms (GI, intracranial) outweigh small CVD benefits with age, especially ≥60.

## 5. Limitations
- USPSTF scope = US adults without prior CVD; secondary prevention not addressed.
- CRC chemopreventive effect previously suggested no longer drives recommendation (downgraded based on newer data).
- Individual clinician-patient decision still required for 40-59 y group.

## 6. Related Work
- Bibbins-Domingo 2016 — prior USPSTF aspirin guideline.
- ASPREE, ARRIVE, ASCEND (2018) — RCTs that motivated the downgrade.

## 7. Glossary
- USPSTF: US Preventive Services Task Force
- CVD: Cardiovascular disease
- Grade C: small net benefit, offer to select individuals
- Grade D: recommend against
- CRC: Colorectal cancer
