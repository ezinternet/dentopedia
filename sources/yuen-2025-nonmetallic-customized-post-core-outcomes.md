## Why Ingested

Covers the newest digital/customized post materials (zirconia CAD/CAM, PEEK, PEKK, EverStick, Ribbond, bundle fiber posts) — extends the fiber vs metal post discussion to CAD/CAM-era materials with the most recent (2025) SR evidence. Extends [[post-and-core/figueiredo-2015-fiber-post-survival-systematic-review]] and fills the gap for individually fabricated systems not covered by prefabricated-fiber-post reviews.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review (12 studies, 829 teeth): CAD/CAM zirconia posts comparable to cast metal; PEEK/PEKK posts prone to debonding; individually formed fiber-reinforced composite systems show favorable (repairable) failure modes.

## 한줄요약

체계적 문헌고찰 (12편, 829치): CAD/CAM 지르코니아 포스트는 주조 포스트와 유사한 임상 결과; PEEK/PEKK는 탈락률 높음; 개별 성형 섬유강화복합 시스템은 수복 가능한 유리한 파절 양상.

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Journal of Endodontics, 52(4):525–537
- **Publication date**: 2025-11-21
- **Study type**: Systematic Review
- **Databases searched**: 5 databases (comprehensive search)
- **Included studies**: 12 studies, 829 teeth

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR to specifically evaluate **customized** (individually fabricated) nonmetallic post-and-core systems
- Covers both indirect (CAD/CAM: zirconia, PEEK, PEKK) and direct chairside methods (individually formed FRC: EverStick Post, Ribbond; rigid FRC: bundle fiber posts, prefabricated fiber posts relined with composite; corono-radicular direct composite)
- Highlights the distinction between **customized** systems (replicating root canal morphology) and standard prefabricated posts

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Comprehensive database search (5 databases)
- 12 studies included, 829 teeth total
- Indirect fabrication materials: CAD/CAM milled zirconia, PEEK, PEKK
- Direct chairside methods: EverStick Post, Ribbond (individually formed); bundle fiber posts, prefabricated fiber posts relined with composite resin (rigid); corono-radicular direct composite
- Outcome: clinical survival, failure modes, debonding incidence
- Limitation: limited data with considerable variability → significant heterogeneity

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Material | Outcome |
|---|---|
| CAD/CAM milled zirconia | Comparable to conventional cast posts |
| PEEK posts | Lower survival; high incidence of debonding |
| PEKK posts | Lower survival; high incidence of debonding |
| Individually formed FRC (EverStick, Ribbond) | Favorable failure patterns (repairable) |
| Rigid FRC systems | Included but specific outcomes not detailed in abstract |

- Overall: "promising" but evidence is limited with considerable variability and significant heterogeneity

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Available data limited across all system types
- Considerable variability across studies → significant heterogeneity
- Authors call for more standardized, higher-quality clinical trials on customized nonmetallic systems

## 6. Related Work

- Prefabricated fiber post SRs (existing body of literature)
- Cast metal post-and-core remains the comparator for zirconia customized posts
- EverStick Post and Ribbond systems evaluated as direct chairside alternatives

## 7. Glossary

- **Customized post-and-core**: individually fabricated to replicate specific root canal morphology (vs. prefabricated standard sizes)
- **CAD/CAM milled zirconia post**: computer-designed/machined zirconia post — indirect method
- **PEEK**: polyetheretherketone — polymer post material
- **PEKK**: polyetherketoneketone — polymer post material
- **EverStick Post / Ribbond**: individually formed fiber-reinforced composite systems — direct chairside
- **Bundle fiber post**: multiple fiber strands bundled to fit canal — rigid FRC category
- **Corono-radicular direct composite**: direct composite build-up extending into the root canal
