## One-line Summary
J Dent Shiraz 2018;19(1):48-56: Z-Prime Plus (conventional MDP) > All-Bond Universal (MDP) > control for zirconia SBS; both primers superior to untreated; Z-Prime highest (22.45 MPa).

## 1. Document Information
Journal: J Dent Shiraz Univ Med Sci 2018;19(1):48-56
Authors: Sharafeddin F, Shoale S

## 2. Key Results
- Group I (control): 7.58±1.62 MPa
- Group II (All-Bond Universal): 17.51±1.34 MPa
- Group III (Z-Prime Plus): 22.45±3.60 MPa
- Z-Prime Plus significantly higher than All-Bond Universal (p<0.002)
- Mixed failures in primer groups; adhesive failure only in control
