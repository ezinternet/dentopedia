## One-line Summary
Ex-vivo extracted mandibular molars (n=51, 25-45° curvature): manual SS K-file이 PathFile·Scout RaCe rotary 대비 3·9 mm level transportation 유의 증가; rotary 두 시스템 NS 차이.

## 2. Key Contributions
- 자연치(human dentin) 기반 glide path transportation 비교
- 3·6·9 mm level별 transportation 측정 (CBCT pre/post)
- Manual instrumentation의 부정적 결과 명확화

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- 51 extracted mandibular 1st/2nd molars, mesiobuccal root, 25-45° curvature
- 3 그룹: SS K-file manual / PathFile rotary / Scout RaCe rotary (n=17/group)
- Pre/post CBCT, 3·6·9 mm from apex transportation 측정
- Kruskal-Wallis + Friedman, p<0.05

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Manual SS: 3 mm + 9 mm level transportation 유의 더 큼 (vs rotary, p<0.05)
- PathFile vs Scout RaCe: NS 차이

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 51 표본 작음
- CBCT voxel resolution micro-CT 대비 낮음
- Operator 숙련도 표준화 미명시
- 한 가지 root만 — single curvature
