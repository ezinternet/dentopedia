## Why Ingested

기존 [[endodontics/irrigation/padmakumar-2022-irrigating-solutions-dentin-structure-ftir]]의 FTIR 단일 관점을 30편 systematic review로 확장. NaOCl이 유기질(amide), EDTA/산이 무기질(phosphate)에 미치는 영향을 분광학적으로 정리해 [[endodontics/irrigation/mohammadi-2019-smear-layer-irrigants-review]] 보강.

## One-line Summary
Systematic literature review (30 of 3452 studies) of FTIR-detected chemical changes to root dentin by irrigants — NaOCl mainly reduces organic (amide/phosphate ratio), EDTA mainly affects mineral, with glycolic acid stronger than EDTA.

## 한줄요약
SLR(3452편 중 30편): FTIR로 본 세정제의 상아질 화학 변화 — NaOCl은 유기질(amide/phosphate 비) 감소, EDTA는 무기질 영향, 글리콜산이 EDTA보다 강함.

## 1. Document Information
- Dental Materials 2024;40:1138–1163. Open Access (CC BY). Charité Berlin / Halle-Wittenberg.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First systematic FTIR-focused synthesis of irrigant-induced dentin chemistry changes.
- Separates organic (NaOCl) vs inorganic (EDTA/acids) effects; concentration/time dependence.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Search 1983–2023; OHAT risk of bias; in vitro extracted human/bovine teeth, FTIR with spectral data required.
- 3452 screened → 30 included; ATR-FTIR most common (21 studies).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Solutions: NaOCl, EDTA, HEDP, PAA, glycolic acid (GA), citric acid (CA).
- NaOCl reduces amide/phosphate ratio (organic loss) with rising concentration; EDTA effect on mineral increases with time/concentration; GA stronger than EDTA.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneous protocols/concentrations; only 30 studies presented usable spectra; in vitro.

## 6. Related Work
- FTIR-method companion to broader irrigant-on-dentin reviews.

## 7. Glossary
- FTIR/ATR: Fourier-transform infrared / attenuated total reflection.
- Amide/phosphate ratio: organic-to-mineral spectral marker of dentin.
