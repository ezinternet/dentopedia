## Why Ingested

가열 HF 산 처리가 세라믹 표면 거칠기와 레진 접착강도에 미치는 영향을 정량화한 논문으로, [[resin-bonding/venturini-2015-hf-acid-concentration-feldspathic-ceramic-bond]]의 HF 농도 연구를 온도 변수로 확장하고, [[resin-bonding/dimitriadi-2019-silane-reactivity-lithium-disilicate-bond-strength]]의 리튬 디실리케이트 접착 프로토콜에 물리적 표면 처리 근거를 보강한다.

## One-line Summary

In vitro (n per group, 9% HF ± heating to 70°C): Heated HF significantly increased surface roughness and bond strength (μTBS) for lithium disilicate (L70: 29.11 MPa) but not for feldspathic ceramic; room-temperature HF groups for both ceramics showed similar μTBS (~18–20 MPa).

## 한줄요약

In vitro (9% HF ± 70°C 가열): 리튬 디실리케이트에서 가열 HF가 표면 거칠기와 μTBS를 유의하게 증가(29.11 MPa)시켰으나, 장석계 세라믹에서는 가열 효과 없음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Tested heated (70°C) vs. room-temperature 9% HF etching on two clinically important ceramics
- Heated HF significantly improved μTBS only for lithium disilicate, not feldspathic ceramic
- L70 group achieved highest μTBS overall (29.11 ± 8.26 MPa)
- Both ceramics showed significantly increased surface roughness with heated HF vs. room-temperature HF
- No significant difference between FC, F70, and LC groups (~18–20 MPa range)
- SEM analysis confirmed differential microstructural etching patterns

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Design**: In vitro, controlled laboratory study.

**Material**: Feldspathic ceramic and lithium disilicate glass-ceramic disk specimens.

**Groups**:
- FP: Feldspathic ceramic polished surface (no treatment)
- FC: Feldspathic ceramic + 9% HF, 60 s, room temperature
- F70: Feldspathic ceramic + 9% HF, 60 s, 70°C
- LP: Lithium disilicate polished surface (no treatment)
- LC: Lithium disilicate + 9% HF, 20 s, room temperature
- L70: Lithium disilicate + 9% HF, 20 s, 70°C

**Surface roughness**: Contact stylus profilometer measuring Ra and Rq parameters.

**Bond testing**: All etched/heated groups silanized → bonded with dual-cure composite cement to resin composite disks → 24h → sectioned for μTBS.

**SEM**: Morphological analysis of etched surfaces.

**Statistics**: Welch's ANOVA + Games-Howell post-hoc test.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Surface roughness**: Both ceramics showed significantly increased Ra/Rq with HF etching vs. polished. Heated HF (70°C) further significantly increased surface roughness vs. room-temperature HF for both ceramics.

**Microtensile bond strength (μTBS)**:
| Group | μTBS (MPa ± SD) |
|-------|-----------------|
| L70 | 29.11 ± 8.26 — highest, significantly superior |
| FC | 19.94 ± 4.14 |
| F70 | 18.24 ± 5.29 |
| LC | 17.87 ± 6.96 |

- FC, F70, LC: no significant differences (p > 0.05)
- L70 significantly higher than all other groups (p < 0.001)

**Conclusion**: Heated HF (70°C) improves bond strength specifically for lithium disilicate; feldspathic ceramic does not benefit similarly from heated HF in terms of μTBS.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Abstract-only data extraction (publisher paywall)
- No aging/durability testing reported; immediate μTBS only
- Specific etching times differ between ceramics (60 s vs. 20 s), reflecting material-specific protocols — direct comparison limited
- Heating protocol not standardized clinically; practical implementation challenges

## 7. Glossary

- **Heated HF (가열 불화수소산)**: HF 산을 70°C로 가열하여 적용; 리튬 디실리케이트에서 미세구조 에칭 효과 증대
- **μTBS (Microtensile Bond Strength, 마이크로인장접착강도)**: 소형 bar 시편 인장 접착강도
- **Ra (Average Roughness, 평균 거칠기)**: 표면 거칠기 파라미터; 중심선 평균
- **Rq (Root Mean Square Roughness, 제곱평균제곱근 거칠기)**: 거칠기의 RMS 값
- **Feldspathic ceramic (장석계 세라믹)**: 유리질 함량 높은 도재; HF로 잘 에칭됨
- **Lithium disilicate (리튬 디실리케이트)**: IPS e.max 계열; HF 에칭으로 결정상 노출 후 높은 강도와 우수한 접착력
- **Dual-cure composite cement (이중중합 복합레진 시멘트)**: 광중합+화학중합; 세라믹 수복물 접착에 사용
