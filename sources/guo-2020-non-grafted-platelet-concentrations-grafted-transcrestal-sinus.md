## One-line Summary
SR+MA (18 studies; 13 non-grafted TSFE + 5 platelet-grafted TSFE): 1-year implant survival rate (ISR) statistically equivalent — non-grafted 97% vs PC-grafted 99%. Suggests **graft material may be optional** for transcrestal sinus elevation with sufficient RBH.

## 1. Document Information
Scientific Reports open-access SR+MA. Search through April 2019. 18 studies meeting inclusion: 3 controlled, 15 non-randomized (prospective and retrospective cohort).

## 2. Key Contributions
- 1-year ISR for **non-grafted TSFE = 97%** (95% CI 0.96–0.99)
- 1-year ISR for **PC-TSFE = 99%** (95% CI 0.97–1.00)
- **No statistically significant difference** between groups
- Sub-analysis by RBH within non-grafted group: 1-year ISR not significantly different across RBH strata

## 3. Methodology
- PRISMA-compliant SR+MA
- Inclusion: clinical controlled trials, retrospective and prospective cohort
- Outcomes: implant survival rate (ISR), MBL/CBL, endo-sinus bone gain (ESBG)
- Meta-analysis on 1-year-based values
- 13 non-grafted TSFE studies / 5 PC-grafted (PRP, PRF, CGF, PRGF)
- Quality: 15 non-randomized studies all rated low risk of bias; controlled studies mixed

## 4. Key Results
| Group | n studies | 1-yr ISR | 95% CI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Non-grafted TSFE | 13 | 97% | 0.96–0.99 |
| PC-grafted TSFE | 5 | 99% | 0.97–1.00 |

## 5. Limitations
- Heterogeneous study designs (mostly non-randomized)
- Search ends 2019
- Various platelet concentrate types pooled (PRP / PRF / CGF / PRGF)
- Only 1-year data; long-term unclear
