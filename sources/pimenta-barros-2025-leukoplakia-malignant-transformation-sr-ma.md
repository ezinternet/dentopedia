## Why Ingested
기존 [[oral-medicine/opmd/aguirre-urizar-2021-leukoplakia-malignant-transformation-sr-ma]]는 2021년까지 데이터를 다뤘으나, 이 논문은 1934년부터 2024년 6월까지 전 기간을 포괄하는 최대 규모(n=41,231) SR+MA로 OL 악성변환율의 시대적 변화까지 분석. 기존 estimates를 갱신하는 가장 comprehensive한 데이터.

## One-line Summary
SR+MA of 55 studies (n=41,231 with OL): pooled malignant transformation rate 6.64% (95% CI 5.21–8.21); non-homogeneous subtype (RR=4.23), epithelial dysplasia (RR=2.75), lateral tongue (RR=2.09), and smoking (RR=1.64) are the strongest MT risk factors.

## 한줄요약
55개 연구 SR+MA(n=41,231): 구강백반증 악성변환율 6.64%; 비균일형(RR=4.23), 상피이형성(RR=2.75), 설측연(RR=2.09), 흡연(RR=1.64)이 주요 위험인자.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Most comprehensive OL MT meta-analysis to date: 55 studies, n=41,231, covering 1934–June 2024
- Pooled MT = 6.64% — lower than prior estimates (Aguirre-Urizar 2021: 9.8%; Warnakulasuriya 2016: 3.5%)
- MT rates did NOT significantly change across time periods (pre-1978, 1979–2007, post-2007): p=0.75
- Quantified RR for key risk factors with precise confidence intervals
- Lateral tongue location carries 12.71% absolute MT rate — highest of any subsite

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Design: SR+MA following Cochrane and Joanna Briggs Institute CoCoPop framework
- Databases: MEDLINE, Embase, Web of Science, Scopus; no lower date limit, through June 2024
- Inclusion: longitudinal cohort studies reporting OL MT probability; no language restriction
- Exclusion: PVL, OLP, OSMF, case-control, cross-sectional, RCTs altering natural history
- Risk of bias: Joanna Briggs Institute tool for meta-analyses of proportions
- Heterogeneity exploration: subgroup meta-analyses
- 55 studies included, n=41,231

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Pooled MT: **6.64%** (95% CI = 5.21–8.21)
- By time period: 5.35% (pre-1978), 7.06% (1979–2007), 6.97% (post-2007) — no sig. difference
- Non-homogeneous OL: RR = **4.23** (95% CI 3.31–5.39, p<0.001)
- Larger size: RR = **2.08** (95% CI 1.45–2.96, p<0.001)
- Lateral tongue: MT = 12.71%; RR = **2.09** (95% CI 1.48–2.95, p<0.001)
- Smoking: RR = **1.64** (95% CI 1.25–2.15, p<0.001)
- Epithelial dysplasia: RR = **2.75** (95% CI 2.26–3.35, p<0.001)

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Heterogeneous OL diagnostic criteria across 90 years of included studies
- Retrospective cohort designs dominant; selection bias
- Follow-up duration highly variable across studies
- Lack of standardized dysplasia grading systems across eras
- Authors call for prospective registries with standardized OL classification

## 6. Related Work
