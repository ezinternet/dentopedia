## Why Ingested

Anchors the trauma–regenerative-endodontics bridge in the new `dental-trauma` category: it isolates *trauma-related* pulp necrosis as a 3.59× risk factor for root-development failure in REPs. Extends the device-/protocol-centric REP synthesis in [[overviews/regenerative-endodontics-rep-overview]] with a prognosis lens, and connects to the trauma necrosis upstream in [[dental-trauma/tewari-2024-pulp-necrosis-lateral-luxation]].

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (20 studies, 13 observational + 7 RCT; GRADE very low): REP clinical/radiographic healing >81% regardless of pre-op factors, but trauma-related necrosis (RR 3.59), incisors (RR 1.90), and apical lesions (RR 2.55) carry higher root-development-failure risk.

## 한줄요약

SR+MA (20편, 관찰 13 + RCT 7; GRADE 매우 낮음): 재생근관치료(REP)의 임상·방사선학적 치유는 술전 인자와 무관하게 81% 초과이나, 외상성 치수괴사(RR 3.59)·절치(RR 1.90)·치근단 병소(RR 2.55)는 치근발육 실패 위험이 높다.

## 1. Document Information

- Type: Systematic review + meta-analysis (meta-analysis of proportions + effect sizes).
- Journal: International Endodontic Journal 2025;58(12):1814-1834.
- Abstract-only — full text not retrieved (PMC unavailable).

## 2. Key Contributions

- First to isolate aetiology (trauma vs anatomic anomaly) as a prognostic factor for REP root development.
- Separates the healing endpoint (insensitive to pre-op factors) from the harder continued-root-development endpoint (sensitive).
- Quantifies tooth-type (incisor) and apical-lesion penalties.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Six databases + grey literature.
- RoB: Newcastle-Ottawa Scale (observational), Cochrane RoB 2 (RCT).
- Meta-analyses of proportions (pooled rates) and effect sizes (RR); GRADE certainty.
- 20 studies (13 observational, 7 RCT); most moderate–high RoB.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- Clinical & radiographic healing: >81% across all pre-op factors.
- Root development achieved: 52–95%.
- Complete apical closure: 32–91%.
- Trauma necrosis → root-development failure: RR 3.59 (95% CI 1.21–10.67, p=0.02) vs anatomic anomaly.
- Incisors → root-development failure RR 1.90 (1.37–2.63); incomplete apical closure RR 1.98 (1.34–3.13) vs premolars.
- Apical lesion → root-development failure RR 2.55 (1.63–4.86, p=0.01).
- Certainty: GRADE very low.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very low certainty; between-study heterogeneity; moderate-to-high RoB.
- Pre-op factors not associated with healing — caution before clinical extrapolation.
- Need standardized REP outcome reporting.

## 7. Glossary

- REP: regenerative endodontic procedure — blood-clot/scaffold revitalization of necrotic immature teeth.
- Root-development failure: lack of continued root lengthening/wall thickening despite clinical healing.
- Apical closure: narrowing/closure of the open apex over time.
- RR: relative risk.
