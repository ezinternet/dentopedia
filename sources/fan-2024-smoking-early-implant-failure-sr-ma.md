## One-line Summary
SR+MA (J Dent 2024, 32 observational studies, n=59,246 implants / 14,115 patients, 1994-2024): 흡연 → 조기 임플란트 실패 OR 2.59 (95% CI 2.08-3.23) at implant level; 환자 단위에서도 유의한 증가.

## 2. Key Contributions
1. 가장 큰 흡연-조기 임플란트 실패 SR+MA (n=59,246 임플란트).
2. OR 2.59 (95% CI 2.08-3.23) — 정량적 anchor.
3. Implant-level + patient-level 분석.

## 3. Methodology
PubMed/Embase/Scopus/WoS/Cochrane through June 2024; observational studies on smoking and early implant failure; 21 cohort studies for MA at implant level.

## 4. Key Results
[근거강함] Implant level: OR 2.59 (2.08-3.23) for early failure in smokers vs non-smokers. Patient-level data also significant.

## 5. Limitations
- Observational
- "Early failure" definition varies
