## Why Ingested

기존 [[oral-surgery/hajibagheri-2025-prf-alveolar-osteitis-post-extraction-sr]] 및 [[oral-surgery/pattnayak-2024-prf-prp-third-molar-extraction-sr]]는 PRP/PRF/L-PRF를 다루지만, CGF(Concentrated Growth Factors)는 별도 원심분리 프로토콜로 생성되는 2세대 이후 농축물로 기존 SR에 포함되지 않음. 본 SR은 CGF 단독 효과를 평가한 첫 종합 review.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review of 11 RCTs evaluating CGF application after mandibular third molar extraction: pain improved in 75% of studies, swelling in 57%, analgesic use reduced in 100%, and radiographic bone fill improved in 80%; effects on trismus and alveolar osteitis remain uncertain.

## 한줄요약

사랑니 발치 후 CGF(농축 성장인자) 적용을 평가한 11개 RCT SR — 통증 개선 75%, 부기 57%, 진통제 소비 100% 감소, 방사선 골충전 80% 개선; 개구제한·치조골염 효과는 불명확.

## 2. Key Contributions
- First SR specifically on CGF (not PRF, L-PRF, or PRP) after third molar extraction
- Evaluated 11 RCTs from 380 initially identified studies
- CGF consistently reduces pain (75% of studies) and analgesic consumption (100% of studies)
- Radiographic bone fill improved in 80% of studies (4/5)
- Periodontal parameters distal to the second molar improved in all 4 studies that assessed this
- Soft tissue healing improved in both studies evaluating it

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Design**: Systematic review (quantitative synthesis not feasible due to high heterogeneity)
- **PICOS**: Healthy adults undergoing third molar extraction, CGF vs no-CGF or placebo
- **Included studies**: 11 RCTs (from 380 identified)
- **Excluded**: Non-RCTs, non-adult patients, case reports, non-third molar extractions
- **Risk of bias**: 5 studies — unclear risk; 6 studies — low risk (modified Cochrane tool)
- **Reason no meta-analysis**: High heterogeneity in study designs, CGF preparation protocols, and outcome measurement

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

| Outcome | Studies evaluating | CGF benefit |
|---|---|---|
| Pain | 8 studies | 6/8 (75%) improved |
| Swelling/edema | 7 studies | 4/7 (57%) improved |
| Analgesic consumption | 2 studies | 2/2 (100%) reduced |
| Trismus | 4 studies | 2/4 (50%) improved |
| Alveolar osteitis (dry socket) | 3 studies | 1/3 (33%) reduced; 2 NS |
| Soft tissue healing | 2 studies | 2/2 (100%) improved |
| Radiographic bone fill | 5 studies | 4/5 (80%) improved |
| Periodontal parameters | 4 studies | 4/4 (100%) improved |
| Patient-reported outcomes | 2 studies | ≥1 domain improved in 2/2 |

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- No meta-analysis possible due to heterogeneity — limits evidence strength
- Small number of included studies (11/380)
- Variable CGF preparation protocols across studies
- Unclear mechanism of action distinction from PRF/L-PRF not discussed
- Need for large-scale standardized RCTs

## 7. Glossary
- **CGF (Concentrated Growth Factors)**: 농축 성장인자 — Medifuge 원심분리기를 이용한 자가 혈소판 농축물; PRF보다 더 조밀한 피브린 매트릭스 형성
- **L-PRF**: Leukocyte- and Platelet-Rich Fibrin — 백혈구 함유 혈소판풍부피브린
- **A-PRF**: Advanced PRF — 저속 원심분리 PRF 프로토콜
- **Alveolar osteitis (dry socket)**: 치조골염 — 발치 후 혈병 소실로 인한 통증 합병증
