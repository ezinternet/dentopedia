## Why Ingested

[[wiki/sinus-lift/transcrestal/zhao-2018-transalveolar-osteotome-sinus-floor-rct-protocol]]이 참조 근거로 RFA 추적 설계를 채택했으며, 이 설계의 foundational prospective 데이터가 wiki에 없음. Lai 2008은 graftless OSFE의 ISQ stability dip 패턴을 20주간 tracking한 가장 초기 전향적 연구 중 하나.

## One-line Summary
Prospective (n=42 ITI implants, 32 patients, 20-week RFA): graftless OSFE survival 95.2%; ISQ dips at weeks 2–6, recovers by week 16; RBH and bone type do not predict ISQ.

## 한줄요약
전향적(42 ITI 임플란트, 32환자, 20주 RFA): 골이식 없는 OSFE 생존율 95.2%; ISQ는 2–6주 dip 후 16주에 회복; RBH·골형은 ISQ 예측인자 아님.

## 1. Document Information
- **Journal**: Clinical Oral Implants Research 2008;19(5):469–475
- **DOI**: 10.1111/j.1600-0501.2007.01501.x
- **Published**: 26 March 2008
- **Study type**: Prospective cohort
- **n**: 42 implants / 32 patients
- **Follow-up**: 20 weeks (RFA at day 0, weeks 2, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20)
- **Institution**: Shanghai Ninth People's Hospital / Shanghai Jiaotong University

## 2. Key Contributions
- Graftless OSFE에서 ISQ stability dip 패턴 최초 체계적 기록
- RBH(4–8mm), 임플란트 길이, 골형(Lekholm & Zarb type 3 vs 4)이 ISQ에 영향 없음
- 2주–6주 dip → 16주 recovery 패턴 → 이후 안정

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- **Technique**: OSFE without bone grafting; RBH 4–8mm (mean 6.36mm)
- **Implant**: ITI (SLA surface)
- **Bone type**: Lekholm & Zarb type 1–4 (groups 3 and 4 most common in posterior maxilla)
- **RFA measurement**: Osstell; 8 timepoints over 20 weeks
- **Outcome**: ISQ trajectory, survival, factors affecting ISQ

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- **Survival**: 40/42 osseointegrated (95.2%)
- **ISQ pattern**: good primary stability → dip at weeks 2–6 → recovery by week 16 → stable plateau
- **ISQ at placement**: adequate primary stability in all 40 survivors (specific mean not extracted from web-PDF)
- **Bone type 3 vs 4**: no significant ISQ difference at placement or follow-up
- **RBH (4–8mm)**: no significant effect on ISQ pattern
- **Implant length**: no significant effect on ISQ

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- 5-month follow-up only — no loading outcome data
- Web-captured PDF from Wiley (full numerical tables not readable)
- Single center, single operator likely
- Pre-dates Osstell ISQ v2/Mentor; absolute ISQ values may not translate directly

## 6. Related Work
- Volpe 2022: graftless OSFE long-term (ISQ 73→76 at 8.5yr)
- Zhao 2018 RCT: uses same RFA 6-point design (baseline, 6, 8, 12, 16, 26wk)
- Sansupakorn 2024: ISQ in OSFE ±BCP (mean ISQ ~79 at 1yr)

## 7. Glossary
- **ITI implant**: Institut Straumann/ITI SLA surface (sandblasted large-grit acid-etched)
- **RFA** (Resonance Frequency Analysis, 공명 주파수 분석): Osstell 장비로 ISQ 측정
- **ISQ dip**: secondary stability 전환 과정에서 보이는 일시적 안정성 감소 (2–6주)
- **Lekholm & Zarb**: 골 밀도 분류 type 1(dense) ~ type 4(soft)
