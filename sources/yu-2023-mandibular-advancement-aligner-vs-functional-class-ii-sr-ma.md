## Why Ingested

`clear-aligner` 복잡/Class II 축: distalization([[wiki/orthodontics/bhate-2025-cat-maxillary-molar-distalization-class-ii-sr]]) 외 또 다른 Class II 전략(mandibular advancement, MA aligner)을 functional appliance와 비교. PMC OA 전문(efetch).

## One-line Summary

SR+MA (9 controlled trials, n=283): mandibular advancement aligners and traditional functional appliances give comparable skeletal/dental Class II correction (SNA/SNB/ANB/overjet n.s.); aligners control lower-incisor proclination better (1.94 deg less) but produce less mandibular ramus growth (Co-Go 1.10 mm less).

## 한줄요약

SR+MA(대조연구 9편, n=283): MA aligner와 전통적 functional appliance의 골격·치성 Class II 교정은 유사(SNA/SNB/ANB/overjet 무의미). aligner가 하악 전치 순측경사를 더 잘 억제(1.94도 적음)하나 하악지 성장(Co-Go)은 1.10 mm 적음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- Compares MA-aligner vs functional appliance for skeletal Class II with cephalometric pooled estimates.
- Highlights the aligner trade-off: better incisor inclination control, less ramus growth.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

PubMed/WoS/Embase/Cochrane + Chinese databases; 9 clinical controlled trials, n=283; ROBINS-I; RevMan 5.4 / Stata 17.0.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- SNA, SNB, ANB, Go-Pog, U1-SN, overjet: no significant difference.
- Mandibular plane angle (MP-SN) difference 0.90 deg between groups.
- Traditional group: mandibular ramus growth (Co-Go) 1.10 mm greater than aligner.
- Aligner group: lower-incisor labial inclination 1.94 deg better controlled.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Controlled (not all randomized) trials; modest n; auxiliary measures suggested to improve ramus growth.

## 7. Glossary

- **Mandibular advancement aligner**: aligner with built-in precision wings to posture the mandible forward (e.g., Invisalign MA).
- **Co-Go**: condylion-to-gonion (ramus height).
- **SNA/SNB/ANB**: sagittal cephalometric angles. **U1-SN**: upper-incisor inclination.
