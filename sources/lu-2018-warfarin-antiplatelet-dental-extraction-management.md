## One-line Summary
대규모 후향 코호트 (Kaohsiung Chang Gung, Taiwan, n=1,331 환자/1,816 occasions): warfarin 지속 (INR<4.0)군 발치 후 출혈률 9.1% vs warfarin-heparin bridging군 — 지속이 안전; aspirin/clopidogrel/DAPT 지속 시 발치 후 출혈은 모두 국소 지혈로 통제 가능; 항혈전제 중단 불필요.

- Authors: Lu Shin-Yu, Lin Liang-Ho, Hsue Shui-Sang

- Type: Original article, retrospective cohort
- Study period: not specified explicitly; published 2018

## 2. Key Contributions
1. Large-scale Taiwanese real-world data on dental extraction outcomes across all antithrombotic regimens: warfarin continued vs heparin-bridged, aspirin, clopidogrel, dual antiplatelet therapy (DAPT), and matched controls (n=1,088).
2. Quantifies post-extraction bleeding incidence by regimen and intervention.
3. Provides Asian-population data complementing Western SR-level evidence.

## 3. Methodology
- Retrospective cohort, single center
- n=1,331 patients across 1,816 extraction occasions
- Groups:
  - Warfarin (n=60): continued (28 pts / 33 occasions, INR<4.0) vs stopped + bridging heparin in hospital (32 pts / 37 occasions)
  - Antiplatelet (n=183): aspirin (125/185), clopidogrel (42/65), DAPT (16/24)
  - Control (n=1,088 / 1,472 occasions): no antithrombotic
- Outcomes: bleeding incidence, clinico-demographic factors, INR, antiplatelet regimen

## 4. Key Results
| Group | Bleeding rate |
|---|---|
| Warfarin continued (INR<4.0) | 9.1% |
| Warfarin stopped + heparin bridging | — (data in paper) |
| Aspirin continued | — |
| Clopidogrel continued | — |
| DAPT continued | — |
| Control (no antithrombotic) | baseline |

[합의수준] Authors conclude: extractions can be done without interrupting warfarin (INR<4.0) or single/dual antiplatelet therapy, with local hemostasis sufficient to control bleeding.

## 5. Limitations
- Single center, retrospective
- INR<4.0 cutoff — does not address very high INR
- Heparin bridging cohort small, comparison statistically limited
- No DOAC data (2018 Taiwan; mostly warfarin era)

## 6. Related Work
- Modern evidence: Inchingolo 2024 SR (anticoagulation in dentistry), Moldovan 2023 SR (anticoagulant oral surgery bleeding).
- Aligns with "continue anticoagulation, control locally" paradigm now standard.

## 7. Glossary
- INR: International Normalized Ratio
- DAPT: Dual antiplatelet therapy (typically aspirin + clopidogrel)
- Heparin bridging: peri-procedural switch from oral anticoagulant to short-acting heparin
