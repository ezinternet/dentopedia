## One-line Summary
Short-term clinical evaluation of titanium mesh combined with CGF (Concentrated Growth Factor) membranes for GBR at immediate implant placement in anterior maxilla. Reports acceptable hard-tissue reconstruction and contour preservation.

## 1. Document Information
- **Title:** Short-Term Evaluation of Guided Bone Reconstruction with Titanium Mesh Membranes and CGF Membranes in Immediate Implantation of Anterior Maxillary Tooth

- **Year:** 2021
- **DOI:** 10.1155/2021/4754078
- **Study type:** retrospective

## 2. Key Contributions
Documents a low-cost autologous-CGF + Ti mesh protocol for immediate-implant GBR in the esthetic zone, with attention to mesh exposure rates.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
Short-term clinical and radiographic follow-up of immediate-implant GBR cases using Ti mesh + CGF membranes.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Acceptable bone regeneration on buccal aspect; gingival contour broadly maintained. Mesh exposure occurred in a subset of cases.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
Short follow-up; non-comparative; CGF preparation operator-dependent.

## 7. Glossary
- **IIP:** Immediate Implant Placement — placement at the time of tooth extraction.
- **IIPP:** Immediate Implant Placement and Provisionalization — IIP combined with same-visit provisional restoration.
- **SRP:** Sagittal Root Position — Kan I/II/III/IV classification of root-to-bone relationship.
- **CBCT:** Cone-Beam Computed Tomography.
- **PES/WES:** Pink/White Esthetic Score — esthetic outcome indices for anterior implants.
- **EMBL:** Early Marginal Bone Loss.
