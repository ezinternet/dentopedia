## Why Ingested

치조제보존술 (Alveolar Ridge Preservation, ARP) 후 조기식립 (early implant placement) 3년 장기 결과를 보고하는 RCT로, 기존 1년 추적 데이터를 보고한 선행 연구들을 3년까지 확장한다. [[bone-regeneration/ridge-preservation/strauss-2024-ridge-preservation-early-implant-aesthetic]]와 비교하여 조기 식립 프로토콜에서 ARP의 장기 효과(외형 변화 및 방사선학적 MBL)를 평가하는 핵심 근거.

## One-line Summary

Exploratory RCT (n=35) comparing DBBM-C/CM, DBBM-C, and spontaneous healing (SH) before early implant placement: all groups showed significant but clinically acceptable contour reduction at 3 years (−0.23, −0.21, −0.33 mm), with no significant differences in marginal bone loss between groups.

## 한줄요약

탈단백 소 골 미네랄 (Deproteinized Bovine Bone Mineral, DBBM)-C/CM, DBBM-C, 자발적 치유 (Spontaneous Healing, SH) 세 군을 조기 식립 전에 비교한 탐색적 무작위 대조 시험 (Randomized Controlled Trial, RCT) (n=35): 3년 추적에서 모든 군 외형 감소 통계적 유의 (−0.23, −0.21, −0.33 mm), 군 간 변연골 소실 (Marginal Bone Level, MBL) 차이는 유의하지 않음.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First reported 3-year follow-up of early implant placement (type 2, 8 weeks post-extraction) following ARP with DBBM-C or DBBM-C/CM vs spontaneous healing
- Demonstrates all three groups achieve stable marginal bone levels and clinical parameters at 3 years; no statistically significant between-group differences in marginal bone level (MBL) change
- ARP groups show marginally better contour maintenance (−0.21 to −0.23 mm) vs SH (−0.33 mm), though between-group differences not statistically significant
- Confirms clinically acceptable outcomes for early implant placement regardless of ARP protocol

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

**Study design**: Exploratory RCT, 3-group parallel design, 3-year follow-up of a study initiated 2015–2018.

**Patients**: 35 patients requiring single-tooth extraction of incisors, canines, or premolars (molars excluded). Inclusion: intact buccal plate or ≤50% buccal dehiscence. Exclusion: systemic diseases, smoking >20 cigarettes/day, PCR >20%, untreated periodontitis, pregnancy.

**Interventions**:
1. DBBM-C/CM: DBBM-C (Bio-Oss Collagen) + collagen matrix seal (Geistlich Mucograft Seal, 8 mm) fixed with 6/0 sutures
2. DBBM-C: Bio-Oss Collagen alone, criss-cross stabilization suture
3. SH: spontaneous healing, no graft

**Implant placement**: 8 weeks post-extraction (early/type 2 protocol). Astra OsseospeedEV implants (Dentsply Sirona). GBR performed if dehiscence/fenestration or buccal bone <1.5 mm. Final crowns: zirconia screw-retained or cemented on Ti/zirconia abutments.

**Outcomes**:
- Profilometric analysis: silicone impressions + digital scans at extraction, implant placement, FDPi, FU-1Y, FU-3Y → superimposed to measure buccal contour change
- Radiographic: periapical radiographs for MBL at FDPi, FU-1Y, FU-3Y
- Clinical: plaque control record (PCR), bleeding on probing (BOP), probing pocket depth (PPD), suppuration, implant mobility

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Contour changes (FDPi → FU-3Y, median)**:
- DBBM-C/CM: −0.23 mm (statistically significant, P<0.05)
- DBBM-C: −0.21 mm (statistically significant, P<0.05)
- SH: −0.33 mm (statistically significant, P<0.05)
- Between-group differences: not reported as statistically significant

**Radiographic MBL (FDPi → FU-3Y, median)**:
- DBBM-C/CM: +0.02 mm (bone gain)
- DBBM-C: −0.5 mm
- SH: −0.18 mm
- Within-group and between-group changes: not statistically significant (P>0.05)

**Clinical parameters**: Stable across all groups throughout 3-year follow-up (PCR, BOP, PPD within acceptable ranges, no suppuration or mobility events reported).

**Key interpretation**: Despite statistically significant contour reductions within all groups, the magnitude (~0.2–0.3 mm) is clinically acceptable. ARP with DBBM-C ± collagen matrix offers slightly but not significantly better contour preservation vs SH at 3 years post early implant placement.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Small sample (n=35) — underpowered for between-group comparisons; exploratory design limits definitive conclusions
- Industry funding (Geistlich Pharma) introduces potential bias
- No blinding of patients or operators (inherent to surgical RCT)
- GBR was performed at implant placement when indicated — introduces confounding variable between groups
- Long recruitment period (2015–2018) may affect standardization
- Missing FU-3Y data for some patients (dropouts not fully reported)
- Future: larger confirmatory RCT; investigation of soft tissue augmentation adjuncts; economic analysis of added ARP cost vs clinical benefit at 3+ years

## 7. Glossary

- **ARP (치조제보존술, Alveolar Ridge Preservation)**: Procedure to minimize ridge resorption following tooth extraction using graft materials ± barrier membranes
- **DBBM-C (탈단백 소 골 미네랄 콜라겐, Deproteinized Bovine Bone Mineral with 10% Collagen)**: Xenogeneic slowly resorbing bone substitute (Bio-Oss Collagen, Geistlich)
- **CM (교원질 기질, Collagen Matrix)**: Soft tissue substitute membrane used to seal the socket (Mucograft Seal, Geistlich)
- **SH (자발적 치유, Spontaneous Healing)**: Control condition — socket heals without any graft or membrane
- **Early implant placement (조기 식립)**: Type 2 implant placement at 4–8 weeks post-extraction, after soft tissue healing
- **FDPi (고정성 보철물 장착일, Fixed Dental Prosthesis Insertion)**: Baseline timepoint for follow-up measurements
- **MBL (변연골 소실, Marginal Bone Level/Loss)**: Radiographic measure of crestal bone around implant
- **Profilometric analysis (외형 계측 분석)**: 3D surface analysis of ridge contour change using superimposed digital scans/impressions
- **GBR (골유도재생술, Guided Bone Regeneration)**: Simultaneous bone augmentation at implant placement when bony defects are present
