Zirconia-bonding technique + material comparison for resin cements; supports [[resin/muller-2022-resin-cements-zirconia-bulk-ageing]] and [[resin/theracem-bisco-product-reference]].

## One-line Summary
In-vitro: resin-cement-to-zirconia adhesion under incremental vs bulk build-up, mould, and ageing; TheraCem among strongest.

## 한줄요약
in-vitro — 지르코니아 합착 적층기법·mould·노화 영향, TheraCem 상위.

## 1. Document Information
Materials 2022; 15: 2186. DOI 10.3390/ma15062186. Open access. University of Zurich (Özcan group).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Isolates application-technique variables alongside material and ageing.
- Comparative zirconia-bonding ranking incl. TheraCem.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
In-vitro shear bond strength of multiple resin cements to zirconia; factors: incremental vs bulk build-up, mould use, ageing (thermocycling).

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
Build-up technique, mould, and ageing influence zirconia bond strength; TheraCem among highest in dry/some aged conditions.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
In-vitro; clinical translation limited.

## 6. Related Work
Mahrous 2020 (MDP cement bonding); Shely 2024 (cement marginal adaptation).

## 7. Glossary
- Bulk vs incremental build-up: single-mass vs layered cement application.
- Zirconia: yttria-stabilized tetragonal zirconia polycrystal restorative ceramic.
