## One-line Summary

Pilot CBCT study (13 patients, RBH <8 mm) of transcrestal sinus elevation by osseodensification with graft, showing significant immediate bone-height gain of 6.65 mm, modest 0.90 mm graft contraction at 12 months, and 100% implant success.

## 한줄요약

CBCT pilot 연구(환자 13명, 잔존골고 <8 mm) — osseodensification+이식재 경치조골 상악동거상으로 즉시 6.65 mm 골고 증가, 12개월째 이식재 수축 0.90 mm, 임플란트 성공률 100%.

## 2. Key Contributions
- Quantifies immediate bone-height gain and 12-month graft contraction for transcrestal OD with graft.
- Assesses correlation between initial/final bone height and implant stability.
- CBCT-based standardized radiographic measurement protocol.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- Pilot prospective study; 13 patients (4 M / 9 F; mean age 54.69 ± 5.86 y).
- One implant each in posterior maxilla; RBH <8 mm, minimum width 5 mm.
- Transcrestal sinus floor elevation by osseodensification + graft material.
- CBCT measurements immediately post-op and at 12 months; SPSS 23, p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Preoperative mean bone height: 5.70 ± 0.95 mm.
- Immediate post-op gain: +6.65 ± 1.06 mm (significant).
- 12-month graft contraction: 0.90 ± 0.49 mm.
- Implant success: 100% at 12 months.
- No correlation found between initial/final bone height and implant stability.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Pilot, single-arm, n=13 — no control group.
- Short (12-month) follow-up; radiographic outcomes only.
- No histology of regenerated bone.
