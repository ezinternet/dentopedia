## Why Ingested

조기 임플란트 실패 surveillance 배치에서 "즉시로딩 full-arch(all-on-four)"라는 고위험 시나리오의 위험인자를 다루는 대규모 후향연구. 일반 식립 코호트인 [[implants/yari-2023-risk-factors-early-implant-failure]]가 즉시 잠정보철을 위험인자(OR 3.42)로 지목한 것을, 즉시로딩만 모은 집단에서 후방상악·흡연으로 재확인·세분화한다. 또한 [[implants/fan-2024-smoking-early-implant-failure-sr-ma]]의 흡연 신호를 all-on-four 맥락에서 보강(OR 2.92).

## One-line Summary

Retrospective cohort (561 patients / 2364 implants, all-on-four immediate loading): 1-year implant-level survival 98.9% (maxilla) vs 99.6% (mandible); multivariate risk factors for early failure were maxilla (OR 3.12) and smoking (OR 2.92), while photofunctionalisation showed a non-significant protective trend (OR 0.51, p=0.25).

## 한줄요약

후향 코호트(환자 561명 / 임플란트 2364개, all-on-four 즉시로딩): 1년 임플란트 단위 생존율 상악 98.9% vs 하악 99.6%; 조기 실패 다변량 위험인자는 상악(OR 3.12)·흡연(OR 2.92)이며, 광기능화(photofunctionalisation)는 보호 경향(OR 0.51)이나 비유의(p=0.25).

## 1. Document Information

- Journal: Bioengineering (Basel), 2024;11(3):223 (2024 Feb 27).
- DOI: 10.3390/bioengineering11030223 · PMID 38534497 · PMCID PMC10968038 (open access, full text retrieved).
- Single private clinic (Malo Dental & Medical Tokyo) + Showa University School of Dentistry; placements Sep 2005-Oct 2020.
- Retrospective cohort; five surgeons each with >10 years implant experience.

## 2. Key Contributions

- First report assessing **photofunctionalisation (chairside UV, 172 nm excimer, 1 min)** specifically in all-on-four full-arch immediate loading early failure.
- Confirms **maxilla** (implant level, OR 3.12) and **smoking** (patient level, OR 2.92) as significant early-failure risk factors even in immediate-loaded full-arch cases.
- Implant length, diameter, angulation, and primary stability (insertion torque bands) were NOT significant for early failure in this cohort.
- Photofunctionalisation lowered failure numerically at every level but never reached significance (OR 0.51, p=0.25) — surface-only on anodic-oxidation implants.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Design: retrospective cohort. Population: edentulous patients treated by all-on-four with immediate loading, both jaws.
- Sample reported: 617 patients / 2453 fixtures in Results (abstract states 561 patients / 2364 implants for analysed all-on-four cohort).
- Implants: anodic-oxidation surface (Nobel Speedy Groovy); posterior tilted ≥4.0 mm, anterior ≥3.3 mm; 17°/30° angled abutments; same-day provisional, final prosthesis at 6 months.
- Photofunctionalisation: applied to implants placed after Aug 2016 (Super Osseo Integration Excimer UV, 172 nm, 20 mW/cm², 1 min, chairside).
- Outcome: early failure = removal within 1 year of implantation (mobility, persistent pain, swelling, inflammation/abscess). Patient-level = ≥1 implant lost.
- Exclusions: zygomatic implants, immunosuppression, active malignancy, IV bisphosphonates, uncontrolled systemic disease (e.g., diabetes).
- Analysis: chi-square univariate → multivariate logistic regression; odds ratios; SPSS 20; p<0.05.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

- 1-year survival (early-failure) rates: maxilla patient 97.1% (2.9%), maxilla implant 98.9% (1.1%); mandible patient 98.5% (1.5%), mandible implant 99.6% (0.4%). Maxilla significantly lower at implant level (p=0.043).
- Univariate: male 3.4% vs female 1.0% (p=0.043); smokers 4.1% vs non-smokers 1.3% (p=0.023); osteoporosis trend (p=0.051).
- Photofunctionalisation: maxilla implant level Yes 0.6% (3/486) vs No 1.3% (12/890); overall implant level Yes 0.5% vs No 0.9% (p=0.25).
- Multivariate: maxilla OR 3.12 (p=0.0444, implant level); smoking OR 2.92 (p=0.0296, patient level); male sex OR 1.25 (p=0.649, NS); photofunctionalisation OR 0.51 (p=0.237, NS).
- Not significant: implant angulation (straight 0.6% vs tilted 1.0%, p=0.24), length, diameter, primary stability.
- Context cited: Derks (Swedish) 9-y failure 7.6% (4.4% before superstructure); Lin (18,199 pts/30,959 implants) early failures = 48.1% of all lost implants.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Single implant surface (anodic oxidation) only → photofunctionalisation effect by surface type (acid-etched/blasted) not assessable.
- Opposing-teeth status, parafunction, and systemic-disease medications not analysed.
- Low absolute event count (≈19 implant failures) limits power, especially for the photofunctionalisation subgroup.
- Discrepancy between abstract n (561/2364) and Results n (617/2453); peri-implant bone/soft-tissue parameters not assessed (early-failure focus).

## 6. Related Work

- Smoking-early-failure pooled estimate (Fan 2024 SR+MA); general risk-factor cohort (Yari 2023).
- Wu et al. meta-analysis cited within: maxilla as risk factor in immediate-loading early failure.
- Malo all-on-four long-term outcome data referenced for survival benchmarks.

## 7. Glossary

- **All-on-four**: full-arch fixed rehabilitation on four implants (two axial anterior, two tilted posterior) with immediate loading.
- **Photofunctionalisation**: UV treatment of titanium to remove hydrocarbons and restore hydrophilicity ("reverse ageing"), improving osseointegration.
- **Anodic oxidation surface**: moderately rough/hydrophilic Ti surface (e.g., TiUnite) formed by electrochemical oxidation.
- **Early failure (here)**: implant removal within 1 year of immediate loading.
