## Why Ingested

Adds furcation-specific regenerative evidence to the wiki's periodontal regeneration base. The intrabony-defect NMA [[wiki/periodontics/stavropoulos-2021-periodontal-regeneration-intrabony-nma]] ranks biologics/grafts/membranes for **intrabony** defects but leaves furcations open; this Miron 2024 SR+MA (21 RCTs, all class II furcations) fills that indication and quantifies where PRF sits versus OFD, bone graft, collagen membrane, PRP and rhBMP2. It also supplies the regenerative counterpart to the prognostic picture in [[wiki/periodontics/nibali-2016-tooth-loss-furcation-involvement-sr-ma]] (furcation-involved molars lose teeth faster) by showing which adjuncts convert class II furcations toward class I. Ingested as one of a set of Miron/Moraschini/Barootchi autologous-platelet-concentrate SR/MAs.

## One-line Summary

Systematic review + meta-analysis of 21 RCTs (all class II furcation defects, 786 patients, mean 9.42-mo follow-up): adding PRF to OFD (or to bone graft) significantly improves PPD/VCAL/HCAL (e.g., OFD vs OFD/PRF: PPD MD 1.73 mm, VCAL MD 1.42 mm), PRF alone is clinically equivalent to bone graft/collagen membrane/PRP/rhBMP2, and layering a small biomolecule (metformin, bisphosphonate, statin) or amniotic membrane onto PRF further improves outcomes — amid very high heterogeneity (I²≈85–94%).

## 한줄요약

21개 RCT(전부 2급 치근이개부병소, 786명, 평균 9.42개월) SR+메타분석: 치은박리소파술(OFD) 또는 골이식(BG)에 혈소판농축피브린(PRF)을 더하면 PPD·수직/수평 부착수준이 유의하게 개선(OFD vs OFD/PRF: PPD 1.73 mm, VCAL 1.42 mm)되고, PRF 단독은 골이식·콜라겐막·PRP·rhBMP2와 임상적으로 동등하며, PRF에 소분자(메트포르민·비스포스포네이트·스타틴)나 양막을 추가하면 결과가 더 좋아진다 — 단 이질성이 매우 높음(I²≈85–94%).

## 1. Document Information

- **Journal**: Periodontology 2000, 2024
- **DOI**: 10.1111/prd.12583 · **PMID**: 39324633 · **PMCID**: PMC11808472
- **Design**: Systematic review + meta-analysis of RCTs (PRISMA; PRISMA-P protocol; INPLASY 2023100045)
- **Scope**: Class II periodontal furcation defects only; 21 RCTs (2011–2023), 786 participants, mean follow-up 9.42 months, no smokers included
- **Databases**: PubMed/MEDLINE, Cochrane CENTRAL, Scopus, Embase, Lilacs (before Oct 2023) + gray literature (Literature Report, OpenGrey)
- **Outcomes**: PPD reduction; vertical & horizontal CAL gain (VCAL, HCAL); vertical & horizontal bone level (VBL, HBL); radiographic bone fill (RBF)
- **Risk of bias**: Cochrane RoB 2; random-effects meta-analysis (RevMan 5.2.8)

## 2. Key Contributions

- Most comprehensive furcation-specific PRF synthesis to date: 11 comparison categories organized into 3 questions — Group 1 (add PRF), Group 2 (PRF vs other modalities), Group 3 (add biomaterial/biomolecule to PRF).
- Quantifies the **additive** benefit of PRF over OFD and over bone graft, with pooled mean differences and 95% CIs.
- Establishes PRF's **clinical equivalence** to bone graft, collagen membrane, PRP, and rhBMP2 for class II furcations.
- Flags PRF as a **drug-delivery matrix**: adding small biomolecules (metformin, bisphosphonates, statins) or amniotic membrane significantly outperforms PRF alone, whereas adding a bone graft to PRF gives only mild/inconsistent gains.
- Documents major evidence gaps: no human histology confirming true periodontal regeneration, wide PRF-preparation variability (RCF under-reported), and sub-12-month follow-up.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- **PICOS / eligibility**: RCTs in periodontitis-related class II furcation defects comparing PRF with (or in combination with) another modality; ≥6-month follow-up; case reports, animal studies, retrospective studies excluded.
- **Extraction** (duplicate): participants, smokers, gender, age, surgical technique, number of treated furcation defects, follow-up, centrifugation parameters, blood volume, centrifugation system, defect type, and mean difference in PPD/VCAL/HCAL/VBL/HBL/RBF.
- **Statistics**: mean difference with 95% CI, generic inverse-variance, random-effects model (chosen for population/follow-up/setting variability). Heterogeneity by χ² (≤25% low, >25–50% moderate, >50% high). p<0.05. Publication-bias tests deferred (no comparison had >10 studies).
- **PRF protocols in included RCTs**: Remi centrifuge in 10/21 (48%); 3000 rpm × 10 min in 12/21 (57%); 4/21 did not report the centrifuge.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

**Group 1 — add PRF (favors PRF):**
- PPD reduction (11 studies, I²=94%): OFD vs OFD/PRF MD **1.73 mm** (95% CI 1.13–2.33); BG vs BG/PRF MD **0.73 mm** (0.25–1.21).
- VCAL gain (11 studies, I²=90%): OFD/PRF MD **1.42 mm** (1.04–1.79); BG/PRF MD **0.82 mm** (0.28–1.37).
- HCAL gain (10 studies, I²=86%): OFD/PRF MD **1.21 mm** (0.70–1.73); BG/PRF MD **1.29 mm** (0.68–1.90).
- Narrative (5 OFD-vs-OFD/PRF RCTs): ~1.3 mm relative PPD decrease, ~1.5 mm CAL gain.
- Furcation closure: Sharma & Pradeep — 66.7% full closure, 27.7% class I conversion with OFD/PRF; Lohi — class I conversion 60% (BG) vs 100% (BG/PRF).

**Group 2 — PRF vs other modalities (all NON-significant → equivalence):**
- PPD (6 studies, I²=45%): BG p=0.26, CM p=0.07, PRP p=0.20, rhBMP2 p=0.93.
- VCAL (5 studies, I²=56%): all NS. HCAL (4 studies, I²=0%): all NS.
- → OFD/PRF ≈ OFD/BG ≈ OFD/CM ≈ OFD/PRP ≈ OFD/rhBMP2.

**Group 3 — add biomaterial/biomolecule to PRF (favors the addition):**
- PPD (8 studies, I²=85%): +AM MD 1.20 mm (p=0.0003); +metformin 1.06 mm (p=0.001); +bisphosphonate 0.83 mm (p<0.00001); +statin 0.94 mm (p=0.0001); **+BG NS** 0.20 mm (−0.28–0.68, p=0.42).
- VCAL (8 studies, I²=0%): +BG 1.20 mm (p=0.003); +AM 0.86 mm; +metformin 0.89 mm; +bisphosphonate 0.86 mm — all significant.
- HCAL (7 studies, I²=0%): +AM 0.80 mm (p=0.02); +metformin 0.80 mm; +bisphosphonate 0.71 mm; +statin 1.08 mm — all significant.
- Furcation closure (PRF vs PRF+metformin): 52% vs 76% full closure.

**Risk of bias (RoB 2):** 1/21 low (all domains), 3/21 high (mainly dropout >22%), 17/21 "some concerns" (mostly missing allocation-concealment detail).

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- **Very high heterogeneity** (I²≈85–94% in Groups 1 & 3) → interpret pooled estimates cautiously; random-effects throughout.
- No human **histology** proving true periodontal regeneration (PDL/cementum/bone) with PRF in furcations.
- Wide, under-reported PRF preparation (most 3000 rpm × 10 min; RCF rarely reported) → poor reproducibility.
- Mean follow-up 9.42 months < EFP-recommended 12 months.
- Hard-tissue outcomes (VBL/HBL/RBF) too sparse to meta-analyze in most comparisons.
- All evidence is class II (no class III); no smokers; predominantly mandibular buccal defects.

## 6. Related Work

- Stavropoulos 2021 (intrabony NMA) — companion indication: ranks biologics/grafts/membranes for intrabony (not furcation) defects.
- Nibali 2016 (furcation & tooth loss SR+MA) — prognostic counterpart: furcation-involved molars lose teeth faster; this paper shows which adjuncts improve them.
- S3-level EFP CPG (Herrera et al.) — recommends bone grafts/resorbable membranes/EMD for furcation regeneration; could not appraise platelet concentrates for lack of evidence — the gap this paper addresses.
- Companion Miron/Moraschini/Barootchi autologous-platelet-concentrate SR/MAs (intrabony defects; root coverage) being ingested alongside.

## 7. Glossary

- **PRF (platelet-rich fibrin)**: autologous, single-spin (anticoagulant-free) second-generation platelet concentrate forming a fibrin matrix that releases growth factors; i-PRF = injectable liquid PRF.
- **Furcation defect (class II)**: horizontal bone loss into the furcation of a multi-rooted tooth, but not through-and-through (that would be class III).
- **OFD (open flap debridement)**: access-flap surgery to debride the defect; the surgical control.
- **PPD / VCAL / HCAL**: probing pocket depth / vertical clinical attachment level / horizontal clinical attachment level.
- **VBL / HBL / RBF**: vertical bone level / horizontal bone level / radiographic bone fill.
- **BG / CM / PRP / rhBMP2 / AM**: bone graft / collagen membrane / platelet-rich plasma / recombinant human bone morphogenetic protein-2 / amniotic membrane.
- **DFDBA**: demineralized freeze-dried bone allograft (a bone graft carrying intrinsic growth factors).
- **MD**: mean difference (pooled effect size, in mm).
- **RCF / rpm**: relative centrifugal force / rotations per minute (PRF spin parameters).
