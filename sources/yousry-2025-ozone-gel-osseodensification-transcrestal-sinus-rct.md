## Why Ingested

경치조골 상악동거상(osteotome/osseodensification) 후 ozone gel이 협측 골판 두께·골 폭을 개선하는지 RCT로 검증하기 위해 인제스트. 기존 [[sinus-lift/transcrestal/starch-jensen-2025-transcrestal-sinus-osseodensification-meta-analysis]] 의 골증대 효과 맥락에 ozone이라는 보조제 변수를 추가하되, 본 RCT는 유의 효과 없음을 보고 — 보조제 과신을 경계하는 근거.

## One-line Summary

RCT (closed/transcrestal sinus lift with osteotome) finding ozone gel adjunct did not significantly increase peri-implant bone width or labial plate thickness vs graftless control at 4 months, though both groups reached acceptable dimensions.

## 한줄요약

경치조골(폐쇄형) 상악동거상 RCT — ozone gel 보조제는 무이식 대조군 대비 4개월째 임플란트 주위 골 폭·협측 골판 두께를 유의하게 증가시키지 못함(둘 다 수용 가능 수준).

## 1. Document Information
- Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, published online 2025-04-14.
- DOI: 10.1007/s10006-025-01367-x. Open access (The Author(s) 2025).
- Trial registration ClinicalTrials.gov NCT06604819 (retrospectively registered 2024-09-20).

## 2. Key Contributions
- Tests ozone gel as a biological adjunct in transcrestal sinus elevation with osteotomes.
- Direct radiographic comparison of bone width and labial plate thickness.
- Negative (null) result — adjunct adds no measurable dimensional benefit.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture
- RCT; closed (transcrestal) sinus floor elevation using osteotomes, implant in posterior maxilla.
- Study group: ozone gel applied; control: graftless.
- Radiographic outcomes at 4 months: bone width, labial (buccal) plate of bone thickness.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks
- Bone width at 4 months: control 8.54 ± 1.46 mm vs study (ozone) 8.96 ± 1.66 mm — not significant.
- Labial plate thickness: control 1.86 ± 0.63 mm vs study 1.89 ± 0.51 mm — not significant.
- Conclusion: ozone gel produced no significant difference; both methods acceptable.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work
- Retrospective trial registration; short (4-month) radiographic endpoint.
- Small dimensional differences within measurement error.
- No histology or long-term survival outcome.

## 6. Related Work
- Osteotome/osseodensification transcrestal sinus elevation literature.

## 7. Glossary
- Closed/transcrestal sinus lift: crestal approach elevating the sinus floor through the osteotomy.
- Labial/buccal plate: facial cortical bone plate around the implant.
