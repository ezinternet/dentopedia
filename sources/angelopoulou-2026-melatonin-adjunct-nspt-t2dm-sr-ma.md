## Why Ingested

기존 [[periodontics/barahim-2024-ozone-gel-srp-diabetic-periodontitis-rct]]는 당뇨병성 치주염에서 NSPT(비외과적 치주치료)에 국소 오존 겔을 보조했을 때의 효과를 다룬 단일 RCT다. 본 SR+MA(Angelopoulou 2026)는 같은 T2DM+치주염 모집단에서 *전신* 멜라토닌(melatonin) 보조요법으로 보조제 종류를 확장하고, 풀링된 정량합성(PPD/CAL/HbA1c/hs-CRP)으로 근거 수준을 끌어올린다. 또한 [[periodontics/lee-2020-watanabe-toothpick-method-srp-diabetic-periodontitis-rct]]의 당뇨병성 치주염 SRP 보조 맥락과 짝을 이룬다.

## One-line Summary

SR+MA of 3 RCTs (149 patients; 2 pooled, n=94) — systemic melatonin (3 mg nightly) adjunct to NSPT in T2DM + periodontitis significantly improved PPD (SMD −1.99), CAL (SMD −1.49), HbA1c (SMD −1.15) and hs-CRP (SMD −0.97) vs NSPT/placebo, but all GRADE-low certainty.

## 한줄요약

RCT 3편(149명, 2편 풀링 n=94) SR+MA — T2DM 치주염 환자에서 비외과적 치주치료(NSPT)에 전신 멜라토닌(밤 3 mg) 보조 시 치주낭깊이(PPD, SMD −1.99)·임상부착수준(CAL, SMD −1.49)·당화혈색소(HbA1c, SMD −1.15)·hs-CRP(SMD −0.97)가 유의하게 개선되나 모두 GRADE 근거수준은 낮음(low).

## 1. Document Information

- **Type**: Systematic review + meta-analysis of RCTs (PRISMA 2020; PROSPERO CRD420251155847)
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Medicine 2026;15(11):4071 (e-pub 2026-05-25)
- **DOI**: [10.3390/jcm15114071](https://doi.org/10.3390/jcm15114071) — PMID 42278933, PMCID PMC13257706
- **Source**: PubMed full text (PMC, JATS); retrieved via PubMed get_full_text_article
- **Question (PICOS)**: P = adults with T2DM + periodontitis; I = systemic melatonin (oral tablets) adjunct to NSPT; C = NSPT or NSPT + placebo; O = clinical/biochemical periodontal + systemic (glycemic, oxidative, inflammatory) outcomes; S = RCTs

## 2. Key Contributions

- First SR+MA focused specifically on **systemic (oral tablet) melatonin** — not local orabase/gel/nanoparticle — as an NSPT adjunct in the T2DM + periodontitis population.
- Pools both **periodontal** (PPD, CAL) and **systemic** (HbA1c, hs-CRP, TNF-α, IL-6, TAC) endpoints, leveraging the bidirectional periodontitis–diabetes link.
- Transparent about a thin evidence base: 3 eligible RCTs, only 2 pooled, all outcomes GRADE-low.

## 3. Methodology and Architecture

- Search through 5 Feb 2026: PubMed, Web of Science, Scopus, Cochrane Library + gray literature (Base, ProQuest, ResearchGate, Google Scholar). 182 + 1 records → 13 full-text → 3 included (Cohen's κ > 0.90).
- RoB: Cochrane **RoB 2**. Certainty: **GRADE** (SoF via GRADEpro).
- Meta-analysis: **SMD (Cohen's d)**, 95% CI, random-effects, RevMan 5.3; single 8-week time point pooled; heterogeneity by Cochran's Q + I².
- **Included RCTs**:
  - *Bazyar et al.* (Iran) — double-blind placebo RCT, n=44 (1:1), 8 wk; mild/moderate periodontitis (Eke) + T2DM (ADA); melatonin 2×250 mg tablets each w/ 3 mg, nightly ×8 wk. Results across 3 publications.
  - *Anton et al.* (Romania) — double-blind placebo RCT, n=50 (25 placebo), 8 wk; same regimen; results across 2 papers.
  - *Sarac Gul et al.* (Turkey) — single-blind RCT, n=55; Stage III/IV grade C periodontitis (2018 classification) + T2DM; melatonin 250 mg w/ 3 mg nightly ×30 d vs NSPT alone; 3- and 6-month follow-up. **Not meta-analyzed** (protocol/follow-up/data differences).
- NSPT = SRP + OHI in all trials.

## 4. Key Results and Benchmarks

Pooled (2 RCTs, n=94, 8 wk) — all favoring adjunctive melatonin, all GRADE-**low**:

| Outcome | SMD (95% CI) | p | I² |
|---|---|---|---|
| PPD | −1.99 (−2.54, −1.43) | <0.00001 | 48% (moderate) |
| CAL | −1.49 (−2.03, −0.95) | <0.00001 | 69% (substantial) |
| HbA1c | −1.15 (−1.63, −0.68) | <0.00001 | 38% (moderate) |
| serum hs-CRP | −0.97 (−1.22, −0.73) | <0.00001 | 0% (none) |
| serum TAC | +0.08 (−0.05, +0.20) | 0.23 (NS) | 83% |
| serum TNF-α | −0.51 (−1.92, +0.90) | 0.48 (NS) | 0% |
| serum IL-6 | −5.04 (−13.97, +3.89) | 0.27 (NS) | 99% |

- **Significant** pooled benefit: PPD, CAL, HbA1c, hs-CRP. **Non-significant**: TAC, TNF-α, IL-6.
- Narrative (incl. Sarac Gul): melatonin associated with greater reductions in BoP/GI/PI, GCF MMP-8 and RANKL; oxidative markers (↓MDA/TOS, ↑SOD/CAT/GPx) favored melatonin in individual trials.

## 5. Limitations and Future Work

- Very small evidence base (3 RCTs eligible, 2 pooled, cumulative n small); 2/3 trials RoB "some concerns" (poor protocol pre-registration), 1 "low risk"; overall **GRADE low** for every outcome.
- Clinical heterogeneity: mild/moderate periodontitis (Eke) vs Stage III/IV grade C (2018); follow-up 8 wk vs 3–6 mo; control = placebo+NSPT (2 trials) vs NSPT alone (1).
- HbA1c MA used change-from-baseline (no baseline-adjusted estimates); IL-6 I²=99%.
- Authors: findings **exploratory, not confirmatory**; need larger, adequately powered, standardized, longer-follow-up RCTs.

## 7. Glossary

- **NSPT** — Non-Surgical Periodontal Therapy (SRP + OHI).
- **PPD / CAL** — Probing Pocket Depth / Clinical Attachment Level.
- **SMD** — Standardized Mean Difference (Cohen's d).
- **HbA1c** — glycated hemoglobin (glycemic control marker).
- **hs-CRP** — high-sensitivity C-reactive protein (systemic inflammation).
- **TAC / TOS / MDA** — Total Antioxidant Capacity / Total Oxidant Status / Malondialdehyde (oxidative stress).
- **GCF MMP-8 / RANKL / OPG** — gingival crevicular fluid markers of tissue degradation and bone metabolism.
- **GRADE** — Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation (certainty of evidence).
